# Relates to requiring training for teachers and administrators in the area of childhood trauma

**Bill ID:** A9001AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Yudelka Tapia
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9001AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9001AA)

## Summary

Relates to requiring training for teachers and administrators in the area of childhood trauma after January 1, 2025.
