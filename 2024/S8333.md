# Relates to health insurance for state officers and employees who receive health insurance benefits from the state

**Bill ID:** S8333
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Ramos
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8333 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8333)

## Summary

Requires health insurance for state officers and employees who receive health insurance benefits from the state to take effect immediately upon employment.
