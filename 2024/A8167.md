# Relates to assaults on seniors on public transportation

**Bill ID:** A8167
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8167 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8167)

## Summary

Provides that an assault on a senior citizen on public transportation shall be a class D violent felony punishable by up to 7 years in prison.
