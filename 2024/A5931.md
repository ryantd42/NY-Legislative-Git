# Permits the town board of the town of East Hampton to provide health insurance coverage for enrolled members of the East Hampton Volunteer Ocean Rescue and Auxiliary Squad

**Bill ID:** A5931
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5931 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5931)

## Summary

Permits the town board of the town of East Hampton to provide health insurance coverage for enrolled members of the East Hampton Volunteer Ocean Rescue and Auxiliary Squad as is currently authorized for volunteer firefighters and volunteer ambulance workers.
