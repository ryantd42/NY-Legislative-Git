# Requires a landlord to pay a fee to a tenant if they evict such tenant without cause

**Bill ID:** A8287
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patrick Burke
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8287 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8287)

## Summary

Requires a landlord to pay a fee to a tenant equivalent to one month's rent if they evict such tenant without cause.
