# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the Trooper Joseph J. Gallagher Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** S5426
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mario Mattera
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5426 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5426)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system on the Long Island Expressway, county of Suffolk, as the "Trooper Joseph J. Gallagher Memorial Bridge".
