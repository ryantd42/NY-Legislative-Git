# Relates to the inclusion of batteries used in bicycles with electric assist in the rechargeable battery recycling program

**Bill ID:** A10665
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10665 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10665)

## Summary

Includes batteries used in bicycles with electric assist in the rechargeable battery recycling program.
