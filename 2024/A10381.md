# Authorizes private membership establishments to have certain licenses for sale of alcohol

**Bill ID:** A10381
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10381 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10381)

## Summary

Authorizes private membership establishments to have certain licenses for sale of alcohol for consumption on premises; establishes a private membership establishment must include one hundred or more members.
