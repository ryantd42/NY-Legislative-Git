# Provides for a personal income tax deduction for certain well water testing

**Bill ID:** A6332
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6332 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6332)

## Summary

Provides for a personal income tax deduction, once every three years up to six hundred dollars, for the testing of potable well water by a certified laboratory.
