## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2611
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING KISS Products Inc. upon the occasion of
  its designation for special recognition by Residents
  Forward at their Gala on June 7, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body to honor those
businesses within the State of New  York  which  distinguish  themselves
through  outstanding  professional  behavior,  corporate innovation, and
enduring commitment to the community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend KISS Products Inc. upon the  occasion  of  its  designation  for
special  recognition  by Residents Forward at their Gala on Friday, June
7, 2024, at the North Hempstead Beach Park Pavilion in Port  Washington,
New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Based  in  Port  Washington,  New York, KISS Products is a
global leader in beauty solutions, catering to  consumers  in  over  100
countries;  renowned  for  its  extensive lineup spanning fashion nails,
false eyelashes, color cosmetics, hair styling tools,  and  accessories,
KISS  is committed to delivering accessible, innovative, top-tier beauty
solutions that empower individuals worldwide; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 1989, John Y. Chang, Sung Yong Chang, and Won Shik  Kang
formed  a small startup company named Dae do in Flushing, NY, creating a
unique niche in the artificial nail market; three years later, under the
brand name KISS, a line of 18 nail products was introduced in  Walgreens
Stores across the U.S.; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  2005,  Kiss Products relocated to Port Washington, New
York, occupying a larger facility to accommodate the company's  newfound
growth  and  expansion; in 2014, the company upgraded to a 60,000 square
foot facility, a testament to its ever-growing and  impressive  success;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Ultimately  becoming  a  globally  renowned business, KISS
moved the company to a new global headquarters in 2018; the magnificent,
ultra-modern, 272,000-square foot business complex in  Port  Washington,
NY,  includes  an  on-site  state  of  the  art Research and Development
Center; KISS currently secures strongholds in key countries to establish
the KISS dominance in Europe, allowing  the  company  to  flourish  even
further in the cosmetic and beauty market; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout their many years in business, KISS Products Inc.
has  worked to develop and introduce new products to the market, such as
product  lines  that  include  color  cosmetics,  hair  care,  grooming
implements,  and  beauty  appliances;  another example is their Broadway
Nails imPRESS Press-ON Manicure  which  has  become  a  staple  in  many
well-known retailers; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Today,  as  the  foremost  manufacturer and distributor of
fashion nails globally and the dominant market leader  in  both  fashion
nails  and  false  eyelashes  in  the  U.S.,  their  portfolio  boasts a
 
versatile array of salon-quality products, ensuring effortless self-care
accessible to all; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Through sponsorships and active involvement, KISS Products
contributes to community events and revitalization projects, such as the
restoration of Blumenfeld Family Park's  gardens  and  support  for  the
Annual Main Street Planter Program; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Beyond local outreach, KISS Products extends its influence
to  support  diverse  causes  encompassing  health,  women  and  youth
empowerment,  and  environmental  sustainability;  by  aligning  their
philanthropic efforts with their core values,  KISS  Products  amplifies
their impact, fostering positive change both locally and globally; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The success of KISS Products Inc. is in direct correlation
to the efforts of its dedicated leadership and staff, whose  involvement
is,  and  always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an
unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard  of  excellence  in
all  endeavors  on  behalf  of  the  businesses and the communities they
serve; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  with  great  pleasure  that  this  Legislative  Body
acknowledges  this  exceptional business, its contributions to the local
and state economies, and its vibrant role in improving  the  quality  of
life  in  its  community,  fully confident it will continue to enjoy the
same sparkling successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  KISS  Products  Inc.  upon  the occasion of its designation for
special recognition by Residents Forward at their Gala on June 7,  2024;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to KISS Products Inc.
