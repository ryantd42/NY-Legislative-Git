# Prohibits the involuntary use of sedatives during an arrest

**Bill ID:** S5064
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5064 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5064)

## Summary

Prohibits the administering or directing the administration of a sedative during the arrest of an individual without the consent of such individual.
