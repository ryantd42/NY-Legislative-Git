# Permits certain persons to leave behind opioid antagonists at the location of a known or suspected opioid overdose

**Bill ID:** A9789
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Slater
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9789 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9789)

## Summary

Permits certain persons to leave behind opioid antagonists at the location of a known or suspected opioid overdose.
