# Appropriates certain monies for capital improvements to the historic Forrest Hills Overpass

**Bill ID:** S9165
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9165 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9165)

## Summary

Appropriates $4,000,000 for capital improvements to the historic Forrest Hills overpass.

 AN ACT appropriating certain monies  for  capital  improvements  to  the
  historic Forrest Hills Overpass
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:

  Section 1. The sum of four million dollars ($4,000,000),  or  so  much
 thereof as may be necessary, is hereby appropriated to the department of
 transportation  out  of  any moneys in the state treasury in the general
 fund to the credit of the general fund, not otherwise appropriated,  and
 made  immediately  available, for the purpose of capital improvements to
 the historic Forrest Hills Overpass. Such moneys shall be payable on the
 audit and warrant of the comptroller on vouchers certified  or  approved
 by the comptroller, in consultation with the commissioner of transporta-
 tion, in the manner prescribed by law.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
