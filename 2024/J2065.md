# Commending the George Washington Carver Coalition upon the occasion of hosting its Brothers & Sisters Fundraising Events

**Bill ID:** J2065
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2065 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2065)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2065
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the George Washington Carver Coalition
  upon the occasion of hosting its Brothers &  Sisters
  Fundraising Events
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to commend those
illustrious organizations of the State  of  New  York  whose  focus  and
intent  have  made  a substantial impact on the quality of life in their
communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the George Washington Carver  Coalition  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its Brothers & Sisters Fundraising Events on Saturday, April 6,
2024, at Babylon Masonic Hall; and
 
  WHEREAS, The George Washington Carver Coalition is led by recognized
Free and Accepted Masons whose joint  works  endeavor  to  assist  their
community through charitable events and organizing; and
 
  WHEREAS, The fundraising efforts that they place forward continue to
assist  in  community  projects,  youth  investments  for  afterschool
programs,  and  local  trips,  aiding  the  youth  in  furthering  their
educational and social experiences; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body recognizes the hard work, efforts and
resilience  that  is shown in all the George Washington Carver Coalition
does, including uplifting all those around, with a special  emphasis  on
strengthening the Black community through their acts of kindness; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the George Washington Carver Coalition is in
direct  correlation to the efforts of its members, whose involvement is,
and always has been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment,  an
unbridled  enthusiasm,  and  an uncompromising standard of excellence in
all endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves;
and
 
  WHEREAS, From time to time  this  Legislative  Body  takes  note  of
certain  altruistic  individuals  and  organizations  it  wishes  to
acknowledge for their philanthropic contributions to our  society  which
enhance  the  basic  humanity among us all; the George Washington Carver
Coalition is one such organization; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the  George  Washington  Carver  Coalition upon the occasion of
hosting its Brothers & Sisters Fundraising Events; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the George Washington Carver Coalition.
