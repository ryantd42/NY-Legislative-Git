# Relates to requiring the board of regents to obtain legislative approval of any rule or regulation containing an unfunded mandate

**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik

## Summary

Relates to requiring the board of regents to obtain legislative approval of any rule or regulation containing an unfunded mandate.
