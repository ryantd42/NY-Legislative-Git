# Relates to the hours adult-use cannabis shall be sold for on-site consumption

**Bill ID:** A10013
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10013 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10013)

## Summary

Relates to the hours adult-use cannabis shall be sold for on-site consumption; provides cannabis products shall only be sold for on-site consumption between the hours of 11 a.m. and 11 p.m.
