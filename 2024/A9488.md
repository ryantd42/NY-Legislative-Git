# Prohibits food service establishments from using a dynamic pricing model in determining the prices on its menu

**Bill ID:** A9488
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9488 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9488)

## Summary

Prohibits food service establishments from using a dynamic pricing model in determining the prices on its menu; requires fixed prices be published on a food service establishment's menu.
