# Requires hotel, motel and inn keepers to offer condoms for sale to registered guests

**Bill ID:** A5829
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5829 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5829)

## Summary

Requires hotel, motel and inn keepers to offer, in the registration office, condoms or similar prophylactic devices for sale to registered guests of such hotel, motel or inn.
