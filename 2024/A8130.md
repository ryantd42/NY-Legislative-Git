# Relates to the classification of the drug xylazine as a controlled substance, and to the criminal sale and possession of xylazine or preparations, compounds, mixtures or substances containing xylazine

**Bill ID:** A8130
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joe DeStefano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8130 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8130)

## Summary

Relates to the classification of the drug xylazine as a schedule I controlled substance, and to the criminal sale and possession of xylazine or preparations, compounds, mixtures or substances containing xylazine.
