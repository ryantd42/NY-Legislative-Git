# Relates to requiring state agencies to offer an option to speak to a live agency representative

**Bill ID:** S6057
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6057 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6057)

## Summary

Requires state agencies to offer an option for a member of the public to speak to a live agency representative when contacting such agency.
