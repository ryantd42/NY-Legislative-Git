# Relates to prohibiting members of law enforcement from using tear gas

**Bill ID:** S5298
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5298 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5298)

## Summary

Prohibits members of law enforcement from using tear gas at any time.
