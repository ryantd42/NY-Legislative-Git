# Relates to the notification of county boards of elections of an individual's death

**Bill ID:** A5277
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Edward Flood
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5277 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5277)

## Summary

Relates to the notification of county boards of elections of an individual's death and the subsequent removal of such voter's name from the applicable registration roll.
