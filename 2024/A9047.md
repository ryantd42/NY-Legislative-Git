# Relates to the regulation of cannabinoid hemp products

**Bill ID:** A9047
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Charles Fall
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9047 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9047)

## Summary

Provides that a retailer licensed by the office of cannabis management shall be permitted to sell any and all forms of cannabinoid hemp.
