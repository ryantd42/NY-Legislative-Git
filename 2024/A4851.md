# Establishes a contraband screening plan which includes a canine at the entrance of each correctional facility

**Bill ID:** A4851
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4851 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4851)

## Summary

Establishes a contraband screening plan which includes a canine at the entrance of each correctional facility to detect the presence of illegal drugs.
