# Allows boards of cooperative educational services to install school bus stop sign cameras

**Bill ID:** A5302
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A5302 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5302)

## Summary

Allows boards of cooperative educational services to install school bus stop sign cameras.
