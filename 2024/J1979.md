# Congratulating Liam Carlin of Burnt Hills-Ballston Lake High School upon the occasion of capturing the 2024 NYSPHSAA Boys Wrestling Championship title in Division 1, 145-pound Weight Class on February 24, 2024

**Bill ID:** J1979
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1979 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1979)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1979
 
BY: Senator TEDISCO
 
  CONGRATULATING Liam Carlin of Burnt Hills-Ballston
  Lake  High School upon the occasion of capturing the
  2024 NYSPHSAA Boys Wrestling Championship  title  in
  Division  1,  145-pound Weight Class on February 24,
  2024
 
  WHEREAS,  Excellence  and  success  in  competitive  sports  can  be
achieved  only  through  strenuous  practice, team play and team spirit,
nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  enhances  the  moral  and  physical
development  of  the  young people of this State, preparing them for the
future by instilling in  them  the  value  of  teamwork,  encouraging  a
standard  of  healthy  living,  imparting  a  desire  for  success  and
developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate  Liam
Carlin,  who  captured  the  Division  1,  2024  NYSPHSAA Boys Wrestling
Championship title in the 145-pound Weight Class at  the  MVP  Arena  in
Albany on Saturday, February 24, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and applause of his excited fans, Liam Carlin
won  the  state title by garnering a 3-1 decision over Washingtonville's
Mason Ketcham; and
 
  WHEREAS, Utilizing outstanding talent  and  exceptional  team  play,
Liam  Carlin  capped off a spectacular season with a 44-3 record and was
the top seed in the 145-pound  bracket  entering  the  state  tournament
matches; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Prior  to  his  finals  match,  Liam  Carlin  recorded two
straight wins, with pins in times of 1:11  and  1:04  during  the  first
rounds of the tournament; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Earlier  in  the season, Liam Carlin was recognized by the
Times Union, a Capital Region newspaper being awarded Boys'  Athlete  of
the Week on December 21, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  athletic  talent displayed by this scholar athlete is
due in great part to the efforts of a great team of coaches, skilled and
inspirational tutors, respected for their ability to  develop  potential
into excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  wrestling,  which demands athletic
prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach Stephen  Jones,  worked  hard  to
hone  the  skills  of  this  champion, teaching this outstanding athlete
lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the mat; and
 
  WHEREAS, In light of his  recent  accomplishments  Liam  Carlin  has
brought  honor  and just pride to his school and community on the local,
and State level; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Liam  Carlin of Burnt Hills-Ballston Lake High School upon
the occasion of capturing the 2024 NYSPHSAA Boys Wrestling  Championship
title in Division 1, 145-pound Weight Class on February 24, 2024; and be
it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Liam Carlin, Head Coach Stephen Jones, Athletic  Director
Joe  Scalise,  Principal  Timothy  Brunson  and  Superintendent  Patrick
McGrath, Jr.
