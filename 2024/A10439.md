# Relates to the identification of correction officers

**Bill ID:** A10439
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Lemondes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10439 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10439)

## Summary

Requires that correction officers employed in correctional facilities be identified by badge number only.
