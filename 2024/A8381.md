# Suspends fees in connection with a REAL ID driver's license until 2026

**Bill ID:** A8381
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8381 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8381)

## Summary

Suspends fees in connection with a REAL ID driver's license or non-driver ID card until 2026; refunds fees paid since 2017.
