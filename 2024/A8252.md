# Establishes the crime of theft of an impounded vehicle

**Bill ID:** A8252
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8252 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8252)

## Summary

Establishes the crime of theft of an impounded vehicle; establishes that such crime is a class A misdemeanor.
