# Compels the delivery of real property subject to a fraudulent transfer to the person entitled thereto

**Bill ID:** S6196
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6196 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6196)

## Summary

Compels the delivery of real property subject to a fraudulent transfer to the person entitled thereto; provides for the recording of judgments and orders relating to a fraudulent conveyance of title to property.
