# Congratulating the Maine-Endwell High School Boys Varsity Baseball Team upon the occasion of capturing the 2024 NYSPHSAA Section IV Class A Boys Baseball Championship

**Bill ID:** K2342
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Donna Lupardo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2342 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2342)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2342
 
BY: M. of A. Lupardo
 
  CONGRATULATING  the Maine-Endwell High School Boys
  Varsity Baseball Team upon the occasion of capturing
  the 2024 NYSPHSAA Section IV Class A  Boys  Baseball
  Championship
 
  WHEREAS,  The  tradition  of  athletic success at Maine-Endwell High
School has continued with the Boys Varsity Baseball  Team's  victory  in
the  NYSPHSAA Section IV Class A Championship game, defeating Owego Free
Academy with a score of 8-6, at Maine-Endwell High  School  in  Endwell,
New  York,  on  Saturday, May 25, 2024, and capturing the team's Class A
Championship title; and
 
  WHEREAS, A five-run fourth inning helped the Spartans triumph in the
deciding game of the Section IV Class A Baseball Tournament;  the  River
Hawks  entered  the  inning  with  a  3-1  advantage; however, a two-run
double, followed by a two-run single, gave Maine-Endwell a 4-3 lead over
Owego; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Spartans scored two more runs in the sixth inning;  the
River Hawks, scored three runs in the seventh inning before the Spartans
recorded a title-clinching strikeout with the bases loaded; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Spartans were loyally and enthusiastically supported by
family, fans, friends, and the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  congratulates  the members of the
Maine-Endwell High School Boys Varsity Baseball Team:  Stratos  Anastos,
Adam  DeSantis,  Jacob  Durgala,  Liam Hadfield, Jack Hennessey, Michael
Jamba, Gage Kelly,  Cooper  Klinger,  Mason  Little,  Santino  Michitti,
Preston  Nezelek,  Austyn  Nyschot,  Braden  Palmer,  Max Policare, Cade
Raleigh, Dominic Randesi, Joe Robillard, Matthew Rombold, Logan Vazquez,
and Grady Wilson; Head Coach, Matt Raleigh;  Athletics  Director,  Kevin
Hores;  and Assistant, Shelly Goldstone, on their outstanding season and
overall team record; and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic  competition  instills  the  values  of  teamwork,
sportsmanship,  pride,  and  accomplishment;  Head  Coach, Matt Raleigh,
Athletics Director, Kevin Hores, Assistant, Shelly Goldstone, and all of
the outstanding athletes have clearly made a contribution to the  spirit
of excellence that is a tradition at Maine-Endwell High School; and
 
  WHEREAS,  These  competitors  have  made  their  mark  through  a
combination of scoring power and outstanding team play; let us celebrate
this championship season; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pauses in its deliberations  to
congratulate  and  honor  the  Maine-Endwell  High  School  Boys Varsity
Baseball Team upon the occasion of capturing the 2024  NYSPHSAA  Section
IV  Class  A Boys Baseball Championship; winning a state championship is
an enormous achievement, made possible this season  through  the  team's
exceptional  skill,  persistence,  teamwork, and energy delivered in the
spirit of  the  best  collegiate,  Olympic,  and  professional  baseball
championship teams; and be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted  to  the  members,  coaches,  and  administrators  of  the
Maine-Endwell High School Boys Varsity Baseball Team.
