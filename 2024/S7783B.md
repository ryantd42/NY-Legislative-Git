# Establishes the "New York junk fee prevention act"

**Bill ID:** S7783BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Gianaris
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S7783BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7783BA)

## Summary

Establishes the "New York junk fee prevention act"; requires clear and conspicuous pricing practices regarding trash junk fees.
