# Concurrent resolution to amend the constitution to increase the length of the term of office and imposing term limits for members of the legislature

**Bill ID:** S5319
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5319 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5319)

## Summary

Increases the length of the term of office of members of the legislature to four years; imposes term limits for members of the legislature.
