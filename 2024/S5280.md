# Establishes a division of early childhood within the office of children and family services

**Bill ID:** S5280
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5280 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5280)

## Summary

Establishes a division of early childhood within the office of children and family services to coordinate, consolidate and integrate early childhood programs.
