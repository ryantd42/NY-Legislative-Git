# Makes certain state lands in the county of Oswego subject to taxation for all purposes

**Bill ID:** S5405
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S5405 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5405)

## Summary

Makes certain state lands in the towns of Albion, Amboy, Boylston, Constantia, Parish, West Monroe and Williamstown, in the county of Oswego, subject to taxation for all purposes.
