# Relates to authorizing and permitting appearance enhancement licensees to apply dye to eyelash and eyebrow hair

**Bill ID:** A8051AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Zaccaro Jr.
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8051AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8051AA)

## Summary

Establishes Sabria's law to authorize and permit appearance enhancement licensees to apply dye to eyelash and eyebrow hair.
