# Enacts the "Freedom of Choice in Health Care Act"

**Bill ID:** A6061
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Josh Jensen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6061 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6061)

## Summary

Preserves New Yorker's freedom of choice in health care.
