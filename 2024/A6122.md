# Creates an after-school challenge program to provide schools with matching funds for resources raised to support after-school programs

**Bill ID:** A6122
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian F. Curran
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6122 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6122)

## Summary

Creates an after-school challenge program to provide schools with up to $50,000 in matching funds for resources raised in private donations to support after-school programs which are curriculum based or community service programs.
