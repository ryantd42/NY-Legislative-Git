# Establishes the offense of obstructing a police officer

**Bill ID:** A5540
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Doug Smith
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5540 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5540)

## Summary

Establishes the offense of obstructing a police officer.
