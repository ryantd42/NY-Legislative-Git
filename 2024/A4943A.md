# Relates to prohibiting the sale or distribution of expanded polystyrene containers that are designed or intended to be used for cold storage

**Bill ID:** A4943AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A4943AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4943AA)

## Summary

Relates to prohibiting the sale or distribution of expanded polystyrene containers that are designed or intended to be used for cold storage beginning January 1, 2026.
