# Separates interscholastic athletic championships between public and non-public schools

**Bill ID:** A10121
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Slater
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10121 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10121)

## Summary

Separates interscholastic athletic championships between public and non-public schools.
