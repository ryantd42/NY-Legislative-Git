# Establishes a one-stop farming hotline with the Cornell cooperative extension

**Bill ID:** A5117
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5117 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5117)

## Summary

Establishes a one-stop farming hotline with the Cornell cooperative extension.
