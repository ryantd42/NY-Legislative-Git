**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1975 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1975)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1975
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Most  Excellent Prince Hall Grand
  Chapter of the State of New York, Mid Island Chapter
  No. 37 upon the occasion  of  hosting  its  Red  and
  Black Affair on March 23, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of the State of New
York, Mid Island Chapter No. 37 upon the occasion of hosting its Red and
Black  Affair  on  Saturday,  March  23, 2024, at the Babylon Historical
Society in Babylon, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of  the  State
of  New  York,  Mid  Island  Chapter  No. 37 is being recognized for its
steadfast and  unwavering  commitment  to  community  service  as  shown
through  its  participation  in  countless  local  initiatives  and
fundraisers; and
 
  WHEREAS, The members of this vital  organization  have  shown  great
dedication  and  discipline,  holding  true  to  their mission and their
mandate of putting others above themselves; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Mid-Island Chapter No. 37 Red and Black Affair has  and
will  continue  to  support the members of its community with a focus on
youth programs, auxiliary groups,  and  community  outreach,  all  while
caring for the sick and shut-in throughout the district; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Furthermore, this organization's desire to do good work in
the community is an inspiration to all and continues to raise the bar by
achieving greatness in all its desired areas;  the  proficiency  of  the
Chapter  and all their good deeds in caring for their neighbors shows in
each benevolent action and a lifetime of  building  true  unity  in  the
community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  Mid  Island  Chapter No. 37 is in direct
correlation to the efforts of  its  dedicated  leadership  and  members,
whose  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an
impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising
standard  of  excellence  in all endeavors on behalf of the organization
and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and commend
the members of Mid Island Chapter No. 37, past  and  present,  who  have
 
labored so conscientiously to secure and improve the quality of life for
the community it serves; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of the State of New
York, Mid Island Chapter No. 37 upon the occasion of hosting its Red and
Black Affair on March 23, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of the State
of New York, Mid Island Chapter No. 37.
