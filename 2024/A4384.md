# Relates to pre-arranged for-hire vehicle transportation services and universal design and accessibility

**Bill ID:** A4384
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4384 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4384)

## Summary

Relates to pre-arranged for-hire vehicle transportation services and the maximization of universal design and accessibility by persons with disabilities.

  (C) "UNIVERSAL DESIGN" OR "UNIVERSALLY DESIGNED" SHALL MEAN THE DESIGN

 VISION;
