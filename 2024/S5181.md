# Prohibits the discharge of any radiological agent into the waters of the state

**Bill ID:** S5181
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5181 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5181)

## Summary

Prohibits the discharge of any radiological agent into the waters of the state.
