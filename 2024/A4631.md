# Relates to the governance of the Westchester county health care corporation

**Bill ID:** A4631
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4631 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4631)

## Summary

Relates to the governance of the Westchester county health care corporation to twenty-one voting directors and provides for the appointment of such members.
