# Relates to the health and environmental impacts of lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles, and the manufacturing thereof

**Bill ID:** A4848
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4848 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4848)

## Summary

Educates the public about the health and environmental impacts of lithium-ion battery use and manufacture on air, water and soil.
