# Prohibits the use of polystyrene foam docks, buoys and floating structures

**Bill ID:** A8142A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A8142A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8142A)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale, offer for sale, or distribution of polystyrene foam docks, buoys and floating structures in the state.

 § 27-3401. DEFINITIONS.
  FOR  THE  PURPOSES  OF  THIS TITLE, THE FOLLOWING TERMS SHALL HAVE THE
 FOLLOWING MEANINGS:
  1. "BUOY" MEANS ANY FLOAT OR MARKER THAT  IS  ATTACHED  TO  A  MOORING

  2.  "DOCK"  MEANS  AN UNENCLOSED STRUCTURE SECURED TO LAND, LAND UNDER

 ENCLOSED OR HAS TWO OR MORE LEVELS.
  3.  "ENCAPSULATED"  MEANS  A  PROTECTIVE  COVERING OR PHYSICAL BARRIER
 BETWEEN THE POLYSTYRENE DEVICE AND THE WATER.
  4. "EXPANDED POLYSTYRENE FOAM"  MEANS  A  THERMOPLASTIC  PETROCHEMICAL
 MATERIAL  UTILIZING  THE  STYRENE MONOMER THAT IS PROCESSED ACCORDING TO

 § 27-3403. PROHIBITION  ON POLYSTYRENE DOCKS, BUOYS, AND FLOATING STRUC-
  TURES.
  1. BEGINNING ONE YEAR AFTER THE  EFFECTIVE  DATE  OF  THIS  TITLE,  NO
 PERSON  SHALL SELL, OFFER TO SELL, OR DISTRIBUTE DOCKS, BUOYS, OR FLOAT-
 ING STRUCTURES MADE WITH EXPANDED POLYSTYRENE FOAM, EXTRUDED POLYSTYRENE
 FOAM, OR RIGID POLYSTYRENE.
  2. UNUSED OR SIGNIFICANTLY  DEGRADED  POLYSTYRENE  DOCKS,  BUOYS,  AND
 FLOATING  STRUCTURES  SHALL  BE REMOVED FROM THE WATERS OF THE STATE AND
 SHALL BE DISPOSED OF IN A MANNER DETERMINED BY THE COMMISSIONER.
  3. POLYSTYRENE DOCKS, BUOYS, AND FLOATING STRUCTURES CURRENTLY IN  USE
 AND  OF  GOOD  CONDITION  SHALL BE ENCAPSULATED COMPLETELY BY A PHYSICAL
 BARRIER BETWEEN THE EXPANDED, EXTRUDED, OR  RIGID  POLYSTYRENE  AND  THE
 WATER. SMALL GAPS UP TO THREE QUARTERS OF AN INCH DIAMETER BALLAST HOLES
 ARE  PERMITTED IN THE PHYSICAL BARRIER OR COVERING PROVIDED THEY ARE 0.1
 PERCENT OR LESS OF THE SQUARE FOOTAGE OF THE  BUOY,  DOCK,  OR  FLOATING
 STRUCTURE.
  ALL  MATERIALS AND METHODS OF ENCAPSULATION SHALL PROVIDE AN EFFECTIVE
 PHYSICAL BARRIER BETWEEN THE EXPANDED, EXTRUDED,  OR  RIGID  POLYSTYRENE
 FOAM  AND  THE WATER FOR A PERIOD NOT LESS THAN TEN YEARS. ANY FASTENERS
 USED TO HOLD  ENCAPSULATION  MATERIALS  TOGETHER  SHALL  BE  EFFECTIVELY
 TREATED OR BE OF A FORM RESISTANT TO CORROSION AND DECAY.
  4. USE OF UNENCAPSULATED POLYSTYRENE DOCKS, BUOYS, AND FLOATING STRUC-
 TURES  SHALL  BE  PROHIBITED  ONE  YEAR AFTER THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF THIS
 TITLE.
 § 27-3405. USE OF EXPANDED, EXTRUDED, OR RIGID POLYSTYRENE FOAM IN PROX-
  IMITY TO WATER.
  NO PERSON SHALL SELL, OFFER TO SELL, OR DISTRIBUTE ANY OF THE  FOLLOW-
 ING  PRODUCTS  IN  OR  ON  THE WATERS OF THE STATE OR WITHIN TWO HUNDRED
 FIFTY FEET OF WATERS OF THE STATE IF THE PRODUCT IS COMPOSED ENTIRELY OR
 IN PART OF EXPANDED, EXTRUDED, OR  RIGID  POLYSTYRENE  FOAM  UNLESS  THE
 PRODUCT  IS  ENCAPSULATED  BY  A  PROTECTIVE  COVERING OR IS DESIGNED TO
 PREVENT THE EXPANDED, EXTRUDED, OR RIGID POLYSTYRENE FOAM FROM  DISINTE-
 GRATING INTO THE WATER:
  1. COOLERS, ICE CHESTS, OR SIMILAR CONTAINERS;
  2. POOL TOYS, BEACH TOYS, OR SWIMMING AIDS; OR
  3. BUOYS, DOCKS, OR FLOATING STRUCTURES.
 § 27-3407. NUISANCE.
  THE  USE OF UNENCAPSULATED EXPANDED, EXTRUDED, OR RIGID POLYSTYRENE AS
 A BUOY, DOCK, OR FLOATING STRUCTURE IS  A  NUISANCE  AND  PUBLIC  HEALTH
 HAZARD  AND MAY BE PROSECUTED ACCORDING TO THE LAWS, RULES, REGULATIONS,
 AND CODES SET FORTH BY THE COMMISSIONER PURSUANT TO THIS CHAPTER.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
