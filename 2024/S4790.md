# Requires medical assistance coverage for medically tailored meals

**Bill ID:** S4790
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S4790 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4790)

## Summary

Requires medical assistance coverage for medically tailored meals and medical nutrition therapy for the purpose of disease management.
