# Establishes the nutrient inactivant application permit

**Bill ID:** S8419A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8419A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8419A)

## Summary

Establishes the nutrient inactivant application permit to allow for the use of products used for controlling phosphorus levels in lakes or ponds to prevent and inhibit harmful algal blooms.

 § 17-2301. DEFINITIONS.
  AS USED IN THIS TITLE THE FOLLOWING TERMS  SHALL  HAVE  THE  FOLLOWING

 PERMIT ISSUED UNDER THIS TITLE FOR THE APPLICATION OF  NUTRIENT  INACTI-

 EIGHT  OF  THIS  ARTICLE AND SHALL HAVE THE SAME REQUIREMENTS AND PRIVI-

 § 17-2303. NUTRIENT INACTIVANT APPLICATOR PERMITS.
  1.  NOTWITHSTANDING  ANY OTHER PROVISION OF LAW OR REGULATION PURSUANT
 TO THIS TITLE, A PERSON WHO HAS A VALID NUTRIENT  INACTIVANT  APPLICATOR
 PERMIT  ISSUED BY THE COMMISSIONER MAY ENGAGE IN THE COMMERCIAL APPLICA-

  2. APPLICATION FOR A NUTRIENT INACTIVANT APPLICATOR  PERMIT  SHALL  BE

 BY THE COMMISSIONER AND SHALL BE ACCOMPANIED BY THE FEE OR FEES PROVIDED

  3. AN APPLICANT FOR A NUTRIENT INACTIVANT APPLICATOR PERMIT SHALL:

 ARTICLE THIRTY-THREE OF THIS CHAPTER.

 HOLDS  A  VALID  IDENTIFICATION CARD ISSUED BY THE COMMISSIONER. CERTIF-
 ICATION IDENTIFICATION CARDS SHALL BE VALID FOR THREE YEARS.
  6. A PERSON WITH A VALID NUTRIENT INACTIVANT APPLICATOR  PERMIT  SHALL
 MAINTAIN  SUCH  RECORDS  AND  SHALL  FURNISH SUCH REPORTS CONCERNING THE
 APPLICATION OF NUTRIENT INACTIVANTS AS THE COMMISSIONER  SHALL  REQUIRE.
 ALL  RECORDS  REQUIRED  TO BE KEPT UNDER THIS SECTION SHALL BE AVAILABLE
 FOR INSPECTION BY THE COMMISSIONER.
 § 17-2305. DENIAL OR REVOCATION OF PERMITS.
  THE COMMISSIONER, AFTER DUE NOTICE AND OPPORTUNITY OF  HEARING  TO  AN
