# Extends provisions related to services to out-of-state school districts by boards of cooperative educational services

**Bill ID:** A9470
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Benedetto
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A9470 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9470)

## Summary

Extends provisions related to services to out-of-state school districts by boards of cooperative educational services to July 1, 2029.
