# Requires the establishment of a plant-based agriculture grant program

**Bill ID:** S9656
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jabari Brisport
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9656 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9656)

## Summary

Requires the establishment of a plant-based agriculture grant program to assist small and medium-sized farms transition to plant-based agriculture.

  (I)  "MIDSIZE FARM" MEANS A FARM WITH AN ANNUAL GROSS CASH FARM INCOME
 BETWEEN THREE HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND AND NINE HUNDRED NINETY-NINE  THOU-

  2.  THE  DEPARTMENT  SHALL  ESTABLISH  A PLANT-BASED AGRICULTURE GRANT
 PROGRAM TO ASSIST SMALL AND MEDIUM-SIZED FARMS TRANSITION TO PLANT-BASED
 AGRICULTURE.  SUCH PROGRAM SHALL, AT A MINIMUM:
  (A) PROVIDE GRANTS TO PERSONS FARMING ON SMALL  TO  MIDSIZE  FARMS  TO
 TRANSITION  THE  USE  OF THE LAND FROM RAISING LIVESTOCK OR GROWING FEED
 CROPS TO PLANT-BASED AGRICULTURE.
  (B) DEVELOP BEST PRACTICES FOR TRANSITIONING  LAND  USED  FOR  RAISING
 LIVESTOCK OR GROWING FEED CROPS TO PLANT-BASED AGRICULTURE.
  (C)  PROVIDE  TECHNICAL  ASSISTANCE,  IN  CONSULTATION  WITH TECHNICAL
 ASSISTANCE PROVIDERS, TO PERSONS FARMING ON SMALL TO MIDSIZE FARMS.
  3. THE DEPARTMENT SHALL REQUIRE AN APPLICANT FOR A GRANT TO SUBMIT THE
 FOLLOWING TO THE DEPARTMENT IN THE APPLICATION:
  (A) A DESCRIPTION OF ANY CROPS CURRENTLY PRODUCED ON THE FARM;
  (B) A DESCRIPTION OF THE LAND THAT WILL BE TRANSITIONED  FROM  RAISING
 LIVESTOCK OR GROWING FEED CROPS TO PLANT-BASED AGRICULTURE;
  (C)  A  PLAN  THAT DEMONSTRATES HOW THE APPLICANT WILL TRANSITION SUCH
 LAND TO PLANT-BASED AGRICULTURE USING THE BEST  PRACTICES  DEVELOPED  BY
 THE  DEPARTMENT  PURSUANT  TO  PARAGRAPH  (B) OF SUBDIVISION TWO OF THIS
 SECTION;
  (D)  A DESCRIPTION OF WHAT THE FARM WILL TRANSITION TO PRODUCING USING
 PLANT-BASED AGRICULTURE;
  (E) A PROJECTED TIMELINE FOR THE COMPLETION OF THE TRANSITION; AND
  (F) ANY OTHER INFORMATION THE DEPARTMENT DEEMS NECESSARY.
  4. GRANTS SHALL BE AWARDED ON A COMPETITIVE BASIS  THROUGH  A  REQUEST
 FOR  PROPOSAL  PROCESS  WHICH  SHALL  SET  FORTH  THE  STANDARDS FOR THE
 SELECTION PROCESS, THE REQUIRED PROJECT PROPOSAL FORMAT, COSTS  ELIGIBLE
 FOR  FUNDING,  PROJECT  IMPLEMENTATION, REPORTING REQUIREMENTS, AND SUCH
 OTHER PROVISIONS AS THE DEPARTMENT MAY DEEM NECESSARY OR  BENEFICIAL  IN
 IMPLEMENTING THE PROGRAM.
  5.  THE  DEPARTMENT  SHALL  REQUIRE  A  PERSON WHO RECEIVES A GRANT TO
 PROVIDE A REPORT, IN CONSULTATION WITH A TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE  PROVIDER,
 TO  THE DEPARTMENT THAT DEMONSTRATES THAT THE GRANT RECIPIENT IS TRANSI-
 TIONING TO PLANT-BASED AGRICULTURE.
