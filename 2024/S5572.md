# Relates to the payment of wages for certain persons employed in a bona fide executive, administrative, or professional capacity

**Bill ID:** S5572
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5572 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5572)

## Summary

Increases the threshold for applicability of wage payment protections for certain persons employed in a bona fide executive, administrative, or professional capacity, from $900 to $1,300 per week.
