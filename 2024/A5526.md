# Designates a portion of the state highway system the "Trooper Gerard B. Kane and Trooper Arthur B. Reddy Memorial Bridge"

**Bill ID:** A5526
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A5526 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5526)

## Summary

Designates the bridge over the Southern State Parkway on Deer Park Avenue between Parkdale Drive and Sylvan Road in the town of Babylon, county of Suffolk, the "Trooper Gerard B. Kane and Trooper Arthur B. Reddy Memorial Bridge".
