# Relates to advertising restrictions for mobile sports wagering licensees

**Bill ID:** A10658
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10658 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10658)

## Summary

Expands advertising restrictions for gaming facility licensees and applicants to include mobile sports wagering licensees.
