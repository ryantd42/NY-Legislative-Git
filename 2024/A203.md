# Requires the MTA to file a copy of proposed station closings with various governmental agencies

**Bill ID:** A203
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Simcha Eichenstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A203 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A203)

## Summary

Requires the MTA to file a copy of proposed station closings with various governmental agencies; provides that the MTA shall hold a public hearing on any proposed closings.
