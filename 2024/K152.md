## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 152
 
BY: M. of A. Norris
 
  HONORING  James A. Powers upon the occasion of his
  designation as recipient of  the  Lifetime  Achiever
  Award  by  the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March
  9, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
commend  those distinguished citizens who would devote their life's work
and purpose to community service, contributing to  the  welfare  of  the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
James A. Powers upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the
Lifetime Achiever Award by the  Clarence  Chamber  of  Commerce,  to  be
celebrated  at its 50th Annual Awards Dinner on Thursday, March 9, 2023,
at Samuel's Grande Manor in Clarence, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, James A. Powers is receiving this prestigious award for his
steadfast  and  unremitting  commitment  to  his  beloved  community  of
Clarence; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  service to his community, James A. Powers was a member
of the Clarence Board of Education for 21 years, including 15  years  as
Board  President;  during  that  period,  he  helped guide the board and
district through significant changes in school population; he  was  also
active  with the Regional and State School Board Association; in 2007 he
was inducted into  the  Clarence  High  School  Wall  of  Fame  for  his
contributions to the Clarence School District; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For two years, James A. Powers gave of his time to operate
as  the  hands-on local project manager for the construction and opening
of a house for 26 homeless veterans in Buffalo, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, He and his wife  are  members  of  the  Nativity  of  B.V.M
Parish, where Jim has served on numerous parish committees; and
 
  WHEREAS,  James  A.  Powers  served  as  President of the Clarence
Community Food Pantry Board of Directors from 2005 to  2022;  this  food
pantry  was  the Clarence Chamber of Commerce's Organization of the Year
in 2012; and
 
  WHEREAS, When youth soccer came to Clarence in  the  70's,  Jim  got
involved, first as a coach of house and travel league teams, and then as
President  of the Soccer Club; due to the explosive growth of the sport,
he conceived the idea of a large complex of fields  and  facilities  and
led  the  project  to  bring the idea to fruition as the Clarence Soccer
Center, using local contractors and parental volunteers; and
 
  WHEREAS, James A. Powers served for 27 years as a  weekly  volunteer
reader  for  the Niagara Frontier Radio Reading Service which uses radio
and the internet to bring print material such as books,  magazines,  and
newspapers,  to  thousands of blind and visually impaired individuals in
Western New York and Southern Ontario; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  With  him  throughout  have  been  his wife, Kathleen, and
children, Kevin, Amy, and Mark, all of whom feel privileged to be a part
of his life and rejoice in his achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  that  those  who
enhance  the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown a
long and sustained commitment to excellence certainly  have  earned  the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor James A. Powers upon the occasion of his designation as  recipient
of  the  Lifetime Achievement Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to James A. Powers.
