# Designates a portion of the state highway system as "Charlie Bunger Sr. Way"

**Bill ID:** S8790A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8790A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8790A)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system as "Charlie Bunger Sr. Way" in the village of Babylon, county of Suffolk.
