# Limits the number of cases a foster care worker may have under his or her supervision at any one time to 16 cases

**Bill ID:** S5031
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jabari Brisport
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5031 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5031)

## Summary

Limits the number of cases a foster care worker may have under his or her supervision at any one time to 16 cases.
