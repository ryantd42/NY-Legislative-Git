# Provides for accidental disability retirement for deputy sheriffs in Orange county

**Bill ID:** S9628A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9628A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9628A)

## Summary

Provides for accidental disability retirement for deputy sheriffs in Orange county.
