# Commending Colleen Kain Martin upon the occasion of her designation as Grand Marshal of the 2023 Utica St. Patrick's Day Parade

**Bill ID:** K173
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K173 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K173)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 173
 
BY: M. of A. Buttenschon
 
  COMMENDING  Colleen  Kain Martin upon the occasion
  of her designation as  Grand  Marshal  of  the  2023
  Utica St. Patrick's Day Parade
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
individuals  of  true  purpose  and  worthy  accomplishment  who  work
diligently to preserve and celebrate their rich cultural heritage; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Colleen Kain Martin upon the  occasion  of  her  designation  as
Grand Marshal of the 2023 Utica St. Patrick's Day Parade; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  acknowledgment  of  this most prestigious appointment,
Colleen Kain Martin will  have  the  honor  of  leading  the  Utica  St.
Patrick's Day Parade on Friday, March 3, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  tradition  of  the  Utica St. Patrick's Day Parade is
steeped in 200 years of history, with John C. Devereux leading the first
march down the Historic Brewery and Arts District of Utica;  today,  the
Utica  Parade  draws  approximately  30,000  spectators,  as  over 5,000
marchers from across the state step off  from  Oneida  Square  and  head
north to Genesee Street; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  committee  for  this  year's  Utica,  New  York,  St.
Patrick's Day Parade  designated  Colleen  Kain  Martin  as  the  highly
esteemed  Grand  Marshal  in recognition of her devotion and passion for
the preservation and celebration of Irish culture in her community; and
 
  WHEREAS, A long-time contributor to  the  Irish  community,  Colleen
Kain  Martin is the past Vice President and current member of the Ladies
Ancient  Order  of  Hibernians,  a  Provincial  Delegate  of  Cornhaltas
Ceoltoiri  Eireann,  and local branch Secretary of Craobh Dugan 'Looney;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition, she helped  plan  the  Annual  Great  American
Irish Festival for many years, and has also served as a longtime special
events coordinator for the Utica St. Patrick's Day Parade; and
 
  WHEREAS, Active in her community, this year's Grand Marshal, Colleen
Kain  Martin,  is  a  member  of  the  Irish  Cultural  Center  Program
Development Committee, a charter member of the HARP Museum, Chair of the
Annual ICC Banquet and Awards event, and is a member of the  Network  of
Irish Cultural Centers of North America; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In recognition of her tireless efforts in making the Utica
St. Patrick's Day Parade possible and enjoyable for so many years, it is
Colleen Kain Martin's turn to lead  the  parade,  wielding  the  coveted
shillelagh; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
 
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of the great State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Colleen Kain Martin upon the  occasion  of  her  designation  as
Grand  Marshal  of  the  2023  Utica St. Patrick's Day Parade; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Colleen Kain Martin.
