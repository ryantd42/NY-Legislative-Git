# Extends the authorization of the county of Genesee to impose a county recording tax on obligation secured by a mortgage on real property

**Bill ID:** A5773AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stephen Hawley
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5773AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5773AA)

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Genesee to impose a county recording tax on obligation secured by a mortgage on real property from 2023 to 2025.
