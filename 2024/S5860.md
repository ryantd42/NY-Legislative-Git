# Provides that instructions to the jury shall include certain defenses

**Bill ID:** S5860
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5860 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5860)

## Summary

Provides that instructions to the jury shall include certain defenses; provides that such instructions shall include, but not be limited to, "Do not let bias, sympathy, prejudice, or public opinion influence your decision"; defines the term "bias".

 or more of those characteristics.

  (f) The legislature is concerned about the use  of  societal  bias  in

 use of so-called "panic strategies" by  defendants  in  criminal  trials

 with one or more of those characteristics.
  (g) It is against public policy for a defendant to be acquitted  of  a
 charged  offense or convicted of a lesser included offense based upon an
 appeal to the societal bias that may be possessed by members of a jury.
