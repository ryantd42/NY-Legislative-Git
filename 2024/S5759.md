# Enacts the "New York urban and community tree planting and forestry program act"

**Bill ID:** S5759
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5759 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5759)

## Summary

Enacts the "New York urban and community tree planting and forestry program act"; provides for education, technical assistance and support for tree planting and forestry projects in urban areas and disadvantaged communities.

 § 9-2305. PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS.
  1. EVERY TREE PURCHASED PURSUANT TO THE PROGRAM  ESTABLISHED  BY  THIS
 TITLE  SHALL BE IN COMPLIANCE WITH ALL APPLICABLE REQUIREMENTS SET FORTH
 IN THE LATEST EDITION OF THE AMERICAN STANDARD FOR NURSERY  STOCK  (ANSI
 Z60.1), AS REVISED FROM TIME TO TIME BY THE AMERICAN HORTICULTURE INDUS-
 TRY ASSOCIATION OR A SUCCESSOR ENTITY THERETO.
  2. EVERY SUCH TREE SHALL BE PURCHASED FROM A NURSERY GROWER OR NURSERY
 DEALER  REGISTERED PURSUANT TO THE PROVISIONS OF ARTICLE FOURTEEN OF THE
 AGRICULTURE AND MARKETS LAW OR OTHERWISE CERTIFIED BY THE COUNTRY, STATE
 OR PROVINCE OF ORIGIN PURSUANT TO SECTION ONE HUNDRED SIXTY-THREE OF THE
 AGRICULTURE AND MARKETS LAW. THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SUBDIVISION  SHALL
 BE POSTED IN ALL BIDDING DOCUMENTS.
  3.  EVERY  TREE  PROPOSED TO BE PLANTED IN A NATURALIZED AREA SHALL BE
 FROM SEED SOURCED LOCALLY. FOR THE PURPOSES  OF  THIS  SECTION  SEED  IS
 SOURCED "LOCALLY" IF IT ORIGINATED WITHIN ONE HUNDRED FIFTY MILES OF THE
 LOCATION WHERE SUCH TREE IS PROPOSED TO BE PLANTED.
  4.  TREES  PLANTED  ACROSS  PUBLIC  AND PRIVATE PROPERTY SHALL REFLECT
 SPECIES DIVERSITY TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PRACTICABLE.
  5. EVERY TREE PLANTED OR REPLANTED SHALL BE CARED FOR  AND  MAINTAINED
 IN  COMPLIANCE  WITH THE STANDARDS AND PRACTICES SET FORTH IN THE LATEST
 EDITION OF THE AMERICAN NATIONAL STANDARD FOR TREE CARE OPERATIONS (ANSI
 A300), AS REVISED FROM TIME TO TIME BY THE TREE  CARE  INDUSTRY  ASSOCI-
 ATION OR A SUCCESSOR ENTITY THERETO.
  6.  NOTHING  IN  THIS  SECTION  SHALL  AUTHORIZE  THE  SALE, PURCHASE,
 POSSESSION,  PROPAGATION,  INTRODUCTION,  IMPORTATION,  TRANSPORT  AND
 DISPOSAL  OF  AN  INVASIVE  SPECIES  PROHIBITED  IN  ACCORDANCE WITH THE
 PROVISIONS OF TITLE SEVENTEEN OF THIS ARTICLE OR OTHERWISE PROHIBITED BY
 FEDERAL LAW, RULE OR REGULATION.

 § 9-2309. PROGRAM GOALS.
  NOTWITHSTANDING  ANY  CONTRARY PROVISION OF THIS TITLE, THE DEPARTMENT
 SHALL INVEST OR DIRECT AVAILABLE AND RELEVANT  PROGRAM  RESOURCES  IN  A
 MANNER  DESIGNED  TO  ACHIEVE  A  GOAL  FOR DISADVANTAGED COMMUNITIES TO

 PROGRAM,  INCLUDING  BUT  NOT  LIMITED  TO  ANY  WORKFORCE  DEVELOPMENT,
 POLLUTION REDUCTION, INVENTORY, AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT RESULTING  FROM
 SUCH  SPENDING;  PROVIDED, HOWEVER, THAT DISADVANTAGED COMMUNITIES SHALL
 RECEIVE NO LESS THAN FORTY PERCENT OF THE OVERALL BENEFITS  OF  SPENDING
 ON  THE  URBAN  AND  COMMUNITY  TREE  PLANTING AND FORESTRY PROGRAM, AND
 PROVIDED FURTHER  THAT  THIS  SECTION  SHALL  NOT  ALTER  FUNDS  ALREADY
 CONTRACTED OR COMMITTED AS OF THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF THIS SECTION.

 PURPOSES  OF  ESTABLISHING AND IMPLEMENTING THE URBAN AND COMMUNITY TREE
 PLANTING AND FORESTRY PROGRAM ESTABLISHED PURSUANT TO THIS TITLE.
  2. SUCH FUND SHALL CONSIST OF MONEYS APPROPRIATED, CREDITED OR  TRANS-
 FERRED  THERETO  FROM ANY OTHER FUND OR SOURCE PURSUANT TO LAW.  NOTHING
 CONTAINED HEREIN SHALL PREVENT THE STATE FROM RECEIVING GRANTS, GIFTS OR
 BEQUESTS FOR THE PURPOSES OF THE FUND AS DEFINED  IN  THIS  SECTION  AND
 DEPOSITING THEM INTO THE FUND ACCORDING TO LAW.
  3.  MONEYS  SHALL  BE PAID OUT OF THE FUND ON THE AUDIT AND WARRANT OF

 THE COMMISSIONER'S DESIGNEE IN A MANNER CONSISTENT WITH RULES AND  REGU-
 LATIONS  ADOPTED FOR SUCH PURPOSE BY THE DEPARTMENT IN CONSULTATION WITH

  4. MONEYS OF SUCH FUND SHALL BE AWARDED ANNUALLY TO ELIGIBLE  COMMUNI-
 TIES  AND ORGANIZATIONS, ON A COMPETITIVE BASIS, FOR URBAN AND COMMUNITY
