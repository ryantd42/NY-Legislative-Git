# Imposes a term limit on town supervisors

**Bill ID:** A5536
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5536 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5536)

## Summary

Imposes a term limit of four years on town supervisors.
