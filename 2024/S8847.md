# Relates to the revocation of certain licenses for the possession or sale of illicit cannabis in violation of the cannabis law

**Bill ID:** S8847
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8847 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8847)

## Summary

Provides for the revocation of licenses to sell cigarettes, tobacco products, alcohol and lottery tickets for the possession or sale of illicit cannabis in violation of the cannabis law.
