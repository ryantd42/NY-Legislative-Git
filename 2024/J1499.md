## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1499
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMENDING PJ Green, Inc. of Utica, New York, upon
  the occasion of celebrating its 95th Anniversary
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the
achievements of those individuals  and  businesses  which  substantially
contribute  to the economic vitality of their communities and the entire
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend PJ Green, Inc.  upon  the  occasion  of  its  95th  Anniversary,
celebrated  at  a  party on Friday, March 17, 2023, and with a myriad of
events held throughout the year; and
 
  WHEREAS, One of the  largest  variable  printing  companies  in  the
Northeast,  Philip  J. "PJ" Green established PJ Green, Inc. as PJ Green
Advertising Service in 1928; later, Philip's sons, Terry and Pat  joined
their father and helped him to continue the business; and
 
  WHEREAS, Today, PJ Green is run by Terry Green's children along with
Philip  Green's  great-grandchildren,  making  it  a  fourth-generation
family-run business;  it  is  both  a  certified  WMBE  business  and  a
RecycleOne operation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With three office locations in Utica, Syracuse and Albany,
New York, PJ Green and its graphic designers create  visually  appealing
designs  for  their  clients' varied products using environmentally safe
inks and fluids without toxic emissions and waste; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of PJ Green is in  direct  correlation  to  the
efforts  of  its  leadership  and their staff, whose involvement is, and
always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the institution and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges contributions of PJ Green to the local and State economies,
and extends its highest commendation to this corporate enterprise, fully
confident that it will continue to enjoy the sparkling  success  it  has
enjoyed thus far; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend PJ Green,  Inc.  upon  the  occasion  of  celebrating  its  95th
Anniversary; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Rick Green, PJ Green, Inc.
