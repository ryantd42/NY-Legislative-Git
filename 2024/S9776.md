# Extends the effectiveness of certain provisions relating to reporting data on child welfare preventive services

**Bill ID:** S9776
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9776 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9776)

## Summary

Extends the effectiveness of certain provisions relating to reporting data on child welfare preventive services to September 1, 2027.
