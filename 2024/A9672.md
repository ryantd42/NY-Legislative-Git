# Relates to conversions of real property in public housing developments in relation to the RAD program

**Bill ID:** A9672
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9672 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9672)

## Summary

Relates to conversions of real property in public housing developments in relation to the RAD program; sets notice requirements for public housing authorities.
