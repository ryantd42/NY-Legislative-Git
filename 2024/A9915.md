# Relates to the Long Island rail road commuter's council

**Bill ID:** A9915
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9915 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9915)

## Summary

Decreases the vacancy appointments by the county executives of Nassau, Suffolk, Queens or Brooklyn county from three members each to one member each.
