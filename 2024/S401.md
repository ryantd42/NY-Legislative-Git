# Provides an exemption for drugs and medicines for companion and farm animals

**Bill ID:** S401
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S401 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S401)

## Summary

Relates to providing an exemption for drugs and medicines for companion and farm animals.
