# Establishes the fourteenth judicial district

**Bill ID:** A10088
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10088 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10088)

## Summary

Establishes the fourteenth judicial district which shall consist of the counties of Putnam, Dutchess, Orange and Rockland.
