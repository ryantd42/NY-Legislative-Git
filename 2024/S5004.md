# Provides an exemption for the sale and installation of residential and commercial geothermal heat pump systems equipment

**Bill ID:** S5004
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5004 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5004)

## Summary

Provides an exemption for the sale and installation of residential and commercial geothermal heat pump systems equipment.
