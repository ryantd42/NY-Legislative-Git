# Creates the office of older adult workforce development within the office for the aging

**Bill ID:** S8656
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8656 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8656)

## Summary

Creates the office of older adult workforce development within the office for the aging to address the needs of older adults regarding the workplace.
