# Establishes in the city of New York a bicycle lane safety program to enforce certain restrictions on the use of bicycle lanes by means of bicycle lane photo devices

**Bill ID:** A4637
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Zohran Mamdani
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4637 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4637)

## Summary

Establishes in the city of New York a bicycle lane safety program to enforce certain restrictions on the use of protected bicycle lanes by means of bicycle lane photo devices.
