# Requires persons issued a driver's license or permit to report the instance of a medically diagnosed seizure to the commissioner of motor vehicles

**Bill ID:** S4769
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4769 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4769)

## Summary

Requires a person issued a driver's license or permit to report the instance of a medically diagnosed seizure to the commissioner of motor vehicles within sixty days of such seizure.
