# Establishes the Northport high school peer mentoring pilot program

**Bill ID:** A5535
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5535 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5535)

## Summary

Establishes the Northport high school peer mentoring pilot program.

 ing, monitoring and support, and closure;

 both mentors, mentees and stakeholders throughout the community;
  (e) the development of an articulated recruitment and support outreach
 plan;
  (f) the implementation of a mentor screening tool;
  (g)  the  creation  of communication tools and marketing collateral to
 support recruitment and communication efforts, including, but not limit-
 ed to:
  (i) position description;
  (ii) promotional materials;
