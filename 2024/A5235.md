# Makes the first one hundred thousand dollars of an individuals' private pension non-taxable

**Bill ID:** A5235
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5235 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5235)

## Summary

Makes the first one hundred thousand dollars of an individual's private pension non-taxable.
