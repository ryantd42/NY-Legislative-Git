# Requires the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation to prohibit the sale of single use plastic and polystyrene foam in state parks

**Bill ID:** A5554
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5554 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5554)

## Summary

Requires the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation to prohibit the sale of single use plastic and polystyrene foam in state parks.
