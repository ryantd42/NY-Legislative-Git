# Provides internet access and access to internet-enabled devices to recipients of public assistance and to adults and children in shelters and similar facilities

**Bill ID:** A5503
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5503 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5503)

## Summary

Provides for internet access and access to internet-enabled devices to recipients of public assistance and to adults and children in shelters, domestic violence residence programs, and other adult and children residence facilities.
