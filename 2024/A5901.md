# Exempts zero-emission school buses from sales and use taxes

**Bill ID:** A5901
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5901 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5901)

## Summary

Exempts the sale of zero-emission school buses from sales and use taxes.
