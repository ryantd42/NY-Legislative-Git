# Requires candidates for office to caption their video advertisements

**Bill ID:** A4962
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jen Lunsford
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4962 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4962)

## Summary

Requires that all candidates for elected local, state or federal office shall be required to caption any video advertisement which is viewed by the general public.
