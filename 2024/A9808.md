# Prohibits food service establishments from modifying the purchase price of items based on consumer demand

**Bill ID:** A9808
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenny Burgos
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A9808 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9808)

## Summary

Prohibits food service establishments from modifying the purchase price of items based on consumer demand except for when lowering the purchase price of a food or beverage item during specific times of the day.
