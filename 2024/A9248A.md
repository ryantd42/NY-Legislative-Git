# Requires the installation of climb deterrent fencing on certain bridges

**Bill ID:** A9248A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9248A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9248A)

## Summary

Requires the installation of climb deterrent fencing on all bridges under the jurisdiction of the New York State Bridge Authority.
