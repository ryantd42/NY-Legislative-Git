# Relates to an annual inventory of short-term rental locations and capacities, and monthly utilization data

**Bill ID:** A8226
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8226 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8226)

## Summary

Requires short-term rental companies to provide an annual inventory of short-term rental locations and capacities, and monthly utilization data for each county in the state.

  ARTICLE 42
  SHORT-TERM RENTAL INVENTORY
 SECTION 1100. DEFINITIONS.
  1101. ANNUAL INVENTORY.
  1102. MONTHLY REPORTING.
  1103. ENFORCEMENT AND PENALTIES.
  §  1100.  DEFINITIONS.  AS  USED  IN  THIS ARTICLE, UNLESS THE CONTEXT
 OTHERWISE REQUIRES:
  1. "SHORT-TERM RENTAL" OR "STR" MEANS ANY INDIVIDUALLY OR COLLECTIVELY
 OWNED SINGLE-FAMILY OR ONE-TO-FOUR FAMILY HOUSE OR DWELLING UNIT, OR ANY
 UNIT OR GROUP OF UNITS IN A CONDOMINIUM, COOPERATIVE OR TIMESHARE,  THAT
 IS OFFERED FOR A FEE AND FOR LESS THAN THIRTY CONTINUOUS DAYS.
  2.  "STR  COMPANY" MEANS ANY ORGANIZATION, CORPORATION, NETWORK, PLAT-
 FORM OR OPERATOR THAT FACILITATES THE PROVISION  OF  SHORT-TERM  RENTALS
 AND COLLECTS OR RECEIVES A FEE IN CONNECTION WITH SUCH FACILITATION.
  §  1101.  ANNUAL  INVENTORY.  1. EACH STR COMPANY OPERATING WITHIN THE
 STATE OF NEW YORK SHALL PROVIDE AN ANNUAL  INVENTORY  TO  THE  GOVERNING
 BODY  OF  EACH  COUNTY  WITHIN  THE  STATE. FOR A COUNTY WHICH IS WHOLLY
 CONTAINED WITHIN A CITY, SUCH INVENTORY SHALL BE PROVIDED  TO  THE  CITY

 COUNCIL;  PROVIDED THAT THE INFORMATION REQUIRED SHALL BE BROKEN DOWN BY
 COUNTY.
  2.  THE  INVENTORY  SHALL  INCLUDE THE LOCATIONS AND CAPACITIES OF ALL
 SHORT-TERM RENTALS WITHIN THE JURISDICTION OF THAT COUNTY.
  3. THE INVENTORY SHALL BE PROVIDED NO LATER THAN JANUARY  THIRTY-FIRST
 OF  EACH  YEAR,  WITH  DATA  ACCURATE AS OF DECEMBER THIRTY-FIRST OF THE
 PRECEDING YEAR.
  § 1102. MONTHLY REPORTING. 1. EACH STR COMPANY  OPERATING  WITHIN  THE
 STATE  OF NEW YORK SHALL PROVIDE A MONTHLY REPORT TO THE GOVERNING BOARD
 OF EACH COUNTY WITHIN THE STATE. FOR A COUNTY WHICH IS WHOLLY  CONTAINED
 WITHIN  A  CITY,  SUCH  INVENTORY SHALL BE PROVIDED TO THE CITY COUNCIL;
 PROVIDED THAT THE INFORMATION REQUIRED SHALL BE BROKEN DOWN BY COUNTY.
  2. SUCH REPORT SHALL INCLUDE DATA ON  THE  UTILIZATION  OF  SHORT-TERM
 RENTALS  WITHIN THE JURISDICTION OF THAT COUNTY, INCLUDING THE NUMBER OF
 DAYS EACH UNIT WAS RENTED AND THE TOTAL NUMBER OF OCCUPANTS.
  3. SUCH REPORT SHALL ALSO INCLUDE DATA ON THE LENGTH OF STAY FOR  EACH
 RENTAL  UNIT,  INCLUDING  THE  AVERAGE,  MEDIAN, AND RANGE OF LENGTHS OF
 STAY.
  4. THE REPORT SHALL BE PROVIDED NO LATER THAN THE TENTH  DAY  OF  EACH
 MONTH, WITH DATA ACCURATE AS OF THE LAST DAY OF THE PRECEDING MONTH.
  § 1103. ENFORCEMENT AND PENALTIES. 1. EACH COUNTY, OR IN THE CASE OF A
 COUNTY  WHOLLY CONTAINED WITHIN A CITY, THE CITY COUNCIL, SHALL HAVE THE
 AUTHORITY TO ENFORCE THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ARTICLE.
  2. ANY STR COMPANY THAT FAILS TO COMPLY WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF  THIS
 ARTICLE  SHALL BE SUBJECT TO A PENALTY OF UP TO ONE THOUSAND DOLLARS PER
 DAY FOR EACH DAY THE VIOLATION CONTINUES.
  § 3. This act shall take effect on the sixtieth  day  after  it  shall
 have become a law.
