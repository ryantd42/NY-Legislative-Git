# Relates to the powers and duties of fire district commissioners

**Bill ID:** A10535
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10535 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10535)

## Summary

Treats lease payments made by the East Greenbush Fire District for real property the same as bond payments without the adoption of a proposition.
