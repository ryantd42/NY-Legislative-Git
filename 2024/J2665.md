# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian Subhash Singh and Salutatorian Josephine Munoz of Transit Tech Career and Technical Education High School, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2665 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2665)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2665
 
BY: Senator PERSAUD
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024  Valedictorian Subhash
  Singh and Salutatorian Josephine  Munoz  of  Transit
  Tech  Career and Technical Education High School, in
  recognition of their outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions,  to  honor the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2024 Valedictorian Subhash Singh and  Salutatorian  Josephine  Munoz  of
Transit  Tech Career and Technical Education High School, in recognition
of their outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Subhash  Singh  and  Josephine  Munoz  will  graduate  from
Transit  Tech Career and Technical Education High School on Monday, June
24, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Subhash Singh and Josephine Munoz  represent  the  best  of
developed  potential  inherent in our most precious resource, our youth;
their achievements have brought enduring honor  to  their  families  and
communities and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As Subhash Singh and Josephine Munoz take leave of Transit
Tech Career and Technical Education High School, they bestow a legacy of
academic achievement, school involvement and community service to  which
others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Subhash Singh and Josephine Munoz may now stand with pride
as they assess their achievements, experience the satisfaction of  their
labors  and  the  joy  of  their  accomplishments, eager to face the new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Class  of  2024  Valedictorian  Subhash  Singh and Salutatorian
Josephine Munoz of Transit Tech  Career  and  Technical  Education  High
School,  in  recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Valedictorian  Subhash Singh and Salutatorian Josephine
Munoz.
