# Directs the center for agricultural medicine and health to create silo safety warning placards and a silo safety advocacy program; and relates to funding of such placards and advocacy program

**Bill ID:** S6243
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6243 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6243)

## Summary

Directs the center for agricultural medicine and health to design and create silo safety warning placards and a silo safety advocacy program; creates a silo safety fund and state income tax gift option for funding of such placards and advocacy program.
