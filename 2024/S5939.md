# Permits the Albany Cemetery Association to file a certificate of amendment of certificate of incorporation with the department of state

**Bill ID:** S5939
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5939 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5939)

## Summary

Permits the Albany Cemetery Association to file a certificate of amendment of certificate of incorporation with the department of state within one year of the effective date.
