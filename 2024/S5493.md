# Relates to establishing a variable supplements fund for sanitation members of the New York city employees' retirement system

**Bill ID:** S5493
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5493 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5493)

## Summary

Relates to establishing a variable supplements fund for sanitation members of the New York city employees' retirement system; creates a board to manage such fund and to make payments.
