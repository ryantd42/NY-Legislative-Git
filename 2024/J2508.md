# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian, Stephen Wyss, of Malverne High School, in recognition of his outstanding accomplishments

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2508 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2508)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2508
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024 Valedictorian, Stephen
  Wyss, of Malverne High School, in recognition of his
  outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions  to  honor  the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2024  Valedictorian,  Stephen  Wyss,  of  Malverne  High  School,  in
recognition of his outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian,  Stephen  Wyss,  represents  the  best  of
developed potential inherent in our most precious resource,  our  youth;
his achievements have brought enduring honor to his family and community
and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  Valedictorian,  Stephen  Wyss, takes leave of Malverne
High School,  he  bestows  a  legacy  of  academic  achievement,  school
involvement, and community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS, Valedictorian, Stephen Wyss, may now stand with pride as he
assesses his achievements and experiences the satisfaction of his labors
and the joy of his accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of
a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2024 Valedictorian,  Stephen  Wyss,  of  Malverne  High
School,  in  recognition  of  his outstanding accomplishments; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Valedictorian, Stephen Wyss.
