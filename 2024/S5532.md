# Establishes the LGBTQ+ advisory board

**Bill ID:** S5532
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5532 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5532)

## Summary

Establishes the LGBTQ+ advisory board to make recommendations, conduct research, and more regarding the LGBTQ+ community in New York state.
