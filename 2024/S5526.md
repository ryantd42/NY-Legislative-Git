# Lowers the threshold for eligibility for the appropriation of moneys for the promotion of agriculture and domestic arts

**Bill ID:** S5526
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5526 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5526)

## Summary

Lowers the threshold for eligibility for the appropriation of moneys for the promotion of agriculture and domestic arts from five thousand dollars to two thousand five hundred dollars.
