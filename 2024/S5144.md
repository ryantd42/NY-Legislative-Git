# Establishes a parent and student training center within the City University of New York

**Bill ID:** S5144
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5144 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5144)

## Summary

Establishes a parent and student training center within the City University of New York.
