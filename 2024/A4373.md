# Relates to the possession and sale of methamphetamine

**Bill ID:** A4373
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4373 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4373)

## Summary

Makes penalties for the possession and/or sale of methamphetamine similar to that of the penalties for possession and/or sale of heroin and cocaine.
