# Requires limited use motorcycles to be registered at point of sale

**Bill ID:** A8450BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alex Bores
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A8450BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8450BB)

Requires limited use motorcycles to be registered at point of sale.
