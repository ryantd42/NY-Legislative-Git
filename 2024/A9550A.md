# Extends the effectiveness of an occupancy tax imposed in the city of Yonkers

**Bill ID:** A9550AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9550AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9550AA)

## Summary

Increases the occupancy tax rate authorized in the city of Yonkers from 3 to 5 7/8ths; extends the effectiveness of the occupancy tax imposed in the city of Yonkers to September 1, 2027.
