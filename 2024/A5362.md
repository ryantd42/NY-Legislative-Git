# Provides support to refugees from Afghanistan and Ukraine

**Bill ID:** A5362
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica P. Wallace
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5362 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5362)

## Summary

Provides support to refugees from Afghanistan and Ukraine who have been displaced through no fault of their own.
