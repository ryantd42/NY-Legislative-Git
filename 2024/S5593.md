# Mandates budget expenditures by gas and electric corporations to improve power outage response and to stormproof power lines

**Bill ID:** S5593
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5593 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5593)

## Summary

Relates to mandating budget expenditures by gas and electric corporations to improve power outage response and to stormproof power lines.
