# Designates officers and members of a fire investigation unit as peace officers in Franklin county

**Bill ID:** A10321
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10321 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10321)

## Summary

Designates officers and members of a fire investigation unit as peace officers in Franklin county.
