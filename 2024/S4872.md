# Exempts veterans eighty-five years of age and older from the obligation to pay New York state income tax

**Bill ID:** S4872
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4872 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4872)

## Summary

Exempts veterans eighty-five years of age and older from the obligation to pay New York state income tax.
