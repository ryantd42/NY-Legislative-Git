# Extends the authorization of the city of New Rochelle to impose an additional sales and compensating use tax

**Bill ID:** S6083A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S6083A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6083A)

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the city of New Rochelle to impose an additional sales and compensating use tax until 2025.
