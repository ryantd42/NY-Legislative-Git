# Commending the ADA Celebration Committee for Madison County upon the occasion of hosting an esteemed event commemorating the 34th Anniversary of the Signing of the Americans with Disabilities Act on July 30, 2024

**Bill ID:** K2290
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian D. Miller
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2290 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2290)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2290
 
BY: M. of A. Miller
 
  COMMENDING  the  ADA  Celebration  Committee  for
  Madison County  upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  an
  esteemed event commemorating the 34th Anniversary of
  the  Signing  of the Americans with Disabilities Act
  on July 30, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, The State of New York recognizes the  vital  importance  of
organizations  that promote inclusivity, equality, and the protection of
civil rights for all individuals; and
 
  WHEREAS, On July 26, 1990, President George H. W.  Bush  signed  the
historic  Americans  with  Disabilities  Act  (ADA)  into law, marking a
significant milestone in the ongoing struggle for disability rights  and
equality; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  landmark  piece  of  legislation,  the  Americans  with
Disabilities Act (ADA) prohibits discrimination against individuals with
disabilities and promotes their full participation  in  all  aspects  of
society,  including  employment,  public accommodations, transportation,
and telecommunications; and
 
  WHEREAS, The ADA has had a profound impact on the lives of  millions
of  Americans,  ensuring  equal  opportunities and removing barriers for
individuals  with  disabilities  to  fully  engage  in  educational,
employment, recreational, and social activities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Having  fostered  a more accessible and inclusive society,
the ADA requires the implementation of  reasonable  accommodations,  the
removal  of  architectural  barriers, and the promotion of accessibility
standards in various sectors; and
 
  WHEREAS, Madison County has long been  committed  to  upholding  the
principles  enshrined  in  the  ADA,  advancing  disability  rights, and
fostering an environment where individuals with disabilities can thrive,
contribute, and achieve their full potential; and
 
  WHEREAS, Let us celebrate the achievements of the  ADA,  reflect  on
the  work that still lies ahead, and reaffirm our dedication to building
a society that recognizes the inherent  worth  and  abilities  of  every
individual; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the ADA  Celebration  Committee  for  Madison  County  upon  the
occasion of hosting an esteemed event commemorating the 34th Anniversary
of  the Signing of the Americans with Disabilities Act on July 30, 2024;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the ADA Celebration Committee for Madison County.
