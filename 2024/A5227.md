# Relates to including bodegas and delicatessens in the definition of food service establishment

**Bill ID:** A5227
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A5227 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5227)

## Summary

Requires the department of agriculture and markets to post the results of its graded inspections of retail food stores on their website and provide information as to where a customer may file a consumer report.
