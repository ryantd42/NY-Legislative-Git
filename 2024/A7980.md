# Provides free hunting, fishing, and trapping licenses for volunteer instructors

**Bill ID:** A7980
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David DiPietro
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A7980 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A7980)

## Summary

Provides free hunting, fishing, and trapping licenses for qualified volunteer instructors.
