# Congratulating the New York Liberty upon the occasion of capturing the 2024 Women's National Basketball Association Championship

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [J2733 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2733)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2733
 
BY: Senator MYRIE
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  New  York  Liberty  upon  the
  occasion of  capturing  the  2024  Women's  National
  Basketball Association Championship
 
  WHEREAS,  Excellence  and  success  in  competitive  sports  can  be
achieved only through strenuous practice, team  play  and  team  spirit,
nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor and pay
just tribute to those prominent individuals whose  esteemed  careers  in
athletics  and  contributions to the game of basketball have evolved the
nature of the game, greatly contributing  to  the  enrichment  of  their
community  and  the  world,  and  to  pay  homage  to  the  women  whose
unprecedented success and athletic achievements inspires us all; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and  in  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate the New York Liberty upon the  occasion  of  capturing  the
2024 Women's National Basketball Association Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  New  York Liberty are the 2024 Women's National Basketball
Association Champions, beating the Minnesota Lynx in game  five  of  the
finals on Sunday, October 20, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The athletic talent displayed by this team is due in great
part to the efforts of their coaches, skilled and inspirational leaders,
respected for their ability to develop potential into excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, The team's overall record is outstanding,  and  members  of
the  Liberty  were  loyally  and  enthusiastically  supported by family,
friends, countless fans and residents of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Originally established on October 30, 1996,  the  New  York
Liberty  are  one of the first original eight franchises in the WNBA and
part of the Eastern Conference; and
 
  WHEREAS, New York Liberty has  made  it  to  the  WNBA  Playoffs  an
astounding  19  times,  and  to  the  WNBA  Finals,  an  incredible  and
challenging feat, six times since the team's conception, holding  a  tie
record for the most Finals appearances; and
 
  WHEREAS, The hallmarks of New York Liberty, from the opening game of
the  season  to  participation in the championship, were a sisterhood of
athletic ability, of good sportsmanship, of honor  and  of  scholarship,
demonstrating that these team players are second to none; and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletically, the team members have proven themselves to be
an  unbeatable  combination  of  talents,  reflecting favorably on their
State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, The esteemed coaches of this dedicated  and  talented  team
have  done  a superb job in guiding, molding, and inspiring the New York
Liberty members toward their goals; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  Sports competition instills the values of teamwork, pride,
and accomplishment, and the coaches and outstanding athletes of New York
Liberty have clearly made a contribution to  the  spirit  of  excellence
which is a tradition of their State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of New York Liberty: Jaylyn Sherrod,  Marquesha
Davis,  Kennedy  Burke,  Kayla  Thornton, Nyara Sabally, Leonie Fiebich,
Ivana Dojkic, Sabrina Ionescu,  Courtney  Vandersloot,  Breanna  Stewart
Jonquel  Jones,  and  Betnijah  Laney-Hamilton;  and Coaches: Head Coach
Sandy Brondello, and Assistant Coaches, Olaf Lange, Roneeka Hodges,  and
Zach  O'Brien,  on their outstanding season and overall team record; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the members of New York Liberty and to the aforementioned
Coaches.
