# Relates to the recruitment incentive and retention program for members of the New York state organized militia; repealer

**Bill ID:** A10186
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lester Chang
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10186 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10186)

## Summary

Includes New York guard members to the recruitment incentive and retention program for members of the New York state organized militia; permits tuition benefits for post-graduate study.
