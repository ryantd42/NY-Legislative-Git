# Relates to an exemption for certain property from the prohibition of alcohol sales within a certain distance of a church

**Bill ID:** A9049
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A9049 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9049)

## Summary

Provides for an exemption for certain property from the prohibition of alcohol sales within a certain distance of a church.
