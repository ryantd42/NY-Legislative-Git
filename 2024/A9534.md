# Establishes a tax deduction for certain homeowners for the cost of sidewalk repairs

**Bill ID:** A9534
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9534 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9534)

## Summary

Establishes a tax deduction for homeowners in a city with a population of one million or more for the cost of sidewalk repairs.
