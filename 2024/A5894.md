# Establishes a registry in New York city for residents who do not want circulars or other printed material or samples of a commercial nature left at their residence

**Bill ID:** A5894
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5894 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5894)

## Summary

Establishes a registry in New York city for residents who do not want circulars or other printed material or samples of a commercial nature left at their residence.
