# Raises the maximum fine for persons who violate the law regulating telemarketing to twenty thousand dollars

**Bill ID:** A4456
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alex Bores
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A4456 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4456)

## Summary

Raises the maximum fine for persons who violate the law regulating telemarketing to twenty thousand dollars.
