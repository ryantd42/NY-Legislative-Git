## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2599
 
BY: Senator WALCZYK
 
  HONORING  Lambert  Will  posthumously  upon  the
  occasion of being inducted into the New  York  State
  Basketball Hall of Fame on April 21, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
outstanding  athletes  who  have  distinguished themselves through their
historical contribution to the sport,  attaining  unprecedented  success
and the highest level of personal achievement; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Lambert Will posthumously upon the occasion of being inducted  into  the
New  York  State  Basketball  Hall  of Fame on Sunday, April 21, 2024 in
Glens Falls, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  founder  of  basketball  in  1891,  Lambert  Will  is
deservedly being inducted into the Class of 2024 for the Basketball Hall
of Fame; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Lambert  Will  resided  in  Herkimer,  New  York  and  was
determined to be the founder of basketball after years  of  research  by
Scott Flansburg due to the myths surrounding basketball's founding; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  a  testament of his contribution to the sport, Lambert
Will was also nominated for inducted into Springfield in  the  Class  of
2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Highlighting his vision and dream, Lambert Will's induction
puts  an  end  to  speculation  surrounding  the origins of the sport of
basketball and corrects the historical record; and
 
  WHEREAS, Upstate New York will now be recognized as  the  birthplace
of  basketball,  alongside  baseball, and for its contributions to these
sports; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of such outstanding athletic accomplishments are brought to
our attention, they should be recognized by all  the  citizens  of  this
great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Lambert Will posthumously upon the occasion of being inducted into
the New York State Basketball Hall of Fame on April 21, 2024; and be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the family of Lambert Will.
