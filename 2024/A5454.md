# Relates to providing a tax exemption for volunteer firefighters and volunteer ambulance workers

**Bill ID:** A5454
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5454 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5454)

## Summary

Relates to providing a tax exemption for volunteer firefighters and volunteer ambulance workers in a county with a population of more than 250,000 and less than 500,000 persons.
