# Requires insurers to provide information on resources for insureds or enrollees recently diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease

**Bill ID:** A8348
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8348 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8348)

## Summary

Requires insurers to provide information on resources for insureds or enrollees recently diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease.
