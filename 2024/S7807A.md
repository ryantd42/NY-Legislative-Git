# Requires the Office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation to establish standards for the closing of trails on the Empire state trail

**Bill ID:** S7807A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** José M. Serrano
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [S7807A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7807A)

## Summary

Requires the office of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation to establish standards for the closing of segments of trails on the Empire state trail, and provide notice of such closings to the public.
