# Provides an itemized deduction for expenses for course-mandated supplies for eligible college students paid by taxpayers

**Bill ID:** A5417
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5417 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5417)

## Summary

Provides an itemized deduction for expenses for course-mandated supplies for eligible college students paid by taxpayers.
