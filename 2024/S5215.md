# Authorizes the town of Brookhaven, county of Suffolk to amend a conservation easement

**Bill ID:** S5215
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dean Murray
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5215 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5215)

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Brookhaven, county of Suffolk to amend an existing conservation easement to allow for rail access and service.
