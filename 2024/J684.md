## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 684
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING  Sonia  Daly  upon  the  occasion  of her
  special  recognition  by  Schneps  Media  at  the
  Caribbean Impact Awards on March 30, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to commend those
illustrious organizations of the State  of  New  York  whose  focus  and
intent  have  made  a substantial impact on the quality of life in their
communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Sonia Daly upon the occasion of her special recognition by Schneps Media
at  the Caribbean Impact Awards on March 30, 2023, at Russo's On The Bay
in Queens, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The annual Caribbean Impact Awards, is a  celebrated  event
hosted by Caribbean Life and presented by Elliot Ifraimoff & Associates,
Injury  Attorneys  that recognizes the achievements and contributions of
outstanding individuals of Caribbean background  who  have  created  the
most  impact in their respective career fields, ranging from healthcare,
the arts, to advocacy, entrepreneurship, and much more; and
 
  WHEREAS, Schneps Media is the leading media company serving New York
City, Westchester, Long Island, and Philadelphia, is  the  publisher  of
Caribbean  Life,  and  is  proud  to  bring  together  these outstanding
individuals at the annual Caribbean Impact Awards  to  connect,  support
one another, conduct business, and build a community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The celebrated honorees at the annual Awards are committed
to creating and framing the path for incoming immigrants  and  inspiring
generations to come; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Born  in Georgetown, Guyana, to Guyanese immigrants, Sonia
Daly has a deep respect and love for her culture; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sonia Daly is a graduate  of  Utica  College  and  Syracuse
University; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Using  her  education and passion to make positive change,
Sonia Daly has dedicated herself for the  past  15  years  to  providing
access  to  immigrants  from  the  Caribbean  and  continental  African
diaspora; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sonia Daly's truly commendable drive in her current  career
with the Municipal Identification Card Program, IDNYC, shines through in
all  her  successes;  in  2018,  the Mayor's Office of Immigrant Affairs
commissioner sent her to Stockholm, Sweden to share  best  practices  in
launching a successful municipal ID card program to a consortium of over
100 European cities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Previously,  Sonia  Daly dedicated her time to the YMCA of
Greater New York, writing an award-winning grant, "Strings for Life", to
 
provide  Caribbean  and  African  American  preschoolers  with  violin
instruction at the Flatbush YMCA; and
 
  WHEREAS, From 2008 to 2013, Sonia Daly also served as Chief of Staff
to long-time State Senator Kevin Parker; and
 
  WHEREAS,  An advocate for maternal wellness, Sonia Daly authored and
recorded a guided meditation for  women  who  have  suffered  through  a
miscarriage  and  plans  to  record other meditations in the future; she
recently received the first of her birth doula certifications and  hopes
to support those who don't have access to prenatal wellness care; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor Sonia Daly upon the occasion of her special recognition by Schneps
Media  at  the  Caribbean  Impact  Awards  on  March 30, 2023; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Sonia Daly.
