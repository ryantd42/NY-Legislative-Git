# Relates to including outpatient care provided by creative arts therapists in certain insurance policies

**Bill ID:** A6173
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [A6173 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6173)

## Summary

Relates to including outpatient care provided by creative arts therapists in certain insurance policies covering care for other mental health services.
