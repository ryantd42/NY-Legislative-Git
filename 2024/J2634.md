# Commending the Verona Fire Department upon the occasion of hosting their 70th Annual Verona Volunteer Fire Department Field Day on June 7-9, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2634
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2634 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2634)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2634
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMENDING  the  Verona  Fire  Department upon the
  occasion  of  hosting  their  70th  Annual  Verona
  Volunteer  Fire  Department  Field  Day on June 7-9,
  2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the Verona Fire Department upon the occasion  of  hosting  their
70th Annual Verona Volunteer Fire Department Field Day on Friday, June 7
to Sunday, June 9, 2024, at 5860 Rock Road in Verona, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Founded  in  1928, the Verona Fire Department protects the
lives and property of  the  Verona,  New  York  community  by  providing
services  in  the event of fire, medical emergencies, and disasters that
may threaten the public welfare; and
 
  WHEREAS, 2024  marks  the  70th  Anniversary  of  the  first  Verona
Volunteer  Fire  Department  Field  Day;  the  event will feature rides,
games, a parade, performers, and fireworks; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  that  those  who
enhance  the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown a
long and sustained commitment to excellence certainly  have  earned  the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Verona Fire Department upon the occasion  of  hosting  their
70th  Annual  Verona  Volunteer  Fire  Department Field Day on June 7-9,
2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Verona Fire Department.
