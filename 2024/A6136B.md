# Provides that the thruway authority shall issue permits for ambulances and fire vehicles for toll exemptions

**Bill ID:** A6136BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Christopher Eachus
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [A6136BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6136BA)

## Summary

Provides that the thruway authority shall issue permits for ambulances and fire vehicles exempting such vehicles from paying tolls on the thruway when engaged in an emergency operation.
