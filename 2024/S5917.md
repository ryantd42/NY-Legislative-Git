# Relates to the harassment of certain employees of a transit agency or authority

**Bill ID:** S5917
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5917 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5917)

## Summary

Relates to the harassment of certain employees of a transit agency or authority whose operation is authorized by New York state or any of its political subdivisions.
