# Requires disclosures required in advertisements using the title "coach"

**Bill ID:** A4722
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4722 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4722)

## Summary

Requires disclosures required in advertisements using the title "coach"; defines the title "coach" as a professional designation, credential, certification, or professional description that indicates the person has expertise or training in issues specifically related to providing care or help in choosing a career in their field; provides civil penalties for violations; makes related provisions.
