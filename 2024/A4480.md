# Relates to non-insurance benefits

**Bill ID:** A4480
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4480 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4480)

## Summary

Relates to authorizing non-insurance benefits or services to be offered as part of group life or group or blanket accident or health insurance policies.
