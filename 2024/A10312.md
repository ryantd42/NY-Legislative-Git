# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the CW2 Casey N. Frankoski Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** A10312
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10312 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10312)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the city of Rensselaer, county of Rensselaer, as the "CW2 Casey N. Frankoski Memorial Bridge".
