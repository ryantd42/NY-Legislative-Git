# Creates the substance use disorder treatment bill of rights to be posted in a conspicuous place in all treatment facilities

**Bill ID:** S6009AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6009AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6009AA)

## Summary

Creates the substance use disorder treatment bill of rights to be posted in a conspicuous place in all treatment facilities.
