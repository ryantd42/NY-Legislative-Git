# Increases the federal poverty level requirement for recipients where it concerns the one-time disregard of earned income

**Bill ID:** A10155AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Maritza Davila
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10155AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10155AA)

## Summary

Increases the federal poverty level requirement for recipients where it concerns the one-time disregard of earned income following job entry.

 AN ACT to amend the social services law, in relation to  increasing  the
  federal  poverty  level  requirement for recipients of social services
