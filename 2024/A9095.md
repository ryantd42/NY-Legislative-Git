# Establishes term limits for the governor, lieutenant-governor, comptroller, and attorney-general

**Bill ID:** A9095
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica P. Wallace
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9095 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9095)

## Summary

Establishes term limits for the governor, lieutenant-governor, comptroller, and attorney-general of three terms in such office.
