# Permits the sale or promotional gifting of certain complementary products for wine and spirits

**Bill ID:** A6050A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A6050A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6050A)

## Summary

Permits the sale or promotional gifting of certain complementary products for wine and spirits by certain persons or businesses licensed to sell wine and spirits.
