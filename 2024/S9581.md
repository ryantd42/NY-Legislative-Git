# Grants Rockland county residents who are charged the central business district toll a rebate

**Bill ID:** S9581
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Weber
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9581 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9581)

## Summary

Grants Rockland county residents a rebate who are charged the central business district toll; establishes the "Rockland county central business district toll account".
