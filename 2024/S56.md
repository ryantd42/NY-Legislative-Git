# Requires free education for grades pre-K through undergraduate

**Bill ID:** S56
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S56 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S56)

## Summary

Requires free education for grades pre-K through undergraduate degree programs.
