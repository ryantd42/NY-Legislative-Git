# Relates to income earned while working at the polls

**Bill ID:** A5477AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [A5477AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5477AA)

## Summary

Exempts income earned working at the polls from the definition of income.
