# Expands enhanced assault protection for highway workers, motor carrier investigators, and motor carrier inspectors

**Bill ID:** S9782
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9782 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9782)

## Summary

Expands enhanced assault protection for highway workers, motor carrier investigators, and motor carrier inspectors; defines terms.
