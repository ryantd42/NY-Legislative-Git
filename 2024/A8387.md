# Includes second degree relatives in certain criteria for breast cancer screenings to be covered under insurance plans

**Bill ID:** A8387
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rebecca Seawright
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8387 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8387)

## Summary

Provides that individuals may receive breast cancer screenings under an insurance plan when such individual has a second degree relative with a prior history of breast cancer.
