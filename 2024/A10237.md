# Extends certain provisions relating to the membership composition of the metropolitan transportation authority board

**Bill ID:** A10237
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Tony Simone
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10237 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10237)

## Summary

Extends certain provisions relating to the membership composition of the metropolitan transportation authority board until 2028.
