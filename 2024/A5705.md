# Prohibits the discharge of bilge water into waters of the state

**Bill ID:** A5705
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5705 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5705)

## Summary

Prohibits the discharge of bilge water into waters of the state from any vessel, watercraft, marina or mooring.
