# Relates to the execution of certificates by persons conducting business under assumed names or as partners

**Bill ID:** A10664
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10664 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10664)

## Summary

Removes the requirement that each certificate be duly acknowledged by the person or persons conducting business.
