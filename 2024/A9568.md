# Allows a parent who suffers an involuntary loss of pregnancy to automatically receive temporary disability benefits

**Bill ID:** A9568
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9568 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9568)

## Summary

Allows a parent who suffers an involuntary loss of pregnancy to automatically receive temporary disability benefits upon notice that such parents suffered an involuntary loss of pregnancy.
