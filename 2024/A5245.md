# Relates to representations by insureds having claims under a life insurance policy

**Bill ID:** A5245
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5245 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5245)

## Summary

Requires persons having claims under a life insurance policy to provide reasonable assistance to the insurer in obtaining information relating to misrepresentations made by the insured.
