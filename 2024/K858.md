**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K858 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K858)

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 858
 
BY: M. of A. Beephan
 
  CONGRATULATING  Ariel Staffin upon the occasion of
  her designation as Miss Hudson Valley 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to commend and pay
tribute to those people who, by achieving outstanding success  in  their
competitive  endeavors,  have inspired and brought pride to our majestic
Empire State; Ariel Staffin is one such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is also the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize
the dedication and  commitment  of  people  who  distinguish  themselves
through excellence in statewide contests; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  Ariel Staffin upon the occasion of her designation as Miss
Hudson Valley 2024, on Sunday, December 10, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ariel Staffin, truly exhibiting consistent  distinction  in
her  endeavors  and  pride  in her State, has, through her obtaining the
esteemed title of Miss Hudson Valley,  has  earned  her  eligibility  to
compete for the renowned Miss New York 2024 crown as well; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  2022  graduate  of Cornell University's School of Hotel
Administration, Ariel Staffin is also a restaurant  manager  in  Aurora,
New York, and currently resides in Ithaca; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Ariel Staffin enlisted in the New York Army National Guard
in 2020 and served in a mechanized infantry unit, Delta  Company  2-108,
while simultaneously completing her commissioning through Cornell's Army
ROTC  program;  when  she  graduated,  she  was commissioned as an Armor
Officer and is now a platoon leader in  a  cavalry  troop,  Alpha  Troop
2-101, and was recently promoted to the rank of 1st Lieutenant; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  her  tenure  as Miss Hudson Valley 2024, Ariel Staffin
promotes her service initiative, SALUTE, which she created in  2019,  to
share  the  stories  of  servicemen  and  women  with  people around the
country, giving them  a  voice,  and  empowering  them  to  share  their
stories; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Ariel  Staffin  successfully  furthers the goals of SALUTE
through service member spotlights, outreach  and  advocacy  for  service
members,  and  partnering  with  organizations  whose mission is also to
support service members; and
 
  WHEREAS, While Ariel Staffin's personal  experience  is  within  the
military,  her  community  service  initiative,  SALUTE,  works with law
enforcement, firefighters, medical professionals, meaningfully expanding
the bounds and potential reach of her impact; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  appropriate  to  celebrate  the  individual
accomplishments  of  remarkable people in statewide competitions in this
great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is  justly  proud  to  recognize  and
applaud  Ariel Staffin for her noteworthy endeavors and accomplishments;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Ariel Staffin upon the occasion of her designation as  Miss
Hudson Valley 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Ariel Staffin, Miss Hudson Valley 2024.
