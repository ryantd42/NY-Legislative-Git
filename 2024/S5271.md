# Relates to tuition assistance program awards

**Bill ID:** S5271
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5271 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5271)

## Summary

Increases the minimum tuition assistance program award; increases the maximum parental income amount.
