# Establishes a notarial recordkeeping and reporting exemption for acts relating to the designation and nomination of candidates

**Bill ID:** A6207
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A6207 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6207)

## Summary

Establishes a notarial recordkeeping and reporting exemption for acts relating to the designation and nomination of candidates.
