# Prohibits new cannabis dispensaries from opening up within a certain distance from other licensed cannabis dispensaries

**Bill ID:** S5887
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5887 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5887)

## Summary

Prohibits new cannabis dispensaries from opening up within five hundred feet of other licensed cannabis dispensaries; provides an exception if the location of such premises is in the public interest.
