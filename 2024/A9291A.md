# Prohibits the use of virtual reality headsets while operating a motor vehicle

**Bill ID:** A9291A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9291A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9291A)

## Summary

Prohibits the use of virtual reality headsets while operating a motor vehicle, limited use automobile, limited use motorcycle or bicycle.
