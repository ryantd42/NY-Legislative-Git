# Provides for the licensing of apartment information vendors; defines such vendors as people who, for a fee, provide information on available apartments; repealer

**Bill ID:** A5963
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5963 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5963)

## Summary

Provides for the licensing of apartment information vendors; defines such vendors as persons who, for a fee, furnish information concerning the location and availability of rental real property.
