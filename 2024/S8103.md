# Establishes the New York state commission on out-migration

**Bill ID:** S8103
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8103 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8103)

## Summary

Establishes the New York state commission on out-migration which examines why the state of New York is losing residents and offering recommendations to reverse such trend.
