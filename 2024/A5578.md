# Establishes the New York state familial search policy and the release of information for familial DNA searches

**Bill ID:** A5578
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Doug Smith
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5578 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5578)

## Summary

Establishes the New York state familial search policy; relates to the release of certain information for familial DNA searches where a written agreement governing the use and dissemination of such DNA records exists.
