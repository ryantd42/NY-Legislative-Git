**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2279 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2279)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2279
 
BY: Senator WALCZYK
 
  HONORING  Mike  Hauser  upon  the  occasion of his
  designation as recipient of the 2024  Fulton  County
  Liberty  Bell  Award  presented by the Fulton County
  Bar Association
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  that  those  who
enhance the quality of life in their community and have shown a long and
sustained  commitment  to  the  maintenance  of  high standards in their
profession, certainly have earned the recognition and  applause  of  all
the citizens of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Mike Hauser upon the occasion of his designation  as  recipient  of  the
2024 Fulton County Liberty Bell Award presented by the Fulton County Bar
Association; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Born  and  raised  in  Gloversville, New York, Mike Hauser
graduated  from  Gloversville  High  School  in  1986;  furthering  his
education, he earned an Associate of Business Administration from Fulton
Montgomery  Community  College  in  1989  and  a  Bachelor of Science in
Accounting from SUNY Polytechnic Institute in 1991; and
 
  WHEREAS, Developing a passion for sports promoting while in college,
Mike Hauser spent six years  of  his  career  as  an  accountant  before
utilizing his marketing skills in the sports world; he transitioned into
a  sales  career with Taylor Made Products, where he was involved in the
creation of the Aqua Training Bag, and worldwide distribution; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Honing  his  work,  Mike  Hauser  found  success  through
establishing  a  national marine distribution network called Spray Nine,
which included placement of Marine Spray Nine into 4,400 Walmart stores;
he also set up world-wide distribution of  a  night  vision  camera  for
boats called Nightwave for Sionyx; and
 
  WHEREAS, With Twin Cities Sports Promotions, Mike Hauser created and
promoted  sports  memorabilia  shows and player appearances for 25 years
and the Adirondack Outdoorsman Show for last 20 years; and
 
  WHEREAS, A published author, Mike Hauser produces a  monthly  sports
history  column  in the Leader-Herald called "Upstate N.Y. Sports Lore";
he also published three sports history books under the "Hometown  Sports
Heroes" series and is currently working on another; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Devoted  to  his community, Mike Hauser has been the coach
and administrator for the Gloversville Little League  for  the  last  25
years;  serves as Vice President of the Parkhurst Field Foundation; is a
board member of the Fulton County Historical Society of over  20  years;
founded  the  Fulton County Baseball & Sports Hall of Fame; serves as an
advisor to the New  York  State  Baseball  Hall  of  Fame;  and  is  the
Cooperstown/Upstate  NY  Chapter  President  to the Society for American
Baseball Research (SABR); and
 
  WHEREAS, With him throughout has been his wife, Lori, and their  two
daughters,  Katie and Kerri, all of whom feel privileged to be a part of
his life and rejoice in his achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Mike  Hauser upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of
the 2024 Fulton County Liberty Bell Award presented by the Fulton County
Bar Association; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Mike Hauser.
