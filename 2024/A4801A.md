# Prohibits fuel oil, grade number four in any building or facility in the state

**Bill ID:** A4801AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4801AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4801AA)

## Summary

Prohibits fuel oil, grade number four in any building or facility in the state after July 1, 2028.
