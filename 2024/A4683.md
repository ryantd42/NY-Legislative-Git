# Requires the electronic and print publication of certain school district reports; defines "local officer or employee" to include school officials

**Bill ID:** A4683
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4683 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4683)

## Summary

Requires the electronic and print publication of certain school district reports within 7 days after approval; defines "local officer or employee" to include certain school officials.
