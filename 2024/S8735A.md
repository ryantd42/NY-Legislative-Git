# Establishes protections for minors who are featured in influencer-generated content

**Bill ID:** S8735AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Liu
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8735AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8735AA)

## Summary

Establishes protections for minors who are featured in influencer-generated content that generate earnings from being shared on social media or other video-sharing websites.

  2.  A  MINOR SATISFYING THE CRITERIA DESCRIBED IN SUBDIVISION THIRTEEN
 OF SECTION ONE HUNDRED FIFTY OF THE LABOR LAW MUST BE COMPENSATED BY THE
 INFLUENCER. THE INFLUENCER SHALL SET ASIDE GROSS EARNINGS ON  THE  VIDEO
