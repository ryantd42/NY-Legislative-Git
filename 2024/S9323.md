# Relates to transportation contracts of the Hicksville union free school district

**Bill ID:** S9323
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Rhoads
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9323 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9323)

## Summary

Legalizes, validates, ratifies and confirms certain transportation contracts of the Hicksville union free school district.
