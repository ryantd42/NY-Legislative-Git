# Relates to death certificates for persons whose death is caused by any act or event in which alcohol is a factor

**Bill ID:** A4317
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4317 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4317)

## Summary

Provides that where a death is caused by any act or event in which alcohol is a factor, such information shall be indicated on the death certificate.
