# Relates to installing fiber optic utility along certain highways

**Bill ID:** A9398
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9398 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9398)

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of transportation to install and maintain fiber optic utility along all highways and thruways in the state for access to fiber optic and broadband services.
