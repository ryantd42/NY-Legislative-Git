# Authorizes the village of Haverstraw to alienate certain parklands

**Bill ID:** S8894AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Weber
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8894AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8894AA)

## Summary

Authorizes the village of Haverstraw to discontinue the use as parkland and alienate certain lands.
