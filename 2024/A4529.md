# Relates to the secure choice savings program and participating individuals

**Bill ID:** A4529
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A4529 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4529)

## Summary

Relates to the secure choice savings program and participating individuals; authorizes freelancers and the self-employed the option of enrolling in such program.
