# Requires that health services paid for by a school district for a student attending outside the district be no greater than the district's cost

**Bill ID:** A4648
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4648 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4648)

## Summary

Requires that health and welfare services and facilities paid by a school district for a student attending school outside the district be no greater than the district's cost.
