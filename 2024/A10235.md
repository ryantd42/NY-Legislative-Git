# Relates to the real property tax exemption for un-remarried spouses of volunteer firefighters or volunteer ambulance workers killed in the line of duty

**Bill ID:** A10235
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10235 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10235)

## Summary

Amends the real property tax exemption for un-remarried spouses of volunteer firefighters or volunteer ambulance workers killed in the line of duty to permit continuation of such exemption for volunteer members with service between two and five years.
