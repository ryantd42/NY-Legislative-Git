# Prohibits co-location of a charter school in a public school building in a city school district in a city having a population of one million or more inhabitants; repealer

**Bill ID:** S6138
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6138 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6138)

## Summary

Prohibits co-location of a charter school in a public school building in a city school district in a city having a population of one million or more inhabitants.
