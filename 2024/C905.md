## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 905
 
BY: M. of A. Benedetto
 
  CONCURRENT  RESOLUTION  OF  THE  SENATE AND ASSEMBLY
  providing for the election of  six  Regents  of  the
  University of the State of New York
 
  WHEREAS,  The  current terms of the Regents of the University of the
State of New York elected from the second and fifth  Judicial  Districts
are vacant due to resignation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The terms of the Regents of the University of the State of
New York elected from the third and thirteenth  Judicial  Districts  and
the  terms  of  two  Regents  of the University of the State of New York
elected At Large expire on the thirty-first day of March in the year two
thousand twenty-four; and
 
  RESOLVED, (if the &HOUSE concur), That pursuant to the provisions of
section  202  of  the  education  law,  the  vacancies  created  by  the
expiration  of  these  terms  and  for  other cause are hereby filled by
concurrent resolution; and
 
  RESOLVED, (if the &HOUSE concur), That Hasoni Pratts of  the  county
of  Kings  be  elected Regent of the University of the State of New York
from the second Judicial District for a  term  of  one  year  commencing
immediately; and
 
  RESOLVED,  (if  the  &HOUSE  concur),  That  Dr. Seema Rivera of the
county of Albany be elected Regent of the University of the State of New
York from  the  third  Judicial  District  for  a  term  of  five  years
commencing  on  the  first  day  of  April  in  the  year  two  thousand
twenty-four; and
 
  RESOLVED, (if the &HOUSE concur), That Patrick  A.  Mannion  of  the
county  of  Onondaga be elected Regent of the University of the State of
New York from the fifth Judicial  District  for  a  term  of  two  years
commencing immediately; and
 
  RESOLVED,  (if  the &HOUSE concur), That Dr. Christine D. Cea of the
county of Richmond be re-elected Regent of the University of  the  State
of  New  York  from  the thirteenth Judicial District for a term of five
years commencing on the first day of April  in  the  year  two  thousand
twenty-four; and
 
  RESOLVED,  (if the &HOUSE concur), That Dr. James E. Cottrell of the
county of New York be re-elected At Large a Regent of the University  of
the  State  of New York for a term of five years commencing on the first
day of April in the year two thousand twenty-four; and
 
  RESOLVED, (if the &HOUSE concur), That Wade S. Norwood of the county
of Monroe be re-elected At Large a Regent of the University of the State
of New York for a term of five years commencing  on  the  first  day  of
April in the year two thousand twenty-four.
