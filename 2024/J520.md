## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 520
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING  Troy  "TJ"  Jackson  Sonesta  upon  the
  occasion of his designation for special  recognition
  by Abu Bekr Temple No. 91, Oasis of Brooklyn, Desert
  of New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize that
the quality and character of life in the  communities  across  New  York
State  are  reflective  of  the concerned and dedicated efforts of those
organizations and individuals who would devote themselves to the welfare
of the community and its citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Troy  "TJ"  Jackson  Sonesta  upon  the  occasion of his designation for
special recognition by Abu Bekr Temple No. 91, Oasis of Brooklyn, Desert
of New York, at its Annual Potentates Ball, March 31-April 2,  2023,  at
Sonesta White Plains Downtown, White Plains, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  lifelong  resident  of  Staten  Island,  New York, Troy
Jackson Sonesta was born on June 19, 1966, and graduated from  Susan  E.
Wagner  High  School  in 1984; he went on to work as an Assistant to the
Communications Director at the Charter Revision Commission from 1987  to
1990; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Troy  Jackson Sonesta began his illustrious 33-year career
with Richmond University Medical Center as a certified  New  York  State
Emergency  Medical  Technician (EMT) in 1990, and one year later, became
an EMT Certified Lab Instructor (CLI), teaching First Aid and CPR; and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore, from 2007 to 2010, he worked as  the  Director
of  Operations at Essex Valley EMS out of East Orange Hospital, covering
the cities of East Orange and Orange, New Jersey; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 2003, Troy  Jackson  Sonesta  graduated  from  John  Jay
College  of  Criminal  Justice  with  a  Bachelor  of  Science degree in
Criminal Justice and a minor in Security Management; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  a  steadfast  and  unwavering  commitment  to  his
community,  Troy  Jackson  Sonesta  became  a  Master Mason in 2005 with
Silver Lake Lodge No. 59 of the Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand  Lodge
for  the State of New York; he served as its Worshipful Master from 2015
to 2016, and became District Deputy Grand Master of  the  2nd  District,
covering  3  lodges  in Brooklyn and Staten Island, in 2017; he became a
Grand Trustee for the Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand  Lodge  for  the
State of New York in 2022; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  Noble  in Abu Bekr Temple No. 91 since October of 2016,
Troy Jackson Sonesta currently serves as its 72nd Illustrious Potentate,
the esteemed position he has held since  his  election  in  December  of
2022; and
 
  WHEREAS, Troy Jackson Sonesta has also served as a semi-professional
football  coach  with  the  New  York  Predators  for 15 years, and most
 
recently, as the defensive and special teams' coach for the  New  Jersey
Unit;  in  addition, he referees local youth football games ranging from
Pre-K to high school level; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body that when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Troy "TJ" Jackson Sonesta upon the occasion of his designation for
special recognition by Abu Bekr Temple No. 91, Oasis of Brooklyn, Desert
of New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Troy "TJ" Jackson Sonesta.
