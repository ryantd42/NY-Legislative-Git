# Amends a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for a certain appropriation for the 2022-2023 state fiscal year for grants in aid to certain agricultural organizations

**Bill ID:** R2686
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R2686 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/R2686)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2686
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R2861  of  2022
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  a  certain  appropriation for the
  2022-2023 state fiscal year for  grants  in  aid  to
  certain agricultural organizations
 
  RESOLVED,  That  funds originally appropriated in 2022 pursuant to
section 1 of chapter  53  of  the  laws  of  2022  and  reappropriated
pursuant  to  section 1 of chapter 53 of the laws of 2024 which enacts
the  aid to localities, local assistance account from grants in aid to
certain  agricultural  organizations,  including  sub-allocation  or
transfer  to any  department, agency,  or  public  authority.  Funds
from  this appropriation  shall  be  allocated  pursuant  to  a  plan
approved  by  the temporary president of the senate and the director
of the budget which sets  forth  either an itemized list  of  grantees
with  the  amount  to  be  received  by  each  or  the methodology for
allocating such appropriation.  Such plan  and  the  grantees  listed
therein  shall  be  subject  to  the approval  of  the  director  of
the  budget  and  thereafter shall be included in a resolution calling
for the expenditure of  such  monies, which  resolution  must  be
approved  by a majority vote of all members elected to the senate upon
a roll call vote  in  accordance  with  the following  schedule  which
shall  be  added  to  the  schedule  originally  approved  in  Senate
Resolution  R2861  of  2022, as amended by Senate Resolution  R1391
of the laws of 2023:
 
Dare to Be Different Westchester 
  25,000
Lincoln Park Conservancy, Inc. 
  25,000
Red Hook Initiative, INC. 
  40,000
Teens for Food Justice, Inc. 
  25,000
Teens for Food Justice, Inc. 
  10,000
