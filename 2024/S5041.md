# Authorizes certain organizations in N.Y. city to be known as "fire patrols" under the not-for-profit corporation law

**Bill ID:** S5041
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5041 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5041)

## Summary

Authorizes certain organizations in a city of one million or more to be known as "fire patrols" under the not-for-profit corporation law; includes fire patrol vehicles as emergency vehicles under the vehicle and traffic law.
