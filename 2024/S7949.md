# Relates to creating tax parity by imposing an eight and one-half percent tax on all combative sport event ticket sales

**Bill ID:** S7949
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7949 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7949)

## Summary

Relates to creating tax parity by imposing an eight and one-half percent tax on all combative sport event ticket sales.
