## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1698
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  HONORING  David  C. Nolan upon the occasion of his
  retirement after more than 12 years of distinguished
  service to the City of Rome, New York
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  seeks  to  acknowledge  those
individuals  whose  professional  lives  and  civic  endeavors  serve to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, For the past 12 years, David C.  Nolan  rendered  faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to the city of Rome, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  his  capacity as Treasurer, David C. Nolan served with
loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS, After obtaining  his  bachelor's  degree  in  Management  &
Marketing  and  a  master's  degree in Business Administration, David C.
Nolan went on to work at the Rome Savings Bank from February of 1984  to
March of 2011, climbing the ranks to become the Executive Vice President
and CFO; and
 
  WHEREAS,  David C. Nolan, dedicated to his career and interests, has
furthered  his  education,  and  received  numerous  certifications  and
licenses,  including  becoming  a  Certified  Management  Accountant,
Certified in Financial Management, and  Certified  Government  Financial
Manager;  he  has  also a been a member of the Association of Government
Accountants since 2013; and
 
  WHEREAS, Selflessly volunteering his time  and  energies,  David  C.
Nolan  serves  as the Vice Chairman of the Board of Trustees at the Rome
Memorial Hospital, is a Trustee at the Rome Family YMCA, and is a  rider
for the Ride for the Missing Children; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his official acts, David C. Nolan was governed by a keen
sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and
 
  WHEREAS,  During his tenure in public service, David C. Nolan earned
the admiration, esteem, and affection of his colleagues; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor David C. Nolan upon the occasion of his retirement after more than
12 years of distinguished service; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to David C. Nolan.
