# Prohibits approved providers of child health insurance coverage from discriminating against health care providers not participating in the insurer's network

**Bill ID:** A9541
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9541 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9541)

## Summary

Prohibits approved organizations providing coverage under the child health insurance plan from discriminating against health care providers which do not participate in the organization's health care network.
