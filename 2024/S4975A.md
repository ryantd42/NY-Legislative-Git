# Extends the authorization for the county of Seneca to impose an additional one percent sales and compensating use tax

**Bill ID:** S4975AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S4975AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4975AA)

## Summary

Extends the expiration of the authorization for the county of Seneca to impose an additional one percent sales and compensating use tax for two years.
