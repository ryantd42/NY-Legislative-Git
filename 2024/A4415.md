# Authorizes and directs the metropolitan transportation authority to implement a fare refund program for canceled or delayed Long Island Rail Road trains

**Bill ID:** A4415
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Durso
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4415 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4415)

## Summary

Authorizes and directs the metropolitan transportation authority to implement a fare refund program for canceled or delayed Long Island Rail Road trains.

 AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation  to  authorizing
  and directing the metropolitan transportation authority to implement a
  fare  refund  program  for  canceled  or delayed Long Island Rail Road

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1.  Section  1266 of the public authorities law is amended by
 adding a new subdivision 20 to read as follows:
  20. NOTWITHSTANDING ANY OTHER PROVISIONS OF LAW OR THE  TERMS  OF  ANY
 CONTRACT, THE AUTHORITY, AND ANY OF ITS SUBSIDIARY CORPORATIONS INVOLVED
 WITH  THE  LONG  ISLAND  RAIL ROAD, SHALL ESTABLISH AND IMPLEMENT A FARE
 REFUND PROGRAM FOR THE LONG ISLAND RAIL ROAD. THE  FARE  REFUND  PROGRAM
 SHALL,  WHEN  A  TRAIN  IS CANCELED OR DELAYED BY MORE THAN TEN MINUTES,
 ISSUE REFUNDS FOR THE FULL AMOUNT OF EACH  AFFECTED  RIDER'S  FARE.  THE
 FARE  REFUND PROGRAM ESTABLISHED AND IMPLEMENTED PURSUANT TO THIS SUBDI-
 VISION SHALL BE IN OPERATION DURING ALL HOURS IN  WHICH  SUCH  AUTHORITY
 PROVIDES  RAILROAD,  SUBWAY  AND/OR  OMNIBUS  SERVICES, AND SUCH PROGRAM
 SHALL NOT BE LIMITED IN OPERATION TO OFF-PEAK  HOURS  OR  TO  ANY  OTHER
 HOURS DESIGNATED BY THE AUTHORITY OR ITS SUBSIDIARY CORPORATIONS; HOWEV-
 ER,  THE  FARE REFUND PROGRAM MAY BE SUSPENDED TEMPORARILY WHEN AN EMER-
 GENCY SITUATION ARISES.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
