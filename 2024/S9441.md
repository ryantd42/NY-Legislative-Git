# Relates to authorizing qui tam proceedings for civil violations of the cannabis law

**Bill ID:** S9441
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9441 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9441)

## Summary

Authorizes qui tam proceedings for civil violations of the cannabis law.
