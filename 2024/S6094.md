# Establishes certified recovery residences for individuals recovering from substance use disorder

**Bill ID:** S6094
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6094 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6094)

## Summary

Establishes certified recovery residences for individuals recovering from substance use disorder; provides for state aid and municipal site approval.
