# Relates to discrimination against vaccinated persons

**Bill ID:** S5199
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5199 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5199)

## Summary

Prohibits discrimination against vaccinated persons.
