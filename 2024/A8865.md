# Relates to the salary of certain teachers providing instruction in career and technical education to school age students

**Bill ID:** A8865
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8865 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8865)

## Summary

Relates to the salary of certain teachers employed by a board of cooperative educational services providing instruction in career and technical education to school age students.
