# Requires state agencies and contractors to recycle 50% of the waste generated on public works construction and demolition sites

**Bill ID:** S4826
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4826 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4826)

## Summary

Requires state agencies and contractors to recycle a certain percentage of the waste generated on public works construction and demolition sites.
