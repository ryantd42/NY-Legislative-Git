# Establishes an immigrant integration advisory board for each economic development region

**Bill ID:** A8406
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phara Souffrant Forrest
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8406 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8406)

## Summary

Directs the regional economic development council for each economic development region to establish an immigrant integration advisory board.
