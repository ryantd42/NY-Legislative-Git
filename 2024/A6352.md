# Relates to reducing pharmacy benefit manager costs

**Bill ID:** A6352
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6352 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6352)

## Summary

Relates to reducing pharmacy benefit manager costs; defines "pharmacy benefit manager".
