# Enacts the climate resilient New York act of 2024; establishes the office of resilience to assess and identify climate related threats

**Bill ID:** S8158A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8158A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8158A)

## Summary

Enacts the climate resilient New York act; establishes the office of resilience and a resilience task force to assess and identify climate related threats and develop a statewide resilience plan.

  §  934.  STATEWIDE  RESILIENCE  PLAN.  1. TO COORDINATE AND STRENGTHEN

 SHALL PRIORITIZE RISKS FROM INLAND AND COASTAL FLOODING.
  2. SUCH PLAN SHALL INCLUDE, BUT NOT BE LIMITED TO, THE FOLLOWING:
  (A) ARTICULATION OF THE STATE'S RESILIENCE GOALS AND OBJECTIVES;

 PROJECTS, AND PROGRAMS TO  ACHIEVE  THE  STATE'S  RESILIENCE  GOALS  AND

 SERVICES, AND POPULATIONS SERVED;
