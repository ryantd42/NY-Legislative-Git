# Extends the authorization granted to the county of St. Lawrence to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A5021AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5021AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5021AA)

## Summary

Extends the authorization granted to the county of St. Lawrence to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes until November 30th, 2025.
