# Requires utility corporations and municipalities include in an email regarding a bill for service the amount owed on such bill for service

**Bill ID:** S8416
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8416 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8416)

## Summary

Requires utility corporations and municipalities include in an email regarding a bill for service the amount owed on such bill for service where the amount owed is prominently displayed and accurate.
