# Authorizes the commissioner of finance of the city of New York to establish a property tax amnesty program

**Bill ID:** A5810
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Daniel Rosenthal
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A5810 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5810)

## Summary

Authorizes the commissioner of finance of the city of New York to establish a property tax amnesty program; provides that such program shall last for 4 months and shall forgive up to $1000 of interest on a delinquent tax bill if 100 percent of the principal bill is paid.

 AN ACT authorizing the commissioner of finance of the city of  New  York

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the
 commissioner of finance of the city  of  New  York  shall  establish  an
 amnesty program for any property owner who has a delinquent property tax
 balance of more than $1,000 for more than one year. Such amnesty program
 shall last for a period of 4 months and shall:
  1. Forgive 100 percent of the interest on such delinquent property tax
 balance, if 100 percent of the principal bill is paid;
  2.  Forgive 75 percent of the interest on such delinquent property tax
 balance, if 50 percent of the principal bill is paid; and
  3. Forgive 50 percent of the interest on such delinquent property  tax
 balance, if 25 percent of the principal bill is paid.
  Any property owner with a delinquent property tax balance of less than
 $1,000  shall be eligible for forgiveness of 100 percent of the interest
 on such delinquent property tax balance if 100 percent of the  principal
 bill  is  paid.  Any property owner with a remaining delinquent property
 tax balance after participation  in  the  program  shall  enter  into  a
 payment agreement with the commissioner of finance.
