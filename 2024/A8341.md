# Authorizes and directs the office of children and family services to conduct a study on the family assessment response program

**Bill ID:** A8341
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8341 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8341)

## Summary

Authorizes and directs the office of children and family services to conduct a study on the family assessment response program; provides for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof.
