# Requires certain facilities to have a staff member specifically trained in filing assisted outpatient treatment petitions

**Bill ID:** A5185
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5185 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5185)

## Summary

Requires certain hospitals and correctional facilities to have a staff member specifically trained in filing assisted outpatient treatment petitions.
