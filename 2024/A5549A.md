# Relates to manufactured home parks

**Bill ID:** A5549A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5549A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5549A)

## Summary

Removes certain requirements for prospective purchasers of manufactured home parks.
