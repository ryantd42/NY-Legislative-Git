# Directs the Metropolitan Transportation Authority to provide discounted monthly passes to college, graduate and career education students for the purpose of commuting to and from classes

**Bill ID:** A9239
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9239 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9239)

## Summary

Directs the Metropolitan Transportation Authority to provide discounted monthly passes to college, graduate and career education students for the purpose of commuting to and from classes.

  Section 1. a. Notwithstanding  any  provision  to  the  contrary,  the

 provisions  of  its  Monthly  Tickets  for  Schools  program  providing
 discounted monthly transportation passes to include  students  utilizing
 the  Long  Island Railroad and the Metro-North Commuter Railroad Company

  b.  In order to qualify for the the College and Career Education Part-
 nership Program, a college, university or career education school must:

 Commissioner  of  Education  or  accredited  by  a nationally recognized
 accrediting agency or association accepted as such by the regents of the
 state of New York;
  (ii) make application to the Authority to be  added  to  the  list  of
 colleges,  universities  and  career  education schools approved for the
 program; and
  (iii) upon notification of Authority approval, provide access  through
 its official website for full-time college and career education students
 attending the college, university or career education school to register
 through their website for the reduced fee passes.
  c. A qualifying college may be a community college.
  d. Any full-time student attending a qualifying college, university or
 career education school may purchase the reduced fare pass. A student in
 their  final term prior to graduating from a qualifying college, univer-
