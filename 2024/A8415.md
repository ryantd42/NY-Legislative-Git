# Exempts from the payment of sales tax the purchase of oral hygiene products

**Bill ID:** A8415
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8415 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8415)

## Summary

Exempts from the payment of sales tax the purchase of oral hygiene products.
