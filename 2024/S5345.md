# Increases the available number of nominations for the historic business preservation registry per year

**Bill ID:** S5345
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5345 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5345)

## Summary

Increases the available number of nominations per elected official for the historic business preservation registry per year.
