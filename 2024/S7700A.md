# Authorizes a prenatal and postpartum informational mobile application

**Bill ID:** S7700AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S7700AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7700AA)

## Summary

Authorizes a prenatal and postpartum informational mobile application for individuals who are eligible for Medicaid.
