# Provides for the conducting of meetings by public bodies through the use of videoconferencing or other electronic means; repealer

**Bill ID:** A9268
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9268 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9268)

## Summary

Provides for the conducting of meetings by public bodies through the use of videoconferencing or other electronic means; sets rules and procedures related thereto.
