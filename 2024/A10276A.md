# Lowers quorum requirements for meetings of community boards held by videoconferencing in N.Y. city

**Bill ID:** A10276A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alex Bores
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10276A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10276A)

## Summary

Provides for a quorum of one quarter of the members and the presiding officer of a community board physically be present at a location where the public can attend for such board to conduct videoconferencing.
