# Requires the collection of oaths of responsible use from users of certain generative or surveillance advanced artificial intelligence systems

**Bill ID:** A8105A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8105A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8105A)

## Summary

Requires the collection of oaths of responsible use from users of certain generative or surveillance advanced artificial intelligence systems by the operators of such systems, and transmission of such oaths to the attorney general.
