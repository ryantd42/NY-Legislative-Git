# Provides for the issuance of pet insurance

**Bill ID:** S7845
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S7845 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7845)

## Summary

Provides for the issuance of pet insurance that provides coverage for accidents and illnesses of pets.
