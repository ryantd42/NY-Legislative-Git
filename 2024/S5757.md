# Establishes the position of an insurance liaison on the disaster preparedness commission

**Bill ID:** S5757
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [S5757 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5757)

## Summary

Establishes the position of an insurance liaison on the disaster preparedness commission to be appointed by the governor and serve as a non-voting member.
