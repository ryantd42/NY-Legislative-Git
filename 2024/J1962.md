# Commending the Desert of New York upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Desert Conference on May 18, 2024

**Bill ID:** J1962
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1962 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1962)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1962
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Desert  of  New  York  upon  the
  occasion of hosting its Annual Desert Conference  on
  May 18, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to commend those
illustrious organizations of the State  of  New  York  whose  focus  and
intent  have  made  a substantial impact on the quality of life in their
communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the Desert of New York upon the occasion of hosting  its  Annual
Desert Conference on Saturday, May 18, 2024, in Suffern, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Desert of New York Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles
Mystic  Shrine of North and South America, Inc. is an organization which
embraces the basic fundamental concepts of  the  Masonic  Order  with  a
broader spectrum of dispensing charity in the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Each  year, this auspicious conference honors the selfless
work and undertakings of this grand body, and pays just tribute  to  its
dedicated  leadership  and members for their ceaseless dedication to the
local community,  tirelessly  giving  of  their  time  and  energies  to
countless initiatives and programs throughout the year; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  gathering  of  all  Imperial  Officers,  Illustrious
Potentates and  Commandresses,  current  ranking  officers,  Nobles  and
Daughters  puts  forth a light and celestial joy few words can describe,
while  truly  cherishing  the  tradition  and  history  of  this  iconic
organization; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Furthermore, they come together to recognize the good work
being brought forth across the  entirety  of  the  State  of  New  York,
gathering  in harmony, sisterhood, and brotherhood to share ideas, hope,
and continuing to strengthen the  bond  between  each  other  and  their
communities both near and far; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Under  the  guidance and leadership of the honorable, most
benevolent Imperial Deputy of the Desert, Damien E. Hall, the Nobles  of
the Desert of New York continue to conduct virtuous acts, bringing honor
to their Temples and the State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Through  the Imperial Commandress Dr. Karen Wright-Chisolm
and under the guidance and  leadership  of  the  most  elegant  Imperial
Deputy  of  the  Desert Cassandra Duffy, they will continue to usher our
daughters forward in all  their  most  excellent  endeavors,  being  the
backbones of their communities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over its distinguished history, the Desert of New York has
demonstrated continuous service to the residents and  communities  which
it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  the  Desert  of  New  York  is in direct
correlation to the efforts of  its  dedicated  leadership  and  members,
whose  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an
impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising
standard  of  excellence  in all endeavors on behalf of the organization
and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, This auspicious occasion presents an opportunity  for  this
Legislative Body to recognize and pay tribute to the Desert of New York;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body that when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Desert of New York upon the occasion of hosting  its  Annual
Desert Conference on May 18, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Desert of New  York  Ancient  Egyptian  Arabic  Order
Nobles Mystic Shrine of North and South America, Inc.
