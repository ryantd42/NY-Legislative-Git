# Authorizes a public employee's contractual rights extended for six months beyond expiration date of current agreement for negotiation of new agreement

**Bill ID:** A4700
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4700 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4700)

## Summary

Authorizes a public employee's contractual rights be extended for up to six months beyond the expiration date of such employee's agreement for the sole purpose of negotiating a new agreement.
