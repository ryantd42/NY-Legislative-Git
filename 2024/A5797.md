# Allows voters age sixty-five or older to request an absentee ballot

**Bill ID:** A5797
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5797 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5797)

## Summary

Allows voters age sixty-five or older to request an absentee ballot.

  (d) absent from his or her voting  residence  because  he  or  she  is
