# Relates to late fees for failure to timely complete the on-line training course for lobbyists and clients

**Bill ID:** S9257
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9257 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9257)

## Summary

Authorizes the imposition of late fees for failure to timely complete the on-line training course for lobbyists and clients.
