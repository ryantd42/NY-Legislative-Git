# Allows for retail license for on-premises consumption to be granted to establishments located near a school, church, synagogue or other place of worship; repealer

**Bill ID:** A10122
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steven Raga
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10122 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10122)

## Summary

Allows for retail license for on-premises consumption to be granted to establishments located on the same street or avenue and within two hundred feet of a building occupied exclusively as a school, church, synagogue or other place of worship.
