# Relates to registration of certain companies whose primary business purpose is related to artificial intelligence

**Bill ID:** S8214AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8214AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8214AA)

## Summary

Requires the registration of certain companies whose primary business purpose is related to artificial intelligence as evidenced by their NAIC code.
