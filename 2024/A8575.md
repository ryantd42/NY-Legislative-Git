# Permits the use of snowmobiles on the right-of-way of certain public highways within the village of Bemus Point in Chautauqua county

**Bill ID:** A8575
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A8575 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8575)

## Summary

Permits the use of snowmobiles on the right-of-way of certain portions of state route nine hundred fifty-four-h within the village of Bemus Point in Chautauqua county.
