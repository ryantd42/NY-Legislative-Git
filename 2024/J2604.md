# Commending Jack Beyer upon the occasion of his designation as State President of the Future Farmers of America

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2604 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2604)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2604
 
BY: Senator WALCZYK
 
  COMMENDING  Jack  Beyer  upon  the occasion of his
  designation as State President of the Future Farmers
  of America
 
  WHEREAS, Individuals who give of their time and energies  and  serve
the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration
and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Jack Beyer
upon  the  occasion  of his designation as State President of the Future
Farmers of America; it is with great pleasure that this Legislative Body
acknowledges noteworthy events which deserve special recognition; and
 
  WHEREAS, At age 17, Jack Beyer is a third-generation farmer at Glory
Days Farm in Lowville, New York; the farm has 120 cows and is  comprised
of 500 acres; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Jack Beyer has been an active member of Lowville Academy's
Future Farmers of America (FFA) chapter for six years; after a  rigorous
interview process, he was elected as the State President; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Jack Beyer has given not only of his time and energies but
also of his competence, intelligence, and leadership, consequently being
designated for this special honor; he attributes his selection as  State
President  to  his  passion  and  drive,  recently  completing  officer
training; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  State  President,  Jack  Beyer  plans  to  facilitate
workshops,  visit  other chapters of the FFA, vote on national FFA laws,
and advocate for agriculture, a significant part of the foundation  upon
which our distinct American heritage was built; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attending  an agricultural college and potentially serving
as a lobbyist or working in a governmental agency are Jack Beyer's  main
objectives;  he  hopes  to  inspire  other students to take advantage of
opportunities which are offered by the FFA; he is enthusiastic about the
upcoming year during which he will lead New York State's Future  Farmers
of America and work with his team; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefit of others  which  Jack  Beyer  has  displayed
throughout his life; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Jack Beyer  upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation  as  State
President of the Future Farmers of America; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Jack Beyer.
