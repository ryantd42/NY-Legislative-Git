# Establishes the community disaster relief and recovery act of 2023

**Bill ID:** S4928
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4928 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4928)

## Summary

Establishes the community disaster relief and recovery act which will create an emergency assistance program for undocumented individuals impacted by a natural disaster.
