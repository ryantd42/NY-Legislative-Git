# Provides for the use of public campsites, parks and other public places of recreation in the state by individuals with a mental disability free of charge

**Bill ID:** S6137
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6137 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6137)

## Summary

Allows for individuals with a mental disability who receive or are eligible to receive services for such disability to use any of the public campsites, parks and other public places of recreation in the state without the payment of any fees or other charges.
