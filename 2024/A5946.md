# Requires that the NYS board of regents holds at least two meetings a year in the city of New York

**Bill ID:** A5946
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5946 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5946)

## Summary

Requires that the NYS board of regents hold at least two meetings a year in New York city; requires announcements of the time, date, location and agenda thereof.
