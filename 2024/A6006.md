# Authorizes the county of Nassau assessor to accept an application for a retroactive real property tax exemption from the Incorporated Village of Hempstead Community Development Agency

**Bill ID:** A6006
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Taylor Darling
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6006 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6006)

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Nassau assessor to accept an application for a retroactive real property tax exemption from the Incorporated Village of Hempstead Community Development Agency for the 2020--2021 school taxes and the 2021 general taxes for the property at 40 Byrd Street.
