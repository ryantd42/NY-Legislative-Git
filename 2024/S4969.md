# Relates to placing limitations on initial regulated rents

**Bill ID:** S4969
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4969 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4969)

## Summary

Places limitations on initial regulated rents to not exceed the average rent for a comparable rent regulated housing accommodation.
