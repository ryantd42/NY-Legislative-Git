# Enables the victim of a misdemeanor to make a statement at the sentencing of the defendant

**Bill ID:** S5313
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5313 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5313)

## Summary

Enables the victim of a misdemeanor to make a statement at the sentencing of the defendant.
