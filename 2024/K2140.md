# Congratulating Wayne Jagow upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the 19th Annual Key to the Locks Award on May 18, 2024

**Bill ID:** K2140
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2140 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2140)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2140
 
BY: M. of A. Norris
 
  CONGRATULATING  Wayne  Jagow  upon the occasion of
  his designation as recipient of the 19th Annual  Key
  to the Locks Award on May 18, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  Individuals  who give of their time and energies and serve
the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration
and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate Wayne  Jagow  upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation  as
recipient of the 19th Annual Key to the Locks Award, to be recognized at
the Erie Discovery Center on Saturday, May 18, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Key  to  the  Locks  Award was established in 2006 to
recognize persons in Niagara County who exemplify the community  spirit,
or promote the heritage or preservation, of the historic Erie Canal; and
 
  WHEREAS, Wayne Jagow has been named the recipient of the 19th Annual
Key  to  the  Locks  Award  due  to  his lifetime of volunteer community
service to residents and organizations of Niagara County; and
 
  WHEREAS, A retired and Lutheran minister, past Niagara County clerk,
and past Niagara County  Sheriff's  deputy,  has  donated  his  time  to
numerous service organizations for over the past 40 years; and
 
  WHEREAS, With a strong belief in active community involvement, Wayne
Jagow  is  an exempt member of Wrights Corners Volunteer Fire Company, a
past member and president of the Cornell Cooperative  Extension's  Board
of  Directors,  and  a co-founder and past president of Big Brothers/Big
Sisters of  Niagara  County;  he  also  served  on  the  Niagara  County
Commission  on  the  Status  of  Women, was the first coordinator of the
Niagara  County  Bicycle  Safety  and  Junior  Fire  Setters  Prevention
programs,  co-chaired  the  Niagara  County Bicentennial Commission, and
faithfully served with a number of other community associations; and
 
  WHEREAS, Wayne Jagow has been called upon to contribute his time and
talents to countless civic and charitable endeavors and has always given
of himself unstintingly; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout the entire period of his service,  a  period  of
constructive  involvement,  Wayne  Jagow  has stood constant in dignity,
good grace, and humor; and
 
  WHEREAS, With him throughout have been his wife, Maureen, and  their
two  children,  Andrew  and Bethany, all of whom feel privileged to be a
part of his life and rejoice in his plentiful achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by  an
individual  for  the  benefit  of others which Wayne Jagow has displayed
throughout his life; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Wayne  Jagow  upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation as
recipient of the 19th Annual Key to the Locks Award on May 18, 2024; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Wayne Jagow.
