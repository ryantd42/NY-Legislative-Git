# Authorizes an increase in the maximum speed limit for travel on certain roadways

**Bill ID:** A5044
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5044 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5044)

## Summary

Authorizes an increase in the maximum speed limit to 70 mph for travel on roadways that currently have a maximum speed limit of 65 mph.
