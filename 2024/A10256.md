# Authorizes the Dormitory Authority to provide public financing options to build new police facilities in certain areas

**Bill ID:** A10256
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Ron Kim
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10256 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10256)

## Summary

Authorizes the Dormitory Authority to provide public financing options to build new police facilities in areas that currently have one police precinct or facility covering 300,000 or more people.
