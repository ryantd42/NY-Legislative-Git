# Categorizes members of an auxiliary police program with police officers and peace officers for purposes of certain felony classifications

**Bill ID:** A6237
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6237 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6237)

## Summary

Categorizes members of an auxiliary police program organized and maintained by a state or local police department with police officers and peace officers for purposes of certain felony classifications.
