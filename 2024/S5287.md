# Extends the authority of NYSARC, Inc., to maintain bonds

**Bill ID:** S5287
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5287 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5287)

## Summary

Extends the authority of NYSARC, Inc., to maintain bonds until 2028.
