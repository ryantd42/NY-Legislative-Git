# Categorizes the assault of an individual responsible for local parking enforcement as a class D felony

**Bill ID:** A5891
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5891 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5891)

## Summary

Categorizes the assault of an individual responsible for local parking enforcement performing his or her lawful duties under such position as a class D felony.
