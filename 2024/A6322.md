# Extends the authorization granted to the county of Broome to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A6322
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Donna Lupardo
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A6322 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6322)

## Summary

Extends from November 30, 2023 to November 30, 2025, the authorization granted to the county of Broome to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes.
