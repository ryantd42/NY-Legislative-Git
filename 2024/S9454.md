# Extends the provisions of chapter 777 of the laws of 1986 relating to small loans to owners of multiple dwellings to remove substandard or insanitary conditions

**Bill ID:** S9454
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9454 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9454)

Extends the provisions relating to small loans to owners of multiple dwellings to remove substandard or insanitary conditions.
