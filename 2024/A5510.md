# Relates to public assistance applications

**Bill ID:** A5510
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5510 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5510)

## Summary

Requires the office of temporary and disability assistance to simplify the application process for public assistance benefits.
