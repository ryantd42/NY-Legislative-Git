# Relates to charter schools

**Bill ID:** A4458
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Benedetto
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4458 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4458)

## Summary

Provides additional requirements; applies additional prohibitions to charter schools.
