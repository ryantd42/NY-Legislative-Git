# Increases penalty for leaving scene of an accident without reporting to class E felony

**Bill ID:** S5861
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5861 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5861)

## Summary

Increases the penalty for leaving the scene of an accident without reporting to a class E felony.
