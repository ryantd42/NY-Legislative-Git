# Provides for the automatic issuance of identification cards

**Bill ID:** A8948
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8948 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8948)

## Summary

Provides for the automatic issuance of identification cards for any person whose driver's license or learner's permit is expired, suspended, revoked or surrendered.
