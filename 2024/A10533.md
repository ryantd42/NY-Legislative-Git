# Extends the provisions of chapter 371 of the laws of 2009 amending the public health law relating to the residential care off-site facility demonstration project

**Bill ID:** A10533
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Sarah Clark
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10533 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10533)

## Summary

Extends provisions relating to the residential care off-site facility demonstration project until June 30, 2027.
