# Prohibits the sale of certain hazardous inhalants to persons under the age of twenty-one

**Bill ID:** S8156
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8156 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8156)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of certain hazardous inhalants to persons under the age of twenty-one.
