## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2201
 
BY: Senator TEDISCO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  Centennial Anniversary of Lake
  Avenue Elementary School in  Saratoga  Springs,  New
  York
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend those institutions of  commitment  and  high  achievement  whose
purpose and endeavor have been dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in
the education of the youth of this noble Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  100th  Anniversary of Lake Avenue Elementary School in
Saratoga Springs, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Lake Avenue Elementary School,  established  in  1924,  has
remained  unwavering  in  its  commitment  to  educating  young children
garnering a Blue-Ribbon School Designation on three different occasions;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Located at 126 Lake Avenue in Saratoga Springs and built in
1923, the school serves 395 students in grades K-5, emphasizing  a  warm
welcome  and  sense  of  belonging in supporting each student's personal
pathway for learning; and
 
  WHEREAS, Since its inception, multiple generations of families  have
come  to  Lake  Avenue  Elementary School to embark on their educational
journeys; and
 
  WHEREAS, Lake Avenue School boasts a Building Leadership  Team  made
up of parents, guardians and elementary school staff who meet throughout
the  year  to  collaboratively  discuss topics that are important to the
school; and
 
  WHEREAS, Lake Avenue School is the only  school  in  its  respective
district to provide an Annual Outdoor Education Program in which all 5th
graders  stay  two nights and three days, taking special outdoor classes
at campgrounds in the Adirondacks, a Drama Club  in  which  children  in
3rd-5th grade can learn to performance arts and stage crew skills, and a
STEM Science Lab for all grades; and
 
  WHEREAS,  To  celebrate their many years of academic excellence, the
school will hold a Centennial Celebration for both current  Lake  Avenue
students, families and alumni on Saturday, May 4, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Under  the  able  leadership  of  its Principal, Elizabeth
Carroll  and  District  Superintendent  Michael  Patton,  Lake  Avenue
Elementary  School  has  been  steadfast  in  the  District's mission to
empower all  learners  in  an  engaging,  collaborative  environment  to
discover their personal pathways to the future; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  school's  curriculum  includes a Community Garden and
Food Lab for all grades offering a unique, multi-sensory,  inquiry-based
 
educational opportunity which promotes a "locally grown" concept to food
production; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Steeped  in a proud and distinguished history, Lake Avenue
Elementary School may look back with pride upon its  humble  beginnings,
find just satisfaction in its purposeful growth and hold true of purpose
in  its  commitment  to serving the educational needs of the citizens of
Saratoga County and beyond; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the Centennial Anniversary of Lake Avenue Elementary School
in Saratoga Springs, noting the propriety of its goals, the constancy of
its commitment, and the significance of its accomplishments in preparing
the youth of today and leaders of tomorrow; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Lake  Avenue  Elementary  School  Principal  Elizabeth
Carroll, Saratoga Springs City School  District  Superintendent  Michael
Patton, and School Board Members.
