# Relates to certain payments to the horsemen's organization

**Bill ID:** S8440
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [S8440 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8440)

## Summary

Relates to certain payments to the horsemen's organization; extends such provisions to 2025.
