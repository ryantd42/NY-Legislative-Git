# Requires a sworn affidavit stating the seller was informed of the fair market value of the property

**Bill ID:** A10294
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenny Burgos
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A10294 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10294)

## Summary

Requires a sworn affidavit stating the seller was informed of the fair market value of the property which includes an estimate of the fair market value of the property be included in the real property transfer form.
