# Relates to action arising out of consumer debt

**Bill ID:** S4750
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4750 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4750)

## Summary

Relates to action arising out of consumer debt; defines consumer debt and replaces consumer credit transaction with such term.
