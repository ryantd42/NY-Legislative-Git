# Relates to establishing a uterine fibroids awareness and education program

**Bill ID:** A10048
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Gonzalez-Rojas
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10048 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10048)

## Summary

Establishes a uterine fibroids awareness and education program on the symptoms, diagnosis and treatment of uterine fibroids and the elevated risk for minority women.
