# Relates to authorizing community colleges offering an associates degree program in nursing to offer a four-year bachelors degree program in nursing

**Bill ID:** A4647
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4647 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4647)

## Summary

Authorizes community colleges offering an associates degree program in nursing to offer a four-year bachelor degree program in nursing.
