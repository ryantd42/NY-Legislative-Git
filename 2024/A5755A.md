# Authorizes the county of Jefferson to impose additional sales tax

**Bill ID:** A5755A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5755A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5755A)

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Jefferson to impose additional sales tax until November 30th, 2025.
