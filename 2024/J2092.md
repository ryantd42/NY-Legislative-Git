# Congratulating the Utica-Notre Dame High School Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Basketball Class B Championship on March 16, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2092
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2092 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2092)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2092
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Utica-Notre  Dame High School
  Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing
  the NYSPHSAA  Basketball  Class  B  Championship  on
  March 16, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Utica-Notre Dame High School Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion  of
capturing  the  2024  NYSPHSAA  Basketball Class B Championship, held on
Saturday, March 16, 2024 at Hudson Valley Community College in Troy, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and  applause  of  their  excited  fans,  the
Utica-Notre  Dame  High School Girls Basketball Team took its first lead
of the game with under 4 minutes left in  the  last  quarter  to  defeat
top-ranked Albany Academy in a decisive 55-51 victory; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Their  first state title since 2014, the Jugglers utilized
their strengths by applying pressure  with  defense  and  making  timely
offensive plays; trailing 28-18 at half-time, the team was able to close
the score gap and dig deep to achieve the win; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the school's colors of navy blue and old
gold,  family,  friends,  and  the  community  at  large  loyally  and
enthusiastically supported the Jugglers throughout their journey as they
ended their season with an impressive 24-3 record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  basketball, which demands athletic
prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach John Snyder and Assistant Coaches
Tom Pederson, Gillian Keeler, and Gennaro Durso have worked hard to hone
the  skills  of  this  championship  team,  teaching  these  outstanding
athletes  lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the court;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach John Snyder,  Assistant  Coaches  Tom  Pederson,
Gillian  Keeler,  Gennaro  Durso, and all of the outstanding athletes on
the Utica-Notre Dame High School  Girls  Basketball  Team  have  clearly
utilized  dedication,  determination and teamwork in providing a lasting
contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition  of  their
school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of  the  Utica-Notre  Dame  High  School  Girls
 
Basketball  Team:  Cory  Kearney,  Erin  Trinkaus,  Lauren Kulpa, Maggie
Trinkaus, Emma Johnson, Vita Waters, Tessa Arcuri, Amanie Jadwick,  Ella
Trinkaus,  Julianna  Otis,  Jenna  Herron,  Abbie  Kotary, Lily Johnson,
Olivia  Russitano,  and  Vega  Rosemyer;  Head  Coach  John  Snyder; and
Assistant Coaches Tom Pederson, Gillian Keeler, and Gennaro  Durso  upon
the  occasion  of  capturing  the  2024  NYSPHSAA  Basketball  Class  B
Championship on March 16, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  members  of the Utica-Notre Dame High School Girls
Basketball Team, Head Coach John Snyder, and the aforementioned Assitant
Coaches.
