# Authorizes the city of New York to impose a personal income surcharge on high income residents to fund transit improvements

**Bill ID:** A4474AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4474AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4474AA)

## Summary

Authorizes the city of New York to impose a personal income surcharge on high income residents to fund transit improvements.
