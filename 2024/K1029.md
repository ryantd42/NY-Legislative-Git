# Congratulating the Queensbury Boys Nordic Ski Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Nordic Ski Championship on February 27, 2024

**Bill ID:** K1029
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Simpson
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K1029 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K1029)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 1029
 
BY: M. of A. Simpson
 
  CONGRATULATING the Queensbury Boys Nordic Ski Team
  upon  the  occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Nordic
  Ski Championship on February 27, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
Queensbury  Boys  Nordic  Ski  Team  upon  the occasion of capturing the
NYSPHSAA Nordic Ski Championship on Tuesday, February 27, 2024, held  at
Mount Van Hoevenberg in Lake Placid, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  To  the  praise  and  applause  of their excited fans, the
Queensbury Boys Nordic Ski Team captured their fifth state title,  their
last one being in 2020; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Family,  friends,  and  the community at large loyally and
enthusiastically  supported  the  Queensbury  Boys  Nordic  Ski  Team
throughout  their  journey  as  their  season  ended  with  impressive
individual and relay team wins to bring home the championship; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as skiing, which demands athletic  prowess,
speed,  and  agility, Head Coach Paul Allison and Assistant Coaches Matt
Flint and Chris Frielinghaus have worked hard to hone the skills of this
championship team, teaching these  outstanding  athletes  lessons  which
will prove invaluable both on and off the slopes; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Head  Coach Paul Allison, Assistant Coaches Matt Flint and
Chris  Frielinghaus,  and  all  of  the  outstanding  athletes  on  the
Queensbury  Boys  Nordic  Ski  Team  have  clearly  utilized dedication,
determination, and resilience in providing a lasting contribution to the
spirit of  excellence  which  is  a  tradition  of  their  school;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of the Queensbury Boys Nordic Team: Ben Jenkin,
Alden Guay, Josh Jenkin, Liam Davidson, and Patrick Cirillo; Head  Coach
Paul  Allison;  and  Assistant Coaches Matt Flint and Chris Frielinghaus
upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Nordic Ski  Championship  on
February 27, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members of the Queensbury Boys Nordic Ski Team;  Head
Coach  Paul  Allison;  and  Assistant  Coaches  Matt  Flint  and  Chris
Frielinghaus.
