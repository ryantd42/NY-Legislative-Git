# Prohibits increases in rates of homeowners' insurance in excess of twenty-five percent per year

**Bill ID:** S8481
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8481 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8481)

## Summary

Prohibits increases in rates of homeowners' insurance in excess of twenty-five percent per year except where agreed to in the policy.
