# Waives the fee for a commercial driver's license for a member of the military or a veteran

**Bill ID:** S6060
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6060 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6060)

## Summary

Waives the fee for a commercial driver's license for a member of the military or a veteran when they submit an application for military skills test waiver as part of his or her application.
