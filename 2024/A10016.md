# Permits certain not-for-profit corporations engaged in engineering for certain conservation efforts to do business or provide professional engineering, land surveying or professional geology services in the state

**Bill ID:** A10016
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Sarah Clark
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10016 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10016)

## Summary

Relates to permitting certain not-for-profit corporations engaged in engineering for certain conservation efforts to do business or provide professional engineering, land surveying, or professional geology services in the state.
