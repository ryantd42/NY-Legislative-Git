# Relates to number stickers for electric vehicles

**Bill ID:** A8418
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8418 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8418)

## Summary

Authorizes the commissioner of motor vehicles to develop number stickers to be used on electric vehicles in the place of number plates.
