# Provides that crimes committed against a person on school grounds shall be deemed to be one category higher than the specified crime the person committed

**Bill ID:** A5953
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5953 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5953)

## Summary

Provides that crimes committed against a person on school grounds shall be deemed to be one category higher than the specified crime the person committed; enacts "Suzanne's Law".
