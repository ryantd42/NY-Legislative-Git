# Relates to prohibiting the establishment of new for-profit hospices

**Bill ID:** A6032
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A6032 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6032)

## Summary

Prohibits the establishment of new for-profit hospices or increasing the capacity of existing for-profit hospices.
