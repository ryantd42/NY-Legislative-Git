# Grants real property tax exemption to disabled veterans regardless of whether he or she served during a "period of war"

**Bill ID:** A4362
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4362 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4362)

## Summary

Grants real property tax exemption to disabled veterans regardless of whether he or she served during a "period of war".
