# Exempts community colleges from the imposition of the metropolitan commuter transportation mobility tax

**Bill ID:** S4996
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dean Murray
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4996 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4996)

## Summary

Exempts community colleges from the imposition of the metropolitan commuter transportation mobility tax.
