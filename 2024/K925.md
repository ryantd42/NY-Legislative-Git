# Congratulating Christopher Bucaj upon the occasion of receiving the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout, the most prestigious of Scouting honors

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K925 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K925)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 925
 
BY: M. of A. Burdick
 
  CONGRATULATING Christopher Bucaj upon the occasion
  of  receiving the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout,
  the most prestigious of Scouting honors
 
  WHEREAS, Christopher  Bucaj,  through  his  diligence  and  rigorous
efforts,  has  achieved  Scouting's  highest  and most prestigious rank,
Eagle Scout; the Eagle Court of Honor ceremony will be held at Our  Lady
of Sorrows Church in White Plains, New York, on Saturday, April 6, 2024;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Christopher  Bucaj  is  a  member of Boy Scouts of America
Troop 73, part of the Greater Hudson Valley Council,  in  White  Plains,
New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  America has achieved her greatest heights of glory through
the dedication, determination, and tireless efforts of  the  outstanding
citizens of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our
youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain her glory; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Boy Scouts of America and its members are dedicated to
the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in  Scouting,  earned
by  approximately  five  percent of all Boy Scouts, is the distinguished
rank of Eagle Scout; and
 
  WHEREAS, This outstanding young man has been  involved  in  Scouting
for  many years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals, and has attained all
of the necessary  achievements,  including  completion  of  a  community
service  project,  which  was  successfully  planned,  developed,  and
executed; and
 
  WHEREAS, In August of 2022, Christopher Bucaj contributed  his  time
and  hard  work  to  complete  his  project  for the White Plains Police
Department; he constructed a large, complex training city which included
two large tables, 20 model buildings, and handmade cardboard replicas of
White Plains landmarks; and
 
  WHEREAS, His innovative creation has been instrumental  in  training
police  officers  for  emergency  situations; for this ingenuity, he was
awarded a Certificate of Appreciation by the White Plains Department  of
Public Safety during an award ceremony on October 12, 2022; and
 
  WHEREAS,  On October 19, 2023, Christopher Bucaj officially obtained
the Eagle  Scout  rank  after  ambitiously  advancing  through  numerous
leadership  positions  in  his Troop, including Senior Patrol Leader for
one year; he has also earned 33 merit badges, a Bronze Palm, and a  Gold
Palm;  a  highlight  of his scouting career was the seven-day wilderness
trek in the rugged terrain of the  Philmont  Scout  Reservation  in  New
Mexico; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  Additionally,  Christopher  Bucaj  is an exceptional honor
roll student at White Plains High School and a  National  Honor  Society
inductee; active in sports, he is a co-captain of the varsity swim team;
playing the viola, he is a member of the symphony orchestra; and
 
  WHEREAS, Outside of Scouting and academics, Christopher Bucaj enjoys
volunteering  in his community, spending Friday evenings at the Ridgeway
Alliance Food Pantry where he assists with  organizing  food  deliveries
and  distributing  food donations to disadvantaged Westchester families;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Christopher Bucaj's  perseverance  in  rising  through  the
ranks  to  Eagle  Scout  is a strong indication of his ability to master
difficult tasks; his accomplishment will  enable  him  to  emerge  as  a
leader,  fully  equipped  to  meet  the  challenges  of  today's complex
society; and
 
  WHEREAS, Christopher Bucaj's dedication to  the  well-being  of  the
citizens  of  White  Plains  is  represented  through  his  project  and
demonstrates his devotion to both community and  commitment  to  others;
and
 
  WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to his family, his school,
his  community,  his State, and his Nation; undoubtedly, his future will
be filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now,  therefore,  be
it
 
  RESOLVED,  That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in
recognition of the significance  of  this  meritorious  achievement,  to
congratulate  and honor Christopher Bucaj on achieving the rank of Eagle
Scout; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Christopher Bucaj.
