# Establishes parity in reimbursement rates for supportive stabilization services provided that are substantially equivalent to crisis stabilization center services

**Bill ID:** S5367
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5367 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5367)

## Summary

Establishes parity in reimbursement rates for the first twenty-four hours of supportive stabilization services provided that are substantially equivalent to crisis stabilization center services.
