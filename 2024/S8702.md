# Exempts residents from Rockland county or Orange county from congestion pricing in New York city

**Bill ID:** S8702
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8702 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8702)

## Summary

Provides that any resident of Rockland county or Orange county shall not be charged a toll for entry into or remaining in the central business district in New York city.
