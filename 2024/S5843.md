# Provides line of duty sick leave for first responders who are diagnosed with or display symptoms of a pandemic virus

**Bill ID:** S5843
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5843 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5843)

## Summary

Provides line of duty sick leave for first responders who are diagnosed with or display symptoms of a pandemic virus.
