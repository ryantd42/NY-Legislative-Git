# Requires mandatory health insurance coverage for prosthetic devices

**Bill ID:** S6039
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6039 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6039)

## Summary

Requires mandatory health insurance coverage for prosthetic devices; requires coverage where the model is determined to adequately meet the medical needs of the policy holder.
