# Congratulating the Ward Melville High School Varsity Cheerleading Team upon the occasion of capturing the Class B NYSPHSAA Cheerleading State Championship on March 2, 2024

**Bill ID:** K945
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Edward Flood
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K945 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K945)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 945
 
BY: M. of A. Flood
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Ward  Melville  High  School
  Varsity  Cheerleading  Team  upon  the  occasion  of
  capturing  the  Class  B NYSPHSAA Cheerleading State
  Championship on March 2, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
Ward Melville High School Varsity Cheerleading Team upon the occasion of
capturing  the  Class  B  NYSPHSAA  Cheerleading  State  Championship on
Saturday, March 2, 2024, at  the  Visions  Veterans  Memorial  Arena  in
Binghamton, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Family,  friends,  and  the  community  loyally  and
enthusiastically supported the  Patriots  throughout  their  journey  to
capturing the championship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Suffolk  crowning  its  third  state  champion,  the  Ward
Melville High School Varsity  Cheerleading  Team  capped  an  undefeated
season  with  their  first  trip  to the state championship and the coed
title; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as  cheerleading,  which  demands  athletic
prowess  and  agility,  Coaches  Georgia  Gass and Margaret Hurley, have
worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these
outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off
the competition stage; and
 
  WHEREAS, Coaches Georgia Gass and Margaret Hurley  and  all  of  the
outstanding  athletes  on  the  Ward  Melville  High  School  Varsity
Cheerleading Team have clearly utilized dedication,  determination,  and
teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence
which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members  of  the  Ward  Melville  High  School  Varsity
Cheerleading  Team:  Kate  Biondi,  Juliet  Britman, Emma Clontz, Sophia
Corpac, Shea Curtin, Sasha Dabrowski, Madison Esposito, Fawn Honor, Emma
Jackson, Ian Licavoli, Emma Miller, Kaylee Murphy, Keira Pirozzi,  Kiera
Riddle,  Isabella Riegel, Olivia Riegel, Jessica Scardino, Mattea Shank,
Abby Stabile, Kimberly Vosswinkel, and Coaches Georgia Gass and Margaret
Hurley upon the occasion of capturing the Class B NYSPHSAA  Cheerleading
State Championship on March 2, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members of the  Ward  Melville  High  School  Varsity
Cheerleading Team and Coaches Georgia Gass and Margaret Hurley.
