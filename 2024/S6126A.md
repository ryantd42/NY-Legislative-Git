# Authorizes the town of Brookhaven to alienate and replace certain parklands for the purpose of constructing a municipal drainage system

**Bill ID:** S6126AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dean Murray
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S6126AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6126AA)

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Brookhaven to alienate certain parklands for the purpose of constructing a municipal drainage system and acquire and dedicate certain other lands as parklands.
