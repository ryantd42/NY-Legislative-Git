# Prohibits the state liquor authority from conditioning a liquor license based on a cabaret law that has been repealed by a local jurisdiction

**Bill ID:** A9393
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monique Chandler-Waterman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9393 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9393)

## Summary

Prohibits the state liquor authority from conditioning a liquor license based on a cabaret law that has been repealed by a local jurisdiction or social practice or behavior otherwise allowed and not prohibited or banned by state or local law.
