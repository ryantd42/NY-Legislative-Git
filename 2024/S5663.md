# Prohibits the sale of cleaning products containing triclosan, triclocarban or derivatives of such antibacterial compounds

**Bill ID:** S5663
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S5663 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5663)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of cleaning products containing triclosan, triclocarban or derivatives of such antibacterial compounds.
