# Relates to requiring incarcerated individual sexual harassment complaints made against department employees to be investigated by the attorney general

**Bill ID:** A4482
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4482 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4482)

## Summary

Requires incarcerated individual sexual harassment complaints made against department employees to be investigated by the attorney general; makes the findings and recommendations of the attorney general final and binding on the part of the department.
