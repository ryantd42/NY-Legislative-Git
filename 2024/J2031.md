# Congratulating Piper Dock upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHSAA Slalom Championship on February 26, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2031
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2031 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2031)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2031
 
BY: Senator STEC
 
  CONGRATULATING  Piper  Dock  upon  the occasion of
  winning the NYSPHSAA Slalom Championship on February
  26, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Piper
Dock upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHSAA Slalom  Championship;  to
the  praise and applause of her excited fans, the Black Bear beat all of
her competitors in  a  landmark  victory  to  win  the  Championship  on
Tuesday,  February  27,  2024, at Whiteface Mountain in Lake Placid, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Family, friends, and the community  at  large  loyally  and
enthusiastically  supported  Piper  Dock  throughout  her journey as she
ended her season with the slalom championship title with a combined time
of 1:38.98; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as skiing, which demands athletic  prowess,
speed,  and  agility, her father and Head Coach Jeff Dock worked hard to
hone the skills of this championship skier  and  his  beloved  daughter,
teaching  this  outstanding  athlete lessons which will prove invaluable
both on and off the slopes; and
 
  WHEREAS, Coach Jeff  Dock  and  champion  athlete  Piper  Dock  have
clearly  utilized dedication, determination, and resilience in providing
a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a  tradition
of their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Piper Dock of Glens Falls High School and Head  Coach  Jeff
Dock on their outstanding season; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Piper Dock and Head Coach Jeff Dock.
