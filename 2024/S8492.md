# Relates to the sale of digital electronic equipment and providing diagnostic and repair information

**Bill ID:** S8492
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8492 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8492)

## Summary

Modifies what qualifies as an original equipment manufacturer and the manufacture date when hardware must comply with the right to repair law; repeals certain limitations on the right to repair law.
