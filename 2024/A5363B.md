# Prohibits the sale and distribution of anti-fogging sprays or wipes containing perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances

**Bill ID:** A5363BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Emily Gallagher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5363BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5363BB)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale and distribution of anti-fogging sprays or wipes containing perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances; provides penalties for violations.
