# Requires all persons in a city having a population of one million or more operating a bicycle, electric bicycle or electric scooter to wear a helmet

**Bill ID:** A8092
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rebecca Seawright
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8092 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8092)

## Summary

Requires all persons in a city having a population of one million or more operating a bicycle, electric bicycle or electric scooter to wear a helmet; imposes a civil penalty for violation of such requirement.
