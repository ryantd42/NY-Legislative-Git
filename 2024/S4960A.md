# Provides for calculating the area median income for affordable housing programs in a city with a population of one million or more

**Bill ID:** S4960A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4960A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4960A)

## Summary

Establishes that area median income shall be calculated either per each zip code or per region in the city of New York, whichever is lower.
