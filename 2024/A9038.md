# Relates to funding for highway construction and improvements

**Bill ID:** A9038
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David DiPietro
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9038 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9038)

## Summary

Relates to funding for highway construction and improvements in New York state from funds appropriated to the MTA; appropriates funds therefor.

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
