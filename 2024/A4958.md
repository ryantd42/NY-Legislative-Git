# Relates to tuition assistance for students completing programs of micro-credentialing

**Bill ID:** A4958
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4958 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4958)

## Summary

Provides tuition assistance program awards for students completing programs of micro-credentialing; includes micro-credential programs in reports which determine community college funding.
