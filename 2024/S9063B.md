# Authorizes the city of Niagara Falls to alienate and discontinue the use of certain parklands

**Bill ID:** S9063B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9063B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9063B)

## Summary

Authorizes the city of Niagara Falls to alienate and discontinue the use of certain parklands and transfer the property to The Papermill, LLC.
