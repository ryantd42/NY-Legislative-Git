# Provides for revocation of drivers' licenses and registrations for multiple driving while intoxicated convictions

**Bill ID:** A5182
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5182 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5182)

## Summary

Relates to revocation of drivers' licenses and registrations for multiple driving while intoxicated convictions; provides an automatic 10 year license revocation for 3rd offense.
