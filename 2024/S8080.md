# Directs the state department of social services to establish and maintain a community doula directory for doulas; repealer

**Bill ID:** S8080
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Samra Brouk
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8080 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8080)

## Summary

Directs the state department of social services to establish and maintain a community doula directory for doulas for the purposes of Medicaid reimbursement and promoting doula services to Medicaid recipients.
