# Authorizes the county of Nassau assessor to accept an application for a real property tax exemption from Rock Community Church, Inc.

**Bill ID:** A10051AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Charles Lavine
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10051AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10051AA)

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Nassau assessor to accept an application for a real property tax exemption from Rock Community Church, Inc. for all of the 2023 general taxes and all of the 2022-2023 school taxes.
