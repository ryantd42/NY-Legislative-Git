# Criminalizes the operation of a school bus by a person having any amount of alcohol in his or her blood and providing sanctions for violations

**Bill ID:** A4692
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4692 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4692)

## Summary

Criminalizes the operation of a school bus by a person having any amount of alcohol in his or her blood and provides sanctions for violations.
