# Establishes a committee to advise the Governor that the Assembly has adjourned the 2023 session and reconvened and is ready to proceed

**Bill ID:** E720
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E720 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/E720)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 720
 
BY: M. of A. Peoples-Stokes
 
  ASSEMBLY  RESOLUTION  establishing a committee of
  the Assembly to advise the Governor
 
  RESOLVED, That a committee of two be appointed  by  the  Speaker  to
advise  the  Governor  that the Assembly has adjourned its 2023 session
sine die and has again convened and is organized and ready to  proceed
to business.
