# Extends certain provisions of chapter 777 of the laws of 1986 amending the private housing finance law relating to small loans to owners of multiple dwellings to remove substandard or insanitary conditions

**Bill ID:** A10218
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Zaccaro Jr.
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10218 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10218)
