# Requires proof of acceptable vision for the renewal of a drivers' license

**Bill ID:** A6230
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6230 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6230)

## Summary

Requires proof of acceptable vision for the renewal of a driver's license; allows the submission of a doctor's certification online.
