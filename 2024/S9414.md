# Extend the effectiveness of chapter 84 of the laws of 1993 relating to establishment of initial rents in certain housing accommodations

**Bill ID:** S9414
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9414 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9414)

## Summary

Extends provisions relating to establishment of initial rents in housing accommodations rehabilitated with loans to housing development companies.
