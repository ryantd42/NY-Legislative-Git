# Relates to designating a portion of the state highway system as the 9-11 Fallen Firefighter Memorial Highway

**Bill ID:** A5546
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Doug Smith
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5546 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5546)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the Long Island Expressway as the "9-11 Fallen Firefighter Memorial Highway".
