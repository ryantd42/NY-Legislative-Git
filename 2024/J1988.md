**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1988 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1988)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1988
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  HONORING  Wayne Howard Lipton upon the occasion of
  his designation for special recognition by RVC  Blue
  Speaks on April 13, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body, in keeping with
its time-honored traditions, to  recognize  and  pay  tribute  to  those
illustrious  organizations  and individuals who devote themselves to the
welfare of the community and its citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Wayne Howard
Lipton upon the occasion of his designation for special  recognition  by
RVC  Blue Speaks on Saturday, April 13, 2024, at the 6th Annual Light it
Up Blue Gala for Autism, held at The Coral House in Baldwin,  New  York;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  The mission of RVC Blue Speaks is to promote awareness and
acceptance of those affected by Autism Spectrum Disorder so  that  their
quality  of  life  is  enhanced and their potential is fulfilled; a 2024
Gala Honoree, Wayne Howard Lipton works to change the conversation about
Autism into action and progress; and
 
  WHEREAS, The auspicious occasion of the Gala presents this  esteemed
opportunity  for  Wayne  Howard Lipton and his wife Karen Lipton to also
honor their late son, Adam who passed away in April of 2020; as a child,
he was diagnosed with autistic tendencies and benefited greatly from the
early intervention programs then available through the Rockville  Centre
Schools  and  Camp Anchor, however, tragically, he was seriously injured
in an accident at the age of 10 and survived until the age of 37; and
 
  WHEREAS, A multi-faceted man with a remarkable drive,  Wayne  Howard
Lipton  is  an  entrepreneur, businessman, and musician; he is currently
the CEO and Managing Partner of Concierge Choice Physicians LLC; and
 
  WHEREAS, Wayne  Howard  Lipton  was  raised  in  Valley  Stream  and
Baldwin;  he  graduated  from Baldwin Senior High School; furthering his
education, he graduated from Harvard College in 1973 and obtained a B.A.
in Biochemistry; and
 
  WHEREAS, Displaying his entrepreneurial ethics, Wayne Howard  Lipton
owned Richmind Way Stores for many years, a chain of drug stores; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Founding  his company in 2005, Concierge Choice Physicians
LLC  services  physicians  nationally  in  marketing  and  administering
concierge  programs,  with its main office residing in Rockville Centre,
New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, A man of faith, Wayne  Howard  Lipton  has  served  on  the
Boards of Central Synagogue and Temple B'nai Sholom; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Heavily  involved  in  the  community, Wayne Howard Lipton
served as a Trustee and Deputy Mayor of the Village of Rockville  Centre
from  1999  to  2003 with Mayor Eugene J. Murray until they both retired
from the Board in 2007; from 1989 to 1999, he was a member  of  the  RVC
 
Planning  Board  and served as its Chairman for eight of those years and
has also served as a Board Member of Mount Sinai South  Nassau  Hospital
for  ten years, was a founding Trustee of the Rockville Centre Education
Foundation  and a Gala Honoree, and recently, he has joined the Board of
Trustees at Molloy University; and
 
  WHEREAS, A patron of the arts, Wayne Howard  Lipton  serves  as  the
President  of  the  Rockville Centre Guild for the Arts and President of
the South Shore Symphony, bringing the orchestra to Rockville Centre  in
1991; a fine cellist, the orchestra has performed annually in July since
1993 at the Centennial of the Village; he has co-produced the Nutcracker
Ballet with the orchestra, Leggz Ltd Ballet, and professional guest star
dancers,  the  orchestra  also performs at the Madison Theatre at Molloy
University over seven times a year and annually  in  collaboration  with
Michael Bower and the voices of St. Agnes Cathedral each June; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Residing  in  Rockville  Centre  for  over 44 years, Wayne
Howard Lipton and his wife, Karen Lipton, have been honored  by  various
organizations,  including  the  RVC  Education  Foundation,  Molloy
University, and Mt. Sinai South Nassau Hospital; Wayne individually  has
been honored by The RVC Lions Club, The Rockville Centre Community Fund,
and UJA Federation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  him  throughout have been his wife, Karen, their two
children, Sarah and Alex, and their oldest son, Adam, who  sadly  passed
in  2020,  all  of  whom  feel  privileged  to be a part of his life and
rejoice in his achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
organizations  of such noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the  citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Wayne Howard Lipton upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation  for
special  recognition  by  RVC Blue Speaks on April 13, 2024, and to wish
him continued success in all his future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Wayne Howard Lipton.
