# Authorizes communities statewide to establish community preservation funds and authorizes the provision of grants of such funds to historic preservation societies

**Bill ID:** A10690
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10690 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10690)

## Summary

Authorizes communities statewide to establish community preservation funds; authorizes the provision of grants of such funds to historic preservation societies.
