# Relates to the sale of a mortgaged premises after a judgment of sale has been entered

**Bill ID:** S9447
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9447 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9447)

## Summary

Provides that a judgment of sale as a result of an action to foreclose a mortgage shall direct the mortgaged premises to be sold within forty-five days of the date of the judgment.
