# Requires certain health care providers to disclose certain facts in regards to probation to current and new patients

**Bill ID:** A8117
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8117 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8117)

## Summary

Requires certain health care providers to disclose certain facts in regards to probation to current and new patients.
