# Honoring Tom Neumann upon the occasion of his retirement from President and Chief Executive Officer after 30 years of distinguished service to First Source Federal Credit Union

**Bill ID:** K1044
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian D. Miller
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K1044 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K1044)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 1044
 
BY: M. of A. Miller
 
  HONORING  Tom  Neumann  upon  the  occasion of his
  retirement  from  President  and  Chief  Executive
  Officer  after  30 years of distinguished service to
  First Source Federal Credit Union
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this  Legislative  Body  to  acknowledge
those  individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Tom Neumann upon the occasion of his retirement from President and Chief
Executive  Officer  after  30  years  of  distinguished service to First
Source Federal Credit Union; and
 
  WHEREAS, As of the first  quarter  of  2025,  Tom  Neumann  will  be
officially  retiring  as  President and Chief Executive Officer of First
Source Federal Credit Union;  the  credit  union  has  branches  in  New
Hartford,  Washington  Mills,  south  Utica,  north Utica, Herkimer, and
Rome; and
 
  WHEREAS, Joining the credit union in 1994,  Tom  Neumann  throughout
the  years  of  his career became the company's Chief Operating Officer,
then Executive Vice President, and finally was appointed  President  and
Chief Executive Officer in 2017; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  the  past  30  years,  Tom Neumann rendered faithful,
conscientious, and valuable  service  to  First  Source  Federal  Credit
Union;  under his leadership, the company reaffirmed their commitment to
their  vision,  mission,  and  Member  Value,  established  financial
sustainability,  sharpened  strategies  for  growth  and innovation, and
developed an exceptional leadership for the future; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his capacity as President and Chief  Executive  Officer,
Tom Neumann served with loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  his  official acts, Tom Neumann was governed by a keen
sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and
 
  WHEREAS, During his tenure with First Source Federal  Credit  Union,
Tom  Neumann  earned  the  admiration,  esteem  and  affection  of  his
colleagues; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor Tom Neumann upon the occasion of his retirement from President and
Chief Executive Officer after 30 years of distinguished service to First
Source Federal Credit Union; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Tom Neumann.
