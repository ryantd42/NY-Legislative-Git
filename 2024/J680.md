# Congratulating the Shenendehowa Girls Varsity Swim & Dive Team and Head Coach Alyssa Di Fabio upon the occasion of achieving the distinction of the number one ranking in New York State for the 2022 season

**Bill ID:** J680
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J680 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J680)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 680
 
BY: Senator TEDISCO
 
  CONGRATULATING the Shenendehowa Girls Varsity Swim
  &  Dive Team and Head Coach Alyssa Di Fabio upon the
  occasion of achieving the distinction of the  number
  one ranking in New York State for the 2022 season
 
  WHEREAS,  Excellence  and  success  in  competitive  sports  can  be
achieved only through strenuous practice, team  play  and  team  spirit,
nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  enhances  the  moral  and  physical
development of the young people of this State, preparing  them  for  the
future  by  instilling  in  them  the  value  of teamwork, encouraging a
standard  of  healthy  living,  imparting  a  desire  for  success  and
developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Shenendehowa  Girls Varsity Swim & Dive Team attained
New York  State's  number  one  ranking  for  the  year  2022  after  an
outstanding season; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Furthermore,  the  Shenendehowa  Girls Varsity Swim & Dive
Team has been a model of consistency having garnered the State's  number
one ranking for a 2nd consecutive year, back-to-back Section 2, Division
1 titles, and a 9-0 overall record for the dual meet season; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition  to  exceptional  team  performance on diving
boards and in swimming pools, the Shenendehowa Plainsmen excelled in the
classroom garnering a collective 95.7 grade point average; and
 
  WHEREAS, The athletic talent displayed by this team is due in  great
part  to  the  efforts  of  Head  Coach  Alyssa  Di Fabio, a skilled and
inspirational tutor, respected for her ability to develop potential into
excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, The hallmarks of the Shenendehowa Girls Varsity Swim & Dive
Team, from the opening meet of the season to its recognition as the  New
York State's top program, were a sisterhood of athletic ability, of good
sportsmanship,  of  honor  and  of scholarship, demonstrating that these
student-athletes are second to none; and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach Alyssa Di Fabio, and her Assistant Coaches  have
done  a  superb  job in guiding, molding, and inspiring the team members
toward their goals; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sports competition instills the values of  teamwork,  pride
and accomplishment, and Head Coach Alyssa Di Fabio and these exceptional
athletes  have  clearly made a contribution to the spirit of excellence,
which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the  Shenendehowa  Girls  Varsity  Swim  &  Dive Team; its
members: Allison Bieniek,  Izabella  Czajkowski,  Morgan  Davey,  Sophia
Donofrio,  Calista  Fasser,  Isabella  Gayle,  Charlotte Gaylord, Mahika
Guha, Helena Gutjahr, Hannah Harpootlian, Zoe Lassetter,  Sophia  McCoy,
 
Delaney  Michels,  Amber  Mohammed,  Alana  Pacinella, Carlie Pazderski,
Varshana Ramgulam, Katrina Ryan, Samantha Sands, Katie Schloth,  Miriana
Shorkey, Genevieve Silaika, Sophie Silaika, Riley Simmons, Ishita Singh,
Ataya  Southwick,  Anna  Stoup,  Addy Tolan, Evelyn Vieira, Emma Walter,
Erica Wang, Annabelle Zhang, Stella  Zhang,  Sophie  Ziter;  Head  Coach
Alyssa  Di Fabio, Assistant Coaches Dennis Hogan, Dave Washburn and Kate
Beidl on their outstanding season and overall team  record;  and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Shenendehowa Girls Varsity  Swim  &  Dive  Team,  the
aforementioned  coaches, Athletic Director Christopher Culnan, Principal
Ron Agostinoni and Superintendent Dr. Oliver Robinson.
