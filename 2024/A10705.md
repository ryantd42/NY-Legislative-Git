# Designates the Nassau-Suffolk transportation coordinating committee as the metropolitan planning organization for Long Island

**Bill ID:** A10705
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10705 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10705)

## Summary

Designates the Nassau-Suffolk transportation coordinating committee as the metropolitan planning organization for Long Island; provides for the withdrawal of the Nassau-Suffolk coordinating committee from the New York Metropolitan transportation council.

  Section 1. Nassau-Suffolk transportation coordinating  committee.  The

 ment is not intended to indicate a proportional or  any  other  kind  of
