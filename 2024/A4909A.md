# Provides public access to epinephrine auto-injector devices at schools

**Bill ID:** A4909AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nader Sayegh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4909AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4909AA)

## Summary

Provides public access to epinephrine auto-injector devices at schools; establishes public access epinephrine auto-injector device providers.
