# Exempts certain veterans service organizations from taxes on the sales of beer, wine and soda

**Bill ID:** A4614
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4614 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4614)

## Summary

Exempts certain veterans service organizations from taxes on the sales of beer, wine and soda.
