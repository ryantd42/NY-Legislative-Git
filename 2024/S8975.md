# Exempts health care workers from congestion pricing

**Bill ID:** S8975
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8975 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8975)

## Summary

Directs the Triborough bridge and tunnel authority to implement a plan that exempts health care workers from congestion pricing.
