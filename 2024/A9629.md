# Relates to providing access to laptops for high school and higher education students and textbooks for higher education students

**Bill ID:** A9629
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9629 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9629)

## Summary

Relates to providing access to laptops for high school and higher education students and textbooks for higher education students; provides access to laptops and textbooks without charge.
