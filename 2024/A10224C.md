# Relates to uses permitted at pier 76 within the Hudson river park

**Bill ID:** A10224CA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Tony Simone
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10224CA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10224CA)

## Summary

Amends and expands some uses of the Hudson river park; permits additional uses and pier 76.
