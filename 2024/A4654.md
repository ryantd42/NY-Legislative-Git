# Creates the crime of crimes against a delivery person when a specified offense is committed against a restaurant delivery person

**Bill ID:** A4654
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4654 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4654)

## Summary

Creates the crime of crimes against a delivery person when a specified offense is committed against a restaurant delivery person; establishes for purposes of this crime a delivery person shall mean a person employed by a restaurant or put into service by a restaurant to deliver prepared food to a location outside the restaurant pursuant to an order placed by the purported recipient of the food.
