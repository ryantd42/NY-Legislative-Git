# Requires accident and health insurance policies to cover the cost of genetic testing of persons with a family history of cancer

**Bill ID:** A4811
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4811 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4811)

## Summary

Requires accident and health insurance policies to cover the cost of genetic testing of persons with a family history of cancer when the attending physician determines such person has a significant risk thereof.
