# Authorizes the county of Wyoming to impose a county recording tax on obligations secured by a mortgage on real property

**Bill ID:** A9263
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David DiPietro
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9263 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9263)

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Wyoming to impose a county recording tax on obligations secured by a mortgage on real property from December 1, 2024 to December 1, 2027.
