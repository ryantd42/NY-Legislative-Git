# Extends the effectiveness of authorizing big game hunting in Albany county

**Bill ID:** S9737
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9737 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9737)

## Summary

Extends provisions authorizing big game hunting in Albany county until October 1, 2026.
