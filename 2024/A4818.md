# Relates to increasing penalties for violations relating to test agencies and standardized testing

**Bill ID:** A4818
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4818 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4818)

## Summary

Increases penalties from $1000 to $2000 for violations relating to test agencies and standardized testing.
