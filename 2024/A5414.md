# Relates to the powers, functions and duties of the New York state public authorities control board

**Bill ID:** A5414
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5414 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5414)

## Summary

Provides that minority members of the public authorities control board shall have voting privileges and shall act upon a unanimous vote.
