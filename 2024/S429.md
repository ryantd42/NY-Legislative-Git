# Relates to notice regarding the rent increase exemption for low income elderly persons and persons with disabilities programs

**Bill ID:** S429
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Liz Krueger
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S429 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S429)

## Summary

Requires certain agencies and individuals to provide notice regarding the rent increase exemption for low income elderly persons and persons with disabilities programs to tenants upon the occurrence of certain events.
