# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Firefighter Peyton L. S. Morse Memorial Highway"

**Bill ID:** A5128A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5128A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5128A)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Firefighter Peyton L. S. Morse Memorial Highway" in the hamlet of La Fargeville, town of Orleans, Jefferson county.
