# Establishes the class A misdemeanor of unlawful dissemination of a security body scan

**Bill ID:** A6259
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alec Brook-Krasny
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6259 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6259)

## Summary

Establishes the class A misdemeanor of unlawful dissemination of a security body scan.
