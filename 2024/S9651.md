# Expands the definition of wellness programs

**Bill ID:** S9651
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Samra Brouk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9651 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9651)

## Summary

Expands the definition of wellness programs to include a program to address the impacts of technology use on the mental and physical well-being of youth.
