# Relates to membership on the cannabis control board and the cannabis advisory board

**Bill ID:** A9356
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9356 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9356)

## Summary

Increases membership on the cannabis control board and the cannabis advisory board.
