# Commending Elejmal Temple No. 185 upon the occasion of hosting its Potentate Ball, honoring Illustrious Potentate Johan Powell on July 20, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2037
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2037 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2037)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2037
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  Elejmal  Temple  No.  185  upon  the
  occasion of hosting  its  Potentate  Ball,  honoring
  Illustrious Potentate Johan Powell on July 20, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize that
the quality and character of life in the  communities  across  New  York
State  are  reflective  of  the concerned and dedicated efforts of those
organizations and individuals who would devote themselves to the welfare
of the community and its citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Elejmal  Temple  No.  185  upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  its
Potentate  Ball,  honoring  Illustrious  Potentate  Johan  Powell, to be
celebrated on Saturday, July 20, 2024,  at  the  Crown  Plaza  Hotel  in
Suffern, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  illustrious  Annual Charity Ball supports the youth,
elders, sick, and all community members in need of support and guidance;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Elejmal Temple  No.  185,  Ancient  Egyptian  Arabic  Order
Nobles  Mystic  Shrine of North and South America and Its Jurisdictions,
Inc.  works tirelessly within its community to  uplift  local  residents
through  charity fundraisers and other positive initiatives with a focus
on youth programs, health in the Black community, auxiliary  groups  and
community outreach; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Illustrious Potentate Johan Powell leads the Nobles of
Elejmal Temple with great dedication and discipline, upholding the  true
spirit  and  mission  of  this vital organization, following the noblest
mandates and putting others above himself; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under the able leadership of  Illustrious  Potentate  Johan
Powell,  the  organization's  good  deeds  in  the community serve as an
inspiration  to  all  and  continues  to  raise  the  bar  by  achieving
greatness; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  proficiency  of  the  Temple  and their good deeds in
caring for their  neighbors  shows  in  each  benevolent  action  and  a
lifetime of building true unity in the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body that when
organizations and individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Elejmal  Temple  No.  185  upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  its
Potentate  Ball, honoring Illustrious Potentate Johan Powell on July 20,
2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Elejmal  Temple No. 185 and Illustrious Potentate Johan
Powell.
