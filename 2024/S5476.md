# Imposes an excise tax on the sale of ammunition to be deposited into the gun violence impact fund; establishing the gun violence impact fund

**Bill ID:** S5476
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5476 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5476)

## Summary

Imposes an excise tax on the sale of ammunition to be deposited into the gun violence impact fund; establishes the gun violence impact fund; authorizes the director of the office of victim services to administer grants from the gun violence impact fund.

  §  92-J.  GUN VIOLENCE IMPACT FUND. 1. THERE IS HEREBY ESTABLISHED, IN

 OF THIS SECTION.
  3.  MONEYS  OF  THE  GUN  VIOLENCE IMPACT FUND SHALL BE USED SOLELY TO
 AWARD GRANTS, ACCORDING TO THE RULES AND REGULATIONS OF THE DIRECTOR  OF
 THE  OFFICE OF VICTIM SERVICES, TO OR ON BEHALF OF QUALIFYING RECIPIENTS
 AS PROVIDED IN SUBDIVISION TWENTY-FOUR OF SECTION  SIX  HUNDRED  TWENTY-
 THREE OF THE EXECUTIVE LAW.
  4.  MONIES IN THE GUN VIOLENCE IMPACT FUND SHALL BE KEPT SEPARATE FROM
 AND SHALL NOT BE COMMINGLED WITH ANY OTHER MONEYS IN THE CUSTODY OF  THE
 COMPTROLLER.
  5.  MONIES  IN  THE  GUN VIOLENCE IMPACT FUND SHALL BE PAID OUT OF THE
 FUND ON THE AUDIT AND WARRANT  OF  THE  STATE  COMPTROLLER  ON  VOUCHERS
 APPROVED BY THE DIRECTOR OF THE OFFICE OF VICTIM SERVICES.  ANY INTEREST
 RECEIVED  BY  THE  COMPTROLLER  ON MONIES ON DEPOSIT IN THE GUN VIOLENCE
 IMPACT FUND SHALL BE RETAINED IN AND BECOME PART OF SUCH FUND.
