# Requires insurer to respond within thirty days of a request from an insured for his or her health plan documents

**Bill ID:** A5957
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5957 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5957)

## Summary

Requires insurer to respond within thirty days of a written request from an insured for his or her health plan documents, including copies of most recent group or individual contracts.
