# Establishes the New York state school mental health services program coordinator

**Bill ID:** S5683
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5683 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5683)

## Summary

Establishes the school mental health services program coordinator to provide qualifying public and nonpublic schools with mental health services professionals.
