# Authorizes a tax abatement for alterations and improvements to multiple dwellings

**Bill ID:** S5790AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5790AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5790AA)

## Summary

Authorizes a tax abatement for alterations and improvements to multiple dwellings for purposes of preserving habitability in affordable housing.
