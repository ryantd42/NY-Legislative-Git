# Allows the growth, cultivation, and adult use of psilocybin for the treatment of certain health conditions

**Bill ID:** A10375
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10375 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10375)

## Summary

Allows the growth, cultivation, and regulated adult use of psilocybin for the treatment of certain health conditions; provides for the certification of support service providers and the licensure of cultivators.

 INCLUDES  PRODUCTION,  MANUFACTURE, PROCESSING, AND REQUIRED TESTING AND
 DISCLOSURES.  COMMERCIAL  TRANSACTION  OF  PSILOCYBIN-CONTAINING  FUNGI
 INCLUDE, SALE, DISTRIBUTION, AND DELIVERY.

 SATISFY THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SECTION. MAINTENANCE  OF  A  CULTIVATOR
 LICENSE  REQUIRES  THE DEMONSTRATION OF THE SATISFACTION OF ALL REQUIRE-
 MENTS OF THIS SECTION AND ANY REGULATIONS PROMULGATED BY THE  DEPARTMENT
 PURSUANT TO THIS SECTION.
  4. A LICENSED CULTIVATOR MUST CULTIVATE PSILOCYBIN-CONTAINING FUNGI IN
 A  PROPERLY ZONED AND LICENSED FACILITY APPROVED FOR CULTIVATION.  PROC-
 ESSING AND PACKAGING OF PSILOCYBIN-CONTAINING FUNGI MUST BE PERFORMED IN
 A MANNER CONSISTENT WITH THE RELEVANT SAFETY REGULATIONS.  PSILOCYBIN-
 CONTAINING FUNGI MUST BE STORED UNDER SANITARY CONDITIONS.

 USING CONTROLLED CONDITIONS THAT ADHERE TO CURRENT FOOD SAFETY  PROTOCOL
 IN ORDER TO REASONABLY REDUCE FUNGI CONTAMINANTS.

 FUNGI  WITH THE REQUIRED DISCLOSURES, INCLUDING THE FULL REGISTERED NAME
 OF  THE  LICENSED  CULTIVATOR,  SUCH  CULTIVATOR'S  OFFICE  ADDRESS,  A
 DESCRIPTION  OF CONTENTS PROVIDING THE DISCLOSURE OF THE STRAIN, AND THE
 CULTURE-SPECIFIC APPROXIMATE PSILOCYBIN EQUIVALENT POTENCY OF THE PSILO-
 CYBIN-CONTAINING FUNGI.

 CULTURE USED FOR CULTIVATION IN ORDER TO REASONABLY CONFIRM SATISFACTION
 OF  ALL RELEVANT PROTOCOL, INCLUDING STRAIN AND POTENCY, THROUGH AUTHOR-
 IZED IN-STATE TESTING FACILITIES, AS REGULATED BY  THE  DEPARTMENT.  ANY
 NEW CULTURES SHALL BE SUBJECT TO THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SECTION.
  8. A LICENSED CULTIVATOR MAY SELL DIRECTLY TO PERMIT HOLDERS WITHIN AN
 INTRASTATE  SYSTEM  ESTABLISHED  BY  THE  DEPARTMENT.  SALES BY LICENSED
 CULTIVATORS MAY  BE  ACCOMPLISHED  USING  DIRECT  TO  CONSUMER  DELIVERY
 SERVICES SOLELY TO PERMIT HOLDERS IN THE STATE. DELIVERIES MAY BE FACIL-
 ITATED  BY  CONTRACTING  WITH ADDITIONAL PERSONS OR BUSINESS ENTERPRISES
 AND REMAIN AN AUTHORIZED ACTIVITY PURSUANT TO A CULTIVATOR LICENSE.
  9.  SALES SHALL NOT NEED TO BE  THROUGH  A  DISPENSARY,  HOWEVER  THE
 DEPARTMENT  MAY PROMULGATE RULES RELATED TO A DISPENSARY CONTRACTED WITH
 A LICENSED CULTIVATOR AND MEETS OTHER APPLICABLE  REQUIREMENTS  OF  THIS
 TITLE.

 DOCUMENT  THAT GIVES INFORMATION ABOUT STRENGTH OF PSILOCYBIN-CONTAINING
 FUNGI AND DOSING AND IDENTIFIES OPTIONAL SUPPORT SERVICE PROVIDERS.
 A. 10375  9
 
  11. ANY PERSON OR BUSINESS ENTERPRISE THAT IS CONTRACTED FOR  SERVICES
 BY  A  LICENSED  CULTIVATOR, FOR OR TO ACCOMPLISH AN AUTHORIZED ACTIVITY
 UNDER SUCH CULTIVATOR LICENSE, SUCH AS DELIVERY SERVICES TO PERMIT HOLD-
 ERS OR TESTING OF POTENCY, SHALL BE CONSIDERED A LICENSEE TO THE  EXTENT
 OF  THE  PERFORMANCE  OF  THE  CONTRACTED  SERVICES  AND SHALL NOT BE IN
 VIOLATION OF THIS TITLE.

 PRIOR  TO  DISTRIBUTION  TO  PREVENT DIVERSION AND IN A MANNER THAT WILL
 MAINTAIN THE DISCLOSED POTENCY AND PREVENT CONTAMINATION.

 PRODUCTION  AND SALES. UPON REQUEST BY THE DEPARTMENT, LICENSED CULTIVA-
 TORS MUST PROVIDE THESE RECORDS AND ENGAGE IN ANNUAL INSPECTIONS.

 LICENSED FOR PERMIT HOLDERS TO PURCHASE PSILOCYBIN-CONTAINING FUNGI.
