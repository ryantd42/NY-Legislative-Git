# Extends provisions of law relating to temporary investments by local governments

**Bill ID:** S6122
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S6122 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6122)

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to temporary investments by local governments from July 1, 2023 until July 1, 2026.
