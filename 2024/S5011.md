# Relates to unlawful surveillance

**Bill ID:** S5011
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5011 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5011)

## Summary

Relates to unlawful surveillance; adds additional actions constituting unlawful surveillance in the second degree and in the first degree.
