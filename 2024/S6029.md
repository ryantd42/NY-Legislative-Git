# Authorizes the county of Rockland to impose an additional rate of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S6029
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Weber
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S6029 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6029)

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Rockland to impose an additional rate of sales and compensating use taxes until November 30, 2025.
