# Relating to prohibiting the sale or rental of illegally converted dwellings by real estate brokers

**Bill ID:** S16
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S16 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S16)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale or rental of illegally converted dwellings by real estate brokers, agents or salespersons; directs increased fines and revocation of real estate license for violation.
