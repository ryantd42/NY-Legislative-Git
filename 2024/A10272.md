# Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to adopt regulations for hunting in the northern zone

**Bill ID:** A10272
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10272 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10272)

## Summary

Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to adopt regulations for hunting in the northern zone to December 31, 2027.
