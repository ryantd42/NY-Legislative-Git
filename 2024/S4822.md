# Enacts the "economic development performance review act"; directs state agencies to conduct annual comprehensive performance review of economic development activities

**Bill ID:** S4822
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4822 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4822)

## Summary

Enacts the economic development performance review act; prescribes the functions and purposes of the special evaluation program bond act; requires state agencies involved in economic development activities to prepare an annual comprehensive performance review of their programs.
