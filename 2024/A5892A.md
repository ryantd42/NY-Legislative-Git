# Relates to pressure regulated sprinklers

**Bill ID:** A5892AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5892AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5892AA)

## Summary

Provides that spray sprinkler bodies shall meet certain water saving performance standards.
