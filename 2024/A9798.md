# Relates to establishing a central business district toll exemption for taxicabs

**Bill ID:** A9798
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9798 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9798)

## Summary

Establishes a central business district toll exemption for taxicabs.
