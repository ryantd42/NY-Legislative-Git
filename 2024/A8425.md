# Makes technical corrections to the definition of the weapon designated as a throwing star or shuriken

**Bill ID:** A8425
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alex Bores
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A8425 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8425)

## Summary

Makes technical corrections to the definition of the weapon designated as a "throwing star" or "shuriken"; updates such term from the outdated term "Kung Fu star" to reflect such term's correct etymology.
