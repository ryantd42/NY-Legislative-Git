# Permits state lottery winners to remain anonymous to the general public

**Bill ID:** A9551
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9551 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9551)

## Summary

Permits state lottery winners to remain anonymous to the general public.
