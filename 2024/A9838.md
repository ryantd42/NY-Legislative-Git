# Permits the Niskayuna central school district to create an insurance reserve fund

**Bill ID:** A9838
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9838 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9838)

## Summary

Permits the Niskayuna central school district to create an insurance reserve fund.
