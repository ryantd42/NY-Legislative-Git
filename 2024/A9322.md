# Relates to owner liability for failure of operator to stop for a school bus displaying a red visual signal and stop-arm

**Bill ID:** A9322
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9322 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9322)

## Summary

Expands provisions relating to owner liability for failure of operator to stop for a school bus displaying a red visual signal and stop-arm; extends such program until 2029.
