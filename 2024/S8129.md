# Expands felony sex offenses to include certain offenses related to prostitution and human trafficking

**Bill ID:** S8129
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8129 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8129)

## Summary

Expands felony sex offenses to include promoting prostitution in the second degree, promoting prostitution in the first degree, compelling prostitution, sex trafficking, and sex trafficking of a child.
