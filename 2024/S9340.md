# Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage monkfish

**Bill ID:** S9340
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9340 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9340)

## Summary

Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage monkfish until December 31, 2027.
