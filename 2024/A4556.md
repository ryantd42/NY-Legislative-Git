# Allows localities to exclude disability pension in determining eligibility level for partial exemption from real property tax

**Bill ID:** A4556
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4556 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4556)

## Summary

Allows localities the option to exclude income from any disability pension or benefit in computation of income for purposes of determining eligibility for the partial real property tax exemption granted to persons 65 years of age or over.
