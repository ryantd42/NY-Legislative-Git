# Authorizes cellular phone companies to provide notice of bills to third parties

**Bill ID:** A8509
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nily Rozic
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A8509 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8509)

## Summary

Authorizes cellular phone companies to provide notice of bills to third parties at the request of the customer.
