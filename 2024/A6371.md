# Relates to private residences rented out for a monetary payment

**Bill ID:** A6371
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6371 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6371)

## Summary

Relates to tax imposed on private residences rented out for a monetary payment.
