# Relates to establishing a revolving fund for the establishment of municipal internet service providers; appropriation

**Bill ID:** A10476
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10476 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10476)

## Summary

Establishes a revolving fund for the establishment of municipal internet service providers; appropriates $250 million therefor.
