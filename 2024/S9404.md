# Establishes a supplemental household and dependent care credit payment

**Bill ID:** S9404
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Rolison
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9404 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9404)

## Summary

Establishes a supplemental household and dependent care credit payment for taxpayers who are eligible for certain household and dependent care services necessary for gainful employment.
