# Repeals section 17 of chapter 7 of the laws of 2024 relating to establishing the village incorporation commission; and repeals section 6 of chapter 10 of the laws of 2024 relating to the incorporation of villages

**Bill ID:** A10112
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** MaryJane Shimsky
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10112 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10112)

## Summary

Repeals provisions relating to establishing the village incorporation commission; repeals provisions relating to the incorporation of villages.
