# Relates to aggravated harassment in the first degree

**Bill ID:** S5950
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5950 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5950)

## Summary

Relates to aggravated harassment in the first degree; includes the display of a swastika in public view in aggravated harassment in the first degree.
