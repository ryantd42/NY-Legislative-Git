# Disregards the federal child tax credit in calculating an individual taxpayer's federal adjusted gross income

**Bill ID:** A8350
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8350 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8350)

## Summary

Disregards any amount included in an individual taxpayer's federal adjusted gross income as a result of the federal child tax credit for purposes of calculating an individual taxpayer's federal income tax deduction.
