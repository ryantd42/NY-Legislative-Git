# Honoring Karen Carey upon the occasion of her retirement after 12 years of distinguished service as Market President and Chief Revenue Officer for Townsquare Media in Utica, New York

**Bill ID:** J1770
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1770 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1770)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1770
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  honoring  Karen  Carey  for nearly 50 years of
  distinguished service to the media industry and upon
  the occasion of her retirement from Townsquare Media
  in Utica, New York, after serving 12 years as Market
  President and Chief Revenue Officer
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this  Legislative  Body  to  acknowledge
those  individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Karen  Carey  upon  the  occasion  of  her  retirement after 12 years of
distinguished service as Market President and Chief Revenue Officer  for
Townsquare  Media,  to  be celebrated on Wednesday, February 7, 2024, at
The Tailor and the Cook in Utica, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Following a fulfilling 37-year career in media and 12 years
at Townsquare Media as Market President and Chief Revenue Officer, Karen
Carey has decided to retire and start  the  next  chapter  of  her  life
spending more time with her husband, daughter, family, and friends; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  highly  educated  individual,  Karen  Carey  earned  an
Associate Degree in Liberal Arts and Sciences/Liberal Studies  in  1985,
followed  by  a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communications and Journalism
from St. John Fisher College in 1987; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rising through the ranks of the media industry,  from  1998
to 2002 Karen Carey served as General Sales Manager for WVOR FM, then as
Director  of  Sales for the next year at Jacor Communications; from 2003
to 2010, she served as Vice  President  and  General  Manager  at  Clear
Channel  Radio  before  taking  a  position  as  Account  Manager to MPW
Marketing, which ultimately led to her accepting her role at  Townsquare
Media, Big Frog 104.3-WFRG; Lite 98.7-WLZW; 96.1 The Eagle-WODZ; and 950
AM WIBX, in August of 2012; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Karen  Carey  is an inspiring leader, that leaves behind a
legacy at Townsquare Media; the company grew from her  efforts  to  lead
the  local  team,  work  closely  with  community  members, clients, and
listeners to make Utica a better place; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dedicated to serving her community, Karen Carey  serves  on
the  boards  of  a  myriad  of  organizations,  such as the Bivona Child
Advocacy Center, City of Rochester Mayor's Literacy Foundation, Heart of
Gold  Children's  Foundation,  Nazareth  College  School  of  Management
Advisory Council, and New York State Broadcasters Association; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  the past 12 years, Karen Carey has rendered faithful,
conscientious and valuable service to Townsquare  Media  in  Utica,  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  her  capacity  as  Market  President and Chief Revenue
Officer, Karen Carey served with loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  In  her  official acts, Karen Carey was governed by a keen
sense of duty and always  showed  a  unique  grasp  of  human  problems,
earning  the  admiration,  esteem, and affection of her colleagues; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Karen Carey upon the occasion of her retirement after 12 years of
distinguished service as Market President and Chief Revenue Officer  for
Townsquare Media in Utica, New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Karen Carey.
