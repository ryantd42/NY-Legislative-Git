# Adjusts the base pilotage tariffs at Sandy Hook, Sands Point and Execution Rocks

**Bill ID:** S8469A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8469A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8469A)

## Summary

Adjusts the base pilotage tariffs at Sandy Hook, Sands Point and Execution Rocks for vessels over 1650 pilotage units.
