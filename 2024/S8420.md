# Authorizes certain school districts in Nassau county to enter into binding arbitration

**Bill ID:** S8420
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Ramos
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8420 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8420)

## Summary

Authorizes certain school districts in Nassau county to enter into binding arbitration.
