## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1890
 
BY: Senator WALCZYK
 
  CONGRATULATING  Patrick  "Pat"  Conners  upon  the
  occasion of his induction into the  New  York  State
  Wrestling Hall of Fame on February 24, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the sense of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
outstanding athletes who have  distinguished  themselves  through  their
exceptional performance, attaining unprecedented success and the highest
level of personal achievement; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  Patrick  "Pat"  Conners upon the occasion of his induction
into the New York State Wrestling Hall of Fame on Saturday, February 24,
2024, at MVP Arena, Albany, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, A man who has made significant contributions to  the  sport
of wrestling, Pat Conners was inducted into the New York State Wrestling
Hall of Fame during the state championship final matches; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  1984 graduate of Baldwinsville High School, Pat Conners
is a two-time Section III state champion in the 177-pound weight  class,
having won the championship in 1983 and 1984; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  1984,  Pat Conners left a momentous legacy in his last
year wrestling for Baldwinsville; he placed sixth at States and received
all state honors; and
 
  WHEREAS, Embarking on an  illustrious  career  teaching  youth,  Pat
Conners  began  as a teacher and coach at South Jefferson Central School
in 1989; he took over as wrestling head coach in 1992; and
 
  WHEREAS, Contributing to the school and community for 21 years,  Pat
Conners  retired  for  two years before ultimately deciding to not leave
his coaching career and return to the sport; and
 
  WHEREAS, With a truly remarkable record of 451-98-1  throughout  his
career,  Pat  Conners  led  the  South  Jefferson  wresting  team to the
following achievements: 20 Frontier League Titles, 10 Section III  Class
B  Championships, three Section III Interclass/Divisional Championships,
two Section III Dual Meet Championships, and in 2013 his team was ranked
No.  1 in the Division II New York State Sportswriter's Dual Meet  Poll;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Dedicated  to  his community, Pat Conners created and runs
the 1000 Islands Summer Duals in Clayton, which is one  of  the  premier
off-season tournaments in the Northeast for the sport of wrestling; and
 
  WHEREAS, Coaches such as Pat Conners provide a tremendous service to
the  future  of  our  State  and  Nation,  inspiring  generations  of
student-athletes in their athletic journeys and ensuring  their  success
in their future years; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is moved to honor Pat Conners for his
extraordinary coaching accomplishments which are a tribute to his skill,
dedication and commitment; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Patrick "Pat" Conners upon the occasion  of  his  induction
into the New York State Wrestling Hall of Fame on February 24, 2024; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Patrick "Pat" Conners.
