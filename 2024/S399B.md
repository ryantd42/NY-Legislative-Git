# Relates to the enactment of the safer consumption services pilot program act

**Bill ID:** S399BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S399BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S399BA)

## Summary

Enacts the safer consumption services pilot program act which provides for the establishment of a pilot program to provide overdose prevention center pilot programs.
