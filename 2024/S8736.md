# Extends the city of Rye's occupancy tax

**Bill ID:** S8736
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8736 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8736)

## Summary

Extends the city of Rye's occupancy tax to September 1, 2027.
