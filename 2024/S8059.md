# Relates to the disclosure of beneficial owners of limited liability companies

**Bill ID:** S8059
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8059 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8059)

## Summary

Relates to the disclosure of beneficial owners of limited liability companies and foreign limited liability companies and certain information about such beneficial owners.
