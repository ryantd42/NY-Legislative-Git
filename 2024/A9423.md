# Establishes the savings bank, savings and loan association or credit union municipal deposit program

**Bill ID:** A9423
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Al Taylor
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9423 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9423)

## Summary

Establishes the savings bank, savings and loan association or credit union municipal deposit program.
