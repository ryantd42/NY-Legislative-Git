# Relates to qualification for and establishment of a building construction, demolition and repair work insurance credit

**Bill ID:** A10179
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10179 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10179)

## Summary

Authorizes a building construction, demolition and repair work insurance tax credit for certain qualifying insurance premium payments for personal injury liability insurance and property damage liability insurance.
