# Requires testing the radon level of gas produced, sold, purchased, acquired, stored or injected and prohibits certain gas from entering the distribution system

**Bill ID:** A4950
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4950 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4950)

## Summary

Requires testing the radon level of gas produced, sold, purchased, acquired, stored or injected; prohibits gas with a radon content greater than 2 pCi/L from entering the distribution system.
