# Making a technical change to a provision requiring a petition in a summary proceeding to recover possession of real property in the city of Syracuse to allege proof of compliance with local laws

**Bill ID:** S8014
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8014 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8014)

## Summary

Makes a technical change to a provision requiring a petition in a summary proceeding to recover possession of real property in the city of Syracuse to allege proof of compliance with local laws requiring the registration or licensure of residential rental dwellings.
