**Bill ID:** A5950

**Sponsor:** Khaleel Anderson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5950 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5950)

Relates to prohibiting school buses from parking overnight on a city street.
