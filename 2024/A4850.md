# Directs the commissioner of transportation to conduct a statewide study and review of the transportation of individuals with developmental disabilities

**Bill ID:** A4850
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4850 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4850)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of transportation, in consultation with the commissioner of the office for people with developmental disabilities, to conduct a statewide study and review of the transportation of individuals with developmental disabilities.
