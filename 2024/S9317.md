# Extends provisions relating to the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage deer and bear

**Bill ID:** S9317
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9317 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9317)

## Summary

Extends provisions relating to the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage deer and bear to December 31, 2027.
