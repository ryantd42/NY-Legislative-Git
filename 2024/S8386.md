# Relates to requiring high-sugar beverages to be labeled with a safety warning

**Bill ID:** S8386
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8386 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8386)

## Summary

Requires high-sugar beverages to be labeled with a safety warning stating that the beverage contains 100% or more of the FDA recommended daily intake of added sugar.
