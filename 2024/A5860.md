# Establishes the office of nonprofit development within the empire state development corporation

**Bill ID:** A5860
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monique Chandler-Waterman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5860 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5860)

## Summary

Establishes the office of nonprofit development within the empire state development corporation to assist nonprofit organizations with business development, capacity building, technical support and education.
