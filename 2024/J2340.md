# Honoring Mt. Zion Chapter No. 59 upon the occasion of hosting its Five Point Tea Fundraiser on June 23, 2024

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2340 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2340)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2340
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING Mt. Zion Chapter No. 59 upon the occasion
  of hosting its Five Point Tea Fundraiser on June 23,
  2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Mt. Zion Chapter No. 59 upon the occasion of hosting its Five Point  Tea
Fundraiser on Sunday, June 23, 2024, at the Brooklyn Prince Hall Masonic
Temple Ballroom; and
 
  WHEREAS, Worthy Matron Kawana Howard, through her strong leadership,
steady  teaching, and unwavering love for her community, family, and her
fellow sisters, has shown that leadership can be done  with  true  grace
and elegance in all facets; and
 
  WHEREAS, The lovely women of Mt. Zion Chapter No.59 have proven that
through  cohesion, hard work, and partnership, harmony can be brought to
the community which they faithfully and unremittingly serve; and
 
  WHEREAS, Members of Mt. Zion Chapter No.59 have shown that they  not
only  have  the  desire  to  do honorable work in the community, but the
long-standing wherewithal to  continue  to  persevere  in  the  name  of
community-based excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The honorable work of Mt. Zion Chapter No. 59 in providing
for the youth,  supporting  summer  programs  and  initiatives  to  help
inspire and better position children to remain and become successful, to
aid  in the endeavor of providing safe places, events, and functions for
all the community youth, and especially providing young Black and  Brown
girls  successful  models to look up to and aspire to be, deserves to be
applauded by all citizens of this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, The  success  of  Mt.  Zion  Chapter  No.59  is  in  direct
correlation  to  the  efforts  of its members, whose involvement is, and
always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body that when
individuals and institutions of such noble aims and accomplishments  are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Mt.  Zion  Chapter  No.  59 upon the occasion of hosting its Five
Point Tea Fundraiser on June 23, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Mt. Zion Chapter No. 59, and Worthy Matron Kawana Howard.
