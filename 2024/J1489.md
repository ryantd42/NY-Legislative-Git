## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1489
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  HONORING Dr. Bruce Stewart, DDS, upon the occasion
  of  his  designation  as  recipient  of  the  2023
  Distinguished Service Award by  the  Greater  Oneida
  Chamber of Commerce on October 27, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
honor those distinguished citizens who would devote  their  life's  work
and  purpose  to  community  service, contributing to the welfare of the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Dr. Bruce Stewart, DDS, upon the occasion of his designation  as
recipient  of the 2023 Distinguished Service Award by the Greater Oneida
Chamber of Commerce of  Oneida,  New  York,  to  be  celebrated  at  the
chamber's  annual dinner at Vernon Downs in Vernon, New York, on Friday,
October 27, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Distinguished Service Award is given every  year  to  a
community member, business, or organization who has demonstrated service
and  commitment  to  improving the local community and making our area a
better, more vibrant place to live; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Bruce  Stewart  received  his  degree  in  biology  from
Binghamton  University  in  1978; in 1982, he obtained his dental degree
from University at Buffalo School of Dentistry; and
 
  WHEREAS, After a one-year residency in general practice  at  Upstate
Medical  Center  in Syracuse, New York, and a four-year associateship in
Syracuse, Dr. Bruce Stewart established his dental practice  in  Oneida,
New York, in 1987; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  a  desire  to provide a facility that could meet his
patients' needs, Dr. Bruce Stewart and his staff  utilize  technological
advancements  in  dentistry;  the benefits decrease visits to the office
and increase positive outcomes; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  those
individuals,  who have enhanced the quality of life in their communities
and have shown a long and sustained commitment  to  the  maintenance  of
high  standards  in  their  professions,  unquestionably have earned the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, It has always been the objective of this  Legislative  Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment  to  their  communities,  and  it  is  the  intent  of  this
Legislative  Body  to  inscribe,  upon  its records, this tribute to Dr.
Bruce Stewart to recognize and appreciate  his  admirable  character  as
well  as  the  respect and esteem in which he is held by his peers; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Dr.  Bruce  Stewart, DDS, upon the occasion of his designation as
recipient of the 2023 Distinguished Service Award by the Greater  Oneida
Chamber of Commerce on October 27, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Dr. Bruce Stewart, DDS.
