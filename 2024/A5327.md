# Authorizes Kim Bowers to receive certain service credit with the New York state and local employees' retirement system

**Bill ID:** A5327
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patrick Burke
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5327 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5327)

## Summary

Authorizes Kim Bowers to receive certain service credit with the New York state and local employees' retirement system for her employment with the town of Orchard Park Zoning Board of Appeals.
