# Directs the division of criminal justice services to establish a task force on missing women and girls who are black, indigenous and people of color; repealer

**Bill ID:** S8026
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8026 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8026)

## Summary

Directs the division of criminal justice services to establish a task force on missing women and girls who are black, indigenous and people of color (BIPOC).
