# Congratulating Hudson Montgomery upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Giant Slalom, Slalom, and Alpine Combined Championships on February 26 and 27, 2024

**Bill ID:** K1033
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Simpson
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K1033 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K1033)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 1033
 
BY: M. of A. Simpson
 
  CONGRATULATING Hudson Montgomery upon the occasion
  of  capturing the NYSPHSAA Giant Slalom, Slalom, and
  Alpine Combined Championships on February 26 and 27,
  2024
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to  congratulate
Hudson  Montgomery  upon  the  occasion  of capturing the NYSPHSAA Giant
Slalom, Slalom, and Alpine Combined  Championships,  held  from  Monday,
February  26  to  Tuesday,  February  27, 2024, at Whiteface Mountain in
Wilmington, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and applause  of  his  excited  fans,  Hudson
Montgomery  claimed  a  state  title for the Queensbury Boy's Alpine Ski
Team and became the third Spartan male in the school's  program  history
to  earn  first place finishes in the giant slalom, slalom, and combined
categories; and
 
  WHEREAS, Family, friends, and the community  at  large  loyally  and
enthusiastically  supported  Hudson Montgomery throughout his journey as
his  season  ended  with  impressive  finishes  in  all  the  races  he
participated in; only a freshman, he achieved a total time of 1:37.35 in
the slalom championship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In a sport such as skiing, which demands athletic prowess,
speed, and agility, Head Coach Katherine Vaughn and Assistant Coach Lynn
Potter have worked hard to hone the skills of this  championship  skier,
teaching  this  outstanding  athlete lessons which will prove invaluable
both on and off the slopes; and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach Katherine Vaughn, Assistant  Coach  Lynn  Potter
and champion athlete Hudson Montgomery have clearly utilized dedication,
determination, and resilience in providing a lasting contribution to the
spirit  of  excellence  which  is  a  tradition  of  their  school; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Hudson  Montgomery  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing the
NYSPHSAA Giant Slalom, Slalom,  and  Alpine  Combined  Championships  on
February 26 and 27, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to  Hudson  Montgomery,  Head  Coach  Katherine  Vaughn  and
Assistant Coach Lynn Potter.
