# Requires the review of the bylaws of certain resident councils to ensure compliance with federal and state law

**Bill ID:** A10449
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Maritza Davila
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10449 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10449)

## Summary

Requires a municipal housing authority in a city having a population of one million inhabitants or more to review the bylaws of resident councils to ensure compliance with federal and state law.
