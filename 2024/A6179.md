# Places a limit on the personal income tax levy by New York state

**Bill ID:** A6179

**Sponsor:** David DiPietro
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6179 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6179)

## Summary

Places a limit on the personal income tax levy by New York state.
