# Authorizes the City of White Plains to alienate certain property owned by the City of White Plains and operated as the former Galleria of White Plains public parking garage

**Bill ID:** S9291
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9291 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9291)

## Summary

Relates to the parcels that may be alienated by the City of White Plains and operated as the former Galleria of White Plains public parking garage.
