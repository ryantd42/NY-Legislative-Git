# Creates the crime of unlawful possession of a radio frequency jamming device

**Bill ID:** S5740
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5740 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5740)

## Summary

Creates the crime of unlawful possession of a radio frequency jamming device.
