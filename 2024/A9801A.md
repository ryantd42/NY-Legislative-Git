# Requires uniform criteria for evaluation and selection of grants and loans by the New York state industrial development agency

**Bill ID:** A9801A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9801A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9801A)

## Summary

Relates to loans and grants by industrial development agencies; requires uniform criteria for evaluation and selection of an eligible entity for a loan; defines terms; outlines such criteria.
