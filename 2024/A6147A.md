# Requires hands-free gas dispensing at self-service gas stations in Nassau county

**Bill ID:** A6147A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Taylor Darling
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6147A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6147A)

## Summary

Allows for hands-free gas dispensing at self-service gas stations in Nassau county by equipping hold open clips on gasoline and diesel fuel dispensing nozzles.
