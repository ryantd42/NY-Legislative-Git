# Relates to authorizing the World Restoration Center Inc. to receive retroactive real property tax exempt status

**Bill ID:** S5176
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5176 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5176)

## Summary

Authorizes the assessor of Nassau county to grant the World Restoration Center Inc. retroactive real property tax exempt status upon an application therefor.
