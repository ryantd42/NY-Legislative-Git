**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J660 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J660)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 660
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  EMT  Logan  Yates upon the occasion of
  his designation for special recognition by  the  Sea
  Cliff  Fire  Department  in  honor of his prompt and
  heroic actions on March 5, 2022
 
  WHEREAS, Citizens across our State and Nation are  inspired  by  and
indebted  to  our  noble Emergency Medical Technicians (EMT) who exhibit
courage and bravery every day in the course of their duties; and
 
  WHEREAS, EMT's exemplify the  power  of  human  compassion  and  the
strength  of  the  American  spirit  through  actions of the most heroic
magnitude; their sacrifices and their selfless dedication merit  tribute
and recognition by all citizens of this great country; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend EMT Logan
Yates upon the occasion of his designation for  special  recognition  by
the  Sea Cliff Fire Department in honor of his prompt and heroic actions
on March 5, 2022, to be celebrated at its Annual Installation Dinner  on
Friday,  April  28,  2023,  at  the Crescent Beach Club in Bayville, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, EMT Logan Yates has consistently demonstrated  a  level  of
commitment,  dedication,  integrity  and  professionalism  which  truly
exemplifies excellence in the volunteer service; and
 
  WHEREAS, On March 5, 2022, the Sea Cliff Fire Department was alerted
for a person pinned by a car in the rear of  St  Luke's  Church  in  Sea
Cliff, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Upon arrival on scene, EMT Logan Yates and his fellow crew
members located an elderly woman with her foot stuck between  the  wheel
of  her car and brake caliper; remaining calm under pressure and without
hesitation, these courageous men executed  a  plan  and  the  woman  was
successfully  extricated  and transported to North Shore Manhasset where
doctors were able to save her foot with minimal damage; and
 
  WHEREAS, On many occasions, Logan Yates has acted with  bravery  and
steady  precision  in  dealing  with  the  risk  and  drama of emergency
situations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  the  performance  of  his  duties,  Logan  Yates  has
demonstrated his character and his compassion for the welfare of others;
he  is a tremendous asset to his EMS Unit and a distinguished citizen of
the State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body cannot express  sufficient  gratitude
to those devoted individuals who recognize that the preservation of life
is a sacred responsibility and who make that responsibility their own by
serving as Emergency Medical Technicians; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend EMT Logan Yates upon the occasion of his designation for special
 
recognition by the Sea Cliff Fire Department in  honor  of  his  prompt,
heroic and lifesaving actions on March 5, 2022; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to EMT Logan Yates.
