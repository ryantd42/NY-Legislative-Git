# Extends the authorization of the county of Cattaraugus to impose an additional mortgage recording tax

**Bill ID:** S6147
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S6147 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6147)

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Cattaraugus to impose an additional mortgage recording tax until December 1, 2025.
