# Relates to establishing a mollusk shell recycling tax credit

**Bill ID:** S8436
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8436 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8436)

## Summary

Establishes a mollusk shell recycling tax credit; directs DEC to promulgate necessary regulations.
