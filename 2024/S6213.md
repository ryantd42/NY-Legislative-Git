# Requires information concerning services for human trafficking victims to be posted in lactation rooms at truck stops

**Bill ID:** S6213
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S6213 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6213)

## Summary

Requires information concerning services for human trafficking victims to be posted in lactation rooms at truck stops; defines "lactation room".
