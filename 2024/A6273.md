# Relates to gift certificates and gift cards; repealer

**Bill ID:** A6273
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6273 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6273)

## Summary

Prohibits expiration dates and dormancy service fees in certain instances; requires retailers to redeem card balances of $10 or less for cash at consumer's request.
