# Exempts antique motor vehicles from annual motor vehicle safety inspections

**Bill ID:** A4641
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4641 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4641)

## Summary

Exempts antique motor vehicles from annual motor vehicle safety inspections; modifies the definition of antique or historical motor vehicles.
