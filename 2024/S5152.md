# Relates to the application of the dignity for all students act

**Bill ID:** S5152
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5152 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5152)

## Summary

Provides that the dignity for all students act shall apply to private, religious or denominational educational institutions.
