# Repeals the requirement that applicants must pass an examination in order to qualify as a licensed master social worker

**Bill ID:** A5291
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Gonzalez-Rojas
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5291 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5291)

## Summary

Repeals the requirement that applicants must pass an examination in order to qualify as a licensed master social worker.
