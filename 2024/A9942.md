# Updates the definition of the term reusable bag

**Bill ID:** A9942
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9942 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9942)

## Summary

Updates the definition of the term "reusable bag", as used in the environmental conservation law, to specify minimum material and durability standards.
