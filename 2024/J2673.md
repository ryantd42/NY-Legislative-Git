## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2673
 
BY: Senator RHOADS
 
  COMMEMORATING the 125th Anniversary of the Wantagh
  Fire Department
 
  WHEREAS,  The  brave firefighters of the Wantagh Fire Department are
prepared to risk their lives every day to help save the lives of  others
and  to  protect homes and property from the destruction caused by fire;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to the skill and devoted  service  demonstrated
by  individual  firefighters,  the  Wantagh Fire Department reflects the
history and development of firefighting over the past 125 years; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proud  to  commemorate  the  125th
Anniversary  of  the  Wantagh  Fire Department and to honor its past and
present firefighting members for 125 years of  fire  protection  to  the
citizens of Wantagh, New York and its surrounding communities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Originally chartered as the Wantagh Hook Ladder and Engine
Company No. 1 in 1899, the Wantagh Fire Department  serves  one  of  the
largest  Fire  Districts  in  Nassau  County,  serving Wantagh, parts of
Levittown, Seaford, East Meadow, Bellmore, as well as Jones Beach  State
Park and Tobay Beach; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Having  responded  to  more  than 2700 calls for emergency
services within the last  year,  the  Wantagh  Fire  Department  readily
answers  Fire  and  EMS  alarms,  auto  accidents,  and  other  types of
miscellaneous emergency  calls;  the  Wantagh  Fire  Department  has  an
Incident  Management  Team,  Hazardous  Materials  Team and a Dive/Water
Rescue Team, which works in conjunction with a 31-foot Fire/Rescue  boat
that  provides  emergency  services  for the many waterways within their
Fire District; and
 
  WHEREAS, Its members upholding the highest level of dedication,  the
Wantagh Fire Department has approximately 250 resolute men and women who
volunteer  their  time  and  who respond to your needs 24 hours a day, 7
days a week, 365 days a year; in addition to emergency response, members
train at a minimum of once a week to  maintain  their  skills  and  also
attend  classes  at  the  Nassau  Fire Academy to keep proficient in the
services that they provide; and
 
  WHEREAS, Truly devoted to the community, the Wantagh Fire Department
tirelessly plans Fire/Rescue and EMS leadership  for  the  Bethpage  Air
Show,  the  July  4th  Fireworks  at Jones Beach, and many other special
events  that  occur  throughout  they  year;  inspiring  youth  and  the
community, volunteers also educate residents and students through public
outreach and Fire Prevention programs; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body cannot express sufficient gratitude
to those devoted individuals who recognize that the preservation of life
and property is a sacred responsibility and who make that responsibility
their own by serving as firefighters; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  that  we  should
give  special  recognition  to  those  who  work  so assiduously for the
betterment of their communities, and  acknowledge  publicly  the  heroic
good  works  performed  by  the volunteer fire departments of this great
Empire State, their officers and auxiliaries; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
proudly  commemorate  the  125th  Anniversary  of  the  Wantagh  Fire
Department; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Wantagh Fire Department.
