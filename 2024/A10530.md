# Extends the application deadlines for tax abatements for certain industrial and commercial properties in N.Y. city

**Bill ID:** A10530
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Landon C. Dais
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10530 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10530)

## Summary

Extends the application deadlines for tax abatements for certain industrial and commercial properties in a city of one million or more persons.
