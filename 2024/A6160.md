# Relates to the issuance of distinctive license plates to volunteer firefighters

**Bill ID:** A6160
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6160 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6160)

## Summary

Eliminates the registration fees charged for the issuance of volunteer firefighter distinctive license plates.
