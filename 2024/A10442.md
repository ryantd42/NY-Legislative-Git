# Exempts the South Onondaga Fire Department from the requirement that the percentage of non-resident fire department members not exceed forty-five percent of the membership

**Bill ID:** A10442
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela J. Hunter
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10442 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10442)

## Summary

Exempts the South Onondaga Fire Department within the town of Onondaga, Onondaga county, from the requirement that the percentage of non-resident fire department members not exceed forty-five percent of the membership.
