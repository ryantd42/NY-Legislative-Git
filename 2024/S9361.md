# Relates to requiring seat belts on charter buses for persons age 8 through 16

**Bill ID:** S9361
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9361 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9361)

## Summary

Requires that seat belts be available and used on charter buses for persons age 8 through 16.
