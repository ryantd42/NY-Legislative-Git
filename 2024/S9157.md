# Relates to public access to appellate briefs

**Bill ID:** S9157
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9157 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9157)

## Summary

Requires the court of appeals to provide copies of appellate briefs to any person upon request.
