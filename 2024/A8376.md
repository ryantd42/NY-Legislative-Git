# Relates to establishing a premises license for businesses retailing certain smoking products and paraphernalia

**Bill ID:** A8376
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8376 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8376)

## Summary

Requires a premises license issued by the department of taxation and finance for businesses retailing certain smoking products and paraphernalia; directs the department to issue regulations.
