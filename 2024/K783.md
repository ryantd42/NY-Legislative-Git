# Honoring Waspagne Preval upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of a Lincoln-King Visionary Award for her commitment and service to her community

**Bill ID:** K783
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K783 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K783)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 783
 
BY: M. of A. Cook
 
  HONORING  Waspagne Preval upon the occasion of her
  designation as recipient of a Lincoln-King Visionary
  Award  for  her  commitment  and  service  to  her
  community
 
  WHEREAS,  Individuals  who give of their time and energies and serve
the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration
and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS, Waspagne Preval has given not only of her time and energies
but also of her competence, intelligence and leadership and consequently
has been designated for special honor; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly  proud  to  honor  Waspagne
Preval  upon  the  occasion  of  her  designation  as  recipient  of  a
Lincoln-King Visionary Award for  her  commitment  and  service  to  her
community,  to  be celebrated at the Lincoln-King Visionary Awards Gala,
"From Dream to Greatness," on Saturday, February 10, 2024, at  the  Sand
Castle in Franklin Square, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  auspicious  community gala is held annually to unite
the international community and to celebrate unity  in  diversity  while
recognizing community leaders for their services; and
 
  WHEREAS, A resident of Queens Village, New York, Waspagne Preval has
been  called  upon to contribute her time and talents to countless civic
and charitable endeavors and has always given of  herself  unstintingly;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Waspagne  Preval  was  born  and raised in Port Au Prince,
Haiti, the third oldest of seven children; she and her family  moved  to
the United States in the 1980s, and later settled in New York City where
she was married and gave birth to five children; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As a mother, Waspagne Preval always put her children first
and taught them that they could achieve anything they  put  their  minds
to;  over  the years, despite raising five children, she opened her home
to countless others in need of a place to stay; and
 
  WHEREAS, With a  steadfast  and  unwavering  commitment  to  helping
others,  Waspagne  Preval  is  active  in several community churches and
organizations, dedicating her time and talents to making delicious  food
for those in need; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  woman  of  indomitable  faith  and dedication, Waspagne
Preval prays often and encourages others to do the same; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Waspagne  Preval  began  her  illustrious  career  at  in
Histology  at  Harlem  Hospital  on  April  18,  1988,  as  a  Medical
Technologist; today, we can congratulate her for completing 35 years and
10 months of service to The New York City Health and  Hospitals  Harlem;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  the  entire period of her community service, a
period of constructive involvement, Waspagne Preval has  stood  constant
in dignity, good grace, and humor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefit of others which Waspagne Preval has displayed
throughout her life; and
 
  WHEREAS, It has always been the objective of this  Legislative  Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment to the betterment of their communities, and it is the  intent
of  this  Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this tribute to
Waspagne Preval, that future generations may  know  and  appreciate  her
admirable  character,  her  many  benevolent  deeds, and the respect and
esteem in which she is held by her peers; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Waspagne Preval upon the occasion of her designation as recipient
of a Lincoln-King Visionary Award for her commitment and service to  her
community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Waspagne Preval.
