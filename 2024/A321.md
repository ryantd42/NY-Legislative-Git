# Relates to the order of names on ballots for special elections

**Bill ID:** A321
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A321 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A321)

## Summary

Orders the names on ballots for special elections in the same manner as ordered for general and primary elections.
