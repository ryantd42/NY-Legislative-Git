# Relates to an exemption for certain property located in the county of Tompkins from the prohibition of alcohol sales within a certain distance from a church

**Bill ID:** A10376A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10376A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10376A)

## Summary

Relates to an exemption for certain property located in the county of Tompkins from the prohibition of alcohol sales within a certain distance from a school or church.
