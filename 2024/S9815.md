# Adds Moodna Creek in Orange County to the definition of inland waterways

**Bill ID:** S9815
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S9815 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9815)

## Summary

Adds Moodna Creek in Orange County to the definition of inland waterways for waterfront revitalization.
