# Relates to temporary authorizations for practice as a registered nurse, licensed practical nurse, or physician

**Bill ID:** A10096AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10096AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10096AA)

## Summary

Relates to temporary authorizations for practice as a registered nurse, licensed practical nurse, or physician; extends the effectiveness of certain provisions relating thereto.
