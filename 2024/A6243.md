# Relates to increasing membership on the New York city transit authority advisory council

**Bill ID:** A6243
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6243 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6243)

## Summary

Increases membership on the New York city transit authority advisory council to seventeen, adding a member appointed upon the recommendation of the council of "LiveOn NY".
