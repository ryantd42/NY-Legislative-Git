## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1888
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  40th  Anniversary of Kris-Tech
  Wire of Rome, New York
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of  this  Legislative  Body  to  recognize
those  businesses  within  the State of New York which contribute to the
economic health and vitality of the communities they serve; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commemorate the 40th Anniversary of Kris-Tech Wire of  Rome,  New  York;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  In 1984, twin brothers Gerry and Glenn Brodock, both World
War II veterans turned industry leaders, founded Kris-Tech Wire  with  a
vision to revolutionize the copper wire industry; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Fast forward 40 years, and Kris-Tech Wire has evolved into
a national powerhouse, boasting a workforce of over  140  employees  and
serving  multiple  industries  from  its  headquarters on Otis Street in
Rome; its second location, Kris-Tech Gulf Coast, is located in  Houston,
Texas; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Despite  its  substantial  growth,  Kris-Tech Wire remains
deeply rooted in its values as a family-owned business; and
 
  WHEREAS, Community engagement has always been a priority,  with  the
company  actively  participating in local events and initiatives such as
Honor America Days and Rome Halloween celebrations; this  commitment  to
community building has become a hallmark of Kris-Tech's ethos; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  2023,  the company embarked on a significant expansion
project at its Griffiss Technology  Park  manufacturing  facility;  this
expansion will double Kris-Tech's footprint and pave the way for further
growth and innovation in the Rome community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  year,  in  2024,  Kris-Tech  Wire  shows no signs of
slowing down; with plans to increase job opportunities,  expand  product
offerings, and unveil new initiatives, the company is poised for another
year of growth and success; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  Kris-Tech  Wire  celebrates  its 40th anniversary, the
company remains grateful for the support of Rome and the Mohawk  Valley,
reaffirming  its  commitment  to  contributing  to  the  region's bright
future; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of Kris-Tech Wire is in direct  correlation  to
the efforts of its dedicated owners and staff, whose involvement is, and
always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the business and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  with  great  pleasure  that  this  Legislative  Body
acknowledges  this  exceptional  business  and  its contributions to the
 
local and State economies, fully confident  that  it  will  continue  to
enjoy  the  sparkling  successes  it  has  experienced  thus  far;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the 40th Anniversary of Kris-Tech Wire of  Rome,  New  York;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Gerry and Glenn Brodock, Kris-Tech Wire, Rome, New York.
