# Directs the metropolitan transportation authority to provide safety briefings to passengers aboard Metro-North trains, and train personnel in safety procedures

**Bill ID:** A9976
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9976 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9976)

## Summary

Directs the metropolitan transportation authority to provide safety briefings to passengers aboard Metro-North trains, and train personnel in safety procedures.

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  The  public  authorities  law  is amended by adding a new

  ยง 1279-J.  SAFETY  BRIEFING  OF  PASSENGERS  PLAN;  IMPLEMENTATION  ON
 METRO-NORTH  COMMUTER  RAILROAD.  THE AUTHORITY IS HEREBY AUTHORIZED AND
 DIRECTED TO IMPLEMENT A SAFETY BRIEFING OF  PASSENGERS  PLAN  NOT  LATER

 PROVISIONS FOR THE FOLLOWING:

 OR THE CONDUCTOR'S DESIGNEE, SHALL IMMEDIATELY COMMUNICATE  LIVE  AND/OR

  (A) EVACUATION PROCEDURES;
  (B) LOCATION AND MEANS FOR OPENING THE PASSENGER ENTRY DOORS AND EMER-
 GENCY EXITS;
  (C) LOCATION OF FIRST AID AND SURVIVAL EQUIPMENT;
  (D) LOCATION AND OPERATION OF FIRE EXTINGUISHERS.
  IN THE EVENT OF A DISASTER OR EMERGENCY, THE CONDUCTOR, OR THE CONDUC-
 TOR'S DESIGNEE SHALL COMMUNICATE THE SAFETY BRIEFING MESSAGE AS OFTEN AS
 NECESSARY TO ENSURE PASSENGER SAFETY.
  2.  THE  BRIEFINGS  REQUIRED BY SUBDIVISION ONE OF THIS SECTION MAY BE
 DELIVERED BY MEANS OF AN APPROVED  RECORDING  PLAYBACK  DEVICE  THAT  IS
 AUDIBLE TO EACH PASSENGER UNDER NORMAL NOISE LEVELS.
  3. THE TRAINING OF CONDUCTORS, TRAINMEN AND OTHER DESIGNATED EMPLOYEES
 IN  EMERGENCY  TECHNIQUES  AND PROCEDURES, INTEROPERABLE COMMUNICATIONS,
 HANDLING AND POSITIONING OF STRICKEN COMMUTERS, AND KNOWLEDGE OF  PROCE-
 DURES AND EQUIPMENT USED DURING EVACUATION PROCEDURES.
