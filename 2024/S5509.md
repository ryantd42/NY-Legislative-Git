# Establishes a central business district toll exemption for certain police officers

**Bill ID:** S5509
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5509 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5509)

## Summary

Establishes a central business district toll exemption for police officers employed in a police force of a city with a population of one million or more.
