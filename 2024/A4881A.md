# Extends the authorization granted to the county of Warren to impose an additional mortgage recording tax

**Bill ID:** A4881A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Simpson
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A4881A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4881A)

## Summary

Extends the authorization granted to the county of Warren to impose an additional mortgage recording tax until December 1, 2025.
