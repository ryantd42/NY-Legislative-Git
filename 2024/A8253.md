# Requires the surrender of registration and license plates for a suspended or revoked license

**Bill ID:** A8253
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8253 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8253)

## Summary

Requires the surrender of registration and license plates for a suspended or revoked license.
