# Sets requirements for the sale, lease, and rental of powered bicycles, powered mobility devices, and storage batteries for such devices

**Bill ID:** A5677AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Yudelka Tapia
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5677AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5677AA)

## Summary

Sets requirements for the sale, lease, and rental of powered bicycles, powered mobility devices, and storage batteries for such devices; provides for penalties for the violation thereof.
