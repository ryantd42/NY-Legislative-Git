# Congratulating Brayden Fahrbach upon the occasion of winning a NYSPHSAA Division II State Wrestling Championship Title

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1953 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1953)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1953
 
BY: Senator PALUMBO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Brayden Fahrbach upon the occasion
  of winning a NYSPHSAA Division  II  State  Wrestling
  Championship Title
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud to congratulate
Brayden Fahrbach upon the occasion of winning  a  NYSPHSAA  Division  II
State  Wrestling  Championship  Title on Saturday, February 24, 2024, at
the MVP Arena in Albany, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, In  a  competitive  matchup,  Mount  Sinai  senior  Brayden
Fahrbach won the NYSPHSAA Division II State Wrestling Championship Title
in  a  6-5  decision  over  Gianni Silvestro of Tioga High School at 138
pounds, stopping numerous takedown attempts  in  the  final  seconds  to
secure his second consecutive state title; and
 
  WHEREAS, For his outstanding achievements in the sport of wrestling,
including four straight Suffolk Division II titles, Brayden Fahrbach was
named the state tournament's Most Outstanding Wrestler; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's  colors of red, black, and
white,  family,  friends,  and  the  community  at  large  loyally  and
enthusiastically  supported  the  Mustang  throughout  his journey as he
ended his season with an impressive 39-0  season  record  and  a  198-13
career record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  wrestling  which  demands athletic
prowess,  endurance,  strength,  and  mental  focus,  Head  Coach  Matt
Armstrong worked hard to hone the skills of this State Champion, Brayden
Fahrbach,  teaching  this  outstanding  athlete lessons which will prove
invaluable both on and off the mat; and
 
  WHEREAS, Brayden Fahrbach of Mount Sinai  High  School  has  clearly
utilized  dedication, determination, and teamwork in providing a lasting
contribution to the spirit of excellence which is  a  tradition  of  his
school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Brayden Fahrbach upon the occasion of  winning  a  NYSPHSAA
Division II State Wrestling Championship Title; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Brayden Fahrbach and Head Coach Matt Armstrong.
