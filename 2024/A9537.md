# Requires that all new motor vehicles sold at new motor vehicle dealerships include a spare tire

**Bill ID:** A9537
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Eric Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9537 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9537)

## Summary

Requires that all new motor vehicles sold at new motor vehicle dealerships include a spare tire, whether or not equipped with standard radial or run-flat tires.
