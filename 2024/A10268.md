# Establishes a mental health first aid training program for police officers and peace officers

**Bill ID:** A10268
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10268 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10268)

## Summary

Provides for the establishment and implementation on an ongoing basis of a mental health first aid training program for all current and new police officers and peace officers.
