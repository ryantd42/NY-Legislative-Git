## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2091
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  The  Reverend James Cesta upon the
  occasion of celebrating the 50th Anniversary of  his
  Ordination to the Priesthood
 
  WHEREAS,  The character and richness of religious and community life
in this noble Empire  State  is  nourished  and  fulfilled  through  the
faithful  concern  and selfless devotion of those who would commit their
energies and purpose to the ministry of others; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative  Body  to  bring  full
recognition  and  just  tribute  to  those  individuals  who distinguish
themselves and the ministry through  their  unremitting  commitment  and
preeminence of service; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate The
Reverend  James  Cesta  upon  the  occasion  of  celebrating  the  50th
Anniversary  of  his  Ordination  to the Priesthood, to be celebrated on
Sunday, May 19, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, James Cesta was born to Carmen  A.  and  Mary  Ann  (Tucci)
Cesta  in  Syracuse, New York, on December 18, 1948; he attended Webster
Elementary School and North High School in Syracuse, graduating in  June
of 1966; and
 
  WHEREAS,  James  Cesta  attended  the  seminary  at  Wadhams Hall in
Ogdensburg, New York, and St. Bernard's School of Theology and  Ministry
in  Rochester; he was officially ordained into the Priesthood on May 18,
1974; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout the years, Reverend James Cesta had appointments
to serve the parishioners as an Associate at numerous  churches  in  New
York  State:  St. John the Baptist Church in Rome; Holy Family Church in
Fairmount; St. Rose of Lima  Church  in  North  Syracuse;  St.  Agatha's
Church  in Canastota; Blessed Sacrament Church in Syracuse; and Our Lady
of Good Counsel Church in Endicott; he served as Pastor at St.  Joseph's
Church  in  Oswego; he was also given a special assignment at Knights of
Columbus in Syracuse; and
 
  WHEREAS, After taking a short sabbatical, Reverend James  Cesta  has
continued  his  ministry  at St. Mary of Mt. Carmel/Blessed Sacrament in
Utica, serving as Temporary Administrator and Pastor;  additionally,  he
has served as Pastor at St. Anthony & St. Agnes in Utica; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Reverend  James  Cesta  has  brought  to  his  ministry an
overwhelming spirit of charity and compassion, acting  from  vision  and
enlightenment  while conducting his administrative and spiritual duties,
always displaying the highest standards  of  personal  and  professional
integrity and dedication; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Reverend  James Cesta has always given guidance and wisdom
to the many entrusted to his care  and  has  ministered  abundantly  and
unstintingly to the spiritual and corporal needs of all; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that when a person
of  such  noble aims and accomplishments is brought to our attention, it
is appropriate to publicly and  jubilantly  proclaim  and  commend  that
individual for the edification of others; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate The Reverend James Cesta upon the occasion  of  celebrating
the  50th  Anniversary  of  his Ordination to the Priesthood, noting his
unremitting and compassionate faith and his exemplary  service  to  God,
man,  and  his community, and extending to him the heartfelt best wishes
of this  Legislative  Body  for  a  future  of  purposeful  success  and
well-being; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to The Reverend James Cesta.
