# Relates to directing the division of homeland security and emergency services to conduct review and analysis of security measures at rail yards and issue related reports and recommendations

**Bill ID:** A9480
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9480 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9480)

## Summary

Directs the division of homeland security and emergency services to conduct a review and analysis of security measures at rail yards and to issue related reports and recommendations.
