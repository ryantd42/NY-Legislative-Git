# Relates to special districts for ambulance services

**Bill ID:** A5351
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Simpson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5351 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5351)

## Summary

Relates to the establishment of special districts for general ambulance services in any county, city, town or village located within the Adirondack park.
