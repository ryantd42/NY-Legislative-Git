# Increases the tax exemption amount on clothing and apparel items from one hundred ten dollars to two hundred dollars

**Bill ID:** S5418
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Simcha Felder
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5418 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5418)

## Summary

Increases the tax exemption amount on clothing and apparel items from one hundred ten dollars to two hundred dollars.
