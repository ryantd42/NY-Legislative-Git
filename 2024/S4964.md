# Provides for student loan assistance for certain attorneys employed by political subdivisions of the state

**Bill ID:** S4964
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4964 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4964)

## Summary

Grants student loan forgiveness for certain municipal attorneys employed by political subdivisions of the state.
