# Extends the authorization of the county of Delaware to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Delaware to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes.
