# Relates to the certification of innovative statewide substance use disorder treatment services

**Bill ID:** S8717
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8717 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8717)

## Summary

Provides for the certification of entities seeking to provide in-home based substance use disorder treatment services.
