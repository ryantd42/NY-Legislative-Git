# Relates to authorizing the New York city transit authority to provide a minimum twenty-five percent discount on mass transit for college students

**Bill ID:** A5233
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5233 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5233)

## Summary

Authorizes the New York city transit authority to contract with mass transit entities including the Long Island Rail Road and the Metro-North Commuter Railroad Company to provide a minimum twenty-five percent discount for college students.
