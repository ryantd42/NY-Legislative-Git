# Provides for the extension of NYSLRS service credit for any service credit lost due to either furlough or layoff of a member from when executive order 202 was issued through December 31, 2020

**Bill ID:** A5725
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Josh Jensen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5725 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5725)

## Summary

Provides for the extension of NYSLRS service credit for any service credit lost due to either furlough or layoff of a member from the period beginning when executive order 202 of 2020 was issued through December 31, 2020.
