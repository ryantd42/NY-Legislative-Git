# Awards high school diplomas to veterans; repealer

**Bill ID:** A5058
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5058 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5058)

## Summary

Allows any veteran who has served this nation to be awarded a high school degree based on their knowledge and experience gained while in service.
