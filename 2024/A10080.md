# Relates to net revenue distributed by regional off-track betting corporations

**Bill ID:** A10080
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10080 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10080)

## Summary

Directs that any net revenue distributed to participating counties or cities by regional off-track betting corporations shall be used exclusively for real property tax relief.
