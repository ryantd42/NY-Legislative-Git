# Congratulating Hunter Gonzalez-Cadillo upon the occasion of being named the 2024 Youth of the Year by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2453 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2453)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2453
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  CONGRATULATING  Hunter  Gonzalez-Cadillo  upon the
  occasion of being named the 2024 Youth of  the  Year
  by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor the youth
of today, and leaders of tomorrow, whose character and achievements best
exemplify  the  ideals  and  values  cherished  by  this great State and
Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate Hunter Gonzalez-Cadillo upon the occasion  of  being  named
the  2024 Youth of the Year by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club, to
be celebrated on Tuesday, May 21, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Each year since 1947, Boys & Girls  Clubs  of  America  has
selected  members  for  this  honor who embody the values of leadership,
community  service,  academic  excellence  and  good  character;  Hunter
Gonzalez-Cadillo truly exemplifies those qualities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Hunter  Gonzalez-Cadillo  has  greatly  benefited from the
Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club which has enabled  him  to  reach  his
full potential as a productive, caring, responsible citizen; and
 
  WHEREAS, As a proud member of the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club,
Hunter  Gonzalez-Cadillo  and  his  peers have been involved in numerous
local projects which have made his community a  better  place  to  live,
grow and work; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  young  man  of  singular  distinction,  Hunter
Gonzalez-Cadillo has distinguished himself as a young man of superlative
ability, establishing a high standard of excellence  and  purposefulness
of life rarely achieved in youthful years; and
 
  WHEREAS, Poised eagerly with enthusiasm and determined purpose, this
fine  young  gentleman  now  stands  honorably,  with  just pride in his
accomplishments  and  in  eager  anticipation  of  future  challenges,
understanding  and  appreciating the caring counsel and support provided
by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club and his family; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Hunter  Gonzalez-Cadillo  upon the occasion of being named
the 2024 Youth of the Year by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Hunter Gonzalez-Cadillo.
