# Relates to prohibiting establishments from refusing to accept cash as payment

**Bill ID:** S326
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S326 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S326)

## Summary

Prohibits food-service and retail establishments from refusing to accept cash as payment for goods or services; calls for a civil fine if an establishment should refuse cash as payment.
