# Allows an operator of a motor vehicle to display or provide such operator's certificate of registration in electronic form

**Bill ID:** A4630
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4630 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4630)

## Summary

Allows an operator of a motor vehicle to display or provide such operator's certificate of registration to a magistrate, motor vehicle inspector, peace officer, acting pursuant to his or her special duties, or police officer in electronic form.
