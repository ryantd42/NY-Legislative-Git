# Requires certain entities with sports facilities that receive money from the state or local government to only contract with companies that pay their employees the minimum wage

**Bill ID:** S5901
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jabari Brisport
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5901 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5901)

## Summary

Requires certain entities with sports facilities that receive money from the state or a local government within the state to only contract with companies that pay their employees the minimum wage; defines terms.
