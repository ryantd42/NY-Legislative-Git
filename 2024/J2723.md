## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2723
 
BY: Senator WALCZYK
 
  HONORING  Dallen  and  Madalynn  Farney  upon  the
  occasion of their designation as recipients  of  the
  Lewis  County Young Farmer Award by the Lewis County
  Dairy Industry Building Committee
 
  WHEREAS, The agricultural industry is part of  the  foundation  upon
which our distinct American heritage is built; and
 
  WHEREAS, New York State and the entire United States is dependent on
our agricultural industry for a variety of food products; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the custom of this Legislative Body to commend the
efforts of our farmers as they continue their hard work  of  cultivating
the  land,  facing  the many challenges of nature such as flood, drought
and heat, in order to grow our food; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Dallen  and  Madalynn  Farney  upon the occasion of their designation as
recipients of the Lewis County Young Farmer Award by  the  Lewis  County
Dairy Industry Building Committee; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Dallen  and  Madalynn  Farney, a fourth generation farming
family, are being recognized for their excellence in farm,  dairy  herd,
crop, and business/financial management; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Farney  farm  was originally purchased by Webster and
Elbeena  Farney  in  1938;  Herbert  and  Berneda  Farney,  the
great-grandparents  of  Dallen,  continued farming the land beginning in
1950; in the early 1980s, brothers Stephen and Dale Farney took over the
farm, with Dallen Farney joining in 2012; and
 
  WHEREAS, Today, the Farneys manage 350 Holstein cows, 300  acres  of
corn, and 400 acres of hay; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  a  steadfast  and  unwavering  commitment  to  their
community, Dallen Farney is  involved  in  Farm  Bureau,  the  Board  of
Directors  of  Lowville  Producers,  and  was previously on the Board of
Directors for Cornell Cooperative Extension of Lewis County; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dedicated to maintaining high standards of  quality,  these
esteemed  farmers  have  worked  tirelessly  to  overcome  obstacles and
challenges, while constantly learning  new  techniques  and  methods  to
achieve the best results in their field; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Through  their  diligence and expertise in farming, Dallen
and Madalynn Farney have brought pride and recognition  to  their  farm,
local community, and to our great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
exceptional farmers of such noble aims and accomplishments  are  brought
to  our  attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the
citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Dallen and Madalynn Farney upon the occasion of their  designation
as recipients of the Lewis County Young Farmer Award by the Lewis County
Dairy Industry Building Committee; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Dallen and Madalynn Farney.
