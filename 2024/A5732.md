# Relates to use of revenues from tolls collected on the Verrazzano-Narrows bridge

**Bill ID:** A5732
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Charles Fall
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5732 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5732)

## Summary

Directs revenues from tolls collected on the Verrazzano-Narrows bridge to be applied to expansion of bus rapid transit and high-occupancy vehicle lanes in the borough of Staten Island.
