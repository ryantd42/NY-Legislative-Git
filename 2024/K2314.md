# Congratulating Christopher Cardello, owner and founder of Subaru of Utica, upon the occasion of his retirement after 35 years of distinguished service

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2314 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2314)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2314
 
BY: M. of A. Buttenschon
 
  CONGRATULATING  Christopher  Cardello,  owner  and
  founder of Subaru of Utica, upon the occasion of his
  retirement after 35 years of distinguished service
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge the
significant  milestones  in  the  distinguished  careers  of  esteemed
residents of this noble Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate Christopher Cardello, owner and founder of Subaru of Utica,
upon  the  occasion  of  his  retirement after 35 years of distinguished
service; he will officially retire on Friday, May 31, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, At just  19  years  old,  Christopher  Cardello  began  his
illustrious  career in the car dealership business with 40 dollars and a
dream;  since  its  inception,  Subaru  of  Utica  has  grown  into  a
full-service  dealership  serving  its  customers  throughout  Utica,
Yorkville, Whitesboro and beyond, with a personalized touch; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under the able leadership of Christopher  Cardello,  Subaru
of  Utica  has  demonstrated  an  outstanding  commitment  to supporting
passions and causes throughout  the  local  community,  including  Utica
Little  League,  the  Stone  Francis Mercurio Skatepark Project, and the
Central New York Children's Dyslexia Center; and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore, Subaru of Utica was nationally  recognized  by
Subaru  of  America,  Inc.  with  its  2024  Love  Promise  Customer and
Community Commitment Award for  its  dedication  to  making  a  positive
impact  on  the  lives  of  its  customers and neighbors through various
partnerships  with  local  charities  and  non-profit  organizations,
including the Stevens-Swan Humane Society, Rome Humane Society, Herkimer
Humane Society, Utica Boiler Maker, Heart Run and Walk, Positively Rome,
Snow  Show  Event  Rome,  Rome  Bike Trail, Paddlefest, Ride for Missing
Children, Rome Clean and  Green,  as  well  as  several  initiatives  to
support local schools and healthcare centers; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  the  past  35  years,  Christopher  Cardello rendered
faithful, conscientious,  and  valuable  service  to  Subaru  of  Utica,
earning the admiration, esteem, and affection of his colleagues; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  Subaru  of Utica, New York, is in direct
correlation to the efforts of Christopher Cardello whose involvement is,
and always has been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment,  an
unbridled  enthusiasm,  and  an uncompromising standard of excellence in
all endeavors on behalf of the business and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed is  the  occurrence  of  such  a  compassionate
blending  of  strength,  leadership,  intellect,  and commitment as that
demonstrated by Christopher Cardello over a lifetime  of  sacrifice  and
dedication to others; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of the great State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Christopher Cardello, owner and founder of Subaru of Utica,
upon the occasion of his retirement  after  35  years  of  distinguished
service,  and  to  wish  him well in all his future endeavors; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Christopher Cardello.
