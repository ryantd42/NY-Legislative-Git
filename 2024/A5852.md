# Establishes the offenses of political coercion in the second degree, political coercion in the first degree and accomplice to political coercion

**Bill ID:** A5852
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monique Chandler-Waterman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5852 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5852)

## Summary

Establishes the offenses of political coercion in the second degree, political coercion in the first degree and accomplice to political coercion relating to the transportation of a person into the state for political or publicity reasons or personal gain.
