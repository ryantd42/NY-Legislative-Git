# Requires local boards of elections to verify the number of enrolled members of a party in an election district

**Bill ID:** A4682
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4682 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4682)

## Summary

Requires local boards of elections to verify the number of enrolled members of a party in an election district and reject all petitions where there are no enrolled members of a party in such district.
