# Relates to the dissemination of information pertaining to Alzheimer's disease and other forms of dementia

**Bill ID:** A5306A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5306A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5306A)

## Summary

Relates to the dissemination of information pertaining to Alzheimer's disease and other forms of dementia through the NY Connects: Choices for Long Term Care program.
