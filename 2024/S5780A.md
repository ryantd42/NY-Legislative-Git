# Relates to establishing an additional mortgage recording tax in Chenango county

**Bill ID:** S5780A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5780A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5780A)

## Summary

Relates to establishing an additional mortgage recording tax in Chenango county; provides for the repeal of such provisions.
