# Provides alternative pricing methods in the sale and offering of funeral merchandise and services

**Bill ID:** A6379
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6379 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6379)

## Summary

Provides alternative pricing methods in the sale and offering of funeral merchandise and services; mandates a report by the consumer protection division on the effectiveness of alternative pricing methods.
