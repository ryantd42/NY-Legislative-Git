# Prohibits the taking of horseshoe crabs for commercial and biomedical purposes

**Bill ID:** A10140
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [A10140 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10140)

## Summary

Prohibits the taking of horseshoe crabs for commercial and biomedical purposes.
