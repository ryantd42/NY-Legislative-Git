# Congratulating Angelina McKane upon the occasion of capturing both the Girls 50-yard Freestyle and the Girls 100-yard Butterfly titles at the NYSPHSAA Girls Swimming and Diving Championship on November 18, 2023

**Bill ID:** K2164
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2164 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2164)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2164
 
BY: M. of A. Palmesano
 
  CONGRATULATING  Angelina  McKane upon the occasion
  of capturing both the Girls  50-yard  Freestyle  and
  the  Girls 100-yard Butterfly titles at the NYSPHSAA
  Girls Swimming and Diving Championship  on  November
  18, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and  team  championships are highly favored in
high  school  sports;  this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud to congratulate
Angelina McKane upon the occasion of capturing both  the  Girls  50-yard
Freestyle  and the Girls 100-yard Butterfly titles at the NYSPHSAA Girls
Swimming and Diving Championship on Saturday, November 18, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Angelina  McKane,  of  Corning,  New  York,  a  senior  at
Corning-Painted  Post  High School, is a true team player; voted as Team
Captain in both her junior and  senior  years,  she  is  driven  to  see
success for her team; and
 
  WHEREAS,  At  the  NYSPHSAA  Championship  in  2023, five-time State
Champion  Angelina  McKane  won  both  the  Scholar  Athlete  Award  and
Outstanding  Competitor Award, presented by the New York State Certified
Swimming Officials Association; she is committed to swimming in Division
I at the United States Military Academy at West Point; and
 
  WHEREAS, Angelina McKane has proven her athletic prowess in previous
years; she holds records for the 100-yard Butterfly, 50-yard  Freestyle,
and  100-yard  Backstroke  in  Section IV; for Corning-Painted Post High
School, she attained 10 team records and  six  pool  records;  in  seven
pools across Section IV, she possesses records for her superior swimming
ability; and
 
  WHEREAS,  An  exceptional  swimmer,  Angelina  McKane  was  the 2023
NYSPHSAA and Federation Champion  in  the  100-yard  Butterfly  and  the
50-yard Freestyle with automatic All-American times in both; she led the
400-yard  Freestyle  Relay  with  an All-American consideration time; in
2022, she was the  NYSPHSAA  and  Federation  Champion  in  the  50-yard
Freestyle,  with  an  All-American  consideration  time  of  23.39,  and
NYSPHSAA  Champion  in  the  100-yard  Butterfly  with  an  automatic
All-American time of 54.05; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Additionally,  Angelina  McKane  was  the  2021  NYSPHSAA
Champion in the  100-yard  Butterfly,  with  a  time  of  54.63  for  an
All-American  consideration  time,  and  claimed fifth place in New York
State in the 50-yard Freestyle; in 2020, she was ranked as first in  the
 
100-yard  Butterfly  for the state's public schools; she held 16th place
in the 100-yard Butterfly in 2019; and
 
  WHEREAS, Adding to the impressive tally of accomplishments, Angelina
McKane  claims  the  following  titles:  Outstanding Performer, voted by
coaches, for the Section IV Class A Swimming &  Diving  Championship  in
2021,  2022,  and  2023;  Section  IV  Class  A  Champion in the 50-yard
Freestyle, 100-yard Butterfly, and 400-yard Freestyle Relay in 2021  and
2022;  Section  IV  Champion  in  200-yard  Individual  Medley, 100-yard
Butterfly, and 400-yard Freestyle Relay  in  2023;  and  the  2021  (F2)
Section  IV  Class  A  Champion  in  the  50-yard Freestyle and 100-yard
Butterfly competitions; and
 
  WHEREAS, Angelina McKane  is  a  four-time  Southern  Tier  Athletic
Conference  All  Star;  she  is  a dedicated swimmer and participates in
United States swimming meets; she competed at the Summer  National  meet
in California in the summer of 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Not only an outstanding athlete, Angelina McKane is also an
exceptional  academic,  maintaining  her  status  as  a  High Honor Roll
student from 2020-2024 and participating as a  Member  of  the  National
Honor Society; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Additionally, Angelina McKane's extracurricular activities
merit recognition; she is a Member of the  Student  Council,  the  Youth
Activation  Committee,  and  the Future Business Leaders of America; she
was a Mentor in her freshman year and a volunteer at Wineglass  Marathon
and Unified Bowling matches; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Sports competition instills the values of teamwork, pride,
and accomplishment, and Angelina McKane has clearly contributed  to  the
spirit of excellence which is a tradition of her school; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Angelina McKane upon the occasion  of  capturing  both  the
Girls  50-yard  Freestyle and the Girls 100-yard Butterfly titles at the
NYSPHSAA Girls Swimming and Diving Championship on  November  18,  2023;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Angelina McKane, Head Coach  Alyssa  Helsing,  and  Coach
Anthony Furstoss.
