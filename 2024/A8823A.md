# Relates to the creation of the "No Hate NY" license plate and the "No Hate NY fund"

**Bill ID:** A8823AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A8823AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8823AA)

## Summary

Relates to the creation of the "No Hate NY" license plate and the "No Hate NY fund" to support hate crime awareness and prevention programs.
