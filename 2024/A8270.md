# Requires on-bill financing be an option for certain energy efficiency services and requires the disclosure of on-bill financing prior to the sale of real property

**Bill ID:** A8270
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patrick Burke
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8270 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8270)

## Summary

Requires on-bill financing be an option for certain energy efficiency services; requires the disclosure of on-bill financing prior to the sale of real property.

  §  4.  Subdivision 4 of section 242 of the real property law, as added
 by chapter 388 of the laws of 2011, is amended to read as follows:
  4. Disclosure prior to the sale of real property  to  which  [a  green
 jobs-green  New  York]  on-bill [recovery charge] FINANCING applies. (a)
 Any person, firm, company, partnership or corporation offering  to  sell
 real  property  which  is subject to a green jobs-green New York on-bill
 recovery charge pursuant to title nine-A of article eight of the  public
 authorities  law  OR  ANY  OTHER ON-BILL FINANCING shall provide written
 notice to the  prospective  purchaser  or  the  prospective  purchaser's
 agent,  stating  as  follows: "This property is subject to a green jobs-
 green New York on-bill  recovery  charge  OR  ANOTHER  FORM  OF  ON-BILL
 FINANCING".  Such  notice  shall  also  state  the  total  amount of the
 original charge, the payment  schedule  and  the  approximate  remaining
 balance,  a  description  of  the  energy efficiency services performed,
 including improvements to the property,  [and]  an  explanation  of  the
 benefit  of  the [green jobs-green New York qualified] energy efficiency
 services, AND WHETHER SUCH ON-BILL FINANCING  WILL  CONTINUE  AFTER  THE
 SALE  IS  COMPLETE  OR IF THE SELLER WILL PAY OFF ANY REMAINING BALANCE.
 Such notice shall be  provided  by  the  seller  prior  to  accepting  a
 purchase offer.
  (b) Any prospective or actual purchaser who has suffered a loss due to
 a  violation  of  this  subdivision  is  entitled  to recover any actual
 damages incurred from the person offering to sell or selling  said  real
 property.
