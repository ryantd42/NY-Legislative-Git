# Directs the authorities budget office to conduct a study on the financial effects of making local governments responsible for the upkeep in certain public areas

**Bill ID:** S8935
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8935 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8935)

## Summary

Directs the authorities budget office to conduct a study on the financial effects of making local governments responsible for the upkeep in certain public areas immediately outside of properties owned or leased by public authorities.
