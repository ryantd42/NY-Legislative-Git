# Relates to creating a tax credit for the adoption of household pets from animal shelter or humane society

**Bill ID:** A221
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A221 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A221)

## Summary

Creates a tax credit for the adoption of household pets from animal shelters or humane societies; up to $100 per animal with a maximum of three household pets.
