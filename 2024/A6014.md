# Creates incentives for counties to investigate and prosecute medicaid fraud

**Bill ID:** A6014
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6014 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6014)

## Summary

Creates incentives for counties to investigate and prosecute medicaid fraud.
