# Requires a personal use pharmaceutical disposal system be provided at the time of dispensing a controlled substance prescription

**Bill ID:** S5738
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5738 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5738)

## Summary

Requires a personal use pharmaceutical disposal system be provided to the ultimate user at the time of dispensing a prescribed controlled substance; requires county health departments to provide such disposal systems at no cost to pharmacies.
