**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1970 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1970)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1970
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Firefighter John D. Alexander upon the
  occasion of his designation for special  recognition
  after  50  years  of dedicated service to Protection
  Fire Engine Co. No. 1
 
  WHEREAS, The firefighters of New York State are renowned  throughout
the  Nation;  their  reputation  is  synonymous  with  excellence in the
arduous field of fire protection and prevention; and
 
  WHEREAS, Firefighters exemplify the power of  human  compassion  and
the  strength  of the American spirit through actions of the most heroic
magnitude; their sacrifices and their selfless dedication merit  tribute
and recognition by all citizens of this great country; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This Legislative Body is proud to commend Firefighter John
D.  Alexander  upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation  for  special
recognition  after  50  years  of  dedicated  service to Protection Fire
Engine Co. No. 1,  to  be  celebrated  at  the  Company's  132nd  Annual
Installation Dinner on Saturday, April 6, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Joining the Protection Fire Engine Co. No. 1 in December of
1974, John D. Alexander currently serves as company sergeant at arms and
has performed his responsibilities diligently for the past five decades;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valiantly  serving  our  Nation,  John  D.  Alexander  was
honorably discharged from the United States Army after  being  stationed
in the 101st airborne division in Vietnam; and
 
  WHEREAS, With him throughout have been his son, Donald J. Alexander,
ex-Captain  and  trustee,  his  daughter-in-law Christina, an ex-Captain
with Fire  medics  in  PWFD,  his  three  grandchildren,  John,  Daniel,
Christian, who have served as members, all of whom feel privileged to be
a part of his life and rejoice in his many achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Volunteer  firefighters  and  fire  departments  not  only
provide the critical service of fire protection but also engage in other
activities of great benefit  and  importance  to  the  communities  they
serve; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  takes  a  special  dedication, a strong desire to help
others and a tireless sense of community to forsake precious  time  with
family  and friends to respond to the signal that a neighbor is in need;
John D.  Alexander is such a firefighter; throughout 50 years of devoted
service, this volunteer heroically performed, above and beyond the  call
of  duty,  those  responsibilities  which  define  the  task  of  fire
protection; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Firefighter  John  D.  Alexander  upon  the  occasion  of  his
designation for special recognition after 50 years of dedicated  service
to Protection Fire Engine Co. No. 1; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Firefighter John D. Alexander.
