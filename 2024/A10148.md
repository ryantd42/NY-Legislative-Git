# Imposes term limits on members of the legislature

**Bill ID:** A10148
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10148 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10148)

## Summary

Imposes term limits on members of the legislature such that no person can serve in a house of the legislature for more than six consecutive terms.
