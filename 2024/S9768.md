# Extends certain provisions of law relating to requiring regulations to permit tuition waivers for certain firefighters and fire officers for CUNY

**Bill ID:** S9768
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9768 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9768)

## Summary

Extends certain provisions of law relating to requiring regulations to permit tuition waivers for certain firefighters and fire officers for CUNY until July 1, 2026.
