# Relates to the continuation of death benefits to a surviving spouse regardless of remarriage

**Bill ID:** A5788
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Slater
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the continuation of death benefits to a surviving spouse regardless of remarriage.
