# Prohibits the sale and distribution of mercury-added lamps; repealer

**Bill ID:** S5730
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5730 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5730)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale and distribution of mercury-added lamps on and after January 1, 2026.
