## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2006
 
BY: M. of A. Solages
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  45th Anniversary of the Elmont
  Memorial High School Model United Nations program
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor  and  pay
just  tribute  to  those  organizations  whose commitment and dedication
contributed to neighborhood empowerment, community enrichment,  and  the
entire State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  45th  Anniversary  of  the Elmont Memorial High School
Model United Nations program; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 1978, Elmont Model United Nations  was  established  and
began  their  journey  through  competition when Lee Marcus accepted the
invitation of the  North  American  Invitational  Model  United  Nations
(NAIMUN)  to  attend  their  conference,  which  hosted many of the most
acclaimed high schools in the country; and
 
  WHEREAS, By 1983, delegates from the  Elmont  Memorial  High  School
team  won the highest honor at the NAIMUN conference as Best Delegation,
marking the beginning of a  legacy  of  success  for  the  Model  United
Nations programs at Elmont Memorial High School and within the Sewanhaka
Central High School District; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over  the  years,  Elmont  Memorial  High  School  has won
countless awards at Model United Nations conferences across the country,
including at  Harvard  University,  Rutgers  University,  University  of
Chicago,  Columbia University, University of California at Berkeley, and
the National High School Model United Nations Conference; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Elmont delegation won international acclaim with  their
performances  at  The  Hague  International  Model  United  Nations
conferences; the team won Outstanding Delegation in  1992,  establishing
Sewanhaka  School  District  as  one  of  the  top  Model United Nations
programs in the world; and
 
  WHEREAS, This year also marks the 30th anniversary of the Lee Marcus
Invitational Sewanhaka Intra-District Model United  Nations  Conference,
hosted  annually  by  each  of  the  five  district Model United Nations
programs on a rotating basis; in addition, it is the 20th anniversary of
the Lee Marcus Model  United  Nations  Program  in  the  six  elementary
schools  in the district, founded by Lee Marcus alongside Superintendent
Al Harper, bringing students together to discuss and debate world issues
and learn lessons  on  compromise,  public  speaking,  negotiation,  and
leadership; and
 
  WHEREAS, Lee Marcus has been a distinguished member of the community
as  a  dedicated teacher, advisor, coach, academic, author, and lecturer
who served as Social Studies Chairperson at New Hyde Park Memorial  High
School and District Coordinator of Social Studies for the five component
schools in the Sewanhaka Central High School District; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Elmont Memorial High School Model United
Nations  program is in direct correlation to the efforts of its founder,
Lee Marcus, and dedicated members, whose  involvement  are,  and  always
have  been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment, an unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the  45th  Anniversary  of  the Elmont Memorial High School
Model United Nations program; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  Elmont  Memorial  High School Model United Nations
program and Founder, Lee Marcus.
