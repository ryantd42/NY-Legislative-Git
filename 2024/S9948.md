# Relates to increasing the New York state housing finance agency bonding authority

**Bill ID:** S9948
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9948 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9948)

## Summary

Increases the New York state housing finance agency bonding authority to $36,280,000,000.
