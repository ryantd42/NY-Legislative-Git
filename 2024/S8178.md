# Directs the department of environmental conservation to develop and implement a tree planting pilot program in the ten zip codes in the state with the highest incidence of asthma; appropriation

**Bill ID:** S8178
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8178 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8178)

## Summary

Directs the department of environmental conservation to develop and implement a tree planting pilot program in the ten zip codes in the state with the highest incidence of asthma.

 AN ACT in relation to directing the department of environmental  conser-

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. Within amounts appropriated  therefor,  the  department  of
 environmental  conservation  shall develop and implement a tree planting
 pilot program in the state. The department of environmental conservation
 shall consult with the department of health to  determine  the  ten  zip
 codes  in the state with the highest incidence of asthma. The department
 of environmental conservation,  in  consultation  with  local  agencies,
 shall  plant  trees  in  such  zip codes to ameliorate the environmental
 conditions in those areas. Such pilot program shall continue for a peri-
 od of five years after the effective date of this act.  At  the  end  of
 such  period, the department of environmental conservation, in consulta-
 tion with the department of health, shall report to  the  governor,  the
 temporary president of the senate and the speaker of the assembly on the
 results  of  such  pilot program and any recommendations for legislative
 action.
