# Requires elections and terms of certain officials occur on even-numbered years except in the city of New York

**Bill ID:** S9826
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S9826 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9826)

## Summary

Requires certain elections take place in even-numbered years for certain municipal and judicial officials except in the city of New York.
