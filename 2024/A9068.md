# Limits the sale of chemical hair relaxers and permanent hairstyle kits

**Bill ID:** A9068
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9068 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9068)

## Summary

Limits the sale of chemical hair relaxers and permanent hairstyle kits to licensed cosmetologists.
