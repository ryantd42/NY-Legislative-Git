# Requires certain safety devices at public boat launches

**Bill ID:** A4757
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Smullen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4757 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4757)

## Summary

Requires owners of public boat launches to maintain at least one ring buoy or life preserver and a life-belt device on the premises.
