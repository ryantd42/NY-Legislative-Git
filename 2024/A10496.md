# Modernizes the access-a-ride program's application process

**Bill ID:** A10496
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steven Raga
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10496 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10496)

## Summary

Modernizes the access-a-ride program's application process by creating an online application process and notifying applicants throughout the process via email.
