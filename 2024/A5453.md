# Designates black dirt as the state soil and to designate the black dirt region as home to the most fertile soil

**Bill ID:** A5453
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5453 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5453)

## Summary

Designates black dirt as the state soil; designates the black dirt region as home to the most fertile soil.
