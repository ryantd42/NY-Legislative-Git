# Establishes the electronic open auction public bond sale pilot program for counties, cities, towns, villages and school districts

**Bill ID:** A5137
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5137 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5137)

## Summary

Establishes the electronic open auction public bond sale pilot program for counties, cities, towns, villages and school districts; provides such provisions shall remain in effect for 3 years.
