# Directs the commissioner of corrections and community supervision to study gender disparity in resources provided to incarcerated individuals

**Bill ID:** A9586
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dana Levenberg
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9586 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9586)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of corrections and community supervision to study gender disparity in resources provided to incarcerated individuals and make a report to the governor and the legislature of his or her findings, conclusions and recommendations.
