# Establishes an occupancy tax in the village of Catskill

**Bill ID:** S9264AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9264AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9264AA)

## Summary

Establishes an occupancy tax in the village of Catskill, in the county of Greene.
