# Establishes standards for the evacuation of individuals with disabilities from high-rise buildings during an emergency

**Bill ID:** A8483
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A8483 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8483)

## Summary

Establishes standards for the evacuation of individuals with disabilities from high-rise buildings during an emergency; provides factors to be considered in the development of such standards.
