# Extends the authorization of the county of Yates to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S5609AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5609AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5609AA)

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Yates to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes through November 30, 2025.
