# Relates to repealing congestion pricing (Part A); and commissioning an independent audit of the metropolitan transportation authority (Part B)

**Bill ID:** S5425
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5425 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5425)

## Summary

Repeals congestion pricing (Part A); directs the metropolitan transportation authority to contract with a certified public accounting firm for the provision of an independent, comprehensive, forensic audit of the authority (Part B).
