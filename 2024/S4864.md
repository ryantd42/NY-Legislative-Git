# Relates to health care and mental hygiene worker bonuses

**Bill ID:** S4864
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4864 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4864)

## Summary

Includes fire districts within the definition of "municipal corporation" to include employees of fire districts within the health care and mental hygiene worker bonuses.
