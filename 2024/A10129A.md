# Authorizes the county of Monroe to provide for an easement across Lehigh Valley Trail Park, for ingress and egress to and from the project site and installation of a medium voltage cable

**Bill ID:** A10129A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10129A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10129A)

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Monroe to convey appropriate instruments to EH Henrietta Solar 1 LLC and EH Henrietta Solar 2 LLC for an easement across Lehigh Valley Trail Park, for ingress and egress to and from the project site and installation of a medium voltage cable; makes related provisions.

 ta Solar 2 LLC install a visual buffer between the Lehigh  Valley  Trail
 Park and EH Henrietta Solar 1 LLC's solar development.
