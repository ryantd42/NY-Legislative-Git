# Relates to prohibiting the location of wind turbines on Lake Erie

**Bill ID:** A5904
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David DiPietro
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5904 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5904)

## Summary

Prohibits the location and construction of wind turbines on Lake Erie.
