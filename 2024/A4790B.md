# Relates to certain duties and responsibilities of cemetery corporations

**Bill ID:** A4790BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Hevesi
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4790BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4790BA)

## Summary

Relates to certain duties and responsibilities of cemetery corporations; requires disclosure of the dimensions of spacing between plots, lots, or parts thereof; provides for the filing of consumer complaints.
