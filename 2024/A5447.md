**Bill ID:** A5447

**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5447 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5447)

Places a permanent moratorium on unfunded mandates.
