# Authorizes the discontinuance of parkland and the granting of easements for improvements to the Metro-North railroad

**Bill ID:** S9132
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9132 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9132)

## Summary

Authorizes the discontinuance of additional parkland and the granting of additional easements for improvements to the Metro-North railroad.
