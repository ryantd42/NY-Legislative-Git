# Prohibits the sale of tobacco products and vapor products within five hundred feet of a public or private school

**Bill ID:** A9224
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chantel Jackson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9224 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9224)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of tobacco products and vapor products within five hundred feet of a public or private school.
