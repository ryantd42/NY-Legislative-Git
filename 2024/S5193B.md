# Establishes a nonpartisan poll monitoring program

**Bill ID:** S5193B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5193B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5193B)

## Summary

Establishes a nonpartisan poll monitoring program.
