# Establishes a nonpartisan poll monitoring program

**Bill ID:** S5193BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5193BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5193BB)

## Summary

Establishes a nonpartisan poll monitoring program.

  (II) MAKE AUDIO OR VIDEO RECORDINGS OF ANY VOTER OR BALLOT.
  (III) IN ANY MANNER INTERVENE OR INTERFERE WITH THE ADMINISTRATION  OF
 THE ELECTION.
  (IV)  FAIL  TO  COMPLY WITH ALL INSTRUCTIONS GIVEN TO THEM BY ELECTION
 OFFICIALS EVEN IF THEY DISAGREE WITH THOSE INSTRUCTIONS.

 NONPARTISAN POLL MONITOR TAKING THE OATH REQUIRED BY  SECTION  8-306  OF
 THIS  ARTICLE; PROVIDED HOWEVER, A NONPARTISAN POLL MONITOR SHALL NOT BE
 REQUIRED TO TAKE SUCH OATH UNLESS  SUCH  MONITOR  WILL  BE  ASSISTING  A
 VOTER,  AND PROVIDED FURTHER, NOTHING IN THIS SECTION SHALL BE CONSTRUED
 TO OTHERWISE PREVENT NON-ELECTIONEERING COMMUNICATION WITH  VOTERS  THAT
 OCCURS OUTSIDE OF A POLLING PLACE.
  11.  A NONPARTISAN POLL MONITOR APPOINTED PURSUANT TO THIS SECTION MAY
 DO ONE OR MORE OF THE FOLLOWING:
  (A) OBSERVE THE MANNER IN WHICH THE DUTIES OF THE ELECTION  INSPECTORS
 ARE BEING PERFORMED.

 INSPECTOR OR OTHER PERSON IN VIOLATION OF THIS CHAPTER.
  (III) AN ELECTION PROCEDURE THAT IS NOT BEING PROPERLY PERFORMED.
  (IV) A VIOLATION OF ELECTION LAW OR OTHER PRESCRIBED  ELECTION  PROCE-
 DURE.
  (C)  REMAIN  DURING  THE  CANVASS  OF VOTES AND UNTIL THE STATEMENT OF
 RETURNS IS DULY SIGNED AND MADE.
  (D) ASSIST ANY VOTER UPON REQUEST.
  (E) KEEP RECORDS OF VOTES CAST.
