# Establishes a tax deduction for cash tips received

**Bill ID:** S9907
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9907 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9907)

## Summary

Establishes an income tax deduction for cash tips received which are considered wages or compensation.
