# Relates to the creation of a database for property seized by law enforcement agencies

**Bill ID:** A5973
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** MaryJane Shimsky
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5973 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5973)

## Summary

Relates to the creation of a database for property seized by law enforcement agencies.
