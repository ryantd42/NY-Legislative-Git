# Initiates a five-year moratorium on the dissolution of any urban rental Mitchell-Lama company and establishes a Mitchell-Lama housing program study

**Bill ID:** A4948
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4948 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4948)

## Summary

Initiates a five-year moratorium on the dissolution of any urban rental Mitchell-Lama company; establishes a Mitchell-Lama housing program study.
