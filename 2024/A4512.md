# Relates to creating provisions regarding the unlawful sale of law enforcement identification

**Bill ID:** A4512
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4512 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4512)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of any badge, shield, identification card or other insignia of any police department or law enforcement agency or a facsimile of such a badge or shield; imposes a civil penalty not to exceed $250 for each violation.
