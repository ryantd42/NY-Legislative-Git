# Relates to recovery for certain overpayments

**Bill ID:** S7713BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S7713BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7713BB)

## Summary

Prohibits the higher education services corporation from seeking recovery of awards made in error during certain academic years due to administrative error.
