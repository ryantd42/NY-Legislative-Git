# Adds certain human services programs to the designated human services programs eligible for a cost of living adjustment

**Bill ID:** A8437A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Hevesi
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8437A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8437A)

## Summary

Adds certain human services programs to the designated human services programs eligible for a cost of living adjustment.

 of other funds that are owed to such local governmental unit  or  direct
 contract provider.
