# Requires public schools to provide at least one plant- or nut-based milk option

**Bill ID:** A5895
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5895 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5895)

## Summary

Requires each public school offering a dairy-based milk option during scheduled meal periods to provide at least one plant- or nut-based milk option.
