# Requires the county seat in Steuben county to have at least one polling place designated for early voting

**Bill ID:** A5087
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5087 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5087)

## Summary

Requires the county seat in Steuben county to have at least one polling place designated for early voting.

 AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to  requiring  the  county

  (b)  The  largest  city  in  the county or, if there is no city in the
