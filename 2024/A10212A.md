# Extends certain provisions relating to new owners of buildings for which administrators have been appointed pursuant to article 7-A of the real property actions and proceedings law

**Bill ID:** A10212AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Maritza Davila
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10212AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10212AA)

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to new owners of buildings for which administrators have been appointed pursuant to article 7-A of the real property actions and proceedings law from June 30, 2024 until June 30, 2027.
