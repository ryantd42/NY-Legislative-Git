# Provides an exemption from certain requirements for a license to sell liquor at retail for consumption on certain premises

**Bill ID:** A10534
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10534 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10534)

## Summary

Provides an exemption from certain requirements for a license to sell liquor at retail for consumption on certain premises located in Orange county.
