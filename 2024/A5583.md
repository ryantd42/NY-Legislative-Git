# Includes information on if a person previously lived in a nursing home on his or her death certificate

**Bill ID:** A5583
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5583 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5583)

## Summary

Includes information on if a person previously lived in a nursing home on his or her death certificate.
