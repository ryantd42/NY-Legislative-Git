# Relates to increasing the phase-out threshold for tuition assistance program awards

**Bill ID:** A8433
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8433 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8433)

## Summary

Relates to increasing the phase-out threshold for tuition assistance program awards from seven thousand dollars to eighteen thousand dollars.
