# Prohibits the creation and dissemination of synthetic media within sixty days of an election with intent to unduly influence the outcome of an election

**Bill ID:** S8400AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8400AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8400AA)

## Summary

Prohibits the creation and dissemination of synthetic media within sixty days of an election with intent to unduly influence the outcome of an election; makes such act a class E felony.
