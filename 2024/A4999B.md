# Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to conduct a study of the technical and economic feasibility and ratepayer impact of a zero emission electrical system

**Bill ID:** A4999B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4999B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4999B)

## Summary

Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to conduct a study of the technical and economic feasibility and ratepayer impact of a zero-emission electrical system and a reduction in greenhouse gas emissions.
