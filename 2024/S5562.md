# Relates to suspending the registration of a vehicle for evading cashless tolls

**Bill ID:** S5562
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5562 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5562)

## Summary

Provides that no suspension of a registration shall occur for a violation regarding cashless tolls absent clear evidence of an owner's intent to repeatedly evade paying such tolls.
