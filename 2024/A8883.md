# Relates to modifications and exceptions to case payment reimbursement rates for neoplastic disease care hospitals

**Bill ID:** A8883
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8883 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8883)

## Summary

Provides per diem rates of payment by governmental agencies for inpatient services provided by facilities designated by the federal department of health and human services as exempt extended neoplastic disease care hospitals.
