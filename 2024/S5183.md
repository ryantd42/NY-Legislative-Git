# Relates to the imposition of sales tax in the county of Orleans

**Bill ID:** S5183
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5183 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5183)

## Summary

Extends the period during which the county of Orleans is authorized to impose additional rates of sales and compensating use taxes.
