# Adjusts the regional labor force cost index for the Hudson Valley region

**Bill ID:** A4607
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4607 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4607)

## Summary

Adjusts the regional labor force cost index for the Hudson Valley region for the 2023-2024 school year and thereafter.
