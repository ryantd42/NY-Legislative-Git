# Exempts party position elections from automatic full manual recounts

**Bill ID:** A10267
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jo Anne Simon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10267 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10267)

## Summary

Exempts party position elections which occur during a primary election from automatic full manual recounts.
