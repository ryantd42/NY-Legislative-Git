# Exempts farmers from commercial driver's license (CDL) requirements when operating a covered farm vehicle

**Bill ID:** S8761
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8761 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8761)

## Summary

Exempts farmers from commercial driver's license (CDL) requirements when operating a covered farm vehicle within one hundred fifty miles of such farmer's farm.
