# Relates to exempting certain counties from the metropolitan commuter transportation district supplemental registration fee

**Bill ID:** A5599
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5599 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5599)

## Summary

Eliminates the obligation for motorists in certain counties to pay the metropolitan commuter transportation district supplemental registration fee.
