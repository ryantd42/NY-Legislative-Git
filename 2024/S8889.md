# Relates to loss prevention programs

**Bill ID:** S8889
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8889 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8889)

## Summary

Authorizes insurers to provide no or low cost loss prevention programs to policyholders, pursuant to regulations.
