# Requires inter-agency records prepared by an outside consultant on behalf of an agency to be subject to FOIL disclosure

**Bill ID:** A6103B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6103B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6103B)

## Summary

Requires inter-agency records prepared on behalf of an agency to be subject to FOIL disclosure.
