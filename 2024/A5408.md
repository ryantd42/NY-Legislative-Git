# Proposes a constitutional amendment to increase the terms of office of members of the legislature to four years

**Bill ID:** A5408
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5408 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5408)

## Summary

Proposes a constitutional amendment to increase the terms of office of members of the legislature to four years and limit the terms of office a member of the legislature may serve.
