# Limits the potency of cannabis products

**Bill ID:** A4890
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4890 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4890)

## Summary

Limits the potency of cannabis flower to 15 percent THC in flowers and 25 percent THC in other cannabis or hemp products.
