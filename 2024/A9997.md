# Relates to establishing the MWBE Center within empire state development corporation

**Bill ID:** A9997
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amanda Septimo
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9997 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9997)

## Summary

Establishes the MWBE Center within the empire state development corporation to make the services of the division of minority and women's business development accessible in-person in cities with a population of one million or more and to provide information assistance and technical support relating to certification.
