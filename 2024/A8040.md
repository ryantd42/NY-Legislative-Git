# Establishes the high school peer mentoring pilot program

**Bill ID:** A8040
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8040 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8040)

## Summary

Establishes the high school peer mentoring pilot program.
