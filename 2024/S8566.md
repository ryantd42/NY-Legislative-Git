# Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to conduct a feasibility study on geothermal energy

**Bill ID:** S8566
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mario Mattera
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8566 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8566)

## Summary

Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to conduct a feasibility study and prepare a report on the benefits and implementation of geothermal energy.
