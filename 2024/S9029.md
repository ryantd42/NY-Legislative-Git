# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Emma Wilcox Memorial Highway"

**Bill ID:** S9029
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9029 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9029)

## Summary

Designates the portion of state route 60 between Shumla Road and Lakeview Road in the town of Stockton, county of Chautauqua, as the "Emma Wilcox Memorial Highway".
