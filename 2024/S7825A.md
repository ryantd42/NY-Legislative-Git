# Limits the amount of rent increases for residential ground lease cooperative apartment buildings and establishes certain rights upon the expiration of such leases

**Bill ID:** S7825AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Liz Krueger
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7825AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7825AA)

## Summary

Limits the amount of rent increases for residential ground lease cooperative apartment buildings; establishes certain rights upon the expiration of such leases.
