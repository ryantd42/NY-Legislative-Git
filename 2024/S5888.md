# Grants medical assistance eligibility for organ transplant expenses for certain residents of New York

**Bill ID:** S5888
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5888 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5888)

## Summary

Grants medical assistance eligibility for organ transplant expenses for residents of New York who are otherwise eligible for benefits except for their immigration status.
