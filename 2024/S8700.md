# Requires that digital coupons are made available to consumers in paper form of identical value

**Bill ID:** S8700
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8700 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8700)

## Summary

Requires that digital coupons are made available to consumers in paper form of identical value; provides for violations and penalties.
