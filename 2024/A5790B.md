# Relates to quality improvement and increased consumer transparency in assisted living residences

**Bill ID:** A5790BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5790BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5790BA)

## Summary

Requires reporting and posting by assisted living facilities relating to quality measures and information concerning rates, rent, and service fees; requires a scoring system of the assisted living quality reporting.
