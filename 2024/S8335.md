# Repeals the clean slate act

**Bill ID:** S8335
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8335 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8335)

## Summary

Repeals the clean slate act in its entirety to prior existing law.
