# Includes outside captive salespersons within the definition of an "employee"

**Bill ID:** A6236
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alec Brook-Krasny
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6236 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6236)

## Summary

Includes outside captive salespersons within the definition of an "employee".
