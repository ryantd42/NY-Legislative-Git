# Relates to a license to sell liquor at retail for consumption on certain premises

**Bill ID:** A6085A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A6085A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6085A)

## Summary

Permits the state liquor authority to issue a license to sell liquor at retail for consumption on certain premises.

 AN  ACT  to  amend  the alcoholic beverage control law, in relation to a

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  Subdivision  7  of  section  64 of the alcoholic beverage

  THE PARCEL IS LOCATED AT 155  MAIN  STREET,  SUFFOLK  COUNTY  TAX  NO.
 0404-9-1-72.3. THE PROPERTY HAS AN APPROXIMATE GLOBAL POSITIONING SYSTEM
