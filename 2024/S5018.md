# Establishes the veteran career assistance program within the SUNY and CUNY systems

**Bill ID:** S5018
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S5018 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5018)

## Summary

Establishes the veteran career assistance program within the SUNY and CUNY systems to provide veterans with assistance in resume writing and employment placement services.
