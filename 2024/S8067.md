# Relates to the term veteran and Gold Star families for the free use of campsites

**Bill ID:** S8067
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8067 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8067)

## Summary

Defines the term "veteran" and "member of a Gold Star family" for the free use of campsites.
