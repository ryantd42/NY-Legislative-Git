# Establishes catastrophe savings accounts

**Bill ID:** A10678
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela J. Hunter
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10678 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10678)

## Summary

Establishes catastrophe savings accounts as a tax-free account to allow homeowners to save money to cover qualified catastrophe expenses.
