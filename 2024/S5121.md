# Relates to prohibiting mortgagors from recovering attorney's fees and/or expenses incurred during a foreclosure

**Bill ID:** S5121
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5121 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5121)

## Summary

Relates to prohibiting mortgagors from recovering attorneys' fees and/or expenses incurred during a foreclosure.
