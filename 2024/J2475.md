## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2475
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  HONORING  Rabbi  Michael  Mishkin and Tamara Noble
  upon the occasion of their  designation  of  special
  recognition  for  15 years of service to Temple Beth
  Israel on June 9, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative  Body  to  acknowledge
outstanding  individuals  whose  professional lives, personal endeavors,
and  community  service  are  an  example  and  inspiration  to  their
colleagues, friends, family, and community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Rabbi Michael Mishkin and  Tamara  Noble  upon  the  occasion  of  their
designation  of  special  recognition  for 15 years of service to Temple
Beth Israel, to be presented on Sunday, June 9, 2024,  at  their  annual
Gala; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Supporting  the Temple Beth Israel community for 15 years,
Rabbi Michael Mishkin and Tamara  Noble  moved  to  Port  Washington  on
August  1, 2009; shortly after, they were married on August 30th of that
year; and
 
  WHEREAS, Raised in St. Louis, Missouri, Rabbi Michael Mishkin earned
his B.A. in  American  History  from  the  University  of  Pennsylvania;
furthering  his education, his commitment to Judaism led him to study at
the Jewish Theological Seminary of America, where he  received  Rabbinic
Ordination in 2001, as well as a Masters of Hebrew Letters; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Welcomed  into  the  Temple  with open arms, Rabbi Michael
Mishkin has led Temple Beth Israel for the past three  years  as  Senior
Rabbi;  he  has  also  served  as the Chairperson of the Port Washington
Interfaith Clergy Association; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  Dallas  native,  Tamara  Noble  graduated  from  Emory
University with a dual-major in English Literature and Italian; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In her professional career, Tamara Noble has worked in the
digital division of marketing and sales for several companies;  she  has
served  as  the  Director of Integrated Marketing Services for the Conde
Nast media group; and
 
  WHEREAS, Devoted to her community, Tamara Noble  volunteers  at  the
Solomon  Schechter  Day School of Long Island along with her support for
Temple Beth Israel; and
 
  WHEREAS, With them throughout have been their children,  Lily,  Ben,
and  Nathan, all of whom feel privileged to be a part of their lives and
rejoice in their achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rabbi Michael Mishkin brings his passion for life  and  his
love  for  Judaism to everything he does; Tamara Noble joins her husband
in their dedication to the Temple Beth Israel community,  opening  their
home to host people on Shabbat and Jewish holidays; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by
individuals for the benefit of others which Rabbi  Michael  Mishkin  and
Tamara Noble have displayed throughout their lives; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  has always been the objective of this Legislative Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment  to the betterment of their communities, and it is the intent
of this Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this  tribute  to
Rabbi Michael Mishkin and Tamara Noble, that future generations may know
and appreciate their admirable character, many benevolent deeds, and the
respect  and  esteem  in  which  they  are  held  by  their  peers; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  proud  to  honor  Rabbi Michael Mishkin and Tamara Noble upon the
occasion of their designation of special recognition  for  15  years  of
service to Temple Beth Israel on June 9, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Rabbi Michael Mishkin; Tamara Noble;  and  Rita  Shumsky,
Temple Beth Israel.
