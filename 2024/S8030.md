# Relates to supplementary uninsured and underinsured motorist coverage for police agencies

**Bill ID:** S8030
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8030 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8030)

## Summary

Relates to supplementary uninsured and underinsured motorist coverage for police agencies; removes provisions of law relating to self-insurance; amends the effective date to remove contracts.
