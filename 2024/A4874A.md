# Relates to the money deposited as bail by charitable bail organizations

**Bill ID:** A4874AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4874AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4874AA)

## Summary

Relates to the money deposited as bail by charitable bail organizations; removes maximum authorized amount of money allowed to be deposited by such organizations.
