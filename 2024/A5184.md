# Expands the definition of aggravated cruelty to animals

**Bill ID:** A5184
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5184 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5184)

## Summary

Expands the definition of aggravated cruelty to animals to include serious physical injuries or the use of a weapon.
