# Establishes a program of tiered eligibility for services offered by OPWDD for persons with learning disabilities and other complex neurological impairments

**Bill ID:** A9996
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9996 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9996)

## Summary

Establishes a program of tiered eligibility for services offered by the office for people with developmental disabilities for persons with learning disabilities and other complex neurological impairments.

 SERVICES ELIGIBILITY PROGRAM;
