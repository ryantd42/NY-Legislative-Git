# Establishes an implied warranty that new homes shall be free from mold caused by defective workmanship

**Bill ID:** A6104
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Emily Gallagher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6104 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6104)

## Summary

Establishes an implied warranty that new homes shall be free from mold caused by defective workmanship for a period of ten years from the date of sale of the new home.
