# Expands insurance coverage for in vitro fertilization to include three complete oocyte retrievals and in vitro fertilization with unlimited embryo transfers; includes coverage under individual policies

**Bill ID:** S9535
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9535 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9535)

## Summary

Expands insurance coverage for in vitro fertilization; includes coverage under individual policies; provides coverage for three complete oocyte retrievals and in vitro fertilization with unlimited embryo transfers.
