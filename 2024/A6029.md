# Relates to the provision of services by school-based health centers

**Bill ID:** A6029
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [A6029 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6029)

## Summary

Provides that services provided by school-based health centers shall not be provided to medical assistance recipients through managed care programs.
