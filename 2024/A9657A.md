# Requires the proper registration of abandoned vehicles

**Bill ID:** A9657AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Benedetto
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9657AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9657AA)

## Summary

Requires that any person claiming an abandoned vehicle which has a false or fraudulent certificate of registration or number plate provide proof of proper registration and insurance.
