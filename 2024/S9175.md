# Authorizes the city of Cortland to establish hotel and motel taxes of up to three percent

**Bill ID:** S9175
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9175 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9175)

## Summary

Authorizes the city of Cortland to establish hotel and motel taxes of up to three percent.
