# Establishes the psilocybin assisted therapy pilot program for veterans and first responders

**Bill ID:** A8349AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patrick Burke
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8349AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8349AA)

## Summary

Establishes the psilocybin assisted therapy pilot program; provides such program will be offered to ten thousand veterans and first responders from the western region of New York state.
