# Eliminates sporting license fees for honorably discharged, disabled veterans

**Bill ID:** A5805
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5805 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5805)

## Summary

Eliminates sporting license fees for honorably discharged, disabled veterans.
