# Permits the town of Kent, Putnam county, to lease certain sports field fences for advertisements

**Bill ID:** S9219B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9219B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9219B)

## Summary

Permits the town of Kent, Putnam county, to lease sports field fences for advertisements with the revenues to be used solely for the care of Guglielmo Field.
