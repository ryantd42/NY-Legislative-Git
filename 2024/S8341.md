# Dissolves the New York state thruway authority, and transferring all functions, duties, debts, and obligations of such authority to the department of transportation; repealer

**Bill ID:** S8341
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8341 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8341)

## Summary

Dissolves the New York state thruway authority; transfers all functions, employees, records, authority, debts and obligations of such authority to the department of transportation.

  Section 1. 1. Dissolution of the New York state thruway authority. The
 New  York  state thruway authority is hereby dissolved and discontinued,
 and all functions, duties, debts, and obligations are hereby transferred
 to the department of transportation in accordance with the provisions of
 this act.
  2. Transfer of functions. All of the functions  and  powers  possessed
 by,  and all of the obligations and duties of the New York state thruway
 authority are transferred and assigned to, assumed by and devolved  upon
 the department of transportation, in accordance with and pursuant to the
 provisions of this act.
  3.  Transfer of employees. Notwithstanding any other provision of law,
 rule, or regulation to the contrary, upon the transfer of functions from
 the New York state thruway authority pursuant to this act, all employees
 of the New York state thruway authority  shall  be  transferred  to  the
 department  of  transportation.  Employees  transferred pursuant to this
 section shall be transferred without further examination  or  qualifica-

 lations, acts, orders, determinations and decisions of the department of
 transportation until duly modified or abrogated by such department.
  8. Terms occurring in laws, contracts and  other  documents.  Whenever
 the  New York state thruway authority, or the board thereof, is referred
 to or designated in any contract or document  pertaining  to  the  func-
 tions, powers, obligations and duties hereby transferred to and assigned
 to the department of transportation, such reference or designation shall
 be deemed to refer to the department of transportation.
  9. Existing rights and remedies preserved. No existing right or remedy
 of  any  character shall be lost, impaired or affected by any provisions
 of this act.
  10. Pending actions and proceedings. No action or  proceeding  pending
 at  the  time when this act shall take effect, brought by or against the
 New York state  thruway  authority,  or  the  board  thereof,  shall  be
 affected by any provision of this act, but the same may be prosecuted or
 defended  in  the  name of the department of transportation. In all such
 actions and proceedings, the department of transportation, upon applica-
 tion of the court, shall be substituted as a party.
  11. Transfer of assets and liabilities. All assets and liabilities  of
 the  New  York  state  thruway  authority  are hereby transferred to and
 assumed by the department of transportation.
