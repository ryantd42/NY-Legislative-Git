# Relates to maintaining bridges over the canal system in a manner to not impede commercial motor vehicles

**Bill ID:** A5081
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5081 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5081)

## Summary

Requires that bridges over the canal system be maintained in a manner to not impede commercial motor vehicles.
