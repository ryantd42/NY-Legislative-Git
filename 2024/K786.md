# Honoring Jean Herve Panier upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of a Lincoln-King Visionary Award for his commitment and service to his community

**Bill ID:** K786
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K786 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K786)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 786
 
BY: M. of A. Cook
 
  HONORING  Jean  Herve  Panier upon the occasion of
  his  designation  as  recipient  of  a  Lincoln-King
  Visionary  Award  for  his commitment and service to
  his community
 
  WHEREAS, Individuals who give of their time and energies  and  serve
the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration
and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Jean  Herve  Panier  has  given  not  only of his time and
energies but also of his competence, intelligence, and  leadership,  and
consequently has been designated for special honor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Jean Herve
Panier  upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation  as  recipient  of  a
Lincoln-King  Visionary  Award  for  his  commitment  and service to his
community, to be celebrated at the Lincoln-King Visionary  Awards  Gala,
"From  Dream  to Greatness," on Saturday, February 10, 2024, at the Sand
Castle in Franklin Square, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, This auspicious community gala is held  annually  to  unite
the  international  community  and to celebrate unity in diversity while
recognizing community leaders for their services; and
 
  WHEREAS, Jean Herve Panier has been called upon  to  contribute  his
time  and  talents  to  countless civic and charitable endeavors and has
always given of himself unstintingly; and
 
  WHEREAS, Owner of Creole Plate  in  Queens,  New  York,  Jean  Herve
Panier  always  had an affinity for the culinary arts, aiding his mother
in the kitchen in his youth; and
 
  WHEREAS, Jean Herve Panier started his career  working  at  multiple
restaurants  after  arriving  to New York in 2001; he started his career
working at the prestigious Ibo LeLe Hotel; and
 
  WHEREAS, In the early 80's, Jean Herve Panier ran  the  kitchen  and
was  perfecting  luxury  catering to socialites in Haiti; he then became
head chef at Hotel El Rancho and Hotel Christopher, and later  became  a
private chef for a well-known French Diplomat who resided in Haiti; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Known  for  his passion and hospitality, Jean Herve Panier
breathes life into any kitchen he works in; he has a one-of-a-kind sense
of humor, mixed with exceptional taste and cultural knowledge of French,
Italian, and Caribbean cuisine; and
 
  WHEREAS, Years after being in the  United  States  and  running  the
kitchen  in multiple restaurants, Jean Herve Panier was able to make his
dream come true by taking full ownership of Creole Plate, Rosedale,  New
York;  his unrelenting dedication to his craft and desire for perfection
have proved him successful beyond doubt in his culinary career; and
 
  WHEREAS, Jean Herve Panier  also  does  large  catering  events  for
weddings,  funerals,  holidays,  and  even intimate, special dinners; he
soon hopes to open another location of Creole Plate as a sit-down dining
and catering hall in the future; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  the  entire period of his community service, a
period of constructive involvement, Jean Herve Panier has stood constant
in dignity, good grace, and humor; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by  an
individual  for  the  benefit  of  others  which  Jean  Herve Panier has
displayed throughout his life; and
 
  WHEREAS, It has always been the objective of this  Legislative  Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment to the betterment of their communities, and it is the  intent
of  this  Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this tribute to
Jean Herve Panier, that future generations may know and  appreciate  his
admirable  character,  his  many  benevolent  deeds, and the respect and
esteem in which he is held by his peers; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Jean  Herve  Panier  upon  the  occasion  of  his  designation as
recipient of a Lincoln-King  Visionary  Award  for  his  commitment  and
service to his community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Jean Herve Panier.
