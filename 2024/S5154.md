# Authorizes the urban development corporation to evaluate the geographical coverage of programs overseen by the corporation

**Bill ID:** S5154
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5154 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5154)

## Summary

Authorizes the urban development corporation to evaluate the geographical coverage of programs overseen by the corporation and to work with not-for-profit corporations to supplement the technical assistance needs for communities.
