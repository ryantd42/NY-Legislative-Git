# Relates to an easement over certain parkland in the town of Brookhaven

**Bill ID:** A10172A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Edward Flood
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10172A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10172A)

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Brookhaven to discontinue a portion of real property currently used as parkland to West Ferry Office, LLC for the construction of sewer facilities.

 AN ACT in relation to authorizing the town of Brookhaven to  discontinue
  a portion of real property in the county of Suffolk as parkland and to
  grant  easements  to  West  Ferry  Office, LLC for the construction of
  sewer facilities

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1. Subject to the provisions of this act, but notwithstanding
 any provision of law to the contrary, the town of Brookhaven  is  hereby
 authorized to discontinue the use as parklands of the lands described in
 section  three  of this act and to grant easements to West Ferry Office,
 LLC, upon such terms and conditions as  the  town  shall  determine,  to
 enable  West  Ferry  Office,  LLC to construct below surface sewer lines
 under such parkland.
  § 2. The authorization provided in section one of this  act  shall  be
 effective  only upon the condition that the town of Brookhaven dedicates
 an amount equal to or greater than the fair market value of the land  to
 be  discontinued towards the acquisition of new parklands and/or capital
 improvements to existing parklands and  recreational  facilities  within
 the town of Brookhaven.
  §  3.  The lands hereby authorized to be discontinued as parklands and
 for which easements may be granted to West Ferry Office, LLC are located
 in the county of Suffolk, town of Brookhaven, village of Port  Jefferson
