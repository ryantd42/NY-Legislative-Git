# Establishes a stakeholder group in the Oswego river basin

**Bill ID:** A8036AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A8036AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8036AA)

## Summary

Establishes a stakeholder group in the Oswego river basin to coordinate water release.
