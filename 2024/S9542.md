# Prohibits the publication of newspaper, magazine, or periodical which was wholly or partially produced or edited through the use of artificial intelligence without significant human oversight

**Bill ID:** S9542
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9542 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9542)

## Summary

Prohibits the publication of a digital or physical newspaper, magazine, or periodical which was wholly or partially produced or edited through the use of artificial intelligence without significant human oversight.
