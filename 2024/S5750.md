# Establishes the office of nutrition and fitness

**Bill ID:** S5750
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5750 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5750)

## Summary

Establishes the office of nutrition and fitness to protect the public from preventable diseases causally related to obesity by developing and implementing a plan to educate and encourage health and nutrition in the state.
