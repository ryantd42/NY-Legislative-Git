# Requires the office of renewable energy siting to develop standards and conditions for the siting of certain large scale renewable energy systems

**Bill ID:** A10723
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Slater
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10723 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10723)

## Summary

Requires the office of renewable energy siting to develop standards and conditions for the siting of certain large scale renewable energy systems; requires approval of municipalities to such siting plans.
