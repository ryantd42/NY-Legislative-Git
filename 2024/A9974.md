# Requires background checks prior to employment in a position involving substantial contact with children while dressed as a children's character

**Bill ID:** A9974
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Erik Dilan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9974 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9974)

## Summary

Requires background checks for a person prior to employment in a position involving substantial contact with children while dressed as a children's character; provides certain penalties for violations.
