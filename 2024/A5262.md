# Relates to increasing penalties for public urination or defecation

**Bill ID:** A5262
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lester Chang
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5262 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5262)

## Summary

Increases penalties for public urination or defecation to $500 for any violation.
