# Clarifies the definition of "tenant" to exclude squatters

**Bill ID:** S5979
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5979 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5979)

## Summary

Extends the time period for tenancy rights from 30 days to 45 days of possession; adds squatting to the definition of criminal trespass in the third degree.
