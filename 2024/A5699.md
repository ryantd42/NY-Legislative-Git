# Relates to the regulation of bus privatization contracts

**Bill ID:** A5699
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5699 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5699)

## Summary

Requires the state comptroller to approve any attempt to privatize a public bus service by a locality.
