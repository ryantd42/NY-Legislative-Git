# Establishes a community wind energy pilot program

**Bill ID:** S5660
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5660 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5660)

## Summary

Establishes a community wind energy pilot program; creates an investor owned wind energy electric generating system.
