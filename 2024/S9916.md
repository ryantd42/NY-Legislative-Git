# Includes certain persons of Middle Eastern and North African heritage and speakers of Haitian Creole as language minorities

**Bill ID:** S9916
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9916 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9916)

## Summary

Includes certain persons of Middle Eastern and North African heritage and speakers of Haitian Creole as language minorities.

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1.  Subdivision 5-a of section 17-204 of the election law,  as

  5-a.  "Language minorities" or "language-minority group" means persons
 who are American Indian, Asian American, Alaskan  Natives,  SPEAKERS  OF

 AND BUDGET'S STATISTICAL POLICY DIRECTIVE FIFTEEN.
