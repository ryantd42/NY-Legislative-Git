# Establishes distinctive "Long Island Sound" license plates

**Bill ID:** A5093
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Charles Lavine
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5093 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5093)

## Summary

Establishes distinctive "Long Island Sound" license plates; creates the "Long Island Sound environmental fund" for the department of environmental conservation to distribute moneys to not-for-profit corporations, educational organizations or local governments undertaking projects aimed at the beautification of and environmental improvements to the Long Island Sound.
