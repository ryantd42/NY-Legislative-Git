# Relates to requiring the chair and commissioners of the state liquor authority to file certain disclosures

**Bill ID:** S8228
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8228 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8228)

## Summary

Requires the chair and commissioners of the state liquor authority to file an annual statement of financial disclosure pursuant to section seventy-three-a of the public officers law.
