# Relates to referrals of certain annexation petitions

**Bill ID:** A8137
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8137 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8137)

## Summary

Requires county planning departments to review specific annexation petitions.
