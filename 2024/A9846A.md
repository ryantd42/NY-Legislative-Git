# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the Assemblyman Pat M. Casale Memorial Highway

**Bill ID:** A9846A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9846A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9846A)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Assemblyman Pat M. Casale Memorial Highway" on state route 40 in the town of Schaghticoke.
