# Establishes a distinctive plate honoring recipients of the Girl Scout Gold Award

**Bill ID:** S5128
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5128 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5128)

## Summary

Establishes a distinctive plate honoring recipients of the Girl Scout Gold Award for which the regular registration fee will apply plus an initial one time service charge of $25.
