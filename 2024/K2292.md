# Honoring Kathy Brousseau upon the occasion of her designation as the 2024 Zontian of the Year by The Zonta Club of Albany

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2292 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2292)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2292
 
BY: M. of A. Woerner
 
  LESLISLATIVE  RESOLUTION  honoring  Kathy  Brousseau
  upon the occasion of her  designation  as  the  2024
  Zontian of the Year by The Zonta Club of Albany
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to commend those
illustrious organizations and individuals within the State of  New  York
whose  focus  and  intent have made substantial impact on the quality of
life in their communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Kathy Brousseau upon the occasion of her designation as the 2024 Zontian
of the Year by The Zonta Club of Albany; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Zonta International is a worldwide service organization of
individuals working together to build  a  better  world  for  women  and
girls; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Zonta  Club of Albany, founded in 1929, advocates for
women and children locally and worldwide; and
 
  WHEREAS, A resident of Saratoga County, Kathy Brousseau  joined  The
Zonta Club of Albany and Zonta International in 2022; she has served The
Zonta  Club  of  Albany  as  a  Director,  The Zonta Club of Saratoga as
treasurer,  and  has  exemplified  Zonta's  mission  of  community  and
international service; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Amongst  26,000  Zonta  members  in  63  countries,  Kathy
Brousseau has continued The Zonta Club of Albany's 95-year tradition  of
giving back to the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Zonta Club of Albany is affiliated with programs such
as The Next Step, The Capital City Rescue  Mission's  New  Faith  Family
Center,  Dr.  Robert  Paeglow's  Koinonia Primary Care Clinic, The St.
Paul's Center, The  Albany  Memorial  Hospital  Chapel,  and  The  Legal
Project; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Kathy Brousseau was an instrumental member of the planning
committee for the 95th Anniversary Celebration  of  The  Zonta  Club  of
Albany;  she  also  supported  many  hours  of technology support to the
planning committee; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of  such  noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the  citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Kathy Brousseau upon the occasion of her designation as  the  2024
Zontian of the Year by The Zonta Club of Albany; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Kathy Brousseau.
