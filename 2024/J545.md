Senate Resolution No. 545
 
BY: Senator SEPULVEDA
 
  celebrating the historic visit of the Honorable
  Dr.  Milton  Ray  Guevara,  President  of  the
  Constitutional  Court  of the Dominican Republic, to
  the New York State Senate and Court of Appeals
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize that
the quality and character of life in the  communities  across  New  York
State  are  reflective  of  the concerned and dedicated efforts of those
individuals who would devote themselves to the welfare of the  community
and its citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
celebrate  the  historic  visit of the Honorable Dr. Milton Ray Guevara,
President of the Constitutional Court of the Dominican Republic, to  the
New  York  State  Senate and Court of Appeals on Monday, April 17, 2023;
and
 
  WHEREAS, President Milton Ray Guevara's visit to the New York  State
Senate  will mark the first time a representative from the highest court
of the Dominican Republic to have done so; and
 
  WHEREAS, The visit by President Milton Ray Guevara and the full body
of the Constitutional Court is an important  milestone  in  establishing
fraternal relations between the highest courts of the Dominican Republic
and  New York, and a testament to the commitment of President Guevara to
share the common values of democracy and respect for the Constitution in
both the Dominican Republic and New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, During the historic visit,  President  Milton  Ray  Guevara
will  offer  the first conferences in the New York State Senate, open to
the public,  highlighting  the  importance  of  constitutional  respect,
collaboration,  and the contributions of dual citizenship, a status held
by many New Yorkers; and
 
  WHEREAS, President Milton Ray Guevara  has  previously  organized  a
series  of  conferences  entitled  "Constitution and Dominican Diaspora:
Advances and Challenges" in coordination with the Consulate  General  of
the Dominican Republic in New York, providing a forum for discussions on
the  Constitution,  Fundamental  Rights,  and  Social Communication, and
emphasizing the importance of the  educational  work  developed  by  the
Constitutional  Court  in  training  Dominicans  living abroad and their
descendants on the content and  scope  of  the  Constitution  and  their
responsibilities  as  constituents in the United States, particularly in
the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, President Milton Ray Guevara has been  a  tireless  fighter
for  minority  rights  in  New  York, lecturing on constitutional law at
various universities  and  assisting  community  organizations  and  the
Dominican  consulate  with his knowledge of constitutional law and legal
procedures to help disadvantaged communities  in  New  York  with  their
needs in the judicial system; and
 
  WHEREAS, President Milton Ray Guevara has highlighted the importance
of democratic participation and the significant and exemplary democratic
advances  of  the  State  of New York, as well as the benefits that dual
citizenship has  brought  to  Dominicans  residing  in  New  York;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  the  New  York State Senate celebrates the historic
visit of  the  Honorable  Dr.  Milton  Ray  Guevara,  President  of  the
Constitutional  Court  of  the Dominican Republic, to the New York State
Senate  and  Court  of  Appeals,  and  recognizes  his  exceptional
contributions to the New York Dominican diaspora through his educational
and advocacy work on constitutional law and minority rights, and extends
its  gratitude for his efforts in promoting democratic participation and
collaboration between the Dominican Republic and the State of New  York;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Honorable Dr. Milton Ray Guevara,  President  of  the
Constitutional Court of the Dominican Republic.
