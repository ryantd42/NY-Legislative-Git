## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2318
 
BY: M. of A. Buttenschon
 
  CONGRATULATING  The Reverend John Buehler upon the
  occasion of celebrating the 50th Anniversary of  his
  Ordination to the Priesthood
 
  WHEREAS,  The character and richness of religious and community life
in this noble Empire State  are  nourished  and  fulfilled  through  the
faithful  concern  and selfless devotion of those who would commit their
energies and purpose to the ministry of others; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative  Body  to  bring  full
recognition  and  just  tribute  to  those  individuals  who distinguish
themselves and the ministry through  their  unremitting  commitment  and
preeminence of service; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate The
Reverend  John  Buehler  upon  the  occasion  of  celebrating  the  50th
Anniversary of his Ordination to the Priesthood; and
 
  WHEREAS,  John  Buehler was born to Paul and Jane (Doyle) Buehler in
Utica, New York, on July 12, 1947; he attended Oriskany  Central  School
in  Oriskany,  New  York, and Notre Dame High School in Utica, New York;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In New York State,  John  Buehler  attended  the  seminary,
studying  Philosophy  at  Wadhams Hall in Ogdensburg and Theology at Our
Lady of Angels in Albany  and  Christ  the  King  in  Syracuse;  he  was
officially ordained into the Priesthood on May 18, 1974; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  the  years,  Reverend  John  Buehler  had
appointments to serve the parishioners as an Associate  at  churches  in
New  York  State:  St.  Matthew's Parish in East Syracuse; Our Lady of
Sorrows Parish in Vestal; and St. Therese Parish in Syracuse; and
 
  WHEREAS, After a sabbatical at Fordham University in New York  City,
Reverend  John  Buehler  served  as Director of Pastoral Care at Loretto
Geriatric Center in Syracuse; additionally, he was Pastor at St.  John's
Parish and St. Francis de Sales Church in Utica; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Reverend  John  Buehler  fulfilled  his  duties  while
commissioned to complete special assignments for  the  Office  of  Vicar
Forane,  the  Councilor  of  Group  Seven,  and  the  Priests' Personnel
Committee prior to his retirement on July 1, 2019; and
 
  WHEREAS, Reverend John  Buehler  has  brought  to  his  ministry  an
overwhelming  spirit  of  charity and compassion, acting from vision and
enlightenment while conducting his administrative and spiritual  duties,
always  displaying  the  highest  standards of personal and professional
integrity and dedication; and
 
  WHEREAS, Reverend John Buehler has always given guidance and  wisdom
to  the  many  entrusted  to  his care and has ministered abundantly and
unstintingly to the spiritual and corporal needs of all; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body that when a person
of such noble aims and accomplishments is brought to our  attention,  it
is  appropriate  to  publicly  and  jubilantly proclaim and commend that
individual for the edification of others; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate The Reverend John Buehler upon the occasion of  celebrating
the  50th  Anniversary  of  his Ordination to the Priesthood, noting his
unremitting and compassionate faith and his exemplary  service  to  God,
man,  and  his community, and extending to him the heartfelt best wishes
of this  Legislative  Body  for  a  future  of  purposeful  success  and
well-being; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to The Reverend John Buehler.
