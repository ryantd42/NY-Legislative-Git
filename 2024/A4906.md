# Requires the division of state police to send renewal notices to holders of a license to carry or possess a pistol or revolver before expiration of such license

**Bill ID:** A4906
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4906 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4906)

## Summary

Requires the division of state police to send renewal notices to holders of a license to carry or possess a pistol or revolver at least 90 days before expiration of such license.
