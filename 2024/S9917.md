# Relates to the receipt of compensation for advising or assisting with veterans' benefits matters

**Bill ID:** S9917
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mario Mattera
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9917 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9917)

## Summary

Establishes certain protections related to the receipt of compensation for advising or assisting with veterans' benefits matters, including penalties for violations.
