# Includes stormwater in the definition of sewage for purposes of certain water, sewer, and water and sewer authorities

**Bill ID:** S8861
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8861 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8861)

## Summary

Includes stormwater in the definition of sewage for purposes of certain water, sewer, and water and sewer authorities; defines stormwater.

  Section  1. Subdivision 12 of section 1045-b of the public authorities
 law, as added by chapter 513 of the laws of 1984, is amended to read  as
 follows:
  12.  "Sewage" shall mean the water-carried human or animal wastes from

 water as may be present. The admixture  with  sewage  of  industrial  or
 other waste also shall be considered "sewage" within the meaning of this
 title.
