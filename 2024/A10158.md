# In relation to the list of duties of the OCFS office of the ombudsman

**Bill ID:** A10158
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Slater
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10158 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10158)

## Summary

Adds to the list of duties of the OCFS office of the ombudsman relating to hearing grievances and complaints.
