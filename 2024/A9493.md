# Provides for the recovery, reuse and recycling of hardcover books

**Bill ID:** A9493
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9493 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9493)

## Summary

Provides for the recovery, reuse and recycling of hardcover books; prohibits the disposal as solid waste and incineration of hardcover books.

 AN ACT to amend the  environmental  conservation  law,  in  relation  to

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:

  Section 1. Article 27 of the environmental conservation law is amended
 by adding a new title 34 to read as follows:
  TITLE 34
  HARDCOVER BOOK RECOVERY, REUSE AND RECYCLING
 SECTION 27-3401. DEFINITION.
  27-3403. DISPOSAL OF HARDCOVER BOOKS; PROHIBITED.
  27-3405. DEPARTMENT RESPONSIBILITIES.
  27-3407. RESPONSIBILITIES OF MUNICIPALITIES.
  27-3409. HARDCOVER BOOK RECYCLERS.
 § 27-3401. DEFINITION.
  AS USED IN THIS TITLE, "HARDCOVER BOOK" MEANS A BOOK BOUND  IN  CLOTH,
 CARDBOARD OR LEATHER. SUCH TERM SHALL NOT INCLUDE PAPERBACK BOOKS.
 § 27-3403. DISPOSAL OF HARDCOVER BOOKS; PROHIBITED.
  IT  SHALL BE UNLAWFUL TO DISPOSE OF A HARDCOVER BOOK AS SOLID WASTE OR
 TO INCINERATE A HARDCOVER BOOK.
 § 27-3405. DEPARTMENT RESPONSIBILITIES.
  THE DEPARTMENT SHALL:
  1. INVENTORY  EXISTING  VOLUNTARY  MUNICIPAL  SOLID  WASTE  MANAGEMENT
 PROGRAMS  THAT  INCLUDE RECOVERY, REUSE AND RECYCLING OF HARDCOVER BOOKS
 AND MAKE THE INFORMATION DERIVED FROM SUCH INVENTORY  AVAILABLE  TO  ALL
 MUNICIPALITIES.  WHEN MUNICIPALITIES ESTABLISH THEIR PERMANENT PROGRAMS,
 THIS INFORMATION SHALL ALSO BE SHARED WITH OTHER MUNICIPALITIES;
  2.  PROMINENTLY POST INFORMATION ABOUT MUNICIPAL HARDCOVER BOOK RECOV-
 ERY, REUSE, AND RECYCLING PROGRAMS ON ITS WEBSITE; AND
  3. ENSURE THAT ALL MUNICIPALITIES ARE NOTIFIED OF THE  HARDCOVER  BOOK
 DISPOSAL PROHIBITION AND THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS TITLE.
 § 27-3407. RESPONSIBILITIES OF MUNICIPALITIES.
 
  EXPLANATION--Matter in ITALICS (underscored) is new; matter in brackets
  [ ] is old law to be omitted.

  MUNICIPALITIES ARE ENCOURAGED TO ESTABLISH PROGRAMS TO COLLECT, REDIS-
 TRIBUTE,  REUSE  AND RECYCLE UNWANTED BOOKS. MUNICIPALITIES MAY CONTRACT
 WITH THIRD PARTY ORGANIZATIONS OR BUSINESSES FOR THE COLLECTION,  REDIS-
 TRIBUTION  AND  RECYCLING  OF UNWANTED HARDCOVER BOOKS.  MUNICIPALITIES
 SHALL  SEND ALL INFORMATION DESCRIBING THE ESTABLISHMENT AND IMPLEMENTA-
 TION OF THEIR VOLUNTARY PROGRAMS  TO  RECOVER,  REDISTRIBUTE,  REUSE  OR
 RECYCLE HARDCOVER BOOKS TO THE DEPARTMENT.
  WITHIN  TWO  YEARS  OF THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF THIS TITLE, EVERY MUNICI-
 PALITY SHALL ESTABLISH A PROGRAM  TO  RECOVER,  REDISTRIBUTE,  REUSE  OR
 RECYCLE  HARDCOVER  BOOKS.  MUNICIPALITIES  MAY ESTABLISH JOINT PROGRAMS
 WITH OTHER MUNICIPALITIES, OR CONTRACT WITH THIRD PARTY ORGANIZATIONS OR
 BUSINESSES TO RECOVER, REDISTRIBUTE, REUSE OR RECYCLE  HARDCOVER  BOOKS.
 MUNICIPALITIES  SHALL  SEND ALL INFORMATION DESCRIBING THE ESTABLISHMENT
 AND IMPLEMENTATION OF THEIR PROGRAMS TO RECOVER, REDISTRIBUTE, REUSE  OR
 RECYCLE HARDCOVER BOOKS TO THE DEPARTMENT.
  MUNICIPALITIES  SHALL  KEEP  RECORDS OF THEIR HARDCOVER BOOK RECOVERY,
 REUSE, REDISTRIBUTION AND  RECYCLING  PROGRAMS,  INCLUDING  TONNAGES  OF
 BOOKS  COLLECTED,  BOOKS REDISTRIBUTED FOR REUSE, BOOKS RECYCLED AND THE
 RECYCLING COMPANIES UTILIZED.
 § 27-3409. HARDCOVER BOOK RECYCLERS.
  EVERY PERSON OR ENTITY ENGAGED IN THE RECOVERY AND RECYCLING OF  HARD-
 COVER  BOOKS  SHALL  ANNUALLY PROVIDE DOCUMENTATION TO THE DEPARTMENT OF
 THE TONNAGE OF HARDCOVER BOOKS  RECEIVED  FROM  EACH  MUNICIPALITY  WITH
 WHICH  IT  CONTRACTS,  AND THE PERSONS OR ENTITIES TO WHICH THE RECYCLED
 HARDCOVER BOOKS ARE SENT.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
