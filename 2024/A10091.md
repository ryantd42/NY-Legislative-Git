# Exempts the town of Pound Ridge from the establishment of retail dispensary licenses and on-site consumption licenses for cannabis

**Bill ID:** A10091
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10091 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10091)

## Summary

Exempts the town of Pound Ridge from the establishment of retail dispensary licenses and on-site consumption licenses for cannabis, provided that the town adopts a local law prohibiting the establishment of such licenses.
