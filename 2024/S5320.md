# Relates to requiring an updated signature and photo image for renewal and display on a renewed license

**Bill ID:** S5320
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5320 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5320)

## Summary

Relates to requiring an updated signature and photo image for renewal and display on a renewed license.
