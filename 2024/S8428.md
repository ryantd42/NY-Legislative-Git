# Requires the department of financial services publish a report on long-term care insurance rates

**Bill ID:** S8428
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8428 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8428)

## Summary

Requires the department of financial services publish a report on long-term care insurance rates including historical rates and the dates and amounts of any rate changes.
