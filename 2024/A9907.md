# Provides for a limited alternative remedy to remove unauthorized persons from residential real property

**Bill ID:** A9907
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9907 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9907)

## Summary

Provides for a limited alternative remedy to remove unauthorized persons from residential real property if certain conditions are met.

  3. UPON RECEIPT OF THE COMPLAINT, THE POLICE OFFICER SHALL VERIFY THAT
