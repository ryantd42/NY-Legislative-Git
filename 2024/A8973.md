# Relates to toll reimbursement for all represented and non-represented persons employed by the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey

**Bill ID:** A8973
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8973 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8973)

## Summary

Provides for toll reimbursement for port authority of New York and New Jersey employees for entering or exiting the port authority port district, the twenty-five mile radius of the Statue of Liberty National Monument.

 MANDATORY TOLL CHARGES INCURRED WHILE ENTERING AND TRAVELING WITHIN  THE

 AGREEMENT  WITH  THE  PORT  AUTHORITY OF NEW YORK AND NEW JERSEY. A PORT
 AUTHORITY OF NEW YORK AND NEW JERSEY EMPLOYEE  SHALL  SURRENDER  E-ZPASS
 UPON RETIREMENT, RESIGNATION OR TERMINATION.
