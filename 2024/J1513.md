# Congratulating the Chazy Boys Soccer Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHAA Class D Championship

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1513 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1513)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1513
 
BY: Senator STEC
 
  CONGRATULATING the Chazy Boys Soccer Team upon the
  occasion of winning the NYSPHAA Class D Championship
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Chazy Boys Soccer Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHAA Class  D
Championship;  to  the  praise  and  applause of their excited fans, the
Eagles put up a competitive long battle which went to  double  overtime,
resulting  in  a  tie and co-victory, resulting in the Chazy Boys Soccer
Team to become a ten-time State Championship-winning team  on  Saturday,
November 11, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's colors of green and white,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Eagles throughout their journey as they ended their season
with an impressive 19-0-2 overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as soccer, which demands athletic  prowess,
skill,  speed,  and agility, Head Coach Rob McAuliffe, with his depth of
knowledge and coaching experience, worked hard to  hone  the  skills  of
this  championship  team,  teaching  these  outstanding athletes lessons
which will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and
 
  WHEREAS, Coach McAuliffe and all the  outstanding  athletes  on  the
Chazy  High  School  Boys  Soccer Team have clearly utilized dedication,
determination, and teamwork in providing a lasting contribution  to  the
spirit  of  excellence  which  is  a  tradition  of  their  school; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the  members  of  the  Chazy High School Boys Soccer Team:
Dylan McAfee; Isaac Merrill; Madox Tyndall; Landon Duprey; Pryce Parker;
Novak Jarus; Ben West; Ryan Demers; Elijah Valentin; Zamir Foster;  Kobe
Hernandez;  Owen  Poirier;  Evan Dwyer; Hudson Ziel; Jordan Juneau; Jack
Rovers; Oliver Stoddard; Landon Frenyea; Owen Kapp; Jacoby  Weeden,  and
Head  Coach  Rob  McAuliffe on their outstanding season and overall team
record; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  members of the Chazy High School Boys Soccer Team,
and Head Coach Rob McAuliffe.
