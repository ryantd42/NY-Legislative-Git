# Provides for enhanced penalties for larceny and criminal possession of stolen property against elderly persons

**Bill ID:** A10689
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10689 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10689)

## Summary

Increases offense categories by one for certain larceny and criminal possession of stolen property offenses committed against elderly persons.
