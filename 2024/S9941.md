# Ensures zero-emission school buses and major components thereof are manufactured and assembled within the United States and provides for waivers of zero-emission school bus requirements in certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** S9941
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9941 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9941)

## Summary

Ensures zero-emission school buses and major components thereof are manufactured and assembled within the United States; provides for waivers of zero-emission school bus requirements in certain circumstances.
