# Extends the authorization granted to the county of Clinton to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A5340AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5340AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5340AA)

## Summary

Extends the authorization granted to the county of Clinton to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes until November 30th, 2025.
