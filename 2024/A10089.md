# Authorizes funeral directing apprenticeships as an alternative to licensing examinations

**Bill ID:** A10089
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10089 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10089)

## Summary

Authorizes funeral directing apprenticeships as an alternative to licensing examinations; requires such apprenticeships to be one- to four-year programs led by licensing funeral directors; makes related provisions.
