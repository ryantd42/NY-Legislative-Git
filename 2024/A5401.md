# Relates to limiting the power of the governor to make an executive order or otherwise direct an executive agency to take any action that contradicts state law; repealer

**Bill ID:** A5401
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Doug Smith
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5401 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5401)

## Summary

Relates to limiting the power of the governor to give an executive order or otherwise direct an executive agency to take any action that contradicts state law.
