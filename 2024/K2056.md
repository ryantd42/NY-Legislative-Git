**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeff Gallahan
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2056 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2056)

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2056
 
BY: M. of A. Gallahan
 
  MEMORIALIZING  Governor  Kathy  Hochul to proclaim
  April 8-12, 2024, America Saves Week in the State of
  New York
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  to  recognize
official days that are set aside to increase awareness of serious issues
that affect the lives of citizens of New York State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
memorialize  Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim April 8-12, 2024, America
Saves Week in the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, America Saves Week was established in 2007,  as  an  annual
celebration  and  call  to  action  for  everyday Americans to commit to
saving successfully; and
 
  WHEREAS, During this week of recognition, individuals are encouraged
to do a financial check-in which allows them to  get  a  clear  view  of
their  finances,  set  savings goals, and create a plan to achieve them;
and
 
  WHEREAS, This year, the week's themes will be: Saving Automatically,
Monday, April 8, 2024; Saving for  the  Unexpected,  Tuesday,  April  9,
2024;  Saving  for  Major  Milestones, Wednesday, April 10, 2024; Paying
Down Debt is Saving, Thursday, April 11, 2024; and Saving  at  Any  Age,
Friday, April 12, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  America  Saves  is  a  non-profit  organization which uses
principles of behavioral economics and  social  marketing  to  motivate,
encourage  and  support  everyday  Americans to save money, reduce debt,
build wealth and create better financial habits; and
 
  WHEREAS, This vital organization encourages individuals and families
to take the America Saves pledge, a tool that empowers them to commit to
save successfully with a plan; thousands of non-profit, government,  and
corporate  organizations  partner  with  America  Saves  through  local,
regional, statewide, and national campaigns both year-round  and  during
America Saves Week; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  an organization, America Saves is dedicated to helping
build financial confidence because it is a place where  we  can  support
ALL people, including the most vulnerable among us; and
 
  WHEREAS,  America  Saves is an initiative of the Consumer Federation
of America (CFA), a non-profit, pro-consumer organization  of  over  270
consumer  education,  advocacy,  and  cooperative  members  dedicated to
advancing consumer interest; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is imperative that there be  greater  awareness  of  the
importance  of  financial  security,  and  more must be done to increase
activity at the local, State and National levels; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
memorialize  Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim April 8-12, 2024, America
Saves Week in the State of New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to The Honorable Kathy Hochul, Governor of the State of  New
York.
