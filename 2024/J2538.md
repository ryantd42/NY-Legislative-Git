## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2538
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  HONORING Georgianna Larry upon the occasion of her
  retirement  after  33  years of dedicated service to
  the Town of Floyd, New York
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge the
significant  milestones  in  the  distinguished  careers  of  esteemed
residents of this noble Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Georgianna Larry upon the occasion of her retirement after 33  years  of
distinguished service to the Town of Floyd, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For the past 33 years, Georgianna Larry rendered faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to the Town of  Floyd,  earning  the
admiration, esteem, and affection of her colleagues; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  her capacity as Town Clerk, served with loyalty, honor
and distinction, and was governed by a keen sense  of  duty  and  always
showed a unique grasp of human problems in her official acts; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Georgianna  Larry  became town clerk on February 11, 1991,
and has served faithfully in this capacity  since;  she  served  as  the
town's  registrar  of  vital  statistics  where  she  completed  birth,
marriage, and death  certificates;  prepared  warrants;  attended  board
meetings;  and  recorded  minutes for these meetings; she also completed
dog licenses, sold hunting and fishing  licenses,  and,  throughout  her
tenure,  has amassed a great depth of knowledge, often giving assistance
and direction to anyone in need; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of  such  noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the  citizens
of the great State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Georgianna Larry upon the occasion  of  her  retirement  after  33
years of distinguished service to the Town of Floyd; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Georgianna Larry.
