# Permits the Fulton city school district to establish an insurance reserve fund

**Bill ID:** S9247
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9247 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9247)

## Summary

Permits the Fulton city school district to establish an insurance reserve fund.
