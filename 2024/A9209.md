# Relates to certain payments to the horsemen's organization

**Bill ID:** A9209
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9209 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9209)

## Summary

Relates to certain payments to the horsemen's organization; extends such provisions to 2025.
