# Extends the authorization of residential property owners in high risk brush fire areas on Staten Island to cut and remove reeds

**Bill ID:** S9765
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9765 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9765)

## Summary

Extends, until December 31, 2025, the authorization of residential property owners in high risk brush fire areas on Staten Island to cut and remove reeds.
