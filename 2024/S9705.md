# Establishes a MWBE or SDVOBE subcontractor tax credit

**Bill ID:** S9705
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9705 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9705)

## Summary

Establishes a tax credit for contractors who award a subcontract to MWBE or SDVOBE subcontractors in furtherance of a state contract.
