# Congratulating the Utica University Men's Ice Hockey Team upon the occasion of capturing the United Collegiate Hockey Conference Men's Ice Hockey Championship

**Bill ID:** K171
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K171 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K171)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 171
 
BY: M. of A. Buttenschon
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Utica  University  Men's  Ice
  Hockey Team  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing  the
  United Collegiate Hockey Conference Men's Ice Hockey
  Championship on March 4, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in collegiate sports;  this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Utica University Men's Ice Hockey Team upon the  occasion  of  capturing
their  second  straight  United  Collegiate  Hockey Conference Men's Ice
Hockey Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and  applause  of  their  excited  fans,  the
number  one seed Pioneers defeated number two seed Nazareth College in a
thrilling 5-3 victory on Saturday, March 4, 2023, at the Utica  Memorial
Auditorium in Utica; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  this  monumental win, the Utica University Men's Ice
Hockey Team has earned an automatic berth into the  2023  NCAA  Division
III Men's Ice Hockey Championship Tournament; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's colors of navy and orange,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Pioneers throughout their  journey  as  they  ended  their
regular season with an impressive 24-2-1 overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  ice hockey, which demands athletic
prowess, speed  and  agility,  Head  Coach  Gary  Keenan  and  Assistant
Coaches, Nick Thierren and Adam Dekker worked hard to hone the skills of
this  championship  team,  teaching  these  outstanding athletes lessons
which will prove invaluable both on and off the ice; and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach Gary Keenan, Assistant Coaches Nick Thierren and
Adam Dekker, and all of the outstanding athletes on the Utica University
Men's Ice Hockey Team have clearly  utilized  dedication,  determination
and  teamwork  in  providing  a  lasting  contribution  to the spirit of
excellence which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the members of the Utica University Men's Ice Hockey Team:
Bryan Landsberger, Brian Scoville, Connor Fedorek, Jayson  Dobay,  Jaxon
Rutkowski,  Shane  Murphy,  John  Gutt,  Chris  Carroll, Matt Wood, Josh
Kagan, Justin Allen, Lucas Herrmann, Blake Holmes,  Drake  Barnish,  Mic
Curran,  Kimball  Johnson,  Joey  Berkopec, Remy Parker, Regen Cavanagh,
 
Jakob Breault, Brandon Osmundson, Buster Larsson,  Andrew  Doran,  Avery
Sturtz,  Khristian  Acosta, Dante Zapata, and John Moncovich; Head Coach
Gary Keenan; and Assistant Coaches, Nick Thierren and Adam  Dekker  upon
the  occasion of capturing the United Collegiate Hockey Conference Men's
Ice Hockey Championship on March 4, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  members  of  the Utica University Men's Ice Hockey
Team, Head Coach Gary Keenan, and Assistant Coaches  Nick  Thierren  and
Adam Dekker.
