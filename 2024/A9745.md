# Enacts the "New York state green new deal for public housing act"

**Bill ID:** A9745
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9745 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9745)

## Summary

Enacts the "New York state green new deal for public housing act", which commissions a study to assist the state of New York in reaching its climate goals.
