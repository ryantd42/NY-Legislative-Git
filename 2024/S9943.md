# Relates to deer hunting with a minor

**Bill ID:** S9943
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9943 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9943)

## Summary

Removes requirements that a parent, guardian or person and the minor remain at ground level at all times while deer hunting.
