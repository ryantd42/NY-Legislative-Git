**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2369 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2369)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2369
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING  the  Youth  Auxiliary Group of Al Tabari
  Court No. 28 upon  the  occasion  of  receiving  the
  Excellence in the Community Award by the Schenectady
  City Council on June 24, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the intent of this Legislative Body to commend and
pay tribute to those young  people  who,  by  demonstrating  outstanding
commitment  to  their community, have inspired, and brought pride to our
majestic Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
the Youth Auxiliary Group of Al Tabari Court No. 28 upon the occasion of
receiving  the Excellence in the Community Award by the Schenectady City
Council on Monday, June 24, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under  the  direction  and  Guidance  of  Past  Illustrious
Commandress  Desiree  M.  Mason-Chaires,  Past  Illustrious  Commandress
Monique King-Banks, and United States Marine Michael P. Borowski, the Al
Tabari  Court  youth  group  has  traveled  the  country  successfully
representing New York in nationally recognized competitions; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Al  Tabari  Court  No.  28's Youth Auxiliary Group is also
being recognized  by  the  Schenectady  NAACP  for  a  decade  of  youth
community service; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  women of the Youth Auxiliary Group of Al Tabari Court
No. 28 have had the privileged opportunity to serve both their community
and communities across the nation, demonstrating what it  means  to  act
with  poise,  intelligence, strength, and outstanding moral character in
all their civic and  charitable  endeavors,  justly  earning  them  this
esteemed recognition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Their  Illustrious  Commandress  Dt.  Tawonna
Williams-Holbrook,  also  affectionately  recognized  as  "Strawberri"
amongst  Nobles  and  Daughters,  has shown her continued commitment and
support in providing the youth group with whatever is needed to  aid  in
their  success  in concert with the men of Al Tabari Temple No. 121, who
also provide support, volunteer time, and provide academic  scholarships
to  community  youth;  the  Youth  Auxiliary  has  delivered  multiple
generations of youth members into higher  education,  career  jobs,  and
business ownership; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Youth  Auxiliary  Group  of  Al  Tabari  Court No. 28
represents the best development of the potential which  is  inherent  in
our most precious resource, our youth; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor the Youth Auxiliary Group of Al  Tabari  Court  No.  28  upon  the
occasion  of  receiving  the  Excellence  in  the Community Award by the
Schenectady City Council on June 24, 2024; and
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Youth Auxiliary Group of Al Tabari Court No. 28.
