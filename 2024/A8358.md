# Exempts senior citizens from jury duty

**Bill ID:** A8358
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8358 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8358)

## Summary

Exempts from jury duty prospective jurors who are age seventy-five or older who make a request to be exempt.
