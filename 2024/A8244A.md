# Relates to regulations to restrict docks, boathouses and moorings on Otsego Lake in the town of Springfield

**Bill ID:** A8244A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Tague
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A8244A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8244A)

## Summary

Permits the town of Springfield to adopt and enforce regulations restricting docks, boathouses and moorings on Otsego lake within such town.
