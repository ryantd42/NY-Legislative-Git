# Requires full family service restroom facilities in public buildings

**Bill ID:** A5096
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Charles Lavine
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5096 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5096)

## Summary

Requires full family service restroom facilities in public buildings to permit a caregiver to attend to the sanitary and hygienic needs of any person affected by any combination of physical and/or cognitive disabilities.
