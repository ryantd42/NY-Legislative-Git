# Authorizes the New York city council to determine annual rental payments charged to the New York city water board

**Bill ID:** S9456
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9456 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9456)

## Summary

Authorizes the New York city council to determine annual rental payments charged to the New York city water board no later than February first of each year.
