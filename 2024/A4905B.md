# Relates to permitting farmers markets in state and local parks

**Bill ID:** A4905BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A4905BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4905BA)

## Summary

Encourages farmers' markets in state parks by posting related information, identifying opportunities and coordinating with other state agencies.
