# Authorizes beneficiaries to choose pension benefits or a death benefit in certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** S9812A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9812A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9812A)

## Summary

Authorizes beneficiaries to choose pension benefits or a death benefit in certain circumstances; provides for the benefit if multiple beneficiaries cannot agree.
