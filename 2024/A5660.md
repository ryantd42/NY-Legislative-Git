# Relates to the dismissal of members on the panel for educational policy

**Bill ID:** A5660
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monique Chandler-Waterman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5660 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5660)

## Summary

Prohibits the discharge or dismissal of a member of the panel for educational policy without providing such member with a thirty day notice of such discharge or dismissal.
