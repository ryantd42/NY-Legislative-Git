# Includes gender equality in the social and economic equity plan of the New York state cannabis control board

**Bill ID:** A4390
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4390 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4390)

## Summary

Includes transgender, gender non-conforming and non-binary individuals in the social and economic equity plan of the New York state cannabis control board relating to the awarding of adult-use cannabis licenses.
