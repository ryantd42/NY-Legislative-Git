# Authorizes certain licensed health service professionals to form limited liability companies

**Bill ID:** S5581AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5581AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5581AA)

## Summary

Authorizes certain licensed health service professionals to form limited liability companies.
