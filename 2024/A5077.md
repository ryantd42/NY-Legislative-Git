# Relates to aggravated unlicensed operation of a motor vehicle in the first degree

**Bill ID:** A5077
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5077 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5077)

## Summary

Makes it a class E felony to negligently cause the death of another person while operating a motor vehicle with a suspended, revoked or otherwise withdrawn license.
