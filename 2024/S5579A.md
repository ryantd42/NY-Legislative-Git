# Requires insurance policies to provide coverage for transvaginal ultrasounds during pregnancy

**Bill ID:** S5579AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5579AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5579AA)

## Summary

Requires insurance policies to provide coverage for transvaginal ultrasounds during pregnancy.
