# Relates to expanding employee ownership assistance for transgender and non-binary individuals

**Bill ID:** S7904
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7904 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7904)

## Summary

Relates to expanding employee ownership assistance for transgender and non-binary individuals; expands such assistance beyond industrial and manufacturing plants and includes transgender and non-binary individuals.
