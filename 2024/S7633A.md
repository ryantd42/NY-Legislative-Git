# Permits certain judges, justices and support magistrates to carry a firearm, rifle, or shotgun in a sensitive location

**Bill ID:** S7633A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7633A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7633A)

## Summary

Permits certain judges, justices and support magistrates to carry a firearm, rifle, or shotgun in a sensitive location.
