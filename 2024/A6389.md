# Relates to leave time for certain employees to volunteer as a poll worker

**Bill ID:** A6389
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alec Brook-Krasny
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6389 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6389)

## Summary

Provides for a paid leave of absence for public officers and certain other employees to volunteer as a poll worker.
