# Extends the authorization of the county of Tioga to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A5775A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Christopher Friend
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5775A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5775A)

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Tioga to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes through November 30, 2025.
