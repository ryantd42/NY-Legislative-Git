# Relates to letters of credit for siting permits under the major renewable energy development program

**Bill ID:** A10004
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10004 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10004)

## Summary

Directs the office of renewable energy siting to develop a procedure to process letters of credit for siting permits under the major renewable energy development program.
