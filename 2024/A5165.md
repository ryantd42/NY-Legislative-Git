# Expands requirements of sheltering of dogs outside

**Bill ID:** A5165
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5165 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5165)

## Summary

Enacts stricter requirements for sheltering of dogs outside including protection from wind and loss of body heat.
