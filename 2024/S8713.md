# Relates to adolescent offenders authorized to be removed to family court

**Bill ID:** S8713
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8713 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8713)

## Summary

Exempts adolescent offenders who were illegally in possession of a loaded firearm from those authorized to be removed to family court.
