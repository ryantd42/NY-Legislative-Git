## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1835
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMEMORATING the Port Washington Garden Club upon
  the occasion of celebrating its 100th Anniversary on
  March 3, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend those organizations of commitment  and  high  achievement  whose
purpose and endeavor have been dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in
floral  design  and  the  promotion  of  civic  and  environmental
responsibility in this noble Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commemorate the  Port  Washington  Garden  Club  upon  the  occasion  of
celebrating  its  100th Anniversary, to be celebrated at their reception
on Sunday, March 3, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, With a mission to beautify the Town of Port Washington, New
York, the Port Washington Garden Club is a non-profit organization  that
ensures  public spaces are properly maintained with foliage and tasteful
decorations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Recruiting  with  the  purpose  of  promoting  members  to
collaborate  to  find the best solution for the upkeep of the community,
the Port Washington Garden Club facilitates the study and performance of
practical gardening and teaches the art of flower arranging; and
 
  WHEREAS, Devoted to brightening the community with lovely plantings,
the Port Washington Garden Club aims to maintain the  highest  standards
in  all  branches  of horticulture; to encourage civic plantings and any
other activity pertaining to business that is of  specific  interest  to
the  garden  club; and lastly, to aid in the conservation of our natural
resources; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Port Washington Garden Club is in direct
correlation to the  efforts  of  its  Executive  Board:  President,  Pat
DeBari;  Vice  President,  Rita  O'Leary;  Recording  Secretary,  Betty
Affisco;  Treasurer,  Dolores  Ilardo;  Corresponding  Secretary,  Penny
Moroney;  Program Coordinator, Kathy Fico; Civic Activities, Irene Wood;
Ways  &  Means,  Anna  Deriu;  Membership,  Tertia  Wallace;  and
Members-At-Large Barbara Kaim and Hedwig Schapfel, whose involvement is,
and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an impressive commitment, an
unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard  of  excellence  in
all endeavors on behalf of the Club and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Steeped  in  a  proud  and distinguished history, the Port
Washington Garden Club may look back with pride and hold true of purpose
in its commitment to serving the Port Washington community  and  beyond;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the  Port  Washington  Garden  Club  upon  the  occasion  of
celebrating its 100th Anniversary on March 3, 2024, noting the propriety
of  its  goals, the constancy of its commitment, and the significance of
 
its accomplishments in improving its community now and  in  the  future;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Pat DeBari, President of the Port Washington Garden Club.
