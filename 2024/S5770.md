# Enacts the "Empire State Windfall Profits Tax Act of 2023"

**Bill ID:** S5770
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5770 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5770)

## Summary

Enacts the "Empire State Windfall Profits Tax Act", establishing an excise tax on the quarterly receipts of a company experiencing a profit windfall.
