# Relates to the effectiveness of certain provisions of the banking law

**Bill ID:** S9382
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9382 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9382)

## Summary

Extends certain provisions of the banking law from September 10, 2024 until September 10, 2029.
