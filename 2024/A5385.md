# Requires a weigh-in to take place the day of a combative sports event

**Bill ID:** A5385
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela J. Hunter
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5385 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5385)

## Summary

Requires a weigh-in of all participants shall occur on the date of a combative sports event.
