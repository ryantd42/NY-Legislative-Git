# Requires contractors to recycle waste generated on construction and demolition sites

**Bill ID:** S5160
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5160 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5160)

## Summary

Requires contractors to recycle a certain percentage of the waste generated on construction and demolition sites.
