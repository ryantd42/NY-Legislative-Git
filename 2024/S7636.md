# Authorizes school districts to borrow against certain reserve funds

**Bill ID:** S7636
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7636 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7636)

## Summary

Authorizes school districts to borrow against certain reserve funds, pursuant to a resolution adopted by the board of education of such school district.
