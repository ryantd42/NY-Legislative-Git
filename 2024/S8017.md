# Relates to amending the effectiveness of a chapter of 2023 relating to public accountants

**Bill ID:** S8017
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8017 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8017)

## Summary

Amends the effectiveness of a chapter of 2023 relating to allowing for non-CPA ownership of firms; provides that such act shall take effect in 6 months.
