# Relates to requiring that certain information be provided to operators of limited use motorcycles

**Bill ID:** A10550
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Gonzalez-Rojas
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10550 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10550)

## Summary

Relates to requiring that certain information be provided to operators of limited use motorcycles concerning rules of the road and consequences for non-compliance.

 AN  ACT  to  amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to requiring
  that operators of limited use motorcycles receive rules  of  the  road
  pamphlets
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Section 2261 of the vehicle and traffic law is  amended  by
 adding a new subdivision 6 to read as follows:
  6.  RULES  OF  THE ROAD PAMPHLET REQUIREMENT. EVERY PERSON OPERATING A
 LIMITED  USE  MOTORCYCLE  REGISTERED  IN  ACCORDANCE  WITH  ANY  OF  THE
 PROVISIONS  OF  THIS ARTICLE, SHALL BE PROVIDED WITH INFORMATION RELATED
 TO THE LEGAL USE OF SUCH LIMITED USE MOTORCYCLE UPON SUCH  REGISTRATION.
 SUCH  INFORMATION  SHALL  BE  PUBLISHED  IN THE TEN MOST COMMONLY SPOKEN
 LANGUAGES IN NEW YORK STATE AND SHALL INCLUDE, BUT NOT  BE  LIMITED  TO,
 THE  RULES  OF THE ROAD, SPEED LIMITS, WHERE SUCH OPERATOR CAN RIDE SUCH
 LIMITED USE MOTORCYCLE, AND CONSEQUENCES OF NON-COMPLIANCE FOR EACH SUCH
 RULE.
