# Commending Class of 2024 Salutatorian, Gabriela Franco, of Malverne High School, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** J2509
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2509 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2509)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2509
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024 Salutatorian, Gabriela
  Franco, of Malverne High School, in  recognition  of
  her outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions to  honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend  Class  of
2024  Salutatorian,  Gabriela  Franco,  of  Malverne  High  School,  in
recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian,  Gabriela  Franco,  represents  the  best  of
developed  potential  inherent in our most precious resource, our youth;
her achievements have brought enduring honor to her family and community
and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Salutatorian, Gabriela Franco, takes leave  of  Malverne
High  School,  she  bestows  a  legacy  of  academic achievement, school
involvement, and community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian, Gabriela Franco, may now stand with pride  as
she  assesses  her  achievements and experiences the satisfaction of her
labors and the joy  of  her  accomplishments,  eager  to  face  the  new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2024 Salutatorian, Gabriela Franco,  of  Malverne  High
School,  in  recognition  of  her outstanding accomplishments; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Salutatorian, Gabriela Franco.
