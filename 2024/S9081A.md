# Relates to microchipping of certain animals possessed by animal protective associations and rescue organizations

**Bill ID:** S9081A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9081A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9081A)

## Summary

Permits microchipping of certain animals possessed by animal protective associations and rescue organizations.
