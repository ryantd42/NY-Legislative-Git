# Establishes a moratorium on certain local laws, ordinances, rules or regulations, by a city with a population of one million or more, related to achieving certain reductions in greenhouse gas emissions

**Bill ID:** S9682
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9682 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9682)

## Summary

Establishes a moratorium on the adoption and/or implementation of certain local laws or ordinances, or certain rules or regulations, by a city with a population of one million or more, related to achieving certain reductions in greenhouse gas emissions.

 to achieving certain reductions in greenhouse gas emissions  which  does
 not  exempt  an  industrial  facility  that produces one hundred percent

 covered buildings under such local law.
