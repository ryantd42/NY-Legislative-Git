**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** Adopted

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2076
 
BY: M. of A. Angelino
 
  HONORING  Dave and Jess Shull upon the occasion of
  their designation as recipients of the Distinguished
  Citizen  Award  by  the  Baden-Powell  Council,  Boy
  Scouts of America on May 9, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the practice of this Legislative Body to take note of
and  publicly  acknowledge  individuals  of  remarkable  integrity  and
character who have inspired and brought pride  to  our  majestic  Empire
Sate  with  their  strong  contributions  to  the  community  and  model
citizenship; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud  to  honor  Dave  and
Jess  Shull  upon the occasion of their designation as recipients of the
Distinguished Citizen Award by the Baden-Powell Council, Boy  Scouts  of
America  on  Thursday,  May  9,  2024,  at the 37th Annual Distinguished
Citizens Award Dinner for Chenango County  at  the  Canasawacta  Country
Club in Norwich, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Distinguished Citizen Award recognizes the noteworthy
and extraordinary leadership of distinguished citizens in the community;
the recipient of this auspicious award lives to serve  and  inspire  the
youth of today, to lead, accept responsibility and care about principles
and causes beyond self-interest; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Exemplifying  what  it means to live by the Scout Oath and
Law, Dave and Jess Shull have set a  positive  example  for  others  and
demonstrated  concern  and  significant  care  for  their  community,
rightfully earning accolades for their accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Born in 1976, Dave Shull was raised  in  Colorado  Springs,
Colorado,  and  was  an  active  member  of  The  Boy Scouts of America,
achieving their highest honor of Eagle Scout  in  1992;  furthering  his
education,  he  attended The University of Northern Colorado in Greeley,
Colorado and studied Sociology with an emphasis in Criminal Justice; and
 
  WHEREAS, Beginning his career, Dave Shull was hired on with  Foley's
Department  Store,  now  known as Macy's, where he worked his way up the
ranks as Loss Prevention Manager;  he  moved  to  Oxford,  New  York  in
October of 2005 to pursue a restaurant business venture; and
 
  WHEREAS, Born in 1980, and raised in Oxford, New York, Jessica Shull
attended  Oxford  Academy where she excelled in school and began working
in her final year; becoming a mother to  two  beautiful  daughters,  she
continued  her  career  completing  her  Associates Degree at Healthcare
online in 2009; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dave and Jessica Shull met in 2006, while Dave was pursuing
a business venture in Oxford, New York, to open The Stadium Sports Bar &
Grill, which opened in January of 2005; Jessica was brought on the small
staff and instantly provided new ideas and knowledge to the  restaurant,
starting to bake house made fresh desserts; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 2007, The Stadium Sports Bar & Grill began hosting "Give
Back  Nights," where the restaurant, facilitated by Dave and Jess Shull,
would welcome local organizations, school groups, and  sports  teams  to
raise  money  and  enjoy  a delicious meal; a percentage of the business
from  that  evening  would  be  donated back to the organization as both
sides worked together to bring people in to support the group  for  that
evening; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over  the  next  few  years,  Dave  Shull and his business
partners would open Roma's Ristorante & Pizzeria in Sidney, New York  in
2009;  then,  he  purchased  one  the  longest family owned and operated
restaurants in the area, Nina's Pizzeria in September of 2011; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dave and Jess Shull got married in August of 2012  and  had
two  daughters;  they continue to illustrate their impressive work ethic
as both contribute to the successful endeavors of their  businesses  and
their community fundraising projects; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Their  restaurants  employing  an  average of 60 employees
yearly, Dave and Jess Shull have hosted Give Back Nights;  donated  food
to  organizations, first responders, and local sporting groups; provided
gift cards; and  sponsored  youth  sports  teams  and  provided  heavily
discounted food for sports teams travel meals and concession stands; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Nina's  has  been  an active major sponsor for many of the
yearly YMCA events and has taken on an active role  with  the  Toys  for
Tots  Campaign  over  the past eight years; in addition, Nina's hosts an
Annual Spaghetti & Meatball Dinner and  Movie  event  and  provides  and
donates  all  the food; partnering with The Barnes Brothers (Rich & Todd
Barnes), The Colonial Theatre, and The  Chawgo's,  the  restaurant  also
helps  raise  thousands of dollars to fill the Chawgo's bus full of toys
that are then distributed locally to families less fortunate right  here
in Chenango County; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Dave Shull been recognized for his commitment to community
by receiving the following awards: Catholic Charities of Chenango County
(Fundraising  Events  Acknowledgement),  2017;  Oxford  Fire  Department
Community  Support  Award,  April 2017; Commitment to Community Award on
behalf of The Norwich Business Improvement District, October  2017;  and
Commitment to Community Award on behalf of Commerce Chenango, 2020; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor and pay
tribute to those individuals whose commitment and creative talents  have
contributed to the entertainment and cultural enrichment of the citizens
of the State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Dave and Jess Shull upon the  occasion  of  their  designation  as
recipients  of  the  Distinguished  Citizen  Award  by  the Baden-Powell
Council, Boy Scouts of America on May 9, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Dave and Jess Shull.
