# Requires certain distribution centers to be equipped with electric vehicle charging stations

**Bill ID:** A9173
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenny Burgos
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A9173 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9173)

## Summary

Requires distribution centers which are 50,000 sq. ft. or more to be equipped with electric vehicle charging stations as determined by the secretary of state and NYSERDA.
