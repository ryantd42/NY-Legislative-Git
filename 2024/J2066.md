# Commending Kingsmen Lodge No.123 upon the occasion of hosting its 11th Annual Bowling Scholarship Fundraiser

**Bill ID:** J2066
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2066 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2066)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2066
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING Kingsmen Lodge No.123 upon the occasion
  of  hosting  its  11th  Annual  Bowling  Scholarship
  Fundraiser
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Kingsmen Lodge No.123 upon the  occasion  of  hosting  its  11th
Annual Bowling Scholarship Fundraiser on Saturday, April 20, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body recognizes the hard work, efforts,
and resilience that is shown in all  the  Kingsmen  Lodge  No.123  does,
including  uplifting  all  those  around,  with  a  special  emphasis on
strengthening the Black community through their acts of kindness; and
 
  WHEREAS, The dedicated Masons of New York  are  men  with  a  social
conscience  and  a  charitable  perspective,  who  are  also  known  as
Freemasons; they are part of a fraternal organization that shares common
moral ideals and esoteric values which are  based  on  the  doctrine  of
Brotherly Love, Relief and Truth; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  the  Kingsmen  Lodge No.123 is in direct
correlation to the efforts of its members,  whose  involvement  is,  and
always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, From time to time, this  Legislative  Body  takes  note  of
certain  altruistic  individuals  and  organizations  it  wishes  to
acknowledge for their philanthropic contributions to our  society  which
enhance  the  basic  humanity among us all; the Kingsmen Lodge No.123 is
one such organization; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Kingsmen  Lodge  No.123  upon  the occasion of hosting its 11th
Annual Bowling Scholarship Fundraiser; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Kingsmen Lodge No.123.
