# Establishes a committee of the Assembly to advise the Senate

**Bill ID:** E721
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E721 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/E721)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 721
 
BY: M. of A. Glick
 
  ESTABLISHING a committee of the Assembly to advise
  the Senate
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  committee  of two be appointed by the Speaker to
advise the Senate that the Assembly has adjourned its 2023 session  sine
die  and  has  again  convened  and is organized and ready to proceed to
business.
