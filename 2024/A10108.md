# Increases the length of the term of office and imposes term limits for members of the legislature

**Bill ID:** A10108
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Samuel Pirozzolo
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10108 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10108)

## Summary

Increases the length of the term of office of members of the legislature to six years; imposes term limits for members of the legislature.
