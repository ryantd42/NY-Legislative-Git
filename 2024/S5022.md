# Exempts livestock guardian dogs and herding dogs from license fees

**Bill ID:** S5022
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5022 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5022)

## Summary

Exempts livestock guardian dogs and herding dogs from license fees relating to the licensing, identification and control of dogs.
