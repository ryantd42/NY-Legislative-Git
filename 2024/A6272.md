# Relates to applying a special Medicaid rate for releasees in nursing homes

**Bill ID:** A6272
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6272 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6272)

## Summary

Relates to applying a special Medicaid rate for releasees in facilities or discrete units within facilities that provide long term nursing or medical services.
