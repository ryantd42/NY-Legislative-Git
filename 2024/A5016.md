# Relates to adding spina bifida to the definition of developmental disability

**Bill ID:** A5016
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5016 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5016)

## Summary

Relates to adding spina bifida to the definition of developmental disability in the mental hygiene law.
