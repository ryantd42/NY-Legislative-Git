# Authorizes the village of Round Lake, in the town of Malta and the county of Saratoga, to extend the lease of certain park lands in such village

**Bill ID:** S9068A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9068A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9068A)

## Summary

Authorizes the village of Round Lake, in the town of Malta and the county of Saratoga, to extend the lease of certain park lands in such village for up to an additional thirty years.
