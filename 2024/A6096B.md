# Establishes a pilot hospital medical debt relief program

**Bill ID:** A6096B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karines Reyes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6096B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6096B)

## Summary

Establishes a three-year pilot hospital medical debt relief program to provide hospital medical debt relief to eligible residents of the state utilizing a not-for-profit organization to identify, acquire and cancel medical debt of such eligible residents directly from health care providers in the state.

  246. MEDICAL DEBT RELIEF PROGRAM.
  § 245. DEFINITIONS. AS USED IN THIS ARTICLE, THE FOLLOWING TERMS SHALL

  1. "ELIGIBLE RESIDENT" MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL THAT  MEETS  THE  FOLLOWING

 RECEIPT OF HEALTH CARE SERVICES, WHETHER OR NOT SUCH OBLIGATION HAS BEEN
 REDUCED TO JUDGMENT.

  2.  WITHIN  AMOUNTS  APPROPRIATED  THEREFOR,  THE  COMMISSIONER  SHALL
 CONTRACT  WITH  A  NOT-FOR-PROFIT  ORGANIZATION  TO  IDENTIFY AND CANCEL
 MEDICAL DEBT OWED BY ELIGIBLE RESIDENTS TO HOSPITALS LOCATED WITHIN  THE
 STATE.  SUCH  NOT-FOR-PROFIT  ORGANIZATION SHALL ENTER INTO AGREEMENTS

 BALANCES STILL OWED, AND OTHER  INFORMATION  NECESSARY  TO  IDENTIFY  AN
 ELIGIBLE  RESIDENT.  THE  NOT-FOR-PROFIT  ORGANIZATION SHALL RETIRE SUCH

 OR RECEIPT AS A DONATION FROM A PARTICIPATING HOSPITAL AND THEN  CANCEL-
 LING SUCH DEBT. ANY SUCH PURCHASE OF MEDICAL DEBT SHALL BE FOR AN AMOUNT
 AT  OR  BELOW THE FAIR MARKET VALUE OF SUCH DEBT. ALL DATA SHARING SHALL
 COMPLY WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THE FEDERAL HEALTH  INSURANCE  PORTABILITY
 AND ACCOUNTABILITY ACT AND ANY OTHER APPLICABLE STATE OR FEDERAL LAW.
  3. PREFERENCE SHALL BE GIVEN TO ELIGIBLE RESIDENTS WHOSE DEBT IS EIGH-
 TEEN MONTHS OR OLDER.
  4. HOSPITALS THAT HAVE ENTERED INTO AGREEMENTS WITH THE NOT-FOR-PROFIT
 ORGANIZATION  FOR  PURPOSES OF THIS SECTION SHALL NOTIFY ITS PATIENTS OR

 SPECIFIC  MEDICAL DEBT HAS BEEN CANCELLED AND PROVIDE DOCUMENTATION, AND
 THAT THE DEBT CANCELATION DOES NOT LEAD TO INCOME  TAX  LIABILITIES  FOR
 PROGRAM  RECIPIENTS.  PROGRAM  RECIPIENT  LETTERS SHALL BE SIGNED BY THE
 COMMISSIONER OR THEIR DESIGNEE AND THE NOT-FOR-PROFIT ORGANIZATION.
  6. THE NOT-FOR-PROFIT ORGANIZATION SHALL CONDUCT AN  OUTREACH  PROGRAM
 TO  HAVE  DISCUSSIONS  WITH  HOSPITALS ABOUT THE BENEFITS OF THE MEDICAL
 DEBT RELIEF PROGRAM TO PATIENTS, COMMUNITIES AND TO THE HOSPITALS  THEM-
 SELVES.

 DEPARTMENT REPORT ANNUALLY ON THE PROGRESS AND SUCCESS  OF  THE  MEDICAL
 DEBT RELIEF PROGRAM ESTABLISHED PURSUANT TO THIS SECTION TO THE GOVERNOR

 THE DEPARTMENT, AND THE CHAIR OF THE SENATE COMMITTEE ON HEALTH AND  THE
 CHAIR  OF  THE  ASSEMBLY  COMMITTEE  ON  HEALTH.  SUCH  REPORT  SHALL BE
