# Prohibits the use of unmanned aerial vehicles to aid in hunting

**Bill ID:** S8863
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8863 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8863)

## Summary

Prohibits the use of unmanned aerial vehicles to search for, scout, locate, hunt, detect, or otherwise aid in the taking of a wild animal to which the hunting season applies.
