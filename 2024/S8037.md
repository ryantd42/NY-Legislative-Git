# Requires one voting member of the metropolitan transportation authority be a transit dependent individual; changes effectiveness

**Bill ID:** S8037
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8037 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8037)

## Summary

Requires one voting member of the metropolitan transportation authority be a transit dependent individual; changes the effectiveness of such provisions to 270 days after it shall have become a law.
