## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1484
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMENDING  Judge  Ralph  J.  Eannace  upon  the
  occasion  of  his  retirement  after  20  years  of
  distinguished service to Utica City Court
 
  WHEREAS, It is incumbent upon the people of the State of New York to
recognize  and  acknowledge outstanding individuals within our midst who
have made significant contributions to the quality of life therein; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is  justly  proud  to  commend  Judge
Ralph  J.  Eannace upon the occasion of his retirement after 20 years of
distinguished service to Utica City Court; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ralph J. Eannace attended schools in Utica,  New  York;  he
attended  Dartmouth  College,  and  later,  Cornell  University where he
obtained his Master of Public Administration and Doctor of Jurisprudence
degrees; and
 
  WHEREAS, Judge Ralph J. Eannace served as an Associate of Rogers and
Wells law firm (now Clifford Chance, LLC) in New  York  City  for  three
years  prior to his return to Utica where he joined the firm established
by his mother, Althea (Phillips) Eannace; during his 25 years in general
practice there, he developed the firm's expertise in municipal  law  and
elder law; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For three terms (1987-1993), Judge Ralph J. Eannace served
the New York State Assembly during which time  he  was  also  a  ranking
member  of  the  Committee  on  Housing  and Committee on Health, Mental
Retardation, and Developmental Disabilities; from 1997-2003,  he  served
as Oneida County Executive; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  2003,  Judge  Ralph  J.  Eannace  has fulfilled his
general duties for the City Court as  well  as  presiding  over  Utica's
Mental  Health  Court;  he has also served as a member of New York State
Office of Court Administration's Advisory Committee on Local Courts; and
 
  WHEREAS, During his distinguished career,  including  20  years  for
Utica  City  Court,  Judge Ralph J. Eannace has significantly influenced
numerous constituents of Oneida County, meriting the  reverence  of  his
colleagues and the members of the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body that those
dedicated individuals who have shown a long and sustained commitment  to
excellence  unquestionably  have  earned the recognition and applause of
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Judge  Ralph J. Eannace upon the occasion of his retirement and
to wish him well in all his future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Judge Ralph J. Eannace.
