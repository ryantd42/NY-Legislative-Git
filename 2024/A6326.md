# Relates to abolishing the office of coroner

**Bill ID:** A6326
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6326 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6326)
