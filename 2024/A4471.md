# Relates to providing a tax credit for the purchase of assistive technology devices

**Bill ID:** A4471
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Ramos
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4471 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4471)

## Summary

Provides a tax credit, up to five hundred dollars, for the purchase of assistive technology devices.
