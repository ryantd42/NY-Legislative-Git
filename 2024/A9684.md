# Grants court reporters peace officer status

**Bill ID:** A9684
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9684 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9684)

## Summary

Grants court reporters and senior court reporters of the unified court system peace officer status.
