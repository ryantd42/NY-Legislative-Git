# Relates to the investment of funds wagered on video lottery gaming for certain horse races; repealer

**Bill ID:** S5438
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5438 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5438)

## Summary

Relates to the investment of funds wagered on video lottery gaming for certain horse races.
