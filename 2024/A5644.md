# Relates to participation in games of bingo by minors

**Bill ID:** A5644
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5644 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5644)

## Summary

Permits participation in games of bingo by minors who are accompanied by an adult.
