# Congratulating the Pace University Women's Lacrosse Team upon the occasion of capturing the 2023 NCAA Division II Women's Lacrosse Championship on May 21, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1667
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1667 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1667)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1667
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Pace  University  Women's
  Lacrosse Team upon the  occasion  of  capturing  the
  2023  NCAA Division II Women's Lacrosse Championship
  on May 21, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  collegiate  sports;  this  Legislative  Body  commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  enhances  the  moral  and  physical
development of the young people of this State, preparing  them  for  the
future  by  instilling  in  them  the  value  of teamwork, encouraging a
standard  of  healthy  living,  imparting  a  desire  for  success  and
developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Pace University Women's Lacrosse Team upon the occasion of capturing the
2023 NCAA Division II Women's Lacrosse Championship on Sunday,  May  21,
2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and applause of their excited fans, the No. 3
ranked  Pace  University  Women's  Lacrosse  Team completed its historic
season with a National Championship after defeating  the  No.  1  ranked
West Chester University in a decisive 19-9 win; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Winning  the  first title in program history, the 19 goals
were the most ever scored in a DII Women's Lacrosse  championship  game,
and the Setters are the first NCAA collegiate team in Westchester County
to win a National Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school colors of blue and gold, family,
friends,  and  the  community  at  large  loyally  and  enthusiastically
supported the Setters throughout  their  journey  as  they  ended  their
season with an impressive overall record of 21-2; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Setters joined 47 teams from schools across the country
who had won National Championships to be recognized on the South Lawn of
the  White  House  as  part  of  College  Athlete  Day; the team had the
unforgettable opportunity to be invited to the White House and  to  meet
Vice President Kamala Harris, who shared a speech; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  lacrosse,  which  demands athletic
prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach Tricia Molfetta  and  her  expert
coaching staff worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team,
teaching  these outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable
both on and off the field; and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach Tricia  Molfetta  and  all  of  the  outstanding
athletes  on  the  Pace  University  Women's  Lacrosse Team have clearly
utilized dedication, determination and teamwork in providing  a  lasting
 
contribution  to  the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their
school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of the Pace University Women's  Lacrosse  Team:
Jennifer Sommer, Maddie Schubert, Mackenzie Carita, Jolie Urraro, Alyssa
Dvorznak,  Sasha  Kulo,  Delilah  Doyle,  Katie  Long,  Jordyn  Mariano,
Alexandra Witucki, Emma  Rafferty,  Sydney  Juvelier,  Aleya  Corretjer,
Alexandra  Quinn,  Audrey  Colucci,  Kayla  Conway,  Erin Bean, Margaret
Flocco, Natasha  Franchina,  Katie  Smaldone,  Lindsey  Gelderman,  Gigi
Genovese,  Nikki  Mottes,  Alyssa  Wolf, Katie Laico, Julianna Febbraio,
Angelina Porcello, Ashley Belfiore,  Payton  Durkin,  Abigail  O'Connor,
Samantha  Tanguay;  Assistant  Coach  Melissa  Alamprese; and Head Coach
Tricia Molfetta on their outstanding season and overall team record; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to the members of the Pace University Women's Lacrosse Team
and Head Coach Tricia Molfetta.
