# Relates to central business district tolls

**Bill ID:** S7728
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7728 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7728)

## Summary

Provides that no toll may be established, charged and collected on any vehicle entering the central business district in any calendar year for the first twelve times; provided, however, on and after entering the central business district for the thirteenth time, a toll shall be charged and collected on such vehicle.
