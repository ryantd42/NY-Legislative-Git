# Prohibits the sale of any vapor or tobacco products containing the substance ethyl menthane carboxamide

**Bill ID:** A4932
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4932 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4932)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of any vapor or tobacco products containing the substance N-Ethyl-p-menthane-3-carboxamide, which creates a cooling sensation similar to menthol.
