# Relates to enacting the "traveling with dignity act"

**Bill ID:** A8373CB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8373CB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8373CB)

## Summary

Provides for adult changing tables to be placed within an enclosed restroom facility or other similar private facility that is for use by persons with disabilities who need help with diapering at commercial places of public amusement and state-owned buildings open to the public.

 LAW.

 INSTALL AND MAINTAIN AT  LEAST  ONE  ADULT  CHANGING  ACCOMMODATION  FOR
 PERSONS  WITH A PHYSICAL DISABILITY THAT IS ACCESSIBLE TO INDIVIDUALS OF
 ANY GENDER WHEN THE FACILITY IS OPEN TO THE  PUBLIC,  AND  SHALL  ENSURE
