# Establishes a tax credit for pet adoption

**Bill ID:** A6390
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alec Brook-Krasny
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6390 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6390)

## Summary

Establishes a tax credit for pet adoption not to exceed three hundred fifty dollars.
