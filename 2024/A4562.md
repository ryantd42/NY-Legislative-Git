# Provides for loan forgiveness for mental health nurses

**Bill ID:** A4562
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4562 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4562)

## Summary

Provides for loan forgiveness for mental health nurses that make a commitment to practice in a "rural" tract or county.
