# Relates to abandoned vehicles

**Bill ID:** A9521A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9521A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9521A)

## Summary

Increases the dollar value of abandoned vehicles which may be seized by a local authority.
