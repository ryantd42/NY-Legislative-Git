# Relates to enforcement of immunization requirements; repealer

**Bill ID:** S8911
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8911 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8911)

## Summary

Removes kindergarten, elementary, intermediate and secondary schools from enforcement of immunization requirements.
