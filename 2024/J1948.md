# Commending the Nobles of Aleppo Temple No. 88 and the Daughters of Aleppo Court No. 140 upon the occasion of hosting their Joint Ball on April 13, 2024

**Bill ID:** J1948
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1948 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1948)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1948
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the Nobles of Aleppo Temple No. 88 and
  the Daughters of  Aleppo  Court  No.  140  upon  the
  occasion  of  hosting  their Joint Ball on April 13,
  2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  commend  those
illustrious  organizations  of  the  State  of  New York whose focus and
intent have made a substantial impact on the quality of  life  in  their
communities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the  Illustrious  Potentate Calvin L. Robinson of Aleppo Temple
No.  88 and the Illustrious Commandress Lady E. Williams of Aleppo Court
No.  140 upon the occasion of hosting their Joint Ball  at  the  Comfort
Inn & Suites Hotel in Cicero, New York, on Saturday, April 13, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine of
North and South America and Its Jurisdictions, Inc., is an  organization
which  embraces  the  fundamental  concepts  of the Masonic Order with a
broader spectrum of dispensing charity in the community; and
 
  WHEREAS, These two organizations will assemble to recognize the good
work bestowed upon the entirety of the State of New York,  gathering  in
harmony,  sisterhood,  and  brotherhood  to  share  ideas  and  hope and
continue to strengthen their bond with each other and their communities;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The most excellent Illustrious Potentate Calvin L. Robinson
is honored by the members of the Temple for accepting the responsibility
and privilege of leading them and  continuing  valuable  work,  bringing
honor and glory to Aleppo Temple, forever sealing his name in the sacred
archives; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  most elegant Illustrious Commandress Lady E. Williams
is honored by the members of the Court for welcoming her role of leading
them into perpetual virtue, forever sealing her legacy in the history of
the archives; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Nobles of Aleppo Temple No. 88  and  the
Daughters  of  Aleppo  Court  No.  140  is  in direct correlation to the
efforts of their dedicated leadership and members, whose involvement is,
and always has been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment,  an
unbridled  enthusiasm,  and  an uncompromising standard of excellence in
all endeavors on behalf of the organizations and  the  communities  they
serve; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  auspicious occasion presents an opportunity for this
Legislative Body to recognize and pay tribute to the  Nobles  of  Aleppo
Temple No. 88 and the Daughters of Aleppo Court No. 140; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body that when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
 
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Illustrious Potentate Calvin L. Robinson  of  Aleppo  Temple
No.  88 and the Illustrious Commandress Lady E. Williams of Aleppo Court
No.  140  upon  the  occasion of hosting their Joint Ball on April 13,
2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Ancient Egyptian Arabic Order Nobles Mystic Shrine of
North and South America and Its Jurisdictions, Inc.
