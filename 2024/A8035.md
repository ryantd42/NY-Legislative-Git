# Relates to electronic notification when prior authorization is required to fill a prescription

**Bill ID:** A8035
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8035 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8035)

## Summary

Requires insurers to provide electronic notification when prior authorization is required to fill a prescription if requested by the insured.
