# Authorizes Richard Lee Johnson to take the competitive civil service examination for the position of police officer in the village of Canastota and be placed on the eligible list for employment as such

**Bill ID:** A10516A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian D. Miller
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10516A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10516A)

## Summary

Authorizes Richard Lee Johnson to take the competitive civil service examination for the position of police officer and be placed on the eligible list for employment as a full-time police officer for the village of Canastota police department.
