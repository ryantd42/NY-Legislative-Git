# Prohibits the sale of vape products that resemble school supplies

**Bill ID:** S7882
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S7882 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7882)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of vape products that resemble school supplies, toys, or are packaged or advertised in any way to appeal to or target minors or the youth population.
