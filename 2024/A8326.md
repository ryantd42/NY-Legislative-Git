# Relates to disallowing county industrial development agencies from offering incentives in municipalities which have their own industrial development agency

**Bill ID:** A8326
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8326 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8326)

## Summary

Disallows county industrial development agencies from offering incentives in municipalities which have their own industrial development agency.
