# Reduces the tax on combative sport event ticket sales to three percent

**Bill ID:** A5254
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A5254 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5254)

## Summary

Reduces the tax on combative sport event ticket sales to three percent.
