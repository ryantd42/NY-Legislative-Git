# Directs the cannabis control board not to issue any license for dispensaries of cannabis until the cannabis control board reviews and closes businesses which are selling cannabis without a license

**Bill ID:** A9179
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Benedetto
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9179 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9179)

## Summary

Directs the cannabis control board not to issue any license, permit or registration for retail dispensaries of cannabis until the cannabis control board reviews and closes businesses which are selling cannabis without a proper license or permit.
