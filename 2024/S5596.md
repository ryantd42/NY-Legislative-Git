# Relates to requiring the notification of a public utility company's or municipality's customers when the supply rate increases by at least one hundred percent

**Bill ID:** S5596
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5596 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5596)

## Summary

Requires the notification of a public utility company's or municipality's customers when the supply rate increases by at least one hundred percent by posting on the utility company's website.
