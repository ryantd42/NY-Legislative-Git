# Increases the length of the term of office to four years for members of the legislature

**Bill ID:** A4897
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4897 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4897)

## Summary

Increases the length of the term of office to four years for members of the legislature.
