# Creates a volunteer firefighter training fund; appropriation

**Bill ID:** S8518
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8518 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8518)

## Summary

Creates a volunteer firefighter training fund; makes an appropriation relating therefor.
