# Requires all police vehicles to be equipped with bulletproof or bullet resistant glass

**Bill ID:** A6370
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6370 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6370)

## Summary

Requires all police vehicles to be equipped with bulletproof or bullet resistant glass on and after January 1, 2025; requires existing police cars to be retrofitted with bulletproof or bullet resistant glass.
