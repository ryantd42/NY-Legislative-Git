# Commending JRG Entertainment, LLC, and The Reset Talk Show upon the occasion of hosting their Third Annual Reset Appreciation Awards Gala on June 8, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2674
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2674 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2674)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2674
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  JRG  Entertainment, LLC, and The Reset
  Talk Show upon the occasion of hosting  their  Third
  Annual  Reset  Appreciation  Awards  Gala on June 8,
  2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend JRG Entertainment,  LLC,  and  The  Reset  Talk  Show  upon  the
occasion of hosting their Third Annual Reset Appreciation Awards Gala In
Brooklyn, New York, on Saturday, June 8, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Annual  Reset Appreciation Awards Gala celebrates its
third year of entertainment, education, unity, inspiration, and  change;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Reset  Talk Show, whose audience expanded from 18,000
listeners to 750,000 within only two years, is a dynamic platform  which
reports  on  current  events,  science,  faith,  culture,  and business,
enhancing  awareness  about  issues  sweeping  across  the  country  and
affecting our communities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Important information and briefings are broadcast from The
Reset  Talk  Show  to  765,000  faithful  listeners  within  numerous
communities every Friday; and
 
  WHEREAS,  JRG  Entertainment,  LLC,  is a dominant force in high-end
entertainment, boasting a remarkable legacy of more than  three  decades
of  producing  fashion shows and themed parties throughout the tri-state
area; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  an  unceasing  dedication  to  innovation,  JRG
Entertainment,  LLC,  and  The  Reset  Talk  Show have collaborated with
for-profit and not-for-profit entities in  New  York  for  the  past  30
years,  creating  inclusive  platforms  that  celebrate  the  talents of
models, fashion designers, makeup artists, hairstylists,  and  musicians
from all backgrounds; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success of JRG Entertainment, LLC, and The Reset Talk
Show is in direct correlation to the efforts  of  their  members,  whose
involvement  is,  and  always  has  been, characterized by an impressive
commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising  standard  of
excellence in all endeavors; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This Legislative Body honors these remarkable achievements
and proudly recognizes the commitment of The Reset  Talk  Show  and  JRG
Entertainment,  LLC,  to provide inclusive spaces which foster education
and growth, looking forward to the continuation of pioneering fresh  and
 
meaningful  experiences  that  enrich  lives  and  promote the values of
inclusivity, diversity, and cultural appreciation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
recognize and commend JRG Entertainment, LLC, and The  Reset  Talk  Show
upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  their  Third Annual Reset Appreciation
Awards Gala on June 8, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to JRG Entertainment, LLC, and The Reset Talk Show.
