## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1662
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  HONORING  Edward  Dibari  upon the occasion of his
  retirement after 25 years of  distinguished  service
  to the Oneida County Sheriff's Office
 
  WHEREAS,  Correctional officers from the State of New York put their
lives on the line daily for the citizens of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Edward  Dibari  upon  the  occasion  of his retirement after 25 years of
distinguished service to the Oneida County Sheriff's Office; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
honor  those  distinguished  officers  who  devote  themselves to public
service, demonstrating great courage and diligence in providing for  the
care  and  welfare  of  the citizens of their communities and this great
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Within every community of the State of New York  there  are
certain  individuals  who, by virtue of their commitment and dedication,
command  the  respect  and  admiration  of  their  community  for  their
exemplary  contributions  and  service  on  behalf of others; Correction
Officer Edward Dibari is one such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout his illustrious tenure, Edward Dibari epitomized
the role  and  accentuated  the  ideals  of  an  outstanding  correction
officer; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  testament  to  his dedication and character, Correction
Officer  Edward  Dibari  received  numerous  training  certificates  and
commendations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body that those
dedicated public servants who unselfishly  devote  their  lives  to  the
preservation  of  order  and the protection of others are worthy and due
full praise for their commitment and noble endeavors; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Edward  Dibari upon the occasion of his retirement after 25 years
of distinguished service to the Oneida County Sheriff's Office;  and  be
it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Edward Dibari.
