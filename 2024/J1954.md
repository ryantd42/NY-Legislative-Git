# Congratulating Gavin Mangano upon the occasion of winning a NYSPHSAA Division II State Wrestling Championship Title

**Bill ID:** J1954
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1954 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1954)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1954
 
BY: Senator PALUMBO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Gavin Mangano upon the occasion of
  winning  a  NYSPHSAA  Division  II  State  Wrestling
  Championship Title
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Gavin
Mangano  upon  the  occasion  of  winning  a  NYSPHSAA Division II State
Wrestling Championship Title on Saturday, February 24, 2024, at the  MVP
Arena in Albany, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  competitive matchup, Shoreham-Wading River freshman
Gavin Mangano won the NYSPHSAA Division II State Wrestling  Championship
Title  with a pin in 5:56 in his match against Jayden Duncanson of Tioga
High School at 131 pounds; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the  school's  colors  of  blue  and  gold,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Wildcat throughout his journey as he ended his season with
an impressive 51-1 season record and a 117-3 career record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  wrestling  which  demands athletic
prowess, endurance, strength, and mental focus, Head  Coach  Joe  Condon
worked  hard  to  hone the skills of this State Champion, Gavin Mangano,
teaching this outstanding athlete lessons which  will  prove  invaluable
both on and off the mat; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Gavin  Mangano  of  Shoreham-Wading  River High School has
clearly utilized dedication, determination, and teamwork in providing  a
lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of
his school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Gavin Mangano and Head Coach Joe Condon.
