# Creates a "purple alert system" for missing victims of domestic violence

**Bill ID:** A5152
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Khaleel Anderson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5152 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5152)

## Summary

Creates a "purple alert system" for missing victims of domestic violence.
