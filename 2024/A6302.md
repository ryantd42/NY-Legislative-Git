# Requires the state capitol and the legislative office building conduct active shooter drills

**Bill ID:** A6302
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6302 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6302)

## Summary

Requires the state police to conduct active shooter drills in the state Capitol and the legislative office building.
