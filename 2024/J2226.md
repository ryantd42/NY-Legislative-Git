# Honoring Tiffany and Al Palagonia upon the occasion of their designation for special recognition by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club on May 31, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2226
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2226 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2226)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2226
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  HONORING  Tiffany  and  Al  Palagonia  upon  the
  occasion  of  their  designation  for  special
  recognition by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club
  on May 31, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  The  unity  of  our  State  and  Nation  is built upon the
compassion of individuals such as Tiffany and Al  Palagonia  who  uphold
the  values  of  community  life  and  who, through their great actions,
epitomize the best of humanity; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Tiffany  and
Al  Palagonia  upon  the  occasion  of  their  designation  for  special
recognition by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club to be celebrated at
its 74th Annual Gala on Friday, May 31,  2024,  at  the  Creek  Club  in
Locust Valley, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Grenville  Baker Boys & Girls Club is a local affiliate of
Boys & Girls Clubs of America,  a  national  organization  dedicated  to
inspiring  and  enabling  all young people to realize their potential as
productive, responsible, and caring citizens and  community  leaders  of
tomorrow; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Tiffany  and  Al  Palagonia,  residents of the North Shore
since 2008, have been called upon to contribute  their  time,  resources
and expertise to countless civic and charitable endeavors, including the
Grenville  Baker  Boys & Girls Club, and have always given of themselves
unstintingly; and
 
  WHEREAS, As principals of Apollo Jets, the largest  private  charter
brokerage  in  the United States, Tiffany and Al Palagonia remain humble
and grounded with their friends and their community; their  devotion  to
helping  others  is contagious as they encourage their children, family,
and friends to get involved  by  volunteering,  supporting  and  hosting
special events such as the 3 on 3 basketball tournament; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Tiffany  and Al Palagonia love spending time out east with
their family, traveling, good food, and the New York Knicks; their  love
is apparent in all they do; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefit of others which Tiffany and Al Palagonia have
displayed throughout their lives; and
 
  WHEREAS, It has always been the objective of this  Legislative  Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment to the betterment of their communities, and it is the  intent
of  this  Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this tribute to
Tiffany  and  Al  Palagonia,  that  future  generations  may  know  and
appreciate  their  admirable character, their many benevolent deeds, and
the respect and esteem in which they  are  held  by  their  peers;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Tiffany and Al Palagonia upon the occasion  of  their  designation
for  special recognition by the Grenville Baker Boys & Girls Club on May
31, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Tiffany and Al Palagonia.
