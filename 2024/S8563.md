# Relates to establishing a public transportation expansion fund

**Bill ID:** S8563
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S8563 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8563)

## Summary

Establishes a public transportation expansion fund for the purpose of ensuring access to public transportation for publicly funded economic development projects.

 ADMINISTRATION,  THE  UNITED  STATES  DEPARTMENT  OF COMMERCE, MUNICIPAL

  §  88-E.  PUBLIC  TRANSPORTATION  EXPANSION  FUND.  1. THERE IS HEREBY
 ESTABLISHED IN THE JOINT CUSTODY OF THE  COMMISSIONER  OF  TAXATION  AND
