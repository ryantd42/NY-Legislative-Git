# Relates to penalties for defaced or obstructed license plates

**Bill ID:** A6198
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6198 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6198)

## Summary

Increases penalties for defaced or obstructed license plates; requires a one year suspension.
