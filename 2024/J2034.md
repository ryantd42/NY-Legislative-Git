# Honoring Rai Fernandez upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition by the White Plains Public Library at its 50-Year Celebration

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2034 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2034)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2034
 
BY: Senator MAYER
 
  HONORING  Rai  Fernandez  upon the occasion of his
  designation for special  recognition  by  the  White
  Plains Public Library at its 50-Year Celebration
 
  WHEREAS,  Individuals  who give of their time and energies and serve
the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration
and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rai Fernandez has given not only of his time  and  energies
but  also  of  his  competence,  intelligence,  and  leadership  and
consequently has been designated for special honor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to  honor  Rai
Fernandez  upon  the occasion of his designation for special recognition
by the White  Plains  Public  Library  at  its  50-Year  Celebration  on
Saturday, May 4, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rai  Fernandez has been called upon to contribute his time
and talents to countless civic and charitable endeavors and  has  always
given of himself unstintingly; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  the  entire period of his community service, a
period of constructive involvement, Rai Fernandez has stood constant  in
dignity and good grace; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rai Fernandez, Partner of Bermello Ajamil & Partners, Inc.,
is honored for his creativity, technical skill, and appreciation for the
magnificent  and  dignified  historic  building that serves as the White
Plains Public Library; and
 
  WHEREAS, With his team, Architect Rai Fernandez  has  revolutionized
most  of  the Library's spaces and enhanced the experience for thousands
of guests annually; and
 
  WHEREAS, Through a partnership  with  Bermello  Ajamil,  &  Partners
Inc.,  spanning  several  years,  Rai  Fernandez and his associates have
created spectacular renovations;  the  entire  first  floor,  the  Trove
(children's  area),  the Edge (teens' area), auditorium, and gallery are
all impressive showcases produced  by  the  collective  talent  of  this
global architecture and engineering firm; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rai  Fernandez  offers more than 30 years of experience in
managing, coordinating, designing,  and  masterfully  planning  numerous
award-winning  accomplishments,  including hotels, airports, residential
buildings, ports, and libraries; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by  an
individual  for  the benefit of others which Rai Fernandez has displayed
throughout his life; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Rai  Fernandez  upon  the occasion of his designation for special
 
recognition  by  the  White  Plains  Public  Library  at  its  50-Year
Celebration; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Rai Fernandez.
