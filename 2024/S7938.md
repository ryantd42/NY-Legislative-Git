# Directs the commissioner of the division of criminal justice services to study gender disparity in sentencing outcomes

**Bill ID:** S7938
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S7938 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7938)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of the division of criminal justice services to study gender disparity in sentencing outcomes and make a report to the governor and the legislature of his or her findings, conclusions and recommendations.
