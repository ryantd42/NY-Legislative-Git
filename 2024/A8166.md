# Establishes the first-time homebuyer assistance program within the state of New York mortgage agency

**Bill ID:** A8166
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8166 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8166)

## Summary

Establishes the first-time homebuyer assistance program within the state of New York mortgage agency to provide assistance to first-time homebuyers of certain residential units.
