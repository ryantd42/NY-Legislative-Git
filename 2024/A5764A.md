# Extends the one percent increased county sales tax for Putnam county

**Bill ID:** A5764A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Slater
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5764A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5764A)

## Summary

Extends the one percent increased county sales tax for Putnam county for two years.
