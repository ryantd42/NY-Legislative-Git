# Amends a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for a certain appropriation for the 2024-2025 state fiscal year

**Bill ID:** R2695
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R2695 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/R2695)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2695
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  Amending Senate Resolution 1396 of 2023 establishing
  a  plan  setting  forth an itemized list of grantees
  for a certain appropriation for the 2023-2024  state
  fiscal  year  for  services  and  expenses of senate
  priorities for housing initiatives, as required by a
  plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees with
  the  amount  to  be  received  by  each,  or  the
  methodology  for allocating such appropriation. Such
  plan  shall  be  subject  to  the  approval  of  the
  temporary  president  of the senate and the director
  of the budget and thereafter shall be included in  a
  resolution  calling  for  the  expenditure  of  such
  monies, which  resolution  must  be  approved  by  a
  majority  vote  of all members elected to the senate
  upon a roll call vote
 
  RESOLVED, That funds originally appropriated in 2023  pursuant  to
section  1  of  chapter  53  of  the  laws  of 2023 and reappropriated
pursuant to section 1 of chapter 53 of the laws of 2024  which  enacts
the  aid  to  localities,  local  assistance account, for services and
expenses of senate priorities  for  housing  initiatives.  Such  funds
shall be apportioned pursuant to a plan setting forth an itemized list
of grantees with the amount to be received by each, or the methodology
for  allocating  such appropriation. Such plan shall be subject to the
approval of the temporary president of the senate and the director  of
the  budget  and  thereafter shall be included in a resolution calling
for the expenditure of such monies, which resolution must be  approved
by  a  majority  vote of all members elected to the senate upon a roll
call vote, in accordance with the following schedule  which  shall  be
removed  from  or  added to the schedule originally approved in Senate
Resolution 1396 of 2023:
 
[North  Brooklyn  Coalition  of
  Neighbors Helping neighbors, Inc.
  40,000]
Neighbors Allied for Good Growth, Inc. 
  40,000
Cortland Housing Assistance Council Inc 
  15,000
Cortland Free Library 
  3,000
