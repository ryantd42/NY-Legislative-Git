# In N.Y. city, requires supportive housing to be disbursed throughout the boroughs of such city in proportion to the borough population

**Bill ID:** A6261
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6261 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6261)

## Summary

Requires, in cities having a population of one million or more, supportive housing be disbursed throughout the boroughs of such city in proportion to the borough population.
