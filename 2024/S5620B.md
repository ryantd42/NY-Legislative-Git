# Adds Doodletown Brook to the definition of inland waterways eligible for the local waterfront revitalization program

**Bill ID:** S5620B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S5620B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5620B)

## Summary

Adds Doodletown Brook to the definition of inland waterways eligible for the local waterfront revitalization program.
