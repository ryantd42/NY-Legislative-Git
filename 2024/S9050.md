# Extends eligibility for real property tax exemptions to veterans who served on a submarine or on classified missions during a period of war

**Bill ID:** S9050
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S9050 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9050)

## Summary

Extends eligibility for real property tax exemptions to veterans who served on a submarine or on classified missions during a period of war as documented by military service records.
