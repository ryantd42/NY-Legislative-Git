# Limits the amount of time to submit supporting documentation for major capital improvements

**Bill ID:** A5643
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Marcela Mitaynes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5643 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5643)

## Summary

Limits the amount of time to submit supporting documentation for major capital improvements; sets such time at one hundred and twenty days.
