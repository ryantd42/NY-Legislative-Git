# Prohibits state or local police from accepting military surplus equipment from the federal government

**Bill ID:** A5954
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Al Taylor
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5954 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5954)

## Summary

Prohibits state or local police from accepting military surplus equipment from the federal government.
