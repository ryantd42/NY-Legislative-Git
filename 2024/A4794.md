# Requires the installation of safety netting on all new and renovated bridges

**Bill ID:** A4794
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4794 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4794)

## Summary

Requires the installation of safety netting to prevent falling objects on all new and renovated bridges, including bridges that cross over other roadways, within the state.
