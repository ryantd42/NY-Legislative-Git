# Limits the amount such fine to be paid for violating the E-ZPass system for failing to pay a toll

**Bill ID:** S9909
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9909 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9909)

## Summary

Limits the amount of the fine to be paid for violating the E-ZPass system for failing to pay a toll to not exceed the amount of such unpaid toll.
