**Bill ID:** A6383

**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6383 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6383)

Establishes the crime of making a threat of mass violence.
