# Relates to providing that all court clerks of the unified court system shall be designated as peace officers

**Bill ID:** S7722
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7722 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7722)

## Summary

Provides that all court clerks of the unified court system shall be designated as peace officers.
