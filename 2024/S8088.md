# Allows municipalities to establish an adjustment percentage to be applied to the exemption percentage schedule for certain exemptions based upon permanent partial disabilities

**Bill ID:** S8088
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8088 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8088)

## Summary

Allows municipalities to adopt a local law or resolution to establish an adjustment percentage to be applied to the exemption percentage schedule for certain exemptions based upon permanent partial disabilities, after a public hearing.
