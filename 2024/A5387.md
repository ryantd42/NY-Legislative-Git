# Relates to bills containing provisions requiring appointments to be made by the legislative leaders

**Bill ID:** A5387
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5387 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5387)

## Summary

Requires certain bills which establish a commission, task force, board, council or any similar body shall have not less than one position appointed by each of the legislative leaders.
