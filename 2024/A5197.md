# Relates to the crime of luring a child

**Bill ID:** A5197
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5197 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5197)

## Summary

Relates to the crime of luring a child; adds additional elements; includes use of electronic communication.
