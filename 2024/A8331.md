# Authorizes the creation of a 9-8-8 license plate

**Bill ID:** A8331
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8331 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8331)

## Summary

Authorizes the creation of a 9-8-8 license plate in support of the 9-8-8 suicide prevention and mental health crisis hotline system.
