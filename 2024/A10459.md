# Relates to deadlines for special presidential ballot applications for military voters

**Bill ID:** A10459
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jo Anne Simon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10459 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10459)

## Summary

Provides that an application for a military ballot from a military voter shall be received at least fifteen days before an election in order to entitle the applicant to vote at such election.
