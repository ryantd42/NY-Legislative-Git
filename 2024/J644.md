# Commending Fleurimond Catering upon the occasion of hosting its 20th Annual Emerald Masquerade Ball on April 22, 2023

**Bill ID:** J644
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J644 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J644)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 644
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  Fleurimond  Catering upon the occasion
  of hosting its 20th Annual Emerald  Masquerade  Ball
  on April 22, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the
achievements of those individuals  and  businesses  which  substantially
contribute  to the economic vitality of their communities and the entire
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Fleurimond Catering upon the occasion of hosting its 20th Annual
Emerald Masquerade Ball on Saturday, April 22, 2023, at the Times Center
in New York, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Fleurimond  Catering  specializes  in  providing  the  most
elegant  catering  and  event planning services in New York City and the
Northeast; its cuisine selections  include  American,  French,  Italian,
Caribbean, and Latin; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  full-service  catering  company,  Fleurimond  Catering
provides event coordination, and decor and event planning services; they
also host many cooking  parties  throughout  New  York  City,  educating
individuals how to cook their delicious dishes; and
 
  WHEREAS, Through their catering services, Fleurimond Catering offers
the ability to travel the world via an eclectic sample of delicious food
offerings;  its elegant and professional event planning services provide
a caring and talented staff who will treat any event, large or small, as
the event that is most important to them; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under the luminous direction of  owner  Nadege  Fleurimond,
utilizing  her Haitian culinary background, Fleurimond Catering pulls in
flavors from Asia, Latin America, and Europe in all of their dishes; she
has catered for several celebrities and venues including Anheuser Busch,
Paul Weiss, New York City Hall, BET, The Colbert Report, Dr. Mehmet  Oz,
and Vivica Fox, just to name a few; and
 
  WHEREAS,  An author and successful entrepreneur, Nadege Fleurimond's
is the author of  Haiti  Uncovered  and  Taste  of  Solitude,  cookbooks
highlighting the food of Haiti; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition  to Fleurimond Catering, Nadege Fleurimond is
the  owner  of  BunNan,  a  restaurant  in  Brooklyn,  New  York,  which
highlights Haitian and Caribbean inspired plantain dishes; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Nadege  Fleurimond  has  been  featured  on HBO's "The Big
Brunch", Conde Nast Travel, Epicurious, Ebony Magazine, Food & Wine  and
many other publications; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the custom of this Legislative Body to applaud and
support the entrepreneurs of small businesses such as Nadege  Fleurimond
 
whose  dedication  and accomplishments contribute to the economic health
and vitality of the communities they serve in the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges this exceptional business  and  its  contributions  to  the
local  and  State  economies,  fully  confident that it will continue to
enjoy  the  sparkling  successes  it  has  experienced  thus  far;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Fleurimond Catering upon the occasion of hosting its 20th Annual
Emerald Masquerade Ball on April 22, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Nadege Fleurimond, Owner, Fleurimond Catering.
