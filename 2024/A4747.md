# Permits school board members who have served ten years to elect to receive state health insurance

**Bill ID:** A4747
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4747 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4747)

## Summary

Allows retired and current school board members who have served for ten years to receive state health insurance benefits.
