# Relates to the removal of weight and height restrictions on vehicles traveling from the Ogdensburg bridge south to the Port of Ogdensburg

**Bill ID:** A5138
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5138 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5138)

## Summary

Removes the weight and height restrictions on vehicles traveling south on the route from the Ogdensburg bridge to the Port of Ogdensburg.
