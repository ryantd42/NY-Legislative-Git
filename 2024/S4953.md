# Provides for the employment of school nurses for the inspection of pupils attending certain public schools

**Bill ID:** S4953
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4953 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4953)

## Summary

Provides for the employment of school nurses for the inspection of pupils attending certain public schools; relates to school districts and boards of cooperative educational services.
