# Relates to verification of an applicant's New York State driving history when used as a rating or underwriting factor for private passenger motor vehicle insurance

**Bill ID:** S8018
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8018 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8018)

## Summary

Limits third party verification of an applicant's driving history when used as a rating or underwriting factor for private passenger motor vehicle insurance to such applicant's New York State driving history; limits application to policies issued on and after the effective date.
