# Requires the board of elections to transmit election results to the clerk of certain cities, towns and villages

**Bill ID:** S9793
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S9793 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9793)

## Summary

Requires the board of elections to transmit election results to the clerk of cities, towns and villages for which it is the board of canvassers.
