## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2243
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING Kim Davies upon the occasion of her
  retirement,  as  Agency Program Aide, after 16 years
  of distinguished  service  to  the  New  York  State
  Division of Homeland Security in Oriskany, New York,
  on Thursday, May 9, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  seeks  to  acknowledge  those
individuals whose  professional  lives  and  civic  endeavors  serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Kim Davies is a graduate of SUNY Institute of Technology at
Utica-Rome (currently SUNY Polytechnic Institute) where she obtained her
Bachelor of Business and Public Management degree; and
 
  WHEREAS, For the past 16 years, Kim Davies, of Whitesboro, New York,
rendered  faithful, conscientious, and valuable service to the residents
of Oriskany and its neighboring communities; prior to that position, she
worked for Kelsey-Hayes Company in Utica, New York, for  more  than  six
years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  her  tenure  with  the  New York State Division of
Homeland  Security,  Kim  Davies  competently  administered  numerous
responsibilities, including monthly reconciliation of Net Card, updating
of  the  New  York  Statewide Financial System (SFS), review of purchase
requests to ensure  they  met  New  York  State  procurement  standards,
assistance  to  students and instructors, and administrative support for
training courses; and
 
  WHEREAS, Kim Davies was included in the "Team of the Quarter"  Award
for October-December in 2018; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  her  throughout  have  been  her  husband, Pete, her
daughter, four stepchildren, and three grandchildren, all of  whom  feel
privileged to be a part of her life and rejoice in her achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  her capacity as Agency Program Aide, Kim Davies served
with loyalty, honor, and distinction; in  her  official  acts,  she  was
governed  by  a  keen  sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of
human  problems,  assisting  others  in  need  and  providing  essential
services to members of her community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Kim Davies has earned the admiration, esteem, and affection
of her colleagues; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Kim Davies upon the occasion of her retirement,  as  Agency
Program  Aide,  after  16 years of distinguished service to the New York
State Division of Homeland Security in Oriskany, New York, on  Thursday,
May 9, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Kim Davies.
