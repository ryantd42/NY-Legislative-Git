# Congratulating Ousmane Duncanson upon the occasion of winning a New York State Public High School Athletic Association Division II Wrestling Championship Title

**Bill ID:** K2192
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Christopher Friend
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2192 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2192)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2192
 
BY: M. of A. Friend
 
  CONGRATULATING Ousmane Duncanson upon the occasion
  of  winning  a  New  York  State  Public High School
  Athletic  Association  Division  II  Wrestling
  Championship Title
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud to congratulate
Ousmane Duncanson upon the occasion of winning a New York  State  Public
High  School  Athletic  Association  Division  II Wrestling Championship
Title on February 23-24, 2024, at the MVP Arena in Albany, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ousmane Duncanson of Tioga High School  won  by  fall  over
Tavyn  MacDonell  of  Bolivar-Richburg  High  School in four minutes and
twenty-two seconds (4:22) in the NYSPHSAA Division II Wrestling  Finals;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Entering the tournament as the top seed, Ousmane Duncanson
became the first-ever  Tioga  High  School  three-time  New  York  State
Champion  in  the school's history, ending his high school career as the
winner of 91 consecutive matches and a flawless 34-0 record; and
 
  WHEREAS, With this historic win, Ousmane Duncanson also  became  the
first  Section IV wrestler to earn three New York State Championships in
his career since the feat was accomplished from 2018 to 2020; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the  school's  colors  of  blue  and  gold,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Tioga Tiger throughout his journey; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  wrestling  which  demands athletic
prowess, endurance, strength, and mental focus,  Head  Coach  Kristopher
Harrington  and  Assistant  Coaches  Jason  Bellis, Ryan MacWhinnie, and
David Crow worked hard to  hone  the  skills  of  this  State  Champion,
Ousmane  Duncanson, teaching this outstanding athlete lessons which will
prove invaluable both on and off the mat; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Ousmane  Duncanson  has  clearly  utilized  dedication,
determination,  and  teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the
spirit of excellence which is a tradition of his school; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Ousmane  Duncanson upon the occasion of winning a New York
 
State Public High School  Athletic  Association  Division  II  Wrestling
Championship Title; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Ousmane Duncanson, Head Coach Kristopher Harrington,  and
Assistant Coaches Jason Bellis, Ryan MacWhinnie, and David Crow.
