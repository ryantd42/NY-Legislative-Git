# Prohibit weapon promotion and sale on public property

**Bill ID:** A5377
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** MaryJane Shimsky
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5377 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5377)

## Summary

Prohibits the promotion, sale, transfer, exchange, giving or disposing of a weapon on property owned by the state or any political subdivision thereof.
