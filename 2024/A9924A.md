# Requires vehicle rental agreements instruct renters to remove personal information before returning the vehicle and permits rental vehicles to have decals informing the renter to remove such information

**Bill ID:** A9924A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chantel Jackson
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9924A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9924A)

## Summary

Requires vehicle rental agreements instruct renters to remove personal information before returning the vehicle; permits rental vehicles to have decals informing the renter to remove such information when returning the vehicle.
