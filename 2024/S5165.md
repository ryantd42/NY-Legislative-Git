# Provides grant funding, where available, for broadband services in non-competitive locations

**Bill ID:** S5165
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5165 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5165)

## Summary

Provides grant funding, where available, for broadband services in non-competitive locations through the ConnectAll deployment program.
