# Reduces the amount a utility can be assessed for costs and expenses of the department of public service and the public service commission from one percent to one-third of one percent

**Bill ID:** S5921
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5921 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5921)

## Summary

Reduces the total amount that can be charged from one percent to one-third of one percent.
