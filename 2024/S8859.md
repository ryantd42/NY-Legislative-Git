# Relates to assault in the second degree of an operator or crew of a passenger commuter ferry

**Bill ID:** S8859
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8859 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8859)

## Summary

Includes with intent to cause physical injury to an operator or crew of a passenger commuter ferry within assault in the second degree.
