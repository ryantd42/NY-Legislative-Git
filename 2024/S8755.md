# Enacts the New York artificial intelligence ethics commission act

**Bill ID:** S8755
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8755 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8755)

## Summary

Establishes the New York artificial intelligence ethics commission.

  ยง  106-C.  1.  NEW YORK ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ETHICS COMMISSION. THE
 NEW YORK ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ETHICS COMMISSION (THE "COMMISSION") IS
 HEREBY ESTABLISHED. THE COMMISSION SHALL SERVE AS A REGULATORY  BODY  TO
 ENSURE ETHICAL USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE (AI) WITHIN NEW YORK STATE
 AGENCIES AND PRIVATE ENTITIES OPERATING WITHIN THE STATE.
  2.  FOR  THE  PURPOSES OF THIS SECTION, THE FOLLOWING TERMS SHALL HAVE
 THE FOLLOWING MEANINGS:
  (A) "ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE"  OR  "AI"  SHALL  MEAN  A  MACHINE-BASED
 SYSTEM  THAT  CAN,  FOR  A  GIVEN  SET OF HUMAN-DEFINED OBJECTIVES, MAKE
 PREDICTIONS, RECOMMENDATIONS, OR DECISIONS INFLUENCING REAL  OR  VIRTUAL
 ENVIRONMENTS.
  (B)  "USE" SHALL MEAN THE DEVELOPMENT, DEPLOYMENT, AND OPERATION OF AN
 AI SYSTEM.
  (C) "CRIME" SHALL MEAN ANY OFFENSE RECOGNIZED BY NEW YORK STATE LAW.
  3. THE COMMISSION SHALL CONSIST OF NINE MEMBERS:  THREE  APPOINTED  BY
 THE  GOVERNOR;  TWO  APPOINTED BY THE TEMPORARY PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE;
 ONE APPOINTED BY THE MINORITY LEADER OF THE SENATE; TWO APPOINTED BY THE
 SPEAKER OF THE ASSEMBLY; ONE APPOINTED BY THE  MINORITY  LEADER  OF  THE
 ASSEMBLY, REFLECTIVE OF DIVERSE EXPERTISE IN AI TECHNOLOGY, ETHICS, LAW,
 AND PUBLIC POLICY.
  4. THE COMMISSION SHALL:
  (A) ESTABLISH ETHICAL GUIDELINES AND STANDARDS FOR THE DEVELOPMENT AND
 DEPLOYMENT OF AI TECHNOLOGIES;

 INATION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION BY AN AI SYSTEM  WITHOUT  THEIR  CONSENT

 MATERIAL LOSS THROUGH THE USE OF AN AI SYSTEM;
  (G)  CONDUCT  AI  RESEARCH  THAT  IS  HARMFUL  OR WITHOUT THE INFORMED
 CONSENT OF THE SUBJECTS;
  (H) INTENTIONALLY DISRUPT, DAMAGE, OR SUBVERSION OF AN  AI  SYSTEM  TO
 UNDERMINE ITS INTEGRITY OR PERFORMANCE; OR
  (I) PARTICIPATE IN THE UNAUTHORIZED USE OF SOMEONE'S PERSONAL IDENTITY
 OR DATA BY ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE SYSTEMS TO COMMIT FRAUD OR THEFT.
  7.  THE COMMISSION SHALL HAVE THE POWER TO IMPOSE PENALTIES, INCLUDING
 FINES AND INJUNCTIONS FOR VIOLATIONS OF ETHICAL  GUIDELINES  ESTABLISHED
 BY  THE  COMMISSION.  PENALTIES  FOR VIOLATIONS UNDER SUBDIVISION SIX OF
 THIS SECTION SHALL BE AS FOLLOWS:
  (A) WHERE HARM IS NON-ECONOMIC, A CIVIL PENALTY, INCLUDING INJUNCTIONS
 AND OTHER DAMAGES.
  (B) WHERE ECONOMIC HARM IS ESTABLISHED OR THERE IS A SYSTEMATIC BREACH
 OF PRIVACY, CRIMINAL OFFENSES SHALL BE PROSECUTED BY THE ATTORNEY GENER-
 AL.
  8. THE COMMISSION SHALL SUBMIT ANNUAL REPORTS  TO  THE  GOVERNOR,  THE
 TEMPORARY  PRESIDENT  OF  THE  SENATE,  AND  THE SPEAKER OF THE ASSEMBLY
 DETAILING ITS ACTIVITIES, AUDIT RESULTS, AND POLICY RECOMMENDATIONS.
