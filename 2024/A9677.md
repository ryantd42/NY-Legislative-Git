# Permits the use of portable electronic devices for making phone calls by incarcerated individuals

**Bill ID:** A9677
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Erik Dilan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9677 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9677)

## Summary

Permits the use of portable electronic devices for making phone calls as part of the telephone service system made available to incarcerated individuals.
