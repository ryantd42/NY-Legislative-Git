# Makes all costs and expenses of the department of public service and public service commission subject to appropriation by the legislature; repealer

**Bill ID:** A9280
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9280 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9280)

## Summary

Makes all costs and expenses of the department of public service and public service commission subject to appropriation by the legislature on the certification of the chairman of the department and upon the audit and warrant of the comptroller.
