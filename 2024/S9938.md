# Relates to requiring certain figures in solid waste removal contracts and invoices

**Bill ID:** S9938
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9938 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9938)

## Summary

Relates to requiring certain figures in solid waste removal estimates and final invoices including the total tare weight of the waste removal project; includes graduated penalties for offenses.
