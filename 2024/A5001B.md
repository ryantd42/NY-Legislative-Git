# Establishes scramble crosswalks leading to and from school buildings during times of student arrival and dismissal

**Bill ID:** A5001BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5001BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5001BB)

## Summary

Establishes scramble crosswalks leading to and from school buildings during times of student arrival and dismissal to ensure all vehicular traffic is stopped while pedestrians are crossing in the same intersection.
