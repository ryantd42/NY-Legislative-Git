# Provides for the sale of salvaged airbags; repealer

**Bill ID:** A6057AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6057AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6057AA)

## Summary

Provides for the sale of salvaged airbags; allows for the sale of non-deployed recycled airbags in New York, provided that such airbags meet required safety standards.
