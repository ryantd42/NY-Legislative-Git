# Relates to the adjournment sine die of the two thousand twenty-three Session of the Legislature

**Bill ID:** C719
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [C719 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/C719)

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 719
 
BY: M. of A. Peoples-Stokes
 
  CONCURRENT  RESOLUTION  OF  THE  SENATE AND ASSEMBLY
  relative to the adjournment  sine  die  of  the  two
  thousand twenty-three Session of the Legislature
 
  RESOLVED  (if  the  &HOUSE  concur),  That  the  two thousand
twenty-three Session of the Legislature adjourn sine die  on  Wednesday,
January  third,  two  thousand  twenty-four,  simultaneously  with  the
commencement of the two thousand twenty-four Legislative Session.
