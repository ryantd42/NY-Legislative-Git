# Authorizes the manufacture of beer, spirits, cider, wine and mead at the community college in Broome county and Cornell university

**Bill ID:** S8069
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8069 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8069)

## Summary

Authorizes the manufacture of beer, spirits, cider, wine and mead at the community college in Broome county and Cornell university, acting on behalf of the New York state college of agriculture and life sciences and the New York state agricultural experiment station.
