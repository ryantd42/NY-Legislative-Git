# Changes the name of the walkway over the Hudson bridge to the Eleanor Roosevelt Walkway

**Bill ID:** S5216
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5216 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5216)

## Summary

Changes the name of the walkway over the Hudson bridge to the Eleanor Roosevelt Walkway.
