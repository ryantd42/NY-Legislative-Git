# Prohibits the use of materially deceptive media in political communications when the information content provider that creates the communication has actual knowledge and without disclosing such use

**Bill ID:** A10260
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alex Bores
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10260 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10260)

## Summary

Prohibits the use of materially deceptive media in political communications when the information content provider that creates the communication has actual knowledge that such communication is materially deceptive without disclosing such use.
