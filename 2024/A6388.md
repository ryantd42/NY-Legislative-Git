# Establishes the crime of criminal use of public records

**Bill ID:** A6388
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alec Brook-Krasny
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6388 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6388)

## Summary

Establishes the class E felony of criminal use of public records for the intentional use of any public record in the course of or in furtherance of the commission of a crime.
