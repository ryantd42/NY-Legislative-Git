# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Westchester Purple Heart Highway"

**Bill ID:** S9301AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Passed Senate
**PDF:** [S9301AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9301AA)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state and local highway system constituting route thirty-five as the "Westchester Purple Heart Highway".
