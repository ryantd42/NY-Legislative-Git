# Relates to bonds and notes of the city of Yonkers

**Bill ID:** S8899
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8899 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8899)

## Summary

Extends certain provisions relating to bonds and notes of the city of Yonkers until June 30, 2025.
