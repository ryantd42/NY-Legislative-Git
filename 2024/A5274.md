# Relates to the residential parking system in the village of Cold Spring in the county of Putnam

**Bill ID:** A5274
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dana Levenberg
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5274 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5274)

## Summary

Adds streets and areas to the residential parking system in the village of Cold Spring in the county of Putnam.
