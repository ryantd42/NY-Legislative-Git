# Authorizes the use of owner-controlled and contractor-controlled insurance

**Bill ID:** A10362B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica P. Wallace
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10362B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10362B)

## Summary

Authorizes the use of owner-controlled and contractor-controlled insurance for certain construction projects; requires a report on the use of such insurance.
