# Prohibits the permanent or temporary housing of migrant populations in schoolhouses or on school grounds in districts outside of N.Y. city

**Bill ID:** A9184
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Durso
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9184 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9184)

## Summary

Prohibits the permanent or temporary housing of migrant populations in schoolhouses or on school grounds in districts outside of a city with a population of one million or more.
