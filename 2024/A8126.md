# Establishes the first-generation homebuyer assistance program

**Bill ID:** A8126
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8126 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8126)

## Summary

Establishes the first-generation homebuyer assistance program to provide a zero-interest, forgivable loan award in the amount of up to $15,000 to first-generation homebuyers.
