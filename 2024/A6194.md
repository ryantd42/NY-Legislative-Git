# Relates to removal of deceased voters and certain persons subject to forfeiture of the right to vote from the statewide voter registration list

**Bill ID:** A6194
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6194 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6194)

## Summary

Requires bi-monthly reporting on deceased voters and certain persons subject to forfeiture of the right to vote in order to accurately maintain the statewide voter registration list.
