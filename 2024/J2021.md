# Congratulating Mr. and Mrs. Edward Kahil upon the occasion of celebrating their 50th Wedding Anniversary

**Bill ID:** J2021
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2021 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2021)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2021
 
BY: Senator OBERACKER
 
  CONGRATULATING  Mr. and Mrs. Edward Kahil upon the
  occasion  of  celebrating  their  50th  Wedding
  Anniversary
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  takes  great  pleasure  in paying
tribute to those flourishing marriages that have stood the test of  time
to serve as a paradigm for present and future generations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the sense of this Legislative Body to congratulate
Mr. and Mrs. Edward Kahil upon the occasion of  celebrating  their  50th
Wedding Anniversary on Saturday, June 15, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Janette Giuliano, daughter of Dino and Florence Giuliano of
West  Shokan  married  Edward Kahil, son of Richard and Blanche Kahil of
Mount Tremper on June 15, 1974,  at  St.  Francis  de  Sales  Church  in
Phoenicia, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Edward and Janette Kahil are the proud parents of Jeremiah
and Thomas, and loving  grandparents  of  Grace,  Joseph,  Lillian,  and
Francesca; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Mr.  and  Mrs. Edward Kahil are, and always have been, a
tower of strength, support, understanding, and limitless love for  their
family; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Their devotion, caring, sensitivity, and responsiveness to
their family, friends, and all who know  them  are  their  hallmark  and
tradition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Mr.  and Mrs. Edward Kahil have lived and continue to live
their lives with great dignity and genuine grace, always demonstrating a
deep and continuing concern for human values and ideals,  and  in  doing
so, have inspired others to do the same; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  meaningful  occasion is the result of the love, hard
work, and spirit this couple has invested in their marriage; and
 
  WHEREAS, They have passed these same values  to  their  loved  ones,
producing  a  family that is close-knit and supportive of its individual
members, public-minded in its relations to the community, and  committed
to the ideals of citizenship upon which this Nation was founded; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Mr.  and Mrs. Edward Kahil have touched the lives of their
family and many friends through the shining example of  their  marriage,
which  is  a testament to the devotion and admiration which they possess
for one another; and
 
  WHEREAS, The longevity of  this  enduring  marriage  truly  marks  a
milestone in life's journey, an event of personal triumph and joy; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this Legislative Body that when an
occasion of such importance is brought to our attention, the same should
 
be memorialized by us for the edification and emulation of others;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Mr. and Mrs. Edward Kahil upon the occasion of  celebrating
their 50th Wedding Anniversary; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Mr. and Mrs. Edward Kahil.
