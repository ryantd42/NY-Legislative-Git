# Authorizes funding that would include services and expenses intended to result in improved compliance with fair housing laws

**Bill ID:** S9660AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9660AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9660AA)

## Summary

Authorizes funding that would include services and expenses intended to result in improved compliance with fair housing laws such as intake and assessment of cases in which fair housing rights may have been violated and enforcement action.
