# Relates to notices for certain payments in lieu of taxes

**Bill ID:** A5969
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5969 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5969)

## Summary

Exempts notices for certain payments in lieu of taxes in New York city.
