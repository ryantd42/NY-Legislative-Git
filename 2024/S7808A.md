# Establishes experiential learning opportunities in the state university of New York and the city university of New York

**Bill ID:** S7808AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S7808AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7808AA)

## Summary

Establishes experiential learning opportunities in the state university of New York and the city university of New York.
