# Prohibits state funding of any college if instructional staff advocates or engages in activities on campus in support of a terrorist organization

**Bill ID:** A10484
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Eric Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10484 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10484)

## Summary

Prohibits state funding of any college if instructional staff promotes, encourages, advocates or engages in activities on campus in support of a terrorist organization as defined in federal statute.
