# Relates to including used vehicles in the drive clean rebate program

**Bill ID:** A5257
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5257 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5257)

## Summary

Relates to including used vehicles in the drive clean rebate program; provides that such rebate for a used vehicle shall not exceed one thousand dollars.
