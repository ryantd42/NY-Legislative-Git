# Extends provisions of law extending the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) benefit for public employee death benefits

**Bill ID:** A10524
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10524 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10524)

## Summary

Extends provisions extending the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) benefit for public employee death benefits until July 31, 2025.
