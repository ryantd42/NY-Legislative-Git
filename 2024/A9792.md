# Relates to making a city school district in N.Y. city eligible for transitional aid for charter school payments

**Bill ID:** A9792
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9792 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9792)

## Summary

Relates to making a city school district in a city having a population of one million or more eligible for transitional aid for charter school payments.
