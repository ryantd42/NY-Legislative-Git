# Relates to the ability of an individual collecting unemployment to obtain part-time work

**Bill ID:** A5589
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5589 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5589)

## Summary

Relates to allowing claimants collecting unemployment to obtain part-time work without losing their unemployment benefits.
