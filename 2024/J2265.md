## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2265
 
BY: Senator MYRIE
 
  HONORING  Earn Your Leisure for its service to the
  Bronx and to all of New York State
 
  WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to recognize  the
work  of  organizations  with  a singular vision who effect enduring and
positive change in their communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Earn  Your  Leisure  for its service to the Bronx and to all of New York
State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Founded  by  Rashad  Bilal,  Troy  Millings,  and  Michael
McDonald,  the platform offers deep dives into various financial topics,
shedding light on lessons from high-profile individuals  and  real-world
business experiences; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Earn  Your  Leisure  (EYL) Network is a revolutionary
network that gives rise to emerging  and  established  content  creators
from  the  world  of  business,  finance,  and  entrepreneurship  whose
perspective, expertise, and in-depth insight have been  undervalued  and
overlooked; and
 
  WHEREAS,  EYL  has a community of over 1,000,000 followers on social
media and 1,000,000 YouTube subscribers; and
 
  WHEREAS, At its  core  The  Earn  Your  Leisure  Network's  ultimate
purpose  is  to  build  as  we  climb  as  a  community,  highlighting
collaboration over  competition;  the  network  currently  features  six
shows: Market Mondays, Inside The Vault, Inner Wealth, Girl CEO Podcast,
Rants and Gems Real Estate Podcast, and High Level Conversations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  EYL  hosts live events across the globe which includes the
biggest financial literacy  festival  in  the  world  "Invest  Fest,"  a
first-of-its-kind,  in-person  experience  to  combine  investing,
entrepreneurship, pop culture, and entertainment  in  a  3-day  festival
setting; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Earn  Your  Leisure  has  garnered  significant  attention
through their conversations with  business  titans  like  Steve  Harvey,
Tyler Perry, Robert F. Smith, Mark Cuban, DJ Khaled, and many more; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Earn Your Leisure for its service to the Bronx and to all  of  New
York State; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Earn Your Leisure.
