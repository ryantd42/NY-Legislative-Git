# Requires the division of housing and community renewal, with the state office for the aging, to establish a database of available senior housing; requires the office for the aging to maintain such database

**Bill ID:** A9878
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amanda Septimo
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9878 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9878)

## Summary

Requires the division of housing and community renewal, in collaboration with the New York state office for the aging, to establish a publicly accessible online database of available senior housing units; requires the New York state office for the aging to maintain the senior housing unit database.
