# Paying tribute to the 1956-58 State Championship Cross Country Teams upon the occasion of being honored as part of a special Legacy Tribute during the 26th Annual Schenectady CSD Athletic Hall of Fame Dinner

**Bill ID:** J2336
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2336 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2336)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2336
 
BY: Senator TEDISCO
 
  PAYING  tribute  to the 1956-58 State Championship
  Cross Country  Teams  upon  the  occasion  of  being
  honored  as  part of a special Legacy Tribute during
  the 26th Annual  Schenectady  City  School  District
  Athletic  Hall  of  Fame  and  Reunion  Dinner  on
  September 16, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
pay tribute to those outstanding athletes who inspired and brought pride
to  their  school  and  community  through  outstanding  achievements in
competition, attaining unprecedented success and the  highest  level  of
personal achievement; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud  to  pay
tribute  to  the 1956-58 State Championship Cross Country Teams upon the
occasion of being honored as part of a special Legacy Tribute during the
26th Annual Schenectady City School District Athletic Hall of  Fame  and
Reunion  Dinner  on  Monday,  September 16, 2024, at the Event Center at
Rivers Casino & Resort in Schenectady, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Excellence  and  success  in  competitive  sports  can  be
achieved  only  through  strenuous  practice, teamwork, and team spirit,
nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  enhances  the  moral  and  physical
development of the youth of this State, preparing them for the future by
instilling  in  them  the  value  of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, imparting a desire for success, and developing  a  sense
of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Coached  by  Schenectady  Hall of Famer Bill Leonard, Nott
Terrace High School won State titles in both 1956  and  1957,  and  Mont
Pleasant  High under the leadership of another Schenectady Athletic Hall
of Fame member Ray Vacca School won the state crown in 1958; and
 
  WHEREAS, The 1956 Nott Terrace team won the Proctor Invitational and
finished second in the NYU Interscholastic  Run  prior  to  winning  its
season State title at Baldwinsville; and
 
  WHEREAS, The 1957 Nott Terrace cross-country team once again won the
Proctor  Invitational  and finished second at the Grout Run (behind city
rival Mont Pleasant), and finished 2nd at the St. John's Run  &  3rd  in
the  NYU  Run,  and concluded its season by winning a second consecutive
State title on Long Island; and
 
  WHEREAS, The 1958 Mont Pleasant cross-country team took the  Proctor
Run,  Grout  Run,  and New York University Interscholastic Cross-Country
Meet prior to winning their State cross-country meet at Colgate; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  and  teams of such outstanding athletic accomplishments are
 
brought to our attention, they should be recognized by all the  citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the 1956 and 1957 Nott Terrace Cross-Country Team  members:
Paul  Landofo, Jason Nogee, John O'Sullivan, Peter Runge, Pete Schuyler,
Phil Spencer, Ray Westendo, John Abele, Mike Cotton, Tom  Finnigan,  Dan
Hackett,  James  Lloyd,  Francis  Mills  and Mark Youmans; and 1958 Mont
Pleasant Cross-Country Team members: Tom Farry, Joe Hartley, Ron  Horan,
Mike Keigher, Bob Lewkowicz, Bob Potter and Jim Scott on their sustained
excellence and overall team record; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members and coaches of the 1956-58 State Championship
Cross Country Teams.
