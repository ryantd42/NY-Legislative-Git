# Relates to the sale of tonic water, bitters and maraschino cherries at liquor stores

**Bill ID:** A10023
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10023 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10023)

## Summary

Provides for the sale of tonic water, bitters and maraschino cherries at liquor stores.
