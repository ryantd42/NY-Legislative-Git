# Relates to ground lease contracts

**Bill ID:** S9721A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9721A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9721A)

## Summary

Relates to ground lease contracts; allows for the extension or renewal of such contracts prior to the expiration of such contracts.
