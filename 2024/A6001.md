# Provides for funding for the consolidated local street and highway improvement program and the COVID-19 pandemic small business recovery grant program

**Bill ID:** A6001
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6001 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6001)

## Summary

Provides for funding for the consolidated local street and highway improvement program and the COVID-19 pandemic small business recovery grant program from a percentage of online sales taxes.
