# Relates to sales and compensating use taxes in the county of Livingston

**Bill ID:** A5768A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Marjorie Byrnes
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5768A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5768A)

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Livingston to impose an additional one percent sales tax.
