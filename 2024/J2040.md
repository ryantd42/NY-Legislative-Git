# Commending Hi-12 Club of African 459 Lodge No. 63 upon the occasion of hosting its World's Fair Marina Banquet on May 11, 2024

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2040 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2040)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2040
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  Hi-12 Club of African 459 Lodge No. 63
  upon the occasion of hosting its World's Fair Marina
  Banquet on May 11, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Hi-12 Club of African 459 Lodge No.  63  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its World's Fair Marina Banquet on Saturday, May 11, 2024, at 1
Marina Road, Corona, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Led by Ex. Officious William E. Boyd and supported by  Most
Worshipful  Gregory  R. Smith Jr., the World's Fair Marina Banquet marks
the beginning of Hi-12  Club  of  African  459  Lodge  No.  63's  spring
fundraising in the name of excellent charitable works; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Hi-12  Club  of  African  459 Lodge No. 63 works to uplift
those in their sphere of influence and to strengthen the Black community
by their acts of kindness and the role they play in showing young people
a good example; and
 
  WHEREAS, The dedicated Masons of Hi-12 Club of African 459 Lodge No.
63 are men with a social conscience and a  charitable  perspective,  who
are  also known as Freemasons; they are part of a fraternal organization
that shares common moral ideals and esoteric values which are  based  on
the doctrine of Brotherly Love, Relief and Truth; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of Hi-12 Club of African 459 Lodge No. 63 is in
direct  correlation  to  the  efforts of its leadership and its members,
whose  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an
impressive  commitment,  an  unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising
standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf  of  the  organization
and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and commend
the members of Hi-12 Club of African 459 Lodge No. 63, Free and Accepted
Masons,  past and present, who have labored so conscientiously to secure
and improve the quality of  life  for  the  community  it  serves;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Hi-12 Club of African 459 Lodge No.  63  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its  World's  Fair  Marina  Banquet  on May 11, 2024; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Hi-12 Club of African 459 Lodge No. 63, Free and Accepted
Masons of the State of New York; Ex. Officious William E. Boyd; and Most
Worshipful Gregory R. Smith Jr.
