# Requires passenger cabs be inspected for carbon monoxide levels and requires information on warning signs of such to be distributed with each operator's license

**Bill ID:** A256
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Latrice Walker
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A256 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A256)

## Summary

Requires passenger cabs be inspected for unsafe carbon monoxide levels; requires information on the warning signs of carbon monoxide poisoning to be distributed with each operator's license, whether issued or renewed.
