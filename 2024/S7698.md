# Relates to requiring insurers participating in the NY state of health marketplace to offer at least one dental plan for individuals age 65 and older

**Bill ID:** S7698
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7698 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7698)

## Summary

Requires insurers participating in the NY state of health marketplace to offer at least one dental plan for individuals age 65 and older.
