# Congratulating the Saratoga Springs Rotary Club upon the occasion of hosting its 50th Annual Home & Lifestyle Show on March 2-3, 2024

**Bill ID:** J1790
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1790 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1790)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1790
 
BY: Senator TEDISCO
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Saratoga  Springs Rotary Club
  upon the occasion of hosting its 50th Annual Home  &
  Lifestyle Show on March 2-3, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize that
the quality and character of life in the communities of New  York  State
are  reflective  of  the  concerned  and  dedicated  efforts  of  those
organizations that distinguish themselves  in  their  endeavors  and  in
service to their community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and fully in accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  the  Saratoga  Springs  Rotary  Club  upon the occasion of
hosting its 50th Annual Home & Lifestyle Show on  March  2-3,  2024,  at
Saratoga City Center in Saratoga Springs, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rotary  International  is  dedicated to the development of
fellowship and understanding among business  and  professional  leaders,
the promotion of community-betterment endeavors and of high standards in
business  and  professional  practices,  and  the  advancement  of
international understanding, goodwill, and peace; and
 
  WHEREAS, Every year, the Rotary Club of  Saratoga  Springs  hosts  a
Home  &  Lifestyle  Show,  with  proceeds being distributed to community
non-profit  organizations,  youth  scholarships,  and  international
humanitarian aid projects; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  members  of  the  Rotary Club of Saratoga Springs are
business and community leaders with  strong  values  of  fellowship  and
service.  working  together  to make a difference in our community and
around the world; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rotarians conduct  themselves  and  their  businesses  with
honesty,  integrity,  friendliness,  and fairness, welcoming new members
with similar values to join them in making a difference; and
 
  WHEREAS, A variety of businesses will exhibit on the main  floor  of
the  Saratoga  Home  & Lifestyle Show, including categories such as home
improvement, landscaping, d√öcor and fine finishes, heating and plumbing,
pools, financial and professional services, and many more; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Demonstrating  a  showcase  for  artistic  endeavors,  the
Saratoga  Home  & Lifestyle Show Art Show is also on the main floor with
more  than  25  artisans  showing  photography,  painting,  woodworking,
jewelry, and wine, with items available for purchase; and
 
  WHEREAS, Adirondack Trust Company, Allerdice ACE Hardware, Granite &
Marble Works, Saratoga.com, Saratoga Today, Cudney's Cleaners, Stewart's
Shops,  Sterling  Manor  Financial, LLC, Gamma Foods, Saratoga Builder's
Association and The Wesley Community all serve as  outstanding  sponsors
for  the  Saratoga  Home and Lifestyle Show, making this event a rousing
success year in and year out; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  and  organizations  host  events which add significantly to
their respective communities are brought to our attention,  they  should
be  celebrated  and recognized by all the citizens of the great State of
New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the Saratoga Springs  Rotary  Club  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting its 50th Annual Home & Lifestyle Show on March 2-3, 2024; and be
it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Saratoga Springs Rotary Club.
