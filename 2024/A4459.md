# Relates to excavation of underground facilities; expands the definition of "excavation" for the purposes of the protection of underground facilities

**Bill ID:** A4459
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4459 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4459)

## Summary

Expands the definition of "excavation" for the purposes of the protection of underground facilities.
