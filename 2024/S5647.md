# Relates to disqualification from receiving unemployment benefits when the claimant's employer maintained, refused or failed to cure a health or safety condition that made the environment unsuitable

**Bill ID:** S5647
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5647 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5647)

## Summary

Relates to disqualification from receiving benefits for separation from employment in cases where the claimant's employer maintained or refused to cure a health or safety condition that made the environment unsuitable.
