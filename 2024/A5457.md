# Authorizes local school districts and municipalities to determine and establish school speed limits

**Bill ID:** A5457
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5457 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5457)

## Summary

Authorizes local school districts and municipalities to determine and establish school speed limits.

 established  a  higher maximum speed limit than the fifty-five miles per
