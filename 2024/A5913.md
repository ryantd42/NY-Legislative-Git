# Extends the authority of NYSARC, Inc., to maintain bonds

**Bill ID:** A5913
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rebecca Seawright
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5913 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5913)

Extends the authority of NYSARC, Inc., to maintain bonds until 2028.
