# Adds xylazine to the depressants designated as controlled substances

**Bill ID:** A8395BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8395BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8395BA)

## Summary

Adds xylazine to the depressants designated as controlled substances with the exception of use in cattle or other nonhuman species if approved by the federal food and drug administration.
