# Establishes the New York title guaranty program and the New York title guaranty corporation; repealer

**Bill ID:** A4500
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4500 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4500)

## Summary

Establishes the New York title guaranty program to provide title insurance for real property within the state; establishes the New York title guaranty corporation to implement the New York title guaranty program.
