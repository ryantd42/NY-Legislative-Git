# Relates to employer contributions to the unemployment insurance fund and establishes the unemployment insurance solvency reserve fund

**Bill ID:** A4657
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4657 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4657)

## Summary

Provides for the rate of employer contributions to the unemployment insurance fund in the 2024 fiscal year; establishes the unemployment insurance solvency reserve fund.
