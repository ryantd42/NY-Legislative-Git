# Relates to pre-arranged for-hire vehicle transportation services

**Bill ID:** A5922
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5922 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5922)

## Summary

Relates to pre-arranged for-hire vehicle transportation services and the maximization of energy efficiency, universal design and accessibility by persons with disabilities.
