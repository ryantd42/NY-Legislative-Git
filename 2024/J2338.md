## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2338
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Most  Excellent Prince Hall Grand
  Chapter of Holy Royal Arch Masons of  the  State  of
  New York & Jurisdiction upon the occasion of hosting
  its 66th Annual Grand Assembly on October 3, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy Royal Arch
Masons of the State of New York &  Jurisdiction  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its 66th Annual Grand Assembly on Thursday, October 3, 2024, at
the Crowne Plaza/Albany-The Desmond Hotel in Albany, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The dedicated Masons of  the  Most  Excellent  Prince  Hall
Grand  Chapter  of  Holy  Royal  Arch  Masons of the State of New York &
Jurisdiction  are  men  with  a  social  conscience  and  a  charitable
perspective,  who  are  also  known  as  Freemasons;  they are part of a
fraternal organization that shares  common  moral  ideals  and  esoteric
values  which  are  based on the doctrine of Brotherly Love, Relief, and
Truth; and
 
  WHEREAS, The men of this ancient and most respected order  of  Royal
Arch  Masons  operate  under the direction and most serene leadership of
Most Worshipful Gregory R Smith Jr, Grand Master New York and in harmony
with M.I Marvin E. Williams, (Kych) Thrice,  Illustrious  Grand  Master,
Most  Excellent  Rex  H.  Garret  Grand  High  Priest; there has been 66
successful years of excellence, and the  sustained  meeting  of  Masters
through the trials and tribulations of this Nation and State; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter
of  Holy Royal Arch Masons of the State of New York & Jurisdiction is in
direct correlation to the efforts of its  leadership  and  its  members,
whose  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an
impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising
standard  of  excellence  in all endeavors on behalf of the organization
and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and commend
the members of the Most Excellent Prince  Hall  Grand  Chapter  of  Holy
Royal  Arch  Masons  of  the  State of New York & Jurisdiction, Free and
Accepted Masons, past and present, who have labored  so  conscientiously
to  secure  and improve the quality of life for the community it serves;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy Royal Arch
Masons of the State of New York &  Jurisdiction  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its  66th  Annual  Grand Assembly on October 3, 2024; and be it
further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the of Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter  of  Holy
Royal  Arch  Masons  of  the  State  of New York & Jurisdiction Free and
Accepted Masons of the State of New York.
