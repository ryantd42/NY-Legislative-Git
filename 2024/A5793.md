# Extends audit authority over industrial development authorities and local development corporations to each county comptroller

**Bill ID:** A5793
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Edward Flood
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5793 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5793)

## Summary

Authorizes county comptrollers to audit industrial development authorities and local development corporations and to audit or review an audit prior to the renewal or renegotiation of a PILOT agreement by such entities.
