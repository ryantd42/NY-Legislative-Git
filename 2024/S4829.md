# Extends, until December 31, 2024, the authorization of residential property owners in high risk brush fire areas on Staten Island to cut and remove reeds

**Bill ID:** S4829
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S4829 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4829)

## Summary

Extends, from December 31, 2023 to December 31, 2024, the expiration of the authorization granted to residential property owners in high risk brush fire areas on Staten Island to cut and remove reeds from their property.
