# Relates to allowing snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles to be registered as farm vehicles

**Bill ID:** S5737
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5737 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5737)

## Summary

Allows snowmobiles and all-terrain vehicles to be registered as farm vehicles upon approval by the commissioner.
