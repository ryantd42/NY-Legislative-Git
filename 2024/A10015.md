# Relates to the use of telematics systems by automobile insurers

**Bill ID:** A10015
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Charles Lavine
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10015 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10015)

## Summary

Requires certain disclosures by automobile insurers relating to the use of telematics systems in determining insurance rates and/or discounts.
