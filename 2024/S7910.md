# Expands the definition of "public body"

**Bill ID:** S7910
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7910 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7910)

## Summary

Relates to expanding the definition of "public body" to include any entity consisting of two or more members created by executive order of the governor.
