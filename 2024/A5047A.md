# Relates to the sale of a non-indemnity legal service plan

**Bill ID:** A5047AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Charles Lavine
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5047AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5047AA)

## Summary

Provides that the sale of a non-indemnity legal service plan shall not constitute doing an insurance business in this state; makes related provisions.
