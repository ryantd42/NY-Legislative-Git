# Adds hazardous air quality to the state definition of a disaster and includes air quality emergency preparedness in local comprehensive emergency management plans

**Bill ID:** S7932
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S7932 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7932)

## Summary

Adds hazardous air quality to the state definition of a disaster; requires explicit inclusion of air quality emergency preparedness in local comprehensive emergency management plans with an action plan and annual inventory of air quality emergency resources.
