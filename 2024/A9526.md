# Relates to criminal mischief in the third degree

**Bill ID:** A9526
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9526 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9526)

## Summary

Adds damaging property of another person when that property is an American flag to the definition of criminal mischief in the third degree.
