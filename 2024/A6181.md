# Relates to the creation of a peer to peer mentoring program

**Bill ID:** A6181
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian F. Curran
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6181 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6181)

## Summary

Creates a peer to peer mentoring program regarding drug use in elementary and secondary schools.
