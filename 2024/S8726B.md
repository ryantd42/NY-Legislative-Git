# Relates to abandoned vehicles

**Bill ID:** S8726B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8726B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8726B)

## Summary

Increases the dollar value of abandoned vehicles which may be seized by a local authority.
