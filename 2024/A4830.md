# Relates to establishing the New York state diplomas to home ownership program; appropriation

**Bill ID:** A4830
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4830 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4830)

## Summary

Establishes the New York state diplomas to homeownership assistance program; provides awards of up to fifteen thousand dollars to eligible applicants for repayment on student loans; makes an appropriation therefor.
