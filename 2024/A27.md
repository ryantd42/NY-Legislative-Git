# Permits persons 65 years of age to enroll in a limited number of state university and city university courses for credit without tuition

**Bill ID:** A27
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A27 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A27)

## Summary

Permits persons 65 years of age or over to enroll in a limited number of state university and city university courses for credit without tuition.
