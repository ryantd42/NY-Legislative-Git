# Allows a nursing home patient to install an electronic monitoring device in his or her room

**Bill ID:** A4616
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4616 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4616)

## Summary

Allows a nursing home patient, or his or her representative, to install, operate and maintain, at his or her own expense, an electronic monitoring device in his or her room.
