# Exempts certain parcels of land from licensing restrictions for manufacturers and wholesalers

**Bill ID:** A9144
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A9144 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9144)

## Summary

Exempts certain parcels of land from licensing restrictions for manufacturers and wholesalers who sell at retail for on-premises consumption in the town of East Hampton, county of Suffolk.
