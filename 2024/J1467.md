# Congratulating Connor Dean upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Division I Pentathlon Championship

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1467 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1467)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1467
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Connor  Dean  upon the occasion of
  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA  Division  I  Pentathlon
  Championship
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud to congratulate
Connor  Dean  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing  NYSPHSAA  Division  I
Pentathlon  Championship  at  the State Track and Field Championships at
Middletown High School over the weekend of  Friday  and  Saturday,  June
9-10, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Camden  Central  School  senior  Connor Dean took home the
coveted championship  title  for  his  outstanding  performance  in  the
outdoor  pentathlon; he won the long jump and the 1500 meters to win the
event by just 90 points over his competition; the athlete  also  managed
to  take  third  place  in  the  high jump and fourth in the shot put to
secure the coveted state championship; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's  colors  of  blue  and  white,
family  members,  friends,  and  the  community  at  large  loyally  and
enthusiastically supported Connor Dean  throughout  his  journey  as  he
ended his season with this magnificent win; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  athletic talent displayed by this young man is due in
great part to the efforts of his coaches and skilled  and  inspirational
tutors,  respected  for  their  ability  to  develop  potential  into
excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sports competition instills the values of teamwork,  pride,
and  accomplishment,  and  Connor  Dean  has  clearly contributed to the
spirit of excellence which is a tradition of his school; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Connor  Dean  upon  the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA
Division I Pentathlon Championship; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Connor Dean.
