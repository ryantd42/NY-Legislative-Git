# Establishes a town of Huntington deer management pilot program

**Bill ID:** S9023BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mario Mattera
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S9023BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9023BB)

## Summary

Establishes a town of Huntington deer management pilot program.
