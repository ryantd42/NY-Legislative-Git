# Creates a moratorium on the annexation of agricultural land by localities for an industrial use

**Bill ID:** A139
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A139 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A139)

## Summary

Creates a moratorium on the annexation of agricultural land by certain localities for an industrial use for one year.
