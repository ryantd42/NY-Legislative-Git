# Permits the possession of firearms in sensitive areas for the purposes of performing historical battle reenactments

**Bill ID:** S6172
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6172 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6172)

## Summary

Permits the possession of firearms in sensitive areas for the purposes of performing historical battle reenactments.
