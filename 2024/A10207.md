# Relates to the effectiveness of authorized sweepstakes with respect to subscriptions to "The Conservationist"

**Bill ID:** A10207
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10207 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10207)

## Summary

Extends the effectiveness of authorized sweepstakes with respect to subscriptions to "The Conservationist" until August 1, 2027.
