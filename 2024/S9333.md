# Exempts minority depository institutions from the corporate franchise tax for a period of ten years

**Bill ID:** S9333
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9333 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9333)

## Summary

Exempts minority depository institutions from the corporate franchise tax for a period of ten years from the date of commencement of business.
