# Increases the maximum sentence of imprisonment for certain misdemeanors to one year; repealer

**Bill ID:** A8280
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8280 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8280)

## Summary

Increases the maximum sentence of imprisonment for certain misdemeanors to one year.
