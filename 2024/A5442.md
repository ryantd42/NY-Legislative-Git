# Relates to calculation of the earned income credit

**Bill ID:** A5442
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5442 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5442)

## Summary

Relates to calculation of the earned income credit for taxable years beginning in 2024, provides the applicable percentage shall be 45%.
