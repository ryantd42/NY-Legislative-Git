# Relates to covering mental illness under the healthy New York program

**Bill ID:** A9266
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Demond Meeks
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9266 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9266)

## Summary

Requires coverage under the healthy New York program for mental illness.
