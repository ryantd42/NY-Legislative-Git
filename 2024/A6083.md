# Designates water development representatives for each department of environmental conservation region of the state

**Bill ID:** A6083
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6083 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6083)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of economic development to designate representatives for each department of environmental conservation region of the state to serve as the point of contact for municipalities to aid in the acquisition of resources and funding for water projects.
