# Requires the commissioner to establish domestic violence awareness curriculum or instruction to be available in school districts; age appropriate

**Bill ID:** A9371
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9371 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9371)

## Summary

Requires the commissioner to establish domestic violence awareness curriculum or instruction for school districts; requires content to be age appropriate.
