# Requires funding for special education services and programs for preschool children with disabilities to cover full-day joint classroom instruction

**Bill ID:** S8353
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8353 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8353)

## Summary

Requires funding for special education services and programs for preschool children with disabilities to cover joint classroom instruction for such children with disabilities and other children for the same number of hours each day.
