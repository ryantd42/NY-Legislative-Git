# Provides that the results of urine tests conducted pursuant to a judicial diversion program shall be private and confidential

**Bill ID:** S5797B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5797B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5797B)

## Summary

Provides that the results of urine tests conducted pursuant to a judicial diversion program shall be private and confidential.
