# Relates to metropolitan transportation authority contracts involving industrial painting and industrial coatings

**Bill ID:** A6099
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Latoya Joyner
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A6099 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6099)

## Summary

Requires certain contractor, subcontractor and worker certifications for metropolitan transportation authority contracts which involve industrial painting or industrial coating work.
