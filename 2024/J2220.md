# Congratulating the New Hartford Indoor Percussion Ensemble upon the occasion of winning the New York State Percussion Circuit Championship on April 7, 2024

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2220 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2220)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2220
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  the New Hartford Indoor Percussion
  Ensemble upon the occasion of winning the  New  York
  State  Percussion  Circuit  Championship on April 7,
  2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  the
dedication and commitment of our young people who distinguish themselves
through excellence in musical competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, Musical competition provides an opportunity for students to
exhibit  their  practical  skills  to an audience, thereby giving them a
sense of pride in their hard work and efforts; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate the  New  Hartford  Indoor  Percussion  Ensemble  upon  the
occasion  of  winning the New York State Percussion Circuit Championship
at Gates Chili High School in Rochester, New York, on Sunday,  April  7,
2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, The New Hartford Indoor Percussion Ensemble (NHIPE), of New
Hartford  Central School, attained the gold medal, scoring 89.825 at the
state championship; performing  its  award-winning  show,  "Out  of  the
Dust,"  the  group  competed  in the Percussion Scholastic A class which
included 14 other groups from New York State; and
 
  WHEREAS, All the members  of  the  New  Hartford  Indoor  Percussion
Ensemble  were  mindful  of  representing  themselves, their school, and
their community by performing with the highest standard  of  excellence;
the  group  is  scheduled  to  compete at the Winter Guard International
Percussion World Championship in Dayton, Ohio, from April  18-20,  2024;
and
 
  WHEREAS, It should be further noted that excellence in field band is
possible only through vigorous practice and discipline, habits that will
guide  these young musicians to a lifetime of success in musical careers
or other vocations; and
 
  WHEREAS, The New Hartford Indoor Percussion Ensemble's  achievements
stand  as  a  proud  testament  to  the hard work of its members and the
dedication of its  parents,  teachers,  administration,  and  community,
illustrating  the  spirit of excellence and achievement; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the members of the New Hartford Indoor Percussion Ensemble
who positively represent their school, serve as  role  models  to  their
classmates,  exemplify the goal of continued excellence, and bring great
pride to their community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  members  of  the  New  Hartford  Indoor Percussion
Ensemble.
