# Relates to transferring credits from a CUNY community college to a New York state university

**Bill ID:** A5240
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5240 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5240)

## Summary

Allows CUNY community college students to transfer all earned credits to a New York state university.
