# Relates to the required time period for the payment of cigarette tax stamps

**Bill ID:** A9874
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9874 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9874)

## Summary

Increases the time period for the payment of cigarette tax stamps by an agent from 30 to 45 days.
