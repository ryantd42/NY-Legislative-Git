# Designates a portion of the state highway system the "Trooper Fabio Buttitta Memorial Bridge"

**Bill ID:** S5246AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5246AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5246AA)

## Summary

Designates the bridge on Bay Shore Road in the town of Islip, county of Suffolk, the "Trooper Fabio Buttitta Memorial Bridge".
