# Relates to disabilities or death of sanitation department employees caused by leptospirosis

**Bill ID:** A9021
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9021 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9021)

## Summary

Relates to evidence that leptospirosis that caused disability or death of a sanitation department employee was incurred in the performance of such sanitation department employee's duties.
