# Relates to untested sexual offense evidence kit collection reports

**Bill ID:** A5188
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5188 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5188)

## Summary

Requires law enforcement agencies to conduct an inventory of all untested sexual offense evidence collection kits; requires a report to the legislature and the governor.
