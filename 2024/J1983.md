# Honoring Dr. Kishore Kuncham upon the occasion of his retirement after 30 years of distinguished service to the Freeport School District

**Bill ID:** J1983
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1983 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1983)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1983
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  HONORING  Dr. Kishore Kuncham upon the occasion of
  his  retirement  after  30  years  of  distinguished
  service to the Freeport School District
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  seeks  to  acknowledge  those
individuals whose  professional  lives  and  civic  endeavors  serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Dr. Kishore Kuncham upon the occasion of his retirement after  30  years
of  distinguished  service  to  the Freeport School District on Tuesday,
August 20, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, For  the  past  30  years,  Dr.  Kishore  Kuncham  rendered
faithful,  conscientious,  and  valuable  service  to  Freeport  School
District; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his role as superintendent for the past  15  years,  Dr.
Kishore  Kuncham's  leadership  has  centered  on  fostering  a holistic
learning environment that goes beyond academics, focusing on the  social
and  emotional  growth of every student as they prepare to become future
leaders and competent global citizens; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  Superintendent  of  Schools,  Dr.  Kishore  Kuncham
emphasized  his  whole  child  approach  to  learning  while introducing
innovative programs  and  services,  including  offering  more  than  30
Advanced  Placement  courses  and  more  than 30 Dual-Enrollment College
Credit courses, resulting in the highest  graduation  rates  in  several
decades  with alumni attending some of the most prestigious colleges and
universities in the country; and
 
  WHEREAS, Students participating in the  district's  music  and  arts
programs  develop  a life-long appreciation of the arts, with performing
groups being  regularly  invited  to  share  their  musical  talents  at
prestigious  venues  while  also  performing  at local community events,
allowing these students to gain valuable artistic  experience  and  make
meaningful connections with community stakeholders; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Freeport  School  District's state-of-the-art science
research programs, in collaboration with world-renowned institutions and
prestigious universities  such  as  Cornell  University  and  Brookhaven
National Laboratory, have flourished during Dr. Kishore Kuncham's tenure
and have provided students with the unique opportunity to work alongside
Nobel Prize laureates, NASA scientists, and astronauts; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Under  the  purview of Superintendent Dr. Kishore Kuncham,
the district's athletic program has  gained  statewide  recognition  for
producing championship teams whose student athletes serve as role models
both on and off the field; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Freeport  School District experienced great financial
stability under this superintendent's  meaningful  15-year  career;  the
 
district  achieved  and  maintained  one of the highest bond ratings, an
accolade earned by only a  select  few  school  districts;  Dr.  Kishore
Kuncham's  prudent  fiscal  management  resulted  in  almost a decade of
consecutive  tax  levy  decreases and approved budgets, while channeling
substantial investments into programs, technology, and facilities at the
benefit of students and staff alike; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dr. Kishore  Kuncham  also  worked  hard  to  obtain  grant
funding  for  students and teachers in his district, ultimately allowing
them to pursue their educational endeavors to  their  greatest  extents;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Dr.  Kishore  Kuncham's  unwavering advocacy for peace and
non-violence has brought the Freeport School District and the  community
together  through  annual  peace  concerts,  peace  marches,  and  an
unprecedented 131-mile peace walk, all of which  have  left  the  valued
superintendent  celebrated  for  his  tireless  fundraising  efforts and
commitment to humanitarianism; and
 
  WHEREAS, An advocate for the  important  role  health  and  wellness
plays  in educating the whole child, Dr. Kishore Kuncham also introduced
the daily practice of  mindfulness,  yoga,  and  guided  breathing  into
Freeport Schools; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  his  visionary  leadership and profound commitment to
education and the  community  at  large,  Dr.  Kishore  Kuncham  is  the
recipient of numerous awards and accolades, including: the highest honor
from  the  Association  of  School  Business Officials International-the
Eagle Award, the Outstanding Superintendent of the Year Award from  more
than  one  organization,  the  New  York State PTA Distinguished Service
Award, the Nassau BOCES Education Partner Award, the Long Island  Herald
Person  of  the  Year, and his guidance throughout the COVID-19 pandemic
earned him the title of Hometown Hero; he also received the  prestigious
"Key  Influencer"  Award  as  one  of  only  two civilians in the entire
country to have the honor to fly with the Blue Angels; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Dr.  Kishore  Kuncham  founded  the  Freeport  Educational
Foundation  and served on the Board of Governors of the New York Schools
Insurance Reciprocal (NYSIR); his service extends  to  board  leadership
roles  in  organizations  such  as Reform Educational Finance Inequities
Today (REFIT),  New  York  Benefits  for  Educators  and  Student  Trust
(NYBEST),  United  Way  of  Long  Island,  Island Harvest, the Cradle of
Aviation Museum, and the Nassau Community College Foundation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Dr.  Kishore  Kuncham's  historic  role  as  the  first
Indian-American  School Superintendent in New York State will forever be
remembered as a testament to his trailblazing spirit; and
 
  WHEREAS, Over his 35 years of dedicated  service  to  the  field  of
public  education,  Dr.  Kishore Kuncham served with loyalty, honor, and
distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his official acts, Dr. Kishore Kuncham was governed by a
keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of  human  problems;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  his  tenure with the Freeport School District, Dr.
Kishore Kuncham earned the admiration,  esteem,  and  affection  of  his
colleagues and community; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Dr. Kishore Kuncham upon the occasion of his retirement  after  30
years of distinguished service; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Dr. Kishore Kuncham.
