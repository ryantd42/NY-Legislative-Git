# Establishes a personal income tax credit for voltage regulation technology equipment

**Bill ID:** S4852
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4852 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4852)

## Summary

Establishes a personal income tax credit for voltage regulation technology equipment.
