# Relates to rights of car wash facility customers to file a complaint regarding damages sustained from such car wash facility

**Bill ID:** A10634
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Charles Fall
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10634 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10634)

## Summary

Requires certain car wash facilities to display a notice to customers that such customers may file a complaint regarding damages sustained from such car wash facility to the consumer protection division.
