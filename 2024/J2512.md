## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2512
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Salutatorian  Etienne  Sasenarine  of
  Freeport  High  School,  in  recognition  of  his
  outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions, to honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to  commend
Salutatorian  Etienne Sasenarine of Freeport High School, in recognition
of his outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian Etienne  Sasenarine  represents  the  best  of
developed  potential  inherent in our most precious resource, our youth;
his achievements have brought enduring honor to his family and community
and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Salutatorian Etienne Sasenarine takes leave of  Freeport
High  School,  he  bestows  a  legacy  of  academic  achievement, school
involvement and community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian Etienne Sasenarine may now stand with pride as
he assesses his achievements, experiences the satisfaction of his labors
and the joy of his accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of
a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Salutatorian  Etienne  Sasenarine  of  Freeport High School, in
recognition of his outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Salutatorian Etienne Sasenarine.
