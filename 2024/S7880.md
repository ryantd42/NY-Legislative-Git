# Includes care and services provided by a child advocacy center under standard coverage for Medicaid recipients

**Bill ID:** S7880
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7880 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7880)

## Summary

Includes care and services provided by a child advocacy center licensed, certified, or otherwise authorized by the office of children and family services under standard coverage for Medicaid recipients.
