# Requires a minimum operator age for amusement devices

**Bill ID:** S9624
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9624 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9624)

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of labor to promulgate rules and regulations to authorize competent operators at least sixteen years of age to operate all amusement devices.
