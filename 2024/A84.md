# Relates to requiring prescription drug containers be labeled with a description of the size, shape, color and imprint of the prescription drug it contains

**Bill ID:** A84
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nader Sayegh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A84 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A84)

## Summary

Requires prescription drug containers be labeled with a description of the size, shape, color and imprint of the prescription drug it contains.
