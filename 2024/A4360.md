# Establishes a distinctive "Pink Ribbon" license plate and provides for the use of the service charge for breast cancer education and support

**Bill ID:** A4360
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4360 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4360)

## Summary

Establishes a distinctive "Pink Ribbon" license plate for which there is an additional annual service charge of $25; establishes the "breast cancer education and support fund"; provides for disbursement of monies in such fund for community-based breast cancer education and support programs.
