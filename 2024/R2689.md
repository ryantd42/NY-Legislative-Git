# Amends a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for certain appropriations for the 2023-2024 state fiscal year for grants-in-aid to environmental conservation initiatives

**Bill ID:** R2689
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R2689 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/R2689)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2689
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R2860  of  2022
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  a  certain  appropriation for the
  2022-23 state fiscal year for additional  grants  in
  aid  to  certain  environmental  conservation
  initiatives, as required by a plan setting forth  an
  itemized  list  of  grantees  with  the amount to be
  received by each, or the methodology for  allocating
  such  appropriation.  Such  plan shall be subject to
  the approval  of  the  temporary  president  of  the
  senate and the director of the budget and thereafter
  shall  be  included  in a resolution calling for the
  expenditure of such monies, which resolution must be
  approved by a majority vote of all  members  elected
  to the senate upon a roll call vote
 
  RESOLVED,  That  funds originally appropriated in 2022 pursuant to
section 1 of chapter  53  of  the  laws  of  2022  and  reappropriated
pursuant  to  section 1 of chapter 53 of the laws of 2024 which enacts
the aid to localities, local assistance account, for additional grants
in aid to certain environmental conservation initiatives.  Such  funds
shall be apportioned pursuant to a plan setting forth an itemized list
of grantees with the amount to be received by each, or the methodology
for  allocating  such appropriation. Such plan shall be subject to the
approval of the temporary president of the senate and the director  of
the  budget  and  thereafter shall be included in a resolution calling
for the expenditure of such monies, which resolution must be  approved
by  a  majority  vote of all members elected to the senate upon a roll
call vote, in accordance with the following schedule  which  shall  be
removed  from  or  added  to  the  schedule originally approved and as
adopted by Senate Resolution  2860  of  2022,  as  amended  by  Senate
Resolution 1394 of 2023:
 
[North Brooklyn Neighbors
  10,000]
Neighbors Allied for Good Growth, Inc. 
  25,000
Newtown Creek Alliance Incorporated 
  25,000
