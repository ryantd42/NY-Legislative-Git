# Relates to allowing health care professionals to perform services at the Ironman Lake Placid

**Bill ID:** A8206
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A8206 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8206)

## Summary

Allows out of state health care professionals to perform services at the Ironman Lake Placid 2024.
