# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the Peter Wheeler Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** A4914B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A4914B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4914B)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in Tompkins county as the "Peter Wheeler Memorial Bridge".
