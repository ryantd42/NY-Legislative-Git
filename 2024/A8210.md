# Establishes a central business district toll exemption for veterans

**Bill ID:** A8210
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8210 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8210)

## Summary

Establishes a central business district toll exemption for veterans who are residents of New York state.
