# Requires introducer's memorandum to include a statement of fiscal impact affecting political subdivisions

**Bill ID:** A305
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A305 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A305)

## Summary

Requires introducer's memorandum to include a statement of fiscal impact affecting political subdivisions.
