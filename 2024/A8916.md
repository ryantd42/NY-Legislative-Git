# Requires commercial garages containing electric vehicle charging stations to ensure public access to such charging stations

**Bill ID:** A8916
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8916 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8916)

## Summary

Requires commercial garages containing electric vehicle charging stations to ensure public access to such charging stations at commercially reasonable rental or fee rates.
