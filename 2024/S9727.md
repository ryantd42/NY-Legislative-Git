# Eliminates the congestion surcharge on medallion taxicab trips

**Bill ID:** S9727
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Liu
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9727 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9727)

## Summary

Eliminates the congestion surcharge on medallion taxicab trips.
