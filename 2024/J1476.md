**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo

**PDF:** [J1476 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1476)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1476
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Bernard Turi, upon the occasion of
  his retirement as Executive Vice President of  Utica
  National Insurance Group, on September 15, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  seeks  to  acknowledge  those
individuals whose  professional  lives  and  civic  endeavors  serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  the  past  36  years, Bernard Turi rendered faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to Utica National  Insurance  Group;
in  his  capacity  in  numerous  positions,  spanning from Senior Claims
Specialist to Executive Vice President, he served with  loyalty,  honor,
and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Utica  National  Insurance  Group  will  honor  this
exceptionally accomplished executive, Bernard Turi, with  a  celebration
of his retirement on Friday, September 15, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, As a cum laude graduate, Bernard Turi obtained a bachelor's
degree in political science from Niagara University, followed by a juris
doctorate from Syracuse University College of Law; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  his  extensive  career  with  Utica  National
Insurance  Group,  Bernard  Turi  has  served  the  company  in  various
positions,  starting  as  a  Senior  Claims Specialist in the Errors and
Omissions department in 1987; and
 
  WHEREAS, Subsequently, Bernard Turi has held the following positions
within Utica National Insurance Group: Staff Attorney (1990);  Associate
Claims  Attorney  (1995);  Claims  Attorney/Director of liability claims
(1999); Associate General Counsel and Claims Attorney  (2004);  Director
of  the  Risk  and  Reinsurance  department  (2011); General Counsel and
Director of the Risk and  Reinsurance  department  (2013);  and  General
Counsel, General Auditor, and Chief Risk Officer (2014); and
 
  WHEREAS,  Bernard  Turi  was  named  as an officer of Utica National
Insurance Group as an Assistant Vice President  (1999);  Vice  President
(2007);  Senior  Vice  President  (2011);  and  Executive Vice President
(2021), the esteemed role from which he will retire; and
 
  WHEREAS, Additionally, Bernard Turi served as the Chair of New  York
Insurance  Association  (NYIA)  in  2014  and 2015, a member of the NYIA
Board of Directors  from  2007-2015,  and  an  officer  with  NYIA  from
2010-2015; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Currently  serving as Vice Chair on the board of directors
and Chair of the finance and audit committees of the New  York  Property
Insurance  Underwriting  Association  (NYPIUA), Bernard Turi is also the
Chair of the Utica National Group Foundation Screening Committee; and
 
  WHEREAS, Appointed  by  the  superintendent  of  the  Department  of
Financial  Services of New York State, Bernard Turi is presently serving
 
his second three-year term as a member of the New York  State  Insurance
Advisory Committee; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Furthermore,  in  New  York  State,  Bernard Turi has been
admitted to practice law before its courts as well as the United  States
District Court for the Northern District of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  his official acts, Bernard Turi was governed by a keen
sense of duty and always showed a unique perspective of the needs of his
clientele; during his tenure with Utica  National  Insurance  Group,  he
earned  the  admiration,  esteem,  and affection of his colleagues; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Bernard Turi upon the occasion of his retirement, after 36
years of distinguished service, on September 15, 2023 and  to  wish  him
success in all his future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Bernard Turi.
