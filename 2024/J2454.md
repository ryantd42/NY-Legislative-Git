**Bill ID:** J2454
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2454 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2454)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2454
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of 2024 Valedictorians, Zachary
  Gruber and Ryan Siegel, and Salutatorian, Dhiya San,
  of Schreiber High School, in  recognition  of  their
  outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions to  honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend  Class  of
2024  Valedictorians,  Zachary Gruber and Ryan Siegel, and Salutatorian,
Dhiya San, of Schreiber High School, in recognition of their outstanding
accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorians,  Zachary  Gruber  and  Ryan  Siegel,  and
Salutatorian,  Dhiya  San,  represent  the  best  of developed potential
inherent in our most precious resource, our  youth;  their  achievements
have brought enduring honor to their families and communities and should
be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  Valedictorians,  Zachary  Gruber  and Ryan Siegel, and
Salutatorian, Dhiya San, take  leave  of  Schreiber  High  School,  they
bestow  a  legacy  of  academic  achievement,  school  involvement,  and
community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorians,  Zachary  Gruber  and  Ryan  Siegel,  and
Salutatorian,  Dhiya  San, may now stand with pride as they assess their
achievements and experience the satisfaction of their labors and the joy
of their accomplishments,  eager  to  face  the  new  experiences  of  a
challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2024 Valedictorians, Zachary Gruber  and  Ryan  Siegel,
and Salutatorian, Dhiya San, of Schreiber High School, in recognition of
their outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Valedictorians,  Zachary  Gruber  and  Ryan  Siegel,  and
Salutatorian, Dhiya San.
