# Prohibits the sale of dry ice to individuals under eighteen years of age

**Bill ID:** S5871
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5871 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5871)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of dry ice to individuals under eighteen years of age; provides a violation shall be punishable by a fine of not more than five hundred dollars.
