# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the War Dog Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** A4941A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Smullen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4941A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4941A)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the village of Herkimer, county of Herkimer, as the "War Dog Memorial Highway".
