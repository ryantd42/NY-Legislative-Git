# Exempts payroll agencies from the definition of hiring party for certain purposes

**Bill ID:** A10649
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10649 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10649)

## Summary

Exempts a payroll agency that provides payroll services on behalf of another person or organization from the definition of hiring party.
