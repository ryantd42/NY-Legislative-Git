# Relates to exempting the Smith Opera house from licensing restrictions prohibiting manufacturers, wholesalers and retailers of alcoholic beverages from sharing an interest in a licensed premises

**Bill ID:** S4999B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S4999B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4999B)

## Summary

Exempts certain parcels of land containing the Smith Opera House from licensing restrictions prohibiting manufacturers, wholesalers and retailers of alcoholic beverages from sharing an interest in a licensed premises.
