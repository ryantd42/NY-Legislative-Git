# Relates to including the Asian longhorned tick and lone star tick on the invasive species list

**Bill ID:** S7852
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7852 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7852)

## Summary

Relates to including the Asian longhorned tick and lone star tick on the invasive species list, and include them in the comprehensive plan for invasive species management.
