# Includes participation in a banking development district as a factor when assessing a banking institution's record of performance

**Bill ID:** A6090
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Khaleel Anderson
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A6090 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6090)

## Summary

Includes participation in a banking development district as a factor when assessing a banking institution's record of performance in relation to the Community Reinvestment Act of 1977.
