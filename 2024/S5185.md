# Relates to prohibiting the sale of high purity sodium nitrite

**Bill ID:** S5185
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5185 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5185)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of high purity sodium nitrite to persons under the age of 21 and without proof of the intended use for institutional or scientific purposes.
