 AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the  city  of  New  York,  in
  relation to certain promotions to lieutenant or sergeant in the detec-
  tive bureau in the city of New York

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1.  Subdivision c of section 14-103 of the administrative code
 of the city of New York, as amended by chapter 755 of the laws of  1990,
 is amended to read as follows:
  c.  The commissioner may designate lieutenants as commanders of detec-
 tive squads, and sergeants as supervisors of detective squads, who while
 performing duty in such bureau and while so designated as commanders  of
 detective  squads  or supervisors of detective squads shall be paid such
 salary as may be determined by the mayor. ANY PERSON  WHO  HAS  RECEIVED
 PERMANENT  APPOINTMENT  AS  A  LIEUTENANT OR SERGEANT AND IS TEMPORARILY
 ASSIGNED TO PERFORM THE DUTIES OF A COMMANDER OF A  DETECTIVE  SQUAD  OR
 SUPERVISOR  OF A DETECTIVE SQUAD SHALL, WHENEVER SUCH ASSIGNMENT EXCEEDS
 EIGHTEEN MONTHS IN DURATION, BE APPOINTED AS A LIEUTENANT COMMANDER OF A
 DETECTIVE SQUAD OR SERGEANT SUPERVISOR OF A DETECTIVE SQUAD AND  RECEIVE
 THE COMPENSATION ORDINARILY PAID TO PERFORM SUCH DUTIES.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
