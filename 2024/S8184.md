# Extends provisions of law relating to the care demonstration program

**Bill ID:** S8184
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8184 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8184)

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to the care demonstration program from March 31, 2024 until March 31, 2026.
