# Relates to the sale of bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters

**Bill ID:** A9604
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9604 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9604)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters unless they are equipped with a fireproof battery container for the safe storage of the battery used as the principal electric power source.
