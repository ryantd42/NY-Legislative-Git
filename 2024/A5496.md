# Relates to registration fees for snowmobiles to be used on frozen bodies of water for sport fishing or for access to hunting or trapping lands solely

**Bill ID:** A5496
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5496 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5496)

## Summary

Provides registration fees for snowmobiles used on frozen bodies of water solely for sport fishing or for access to hunting or trapping lands.

 AN ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to registration

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. Section 2222 of the vehicle and traffic law is  amended  by
 adding a new subdivision 15 to read as follows:
  15.  (A) FEES FOR SNOWMOBILES TO BE USED SOLELY FOR SPORT FISHING ON A
 FROZEN BODY OF WATER OR SOLELY FOR ACCESS TO HUNTING OR TRAPPING  LANDS.
 NOTWITHSTANDING  ANY OTHER LAW, RULE OR REGULATION TO THE CONTRARY, FEES
 FOR REGISTRATION OF A SNOWMOBILE WHICH IS TO BE USED  SOLELY  FOR  SPORT
 FISHING  ON  A  FROZEN  BODY OF WATER OR SOLELY FOR ACCESS TO HUNTING OR
 TRAPPING LANDS SHALL NOT INCLUDE THE ADDITIONAL FEES SET OUT IN SUBDIVI-
 SION FOUR-A OF THIS SECTION, AND SECTION 21.07 OF THE PARKS,  RECREATION
 AND HISTORIC PRESERVATION LAW.
  (B)  THE  REGISTRATION  STICKER  FOR  SNOWMOBILES WHICH ARE TO BE USED
 SOLELY FOR SPORT FISHING ON A FROZEN BODY OF WATER OR SOLELY FOR  ACCESS
 TO  HUNTING  OR  TRAPPING LANDS SHALL BE OF A DIFFERENT COLOR THAN OTHER
 REGISTRATION STICKERS FOR SNOWMOBILES.
  (C) A PERSON WHO OWNS OR OPERATES A SNOWMOBILE WHICH IS REGISTERED  TO
 BE USED SOLELY FOR SPORT FISHING ON A FROZEN BODY OF WATER OR SOLELY FOR
 ACCESS  TO  HUNTING  OR  TRAPPING  LANDS WHO OPERATES THE SNOWMOBILE FOR
 OTHER PURPOSES SHALL BE GUILTY OF A VIOLATION AND SHALL BE SUBJECT TO  A
 FINE OF TWO HUNDRED DOLLARS.
