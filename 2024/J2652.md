## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2652
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  200th  Anniversary  of the St.
  Stephen's Episcopal Church in New Hartford, New York
 
  WHEREAS, Religious institutions, and the many spiritual, social  and
educational  benefits  they confer, play a vital role in the development
of the moral fabric of a responsible citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the tradition of this State and this  Nation  to  pay
tribute  to  those  institutions and individuals who have contributed to
the ethical and spiritual values of their communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body takes pleasure in  commemorating  the
200th Anniversary of the St. Stephen's Episcopal Church in New Hartford,
New York, celebrated on Sunday, June 2, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, The founder of New Hartford, Jedediah Sanger, purchased the
land  for  and  funded  the  initial  construction  of the St. Stephen's
Episcopal Church in 1825; the institution serves as  the  second  oldest
church in the town; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Named after Saint Stephen, one of the first deacons of the
church,  the  St.  Stephen's  Episcopal  Church  has  adapted  to  an
ever-changing society and prides itself in its ability to thrive; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Devoted  to  the  community  in  which  it serves, the St.
Stephen's Episcopal Church participates in  a  myriad  of  outreach  and
community  service  efforts,  such  as  weekly  spaghetti  dinners  for
socializing, "Ashes to-go" on  Ash  Wednesday,  and  donating  to  local
charities and organizations that help disadvantaged people; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The St. Stephen's Episcopal Church stands on the threshold
of tomorrow, prepared to meet the challenges of the coming decades while
retaining that spiritual resolve which characterizes its past; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to take  note  of
enduring  religious  institutions  and to bring such institutions to the
attention of the people of this Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the 200th Anniversary of the St. Stephen's Episcopal Church
in New Hartford, New  York,  fully  confident  that  this  commemoration
reflects  the  belief  in  those  values  which  enhance the dignity and
purpose of life; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to The Reverend Kathlyn Schofield The Reverend Deacon Lynne
Walton, St. Stephen's Episcopal Church in New Hartford, New York.
