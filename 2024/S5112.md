# Relates to creating a school resource officer working group; and provides for the repeal of such provisions upon the expiration thereof

**Bill ID:** S5112
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5112 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5112)

## Summary

Creates a school resource officer working group to study appropriate standards and training for a person to serve as a school resource officer; provides for the repeal of such provisions upon the expiration thereof.
