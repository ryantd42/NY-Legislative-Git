# Relates to crimes committed on metropolitan transportation authority property

**Bill ID:** S5046
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5046 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5046)

## Summary

Makes a crime while in or upon metropolitan transportation authority property an A-1 felony; provides sentencing guidelines for such crime.
