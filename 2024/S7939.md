# Authorizes the town of Cortlandt to impose a hotel and motel tax

**Bill ID:** S7939
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7939 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7939)

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Cortlandt to impose a tax on persons occupying rooms in hotels or motels in such town.
