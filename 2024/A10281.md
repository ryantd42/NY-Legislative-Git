# Requires the public posting of certain contracts which are otherwise subject to the approval of the state comptroller

**Bill ID:** A10281
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [A10281 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10281)

## Summary

Requires the public posting of certain emergency and exempted contracts which are otherwise subject to the approval of the state comptroller.
