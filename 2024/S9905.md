# Establishes the office of gun violence prevention and the gun violence advisory council

**Bill ID:** S9905
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9905 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9905)

## Summary

Establishes the office of gun violence prevention and the gun violence advisory council.
