# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian, Dave Achonu, of Baldwin High School, in recognition of his outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** J2502
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2502 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2502)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2502
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024  Valedictorian,  Dave
  Achonu, of Baldwin High School,  in  recognition  of
  his outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions to  honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend  Class  of
2024  Valedictorian, Dave Achonu, of Baldwin High School, in recognition
of his outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian,  Dave  Achonu,  represents  the  best  of
developed  potential  inherent in our most precious resource, our youth;
his achievements have brought enduring honor to his family and community
and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Valedictorian, Dave Achonu, takes leave of Baldwin  High
School, he bestows a legacy of academic achievement, school involvement,
and community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian, Dave Achonu, may now stand with pride as he
assesses his achievements and experiences the satisfaction of his labors
and the joy of his accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of
a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Class  of  2024  Valedictorian,  Dave  Achonu,  of Baldwin High
School, in recognition of his outstanding  accomplishments;  and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Valedictorian, Dave Achonu.
