# Relates to the use of the Madison and Oneida county correctional facilities; relates to county-tribal detention agreements between the Oneida Indian Nation and Madison county

**Bill ID:** A9441AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela J. Hunter
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9441AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9441AA)

## Summary

Relates to the use of the Madison and Oneida county correctional facilities; relates to county-tribal detention agreements between the Oneida Indian Nation and Madison county and between the Oneida Indian Nation and Oneida county.
