# Relates to verification of driving history when used as a rating or underwriting factor for private passenger motor vehicle insurance

**Bill ID:** S5764BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5764BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5764BA)

## Summary

Requires third party verification of driving history when used as a rating or underwriting factor for private passenger motor vehicle insurance.
