# Authorizes the sale of liquor for consumption on certain premises located within two hundred feet of a building occupied exclusively as a school, church, synagogue or other place of worship

**Bill ID:** A9755AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9755AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9755AA)

## Summary

Authorizes the sale of liquor for consumption on certain premises located within two hundred feet of a building occupied exclusively as a school, church, synagogue or other place of worship in the borough of Manhattan, county of New York.
