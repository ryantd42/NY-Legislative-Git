# Extends a payment in lieu of taxes for Lido Beach in the town of Hempstead

**Bill ID:** S6151AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S6151AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6151AA)

## Summary

Extends the expiration of payments in lieu of taxes for Lido Beach in the town of Hempstead until June 30, 2025.
