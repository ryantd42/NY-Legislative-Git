# Authorizes the county of Nassau assessor to accept an application for a real property tax exemption from New York Jesus Baptist Church

**Bill ID:** S6177BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S6177BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6177BB)

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Nassau assessor to accept an application for a real property tax exemption from New York Jesus Baptist Church with respect to the 2022-2023 assessment roll for all of the 2022-2023 school taxes and all of the 2023 general taxes.
