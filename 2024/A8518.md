# Relates to standards for the prompt investigation and settlement of claims arising out of states of emergency and disasters

**Bill ID:** A8518
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A8518 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8518)

## Summary

Establishes standards for the prompt investigation and settlement of claims arising out of states of emergency and disasters; relates to the effectiveness thereof.
