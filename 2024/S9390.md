# Allows schools and camps to implement a policy to administer over-the-counter medication to students and children attending such schools or camps

**Bill ID:** S9390
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9390 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9390)

## Summary

Allows schools and camps to implement a policy to administer over-the-counter medication to students and children attending such schools or camps upon written request by such students' and children's parents or guardians.
