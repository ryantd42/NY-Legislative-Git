# Relates to eligibility for a real property tax exemption for members of the reserve

**Bill ID:** S6017
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6017 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6017)

## Summary

Provides eligibility for a real property tax exemption for current or retired military reservists based on participation in formal training activities.
