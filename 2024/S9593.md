# Affords certain uniformed members of the fire department pension fund with an increased pension benefit for each year of additional service beyond their required minimum service

**Bill ID:** S9593
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9593 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9593)

## Summary

Affords certain uniformed members of the fire department pension fund with an increased pension benefit of one-fortieth of their average annual earnings for each year of additional service beyond his or her required minimum service.
