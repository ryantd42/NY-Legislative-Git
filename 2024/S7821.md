# Requires continuous work until completion for the design, construction, maintenance, or renovation of any transit facility, transportation facility, or equipment of the metropolitan transportation authority

**Bill ID:** S7821
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Weber
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7821 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7821)

## Summary

Provides that in the design, construction, maintenance, or renovation of any transit facility, transportation facility, or equipment of the metropolitan transportation authority, work shall take place over a continuous period of time until such point at which the project has been completed.
