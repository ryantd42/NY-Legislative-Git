# Caps the monthly maximum payment for hotel rooms to house homeless persons

**Bill ID:** S9937
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9937 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9937)

## Summary

Caps the monthly maximum payment for hotel rooms to house homeless persons based upon the New York city housing authority Section 8 voucher payment standards.
