## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2635
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING the 45th Annual Remsen Barn Festival
  of the Arts on September 28, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the sense of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
those  organizations  of  artistic  significance  whose  creative
contributions  have  helped  further  the  cultural  enrichment  of  our
communities and our Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commemorate the  45th  Annual  Remsen  Barn  Festival  of  the  Arts  on
Saturday, September 28, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Originating  with  barn tours and handmade quilt exhibits,
the festival has evolved into one of  the  Northeast's  premier  events;
today,  over  225 artists and crafters display their work at this annual
festival; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Remsen Barn Festival of the Arts features various  art,
jewelry,  textiles,  fashion  accessories,  woodcrafts,  soaps, pottery,
holiday decorations, furniture, glassware, and candles; and
 
  WHEREAS, Main Street  in  Remsen  is  closed  to  vehicles  for  the
festival,  providing  an  opportunity for residents and purveyors of the
arts to walk freely while they  shop,  dine,  and  enjoy  the  array  of
entertainment provided free of charge; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendees can also enjoy a complimentary quilt show at the
Remsen Arts Center or navigate through the  Corn  Maze  located  at  The
Remsen Depot; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over  the  past  45 years, the Remsen Barn Festival of the
Arts has demonstrated an unrelenting commitment  to  providing  artistic
and  cultural enrichment, as well as entertainment to its community, and
as a result, has brought pride and recognition to  its  local  community
and to our great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the  45th  Annual  Remsen  Barn  Festival  of  the  Arts  on
September 28, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Remsen Barn Festival of the Arts.
