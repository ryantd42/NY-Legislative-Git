# Relates to the establishment of childcare centers in every court in the unified court system

**Bill ID:** A10428
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10428 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10428)

## Summary

Relates to the establishment of free childcare centers in every court in the unified court system for the minor children and dependents of jurors, parties in civil cases, and criminal defendants.
