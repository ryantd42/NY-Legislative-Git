# Requires menstrual products to be offered for free in non-public schools

**Bill ID:** S5913A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5913A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5913A)

## Summary

Requires menstrual products to be offered for free in non-public schools.
