# Requires that in the event a complaint made to local code enforcement results in the issuance of a compliance order, the complainant shall also be provided a copy of such order

**Bill ID:** A4963
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jen Lunsford
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4963 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4963)

## Summary

Requires that when a complaint is made to local code enforcement of a violation of the uniform fire prevention and building code which results in the issuance of a compliance order, the complainant shall also be provided a copy of such order.
