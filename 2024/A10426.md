# Includes the county of Orange within the definition of a designated community

**Bill ID:** A10426
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10426 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10426)

## Summary

Includes the county of Orange within the definition of a designated community for purposes of community preservation funds.
