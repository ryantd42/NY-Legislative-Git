# Authorizes the village of Goshen to impose a hotel and motel tax

**Bill ID:** S6190A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S6190A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6190A)

## Summary

Authorizes the village of Goshen to impose a hotel and motel tax of up to five percent.
