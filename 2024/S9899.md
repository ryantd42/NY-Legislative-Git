# Prohibits certain restrictions on motor vehicle sales

**Bill ID:** S9899
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9899 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9899)

## Summary

Prohibits certain restrictions on motor vehicle sales or use.
