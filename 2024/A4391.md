# Enacts online revenues and expenditures transparency act

**Bill ID:** A4391
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4391 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4391)

## Summary

Enacts the online revenues and expenditures transparency act to provide for the development of a single, searchable budget database website accessible to the public, at no cost.
