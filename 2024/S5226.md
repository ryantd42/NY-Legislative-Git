# Creates the empire state music production tax credit

**Bill ID:** S5226
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5226 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5226)

## Summary

Relates to the creation of the empire state music production tax credit.
