## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2523
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  CONGRATULATING  Eva  Vitale  upon  the occasion of
  earning  the  distinguished  Gold  Award,  the  most
  prestigious of Scouting honors
 
  WHEREAS, Eva Vitale, through her diligence and rigorous efforts, has
achieved  Scouting's highest and most prestigious honor, the Gold Award;
the ceremony will be held on Monday, June 10, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, A resident of Garden City, New York, Eva Vitale is a member
of Girl Scouts of the United  States  of  America,  Troop  1252,  Nassau
County; and
 
  WHEREAS,  America has achieved her greatest heights of glory through
the dedication, determination, and tireless efforts of  the  outstanding
citizens of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our
youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain her glory; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Girl  Scouts  of the United States of America and its
members are dedicated to the development of character and leadership  in
the youth of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  1916,  Senior  Girl Scouts have sought to cap their
years in Girl Scouting with the prized Gold Award which challenges girls
to change the world; and
 
  WHEREAS, In order to earn the Gold Award, a  girl  must  complete  a
rigorous  set  of  service,  leadership  development, and skill-building
tasks leading up to the final  and  most  challenging  requirement,  the
design  and completion of a Take Action Project; this project must be an
issue of interest to the  girl  and  her  community,  employ  team-based
solutions, and educate and inspire others; and
 
  WHEREAS, Eva Vitale's project was entitled, "Epipen Safety"; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This outstanding young woman has been involved in Scouting
for years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals, and has  attained  all  of
the necessary achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Eva  Vitale  is  a  credit  to her family, her school, her
community, her State, and her Nation, and undoubtedly, her  future  will
be  filled  with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations,  in
recognition  of  the  significance  of  this meritorious achievement, to
congratulate and honor Eva Vitale upon the occasion of earning the  Gold
Award; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Eva Vitale.
