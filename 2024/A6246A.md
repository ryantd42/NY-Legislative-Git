# Extends the county of Westchester's additional one percent rate of sales and compensating use tax

**Bill ID:** A6246AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A6246AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6246AA)

## Summary

Extends the county of Westchester's additional one percent rate of sales and compensating use tax until November 30th, 2025.
