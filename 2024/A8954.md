# Relates to the suspension of tolls collected during a state declaration of disaster emergency

**Bill ID:** A8954
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8954 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8954)

## Summary

Requires the suspension of the collection of tolls on public roads during a state declaration of disaster emergency on the New York state thruway.
