# Authorizes Terrence D. McCracken to receive certain service credit with the New York state and local employees' retirement system

**Bill ID:** S9167
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Sean Ryan
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9167 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9167)

## Summary

Authorizes Terrence D. McCracken to receive service credit with the New York state and local employees' retirement system for service as a bus operator for the Niagara Frontier Transit Authority Metro System, Inc.
