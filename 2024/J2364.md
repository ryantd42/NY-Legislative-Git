## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2364
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  10th Anniversary of the Daniel
  Barden Mudfest in Deansboro, New  York,  on  May  4,
  2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this Legislative Body to recognize
organizations of noble aims and  accomplishments  for  their  charitable
endeavors which serve New York communities at large; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  10th  Anniversary  of the Daniel Barden Mudfest at MKJ
Farm in Deansboro, New York, on May 4, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Daniel Barden Mudfest is a mud run obstacle course that
honors the memory of Daniel Barden who died tragically in the 2012 Sandy
Hook Elementary School shooting in Newtown, Connecticut; and
 
  WHEREAS, A niece of one of the  Mudfest  organizers  was  a  student
inside  the  Sandy  Hook  Elementary  school at the time of the tragedy,
where she lost her dear friend,  Daniel  Barden;  this  much-anticipated
community  event and fundraiser serves to preserve the fun-loving, kind,
and gentle character Daniel Barden is known  to  have  always  exhibited
throughout his life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  10th annual event will feature brand new obstacles in
addition to returning favorites; each obstacle is uniquely designed  and
hand-built  by the Mudfest masterminds to challenge runners in ways they
never imagined, invoking senses of competitive spirit through  bouts  of
physical endurance; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Spectators  of  the Mudfest events can expect to marvel at
the Highland Games, an exhilarating  exhibition  of  strength  featuring
thrilling  events  such  as  the  Caber  Toss,  Stone  Put,  and  more;
additionally, the Farmers Carry is a cherished event in  which  everyone
can participate; and
 
  WHEREAS, An opportunity to raise funds for organizations such as the
Sandy  Hook  Promise and local nonprofits, the Mudfest has brought in an
astounding $440,000 and counting over its years; and
 
  WHEREAS, This commendable organization is an asset to the  State  of
New  York,  honoring  victims  of  gun violence with diligence and grace
while providing opportunities for  the  community  to  grow  and  thrive
together for a greater good; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the  10th  Anniversary  of  the  Daniel  Barden  Mudfest  in
Deansboro, New York, on May 4, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the organizers of the Daniel Barden Mudfest.
