# Relates to the certification of dietitians and nutritionists

**Bill ID:** S5645
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5645 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5645)

## Summary

Allows certified dietitians and nutritionists to order therapeutic diets.
