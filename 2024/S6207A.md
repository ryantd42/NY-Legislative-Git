# Establishes the crime of criminal sale of a controlled substance upon the grounds of a drug or alcohol treatment center, or alcoholics anonymous or narcotics anonymous meeting

**Bill ID:** S6207AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6207AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6207AA)

## Summary

Establishes the crime of criminal sale of a controlled substance upon the grounds of a drug or alcohol treatment center, or alcoholics anonymous or narcotics anonymous meeting; designates such crime as a class E felony.
