# Prohibits the production, sale and use of adhesive-based rodent traps

**Bill ID:** A4785A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4785A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4785A)

## Summary

Prohibits the importation, manufacture, sale, distribution and use of glue traps, glue boards, and other adhesive-based animal traps.
