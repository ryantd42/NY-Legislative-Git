# Provides for a minimum reimbursement rate for ambulatory behavioral health services

**Bill ID:** A8839
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8839 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8839)

## Summary

Provides for a minimum reimbursement rate for ambulatory behavioral health services.
