# Requires the promulgation of regulations relating to the commercial trade of human reproductive processes involving semen donation

**Bill ID:** A5739
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5739 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5739)

## Summary

Requires the promulgation of regulations relating to the commercial trade of human reproductive processes involving semen donation; limits the number of live births from a single semen donor.
