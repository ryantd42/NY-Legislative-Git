# Designates certain streets in the city of Newburgh as state highways

**Bill ID:** S5406
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Rolison
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5406 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5406)

## Summary

Designates Water Street, Leroy Place, North Street, Plank Road, and South Street in the city of Newburgh as state highways.
