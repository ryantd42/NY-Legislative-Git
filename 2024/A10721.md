# Relates to punitive measures and the collection of student loans for individuals found guilty of anti-Semitism

**Bill ID:** A10721
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Eric Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10721 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10721)

## Summary

Relates to punitive measures and the collection of student loans and suspension and revocation of tuition assistance awards for individuals found guilty of anti-Semitism.
