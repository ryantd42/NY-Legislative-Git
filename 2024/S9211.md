# Lowers the required service life threshold for consolidated local street and highway improvement program capital projects

**Bill ID:** S9211
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9211 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9211)

## Summary

Lowers the required service life threshold for consolidated local street and highway improvement program capital projects from ten years to five years.
