# Allows school districts to permit home-schooled students to attend career education classes

**Bill ID:** A8002
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8002 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8002)

## Summary

Allows school districts to permit home-schooled students to attend career education classes.
