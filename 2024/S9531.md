# Increases Rockland county's annual minimum allocation through the MTA Dutchess-Orange-Rockland Fund (DORF)

**Bill ID:** S9531
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Weber
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9531 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9531)

## Summary

Increases Rockland county's annual minimum allocation through the MTA Dutchess-Orange-Rockland Fund (DORF) from two million dollars to ten million dollars.
