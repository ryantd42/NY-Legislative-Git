# Authorizes the county seat in Delaware county to have at least one polling place designated for early voting

**Bill ID:** S5114
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5114 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5114)

## Summary

Requires the county seat in Delaware county (Delhi) to have at least one polling place designated for early voting.
