# Relates to contests, competitions, tournaments and derbies to take wildlife; repealer

**Bill ID:** A9098
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9098 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9098)

## Summary

Removes the ban on contests, competitions, tournaments and derbies to take wildlife.
