# Relates to establishing transition shelters for individuals released from prison

**Bill ID:** A5506
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5506 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5506)

## Summary

Establishes transition shelters for individuals released from prison for up to six months to provide housing counseling and prevent the risk of overdose.
