# Commending the Valedictorians and Salutatorians of the Class of 2024 in the 1st Senate District, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** K2123
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2123 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2123)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2123
 
BY: M. of A. Thiele
 
  COMMENDING the Valedictorians and Salutatorians of
  the  Class  of  2024  in the 1st Senate District, in
  recognition of their outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions,  to  honor the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to commend the
Valedictorians and Salutatorians of the Class of 2024 in the 1st  Senate
District, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  These  Valedictorians and Salutatorians represent the best
of developed potential inherent  in  our  most  precious  resource,  our
youth;  their achievements have brought enduring honor to their families
and communities and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Valedictorians  and  Salutatorians  who  are  being
commended  for  their  outstanding  academic  performances and exemplary
achievements include:  Kimberly  Villa,  Justin  Urgiles,  Bridgehampton
School  District;  Nicholas  Cooper,  Leah  Fromm,  East  Hampton School
District;  Emily  Robinson,  Samantha  Kraycar,  Hampton  Bays  School
District;  Isabelle  Caplin,  Chad Federico, Sag Harbor School District;
Meghan  Kelly,  Jessica  Curran,  Westhampton  Beach  School  District;
Nathaniel  Stevenson,  Andrew Campbell, Greenport School District; Jolin
Chen, Charles Pasca, Mattituck-Cutchogue School District; Sophie  Clark,
Leonardo  Dougherty,  Shelter  Island  School  District;  Angelly
Avila-Rosales, Thomas Sklodowski/Amy Estrada, Southold School  District;
John  McCormick,  Ethan  Caskie,  Riverhead  School  District; Alexandra
DeMatteo, Tori  Rudnick,  Center  Moriches  School  District;  Jackeline
Antelo,  Alexander  Kosak,  Comsewogue  School District; Ryan Lee, Layla
Warsaw, Eastport-South Manor District; Gregory Cohen, Katherine  Miller,
Longwood  School  District;  Christopher  Barton, Joseph Ritieni, Miller
Place School District; Anna Fedotov, Anna Rossman,  Mount  Sinai  School
District;  Eric  Chen,  Michelle  Wu,  Port  Jefferson  School District;
Isabella Rooney, Sofia Haviland, Rocky Point School District;  James  T.
Minetti,  Lucas  J.  Portuese,  Shoreham-Wading  River  School District;
Katherine Liang, William McGovern/Michael Zhang,  Three  Village  School
District; and
 
  WHEREAS,  These  Valedictorians and Salutatorians may now stand with
pride as they assess their achievements, experience the satisfaction  of
their labors and the joy of their accomplishments, eager to face the new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Valedictorians and Salutatorians of the Class of 2024 in the
1st  Senate  District,  in  recognition  of  their  outstanding
accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the aforementioned Valedictorians and Salutatorians.
