# Relates to funds allocated to each member of the assembly for the purpose of compensating employees of such member

**Bill ID:** A4708
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4708 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4708)

## Summary

Relates to allocating two hundred fifty thousand dollars to each member of the assembly for the purpose of compensating employees and staff of such member.
