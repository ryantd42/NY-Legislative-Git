# Relates to toll exemptions for certain vehicles under the central business district tolling program

**Bill ID:** S7635
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7635 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7635)

## Summary

Exempts vehicles owned by civil service employees, college students and commercial and residential repair businesses from tolls charged under the central business district tolling program.
