## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2112
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Grand  Lodge  of  the  Free  and
  Accepted Masons of New York State upon the  occasion
  of  hosting  its  178th  Annual  Grand Lodge Session
  Communication on June 14-16, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the Grand Lodge of the Free and  Accepted  Masons  of  New  York
State  upon the occasion of hosting its 178th Annual Grand Lodge Session
Communication on June 14-16, 2024, at the Hilton Albany, in Albany,  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Grand Lodge of Free and Accepted Masons of the State of
New  York  belongs  to the global network of Freemasons, the largest and
oldest fraternal order in the world; it is the administrative body  that
oversees more than 500 Masonic lodges across New York State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Every state in the U.S., the District of Columbia, and many
countries  around  the  world  have  their own sovereign and independent
Grand  Lodge  that  presides  over  Freemasonry  in  their  respective
jurisdiction; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Grand Lodge of the Free and Accepted Masons of New York
State brings men of integrity together in brotherhood with a common bond
in  the shared belief that every man can, and should, step into his best
self and make a difference in the lives of others; and
 
  WHEREAS, An organization of righteous men, the Grand  Lodge  of  the
Free  and  Accepted Masons of New York State is steadfastly committed to
serving its community for a brighter future  through  acts  of  charity,
community  service,  and  good deeds which are an expression of its love
for humanity and a path for self-improvement; and
 
  WHEREAS, In recognition of 178 years of success through  the  trials
and  tribulations  of  this  country and state, the brothers of Free and
Accepted Masons have not only preserved but have found continued ways to
grow and nurture the communities in which they work, pray, and model the
importance of being a man of upright standing to  the  next  generation;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  the Grand Lodge of the Free and Accepted
Masons of New York State is in direct correlation  to  the  efforts  its
most  benevolent  leader,  the  Most  Worshipful Grand Master Gregory R.
Smith Jr., and its dedicated members whose involvement  is,  and  always
has  been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment,  an unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and commend
the  members  of  the Grand Lodge of the Free and Accepted Masons of New
York State, past and present, who have  labored  so  conscientiously  to
secure and improve the quality of life for the community it serves; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Grand Lodge of the Free and  Accepted  Masons  of  New  York
State  upon the occasion of hosting its 178th Annual Grand Lodge Session
Communication on June 14-16, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  Most Worshipful Grand Master Gregory R. Smith Jr.,
Grand Lodge of the Free and Accepted Masons of New York State.
