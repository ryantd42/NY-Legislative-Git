# Prohibits the sale and installation of certain playground equipment and ground covering which contains recycled tires

**Bill ID:** A8269
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patrick Burke
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8269 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8269)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale and installation of certain playground equipment and ground covering which contains recycled tires or any equipment which was made in part or in whole using recycled tires.
