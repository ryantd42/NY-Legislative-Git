# Authorizes the town of Kinderhook to alienate and discontinue the use of certain parklands

**Bill ID:** A9847CA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9847CA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9847CA)

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Kinderhook to alienate certain parklands and to convey such land to the Valatie Volunteer Rescue Squad for the purpose of providing emergency medical services to the town of Kinderhook.
