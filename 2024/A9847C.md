# Authorizes the town of Kinderhook to alienate and discontinue the use of certain parklands

**Bill ID:** A9847CC
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9847CC PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9847CC)

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Kinderhook to alienate certain parklands and to convey such land to the Valatie Volunteer Rescue Squad for the purpose of providing emergency medical services to the town of Kinderhook.

 AN  ACT  authorizing  the  town  of  Kinderhook to transfer ownership of
  certain parkland to the Valatie Volunteer Rescue Squad, Inc.
 
  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1. Subject to the provisions of this act, the town of Kinder-
 hook, county of Columbia, acting by and through its governing body,  and
 upon  such  terms  and  conditions as determined by such body, is hereby
 authorized to discontinue as parklands and to sell and  convey  at  fair
 market  value  the  lands described in section three of this act, to the
 Valatie Volunteer Rescue Squad, Inc.  for use by the  Valatie  Volunteer
 Rescue  Squad,  Inc.  for  the  purpose  of  providing emergency medical
 services to the town of Kinderhook.
