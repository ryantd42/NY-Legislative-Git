# Directs the division of veterans' services to establish a searchable database of veteran-owned businesses

**Bill ID:** S8444
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Ramos
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8444 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8444)

## Summary

Directs the division of veterans' services to establish a searchable database of veteran-owned businesses; requires the use of a special mark to indicate service-disabled veteran-owned businesses.
