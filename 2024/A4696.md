# Relates to assistance for certain small businesses

**Bill ID:** A4696
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Ron Kim
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A4696 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4696)

## Summary

Relates to assistance for certain small businesses including dry cleaners and those that practice nail specialty for necessary code related improvements.
