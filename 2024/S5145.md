# Provides criteria for membership in credit unions and permits the extension of certain services to nonmembers

**Bill ID:** S5145
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5145 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5145)

## Summary

Provides criteria for membership in credit unions; permits the extension of certain services to nonmembers.
