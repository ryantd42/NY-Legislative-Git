# Enacts the "Seneca county water and sewer authority act"

**Bill ID:** S8851
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8851 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8851)

## Summary

Enacts the "Seneca county water and sewer authority act"; establishes the Seneca county water and sewer authority district and the Seneca county water and sewer authority.
