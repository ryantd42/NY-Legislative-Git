# Grants peace officer status to animal control officers of the county of Schuyler

**Bill ID:** S5236A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5236A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5236A)

## Summary

Grants peace officer status to animal control officers of the county of Schuyler; defines "animal control officer".
