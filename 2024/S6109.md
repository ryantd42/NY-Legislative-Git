# Relates to restrictions on certain flight operations at municipal facilities; relates to the use of certain heliports

**Bill ID:** S6109
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6109 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6109)

## Summary

Prohibits municipal corporations from permitting the use of publicly owned land for helicopter air transportation operated for the primary purpose of conducting sightseeing tours over New York city or conducting photography, videography, or similar production activities over New York city; and relates to the use of certain heliports in the city of New York.
