# Relates to directing the public service commission to evaluate hydrogen, sewage thermal energy, and nuclear small modular reactors as renewable energy sources

**Bill ID:** A10703
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Durso
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10703 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10703)

## Summary

Directs the public service commission to evaluate hydrogen, sewage thermal energy, and nuclear small modular reactors as renewable energy sources and report the results of such evaluation to the governor and the legislature.
