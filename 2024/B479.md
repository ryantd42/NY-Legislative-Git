## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 479
 
BY: Senator MAYER
 
  CONCURRENT  RESOLUTION  OF  THE  SENATE AND ASSEMBLY
  providing for the election of three Regents  of  the
  University of the State of New York
 
  WHEREAS,  The  current  term  of the Regent of the University of the
State of New York elected from the seventh Judicial District  is  vacant
due to resignation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The terms of the Regents of the University of the State of
New York elected from the eleventh and twelfth Judicial Districts expire
on the thirty-first day of March in the year two thousand  twenty-three;
and
 
  RESOLVED, (if the &HOUSE concur), That pursuant to the provisions of
section  202  of  the  education  law,  the  vacancies  created  by  the
expiration of these terms and for  other  cause  are  hereby  filled  by
concurrent resolution; and
 
  RESOLVED,  (if the &HOUSE concur), That Adrian I. Hale of the county
of Monroe be elected Regent of the University of the State of  New  York
from  the  seventh Judicial District for a term of four years commencing
immediately; and
 
  RESOLVED, (if the &HOUSE concur), That Judith J. Chin of the  county
of  Queens  be  re-elected  Regent of the University of the State of New
York from the eleventh Judicial  District  for  a  term  of  five  years
commencing  on  the  first  day  of  April  in  the  year  two  thousand
twenty-three; and
 
  RESOLVED, (if the &HOUSE concur), That Dr. Aramina  Vega  Ferrer  of
the  county of Bronx be re-elected Regent of the University of the State
of New York from the twelfth Judicial District for a term of five  years
commencing  on  the  first  day  of  April  in  the  year  two  thousand
twenty-three.
