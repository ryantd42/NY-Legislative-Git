# Congratulating the Division Avenue High School Varsity Cheerleading Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Class B Cheerleading Championship on March 2, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2054
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Rhoads
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2054 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2054)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2054
 
BY: Senator RHOADS
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Division  Avenue  High School
  Varsity  Cheerleading  Team  upon  the  occasion  of
  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA  Class  B  Cheerleading
  Championship on March 2, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
Division  Avenue High School Varsity Cheerleading Team upon the occasion
of capturing NYSPHSAA Class B  Cheerleading  Championship  on  Saturday,
March 2, 2024, at the Visions Veterans Memorial Arena in Binghamton, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's  colors of blue and black,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Dragons throughout  their  journey  as  they  ended  their
season with an impressive overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In a sport such as competitive cheerleading, which demands
athletic prowess, strength and agility, Coaches Liz  Lynch  and  Cristen
Marcy worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching
these  outstanding  athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on
and off the mat; and
 
  WHEREAS, Coaches  Liz  Lynch  and  Cristen  Marcy  and  all  of  the
outstanding  athletes  on  the  Division  Avenue  High  School  Varsity
Cheerleading Team have clearly utilized dedication,  determination,  and
teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence
which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of the  Division  Avenue  High  School  Varsity
Cheerleading  Team:  Chloe  Berju,  Rayanne Choy, Ava Del Giorno, Brooke
DeSena, Riley Duggan,  Mondee  Dumas,  Nikole  Galgano,  Mia  Gray,  Eva
Guerra,  Jenna  Hansen,  Ava Rose Incendio, Alyssa Jules, Sarah Martins,
Shannon  McVetty,  Tara  O'Regan,  Danica  Prokay,  Victoria  Sepulveda,
Jessica  Sobarzo,  Mia  Stimac,  and Coaches Liz Lynch and Cristen Marcy
upon the  occasion  of  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA  Class  B  Cheerleading
Championship; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members of the Division Avenue  High  School  Varsity
Cheerleading Team, Coaches Liz Lynch and Cristen Marcy.
