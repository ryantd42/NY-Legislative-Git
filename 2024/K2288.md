# Congratulating Dylan Peter Hoey upon the occasion of receiving the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout, the most prestigious of Scouting honors

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2288 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2288)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2288
 
BY: M. of A. Burdick
 
  CONGRATULATING  Dylan Peter Hoey upon the occasion
  of receiving the distinguished rank of Eagle  Scout,
  the most prestigious of Scouting honors
 
  WHEREAS,  Through  his  diligence  and rigorous efforts, Dylan Peter
Hoey has achieved Scouting's highest and most  prestigious  rank,  Eagle
Scout;  he  was  confirmed and registered as an Eagle Scout on March 20,
2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Joining in 2017, Dylan Peter Hoey of Chappaqua, New York is
a member of Boy Scouts of America Troop 1 in Katonah, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, As a testament to his work ethic, Dylan Peter Hoey advanced
to Senior  Patrol  Leader  and  took  on  more  challenging  duties  and
responsibilities in his troop; and
 
  WHEREAS,  America has achieved its greatest heights of glory through
the dedication, determination and tireless efforts  of  the  outstanding
citizens of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our
youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain its glory; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Boy Scouts of America and its members are dedicated to the
development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in  Scouting,  earned
by  approximately  five  percent of all Boy Scouts, is the distinguished
rank of Eagle Scout; and
 
  WHEREAS, This outstanding young man has been  involved  in  Scouting
for  many  years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has attained all
the necessary achievements, including completion of a community  service
project, which was successfully planned, developed, and executed; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  his  project,  Dylan Peter Hoey researched, surveyed,
designed, obtained permits for, and constructed two  large  field  stone
masonry  abutments  and a 4 by 18 foot bridge above a stream and wetland
area at "Stepping Stones" in Katonah,  New  York;  The  Stepping  Stones
Foundation's mission is to foster public understanding of alcoholism and
inspire  recovery by preserving and sharing the historic home, archives,
and legacy of Bill and Lois Wilson, co-founders of Alcoholics  Anonymous
and Al-Anon Family Groups; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Through his efforts, Dylan Peter Hoey raised $7,500.00 for
the Service Project and an excess of $2,400.00 will be presented to  The
Stepping  Stones Foundation for use in future projects involving Scouts;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Looking to the future, Dylan Peter Hoey will attend Norwich
University, Corps of Cadets, with an expected graduation year  of  2028;
he  will  affiliate with the NROTC/U.S. Marines Corps Battalion and work
 
towards a minor in Leadership, with a goal to earn a commission  in  the
U.S. Marine Corps; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Dylan  Peter  Hoey  is  currently  enrolled  in  the David
Crawford School of Engineering and is planning to pursue a  Bachelor  of
Science degree in Construction Management; he is the first in his family
to attend a military college as a student athlete; and
 
  WHEREAS, His dedication to the safety and well-being of the citizens
of Katonah, New York is represented through his project and demonstrates
his devotion to both community and commitment to others; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dylan Peter Hoey's perseverance in rising through the ranks
to Eagle Scout is a strong indication of his ability to master difficult
tasks;  his  accomplishment will enable him to emerge as a leader, fully
equipped to meet the challenges of today's complex society; and
 
  WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to his family, his school,
his community, his State and his Nation, and no doubt his future will be
filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations,  in
recognition  of  the  significance  of  this meritorious achievement, to
congratulate and honor Dylan Peter Hoey on achieving the rank  of  Eagle
Scout; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Dylan Peter Hoey.
