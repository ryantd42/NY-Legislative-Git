# Extends the expiration of the provisions authorizing the county of Cattaraugus to impose an additional one percent sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A6087
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A6087 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6087)

## Summary

Extends the expiration of the provisions authorizing the county of Cattaraugus to impose an additional one percent sales and compensating use taxes until November 30, 2025.
