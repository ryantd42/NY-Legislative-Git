# Allows municipal commissions to use alternate lists for filling vacancies with resident and non-resident minority group members and women in order to achieve diversity in police departments

**Bill ID:** A6293
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6293 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6293)

## Summary

Allows municipal commissions to use alternate lists for filling vacancies with resident and non-resident minority group members and women in order to achieve diversity in police departments.

  4-C.  ALTERNATE LISTS FOR POLICE DEPARTMENTS. POLICE DEPARTMENTS WHERE
 A LIST PURSUANT TO SUBDIVISION FOUR OF THIS SECTION DOES NOT  ADEQUATELY

 CANDIDATES  FROM ALTERNATE LISTS BASED ON MERIT AND FITNESS, FURTHER THE
 RULE OF THREE SHALL APPLY.
