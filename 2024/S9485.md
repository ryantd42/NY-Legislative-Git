# Authorizes the empire state development corporation to rescind any approved general project plan for non-compliance

**Bill ID:** S9485
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9485 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9485)

## Summary

Authorizes the empire state development corporation to rescind any approved general project plan for non-compliance with any agreements made as part of such general project plan, including, but not limited to, community benefits agreements.
