# Extends the time frames for the issuance of certain bonds by the village of Washingtonville

**Bill ID:** A9690AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9690AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9690AA)

## Summary

Amends the village of Washingtonville deficit financing act to extend the time frames for the issuance of certain bonds by the village of Washingtonville.
