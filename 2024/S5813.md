# Declares the waters of the state to be public trust resources

**Bill ID:** S5813
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5813 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5813)

## Summary

Declares the waters of the state to be public trust resources.
