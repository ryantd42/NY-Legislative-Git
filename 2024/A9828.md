# Exempts the residency requirement for the comptroller and building inspector in the town of Chester, county of Orange

**Bill ID:** A9828
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A9828 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9828)

## Summary

Waives the residency requirement for the comptroller and building inspector in the town of Chester, county of Orange.
