# Designates a portion of the state highway system the "Trooper Fabio Buttitta Memorial Bridge"

**Bill ID:** A5562A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5562A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5562A)

## Summary

Designates the bridge over the Sagtikos Parkway on G Road in the town of Islip, county of Suffolk, the "Trooper Fabio Buttitta Memorial Bridge".
