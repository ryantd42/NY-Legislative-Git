## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2360
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  200th  Anniversary  of Steuben
  Community Church on June 8, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, Religious institutions, and the many spiritual, social, and
educational benefits they confer, play a vital role in  the  development
of the moral fabric of a responsible citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the tradition of this State and Nation to pay tribute
to  those  institutions  and  individuals  who  have  contributed to the
ethical and spiritual values of their communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body takes pleasure in  commemorating  the
200th Anniversary of Steuben Community Church on Saturday, June 8, 2024,
to  be  celebrated  with  a  history  seminar, music, food trucks, kids'
activities, as well as a workshop service at Steuben Town Hall; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Steuben  Community  Church  stands  as  a  welcoming
institution  to  all  citizens  of  Remsen, New York and beyond, finding
great success in the preaching and teachings by  the  church's  host  of
previous  cherished  pastors,  as  well  as  by  the current pastor, The
Reverend Brenda Brucker; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Steuben Circuit was set forth by the Genesee Conference
in 1824, and was large in size, requiring two ministers who traveled  by
foot and horseback; three decades later, the circuit was changes to need
only one minister; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  1854,  a  group of believers endeavored to construct a
church at Steuben Corners; the structure was finished two years later in
1856 at a cost of $1,063.20; and
 
  WHEREAS, Undergoing updates over the  course  of  its  history,  the
church  bell  was purchased in 1874, stained glass windows were added in
1915, and electric lights were later installed in 1937; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 1936, Steuben, Westernville, and North Western  Churches
were  joined  into  one charge conference; by 2018, these three churches
were separated to function independently; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 2023, the Steuben United Methodist Church was renamed to
Steuben Community Church; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to take  note  of
enduring  religious  institutions  and to bring such institutions to the
attention of the people of this Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate the 200th Anniversary of Steuben Community Church on June 8,
2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Steuben Community Church.
