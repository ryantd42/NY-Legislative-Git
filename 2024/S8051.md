# Relates to the display of green lights on the vehicles of members of mobile crisis teams

**Bill ID:** S8051
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8051 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8051)

## Summary

Relates to the display of green lights on the vehicles of members of mobile crisis teams and the revocation of authorization to display such light.
