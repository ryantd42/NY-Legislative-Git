# Relates to enacting the taxpayer voter registration act

**Bill ID:** A6037
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6037 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6037)

## Summary

Allows taxpayers to register to vote upon paying their taxes or changing their address.
