# Relates to a net price calculator center for colleges on the website of the higher education services corporation

**Bill ID:** A10084
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Demond Meeks
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10084 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10084)

## Summary

Directs the higher education services corporation to create a net price calculator center for colleges on its website.
