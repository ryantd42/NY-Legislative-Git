# Requires grocery stores that offer digital coupons automatically apply such coupons for senior citizens

**Bill ID:** S8864
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8864 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8864)

## Summary

Requires grocery stores that offer digital coupons automatically apply such coupons for senior citizens and loyalty or rewards program members.
