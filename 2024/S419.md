# Authorizes municipalities to adopt a sales tax exemption for clothing and footwear after March first, two thousand twenty-three; and providing for the repeal of such provisions upon expiration thereof

**Bill ID:** S419
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S419 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S419)

## Summary

Authorizes municipalities to adopt a local law, ordinance or resolution providing for a sales tax exemption for clothing and footwear after March 1, 2023 and no later than August 1, 2023; provides for the repeal of such provisions on January 1, 2024.
