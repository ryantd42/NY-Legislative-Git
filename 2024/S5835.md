# Relates to the compensation of deputy public administrators in the city of New York

**Bill ID:** S5835
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5835 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5835)

## Summary

Relates to the compensation of deputy public administrators in the city of New York; permits them to be paid at least two-thirds the amount paid to public administrators within the same county.

 AN  ACT  to  amend  the  surrogate's court procedure act, in relation to

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  Subdivision  2  of  section 1105 of the surrogate's court
 procedure act, as added by chapter 724 of the laws of 1983,  is  amended
 to read as follows:
  2. Each of the deputy public administrators of the counties comprising
 the  city  of New York shall receive as compensation AT LEAST two-thirds
 of the amount paid or hereafter paid to  the  public  administrators  of
 each such county, respectively.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
