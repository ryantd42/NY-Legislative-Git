# Requires payment card networks to use certain merchant category-codes for firearm dealers

**Bill ID:** A9862AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9862AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9862AA)

## Summary

Requires payment card networks to use certain merchant category-codes for firearm and ammunition dealers.
