# Relates to the authority of the capital district regional off-track betting corporation to retain and disburse certain uncashed tickets

**Bill ID:** S9567
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9567 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9567)

## Summary

Relates to the authority of the capital district regional off-track betting corporation to retain and disburse certain uncashed tickets to the corporations' participating counties.
