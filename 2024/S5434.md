# Prohibits the hunting or taking of wildlife with the aid of an unmanned aerial vehicle

**Bill ID:** S5434
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mario Mattera
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5434 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5434)

## Summary

Prohibits the hunting or taking of wildlife with the aid of an unmanned aerial vehicle.
