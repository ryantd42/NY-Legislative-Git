# Requires any student graduating from a high school in New York state be provided with a voter registration form

**Bill ID:** A10309
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenny Burgos
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A10309 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10309)

## Summary

Requires any student graduating from a high school in New York state be provided with a voter registration form.
