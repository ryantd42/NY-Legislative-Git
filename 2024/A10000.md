# Relates to persons with a serious mental illness residing in an adult home

**Bill ID:** A10000
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Christopher Eachus
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10000 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10000)

## Summary

Provides for when the department may limit the census of persons with a serious mental illness residing in an adult home.

  II. ENSURING ACCESS TO AND QUALITY OF MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES;
