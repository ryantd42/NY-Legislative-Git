# Commending the Lions Club of District 20-K1 upon the occasion of hosting its Testimonial Gala and paying tribute to Honoree, District Governor Antonio Robles

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2662 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2662)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2662
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Lions Club of District 20-K1 upon
  the occasion of hosting  its  Testimonial  Gala  and
  paying tribute to Honoree, District Governor Antonio
  Robles
 
  WHEREAS,  Individuals  who give of their time and energies and serve
the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration
and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  commend  those
illustrious  organizations  and  individuals whose focus and intent have
made a substantial impact on the quality of life in  their  communities;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the  Lions  Club of District 20-K1 upon the occasion of hosting
its Testimonial Gala at Jericho Terrace in Mineola, New York, on Sunday,
June 9, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under the leadership of Governor Antonio Robles, the  Lions
Club  of District 20-K1, in Brooklyn and Queens, New York, has organized
programs to benefit  the  visually  and  hearing  impaired,  raised  the
funding  to  combat  cancer and diabetes, provided food and clothing for
the disadvantaged, and partnered with other organizations, including the
New York City Parks  Department,  to  beautify  parks  and  improve  the
aesthetic appearance of the community's environment; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Governor Antonio Robles has given not only of his time and
energies but also of his competence, intelligence,  and  leadership  and
consequently  has  been  designated  for special honor for his dedicated
service to the residents of District 20-K1 in Brooklyn and  Queens,  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Governor Antonio Robles has been called upon to contribute
his time and talents to civic and charitable endeavors and has given  of
himself  unstintingly;  throughout  the  entire  period of his community
service, a period of constructive involvement, he has stood constant  in
dignity and good grace; and
 
  WHEREAS, As his signature project this year, Governor Antonio Robles
has  commissioned the District's Cancer Committee to return the District
to its Flagship Sponsor status with the American  Cancer  Society;  this
prestige was achieved at the 2023 "Making Strides Against Breast Cancer"
Walk in Brooklyn and Queens; the tremendous support for the services and
events fulfills the Governor's theme, "Inspiring Minds Through Service";
and
 
  WHEREAS, The Committee established a multi-faceted plan comprised of
donations  from  Lions  Clubs,  a  fundraising  project conducted by Leo
Clubs,  and  the  Committee's  own  fundraising  ventures;  exceeding
expectations, $25,547 was raised; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefit of others which Governor Antonio  Robles  has
displayed; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  auspicious occasion presents an opportunity for this
Legislative Body to recognize and pay  tribute  to  the  Lions  Club  of
District 20-K1 and Governor Antonio Robles; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Maintaining its tradition of celebrating organizations and
individuals  who  exemplify  altruistic  values  and  inspire  future
generations  with  exceptional service, this Legislative Body now honors
Governor Antonio Robles and the Lions Club of District 20-K1 and eagerly
await the great works they will continue to achieve; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the  Lions  Club of District 20-K1 upon the occasion of hosting
its Testimonial Gala and paying tribute to  Honoree,  District  Governor
Antonio Robles; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Lions Club of District 20-K1  and  District  Governor
Antonio Robles.
