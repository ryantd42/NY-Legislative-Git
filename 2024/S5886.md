# Exempts the Buchanan Engine Company No. 1 from the requirement that the percentage of non-resident fire department members not exceed forty-five percent of the membership

**Bill ID:** S5886
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5886 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5886)

## Summary

Exempts the Buchanan Engine Company No. 1, within the village of Buchanan, county of Westchester from the requirement that the percentage of non-resident fire department members not exceed forty-five percent of the membership.
