# Congratulating the New York State Section XI All-Star Boys Composite Bowling Team upon the occasion of capturing the 2024 NYSPHSAA Bowling Championship on March 9, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2233
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dean Murray
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2233 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2233)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2233
 
BY: Senator MURRAY
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  New  York  State  Section  XI
  All-Star  Boys  Composite  Bowling  Team  upon  the
  occasion  of  capturing  the  2024  NYSPHSAA Bowling
  Championship on March 9, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
New  York State Section XI All-Star Boys Composite Bowling Team upon the
occasion of capturing the 2024 NYSPHSAA Bowling  Championship,  held  on
Saturday,  March  9,  2024  at AMF Strike N Spare Lanes in Syracuse, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and  applause  of  their  excited  fans,  the
Section  XI  All-Star  Boys Composite Bowling Team, hailing from Suffolk
County in New York State had a total pinfall of 6,801 total  pins;  they
were 47 pins above their closest opponent; and
 
  WHEREAS,  These outstanding athletes who together have achieved this
commendable  and  distinguished  championship  victory,  represent  many
school  districts  in  Suffolk County; the champion team members include
Anthony Manetta from Comsewogue  School  District,  Christopher  Rinaldi
from  South  Huntington  Union Free School District, Vinny Petrillo from
Deer Park Union Free School District, Andrew Jacobsen  from  East  Islip
Union  Free School District, Matthew Sundberg from East Islip Union Free
School District, and Corey Volper from Longwood Central School District;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as bowling, which demands athletic prowess,
precision and adaptability, Section XI All-Star Boys  Composite  Bowling
Team  Coach  Doug  Dwyer,  Coach  John Romero, Coach Sonny Casale, Coach
Judith Fischer, Coach John DiGiacomo, and  Coach  Mike  D'Ambrosio  have
worked hard to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these
outstanding athletes lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off
the lanes; and
 
  WHEREAS,  These  fine coaches and all of the outstanding athletes on
the Section  XI  All-Star  Boys  Composite  Bowling  Team  have  clearly
utilized  dedication, determination, and teamwork in providing a lasting
contribution to the  spirit  of  excellence  which  is  a  tradition  of
scholar-athlete teams; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate each member of  the  Section  XI  All-Star  Boys  Composite
 
Bowling  Team  on  their  outstanding  championship  success;  and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members of the Section  XI  All-Star  Boys  Composite
Bowling  Team:  Anthony  Manetta,  Christopher  Rinaldi, Vinny Petrillo,
Andrew Jacobsen, Matthew Sundberg and Corey Volper;  and  their  coaches
Doug  Dwyer,  John Romero, Sonny Casale, Judith Fischer, John DiGiacomo,
and Mike D'Ambrosio.
