# Relates to the residential parking system in the village of Croton-on-Hudson

**Bill ID:** S5932
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5932 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5932)

## Summary

Adds Truesdale Drive, Nordica Drive, Cedar Lane, and Old Post Road South from Truesdale Drive to Morningside Drive to the Croton-on-Hudson residential parking system.
