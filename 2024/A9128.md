# Relates to grants for facilities providing prekindergarten services

**Bill ID:** A9128
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Josh Jensen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9128 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9128)

## Summary

Relates to grants for facilities providing prekindergarten services; promotes the financial viability of such facilities.
