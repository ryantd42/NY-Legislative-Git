# Extends the the effectiveness of certain provisions regarding hotel/motel taxes in Chautauqua county

**Bill ID:** S6205
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S6205 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6205)

## Summary

Extends the effectiveness of certain provisions regarding hotel/motel taxes in Chautauqua county until November 30, 2025.
