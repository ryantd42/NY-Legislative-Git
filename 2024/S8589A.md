# Relates to extreme risk protection orders

**Bill ID:** S8589AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8589AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8589AA)

## Summary

Relates to who can be a petitioner for an extreme risk protection order.
