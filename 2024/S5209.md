# Expands the drug take back act to include single-use and reusable electronic cigarettes

**Bill ID:** S5209
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5209 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5209)

## Summary

Expands the drug take back act to include single-use and reusable electronic cigarettes.
