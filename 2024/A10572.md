# Relates to the acceleration of the downstate casino licenses

**Bill ID:** A10572
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10572 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10572)

## Summary

Relates to the acceleration of the downstate casino licenses; sets forth procedures for the review and approval of applications; makes related provisions.
