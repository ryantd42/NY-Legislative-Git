# Requires health insurance contracts cover postpartum pelvic floor physical therapy

**Bill ID:** S8407
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8407 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8407)

## Summary

Requires health insurance contracts cover postpartum pelvic floor physical therapy for new mothers.
