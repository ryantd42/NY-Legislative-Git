# Establishes a third-party secure vendor mail program

**Bill ID:** A5640
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5640 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5640)

## Summary

Establishes a third-party secure vendor mail program for the delivery of all products to incarcerated individuals housed at correctional facilities.
