# Relates to the automated storage and dispensing of controlled substances using an automated dispensing device

**Bill ID:** S5914BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S5914BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5914BB)

## Summary

Relates to the automated storage and dispensing of controlled substances using an automated dispensing device at licensed nursing homes, residential health care facilities and hospices.
