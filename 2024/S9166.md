# Appropriates certain monies for capital improvements to the Kew Gardens, Lefferts Boulevard bridge

**Bill ID:** S9166
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9166 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9166)

## Summary

Appropriates $2,000,000 for capital improvements, including but not limited to structural and full remediation of the outside wall of the Kew Gardens, Lefferts Boulevard bridge.
