# Relates to providing loan forgiveness for agriculture educators

**Bill ID:** A5479
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5479 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5479)

## Summary

Relates to providing loan forgiveness for agriculture educators who provide didactic instruction to students about agriculture, food, and natural resources.
