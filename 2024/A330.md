# Relates to the voting age

**Bill ID:** A330
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A330 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A330)

## Summary

Provides that the voting age in New York be 16 years old.
