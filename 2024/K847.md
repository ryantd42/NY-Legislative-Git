# Honoring Lavocat's Family Greenhouse and Nursery upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the Large Business of the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 21, 2024

**Bill ID:** K847
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K847 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K847)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 847
 
BY: M. of A. Norris
 
  HONORING  Lavocat's  Family Greenhouse and Nursery
  upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of
  the Large Business of the Year Award by the Clarence
  Chamber of Commerce on March 21, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  the
achievements  of  those  individuals  and  businesses  that  contribute
substantially to the economic vitality  of  their  communities,  thereby
enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Lavocat's Family  Greenhouse  and  Nursery  upon  the  occasion  of  its
designation  as recipient of the Large Business of the Year Award by the
Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be celebrated at its Annual Awards Gala
on Thursday, March 21, 2024, at Samuel's Grande Manor in  Clarence,  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Lavocat's  Family  Greenhouse  and  Nursery  is  a
second-generation family business,  established  in  1980,  in  Clarence
Center, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In 2014, the growing business built their state-of-the-art
garden center at 8441 County Road in East Amherst; and
 
  WHEREAS, Lavocat's Family Greenhouse  and  Nursery  grows  over  800
varieties  of annuals, perennials, and vegetable plants in their one and
a half acre  greenhouse,  and  is  home  to  the  largest  selection  of
houseplants in Western New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Additionally,  the nursery offers a wide variety of shrubs
and trees along with mulch, topsoil, and  natural  stone  products;  the
landscape  division  specializes  in  residential  and  commercial
installation and  clean-ups;  for  do-it-yourselfers,  Lavocat's  offers
landscape  design  and  delivery  services to make every home's exterior
charming and aesthetic; and
 
  WHEREAS, Inside the garden center, patrons can immerse themselves in
a tranquil environment equipped with an impressive Koi Pond and tropical
garden area; the friendly staff provides excellent customer service  and
can answer all gardening questions; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Lavocat's  Family Greenhouse and Nursery is also dedicated
to giving back to the community in a variety  of  ways;  throughout  the
year, they open their doors to help other businesses sell their products
and  services  in  different  vendor  events  and  other  partnership
opportunities; they also work  with  many  small  and  large  non-profit
organizations through different fundraisers and donations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  team  at  Lavocat's  has  also  been  focusing  on
sustainability initiatives; this includes trending  towards  using  100%
biodegradable  pots  and  growing  containers; they also offer a plastic
return program, recycling and reusing customers plastic containers; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success of Lavocat's Family Greenhouse and Nursery is
in direct correlation to the efforts of its dedicated owners and  staff,
whose  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an
impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising
standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of the institution and
the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges  Lavocat's  Family  Greenhouse  and  Nursery's  success,
recognizes  its  contributions  to  the  local  and State economies, and
extends its highest commendation to  the  business  and  its  employees,
fully  confident  that,  in  its  future, it will enjoy the same success
which has so characterized its past; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor honoring Lavocat's Family Greenhouse and Nursery upon the occasion
of  its designation as recipient of the Large Business of the Year Award
by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 21, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Lavocat's Family Greenhouse and Nursery, Clarence Center,
New York.
