# Increases the statewide energy storage capacity

**Bill ID:** A8614
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Didi Barrett
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8614 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8614)

## Summary

Increases the statewide energy storage capacity to six gigawatts by the year 2030.
