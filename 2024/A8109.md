# Prohibits the relocation of migrants from the City of New York to Dutchess county

**Bill ID:** A8109
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8109 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8109)

## Summary

Prohibits the relocation of migrants from the City of New York to Dutchess county for the purpose of permanent or temporary housing.
