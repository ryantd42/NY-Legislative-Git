# Provides for the review of policies and practices pertaining to emerging infectious disease outbreaks in correctional facilities

**Bill ID:** S5468
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5468 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5468)

## Summary

Provides for the review of policies and practices pertaining to infectious disease outbreaks in correctional facilities, including the treatment and prevention of the disease among incarcerated individuals and staff.
