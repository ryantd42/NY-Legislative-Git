# Includes certain lands used in single operation for the production of tree nuts in the definition of agricultural land

**Bill ID:** A5928
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5928 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5928)

## Summary

Includes certain lands used in single operation for the production of tree nuts in the definition of agricultural land; includes tree nuts in the definition of fruits.
