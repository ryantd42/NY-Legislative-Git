# Allows an authorized organization to receive a license to hold a game of chance on Easter Sunday and Christmas Day

**Bill ID:** A4901
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A4901 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4901)

## Summary

Allows an authorized organization to receive a license to hold a game of chance on Easter Sunday and Christmas Day; eliminates time restrictions for such games held on a Sunday.
