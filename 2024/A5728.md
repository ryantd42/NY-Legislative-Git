# Excludes unemployment compensation from state income tax

**Bill ID:** A5728
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phara Souffrant Forrest
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5728 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5728)

## Summary

Excludes unemployment compensation from being included in the computation of state income tax.
