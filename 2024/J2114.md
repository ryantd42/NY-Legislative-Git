# Commending Hadji Court No. 62 of Buffalo, New York upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Illustrious Commandress Ball

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2114 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2114)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2114
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING Hadji Court No. 62 of Buffalo, New York
  upon  the occasion of hosting its Annual Illustrious
  Commandress Ball
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize  that
the  quality  and  character  of life in the communities across New York
State are reflective of the concerned and  dedicated  efforts  of  those
organizations and individuals who would devote themselves to the welfare
of the community and its citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  Hadji  Court  No.  62 of Buffalo, New York upon the occasion of
hosting its Annual Illustrious Commandress Ball at the  Buffalo  Airport
Hotel from September 6-8, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  year's  Annual  Illustrious  Commandress Ball honors
Illustrious Commandress Dt. Alexis P. Onyszkiewicz of  Hadji  Court  No.
62; and
 
  WHEREAS, Illustrious Commandress Dt. Alexis P. Onyszkiewicz, through
her  strong  leadership,  steady  teachings, and unwavering love for her
court, community,  family,  and  her  fellow  sisters,  has  shown  that
leadership can be done with true grace and elegance; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  women  of  Hadji  Court No. 62 continue to show their
efforts with their most honorable deeds and efforts to positively impact
their community and continue to show their desire to improve the  places
they live and worship for all; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Hadji  Court  No.  62 has shown the strong qualities of an
organization that has taken due  notice  of  their  collective  actions,
while  uplifting,  developing,  and showing the way for the youth in the
community, as well as being a caring hand for our beloved elders; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of Hadji Court No. 62 is in direct  correlation
to  the  efforts  of  its  members, whose involvement is, and always has
been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment,  an  unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  and institutions of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Hadji Court No. 62 of Buffalo, New York  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting its Annual Illustrious Commandress Ball; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Illustrious Commandress Dt. Alexis P.  Onyszkiewicz,  and
Hadji Court No. 62.
