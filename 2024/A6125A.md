# Requires victims of firearm violence be offered and/or provided psychiatric care

**Bill ID:** A6125AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6125AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6125AA)

## Summary

Requires victims of firearm violence be offered and/or provided psychiatric care.
