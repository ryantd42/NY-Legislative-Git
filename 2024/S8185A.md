# Relates to interactive gaming

**Bill ID:** S8185A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8185A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8185A)

## Summary

Authorizes interactive gaming and interactive lottery gaming in the state of New York.
