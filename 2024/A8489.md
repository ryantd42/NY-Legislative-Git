# Relates to the renewable energy outreach and community planning program; repealer

**Bill ID:** A8489
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Didi Barrett
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A8489 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8489)

## Summary

Relates to the renewable energy outreach and community planning program; creates reporting requirements.
