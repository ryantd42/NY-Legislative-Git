# Establishes a clean and resilient energy program

**Bill ID:** S4946
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4946 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4946)

## Summary

Establishes a clean and resilient energy program to encourage the development of clean and resilient behind the meter distributed energy resource projects.
