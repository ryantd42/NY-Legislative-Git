# Requires businesses who offer delivery services to provide markings for their vehicles and drivers

**Bill ID:** A8458
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeff Gallahan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8458 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8458)

## Summary

Requires businesses who offer delivery services to provide markings for their vehicles and drivers; provides penalties for failing to do so.
