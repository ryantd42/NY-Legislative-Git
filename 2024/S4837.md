# Expands farm brewery, cidery, winery and distillery privileges

**Bill ID:** S4837
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4837 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4837)

## Summary

Expands the privileges of farm breweries, cideries, wineries and distilleries to allow for the sales of "farm brewed" alcoholic beverages for off-premises consumption.
