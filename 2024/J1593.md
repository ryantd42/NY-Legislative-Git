# Congratulating the Poland Boys Soccer Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHAA Class D Championship

**Bill ID:** J1593
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1593 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1593)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1593
 
BY: Senator WALCZYK
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Poland  Boys Soccer Team upon
  the  occasion  of  winning  the  NYSPHAA  Class  D
  Championship
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Poland Boys Soccer Team upon the occasion of winning the NYSPHAA Class D
Championship; to the praise and applause  of  their  excited  fans,  the
Tornadoes  put  up  a  competitive, long battle, resulting in the Poland
Boys Soccer Team to become a first-time State Championship-winning  team
on Saturday, November 11, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's colors of blue and orange,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Tornadoes throughout their journey  as  they  ended  their
season with an impressive 20-3 overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In a sport such as soccer, which demands athletic prowess,
skill, speed, and agility, Head Coach Greg  Haver,  with  his  depth  of
knowledge  and  coaching  experience,  worked hard to hone the skills of
this championship team,  teaching  these  outstanding  athletes  lessons
which will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Coach Haver and all the outstanding athletes on the Poland
High  School  Boys  Soccer  Team  have  clearly  utilized  dedication,
determination,  and  teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the
spirit of  excellence  which  is  a  tradition  of  their  school;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of the Poland High  School  Boys  Soccer  Team:
Noah  Lamphere, Dax Coe, Kade Haver, Landon Rommel, Brendan Parow, Tommy
Haver, Aidan Alsheimer, Kaiden Stemmer, Grady Dutcher, Daimeon Morrison,
Daetyn Jones, Carter Cookinham, Troy Haver, Jason Victor, Elijah  Jones,
Luke  Malin,  Wyatt  Nebush,  Caleb  Forbes,  Stanley  Swanson,  Michael
Anderson, Colin Haver, Nate Fullem, and Head Coach Greg Haver  on  their
outstanding season and overall team record; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members of the Poland High School Boys  Soccer  Team,
and Head Coach Greg Haver.
