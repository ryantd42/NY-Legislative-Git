# Memorializing Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim November 17, 2024 as Young Entrepreneur Day in the State of New York

**Bill ID:** K2276
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2276 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2276)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2276
 
BY: M. of A. Rules (Bendett)
 
  MEMORIALIZING  Governor  Kathy  Hochul to proclaim
  November 17, 2024 as Young Entrepreneur Day  in  the
  State of New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend those distinguished citizens who would devote their life's  work
and  purpose  to  community  service, contributing to the welfare of the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim Sunday, November 17,  2024
as Young Entrepreneur Day; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Young  entrepreneurs are the doers of their generation and
by building a company from the ground up,  they  serve  our  communities
with dedication and create jobs for citizens of New York State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body that those who
enhance the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown  a
long  and  sustained  commitment to excellence certainly have earned the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim November 17, 2024 as Young
Entrepreneur Day; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to The Honorable Kathy Hochul, Governor of the State of  New
York.
