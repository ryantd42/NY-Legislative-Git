# Relates to mandatory continuing education requirements for coroners and coroner's deputies

**Bill ID:** S9578
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9578 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9578)

## Summary

Requires 30 hours of mandatory continuing education every three years for coroners and coroner's deputies.
