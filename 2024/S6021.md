# Relates to creating a distinctive plate for combat veterans having served in the navy, marine corps, air force or coast guard

**Bill ID:** S6021
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6021 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6021)

## Summary

Relates to creating a distinctive plate for combat veterans having served in the navy, marine corps, air force or coast guard.
