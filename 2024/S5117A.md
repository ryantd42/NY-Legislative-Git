# Relates to site mapping for emergency response data and school safety plans

**Bill ID:** S5117A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5117A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5117A)

## Summary

Authorizes the provision of site mapping for emergency response data with school safety plans; provides for the funding of such mapping data.
