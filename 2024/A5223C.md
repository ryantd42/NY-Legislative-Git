# Relates to designating the Zone Sergeant James G. Sweeney Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** A5223C
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5223C PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5223C)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the town of East Fishkill the "Zone Sergeant James G. Sweeney Memorial Bridge".
