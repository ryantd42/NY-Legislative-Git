# Requires that there be a one hundred foot buffer zone around abortion clinics

**Bill ID:** A9835A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amanda Septimo
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9835A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9835A)

## Summary

Requires that there be a one hundred foot buffer zone around abortion clinics to avoid harassment of patients.
