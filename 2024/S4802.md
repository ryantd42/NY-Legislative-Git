# Exempts certain not-for-profit corporations from the real estate transfer tax if such conveyance is for the purpose of providing food for free to persons experiencing food insecurity

**Bill ID:** S4802
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4802 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4802)

## Summary

Exempts certain not-for-profit corporations from the real estate transfer tax if such conveyance is to a food relief organization for the purpose of providing food for free to persons experiencing food insecurity.
