# Relates to the period of probable usefulness of zero-emission school buses

**Bill ID:** S9292AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9292AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9292AA)

## Summary

Lowers the period of probable usefulness of zero-emission school buses from twelve years to eight years.
