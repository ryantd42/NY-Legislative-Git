# Equips all ambulances and emergency ambulance service vehicles with stop-arms

**Bill ID:** A8890
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8890 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8890)

## Summary

Equips all ambulances and emergency ambulance service vehicles with stop-arms; provides rules and regulations shall be promulgated to provide for such equipment.
