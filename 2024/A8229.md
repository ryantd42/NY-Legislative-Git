# Relates to the establishment of rates of payment for medical assistance for needy persons

**Bill ID:** A8229
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8229 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8229)

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of health to set rates of payment for medical assistance for needy persons to reflect more current base year information.
