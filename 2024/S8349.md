# Exempts from the payment of sales tax the purchase of oral hygiene products

**Bill ID:** S8349
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Samra Brouk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8349 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8349)

## Summary

Exempts from the payment of sales tax the purchase of oral hygiene products.
