# Establishes the state flag for raising awareness of neurodiversity

**Bill ID:** A5780
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rebecca Seawright
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5780 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5780)

## Summary

Establishes the "Neurodiversity Flag" as the official state flag for raising awareness of neurodiversity.
