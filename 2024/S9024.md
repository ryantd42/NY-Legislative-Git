# Raises the minimum fee for temporary interpreters

**Bill ID:** S9024
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Sean Ryan
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9024 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9024)

## Summary

Raises the minimum fee for temporary interpreters; requires that town and village courts share in the compensation of such temporary interpreters.
