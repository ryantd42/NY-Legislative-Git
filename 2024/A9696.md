# Relates to owner liability for failure of a driver to stop for a school bus displaying a red visual signal and stop-arm

**Bill ID:** A9696
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Eric Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9696 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9696)

## Summary

Relates to the fine schedule for owner liability for failure of a vehicle operator to stop for a school bus displaying a red visual signal and stop-arm.

 dollars for each violation for the failure to respond  to  a  notice  of
 liability within the prescribed time period.
