# Prohibits the operation of unmanned aircraft, including drones, over school grounds or critical infrastructure

**Bill ID:** A8176B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8176B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8176B)

## Summary

Prohibits the operation of unmanned aircraft, including drones, over school grounds or critical infrastructure; provides penalties for violations.
