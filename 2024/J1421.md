## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1421
 
BY: Senator COMRIE
 
  CONGRATULATING  Marie Dinkins upon the occasion of
  celebrating her 100th Birthday
 
  WHEREAS, Senior citizens bring a wealth of experience and  knowledge
to  the  increasingly  active  roles they play in today's society; their
past contributions and future participation are a  vital  part  of,  and
valuable asset to, the fabric of community life and activity; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the custom of this Legislative Body to commemorate
and recognize certain milestones celebrated by citizens  of  this  great
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  Marie  Dinkins upon the occasion of her 100th Birthday, to
be celebrated on Saturday, September 12, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Marie Dinkins was very active in her community, credited as
an original member of the TriAngular Block Association, now known as the
Greater TriAngular Civic Association, which she says was  formed  "right
at her kitchen table"; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout her 65 years living in the GTC community, Marie
Dinkins has proved a passionate, dedicated, and  upstanding  individual,
devoting  countless  hours  of  her  time  to  the  betterment  of  her
surroundings; she is a true asset and role model to the citizens of  her
neighborhood, this State, and the Nation at large; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Marie  Dinkins, with her zest for life, has truly inspired
and enhanced the lives of her family and friends; she has proudly raised
two beloved children,  Gary  Jerome,  now  gone  to  rest,  and  Valerie
Lynette, who worked for the City of New York as a NYC Correction Officer
and  as  a  NYPD  Detective;  she is further blessed to call Lisachante,
Lauren Marie, and Maya Angela her granddaughters; and
 
  WHEREAS, This exceptional centenarian has experienced the incredible
joys and sorrows characteristic of  and  reserved  for  those  with  the
stamina and courage to savor a full life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Marie  Dinkins  has enriched the lives of those around her
through her joyous and sincere love for others and through the  profound
charm and wisdom which comes only from a fullness of years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of  this Legislative Body to publicly
recognize those who have reached such a  remarkable  age  and  who  have
witnessed  and  celebrated  the  innovations, cultural developments, and
magnificent achievements of this country during the last century,  while
themselves  contributing  to  the  growth  and  excellence of this great
Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Marie  Dinkins  upon the occasion of celebrating her 100th
Birthday; and be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Marie Dinkins.
