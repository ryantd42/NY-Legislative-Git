# Establishes the JFK Innovation Center

**Bill ID:** S5012
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5012 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5012)

## Summary

Establishes the JFK Innovation Center to launch SmartCities and CleanTech innovations.

 AN ACT to establish the JFK Innovation Center
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. Notwithstanding another section  of  law,  there  shall  be

 New York state agency or agencies and a private-sector company or compa-

 be located near the JFK International Airport in the borough of  Queens.
 The  center  shall  assist  new and startup companies develop by testing
 clean technology, automation, robotics, data  analytics,  smart  infras-
 tructure management, and other technologies.
