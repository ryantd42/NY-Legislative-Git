# Increases the classification of misdemeanor hate crime offenses to a felony

**Bill ID:** A6086
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Simcha Eichenstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6086 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6086)

## Summary

Increases the sentencing classification of misdemeanor hate crime offenses or of an attempt or conspiracy to commit a specified offense that is a misdemeanor to a class E felony.
