# Relates to the availability and use of opioid antagonists on trains

**Bill ID:** A9595
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9595 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9595)

## Summary

Requires the availability and permits the use of opioid antagonists on trains.
