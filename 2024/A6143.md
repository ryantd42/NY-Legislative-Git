# Prohibits the use of cell-site simulators by law enforcement officers

**Bill ID:** A6143
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6143 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6143)

## Summary

Prohibits the use of cell-site simulators by law enforcement officers; provides a right of action for violations of such prohibition.
