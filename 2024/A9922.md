# Requires that patients be presented the opportunity to register to make an anatomical gift during health care visits

**Bill ID:** A9922
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9922 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9922)

## Summary

Requires that patients be presented the opportunity to register to make an anatomical gift in the "donate life registry" for organ, eye and tissue donations during health care visits.
