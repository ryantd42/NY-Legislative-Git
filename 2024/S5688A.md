# Requires notaries public and commissioners of deeds to complete and retain certain documents relating to the transfer of residential real property

**Bill ID:** S5688AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S5688AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5688AA)

## Summary

Requires notaries public and commissioners of deeds to complete and retain certain documents relating to the transfer of residential real property; relates to forgery and offering a false document concerning residential real property.
