## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2684
 
BY: Senator O'MARA
 
  HONORING Elizabeth Kelley upon the occasion of her
  designation  as recipient of the Norman R. McConney,
  Jr. Award for EOP Excellence by The State University
  of New York
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of  this  Legislative  Body  to  honor  the
students  of  today,  and  leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and
achievements best exemplify the ideals  and  values  cherished  by  this
great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Elizabeth Kelley upon the occasion of her designation  as  recipient  of
the  Norman  R.  McConney,  Jr.  Award  for  EOP Excellence by The State
University of New York on Thursday, April 18, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Educational  Opportunity  Program  (EOP)  is  a
comprehensive  support  program  which  plays  a pivotal role in opening
doors to a SUNY education for academically disadvantaged  students  from
low-income  families  in the State of New York; EOP is one of the oldest
and most successful college access programs in the nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This auspicious  award  honors  the  legacy  of  Norman  R.
McConney,  Jr.,  a  University  at  Albany graduate who alongside former
Assembly  Deputy  Speaker  Arthur  O.  Eve,  created  the  Educational
Opportunity  Program,  a  program which has changed the lives of tens of
thousands of students over its more than 50 years; and
 
  WHEREAS, This special honor recognizes EOP students who  have  shown
tremendous leadership academically and socially on their campus, and who
have  overcome  unique  challenges  on  their path to success; Elizabeth
Kelley is one such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS, Elizabeth  Kelley  is  in  the  Liberal  Arts  &  Sciences:
Humanities/Social  Sciences  A.S.  program  at  SUNY  Corning  Community
College, serves as an EOP Peer  Coach,  EOP  Activity  Coordinator,  and
works on campus with the College's dining service; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  proven leader within EOP, Elizabeth Kelley possess both
the motivation and the promise to succeed in higher education; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the State of New York, the strength  of  our
communities,  and  the  vitality  of  American  society depend, in great
measure, upon the dedication of young people like Elizabeth Kelley,  who
use their considerable talents and resources to serve others; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Elizabeth  Kelley,  in achieving this high academic honor,
may take just pride in the inspiration and contribution to the spirit of
excellence she brings to her family, friends, community and school; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is moved to honor Elizabeth Kelley, a
young woman and able scholar, recognizing the value of  her  labors  and
the promise of her future; now, therefore, be it
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Elizabeth Kelley upon the occasion of her designation as recipient
of the Norman R. McConney, Jr. Award for EOP Student Excellence, and  to
extend  to  her the best wishes of this Legislative Body for a future of
purposeful success and well-being; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Elizabeth Kelley.
