# Creates the New York state public sector employee loan forgiveness program

**Bill ID:** A4468
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Durso
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4468 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4468)

## Summary

Creates the New York state public sector employee loan forgiveness program for graduates of SUNY or CUNY schools employed in a state or local public sector position.
