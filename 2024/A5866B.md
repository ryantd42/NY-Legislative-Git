# Makes eligible for provisional appointment and authorizes Paul Strobel to take the competitive civil service examination for the position of part-time police officer for Columbia county

**Bill ID:** A5866BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5866BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5866BA)

## Summary

Makes eligible for provisional appointment and authorizes Paul Strobel to take the competitive civil service examination for the position of police officer and be placed on the eligible list for employment as a part-time police officer for the Columbia County Sheriff's Office.
