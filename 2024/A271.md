# Requires the disclosure of charges for the installation, removal or use of an air conditioning unit and the pro-rating of charges for the use of an air conditioner in certain housing developments

**Bill ID:** A271
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Latrice Walker
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A271 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A271)

## Summary

Relates to prohibiting surcharges for the installation, removal or use of an air conditioning unit.
