# Relates to the filing of financial disclosure statements

**Bill ID:** A5308AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5308AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5308AA)

## Summary

Relates to the filing of financial disclosure statements; relates to the definition of "filing rate".
