# Authorizes a retroactive real property tax exemption for Hicksville United Methodist Church in the county of Nassau

**Bill ID:** S9178
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Rhoads
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9178 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9178)

## Summary

Authorizes a retroactive real property tax exemption for Hicksville United Methodist Church in the county of Nassau for general taxes years 2022 and 2023 and school taxes years 2021-2022 and 2022-2023.
