# Relates to the sentencing for the crime of aggravated cruelty to animals

**Bill ID:** S5325
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S5325 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5325)

## Summary

Enacts "Tucker's law"; removes the provision that provides that any term of imprisonment for a violation of aggravated cruelty to animals may not exceed two years.
