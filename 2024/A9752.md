# Authorizes one percent of mobile sports tax revenue be used for sports programs for underserved youths

**Bill ID:** A9752
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica P. Wallace
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9752 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9752)

## Summary

Authorizes one percent of mobile sports tax revenue, but not less than five million dollars, to be used for sports programs for underserved youths.
