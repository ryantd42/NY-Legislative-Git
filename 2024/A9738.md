# Permits only one traffic infraction per traffic stop for having tinted windows on a motor vehicle

**Bill ID:** A9738
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9738 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9738)

## Summary

Permits only one traffic infraction per traffic stop for having tinted windows on a motor vehicle.
