# Relates to establishing a commission to rename the Robert Moses State Park to reflect on the history of Long Island

**Bill ID:** A4650
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4650 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4650)

## Summary

Establishes a nine member commission to rename the Robert Moses State Park to reflect on the history of Long Island.
