# Commending Women Empowerment Ministry upon the occasion of hosting its Free Senior Luncheon on July 20, 2023

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J522 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J522)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 522
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING the Women Empowerment Ministry upon the
  occasion of hosting its Free Senior Luncheon on July
  20, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the Women Empowerment Ministry upon the occasion of hosting its
Free Senior Luncheon on Thursday, July 20, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, The theme for this  year's  Free  Senior  Luncheon  is  "No
Senior Left Behind"; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Women Empowerment Ministry is an organization created
10 years ago by Winnifred Grant, with the purpose to  inspire  women  to
become more productive members of their communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, The mission of the Women Empowerment Ministry is to empower
women through positive and social changes to help them achieve immediate
and lasting goals in their lives; and
 
  WHEREAS, For the past four years, the Women Empowerment Ministry has
been  feeding  senior  citizens of New York State in and around Flatbush
communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition, the Women  Empowerment  Ministry  has  donated
items  of  clothing  to  women's  shelters,  donated prom dresses to the
Westinghouse Vocational High School, encouraged  women  to  be  business
owners,  given  gifts  to  women  during  Women's  History  Month,  and
acknowledged black business owners during Black History Month; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Women  Empowerment  Ministry  has  worked  alongside
Community  Concerns  Network  to  organize  immigration  seminars  and
workshops; and
 
  WHEREAS, To keep members involved, the  Women  Empowerment  Ministry
features  community  events  like  their  annual trip for prayers at the
White House; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Women Empowerment Ministry is in  direct
correlation  to  the  efforts  of  its leadership and its members, whose
involvement is, and always has  been,  characterized  by  an  impressive
commitment,  an  unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard of
excellence in all  endeavors  on  behalf  of  the  institution  and  the
community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and commend
the  members  of  the  Women Empowerment Ministry, past and present, who
 
have labored so conscientiously to secure and  improve  the  quality  of
life for the community it serves; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Women Empowerment Ministry upon the occasion of hosting  its
Free Senior Luncheon on July 20, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Women Empowerment Ministry.
