# Sets certain requirements for side under-ride guards

**Bill ID:** S7979
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7979 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7979)

## Summary

Sets certain requirements for side under-ride guards for state fleet vehicles.

 OF NEW YORK. THE COMMISSIONER SHALL BE AUTHORIZED  TO  PROMULGATE  ANY
 RULES  NECESSARY  TO INSPECT SIDE UNDER-RIDE GUARDS AND SIDE UNDER-RIDE
 GUARD SPECIFICATIONS FOR COMPLIANCE WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SUBDI-

  55. (A) FOR THE PURPOSES OF THIS SUBDIVISION, "SIDE UNDER-RIDE  GUARD"

  (B)  TWENTY-FOUR  MONTHS  FROM THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF THIS SUBDIVISION,
 ALL VEHICLES WITH A MANUFACTURER'S GROSS VEHICLE WEIGHT RATING EXCEEDING
 TEN THOUSAND POUNDS IN THE STATE  FLEET  SHALL  BE  EQUIPPED  WITH  SIDE
 UNDER-RIDE  GUARDS  AFFIXED  TO THE SIDES OF SUCH VEHICLES IN A MANNER
 CONSISTENT WITH RULES AND REGULATIONS THAT  SHALL  BE  PROMULGATED  BY
 THE  COMMISSIONER,  IN CONSULTATION WITH THE COMMISSIONER OF TRANSPORTA-
 TION. THE COMMISSIONER SHALL HAVE THE AUTHORITY TO PROMULGATE ANY  RULES
 NECESSARY  TO ADMINISTER  THE PROVISIONS OF THIS SUBDIVISION, INCLUDING
 BUT NOT LIMITED TO RULES GOVERNING WHEN  THE  INSTALLATION  OF  SIDE
 UNDER-RIDE  GUARDS  ON CERTAIN VEHICLES  IN THE STATE FLEET IS IMPRACTI-
 CAL OR  WILL  DISRUPT  PROVISION  OF  PUBLIC  SAFETY  OR  PUBLIC  HEALTH
 SERVICES  AND  WILL NOT  BE REQUIRED.  SUCH RULES MAY ALSO GOVERN WHEN
 WRITTEN EXEMPTIONS MAY BE AUTHORIZED, INCLUDING DUE  TO  LIMITATIONS  IN
 VENDOR  CAPACITY  TO  SUPPLY SIDE UNDER-RIDE GUARDS AND CIRCUMSTANCES IN
 WHICH STATE-OWNED VEHICLES ARE IN THE ORDER CYCLE FOR  REPLACEMENT.  THE
 COMMISSIONER  SHALL BE  AUTHORIZED TO PROMULGATE ANY RULES NECESSARY TO
 INSPECT SIDE UNDER-RIDE GUARDS AND SIDE UNDER-RIDE GUARD  SPECIFICATIONS
 FOR COMPLIANCE WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SUBDIVISION.
