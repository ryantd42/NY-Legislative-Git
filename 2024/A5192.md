# Provides for penalties for causing the death of an animal

**Bill ID:** A5192
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5192 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5192)

## Summary

Provides for penalties for causing the death of an animal; makes it a class D felony.
