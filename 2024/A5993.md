# Requires diversity, inclusion and elimination of bias training for certain medical personnel as part of continuing medical education requirements

**Bill ID:** A5993
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5993 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5993)

## Summary

Requires diversity, inclusion and elimination of bias training for physicians, physician assistants and nurses as part of continuing medical education requirements; establishes such training shall be a biennial requirement.
