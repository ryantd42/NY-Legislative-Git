# Extends provisions of law relating to monitor of the Wyandanch union free school district

**Bill ID:** S9102
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9102 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9102)

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to monitor of the Wyandanch union free school district from June 30, 2025 until June 30, 2029.
