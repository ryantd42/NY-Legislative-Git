# Honoring Shauna Fung Yee upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of a 2023 Women-N-Motion Empowerment Award by the Young, Gifted & Black Entrepreneurial Awards Program

**Bill ID:** J561
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J561 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J561)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 561
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING  Shauna Fung Yee upon the occasion of her
  designation as recipient of  a  2023  Women-N-Motion
  Empowerment  Award  by  the  Young,  Gifted  & Black
  Entrepreneurial Awards Program
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  take  note  of
and  publicly  acknowledge individuals who have distinguished themselves
through their exemplary careers, accomplishments, and  purposeful  lives
of service to their community and their profession; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Shauna Fung Yee upon the occasion of her designation as recipient  of  a
2023  Women-N-Motion  Empowerment  Award  by  the  Young, Gifted & Black
Entrepreneurial Awards Program to be celebrated at its Awards Brunch  on
Sunday, March 26, 2023, at the Simpson Restaurant and Bar, Brooklyn, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Founded  by  Carl O. Gray, the Young, Gifted & Black (YGB)
Entrepreneurial Awards Program recognizes individuals  who  continue  to
reach  levels  of  great  success  through  hard work and determination,
proving to be inspiring role models to their community and peers; and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore, in conjunction with the observance of  Women's
History  Month,  this  auspicious  occasion features several outstanding
women who are leaders, motivators,  business  professionals,  innovators
and entrepreneurs; and
 
  WHEREAS, Shauna Fung Yee is a Jamaican-born renowned Caribbean radio
producer,  marketing  and  communications  strategist,  music publicist,
music  business  consultant,  A&R  executive,  songwriter,  and  former
international hair model; she has a 21-year track record working at some
of  Jamaica's leading radio stations, and 18 years of experience working
in the music business, which has given her the opportunity to  associate
and  work  with some of the powerhouse players from the Caribbean, South
America, and Africa; and
 
  WHEREAS, Her illustrious career in radio began in 1999 at Hot 102 FM
as a producer for veteran radio broadcaster Barry  Gordon,  followed  by
KLAS  ESPN Sports Radio in 2001; in 2006 she joined Power 106 FM and its
sister  station  Music  99  FM  as  a  business  development  executive,
copywriter, and producer; and
 
  WHEREAS,  On September 30, 2020, Shauna Fung Yee resigned from radio
to focus wholeheartedly on her brand as a music business consultant,  to
continue  her  studies  in journalism, and to invest quality time in her
company, Down 2  Earth  Productions  (D2E),  the  only  Caribbean  music
business  company to represent African and Latin artists since 2015, and
its subsidiary company Down 2 Earth Agency, a business  development  and
management consulting firm; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Shauna  Fung  Yee  established Down 2 Earth Productions in
2004, a fully integrated music company representing some of the  world's
 
premier  music  luminaries and offering a full suite of services; dubbed
"Caribbean Home of Afrobeats and Latin," it is the only of its  kind  in
the  Caribbean  and  is  the Caribbean home to various African and South
American artists; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  2019,  she  partnered with List Entertainment, home to
global  Afrobeats  superstar  Wizkid,  and  Africa's  biggest  talent
management  company  owned  by  Sunday  Are, Nigeria's and Africa's most
influential music business manager; and
 
  WHEREAS, Due to her network of elite  corporate  figures,  in  2020,
Shauna Fung Yee established Down 2 Earth Agency; its clientele includes:
Jamaica's  Nigeria  High  Commission;  Jamaica  Stock Exchange, which is
ranked as one of the world's top  stock  exchanges;  ICWI,  one  of  the
Caribbean's  leading insurance companies; Bridge 99 FM, Jamaica; Hott 93
FM, Trinidad and Tobago;  The  Wave  FM,  St.  Lucia;  and  VOB  92  FM,
Barbados; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  late  2022, Shauna Fung Yee wrote two songs, one to be
featured on one of Kenya's top music stars forthcoming  album,  and  the
other for a Columbian Reggaeton artist; both songs are set to release in
2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  music  business mentor and connoisseur, Shauna Fung Yee
appreciates all music genres and has a knack for the  business  side  of
music;  she  continues  to strive to make a positive contribution to the
global music  industry  as  well  as  to  aspiring  and  emerging  music
professionals; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Shauna  Fung Yee's years of experience and resilience give
her insight into the ever-changing  music  industry  and  the  corporate
world, by associating with various music stars and business leaders from
different cultures and countries; her work is truly an expression of who
she is and what she represents; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With a steadfast and unwavering commitment to giving back,
over the years she has given of her  time  and  energies  to  inner-city
schools  in  Kingston and organizations such as the Jamaica Constabulary
Force (JCF); and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by  an
individual for the benefit of others which Shauna Fung Yee has displayed
throughout her life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative Body to commend
individuals of outstanding character,  who  have  shown  initiative  and
commitment  toward  constantly pursuing higher goals for themselves, and
acting as role models of leadership to be emulated  by  both  peers  and
young people; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Shauna Fung Yee upon the occasion of her designation as  recipient
of  a 2023 Women-N-Motion Empowerment Award by the Young, Gifted & Black
Entrepreneurial Awards Program; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Shauna Fung Yee.
