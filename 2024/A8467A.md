# Relates to the dispensing of drug adulterant testing supplies by health care professionals or pharmacists

**Bill ID:** A8467AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A8467AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8467AA)

## Summary

Includes xylazine and other substances in the definition of drug adulterant testing supplies for the purposes of the dispensing of such supplies by health care professionals or pharmacists to any person.
