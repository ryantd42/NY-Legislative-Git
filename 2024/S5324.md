# Imposes term limits for members of the assembly and senate of New York

**Bill ID:** S5324
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5324 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5324)

## Summary

Provides that members of the assembly and senate of New York shall not serve more than six two-year terms.
