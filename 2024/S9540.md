# Prohibits data brokers from selling the personal information of current and former military servicemembers

**Bill ID:** S9540
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9540 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9540)

## Summary

Prohibits data brokers from selling the personal information of current and former military servicemembers or their households without consent.
