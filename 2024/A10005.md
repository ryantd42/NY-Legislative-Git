# Renames the "West Islip Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association" to the "West Islip Exempt Firefighter's Benevolent Association"

**Bill ID:** A10005
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Durso
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10005 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10005)

## Summary

Renames the "West Islip Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association" to the "West Islip Exempt Firefighter's Benevolent Association"; changes such association's purpose.
