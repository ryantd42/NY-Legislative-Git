# Requires schools to provide teachers with written informational material on the use of epinephrine auto-injectors

**Bill ID:** S209AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S209AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S209AA)

## Summary

Requires schools to provide teachers with written informational material on the use of epinephrine auto-injectors.
