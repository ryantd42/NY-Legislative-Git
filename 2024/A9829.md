# Prohibits chain restaurants from charging extra for non-dairy alternatives in beverages

**Bill ID:** A9829
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9829 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9829)

## Summary

Prohibits chain restaurants from charging extra for non-dairy alternatives in beverages.

 AN ACT to amend the general business law,  in  relation  to  prohibiting

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section 1. The general business law is amended by adding a new section

  ยง  391-W.  EXTRA  CHARGE FOR NON-DAIRY ALTERNATIVES PROHIBITED. 1.  NO
 CHAIN FOOD SERVICE ESTABLISHMENT SHALL CHARGE AN ADDITIONAL FEE FOR  THE
 USE OF NON-DAIRY ALTERNATIVES IN BEVERAGES.
  2. FOR THE PURPOSES OF THIS SECTION, THE TERM:

 ARE:
  I.  OPERATED  UNDER  THE  SAME  NAME AT DIFFERENT LOCATIONS WITHIN THE
 STATE; AND
  II. OPERATED UNDER THE SAME OWNERSHIP OR BY FRANCHISE AGREEMENT.
  (B) "NON-DAIRY ALTERNATIVES" SHALL MEAN PLANT-BASED PRODUCTS THAT  ARE
 MARKETED AND SOLD AS ALTERNATIVES TO MILK AND ARE MADE FROM NUTS, SEEDS,
 RICE, OATS, OR LEGUMES.
