# Relates to insurance coverage for dental night guards

**Bill ID:** S9929
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9929 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9929)

## Summary

Provides that dental insurance coverage shall include coverage for night guards; defines dental night guards; provides that dental night guards shall be covered under Medicaid.
