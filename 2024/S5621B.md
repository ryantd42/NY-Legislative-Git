# Relates to reporting of digital assets on the annual statement of financial disclosure

**Bill ID:** S5621BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5621BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5621BB)

## Summary

Relates to reporting of digital assets on the annual statement of financial disclosure filed with the legislative ethics commission or the joint commission on public ethics.
