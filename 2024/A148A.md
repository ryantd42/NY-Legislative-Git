# Prohibits state reimbursement of campaign and political committees and legal defense funds for payments made on behalf of the criminal or civil defense of a state employee

**Bill ID:** A148A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Catalina Cruz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A148A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A148A)

## Summary

Prohibits state reimbursement of campaign and political committees, or legal defense funds, for payments made on behalf of the criminal or civil defense of a state employee.
