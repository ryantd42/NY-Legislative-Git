# Relates to assault on a member of an auxiliary police program

**Bill ID:** A5544
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Doug Smith
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5544 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5544)

## Summary

Includes injury to an on-duty auxiliary police officer within the elements of assault in the second degree.
