# Relates to requiring SUNY and CUNY to inform eligible high school seniors of their college options and inform them of their acceptance to community college

**Bill ID:** S7980
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7980 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7980)

## Summary

Requires SUNY and CUNY to inform eligible high school seniors at NY public high schools of their college options and inform them of their acceptance to community college; and to invite them to apply to a state college and provide financial aid information.
