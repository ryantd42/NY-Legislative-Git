# Grants George Jensen an earlier starting date with the New York City Department of Sanitation

**Bill ID:** S5053
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5053 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5053)

## Summary

Grants George Jensen an earlier starting date with the New York City Department of Sanitation of August 1, 2021 as his current start date is November 1, 2021 due to a clerical error.
