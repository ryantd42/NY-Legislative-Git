# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian, Julian Piedrahita, of Oceanside High School, in recognition of his outstanding accomplishments

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2498 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2498)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2498
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024  Valedictorian, Julian
  Piedrahita, of Oceanside High School, in recognition
  of his outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions  to  honor  the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2024 Valedictorian, Julian Piedrahita,  of  Oceanside  High  School,  in
recognition of his outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian,  Julian  Piedrahita, represents the best of
developed potential inherent in our most precious resource,  our  youth;
his achievements have brought enduring honor to his family and community
and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  Valedictorian,  Julian  Piedrahita,  takes  leave  of
Oceanside High School, he bestows  a  legacy  of  academic  achievement,
school  involvement,  and  community service to which others may aspire;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Valedictorian, Julian Piedrahita, may now stand with  pride
as  he assesses his achievements and experiences the satisfaction of his
labors and the joy  of  his  accomplishments,  eager  to  face  the  new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2024 Valedictorian,  Julian  Piedrahita,  of  Oceanside
High  School,  in recognition of his outstanding accomplishments; and be
it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Valedictorian, Julian Piedrahita.
