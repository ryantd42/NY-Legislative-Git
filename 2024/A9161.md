# Grants campus peace officers employed by the city university of New York police powers; repealer

**Bill ID:** A9161
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Benedetto
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9161 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9161)

## Summary

Grants campus peace officers employed by the city university of New York police powers on its campuses and other property of the city university, including any portion of a public highway which crosses or abuts such property.
