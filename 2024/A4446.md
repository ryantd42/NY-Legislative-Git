# Relates to the approval of policy forms

**Bill ID:** A4446
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4446 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4446)

## Summary

Identifies certain group policies, group contracts and certificates issued thereunder exempting them from filing and approval requirements.
