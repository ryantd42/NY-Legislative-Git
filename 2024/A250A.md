# Authorizes payments to nonparticipating or nonpreferred providers of ambulance services licensed under article 30 of the public health law

**Bill ID:** A250A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Authorizes payments to nonparticipating or nonpreferred providers of ambulance services licensed under article 30 of the public health law.
