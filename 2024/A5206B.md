# Adds Doodletown Brook to the definition of inland waterways eligible for the local waterfront revitalization program

**Bill ID:** A5206BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Christopher Eachus
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5206BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5206BB)

## Summary

Adds Doodletown Brook to the definition of inland waterways eligible for the local waterfront revitalization program.
