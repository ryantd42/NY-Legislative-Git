# Congratulating the Hobart Men's Ice Hockey Team upon the occasion of capturing their second consecutive NCAA Division III Men's Ice Hockey Championship on March 23, 2024

**Bill ID:** J2202
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2202 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2202)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2202
 
BY: Senator HELMING
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Hobart  Men's Ice Hockey Team
  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing  their  second
  consecutive  NCAA  Division  III  Men's  Ice  Hockey
  Championship on March 23, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body has long valued  athletic  excellence
at  the  professional,  collegiate,  and  school  levels,  recognizing
exceptional  achievements,  outstanding  accomplishments,  and  national
championships; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Excellence  and  success  in  competitive  sports  can  be
achieved only through strenuous practice, team  play  and  team  spirit,
nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Hobart Men's Ice Hockey Team upon the occasion of capturing their second
consecutive NCAA Division III Men's Ice  Hockey  Championship,  held  on
Saturday,  March  23,  2024,  at  the Koeppel Community Sports Center in
Hartford, Connecticut; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and  applause  of  their  excited  fans,  the
storied  tradition  of  intercollegiate  athletic  success at Hobart and
William Smith Colleges has again risen to a new level  when  the  Hobart
Men's  Ice  Hockey Team bested Trinity College in a 2-0 shutout victory;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The achievement further cements Hobart College's reputation
as one of the most solid and consistent Division  III  programs  in  the
country,  finishing the season with a 28-2-1 record, consistently ranked
among the top Division III programs in the nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Hobart Statesmen program has been built by  Head  Coach
Mark  Taylor,  who  for  the past 24 years, has brought together skilled
collegiate hockey players to  create  a  team  balanced  by  outstanding
defense, wise strategy, and teamwork, where every player was prepared to
get the job done one shift at a time; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Hobart  Hockey  took  advantage  of  home  ice in downtown
Geneva's fabled "Cooler" with an undefeated 17-0-0 record, their  second
undefeated home rink record in the program's history; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Among  Hobart  Hockey's  other  achievements  this  season
included winning their second consecutive NEHC Tournament  Championship,
and  outscoring  their  opponents  by over 130 points across the 2023-24
season and allowing only 4 goals in the NCAA tournament; and
 
  WHEREAS, Celebration and recognition is shared by  the  current  and
past  parents, alumni players, college leadership, and boosters who have
supported Hobart Ice  Hockey  for  decades;  the  leadership  of  former
Athletic  Director Bill Stiles, who made the decision in 1977 to elevate
the decades-old Hobart Club Hockey Team to the NCAA Division III program
greatly inspired the success of the team; and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach Mark Taylor, his expert coaching staff, and  all
of  the  outstanding  athletes  on the Hobart Men's Ice Hockey Team have
clearly utilized dedication, determination and teamwork in  providing  a
lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of
their  school;  the excitement and inspiration they have provided hockey
players and fans of all ages throughout  this  exciting  and  impressive
season is remarkably commendable; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of the Hobart  Men's  Ice  Hockey  Team:  Gagik
Malakyan,  Bauer  Morrissey, Matthieu Wuth, Cooper Swift, Austin Mourar,
Matthew Perryman, Conor  Lally,  Kevin  Lassman,  and  Jared  Patterson;
Forwards:  Ethan Mulhearn, Tanner Daniels, Brandon Sacchetti, Wil Crane,
Matthew Iasenza, Luka Aquaro, Jonah Alexander, Mason Mara, Shane  Shell,
Tristan  Fasig, Christian Duvall, Artem Buzoverya, Kahlil Fontana, Chris
Duclair, Tanner Hartman, Ignat Belov, and Logan  Gotinsky;  Goaltenders:
Damon  Beaver, Graham Burke, and Mavrick Goyer; Hobart and William Smith
President, Mark Gearan; Director of Athletics, Brian Miller; Head Coach,
Mark Taylor; Assistant Coaches, Niko Kovachis and  Derek  Berry;  Hockey
Operations  Assistants,  Joel  Stoneham  and  Mateo  Albinati;
Faculty-Athletic Fellow, John Halfman; Head Equipment Coordinator, Kevin
McDonald;  Associate  Director  of  Athletic  Communications,  Mackenzie
Larsen;  Assistant  Athletic  Trainer,  Sara  Moore; Assistant Equipment
Manager, Nick Prince; and  Graduate  Assistant,  Aaron  Smith  upon  the
occasion  of  capturing their second consecutive NCAA Division III Men's
Ice Hockey Championship on March 23, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Hobart Men's Ice Hockey Team.
