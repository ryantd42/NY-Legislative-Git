# Establishes the New York manufacturing adequate domestic equipment credit (NY MADE)

**Bill ID:** S8853
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8853 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8853)

## Summary

Establishes the New York manufacturing adequate domestic equipment credit (NY MADE); defines medical equipment; makes related provisions.
