# Relates to a no fare program for transportation on New York city transit authority systems, the Long Island Rail Road and the Metro-North Commuter Railroad Company for certain police and retired police officers

**Bill ID:** S8397
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8397 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8397)

## Summary

Includes the New York city transit authority systems and the Metro-North Commuter Railroad Company in the no fare program for police officers; includes retired police officers.
