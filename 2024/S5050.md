# Relates to protecting MTA workers from harassment

**Bill ID:** S5050
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5050 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5050)

## Summary

Expands the definition of aggravated harassment in the second degree to include harassing any MTA worker while performing their job duties.
