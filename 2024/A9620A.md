# Establishes a two generation pilot program

**Bill ID:** A9620A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Maritza Davila
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9620A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9620A)

## Summary

Establishes a two generation pilot program to promote family stability and children's school readiness; provides for the repeal of this section in five years.
