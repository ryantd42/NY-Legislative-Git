# Establishes the crime of package theft in the fourth, third, second and first degrees

**Bill ID:** A8473
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8473 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8473)

## Summary

Establishes the crime of package theft in the fourth, third, second and first degrees; designates such crimes as a class A misdemeanor, class E felony, class D felony, and class B felony, respectively; provides instances that are not a defense to such crimes; provides for certain defenses to such crimes.

 PARTS OF A MEDICAL DEVICE OR DEVICES.
  2. FOR PURPOSES OF THIS SECTION,  THE  TERM  "SERIOUS  ADVERSE  HEALTH
 CONDITION" SHALL MEAN ANY CONDITION SUFFERED BY A PERSON WHICH:
  (A)  SUBSTANTIALLY PROLONGS SUCH PERSON'S POTENTIAL OR EXISTING HEALTH
 CONDITION;
  (B) CAUSES SUCH PERSON TO LOSE A LIMB OR ORGAN;
  (C) CAUSES SUCH PERSON TO PERMANENTLY OR FOR  A  PROLONGED  PERIOD  OF
 TIME LOSE OR HAVE LIMITED A BODILY FUNCTION;
  (D)  SEVERELY  DEGRADES  SUCH PERSON'S QUALITY OF LIFE FOR A PROLONGED
 PERIOD OF TIME;
  (E) SHORTENS THE LENGTH OF SUCH PERSON'S LIFE;
  (F) CAUSES INCAPACITATION OF SUCH PERSON; OR
  (G) CAUSES THE DEATH OF SUCH PERSON.
  PACKAGE THEFT IN THE FIRST DEGREE IS A CLASS B FELONY.

  1. IN ANY PROSECUTION FOR PACKAGE THEFT  IN  THE  FOURTH,  THIRD,  AND

 § 165.80 PACKAGE THEFT; DEFENSES.
  1.  NOTWITHSTANDING  ANY  OTHER  DEFENSES  PROVIDED FOR IN LAW, IN ANY
 PROSECUTION FOR PACKAGE THEFT IN THE FOURTH, THIRD, AND  SECOND  DEGREE,
 IT SHALL BE A DEFENSE THAT:
  (A) THE PERSON WHO TOOK THE PACKAGE GENUINELY BELIEVED THE PACKAGE WAS
 THEIRS OR INTENDED FOR THEM;
  (B)  THE PERSON WHO TOOK THE PACKAGE GENUINELY BELIEVED THAT THEY WERE
 AUTHORIZED OR HAD THE CONSENT OF THE OWNER TO TAKE THE PACKAGE; OR
  (C) THERE WAS A GENUINE DISPUTE OVER THE OWNERSHIP OF THE CONTENTS  OF
 THE PACKAGE.
  2.  IN  ADDITION  TO  THE  DEFENSE PROVIDED IN SUBDIVISION ONE OF THIS
 SECTION, IN ANY PROSECUTION FOR PACKAGE THEFT IN THE  FIRST  DEGREE,  IT
 SHALL  BE A DEFENSE THAT THE WORDS, MARKINGS, OR SYMBOLS INDICATING THAT
 THE PACKAGE CONTAINED PRESCRIPTION MEDICINE, A MEDICAL DEVICE OR DEVICES
 OR PARTS OF A MEDICAL DEVICE OR DEVICES WAS  OBSCURED  AS  A  RESULT  OF
 DAMAGE  TO  THE  PACKAGE OR THE IMPROPER PLACEMENT OF SHIPPING MATERIALS
 COMPLETELY OBSCURING THE WORDS, MARKINGS, OR SYMBOLS DONE  BY  THE  MAIL
 CARRIER  OR  THE  MANUFACTURER  OR  DISTRIBUTOR OF THE MEDICINE, MEDICAL
 DEVICE OR DEVICES OR PARTS OF THE MEDICAL DEVICE OR DEVICES.
