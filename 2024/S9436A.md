# Directs the commissioner of motor vehicles to conduct a study of official inspection stations, safety inspections and fees

**Bill ID:** S9436AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9436AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9436AA)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of motor vehicles to conduct a study to examine and update the requirements of safety inspections conducted to ensure the operational safety of all motor vehicles; requires a report; makes related provisions.
