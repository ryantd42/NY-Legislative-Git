# Makes technical changes relating to the rescindment of certificates of authority

**Bill ID:** S8355
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8355 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8355)

## Summary

Makes technical changes relating to the rescindment of certificates of authority clarifying that a failure to file a report or return relates only to the person's obligation to pay taxes.
