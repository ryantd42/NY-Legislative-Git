# Prohibits lenders from blocking access to funds from a credit line mortgage

**Bill ID:** A6358A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A6358A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6358A)

## Summary

Prohibits lenders from blocking access to funds from a credit line mortgage.
