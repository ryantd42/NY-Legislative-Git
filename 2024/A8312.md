# Provides for school district employees who have been provisionally appointed to a position for at least nine months without an exam being offered for such position to receive a permanent appointment

**Bill ID:** A8312
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nader Sayegh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8312 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8312)

## Summary

Provides for school district employees who have been provisionally appointed to a position for at least nine months without an exam being offered for such position to receive a permanent appointment in such position.

  7. PROVISIONAL APPOINTMENTS WITHIN SCHOOL DISTRICTS.  NOTWITHSTANDING
