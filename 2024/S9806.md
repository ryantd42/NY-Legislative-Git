# Relates to self-exclusion provisions

**Bill ID:** S9806
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S9806 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9806)

## Summary

Relates to self-exclusion provisions; provides for moneys forfeited by self-exclusion to be deposited in certain gaming commission accounts.
