# Establishes a captive insurance program for commuter vans that are engaged in the business of carrying or transporting eight to twenty-four passengers for hire and establishes a commuter van trust fund

**Bill ID:** S4862
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [S4862 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4862)

## Summary

Establishes a captive insurance program for commuter vans that are engaged in the business of carrying or transporting eight to twenty-four passengers for hire; establishes the commuter van trust fund.
