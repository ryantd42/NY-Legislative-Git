# Relates to state HIV rental assistance funding

**Bill ID:** S5720
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S5720 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5720)

## Summary

Provides for sharing of savings among the local district and the health payor to support program administrative costs and provide ongoing HIV housing subsidies.
