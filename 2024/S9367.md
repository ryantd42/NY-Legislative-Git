# Extends certain provisions relating to the management of migratory game birds

**Bill ID:** S9367
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9367 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9367)

## Summary

Extends certain provisions relating to the management of migratory game birds to December 31, 2027.
