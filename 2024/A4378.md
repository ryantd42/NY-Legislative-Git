# Relates to eligibility requirements for rent increase exemptions for persons with disabilities

**Bill ID:** A4378
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4378 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4378)

## Summary

Relates to eligibility requirements for rent increase exemptions for persons with disabilities; includes children who qualify as a person with a disability.
