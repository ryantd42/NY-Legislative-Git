# Prohibits rent minimums in mortgages

**Bill ID:** S9234
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Liz Krueger
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9234 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9234)

## Summary

Prohibits rent minimums in all mortgages regardless of when issued.
