# Authorizes the Praise Tabernacle Church of God to receive retroactive real property tax exempt status

**Bill ID:** S9163AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9163AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9163AA)

## Summary

Authorizes the Praise Tabernacle Church of God to receive retroactive real property tax exempt status with respect to the 2021-2022, 2022-2023, and 2023-2024 assessment rolls.
