# Requires third party oversight over the state's contracting agency's procurement procedure

**Bill ID:** S5077
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5077 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5077)

## Summary

Relates to requiring third party oversight over the state's contracting agency's procurement procedure specific to the use of minority- and women-owned businesses.
