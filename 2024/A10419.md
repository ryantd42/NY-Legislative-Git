# Relates to the frequency of submission of community health assessment reporting

**Bill ID:** A10419
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10419 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10419)

## Summary

Relates to the frequency of submission of community health assessment reporting; requires a report no more frequently than every six years.
