# Relates to the suspension of tolls collected during a state declaration of disaster emergency

**Bill ID:** S7729BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7729BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7729BA)

## Summary

Provides for the suspension of the collection of tolls on public roads during a state declaration of disaster emergency on the New York state thruway.
