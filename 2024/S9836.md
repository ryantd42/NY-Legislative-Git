# Requires quarterly reporting on the amounts awarded and received from the opioid stewardship fund

**Bill ID:** S9836
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9836 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9836)

## Summary

Requires quarterly reporting on the opioid stewardship fund, including the names of the recipients and the amounts awarded and received.
