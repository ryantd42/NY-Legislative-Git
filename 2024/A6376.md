# Requires gas stations to maintain backup generators for gas pumps

**Bill ID:** A6376
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6376 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6376)

## Summary

Requires gas stations to maintain backup generators capable of operating the gas pumps in the event of loss of electricity.
