# Prohibits deceptive rate advertising for certain accommodations

**Bill ID:** A5926A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5926A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5926A)

## Summary

Prohibits deceptive rate advertising for certain accommodations; requires advertised rates to be inclusive of all taxes and automatic fees; requires the non-inclusive rate, and an itemized list of taxes and automatic fees to be provided to the consumer prior to a person finalizing his or her purchase.
