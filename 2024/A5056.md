# Relates to extending the exclusion of indebtedness contracted for sewage facilities

**Bill ID:** A5056
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Conrad
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5056 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5056)

## Summary

Extends the period of time for which a municipality has the power to exclude indebtedness contracted for sewage facilities.
