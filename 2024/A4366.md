# Relating to the timeliness of judicial decisions

**Bill ID:** A4366
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4366 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4366)

## Summary

Relates to the timeliness of judicial decisions; establishes the failure to make a timely decision shall be considered misconduct.
