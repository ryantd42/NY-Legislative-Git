# Eliminates earning limitations for retired persons in positions of public service

**Bill ID:** A6149
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6149 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6149)

## Summary

Eliminates earning limitations for retired persons in positions of public service from 2023 forward.
