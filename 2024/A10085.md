# Relates to the issuance, records, and delivery of certificates of title; requires the establishment of an electronic lien and title program

**Bill ID:** A10085
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10085 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10085)

## Summary

Relates to the issuance, records, and delivery of certificates of title; requires the establishment of an electronic lien and title program.

  §  2119-A.  ELECTRONIC  LIEN  AND  TITLE PROGRAM. (A) THE COMMISSIONER

 LIENHOLDERS WHO THE COMMISSIONER DETERMINES ARE NOT NORMALLY ENGAGED  IN
