# Provides a tax deduction for fertility preservation services

**Bill ID:** S8946
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8946 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8946)

## Summary

Provides a tax deduction for fertility preservation services in an amount up to $1,000.
