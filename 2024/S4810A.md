# Allows for the taxation of state property in the city of Ogdensburg

**Bill ID:** S4810A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4810A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4810A)

## Summary

Allows for the taxation of state property located in the city of Ogdensburg, except the lands controlled and operated on by the Ogdensburg Bridge & Port Authority.
