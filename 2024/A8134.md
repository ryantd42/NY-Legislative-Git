# Establishes the New York state rural mobile communications fund

**Bill ID:** A8134
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Smullen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8134 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8134)

## Summary

Establishes the New York state rural mobile communications fund to provide grants for the purpose of increasing cellular service in low- to no-coverage areas.
