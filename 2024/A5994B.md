# Relates to research and restricting advertisement and promotion of electronic cigarettes

**Bill ID:** A5994BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Taylor Darling
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5994BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5994BA)

## Summary

Prohibits clauses restricting certain research and restricts advertisement and promotion of electronic cigarettes.
