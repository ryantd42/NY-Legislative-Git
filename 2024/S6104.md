# Creates a transportation subsidy to increase access to water safety instruction

**Bill ID:** S6104
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Ramos
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6104 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6104)

## Summary

Creates a transportation subsidy to increase access to water safety instruction.
