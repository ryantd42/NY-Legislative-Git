## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 595
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Dr.  Timothy  E. Sams, Ph. D. upon the
  occasion of his inauguration as the sixth  President
  of  the State University of New York at Old Westbury
  on April 14, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this  Legislative  Body  to  acknowledge
those  leaders in education whose professional lives and civic endeavors
serve to enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend  Dr.  Timothy  E.  Sams,  Ph.  D.  upon  the  occasion  of  his
inauguration  as the sixth President of the State University of New York
at Old Westbury to be celebrated on Friday, April 14, 2023, at the  John
& Lillian Maguire Theater in the Campus Center; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Raised  by his paternal grandparents, Timothy E. Sams grew
up  on  the  south  side  of  Syracuse,  New  York,  where  he  was  a
high-achieving  student,  played  basketball and was an active member of
the local Boy Scouts; and
 
  WHEREAS, Timothy E. Sams earned  his  Bachelor  of  Arts  degree  in
History  and  Sociology from Union College in Schenectady, New York, his
Master of Arts degree in Africana studies from the State  University  of
New  York  at  Albany,  New  York,  and  then furthered his education by
obtaining his Ph.D.  in African American Studies from Temple University;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout his 20 year  career  in  higher  education,  Dr.
Timothy  E.  Sams  was  driven  by the principles of access, excellence,
success,  and  impact  to  achieve  student  transformation  and  social
progress; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  an  unwavering  commitment  to higher education, Dr.
Timothy E. Sams has worked with institutions around the country and  the
world;  he  moved  from  learning  the  fundamentals of building student
success programs to innovating new ones as a senior leader committed  to
educational  justice by inclusive excellence, praxis, and purpose-driven
education; and
 
  WHEREAS, In January of 2021, Dr. Timothy E. Sams  earned  tenure  at
SUNY  Old Westbury, and through his experience at respected institutions
such as Morehouse College and  Rensselaer  Polytechnic,  he  never  lost
sight  of  his  humble  beginnings  as  an  Upward Bound and Educational
Opportunity Program student and always strives to steer each  and  every
one of his students towards success; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body that those who
enhance the educational profile of their communities and  have  shown  a
long  and  sustained  commitment to the maintenance of high standards in
their profession, certainly have earned the recognition and applause  of
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Dr. Timothy E. Sams, Ph.D. upon the occasion of his inauguration
as the sixth President of the  State  University  of  New  York  at  Old
Westbury on April 14, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Dr. Timothy E. Sams, Ph. D.
