# Repeals the offense of unlawful possession of certain ammunition feeding devices

**Bill ID:** S6231
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6231 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6231)

## Summary

Repeals the offense of unlawful possession of ammunition feeding devices containing more than seven rounds of ammunition.
