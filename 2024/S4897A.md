# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Corporal Derick Franklin Brinckerhoff Memorial Highway"

**Bill ID:** S4897AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Rolison
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S4897AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4897AA)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the town of Fishkill, Dutchess county as the "Corporal Derick Franklin Brinckerhoff Memorial Highway".
