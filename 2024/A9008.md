# Establishes a public awareness campaign for maternal depression; appropriation

**Bill ID:** A9008
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9008 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9008)

## Summary

Establishes a public awareness campaign for maternal depression; makes an appropriation therefor.

  Section 1. Article 25 of the public health law is amended by adding  a

  §  2597.  PUBLIC  AWARENESS CAMPAIGN FOR MATERNAL DEPRESSION. THERE IS

 PROGRAM TO EDUCATE THE PUBLIC AND MATERNAL HEALTH CARE  PROVIDERS  ABOUT
 THE RISKS AND REMEDIES ASSOCIATED WITH MATERNAL DEPRESSION.
  § 2. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 97-bbbbb
 to read as follows:
  §  97-BBBBB.  MATERNAL DEPRESSION FUND. 1. THERE IS HEREBY ESTABLISHED
 IN THE JOINT CUSTODY OF THE STATE COMPTROLLER AND  THE  COMMISSIONER  OF
 TAXATION  AND  FINANCE  A  FUND  TO BE KNOWN AS THE "MATERNAL DEPRESSION
 FUND".
  2. THE FUND SHALL CONSIST OF ALL MONIES APPROPRIATED FOR ITS  PURPOSE,
 AND ALL MONIES REQUIRED BY ANY PROVISION OF LAW TO BE PAID INTO OR CRED-
 ITED TO SUCH FUND. NOTHING CONTAINED HEREIN SHALL PREVENT THE DEPARTMENT
 OF  HEALTH  FROM RECEIVING GRANTS, GIFTS OR BEQUESTS FOR THE PURPOSES OF
 THE FUND AS DEFINED IN THIS SECTION AND DEPOSITING THEM  INTO  THE  FUND
 ACCORDING TO LAW.
  3.  MONIES  SHALL BE PAYABLE FROM THE FUND ON THE AUDIT AND WARRANT OF
 THE STATE COMPTROLLER ON VOUCHERS APPROVED AND CERTIFIED BY THE  COMMIS-
 SIONER OF HEALTH.
  §  3. The sum of twenty million dollars ($20,000,000) is hereby appro-
 priated to the department of health out of any moneys in the state trea-
