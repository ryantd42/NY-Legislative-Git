# Establishes a registry for children's non-regulated camps; repealer

**Bill ID:** S8013
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8013 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8013)

## Summary

Requires the department of health to establish and maintain a registry for the purpose of providing education and outreach to children's non-regulated camps.
