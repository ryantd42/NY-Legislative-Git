# Removes library taxes of the Shelter Rock library district from real property located in that portion of such library district that is located within the Bryant library district

**Bill ID:** S4745
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4745 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4745)

## Summary

Removes library taxes of the Shelter Rock library district from real property located in that portion of such library district that is located within the Bryant library district.

 AN ACT in relation to removing library taxes of the Shelter Rock library
  district from real property located in that portion  of  such  library
  district that is located within the Bryant library district

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision  of  law,  the  Shelter
 Rock library district, located in the town of North Hempstead, county of
 Nassau,  shall not impose any library tax for the support of the library
 district upon any parcel of real property located  within  such  library
 district  which  is  also located within the Bryant library district and
 subject to real property taxes to support the Bryant  library  district.
 For  all purposes, any real property removed from the library portion of
 Shelter Rock library district taxes pursuant to this act shall be deemed
 to be within the service area of the Bryant library district established
 pursuant to an absolute charter granted  by  the  board  of  regents  on
 behalf  of the education department and shall be subject to taxation for
 the support of such district.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall apply to assess-
 ment rolls prepared on the basis of taxable status dates occurring on or
 after such date.
