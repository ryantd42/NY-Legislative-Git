# Congratulating Grayson Gall upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Girls Gymnastics Championship in both the uneven bars and balance beam events

**Bill ID:** J698
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J698 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J698)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 698
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Grayson  Gall upon the occasion of
  capturing the NYSPHSAA Girls Gymnastics Championship
  in both the uneven bars and balance beam  events  on
  March 4, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud to congratulate
Grayson  Gall  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA  Girls
Gymnastics  Championship in both the uneven bars and balance beam events
on Saturday, March 4, 2023, at Kenmore West High School in Buffalo,  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, A freshman on the New Hartford High School Girls Gymnastics
Team,  Grayson  Gall  won the uneven bars event with a score of 9.50 and
the  balance  beam  event  with  a  score  of  9.7,  leading  her  to  a
second-place  finish in the NYSPHSAA Girls Gymnastics Team Championship,
and helping her team to a first-place victory; and
 
  WHEREAS, Grayson Gall's overall record  is  outstanding;  throughout
the  season,  she  was  loyally  and  enthusiastically  supported by her
family, teammates, friends and the community at large; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sports competition instills the values of  teamwork,  pride
and  accomplishment, and Grayson Gall has clearly made a contribution to
the spirit of excellence which  is  a  tradition  of  her  school;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Grayson Gall upon the occasion of  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA
Girls  Gymnastics  Championship in both the uneven bars and balance beam
events on March 4, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Grayson Gall.
