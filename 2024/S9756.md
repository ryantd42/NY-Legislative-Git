# Extends provisions authorizing hunting big game by rifle in the county of Tompkins

**Bill ID:** S9756
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9756 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9756)

## Summary

Extends provisions authorizing hunting big game by rifle in the county of Tompkins until 2026.
