# Extends the authority of the county of Madison to impose an additional rate of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A6165
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian D. Miller
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A6165 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6165)

## Summary

Extends the authority of the county of Madison to impose an additional rate of sales and compensating use taxes for two years.
