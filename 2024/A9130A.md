# Relates to authorizing hunting big game by rifle in the county of Niagara

**Bill ID:** A9130AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A9130AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9130AA)

## Summary

Authorizes hunting big game by rifle in the county of Niagara except within the city of Niagara Falls, the city of Lockport and the city of North Tonawanda.
