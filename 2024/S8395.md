# Relates to directing the commissioner of health to issue a statewide standing order for the provision of doula services

**Bill ID:** S8395
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Samra Brouk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8395 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8395)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of health to issue a statewide standing order for the provision of doula services.
