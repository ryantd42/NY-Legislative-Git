# Enacts the "drag racing and speeding enforcement act"

**Bill ID:** S6178
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6178 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6178)

## Summary

Imposes five points on a person's license for being convicted of participating in speed contests and races.
