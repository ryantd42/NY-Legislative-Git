# Permits certain judges, justices and support magistrates to carry a firearm, rifle, or shotgun in a sensitive location

**Bill ID:** A7968AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A7968AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A7968AA)

## Summary

Permits certain judges, justices and support magistrates to carry a firearm, rifle, or shotgun in a sensitive location.
