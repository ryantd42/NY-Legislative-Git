# Prohibits the operation of unmanned aircraft over school grounds or critical infrastructure

**Bill ID:** S7723B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7723B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7723B)

## Summary

Prohibits the operation of unmanned aircraft, including drones, over school grounds or critical infrastructure; provides penalties for violations.
