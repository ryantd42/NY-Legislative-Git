# Relates to the establishment of a healthy birth grant demonstration program; excludes healthy birth grants from income for certain purposes

**Bill ID:** S9940
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9940 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9940)

## Summary

Relates to the establishment of a healthy birth grant demonstration program; excludes healthy birth grants from income for certain purposes.

 DATE  OF  THIS SECTION, SUCH PROGRAM SHALL DISBURSE A GRANT OF ONE THOU-
 SAND EIGHT HUNDRED DOLLARS PER CHILD TO ALL HOUSEHOLDS WITH NEW  PARENTS

 IN A HOUSEHOLD MEET THE DEFINITION OF "NEW PARENT" AS PROVIDED IN  PARA-

 EVENT  THAT  A  PREGNANT INDIVIDUAL IS DEEMED PRESUMPTIVELY ELIGIBLE FOR
 MEDICAL ASSISTANCE  UNDER  SUBDIVISION  SIX  OF  SECTION  THREE  HUNDRED
 SIXTY-FOUR-I  OF  THIS TITLE AND RECEIVES PERINATAL CARE AFTER THE THIRD
 TRIMESTER, SUCH PREGNANT INDIVIDUAL SHALL RECEIVE SUCH  BIRTH  GRANT  AT
 THE  TIME THAT SUCH INDIVIDUAL IS DETERMINED TO BE ACTUALLY ELIGIBLE FOR
 SUCH ASSISTANCE UNDER SECTION THREE HUNDRED SIXTY-SIX OF THIS TITLE.
  (B) FOR THE PURPOSES OF THIS SECTION, "NEW  PARENTS"  SHALL  MEAN  ALL
 LEGAL  PARENTS  OR  GUARDIANS  WHO MEET THE ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS FOR
 MEDICAL ASSISTANCE UNDER SECTION THREE HUNDRED SIXTY-SIX OF THIS  TITLE,

 STATE OR LOCAL INCOME TAX.
