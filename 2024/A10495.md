# Requires body armor worn by New York city emergency services personnel to be replaced every five years

**Bill ID:** A10495
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10495 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10495)

## Summary

Requires body armor worn by New York city emergency services personnel to be replaced every five years.

 AN ACT to amend the administrative code of the  city  of  New  York,  in
  relation  to requiring the city of New York to replace body armor worn
  by emergency services personnel every five years

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1. Subdivision a of section 15-148 of the administrative code
 of the city of New York, as added by local law number 60 of the city  of
 New York for the year 2024, is amended to read as follows:
  a.  The  commissioner shall provide body armor to all employees of the
 department within the bureau of emergency medical services  who  provide
 emergency  medical  services.  Such body armor shall be of the type that
 the commissioner determines would be most suitable for the protection of
 such employees, and shall meet ballistic and stab  resistance  standards
 of  the  national  institute of justice or any successor standards. SUCH
 BODY ARMOR SHALL BE REPLACED EVERY FIVE YEARS.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
