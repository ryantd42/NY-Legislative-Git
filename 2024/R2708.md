# Amends a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for the New York state economic development assistance program

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R2708 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/R2708)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2708
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  Amending  Senate  Resolution  R3725  of  2010,
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  the  New  York  State  Economic
  Development Assistance Program established  pursuant
  to an appropriation in the 2008-09 state fiscal year
  and in Part QQ of chapter 57 of the laws of 2008
 
  RESOLVED,  That  funds  originally  appropriated in 2008 pursuant to
part QQ of chapter 57 of the laws of 2008 and by section 5 of chapter 53
of the laws of 2008 and reappropriated pursuant to section 1 of  chapter
54  of  the  laws  of 2024 which enacts the capital budget appropriating
monies for the New York State Economic Development  Assistance  Program.
Such  funds  shall  be  apportioned  pursuant to a plan setting forth an
itemized list of grantees with the  amount  received  by  each,  or  the
methodology  for  allocating  such  appropriation.  Such plan shall be
subject to the approval of the temporary president of the senate and the
director of the budget and thereafter shall be included in a  resolution
calling  for  the  expenditure  of such monies, which resolution must be
approved by a majority vote of all members elected to the senate upon  a
roll  call  vote,  in accordance with schedule which shall amend, remove
from, or add to the schedule originally approved  in  Senate  Resolution
R3725  of  2010  and as amended by Senate Resolutions R2697, 2698, 2699,
2700, 2701 and 2702 of 2011, R5596 of 2012,  R2682  of  2013,  R6136  of
2014,  R3055 of 2015, R6508 of 2016, R3068 of 2017, R5912 of 2018, R2144
of 2019, R3299 of 2020, R1248 of 2021, R2865 of 2022, and R1395 of 2023:
