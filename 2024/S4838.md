# Relates to using a portion of the public safety communications surcharge to support volunteer fire department initiatives

**Bill ID:** S4838
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4838 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4838)

## Summary

Relates to using a portion of the public safety communications surcharge to support volunteer fire department initiatives; establishes the New York state volunteer fire department auxiliary fund.
