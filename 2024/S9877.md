# Requires that any rules or regulations related to hydrofluorocarbon substances conform with any applicable rules and regulations set by the United States environmental protection agency

**Bill ID:** S9877
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9877 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9877)

## Summary

Requires that any rules or regulations related to hydrofluorocarbon substances conform with, and shall not exceed, any applicable rules and regulations set by the United States environmental protection agency.
