# Relates to payments of grants to farm operations by NYSERDA

**Bill ID:** A4792
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4792 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4792)

## Summary

Relates to payments of grants to farm operations by NYSERDA; provides that payment of such grants shall be made no later than ninety days after notification.
