# Establishes a tax credit for sewer lateral pipe replacement or repair

**Bill ID:** S5726
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S5726 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5726)

## Summary

Establishes a tax credit for the replacement or repair of sewer lateral pipes by homeowners who use the home as their principal residence and have an income of less than two hundred fifty thousand dollars.
