# Relates to enacting "Todd's Law"

**Bill ID:** A5402
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Doug Smith
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5402 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5402)

## Summary

Relates to sentencing and larceny of property of the elderly or disabled.
