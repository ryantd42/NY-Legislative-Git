# Directs the commissioner of motor vehicles to conduce a study of official inspection stations, safety inspections and fees

**Bill ID:** A8190AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [A8190AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8190AA)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of motor vehicles to conduct a study to examine and update the requirements of safety inspections conducted to ensure the operational safety of all motor vehicles; requires a report; makes related provisions.

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
