# Commemorating the Centennial Anniversary of the Lake Avenue Elementary School building in Saratoga Springs, New York

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2020 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2020)

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2020
 
BY: M. of A. Woerner
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  Centennial  Anniversary of the
  Lake Avenue Elementary School building  in  Saratoga
  Springs, New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend those institutions of  commitment  and  high  achievement  whose
purpose and endeavor have been dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in
the education of the youth of this noble Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  100th Anniversary of the Lake Avenue Elementary School
building located at 126 Lake Avenue in Saratoga Springs, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, To celebrate their many years of academic  excellence,  the
school  will  hold a Centennial Celebration for both current Lake Avenue
students, families and alumni on Saturday, May 4, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Lake Avenue Elementary School  building  was  built  in
1923,  and  has  remained an educational institution for a century; over
its distinguished ten decade history, the building has served as a  high
school,  a  junior high school and, in 1972, it became what it is today,
Lake Avenue Elementary School; and
 
  WHEREAS, Since its inception, multiple generations of families  have
come  to  the Lake Avenue school building to embark on their educational
journeys; and
 
  WHEREAS, Set in the heart of  Saratoga  Springs,  the  school  known
today as Lake Avenue Elementary has inspired generations of memories and
has been an incubator for success; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Steeped  in  a  proud  and  distinguished  history,  the
educators, staff, students and families of this renowned school building
may  look  back  with  pride  upon  its  humble  beginnings,  find  just
satisfaction  in  its  purposeful growth and hold true of purpose in its
commitment to serving the educational needs of the citizens of  Saratoga
County and beyond; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the Centennial Anniversary of  the  Lake  Avenue  Elementary
School  building in Saratoga Springs, noting the propriety of its goals,
the  constancy  of  its  commitment,  and  the  significance  of  its
accomplishments in preparing the youth of today and leaders of tomorrow;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted  to  Lake  Avenue  Elementary  School  Principal  Elizabeth
Carroll,  Saratoga  Springs  City School District Superintendent Michael
Patton, and School Board Members.
