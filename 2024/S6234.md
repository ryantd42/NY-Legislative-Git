# Establishes a personal income tax credit for the purchase of a gun safe

**Bill ID:** S6234
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6234 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6234)

## Summary

Establishes up to a $500 personal income tax credit for the purchase of a gun safe.
