# Bans unencapsulated expanded or extruded polystyrene foam in floating docks and floating platforms

**Bill ID:** S4974A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4974A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4974A)

## Summary

Bans unencapsulated expanded or extruded polystyrene foam in floating docks and floating platforms sold, offered for sale, or constructed in the state.
