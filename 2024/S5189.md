# Relates to websites maintained by a board of elections

**Bill ID:** S5189
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S5189 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5189)

## Summary

Requires a ".gov" domain name for websites maintained by a board of elections.
