# Relates to the study of the current prevalence of SNAP/EBT benefit fraud

**Bill ID:** S9649
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S9649 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9649)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of social services to study and report of the prevalence and threat of SNAP/EBT benefit fraud.
