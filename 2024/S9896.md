# Establishes a hydroelectric power tax credit

**Bill ID:** S9896
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9896 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9896)

## Summary

Establishes a hydroelectric power tax credit.
