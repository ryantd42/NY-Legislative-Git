# Provides that a voter may register to vote at any residence to which the voter maintains a continuous connection with an intent to remain

**Bill ID:** A10549
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10549 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10549)

## Summary

Provides that a voter may register to vote at any residence to which the voter maintains a continuous connection with an intent to return, including a second home or a vacation home or a home where a student lives while attending a college or university.
