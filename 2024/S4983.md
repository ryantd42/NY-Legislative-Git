# Designates August thirty-first as "Overdose Awareness Day"

**Bill ID:** S4983
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dean Murray
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S4983 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4983)

## Summary

Designates August thirty-first of each year as a day of commemoration to be known as "Overdose Awareness Day"; requires the flag to be flown at half-staff on such date.
