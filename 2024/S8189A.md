# Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to establish a pilot program to install a microgrid in Glenwood Houses

**Bill ID:** S8189A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8189A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8189A)

## Summary

Directs the New York state energy and research development authority to establish a pilot program to install a microgrid in Glenwood Houses; requires the authority to annually report on the results of such program and any recommendations for expansion.
