# Relates to the practice of natural hair care and braiding

**Bill ID:** A8624BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8624BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8624BB)

## Summary

Relates to the practice of natural hair care and braiding; creates an apprenticeship in such area; makes conforming technical changes.

 LIMITED TO, VARIOUS CURLY, COILY, WAVY OR  WIRY  PATTERNS,  COMBINATIONS
 AND CONFIGURATIONS, HAIR STRANDS, THICKNESS, AND VOLUMES OF HAIR.
