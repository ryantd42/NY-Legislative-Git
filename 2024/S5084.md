# Requires that all metropolitan transit authority buses, excluding express buses, are fare-free

**Bill ID:** S5084
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5084 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5084)

## Summary

Directs the metropolitan transit authority to establish and implement a no fare pilot program for transportation on all authority buses, excluding express buses, for all individuals.
