# Authorizes the city of New York to discontinue the use as parkland and alienate certain land within Flushing Meadows Corona Park

**Bill ID:** A5688
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5688 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5688)

## Summary

Authorizes the city of New York to discontinue the use as parkland and alienate certain land within Flushing Meadows Corona Park.

  §  4.  In  the  event  that the city of New York received any funding,

 this act, such parklands shall revert back  to  the  city  of  New  York
 department  of parks and recreation to be used for park and recreational
 purposes or for the purposes provided by chapter  729  of  the  laws  of
 1961, including any amendments thereto.
  § 7. This act shall take effect immediately.
