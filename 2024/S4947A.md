# Provides for the expansion of the New York bottle bill

**Bill ID:** S4947A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4947A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4947A)

## Summary

Expands the New York bottle bill by including more types of eligible beverages and additional penalties.
