# Relates to incorporating the cost of living adjustment into subsequent maximum state aid rates for providing care to foster children

**Bill ID:** A10647
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew Hevesi
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10647 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10647)

## Summary

Relates to incorporating the cost of living adjustment into subsequent annual maximum state aid base rates for providing care to foster children.
