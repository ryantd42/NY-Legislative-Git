# Provides for the licensing of nail salons

**Bill ID:** S5440
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5440 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5440)

## Summary

Imposes additional rules and requirements for the licensing of nail salons.
