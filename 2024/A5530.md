# Prohibits children twelve years old and younger from playing tackle football

**Bill ID:** A5530
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Benedetto
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5530 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5530)

## Summary

Prohibits children twelve years old and younger from playing tackle football.
