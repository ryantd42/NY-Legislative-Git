# Establishes a highway safety corridor on the Southern State Parkway located in Long Island and makes conforming changes; establishes a demonstration program

**Bill ID:** A6064
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6064 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6064)

## Summary

Establishes a highway safety corridor on the Southern State Parkway located in Long Island and makes conforming changes; relates to certain notices of liability; establishes a demonstration program implementing speed violation monitoring systems in the Southern State Parkway safety zone by means of photo devices.

 been expressly and in terms so amended or repealed.
