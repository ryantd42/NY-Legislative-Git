# Requires social services districts to maintain a waiting list of certain families applying for child care assistance

**Bill ID:** A8342
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8342 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8342)

## Summary

Requires social services districts to maintain a waiting list of certain families applying for child care assistance.
