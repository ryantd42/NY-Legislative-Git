# Authorizes the New York state environmental facilities corporation to award grants and loans to non-public entities for certain water quality projects

**Bill ID:** A9853
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9853 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9853)

## Summary

Authorizes the New York state environmental facilities corporation to award grants and loans to community water systems and non-community water systems for water quality projects that relate to the removal of perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS).
