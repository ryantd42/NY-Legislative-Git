# Relates to peace officers appointed by The Sea Gate Association

**Bill ID:** A5115
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alec Brook-Krasny
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5115 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5115)

## Summary

Removes certain restrictions on peace officers appointed by The Sea Gate Association.
