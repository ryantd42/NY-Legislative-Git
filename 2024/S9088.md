# Makes the crimes of menacing and stalking bail eligible

**Bill ID:** S9088
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9088 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9088)

## Summary

Makes the crimes of menacing and stalking bail eligible.
