# Relates to allowing for unlicensed individuals to collect throat or nasopharyngeal swab specimens and to perform certain nursing tasks during a state of disaster emergency

**Bill ID:** A8055
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8055 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8055)

## Summary

Allows for unlicensed individuals to collect throat or nasopharyngeal swab specimens and to perform certain nursing tasks during a state disaster emergency.
