# Requires the governor to meet with the assembly and with the senate once a month to answer questions relating to the state

**Bill ID:** A4417
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4417 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4417)

## Summary

Requires the governor to meet with the assembly and with the senate once a month for the purpose of answering questions relating to the strategic direction, financial status and general governance of the state.
