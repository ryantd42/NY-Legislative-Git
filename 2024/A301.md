# Relates to the automatic reinstatement of public assistance following the completion of mandated residential drug treatment

**Bill ID:** A301
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A301 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A301)

## Summary

Allows for the automatic reinstatement of public assistance following the completion of mandated residential drug treatment.
