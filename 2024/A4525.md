# Classifies criminal possession of a firearm from a class E felony to a class D felony

**Bill ID:** A4525
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Durso
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4525 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4525)

## Summary

Classifies criminal possession of a firearm from a class E felony to a class D felony.
