# Prohibits vaping in educational institutions; requires educational institutions to educate communities about health risks associated with vaping

**Bill ID:** A10639
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10639 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10639)

## Summary

Prohibits vaping in educational institutions; requires educational institutions to educate communities about health risks associated with vaping and post no-vaping signage on educational institution premises.
