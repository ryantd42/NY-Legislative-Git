# Establishes a crime for the possession of synthetic marihuana in correctional facilities

**Bill ID:** S4990
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4990 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4990)

## Summary

Establishes a crime for the possession of synthetic marihuana in correctional facilities.
