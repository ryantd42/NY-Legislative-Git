# Authorizes pharmacists to dispense HIV pre-exposure and post-exposure prophylaxis

**Bill ID:** A5995
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Didi Barrett
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5995 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5995)

## Summary

Authorizes pharmacists to dispense HIV pre-exposure and post-exposure prophylaxis.
