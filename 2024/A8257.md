# Removes the additional service charge for distinctive Elks Association license plates

**Bill ID:** A8257
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8257 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8257)

## Summary

Removes the additional $25 service charge for distinctive Elks Association license plates.
