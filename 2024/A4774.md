# Requires oil companies to clean up any spillage occurring during oil delivery

**Bill ID:** A4774
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4774 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4774)

## Summary

Requires oil companies to clean up any spillage occurring during oil delivery within 24 hours; imposes a fine of $500 for the first offense with $1000 for each additional offense.
