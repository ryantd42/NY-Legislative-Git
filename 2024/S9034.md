# Relates to electronic appearance in connection with a criminal action

**Bill ID:** S9034
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9034 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9034)

## Summary

Authorizes the court, in its discretion, to conduct an electronic appearance in connection with a criminal action in Monroe county.
