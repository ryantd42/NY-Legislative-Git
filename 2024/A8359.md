# Designates the Tuckahoe Road overpass above the Sprain Brook Parkway as the  Detective Sergeant Frank Gualdino Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** A8359
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nader Sayegh
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A8359 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8359)

## Summary

Designates the Tuckahoe Road overpass above the Sprain Brook Parkway in the city of Yonkers, county of Westchester as the "Detective Sergeant Frank Gualdino Memorial Bridge".
