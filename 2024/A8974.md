# Relates to the filling of vacancies in the office of lieutenant-governor

**Bill ID:** A8974
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Josh Jensen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8974 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8974)

## Summary

Requires the filling of vacancies in the office of lieutenant-governor be done subject to confirmation by a two-thirds vote in the senate and a two-thirds vote in the assembly.
