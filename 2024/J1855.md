## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1855
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  MOURNING  the death of Detective Sergeant Frank D.
  Gualdino,  Yonkers  police  officer,  distinguished
  citizen and devoted member of his community
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of this Legislative Body to convey its
grateful appreciation and heartfelt regret in recognition of the loss of
a courageous police officer who dedicated his purposeful life and career
in faithful service to his family and to the residents of  Yonkers,  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Frank  D.  Gualdino  died from injuries sustained in a car
crash on Thursday, December 1, 2022, at the age of 53; and
 
  WHEREAS, Frank D. Gualdino distinguished himself in  his  profession
and  by  his  sincere  dedication  and  substantial  contribution to the
welfare of his community; and
 
  WHEREAS, A native of Yonkers, New York, Frank  Daniel  Gualdino  was
born  on August 8, 1969 to Tereza and the late Francisco Gualdino, going
on to graduate from Sacred Heart High School in Yonkers and  received  a
degree  in  criminal  justice  from Westchester County Community College
hoping to use his degree to pursue his life-long  dream  of  becoming  a
police officer; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Frank  D. Gualdino joined the New York City Transit Police
Department in 1994 and upon graduation from the Transit  Police  Academy
he  received  the  sharpshooter  award,  beginning a decorated career of
continuously fulfilling his  dream  and  benefitting  all  of  those  he
served, eventually joining the Yonkers Police Department in 1998; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  his  24  year  service with the Yonkers Police
Department, Frank D. Gualdino worked in multiple departments such as the
3rd Precinct, Traffic Court and, after being promoted to  Sergeant,  the
Traffic/Motorcycle  Unit  where  he  always  carried out his duties with
grace, exuberance and diligence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Detective Sergeant Frank D. Gualdino will be sorely  missed
by his family, friends, brothers and sisters in blue, community, and New
York State as a whole; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Armed  with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of
compassion, Detective Sergeant Frank D. Gualdino leaves behind a  legacy
which  will  long  endure  the  passage  of  time  and  will remain as a
comforting memory to all he served and befriended; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
mourn  the death of Detective Sergeant Frank D. Gualdino, Yonkers police
officer, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community;  and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the family of Frank D. Gualdino and  the  Yonkers  Police
Department.
