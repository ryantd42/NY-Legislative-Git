# Relates to the Alert Volunteer Exempt Firefighter's Benevolent Association of Great Neck, New York

**Bill ID:** S9218
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9218 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9218)

## Summary

Relates to the name and the funds of the Alert Volunteer Exempt Firefighter's Benevolent Association of Great Neck, New York.

 AN ACT to amend the Nassau county civil divisions act,  in  relation  to
  the name and the funds of the corporation
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:

  Section 1. Sections 614.0, 614.1, 614.3 and 614.6 of the Nassau county
 civil divisions act, as added by chapter 73 of the  laws  of  1940,  are
 amended to read as follows:
  §  614.0  Incorporation.  The  membership  corporation  by the name of
 "Alert Volunteer Exempt  [Firemen's]  FIREFIGHTER'S  Benevolent  Associ-
 ation, of Great Neck, New York," is hereby continued.
  § 614.1 Persons eligible to membership. Any person who is now or here-
 after  becomes  a  member in good standing of the Alert Engine, Hook and
 Ladder and Hose Company No. 1, of Great Neck, New York, and  any  person
 residing in the area protected by such fire company who has reserved for
 a  period  of  five  years as a volunteer member of such company and has
 been honorably discharged therefrom, and any  person  residing  in  such
 area who has received his certificate as an exempt [fireman] FIREFIGHTER
 from  such  company  or  from any other volunteer fire company, shall be
 eligible to membership therein. Any [fireman] FIREFIGHTER, however,  who
 has  been  removed  for cause, expelled or dropped from the rolls of any
 fire company or department shall not be eligible to membership  in  such
 corporation.
  §  614.3  Powers and duties. a. Such corporation shall have all of the
 powers of a membership corporation, and the provisions of  law  relating
 to  membership corporations shall apply to such corporation except where
 they conflict with the provisions of this article.
  b. Such corporation shall have the power to become  a  member  of  any
 state,  county or national [firemen's] FIREFIGHTER'S association, organ-
 ized for kindred purposes, now or hereafter created,  and  to  pay  dues
 therein or contribute to the support thereof.
  c. The Alert Engine, Hook and Ladder and Hose Company No. 1 may trans-
 fer  and  convey to such corporation, with or without consideration, any

  §  614.6  Precept for payment of foreign fire insurance premium taxes.

 ACT.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
