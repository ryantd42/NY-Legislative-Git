# Relates to sales and compensating sales taxes in the county of Wayne

**Bill ID:** S5118AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5118AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5118AA)

## Summary

Extends the additional one percent sales and compensating use taxes in the county of Wayne.
