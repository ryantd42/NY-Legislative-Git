# Relates to requiring the department of labor to conduct a study on the potential impact of driverless vehicles

**Bill ID:** A233
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A233 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A233)

## Summary

Requires the department of labor to conduct a study on the potential impact of driverless vehicles on occupations and employment.
