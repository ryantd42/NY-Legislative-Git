# Requires cable television companies to offer cable television channels on an individual basis

**Bill ID:** A5154
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5154 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5154)

## Summary

Requires cable television companies to offer cable television channels on an individual basis; provides that the public service commission be responsible for approving rates.
