# Honoring Dragonfly Art and Soul upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the Small Business of the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 21, 2024

**Bill ID:** K845
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K845 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K845)

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 845
 
BY: M. of A. Norris
 
  HONORING  Dragonfly Art and Soul upon the occasion
  of  its  designation  as  recipient  of  the  Small
  Business  of  the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber
  of Commerce on March 21, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  the
achievements  of  those  individuals  and  businesses  that  contribute
substantially to the economic vitality  of  their  communities,  thereby
enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Dragonfly Art and Soul upon the occasion of its designation as recipient
of the Small Business of the Year  Award  by  the  Clarence  Chamber  of
Commerce,  to be celebrated at its Annual Awards Gala on Thursday, March
21, 2024, at Samuel's Grande Manor in Clarence, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Owner of Dragonfly Art  and  Soul,  Sarah  J.  Carr  is  an
artist,  stylist,  visionary,  professional  intuitive,  and  ordained
minister;  the  breadth  of  her  studies  includes  the  fine  arts,
cosmetology,  aromatherapy,  Reiki,  herbalism,  crystals,  meditation,
mediumship,  BioGenesis,  and  countless  metaphysical  and  spiritual
practices, including the Peruvian shamanic path; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  her  career,  Sarah  J.  Carr  has  traveled
extensively, working internationally as  a  photographer,  stylist,  and
makeup  artist  while  also  expanding  her personal knowledge base; she
began her first artistic business venture at  just  sixteen  years  old,
making tie-dyes and traveling to sell them; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Today,  this  successful  entrepreneur  enjoys sharing her
experience and knowledge to support and mentor others (of all  ages)  as
they  begin  a  new  entrepreneurship  venture  or  journey  of personal
discovery; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sarah J. Carr has dedicated her life to  service,  healing,
and helping others discover their gifts at her studio, Dragonfly Art and
Soul, which she runs with her daughter and partner, Darya; she carefully
designed  Dragonfly  as a magical destination where everyone is welcome,
seen, and heard.; and
 
  WHEREAS, Through her careful curation and dedication over  the  past
13  years,  Dragonfly Art and Soul has evolved into a community: a place
of hope, healing, and freedom where everyone's uniqueness and gifts  are
honored; furthermore, this illustrious small business houses the largest
crystal  selection  in  Western  New  York,  as  well  as  a  thriving,
collaborative local artisan gallery and boutique; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dragonfly Art and Soul is an extension of  Sarah's  mission
to  design  spiritual and creative spaces for healing and self-discovery
as inspiration for all to live profoundly, fully, and with grace; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sarah J. Carr loves being part of the  Clarence  Community,
and serves on the executive board at the Clarence Chamber of Commerce as
Co-Chair; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  Dragonfly  Art  and  Soul  is  in direct
correlation to the efforts of its owner, Sarah J. Carr and her  daughter
Darya,  whose  involvement  is, and always has been, characterized by an
impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising
standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of the institution and
the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges  Dragonfly  Art  and  Soul's  success,  recognizes  its
contributions  to the local and State economies, and extends its highest
commendation to the business and its employees, fully confident that, in
its future, it will enjoy the same success which  has  so  characterized
its past; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor  honoring  Dragonfly  Art  and  Soul  upon  the  occasion  of  its
designation  as recipient of the Small Business of the Year Award by the
Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 21, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Sarah J. Carr, Dragonfly Art and Soul.
