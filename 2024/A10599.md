# Relates to the retail sale and on-site consumption of cannabis within one thousand feet of a school or child day care center

**Bill ID:** A10599
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10599 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10599)

## Summary

Prohibits cannabis retail and on-site consumption licensees from operating such premises within one thousand feet of school grounds or a child day care center.
