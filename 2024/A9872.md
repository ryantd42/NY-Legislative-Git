# Establishes a sales tax exemption for background check fees

**Bill ID:** A9872
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9872 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9872)

## Summary

Establishes a sales tax exemption for fees imposed on national instant criminal background checks.
