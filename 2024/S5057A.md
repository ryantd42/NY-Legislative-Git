# Allows Benjamin Doty to be eligible to take the civil service exam for the position of Columbia county deputy sheriff

**Bill ID:** S5057AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5057AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5057AA)

## Summary

Allows Benjamin Doty to be eligible to take the civil service exam for the position of Columbia county deputy sheriff.
