# Requires license plates for certain bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters and requires a person be eighteen years of age to operate or purchase bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters

**Bill ID:** A8838
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chantel Jackson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8838 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8838)

## Summary

Requires license plates for certain bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters; requires a person be eighteen years of age to operate or purchase bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters.

  § 2. The vehicle and traffic law is amended by adding two new sections
 1244 and 1245 to read as follows:
  §  1244.  REGISTRATION  OF  CERTAIN  BICYCLES WITH ELECTRIC ASSIST. 1.
 EVERY BICYCLE WITH ELECTRIC ASSIST WHICH HAS AN ELECTRIC MOTOR THAT DOES
 NOT CEASE TO PROVIDE ASSISTANCE WHEN SUCH BICYCLE OR SCOOTER  REACHES  A
 SPEED  OF  FORTY MILES PER HOUR SHALL BE REGISTERED AND ISSUED A LICENSE
 PLATE BY THE DEPARTMENT. NO PERSON SHALL OPERATE  SUCH  A  BICYCLE  WITH
 ELECTRIC ASSIST ON THE PUBLIC HIGHWAYS UNLESS SUCH BICYCLE WITH ELECTRIC
 ASSIST SHALL HAVE A DISTINCTIVE NUMBER ASSIGNED TO IT BY THE COMMISSION-
 ER  AND  A  NUMBER PLATE ISSUED BY THE COMMISSIONER WITH A NUMBER CORRE-
 SPONDING TO THAT OF THE CERTIFICATE OF REGISTRATION.
  2. SUCH LICENSE PLATES SHALL BE OF SUCH  MATERIAL,  FORM,  DESIGN  AND
 DIMENSIONS  AND CONTAIN OR SET FORTH SUCH DISTINGUISHING NUMBER OR OTHER
 IDENTIFICATION MARKS AS  THE  COMMISSIONER  SHALL  PRESCRIBE,  PROVIDED,
 HOWEVER,  THAT THERE SHALL BE AT ALL TIMES A MARKED CONTRAST BETWEEN THE
 COLOR OF THE NUMBER PLATES AND THAT OF THE NUMERALS OR LETTERS  THEREON,
 AND  PROVIDED  FURTHER  THAT  NO SUCH BICYCLE WITH ELECTRIC ASSIST SHALL
 DISPLAY THE NUMBER PLATES OF MORE THAN ONE STATE AT A  TIME,  NOR  SHALL
 ANY PLATE BE USED OTHER THAN THOSE ISSUED BY THE COMMISSIONER.
  3.  EACH  SUCH  LICENSE PLATE SHALL IDENTIFY WHETHER SUCH BICYCLE WITH
 ELECTRIC ASSIST IS PERSONAL OR COMMERCIAL IN NATURE.
