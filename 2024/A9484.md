# Prohibits time limits or curfews on fares provided to students

**Bill ID:** A9484
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9484 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9484)

## Summary

Prohibits the imposition of time limits or curfews on fares provided to students as part of a student fare program.
