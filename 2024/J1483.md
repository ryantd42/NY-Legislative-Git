## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1483
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  HONORING  Eve  Van de Wal upon the occasion of her
  retirement after 35 years of  distinguished  service
  to Excellus BlueCross BlueShield
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge
those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Eve Van de Wal upon the occasion of her retirement  after  35  years  of
distinguished service to Excellus BlueCross BlueShield on Tuesday, April
30, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, In her official acts, Eve Van de Wal was governed by a keen
sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Eve  Van  de  Wal  is a Registered Nurse with more than 35
years of extensive and diverse experience  in  healthcare  delivery  and
health  insurance; she joined Excellus BlueCross BlueShield in 1998, and
held several management positions throughout the  company  before  being
appointed  Regional President of the Utica/North Country region in 2008;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Eve Van de Wal received a Bachelor  of  Science  Degree  in
Nursing  from  SUNY  Brockport  and  master's degrees in Health Services
Management  and  Business  Administration  from  SUNY  Institute  of
Technology;  she also worked for more than a decade in the Critical Care
Unit of Faxton Hospital in Utica; and
 
  WHEREAS, In her capacity as Utica Regional President, Eve Van de Wal
always served with loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS, In true service to her community, Eve Van de Wal  has  made
giving back, making a difference, and helping others a priority; she has
supported  a variety of civic and philanthropic organizations, including
the Community Foundation of Herkimer and Oneida Counties; Mohawk  Valley
EDGE;  the  United  Way  of  the  Mohawk  Valley;  The  American  Heart
Association; YWCA of the Mohawk Valley; Healthcare Workforce  New  York,
and the Rotary Club of Utica; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  recipient  of  numerous  awards and accolades for her
unrelenting dedication and service, Eve Van  de  Wal  has  received  the
Catholic  Charities  of  Oneida and Madison Counties Starfish Award; The
Genesis Group Joseph R. Carucci Legacy Award; Boys and Girls Club Living
Legend Award; The American Red Cross Great Hero Award; The Oneida County
Historical Hall of Fame Living Legends Award; the  Rotary  International
Paul  Harris Fellow Award; the Successful Women in Business Award of the
Mohawk Valley; and the  YWCA  Salute  to  Outstanding  Women  Award  for
Healthcare; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With her throughout have been her husband, Dale, their two
children, and their four grandchildren, all of whom feel  privileged  to
be a part of her life and rejoice in her many achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  the  past 35 years, Eve Van de Wal rendered faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to  Excellus  BlueCross  BlueShield;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  her tenure with Excellus BlueCross BlueShield; Eve
Van  de  Wal  earned  the  admiration,  esteem,  and  affection  of  her
colleagues; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Eve Van de Wal upon the occasion of her retirement after 35  years
of distinguished service; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Eve Van de Wal.
