# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian Kailey Frank and Salutatorian Erik Maldonado of FDNY Captain Vernon A. Richard High School for Fire and Life Safety, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** J2667
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2667 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2667)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2667
 
BY: Senator PERSAUD
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024  Valedictorian  Kailey
  Frank  and  Salutatorian  Erik  Maldonado  of  FDNY
  Captain  Vernon  A. Richard High School for Fire and
  Life Safety, in  recognition  of  their  outstanding
  accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions, to honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend  Class  of
2024  Valedictorian Kailey Frank and Salutatorian Erik Maldonado of FDNY
Captain Vernon A. Richard High School  for  Fire  and  Life  Safety,  in
recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Kailey  Frank  and  Erik Maldonado will graduate from FDNY
Captain Vernon A. Richard High  School  for  Fire  and  Life  Safety  on
Wednesday, June 26, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Kailey  Frank  and  Erik  Maldonado  represent the best of
developed potential inherent in our most precious resource,  our  youth;
their  achievements  have  brought  enduring honor to their families and
communities and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Kailey Frank and  Erik  Maldonado  take  leave  of  FDNY
Captain  Vernon  A.  Richard  High School for Fire and Life Safety, they
bestow  a  legacy  of  academic  achievement,  school  involvement  and
community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS, Kailey Frank and Erik Maldonado may now stand with pride as
they  assess  their  achievements,  experience the satisfaction of their
labors and the joy of their  accomplishments,  eager  to  face  the  new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2024 Valedictorian Kailey Frank and  Salutatorian  Erik
Maldonado  of  FDNY  Captain  Vernon A. Richard High School for Fire and
Life Safety, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and be
it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Valedictorian  Kailey  Frank  and  Salutatorian  Erik
Maldonado.
