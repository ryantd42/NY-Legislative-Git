# Relates to exemptions for potato storage and potato shipping facilities

**Bill ID:** S421
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S421 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S421)

## Summary

Exempts potato storage and potato shipping facilities permanently affixed to agricultural lands from taxation, special ad valorem levies and special assessments.
