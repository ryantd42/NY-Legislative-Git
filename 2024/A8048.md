# Allows individuals registered with a non-major political party or registered as independent to serve as election inspectors and poll clerks in certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** A8048
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jen Lunsford
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8048 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8048)

## Summary

Allows individuals registered with a non-major political party or registered as independent to serve as election inspectors and poll clerks, so long as such individual makes a declaration of which particular party they intend to work on behalf of.
