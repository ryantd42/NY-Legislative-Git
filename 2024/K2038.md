# Congratulating the Mount St. Mary Academy Varsity Basketball Team upon the occasion of winning the New York State Catholic High School Athletics Association Division B State Championship

**Bill ID:** K2038
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Conrad
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2038 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2038)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2038
 
BY: M. of A. Conrad
 
  CONGRATULATING  the Mount St. Mary Academy Varsity
  Basketball Team upon the occasion of winning the New
  York  State  Catholic  High  School  Athletics
  Association Division B State Championship
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Mount St. Mary Academy Varsity Basketball  Team  upon  the  occasion  of
winning  the  New  York State Catholic High School Athletics Association
Division B State Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and applause of their excited fans, the  Lady
Thunder  defeated  New  York  City's  Cardinal Spellman High School in a
54-43 victory to win the Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS, Family, friends, and the community  at  large  loyally  and
enthusiastically  supported the Lady Thunder throughout their journey as
they ended their season with an impressive overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport  such  as  basketball,  which  demands  athletic
prowess,  speed,  skill,  and agility, Head Coach Kevin Peterson and his
expert  coaching  staff  worked  hard  to  hone  the  skills  of  this
championship  team,  teaching  these  outstanding athletes lessons which
will prove invaluable both on and off the court; and
 
  WHEREAS, Coach Kevin Peterson and all of the outstanding athletes on
the Mount St. Mary Academy Varsity Basketball Team have clearly utilized
dedication,  determination,  and  teamwork  in  providing  a  lasting
contribution  to  the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their
school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the  members  of  the  Mount  St.  Mary  Academy  Varsity
Basketball Team: Hailey McMahon, Amanda Saber, Siobhan Murphy, Jeuryeliz
Marrero, Gianna Greco, Natalie Wroblewski, Grace Eberl,  Brianna  Huddy,
Gabriella  Gonzales,  Ava  Cavallero,  Eliana  Bova,  Claire Gaulin, and
Elizabeth Allen; Head Coach Kevin Peterson; and Assistant  Coaches  Demi
Battin  and  Natassia  West on their outstanding season and overall team
record; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  members  of  the  Mount  St.  Mary Academy Varsity
 
Basketball Team, Head Coach Kevin Peterson, and Assistant  Coaches  Demi
Battin and Natassia West.
