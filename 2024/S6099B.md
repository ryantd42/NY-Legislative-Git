# Removes statute of limitations for certain civil actions related to child sexual assault offenses

**Bill ID:** S6099B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6099B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6099B)

## Summary

Removes statute of limitations for certain civil actions related to child sexual assault offenses.
