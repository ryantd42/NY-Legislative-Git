# Provides for determinations of appropriate educational programs for certain students in N.Y. city

**Bill ID:** A9384
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9384 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9384)

## Summary

Relates to determinations of appropriate educational programs for certain students in a school district in a city having a population of one million or more.
