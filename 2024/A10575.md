# Relates to state contracts for the services of pharmacy benefit managers under health insurance plans for state officers and employees

**Bill ID:** A10575
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10575 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10575)

## Summary

Requires state contracts for the services of pharmacy benefit managers under health insurance plans for state officers and employees to be procured pursuant to a reverse auction process.
