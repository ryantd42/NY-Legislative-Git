# Memorializing Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim July 25, 2024, as Intern Appreciation Day in the State of New York

**Bill ID:** K933
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Novakhov
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K933 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K933)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 933
 
BY: M. of A. Novakhov
 
  MEMORIALIZING  Governor  Kathy  Hochul to proclaim
  July 25, 2024, as Intern  Appreciation  Day  in  the
  State of New York
 
  WHEREAS,  The  State  of  New  York  State recognizes the invaluable
contributions made by interns  who  dedicate  their  time,  energy,  and
talents to the betterment of our state; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to memorialize
Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim July 25, 2024, as Intern  Appreciation
Day  in  the  State  of  New York, in conjunction with the observance of
National Intern Appreciation Day; and
 
  WHEREAS, Interns play a crucial role in supporting  the  legislative
process,  fostering  innovation,  and  contributing  to  the  growth and
development of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  dedication  and  hard  work  of  interns  deserve
acknowledgment  and appreciation from the New York State Legislature and
the citizens of our great state; and
 
  WHEREAS, Intern Appreciation Day, falling on the  last  Thursday  in
July,  provides an opportunity to express gratitude to interns for their
commitment to public service, their enthusiasm for learning,  and  their
role in shaping the future of our state; and
 
  WHEREAS, This esteemed body recognizes the importance of fostering a
positive  and  inclusive  environment  for  interns  to  thrive and gain
valuable experiences; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
memorialize  Governor  Kathy Hochul to proclaim July 25, 2024, as Intern
Appreciation Day in the State of New York, to honor  and  celebrate  the
contributions  of  interns to our legislative process and the state as a
whole; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body encourages all citizens to take
part  in  recognizing  and  appreciating  the  efforts  of  interns,
highlighting  their role in building a brighter future for New York; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That this Resolution, suitably engrossed,  be  transmitted
to The Honorable Kathy Hochul, Governor of the State of New York.
