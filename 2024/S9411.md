# Extends provisions of law relating to new owners of buildings for which administrators have been appointed pursuant to article 7-A of the real property actions and proceedings law

**Bill ID:** S9411
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9411 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9411)

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to new owners of buildings for which administrators have been appointed pursuant to article 7-A of the real property actions and proceedings law from June 30, 2024 until June 30, 2027.
