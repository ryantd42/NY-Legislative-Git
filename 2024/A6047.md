# Relates to the confinement of companion animals in vehicles in extreme temperatures

**Bill ID:** A6047
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo J. Morinello
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6047 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6047)

## Summary

Relates to the confinement of companion animals in vehicles in extreme temperatures; provides such animals may be rescued if certain conditions are met.
