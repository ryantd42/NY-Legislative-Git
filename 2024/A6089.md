# Relates to health insurance coverage for breast ultrasounds for cancer screening

**Bill ID:** A6089
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian F. Curran
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6089 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6089)

## Summary

Requires health insurance policies which provide coverage for mammography screenings also provide coverage for breast ultrasounds for cancer screening.
