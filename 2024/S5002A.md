# Relates to extending the authorization for Ontario county to impose additional rates of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S5002AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5002AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5002AA)

## Summary

Extends the authorization for Ontario county to impose additional rates of sales and compensating use taxes until 2025.
