# Establishes a fuel cell incentive program

**Bill ID:** S5985
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5985 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5985)

## Summary

Establishes a fuel cell incentive program to be managed by NYSERDA to encourage development of fuel cell generating systems.
