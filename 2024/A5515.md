# Defines closed period of eligibility for purposes of certain supplemental security income and includes such time period in the eligibility requirements of such supplemental security income

**Bill ID:** A5515
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [A5515 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5515)

## Summary

Defines "closed period of eligibility" for purposes of supplemental security income for aged, blind, and disabled persons; includes such time period in the eligibility requirements of such supplemental security income.
