# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian Michael Cosmo and Salutatorian Gregory Mears of Centereach High School, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** K2190
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Edward Flood
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K2190 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K2190)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2190
 
BY: M. of A. Flood
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024  Valedictorian Michael
  Cosmo and Salutatorian Gregory Mears  of  Centereach
  High  School,  in  recognition  of their outstanding
  accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions,  to  honor the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2024 Valedictorian Michael  Cosmo  and  Salutatorian  Gregory  Mears  of
Centereach  High  School,  in  recognition  of  their  outstanding
accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Valedictorian Michael Cosmo and Salutatorian Gregory  Mears
represent  the best of developed potential inherent in our most precious
resource, our youth; their achievements have brought enduring  honor  to
their families and communities and should be justly recognized; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  Valedictorian  Michael  Cosmo and Salutatorian Gregory
Mears take leave of Centereach High School,  they  bestow  a  legacy  of
academic achievement, school involvement, and community service to which
others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian Michael Cosmo and Salutatorian Gregory Mears
may now stand with pride as they assess their  achievements,  experience
the  satisfaction of their labors, and the joy of their accomplishments,
eager  to  face  the  new  experiences  of  a  challenging  world;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of  2024  Valedictorian  Michael  Cosmo  and  Salutatorian
Gregory  Mears  of  Centereach  High  School,  in  recognition  of their
outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Valedictorian  Michael  Cosmo  and Salutatorian Gregory
Mears.
