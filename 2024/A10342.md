# Relates to business interruption insurance

**Bill ID:** A10342
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenny Burgos
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10342 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10342)

## Summary

Authorizes stand-alone business interruption insurance.

  (34) "BUSINESS INTERRUPTION INSURANCE" MEANS INSURANCE AGAINST LOSS OF

 NESS PREMISES; OR (C) A GOVERNMENT ORDER.
