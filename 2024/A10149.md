# Directs the division of criminal justice services to expand the crimes against revenue program to all counties within the state

**Bill ID:** A10149
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10149 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10149)

## Summary

Directs the division of criminal justice services to include in the crimes against revenue program each county within the state and provide funding to each such county.
