# Authorizes the Tuckahoe common school district to submit a proposition to reorganize as a union free school district

**Bill ID:** S9183BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9183BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9183BA)

## Summary

Authorizes the trustees of the Tuckahoe Common School District to submit a proposition to reorganize as the Tuckahoe-Shinnecock Union Free School District.
