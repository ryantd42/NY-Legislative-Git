## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1482
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Dr. Kent Hall upon the occasion of
  his retirement after  many  years  of  distinguished
  service  as  Chief  Physician  Executive  at  Mohawk
  Valley Health System's Wynn Hospital
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize  that
the  quality  of  life in the communities across this great State of New
York is enriched by the diligence and compassionate commitment of  those
individuals  whose  singular  purpose is the care and welfare of others;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate Dr. Kent Hall upon the occasion  of  his  retirement  after
many  years  of  distinguished  service  as Chief Physician Executive at
Mohawk Valley Health System's Wynn Hospital; and
 
  WHEREAS, Kent Hall, MD, who has been practicing medicine  since  the
early  1980s,  is  the  Chief Physician Executive (CPE) at Mohawk Valley
Health System (MVHS) in Utica, New York; he will officially retire  from
this esteemed position in January 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  this  capacity  he  is  responsible for overseeing the
medical staff of the new Wynn Hospital, previously St. Elizabeth and St.
Luke's Campus, as well as credentialing, the MVHS Medical Staff  Office,
Family  Medicine Residency Program, Dental Residency Program, Palliative
Care, Medical Libraries, and the MVHS Medical Group; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  his  illustrious  tenure  as  Chief  Physician
Executive, Dr. Kent Hall has been instrumental in the opening of the new
Wynn  Hospital in Utica, and has guided the medical team at MVHS through
the COVID-19 pandemic, and the most recent move into  the  new  downtown
Utica facility; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Furthermore,  he  has  been  a vital component in the high
quality of medical care delivered as well as  for  driving  quality  and
safety  initiatives  throughout  MVHS,  working directly with department
chairs,  directors  of  contracted  medical  groups  and  hospital-based
physicians to coordinate patient care and safety efficiently; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Most  recently,  Dr.  Kent  Hall  served  as Chief Medical
Officer (CMO) at Champlain Valley Physicians  Hospital  in  Plattsburgh,
New  York,  and  Alice Hyde Medical Center in Malone, New York, where he
worked  to  improve  patient  outcomes  and  delivery  through  improved
clinical  quality  processes, as well as operational efficiencies within
and between hospital based clinical services; and
 
  WHEREAS, Prior to  that,  Dr.  Kent  Hall  also  served  as  Medical
Director  of  the  Emergency  Department  at Champlain Valley Physicians
Hospital; and
 
  WHEREAS, Kent Hall completed his residency in Emergency Medicine  at
the  University  of Cincinnati Hospital in Cincinnati, Ohio; he received
 
his medical degree from Upstate Medical College in Syracuse,  New  York,
and his Bachelor of Science in Biology and Psychology from Union College
in Schenectady, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  more  than  four  decades,  Dr.  Kent Hall's tireless
efforts and  notable  achievements  have  significantly  contributed  to
bettering  the  quality  of life in his community, truly earning him the
admiration, affection, and esteem of his colleagues; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout  his  career,  Dr.  Kent  Hall  has  served  his
patients with compassion and care; he has contributed to the maintenance
and  enhancement  of  the  highest  professional standards, sharing with
others his knowledge and  expertise,  as  well  as  his  dedication  and
commitment to quality health care; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Dr. Kent Hall upon the occasion of his retirement,  and  to
wish him well in all his future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Dr. Kent Hall.
