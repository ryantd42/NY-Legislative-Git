# Commending The Reverend Dr. Tyrone H. Robinson upon the occasion of his 10th Pastoral Anniversary to be celebrated on April 28, 2023

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J449 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J449)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 449
 
BY: Senator MAYER
 
  COMMENDING  The  Reverend  Dr.  Tyrone H. Robinson
  upon the occasion of his 10th  Pastoral  Anniversary
  to be celebrated on April 28, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  The character and richness of religious and community life
in this noble Empire  State  is  nourished  and  fulfilled  through  the
faithful  concern  and selfless devotion of those who would commit their
energies and purpose to the ministry of others; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this  Legislative  Body  to  bring  full
recognition  and  just  tribute  to  those  individuals  who distinguish
themselves and the ministry through  their  unremitting  commitment  and
preeminence of service; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  The  Reverend  Dr.  Tyrone H. Robinson upon the occasion of his
10th Pastoral Anniversary to be celebrated at a Banquet on Friday, April
28, 2023, at the Westchester Manor in Hastings-on-Hudson, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, This auspicious occasion will be culminated  at  a  service
led  by  Reverend  Gerald  L.  Dickson on Sunday, April 30, 2023, at the
First Community Baptist Church, in White Plains, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Reverend Dr.  Tyrone  H.  Robinson  has  served  the  First
community Baptist Church in White Plains since his installation in 2013;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  him throughout has been his companion, the Honorable
Lady Nadine Hunt-Robinson, who feels privileged to be a part of his life
and rejoices in his achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, Reverend Dr. Tyrone H. Robinson has brought to his ministry
an overwhelming spirit of charity and compassion, acting from vision and
enlightenment in conducting his  administrative  and  spiritual  duties,
while  always  displaying  the  highest  standards  of  personal  and
professional integrity and dedication; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  distinguished  man  of  God  has  always  given
enlightenment, guidance and wisdom to the many entrusted to his care and
has  administered  abundantly  and  unstintingly  to  the  spiritual and
corporal needs of all; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when  a
person  of  such  noble  aims  and  accomplishments  is  brought  to our
attention it is appropriate to  publicly  and  jubilantly  proclaim  and
commend  that  individual for the edification of others; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  The Reverend Dr. Tyrone H. Robinson, noting his unremitting and
compassionate faith and his exemplary  service  to  God,  man,  and  his
community,  and  extending  to  him  the  heartfelt  best wishes of this
 
Legislative Body for a future of purposeful success and well-being;  and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to The Reverend Dr. Tyrone H. Robinson.
