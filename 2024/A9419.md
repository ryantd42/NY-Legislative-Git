# Requires the New York state thruway authority to provide notice, a description of meeting details and a sign up list prior to meetings of the board

**Bill ID:** A9419
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9419 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9419)

## Summary

Requires the New York state thruway authority to provide notice, a description of meeting details and a sign up list prior to meetings of the board.

 Introduced  by M. of A. HYNDMAN -- read once and referred to the Commit-
  tee on Corporations, Authorities and Commissions
 
 AN ACT to amend the public authorities law, in relation to requiring the
  New York state thruway authority to provide notice, a  description  of
  meeting details and a sign up list prior to meetings of the board

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Section 352 of the public authorities  law  is  amended  by
 adding a new subdivision 5 to read as follows:
  5. (A) PRIOR TO ANY MEETING OF THE BOARD, THE BOARD SHALL:
  (I)  NOT LESS THAN TWO WEEKS PRIOR TO SUCH MEETING, PROVIDE THE PUBLIC
 WITH NOTICE OF SUCH MEETING. SUCH NOTICE SHALL INCLUDE AT A MINIMUM, THE
 DATE, TIME AND LOCATION OF SUCH MEETING;
  (II) NOT LESS THAN ONE WEEK PRIOR TO SUCH MEETING, PROVIDE THE  PUBLIC
 WITH  A  DESCRIPTION  OF  SUCH MEETING, SETTING FORTH THE DETAILS OF ALL
 ITEMS TO BE CONSIDERED AT SUCH MEETING; AND
  (III) NOT LESS THAN ONE WEEK PRIOR TO SUCH MEETING, PROVIDE ANY  PARTY
 WITH  AN  INTEREST IN SUCH MEETING TO SIGN UP FOR PUBLIC COMMENT AT SUCH
 MEETING.
  (B) THE NOTICE, DESCRIPTION  OF  MEETING  DETAILS  AND  SIGN  UP  LIST
 REQUIRED  BY  PARAGRAPH  (A) OF THIS SUBDIVISION SHALL BE MADE AVAILABLE
 AND PUBLISHED IN A CONSPICUOUS LOCATION ON THE WEBSITE OF THE AUTHORITY.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect on the thirtieth day  after  it  shall
 have become a law.
