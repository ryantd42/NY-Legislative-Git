# Provides a tax credit for the installation of fire sprinkler systems

**Bill ID:** A4936
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Gonzalez-Rojas
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4936 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4936)

## Summary

Provides a tax credit for the installation of fire sprinkler systems.
