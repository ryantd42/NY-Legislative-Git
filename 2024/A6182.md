# Relates to the annual extensions of transportation contracts for school districts

**Bill ID:** A6182
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian F. Curran
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6182 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6182)

## Summary

Amends provisions regarding the calculation of increases permitted in annual extensions of transportation contracts for school districts.
