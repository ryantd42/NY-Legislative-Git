# Relates to work authorization for asylum seekers

**Bill ID:** A10648
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10648 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10648)

## Summary

Directs the comptroller to withhold certain payments to be made to the federal government until asylum seekers in the custody of the city of New York are granted documentation or authorization for employment.

  3. Authorization or documentation permitting employment shall include,

 granting  refugee  status,  or  a  new  federal law in effect that makes
 asylum seekers eligible within thirty days of applying for asylum.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall  expire  and  be
 deemed repealed when every adult asylum seeker in New York city's custo-
