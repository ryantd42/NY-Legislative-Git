# Prohibits discrimination against individuals who were prescribed pre-exposure prophylaxis medication for HIV prevention with respect to insurance coverage

**Bill ID:** A8834B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A8834B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8834B)

## Summary

Prohibits discrimination against individuals who were prescribed pre-exposure prophylaxis medication for HIV prevention with respect to life, accident, and health insurance coverage.
