# Issues distinctive epilepsy awareness license plates and establishes an epilepsy awareness fund and an epilepsy research and education program

**Bill ID:** S7829
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S7829 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7829)

## Summary

Issues distinctive epilepsy awareness license plates; establishes an epilepsy awareness fund and an epilepsy research and education program.

  § 2. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section 99-rr to
 read as follows:
  §  99-RR.  THE  NEW  YORK STATE "EPILEPSY AWARENESS FUND". 1. THERE IS
 HEREBY ESTABLISHED IN THE JOINT CUSTODY OF THE COMMISSIONER OF  TAXATION
 AND  FINANCE  AND THE COMPTROLLER, A SPECIAL FUND TO BE KNOWN AS THE NEW
 YORK STATE "EPILEPSY AWARENESS FUND".
  2. SUCH FUND SHALL CONSIST OF ALL REVENUES RECEIVED  PURSUANT  TO  THE
 PROVISIONS  OF  SECTION  FOUR HUNDRED FOUR-FF OF THE VEHICLE AND TRAFFIC
 LAW AND ALL OTHER MONEYS APPROPRIATED, CREDITED, OR TRANSFERRED  THERETO
 FROM  ANY OTHER FUND OR SOURCE PURSUANT TO LAW. NOTHING CONTAINED HEREIN
 SHALL PREVENT THE STATE FROM RECEIVING GRANTS, GIFTS OR BEQUESTS FOR THE
 PURPOSES OF THE FUND AS DEFINED IN THIS SECTION AND DEPOSITING THEM INTO
 THE FUND ACCORDING TO LAW.
  3. MONIES OF THE FUND SHALL BE EXPENDED ONLY FOR EPILEPSY RESEARCH AND
 EDUCATION PROGRAMS. AS USED IN  THIS  SECTION,  "EPILEPSY  RESEARCH  AND
 EDUCATION  PROGRAMS"  MEANS  SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH OR EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMS
 WHICH, PURSUANT TO ARTICLE TWENTY-SEVEN-CCCC OF THE PUBLIC  HEALTH  LAW,
 ARE APPROVED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH.
  4.  MONIES  SHALL BE PAYABLE FROM THE FUND ON THE AUDIT AND WARRANT OF
 THE COMPTROLLER ON VOUCHERS APPROVED AND CERTIFIED BY  THE  COMMISSIONER
 OF HEALTH.
  5.  TO THE EXTENT PRACTICABLE, THE COMMISSIONER OF HEALTH SHALL ENSURE
 THAT ALL MONIES RECEIVED DURING A FISCAL YEAR ARE EXPENDED PRIOR TO  THE
 END OF THAT FISCAL YEAR.
  §  3. The public health law is amended by adding a new article 27-CCCC
 to read as follows:
  ARTICLE 27-CCCC
  EPILEPSY RESEARCH AND EDUCATION PROGRAM
 SECTION 2748. SPECIAL PROGRAMS WITH RESPECT TO EPILEPSY.
  § 2748. SPECIAL PROGRAMS WITH RESPECT TO  EPILEPSY.  THE  COMMISSIONER
 SHALL  ESTABLISH WITHIN THE DEPARTMENT A PROGRAM TO PROMOTE RESEARCH AND
 PATIENT EDUCATION REGARDING EPILEPSY. TO CARRY OUT THE PURPOSES OF  THIS
 SECTION, THE COMMISSIONER SHALL:
  1.  PROMOTE  SCIENTIFIC  INVESTIGATIONS  WITH  RESPECT  TO THE CAUSES,
 PREVENTION AND TREATMENT OF EPILEPSY;
  2. PROMOTE PATIENT EDUCATION PROGRAMS DESIGNED TO RAISE  AWARENESS  OF
 EPILEPSY  AND  REDUCE OR PREVENT THE INCIDENCE AND SEVERITY OF SEIZURES.
 SUCH PATIENT  EDUCATION  PROGRAMS  SHALL  ALSO  INCLUDE  INFORMATION  ON
 SEIZURE RECOGNITION AND FIRST AID;
  3.  ACCEPT  AND  EXPEND ANY GRANTS, AWARDS OR OTHER FUNDS OR APPROPRI-
 ATIONS AS MAY BE AVAILABLE FOR THESE PURPOSES SUBJECT TO LIMITATIONS  AS
 TO  THE APPROVAL OF EXPENDITURES AND AUDIT AS PRESCRIBED FOR STATE FUNDS
 BY THE STATE FINANCE LAW; AND
  4. ENTER INTO CONTRACTS WITHIN THE AMOUNTS AVAILABLE THEREFOR  AS  MAY
 BE NECESSARY TO IMPLEMENT THE PROVISIONS OF THIS SECTION.
