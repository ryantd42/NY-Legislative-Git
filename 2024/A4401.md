# Provides free use of state parks and campsites for volunteer firefighters and members of a volunteer ambulance service

**Bill ID:** A4401
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo J. Morinello
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4401 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4401)

## Summary

Provides free use of state parks and campsites for volunteer firefighters and members of a volunteer ambulance service.
