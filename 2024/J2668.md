# Commending Brooklyn Defender Services upon the occasion of hosting its 2024 Family Defense Benefit on June 10, 2024

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [J2668 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2668)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2668
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  Brooklyn  Defender  Services  upon the
  occasion of hosting its 2024 Family Defense  Benefit
  on June 10, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend Brooklyn Defender Services upon the occasion of hosting its 2024
Family Defense Benefit on Monday, June 10, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Brooklyn  Defender  Services is a non-profit law firm that
centers  its  work  on  individuals  facing  detrimental  results  from
interactions  within  the legal system and has been a beacon of hope for
those who would otherwise be overlooked; and
 
  WHEREAS, More than 63% of Brooklyn residents are  people  of  color,
but  more  than 89% of Brooklyn Defender Services' clients are people of
color, highlighting the need for services which address the  disparities
in the criminal, family, and immigration legal systems; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Brooklyn  Defender  Services,  of  Brooklyn,  New  York,
celebrates 17 years of keeping families together; this Legislative  Body
recognizes  the  assistance this guardian provides for its community and
honors  its  dedication  to  empower  underserved  Brooklyn  residents,
anticipating  future  good  works  and  wishing  the  company  many more
successful years to come; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of Brooklyn  Defender  Services  is  in  direct
correlation  to  the  efforts  of its members, whose involvement is, and
always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
recognize and commend Brooklyn Defender Services upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its  2024  Family  Defense  Benefit on June 10, 2024; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Brooklyn Defender Services.
