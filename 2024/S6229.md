# Relates to wine products

**Bill ID:** S6229
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6229 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6229)

## Summary

Includes within the definition of "wine product", a beverage containing wine which has undergone an alcohol reduction process including but not limited to nanofiltration, reverse osmosis, osmotic distillation, vacuum distillation, spinning cone column, or through a multi-stage membrane-based system.
