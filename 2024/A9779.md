# Relates to requiring the office for people with developmental disabilities to submit an updated waitlist and placement report to the senate and assembly

**Bill ID:** A9779
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9779 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9779)

## Summary

Requires the office for people with developmental disabilities to submit an updated waitlist and placement report to the senate and assembly.
