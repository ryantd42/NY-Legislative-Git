# Directs the state insurance fund to offer title insurance and authorizes the superintendent of financial services to implement such program

**Bill ID:** A6069
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6069 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6069)

## Summary

Directs the state insurance fund to offer title insurance; authorizes the superintendent of financial services to implement such program.
