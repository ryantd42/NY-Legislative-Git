# Extends provisions relating to the mortgage tax in the county of Steuben

**Bill ID:** S5610A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5610A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5610A)

## Summary

Extends provisions relating to the mortgage tax in the county of Steuben until December 1, 2025.
