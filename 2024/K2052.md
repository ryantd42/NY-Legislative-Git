## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2052
 
BY: M. of A. Norris
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  125th Anniversary of the Alden
  Hook & Ladder Fire Company, Inc.
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proud  to  commemorate  the  125th
Anniversary  of  the  Alden Hook & Ladder Fire Company, and to honor its
past and present firefighting members for 125 years of  fire  protection
to the citizens of Alden, New York, and its surrounding communities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Fire  departments  are  an  integral  part  of  community
protection services in every locality of our State; during the past  125
years,  the  courageous  and  devoted  firefighters  of the Alden Hook &
Ladder Fire Company have  valiantly  responded  to  all  types  of  fire
emergencies  and  have won the praise and respect of the community which
they serve; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Alden Hook &  Ladder  Fire  Company  was  approved  and
recognized  by  the  Village  of  Alden Board of Trustees on October 20,
1899, and was originally known as "Rescue Hook & Ladder"; and
 
  WHEREAS, Prior to the creation of  the  Alden  Hook  &  Ladder  Fire
Company,  the  Village  of  Alden  Board  of  Trustees  granted  the
establishment of the Alden Hose Company on July 31, 1899; both the Alden
Hose Company and the Rescue Hook & Ladder  Company  worked  together  to
protect  the  citizens  and  property  of  the Village of Alden, Town of
Alden, and neighboring municipalities; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Alden Hook & Ladder Ladies Auxiliary was established in
1933; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  former  Rescue  Hook  &  Ladder  Company  formally
incorporated  on June 30, 1949, as the Alden Hook & Ladder Fire Company;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Although the Alden Hose Company still exists  on  paper  in
the  company  files,  the  membership  of the Alden Hose" Company became
official members of the Alden Hook & Ladder Fire  Company  in  March  of
1952; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  the  years  of  protecting  the  citizens  and
properties of the Village and Town of Alden, the  Alden  Hook  &  Ladder
Fire  Company  changed  with the times and increased the fire and rescue
services they provided,  including:  the  addition  of  the  Alden  Fire
Company  -  Underwater  Recovery  Team  (SCUBA)  in  the late 1950s; the
establishment of a formal First Aid Squad in 1960; and the establishment
of the Alden Hook & Ladder Benevolent Association in  1979,  to  support
its members and families int heir personal times of need; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Furthermore,  the  department  was one of the first in the
area to purchase a Hurst Auto Rescue Tool known as the "Jaws  of  Life,"
following a tragic accident after the local Prom on Henskee Road; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In 1980, the Alden Hook & Ladder Fire Company accepted its
first female member as an active member of  the  rescue  squad;  in  the
 
summer  of  1985, to increase the protection and decrease response times
in the Town of Alden, the membership of the Alden  Hook  &  Ladder  Fire
Company  built  and  dedicated  the  Alden Substation currently known as
Alden Station No.  2; and
 
  WHEREAS,  On  December 1, 1998, the Alden Hook & Ladder Fire Company
voted unanimously to follow the Village Boards actions and  created  the
first village EMS department in all of New York State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With a membership of dedicated firefighters, the record of
the Alden Hook  &  Ladder  Fire  Company  in  public  service  and  fire
protection is one to be envied; for 24 hours a day, for 365 days a year,
for  125  years, whenever the alarm sounded, these noble volunteers left
their homes and went to the aid of their neighbors; and
 
  WHEREAS, The brave firefighters of the  Alden  Hook  &  Ladder  Fire
Company  are  prepared  to  risk  their lives every day to help save the
lives of others and to protect homes and property from  the  destruction
caused by fire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition to the skill and devoted service demonstrated
by individual  firefighters,  the  Alden  Hook  &  Ladder  Fire  Company
reflects  the  history and development of firefighting over the past 125
years; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body cannot express  sufficient  gratitude
to those devoted individuals who recognize that the preservation of life
and property is a sacred responsibility and who make that responsibility
their own by serving as firefighters; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body that we should
give special recognition to  those  who  work  so  assiduously  for  the
betterment  of  their  communities,  and acknowledge publicly the heroic
good works performed by the volunteer fire  departments  of  this  great
Empire State, their officers and auxiliaries; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
proudly commemorate the 125th Anniversary of the  Alden  Hook  &  Ladder
Fire Company; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Alden Hook & Ladder Fire Company, Inc.
