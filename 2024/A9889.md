# Authorizes Joanne De Vito receive retroactive tier IV status in the New York state and local employees' retirement system

**Bill ID:** A9889
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9889 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9889)

## Summary

Authorizes Joanne De Vito to receive retroactive tier IV status in the New York state and local employees' retirement system.
