# Relates to adding aggravated assault in the third degree to the penal law

**Bill ID:** S5307
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5307 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5307)

## Summary

Provides for the addition of aggravated assault in the third degree to the penal law.
