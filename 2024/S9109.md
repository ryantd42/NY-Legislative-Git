# Establishes safety requirements for commercial bicycle operators and businesses

**Bill ID:** S9109
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9109 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9109)

## Summary

Establishes safety requirements for operators who use bicycles with electric assist for commercial purposes and the businesses that employ such operators.
