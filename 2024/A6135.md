# Relates to real property subject to an authorization for a real property tax exemption

**Bill ID:** A6135
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6135 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6135)

## Summary

Relates to real property subject to an authorization for a real property tax exemption in the town of Smithtown, county of Suffolk, with respect to the 2019 and 2020 assessment rolls.
