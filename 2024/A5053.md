# Provides for income access services in the state

**Bill ID:** A5053
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5053 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5053)

## Summary

Provides for the licensing and regulation of income access services in the state.
