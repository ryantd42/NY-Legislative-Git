# Authorizes any registered voter to vote by absentee ballot

**Bill ID:** A5921
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5921 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5921)

## Summary

Authorizes any registered voter to vote by absentee ballot and to elect to permanently vote by absentee ballot.
