# Relates to increasing the length of the term of office for members of the legislature

**Bill ID:** A5148
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica P. Wallace
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5148 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5148)

## Summary

Increases the length of the term of office for members of the legislature from two to four years.
