# Requires bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters be registered with the department of motor vehicles

**Bill ID:** S9208
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9208 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9208)

## Summary

Requires bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters be registered with the department of motor vehicles and have a license plate affixed to such bicycle with electric assist or electric scooter.
