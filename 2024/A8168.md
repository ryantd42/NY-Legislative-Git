# Provides Medicaid coverage for remote ultrasound scans and remote fetal non-stress tests

**Bill ID:** A8168
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A8168 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8168)

## Summary

Provides Medicaid coverage for remote ultrasound scans and remote fetal non-stress tests.

 FETAL NON-STRESS TESTS and other services determined by the commissioner
 of health; provided, however, that  the  provisions  of  this  paragraph

 certification  or  scope  of practice provision under title eight of the
