# Exempts correction officers of any penal correctional institution from training requirements for security guards

**Bill ID:** A5759
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5759 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5759)

## Summary

Exempts correction officers of any penal correctional institution from training requirements for security guards.
