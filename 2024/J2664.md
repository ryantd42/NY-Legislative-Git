# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian Ashley Valdez and Salutatorian Daniella Griffiths of World Academy For Total Community Health High School, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** J2664
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2664 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2664)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2664
 
BY: Senator PERSAUD
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024  Valedictorian  Ashley
  Valdez and Salutatorian Daniella Griffiths of  World
  Academy  For  Total Community Health High School, in
  recognition of their outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions,  to  honor the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2024 Valedictorian Ashley Valdez and Salutatorian Daniella Griffiths  of
World  Academy For Total Community Health High School, in recognition of
their outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ashley Valdez and Daniella  Griffiths  will  graduate  from
World  Academy For Total Community Health High School on Wednesday, June
26, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ashley Valdez and Daniella Griffiths represent the best  of
developed  potential  inherent in our most precious resource, our youth;
their achievements have brought enduring honor  to  their  families  and
communities and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Ashley Valdez and Daniella Griffiths take leave of World
Academy  For Total Community Health High School, they bestow a legacy of
academic achievement, school involvement and community service to  which
others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Ashley  Valdez  and  Daniella Griffiths may now stand with
pride as they assess their achievements, experience the satisfaction  of
their labors and the joy of their accomplishments, eager to face the new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of  2024  Valedictorian  Ashley  Valdez  and  Salutatorian
Daniella  Griffiths  of  World  Academy  For Total Community Health High
School, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Valedictorian Ashley  Valdez  and  Salutatorian  Daniella
Griffiths.
