# Establishes the central healthcare data system

**Bill ID:** A8292
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patrick Burke
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8292 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8292)

## Summary

Establishes the central healthcare data system which stores medical records and information and provides such medical records and information to authorized users.
