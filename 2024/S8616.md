# Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to increase clean energy distribution infrastructure

**Bill ID:** S8616
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8616 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8616)

## Summary

Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to increase clean energy distribution infrastructure to allow the state to reach peak clean energy consumption and distribution goals.
