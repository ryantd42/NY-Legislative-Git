# Authorizes the granting of retirement service credit in the optional twenty year retirement plan to Duane A. Palma for service as a correction officer

**Bill ID:** A10622
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10622 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10622)

## Summary

Authorizes the granting of retirement service credit in the optional twenty year retirement plan to Duane A. Palma for service as a correction officer at the Monroe county sheriff's office jail bureau.
