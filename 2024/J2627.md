## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2627
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Charles  R.  Gibbs  II,  Senior
  Buildings and Grounds Supervisor, upon the  occasion
  of  his  retirement  after  more  than  16  years of
  distinguished service to New York Power Authority
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative  Body  to  acknowledge
the  significant  milestones  in  the  distinguished careers of esteemed
residents of this noble Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate  Charles  R.  Gibbs  II,  Senior  Buildings  and  Grounds
Supervisor, upon the occasion of his retirement after more than 16 years
of  distinguished  service  to New York Power Authority on Thursday, May
23, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Charles R. Gibbs II attended Whitesboro Central School  and
Mohawk  Valley  Community  College  where  he  obtained his Associate of
Applied Science in Business Accounting degree in 1982; and
 
  WHEREAS, From December of 2007  until  his  retirement,  Charles  R.
Gibbs  II  served as Senior Buildings and Grounds Supervisor at New York
Power Authority's Clark Energy Center in Marcy, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, For 24 years, from 1991-2015, Charles R.  Gibbs  II  served
his  community  as  Town  Supervisor  and  Councilperson for the Town of
Whitestown, New York; currently, he is still the elected Trustee for the
Village of Whitesboro; and
 
  WHEREAS, Active in his community, Charles R. Gibbs II has spent more
than 22 years, beginning in April of 2002, volunteering  his  time  with
the  Whitesboro  Fire  Department;  he  has  dedicated many years to the
service of  the  residents  of  the  Whitesboro  region  and  should  be
applauded for this endeavor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body that, when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of the great State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare, indeed, is the occurrence  of  such  a  compassionate
blending  of  strength and commitment as that demonstrated by Charles R.
Gibbs II over a lifetime of sacrifice and  dedication  to  others;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate  Charles  R.  Gibbs  II,  Senior  Buildings  and  Grounds
Supervisor, upon the occasion of his retirement after more than 16 years
of  distinguished  service  to New York Power Authority, and to wish him
well in all his future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Charles R. Gibbs II.
