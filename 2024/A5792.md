# Relates to certain real property to be conveyed to the town of Huntington

**Bill ID:** A5792
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A5792 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5792)

## Summary

Extends provisions relating to certain real property to be conveyed to the town of Huntington for one year.
