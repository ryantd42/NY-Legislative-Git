## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2386
 
BY: Senator MURRAY
 
  CONGRATULATING  Dolphus  Knowles upon the occasion
  of celebrating his 100th Birthday
 
  WHEREAS, Senior citizens bring a wealth of experience and  knowledge
to  the  increasingly  active  roles they play in today's society; their
past contributions and future participation are a  vital  part  of,  and
valuable asset to, the fabric of community life and activity; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the custom of this Legislative Body to commemorate
and recognize certain milestones celebrated by citizens  of  this  great
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  Dolphus  Knowles  upon  the occasion of his 100th Birthday
celebrated on February 28, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dolphus Knowles was born on February 28, 1924, and  resides
in Hamilton, New York, where he spent most of his life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Devoted to serving his community, Dolphus Knowles has been
an exceptional member of the Brookhaven community for many years; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dolphus  Knowles  recently  was  honored  with  a  birthday
celebration  by  town  officials  when  they,  along  with  Mr. Knowles'
friends, fellow VFW Post members,  and  members  of  his  loving  family
including  his wife Adele, all celebrated his 100th birthday on February
26, 2024, proclaiming this day as "Dolphus Knowles Day"; and
 
  WHEREAS, Having served honorably in the United  States  Army  during
World  War  II,  Dolphus Knowles served with bravery and dedication in a
smoke screen unit; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dolphus Knowles spent many years after the war as a  public
servant working for the United States Postal Service; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dolphus Knowles is a member of the Veterans of Foreign Wars
Post  8300 in East Patchogue, New York, and holds the commendable status
of being the oldest living member of this Post; and
 
  WHEREAS, This distinguished man has experienced the incredible  joys
and  sorrows  characteristic  of and reserved for those with the stamina
and courage to savor a full life; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dolphus Knowles has enriched the lives of those around  him
through his service to his country and community and through the vibrant
charm and infinite wisdom which comes only from a fullness of years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of  this Legislative Body to publicly
recognize those who have reached such a  remarkable  age  and  who  have
witnessed  and  celebrated  the  innovations, cultural developments, and
awesome achievements of this country  during  the  last  century,  while
 
themselves  contributing  to  the  growth  and  excellence of this great
Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Dolphus Knowles upon the occasion of celebrating his  100th
Birthday; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Dolphus Knowles.
