# Relates to the professional certification of doulas

**Bill ID:** S9182
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9182 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9182)

## Summary

Provides for the professional certification of doulas; sets forth requirements for certification as a certified doula; defines terms; makes related provisions.

  § 8875. DEFINITIONS. AS USED IN THIS SECTION:
  1. "CERTIFIED DOULA" MEANS AN INDIVIDUAL CERTIFIED UNDER THIS  ARTICLE
 WHO PROVIDES CERTIFIED DOULA SERVICES.
  2.  "CERTIFIED DOULA SERVICES" MEANS CONTINUOUS EMOTIONAL AND PHYSICAL
 SUPPORT PROVIDED BY A CERTIFIED DOULA THROUGHOUT LABOR  AND  BIRTH,  AND
 INTERMITTENTLY DURING THE PRENATAL AND POSTPARTUM PERIODS.
  § 8876. USE OF TITLE. ONLY A PERSON CERTIFIED UNDER THIS ARTICLE SHALL
 BE AUTHORIZED TO USE THE TITLE "CERTIFIED DOULA".

 CLE.

  1. APPLICATION: FILE AN APPLICATION WITH THE DEPARTMENT.
  2. EDUCATION: SHALL HAVE ATTAINED A HIGH SCHOOL DIPLOMA, OR THE EQUIV-
