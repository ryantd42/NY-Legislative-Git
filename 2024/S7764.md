# Establishes the water resiliency projects fund; appropriation

**Bill ID:** S7764
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7764 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7764)

## Summary

Establishes the water resiliency projects fund for flood mitigation, stormwater mitigation and other water related resiliency projects; makes an appropriation therefor.
