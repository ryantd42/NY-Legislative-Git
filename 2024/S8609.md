# Allows certain firearm licensees to open carry

**Bill ID:** S8609
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8609 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8609)

## Summary

Expands the firearm license to have and carry concealed, without regard to employment or place of possession, to include open carry.
