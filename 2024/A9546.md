# Excludes certain solar or wind energy projects in the county of Lewis from certain appraisal models and discount rates

**Bill ID:** A9546
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9546 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9546)

## Summary

Excludes solar or wind energy projects in the county of Lewis that were already in existence on the effective date of Part N of chapter 59 of the laws of 2023 from certain appraisal models and discount rates.
