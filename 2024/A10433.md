# Establishes a process for designating transfer-on-death beneficiaries for farming implements

**Bill ID:** A10433
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10433 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10433)

## Summary

Establishes a process for designating transfer-on-death beneficiaries for farming implements involving filing a document with the commissioner of agriculture and markets.
