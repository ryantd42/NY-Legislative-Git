**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J1850 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J1850)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1850
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING  Commando  Lodge  No.  1105,  Improved
  Benevolent and Protective Order Elks of  the  World,
  upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  its Annual Charity
  Dinner Dance on April 27, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize that
the quality and character of life in the  communities  across  New  York
State  are  reflective  of  the concerned and dedicated efforts of those
organizations and individuals who are devoted  to  the  welfare  of  the
community and its citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It is also the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize
and pay tribute to fraternal organizations of high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Commando Lodge No. 1105, Improved Benevolent and Protective  Order  Elks
of  the  World,  upon  the occasion of hosting its Annual Charity Dinner
Dance on Saturday, April 27, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, The mission of the Improved Benevolent and Protective Order
Elks of the World is  to  teach  the  principles  of  Charity,  Justice,
Brotherly  Love  and  Fidelity; to recognize a belief in God; to promote
the welfare and enhance the happiness of its  Members;  to  quicken  the
spirit  of  American  patriotism;  to  cultivate  good  fellowship;  to
perpetuate itself as a fraternal organization, and to  provide  for  its
government,  the  Benevolent  and Protective Order of Elks of the United
States  of  America  will  serve  the  people  and  communities  through
benevolent programs, demonstrating that Elks Care and Elks Share; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Celebrated  at the Joseph Zaloga Post No. 1520, the Annual
Charity Dinner Dance is an opportunity for  the  lodge  to  support  the
youth  with  reading  programs,  auxiliary  groups, and community meals,
while also caring for the sick and shut in throughout its district; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under the good grace and able  leadership  of  Fred  Thomas
Jr.,  the  members of Commando Lodge No. 1105 has shown great dedication
and discipline, holding true to their mission of  putting  others  above
themselves in all their charitable and social endeavors; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Organizations  of  singular  distinction  such as Commando
Lodge No.  1105, merit the grateful praise of the community and  of  the
State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  practice  of  this Legislative Body that when
organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are brought to  our
attention,  they  should be recognized by all the citizens of this great
Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Commando Lodge No. 1105, Improved Benevolent and Protective Order
 
Elks of the World, upon the  occasion  of  hosting  its  Annual  Charity
Dinner Dance on April 27, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Commando Lodge No. 1105.
