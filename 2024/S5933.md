# Designates offenses against law enforcement officers as hate crimes and makes graffiti a hate crime

**Bill ID:** S5933
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Rhoads
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5933 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5933)

## Summary

Designates offenses against law enforcement officers as hate crimes; designates making graffiti as a specified offense.
