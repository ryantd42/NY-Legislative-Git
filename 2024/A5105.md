# Relates to the crime of official misconduct for sexual harassment

**Bill ID:** A5105
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rebecca Seawright
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5105 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5105)

## Summary

Relates to the crime of official misconduct for sexual harassment for members of the New York state legislature; makes it a class A misdemeanor.
