# Requires display of building numbers on all buildings to which numbers have been assigned

**Bill ID:** S4980
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dean Murray
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4980 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4980)

## Summary

Requires plainly legible display of building numbers on all buildings to which numbers have been assigned; provides for a fine for violations of such requirement.
