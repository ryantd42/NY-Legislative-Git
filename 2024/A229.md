# Relates to increasing penalties for endangerment of a highway worker, promote work zone safety awareness, and establish a fund for additional work zone safety enforcement

**Bill ID:** A229
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A229 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A229)

## Summary

Increases penalties for endangerment of a highway worker; promotes work zone safety awareness; establishes a fund for additional work zone safety enforcement.
