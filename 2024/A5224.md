# Requires the disclosure of prices and additional charges at appearance enhancement businesses which practice nail specialty

**Bill ID:** A5224
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A5224 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5224)

## Summary

Requires the disclosure of prices and additional charges at appearance enhancement businesses which practice nail specialty.
