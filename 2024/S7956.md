# Requires the department of health to study the effects of certain training criteria established by the Lymphology Association of North America

**Bill ID:** S7956
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7956 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7956)

## Summary

Establishes the Lymphology study; requires the department of health to study the effects of certain training criteria established by the Lymphology Association of North America.
