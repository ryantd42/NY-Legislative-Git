# Includes the city of New York in the aid and incentives for municipalities program

**Bill ID:** S8592
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8592 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8592)

## Summary

Includes the city of New York in the aid and incentives for municipalities program for fiscal years beginning 4/1/2024 and thereafter.
