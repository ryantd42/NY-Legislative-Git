# Relates to appeals to the court of appeals on constitutional grounds

**Bill ID:** A6359
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6359 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6359)

## Summary

Relates to appeals to the court of appeals based upon a constitutional question; sets criteria for a substantial constitutional question.
