# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "General Colin Powell Thruway"

**Bill ID:** A5149
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5149 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5149)

## Summary

Designates a portion of interstate eighty-seven as the "General Colin Powell Thruway".
