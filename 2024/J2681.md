## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2681
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  40th  Anniversary  of Hamilton
  Orthopaedics of New Hartford, New York  in  July  of
  2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize the
achievements of those individuals  and  businesses  which  substantially
contribute  to the economic vitality of their communities and the entire
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commemorate  the  40th  Anniversary  of  Hamilton  Orthopaedics  of  New
Hartford, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Founded  by  Dr. Ivan Gowan in 1984, Hamilton Orthopaedics
Spine & Sports Medicine serves thousands of people in Central  New  York
every  year;  patients  include  athletes,  children, adults, and senior
citizens; and
 
  WHEREAS, Providing advanced orthopaedic care, Hamilton  Orthopaedics
and  its  team  of  highly  renowned orthopedic specialists and surgeons
focus on sports medicine, spine,  joint  replacement,  shoulder,  elbow,
foot, ankle and much more; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Actively  involved  in  local  universities,  Hamilton
Orthopaedics is responsible for the  Sports  Medicine  coverage  of  the
athletic  programs  at Colgate University, Morrisville State College and
Utica University; the  practice  also  attends  games  and  treats  many
athletes  from  high  schools  in Madison, Oneida, Herkimer and Chenango
counties; and
 
  WHEREAS, As an additional service to the sports community,  Hamilton
Orthopaedics  has  instated  Sports  Medicine walk-in clinics offered on
Monday mornings, designed specifically for treatment of  acute  injuries
that did not require immediate emergency treatment; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  Hamilton  Orthopaedics  is  in  direct
correlation to the efforts of its dedicated leadership and staff,  whose
involvement  is,  and  always  has  been, characterized by an impressive
commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising  standard  of
excellence  in all endeavors on behalf of the business and the community
it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, For 40 years, Hamilton Orthopaedics has strived to  improve
the  health  of  community  members  it  serves,  with  a  commitment to
excellence in all it does through quality care and programs; and
 
  WHEREAS , It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this  Legislative  Body
acknowledges  this  exceptional  business  and  its contributions to the
local and State economies, fully confident it will continue to enjoy the
tremendous successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the  40th  Anniversary  of  Hamilton  Orthopaedics  of  New
Hartford, New York in July of 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Hamilton Orthopaedics.
