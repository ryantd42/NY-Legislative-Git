# Designates the month of September as firefighter, police officer and emergency medical technician appreciation month

**Bill ID:** S5258
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5258 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5258)

## Summary

Designates the month of September as firefighter, police officer and emergency medical technician appreciation month.
