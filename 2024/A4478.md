# Relates to creating a distinctive plate for New York state licensed mental health counselors

**Bill ID:** A4478
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4478 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4478)

## Summary

Creates a distinctive plate for New York state licensed mental health counselors.
