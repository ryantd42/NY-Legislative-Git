# Relates to the canvassing of ballots; repealer

**Bill ID:** S8832
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Gianaris
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8832 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8832)

## Summary

Relates to the canvassing of ballots; repeals provisions of law relating to the counting of ballots and challenges to ballots.
