# Requires September eleventh awareness curriculum or instruction for school districts

**Bill ID:** A8347AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Catalina Cruz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8347AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8347AA)

## Summary

Requires September eleventh awareness curriculum or instruction for school districts, regarding the September eleventh, two thousand one attacks on the United States of America.
