# Relates to the submission of claims for services provided by home care agencies

**Bill ID:** A5750
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5750 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5750)

## Summary

Provides that the submission of claims for services provided by home care agencies shall be done on forms approved by the centers for Medicare and Medicaid services.
