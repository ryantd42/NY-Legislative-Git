# Authorizes the liquor authority to establish a liaison with each community board in the city of New York for matters relating to license or permit issuance, renewal, modification and alteration

**Bill ID:** A8921
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Ron Kim
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8921 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8921)

## Summary

Authorizes the liquor authority to establish a liaison with each community board in the city of New York for matters relating to license or permit issuance, renewal, modification and alteration; clarifies the powers and duties of the New York alcoholic beverage control problem premises task force.
