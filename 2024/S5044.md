# Exempts pet food from sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S5044
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5044 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5044)

## Summary

Exempts pet food from sales and compensating use taxes.
