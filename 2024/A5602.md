# Relates to the liability of the state for damages to persons caused by defects in state highways

**Bill ID:** A5602
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Doug Smith
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5602 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5602)

## Summary

Imposes liability on the state for damages to persons caused by defects in state highways year round.
