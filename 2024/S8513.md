# Authorizes the village of Greenwood Lake, county of Orange to allow golf carts on certain streets

**Bill ID:** S8513
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8513 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8513)

## Summary

Authorizes the village of Greenwood Lake, county of Orange to allow golf carts on certain streets under the exclusive control and jurisdiction of such village.
