# Prohibits non-disclosure clauses in leases of real property to be used for hydraulic fracturing

**Bill ID:** A4864
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4864 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4864)

## Summary

Prohibits non-disclosure clauses in leases of real property to be used for hydraulic fracturing.
