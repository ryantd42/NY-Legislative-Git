# Directs a study of linkages between cancer and neurological diseases and contaminated environments

**Bill ID:** A100
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A100 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A100)

## Summary

Directs the department of health and the department of environmental conservation to conduct a study analyzing linkages between cancer and neurological diseases and contaminated environments.

 act, unless the commissioner of health requests in writing, an extension
 of time.
