# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian, Griffin Hon, and Salutatorian, Scout Chen, of Syosset High School, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2456 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2456)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2456
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024 Valedictorian, Griffin
  Hon, and Salutatorian, Scout Chen, of  Syosset  High
  School,  in  recognition  of  their  outstanding
  accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions  to  honor  the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2024 Valedictorian,  Griffin  Hon,  and  Salutatorian,  Scout  Chen,  of
Syosset  High  School,  in  recognition  of  their  outstanding
accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Valedictorian, Griffin Hon, and Salutatorian,  Scout  Chen,
represent  the best of developed potential inherent in our most precious
resource, our youth; their achievements have brought enduring  honor  to
their families and communities and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  Valedictorian,  Griffin  Hon,  and Salutatorian, Scout
Chen, take leave of  Syosset  High  School,  they  bestow  a  legacy  of
academic achievement, school involvement, and community service to which
others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian,  Griffin Hon, and Salutatorian, Scout Chen,
may  now  stand  with  pride  as  they  assess  their  achievements  and
experience  the  satisfaction  of  their  labors  and  the  joy of their
accomplishments, eager to face the  new  experiences  of  a  challenging
world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2024  Valedictorian,  Griffin  Hon,  and  Salutatorian,
Scout  Chen, of Syosset High School, in recognition of their outstanding
accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Valedictorian, Griffin Hon, and Salutatorian, Scout Chen.
