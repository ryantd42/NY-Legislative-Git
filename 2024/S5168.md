# Establishes a veterans' dental insurance program

**Bill ID:** S5168
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5168 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5168)

## Summary

Establishes a veterans' dental insurance program through the department of civil service; appropriates funds.
