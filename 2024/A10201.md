# Exempts certain property from the prohibition of alcohol sales within a certain distance of a church

**Bill ID:** A10201
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A10201 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10201)

## Summary

Exempts certain property from the prohibition of alcohol sales within a certain distance of a church within the county of Washington, state of New York.
