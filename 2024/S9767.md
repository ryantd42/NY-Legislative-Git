# Extends certain provisions relating to expanded polystyrene foam container and polystyrene loose fill packaging ban and relating to moneys collected for violations of such ban

**Bill ID:** S9767
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Liz Krueger
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9767 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9767)

## Summary

Extends certain provisions relating to expanded polystyrene foam container and polystyrene loose fill packaging ban and relating to moneys collected for violations of such ban to January 1, 2030.
