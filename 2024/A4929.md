# Establishes the volunteer fire departments capital fund; appropriation

**Bill ID:** A4929
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4929 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4929)

## Summary

Establishes the volunteer fire departments capital fund under the office of fire prevention and control; appropriates funds for such capital fund.

  3. ADMINISTRATION. THE CUSTODY OF, AND RESPONSIBILITY FOR, THE CAPITAL
 FUND SHALL BE TAKEN UP BY THE OFFICE, IN COMPLIANCE WITH ALL  APPLICABLE
 LAWS,  RULES, REGULATIONS AND OTHER REQUIREMENTS. SUCH ADMINISTRATION OF
 THE CAPITAL FUND SHALL INCLUDE, BUT NOT BE LIMITED TO:
  (A)  THE  OFFICE  SHALL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR THE RECEIPT, MANAGEMENT AND
 EXPENDITURE OF MONIES HELD IN THE CAPITAL FUND;
  (B) THE OFFICE SHALL MAINTAIN BOOKS  AND  RECORDS  PERTAINING  TO  ALL
 MONIES  RECEIVED  AND  DISBURSED PURSUANT TO THIS SECTION AND THE AGREE-
 MENT;
  (C) MONIES IN SUCH CAPITAL FUND SHALL BE UTILIZED FOR THE  PURPOSE  OF
 MAKING  GRANTS  TO QUALIFYING PARTICIPATING GRANTEES FOR PURPOSES DETER-
 MINED BY THE OFFICE;
  (D) PARTICIPATING GRANTEES SHALL BE CHOSEN BY THE  OFFICE  THROUGH  AN
 APPLICATION PROCESS APPROVED BY THE OFFICE AND THE COMPTROLLER;
  (E)  ELIGIBLE USES OF FUNDS SO GRANTED TO PARTICIPATING GRANTEES SHALL
 BE DETERMINED BY THE OFFICE;
  (F) THE OFFICE ADMINISTERING THE CAPITAL FUND SHALL  REPORT  QUARTERLY
 ON  THE  TRANSACTIONS IN THE CAPITAL FUND IN A FORM AND MANNER SPECIFIED
 BY THE COMPTROLLER IN CONSULTATION WITH THE COMMISSIONER OF TAXATION AND
 FINANCE, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO: RECEIPTS OR DEPOSITS TO THE FUND;
 DISBURSEMENTS; LOANS OR CREDIT ENHANCEMENT MADE FROM THE  FUND;  INVEST-
 MENT INCOME; AND THE BALANCE ON HAND AS OF THE END OF THE MONTH FOR EACH
 SUCH QUARTER;
  (G)  THE  OFFICE  SHALL BE REQUIRED TO INVEST MONIES ON DEPOSIT IN THE

 OF  THE  COMPTROLLER, AND ALL INVESTMENT INCOME SHALL BE CREDITED TO THE
 REVOLVING CAPITAL FUND, AND SPENT THEREFROM ONLY FOR  THE  PURPOSES  SET
 FORTH IN THIS SECTION;
  (H)  CAPITAL FUND MONIES SHALL BE HELD IN TRUST AND USED FOR THE BENE-
 FIT OF ELIGIBLE GRANTEES; AND
  (I) ANY OTHER TERM OR CONDITION AS DETERMINED BY THE  OFFICE  AND  THE
 COMPTROLLER,  IN  CONSULTATION  WITH  THE  COMMISSIONER  OF TAXATION AND
 FINANCE.
  4. GRANT DOCUMENTATION. GRANTS FROM THE CAPITAL  FUND  SHALL  BE  MADE
 PURSUANT TO A WRITTEN AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE OFFICE AND THE PARTICIPATING
 GRANTEE, SPECIFYING THE TERMS OF THE GRANT. THE GRANT AGREEMENT SHALL BE

 THE OFFICE, AND MAY INCLUDE  SUCH  OTHER  WRITTEN  DOCUMENTATION  AND/OR
 AGREEMENTS  AS  SHALL BE REQUIRED IN THE JUDGMENT OF THE COMPTROLLER AND

 USE GRANT FUNDS IN ACCORDANCE WITH THIS SECTION  OR  THE  TERMS  OF  THE
 GRANTEE'S AGREEMENT WITH THE OFFICE.
