# Relates to the calculation of selected operating aid per pupil for the Boquet Valley central school district

**Bill ID:** A10481
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Matthew Simpson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10481 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10481)

## Summary

Relates to the calculation of selected operating aid per pupil for the Boquet Valley central school district; provides the date of reorganization, July 1, 2019, shall be used when calculating.
