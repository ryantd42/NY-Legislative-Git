## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2644
 
BY: Senator TEDISCO
 
  COMMEMORATING the 28th Annual Capital Region Buddy
  Walk  for  Down Syndrome at Central Park in the City
  of Schenectady on September 29, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
commend  events  which  promote  inclusion  and  awareness  for  the
achievements of individuals with Down syndrome; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commemorate the 28th Annual Capital Region Buddy Walk for Down  Syndrome
at  Central  Park  in  the  City  of  Schenectady,  New York, on Sunday,
September 29, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Capital Region's Buddy Walk is a short one-mile  stroll
around  the pond in Schenectady's Central Park; along with the walk, the
day is filled with resource information,  entertainment,  music,  games,
crafts, and snacks; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Funds  raised  at  the  Buddy Walk have a direct impact on
individuals with Down syndrome  and  their  families  within  the  local
communities  at  any  stage  of  life which includes parental education,
professional education, Modified Curricular Units  provided  to  Capital
Region school districts, and much, much more; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Down  Syndrome  Aim  High  Resource  Center  has been
steadfast in its mission to provide  parent-to-parent  and  professional
services  and  support  to  individuals  with  Down  syndrome  and their
families and to educate the broader community on Down syndrome; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Center for Disability Services has been one of  upstate
New  York's  largest  providers of programs and services for individuals
who  have  disabilities  with  more  than  80  locations  throughout  15
counties,  annually  providing  programs  and  services  to  over 12,000
individuals,  ranging  from  infants  to  senior  citizens,  and  their
families; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Down  syndrome is a genetic condition in where a person is
born with an extra chromosome, which can affect how their brain and body
develop; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sponsors such as Central Self Storage, Aflac and  Cusack  &
Company  CPA's  join  together  to  make the Capital Region Buddy Walk a
rousing success year in and year out; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the 28th Annual Capital Region Buddy Walk for Down Syndrome
at Central Park in the City of Schenectady on September 29, 2024; and be
it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  hierarchy  of  the Down Syndrome Aim High Resource
Center and Center for Disability Services.
