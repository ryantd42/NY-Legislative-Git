# Honoring Eve Van de Wal upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the Businessperson of the Year Award by the Greater Utica Chamber of Commerce

**Bill ID:** K1043
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian D. Miller
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K1043 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K1043)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 1043
 
BY: M. of A. Miller
 
  HONORING  Eve  Van de Wal upon the occasion of her
  designation as recipient of  the  Businessperson  of
  the  Year  Award  by  the  Greater  Utica Chamber of
  Commerce
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
commend  the  leaders  of  commerce  and  industry whose accomplishments
contribute to the economic health and prosperity of the  communities  of
the  State  of  New  York,  and  whose  purposeful endeavors as involved
members of the community serve to enhance the quality of  life  therein;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Eve Van de Wal upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of  the
Businessperson  of  the  Year  Award  by  the  Greater  Utica Chamber of
Commerce, to be celebrated on Thursday, May 16, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, Eve Van de Wal is a proud life-long resident of the  Mohawk
Valley, creating and maintaining strong relationships with key community
leaders  through her work with Excellus BlueCross BlueShield (BCBS); she
joined the company in 1998 and held  numerous  administrative  positions
prior to her current role of regional president; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  the  course  of  her  over 40-year career, she
generously shared her extensive and  diverse  experience  in  healthcare
delivery  and  health insurance, having enjoyed a successful career as a
critical care and coronary care nurse before joining  the  health  plan;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition  to  her  volunteer  efforts  at  hundreds of
community events throughout her career, Eve Van de Wal  has  served  her
hometown  and  Excellus  BCBS  by  serving on numerous boards including:
Adirondack Bank, Economic  Development  Growth  Enterprises  Corporation
(EDGE),  The United Way of the Mohawk Valley and Greater Utica Area, The
Community Foundation of Herkimer and Oneida Counties,  Health  Workforce
New  York  (HWNY),  Northern  Area  Health and Education Center (NAHEC),
Utica First Insurance Company, and YWCA of the Mohawk Valley; and
 
  WHEREAS, The recipient of numerous awards and accolades, Eve Van  de
Wal  has  received the following honors: Women of Distinction by the New
York State Senate, On Point for College Foundation of Hope, American Red
Cross Great Hero, The Genesis Group Joseph R. Carucci Legacy Award, Boys
and Girls Club Living Legend Award, YWCA Salute to Outstanding Women for
Healthcare, Oneida County Historical Society Hall of Fame Living Legends
Award, Boy Scouts of America Public  Service  Leadership  Award,  Rotary
International Paul Harris Fellow Award, and Successful Women in Business
Award by the Mohawk Valley; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Receiving the esteemed honor of Businessperson of the Year
is a testament to all the hard work and positive contributions  Eve  Van
de  Wal  has so selflessly demonstrated on behalf of her community; this
distinction comes at a meaningful time in her life as Eve Van de Wal  is
 
retiring  from  her cherished position as regional president on Tuesday,
April 30, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  the  past 40 years, Eve Van de Wal rendered faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to  Excellus  BlueCross  BlueShield,
the  greater  Utica  area, and, as a result, the communities of New York
State at large; and
 
  WHEREAS, In her capacity as  regional  president,  Eve  Van  de  Wal
served  with  loyalty,  honor  and  distinction, justly earning her this
honor; and
 
  WHEREAS, With her throughout have been her husband, Dale, their  two
grown  children  and  their  spouses, and her grandchildren, all of whom
feel  privileged  to  be  a  part  of  her  life  and  rejoice  in  her
achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  seeks  to  acknowledge  those
individuals whose  professional  lives  and  civic  endeavors  serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Eve Van de Wal upon the occasion of her designation  as  recipient
of  the Businessperson of the Year Award by the Greater Utica Chamber of
Commerce; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Eve Van de Wal.
