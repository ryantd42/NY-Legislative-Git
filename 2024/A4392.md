# Relates to establishing a county representation system for electing the governor and lieutenant-governor within the state

**Bill ID:** A4392
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo J. Morinello
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4392 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4392)

## Summary

Establishes a county representation system for electing the governor and lieutenant-governor within the state.
