# Requires the office of minority health and health disparities prevention to conduct a study on the health impacts of COVID-19 on transgender and non-binary racial and ethnic minorities in New York state

**Bill ID:** S7897
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7897 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7897)

## Summary

Requires the office of minority health and health disparities prevention to conduct a study on the health impacts of COVID-19 on transgender and non-binary racial and ethnic minorities in New York state.

 need to increase access to health  care  services  and  availability  of

 racial and  ethnic  health  care  discrimination  and  disparities  that

 recovery and mortality rates; and
  (d)  identify  the  extent  to  which  COVID-19  regulations  and/or
 restrictions  led to any disparities in health care treatment for trans-
 gender and non-binary racial and ethnic minorities.
