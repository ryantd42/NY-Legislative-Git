# Extends the authorization of the county of Rensselaer to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A6316A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A6316A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6316A)

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Rensselaer to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes until November 30, 2025.
