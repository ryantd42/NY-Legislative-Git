# Establishes the crime of sexual abuse against a patient or client by a health care provider in the first and second degree

**Bill ID:** A6349
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6349 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6349)

## Summary

Establishes the crime of sexual abuse against a patient or client by a health care provider in the first and second degree; designates such crimes as being class B and E felonies, respectively.
