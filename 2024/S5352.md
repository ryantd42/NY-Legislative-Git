# Requires individuals to provide advanced notice prior to applying to operate certain facilities or services

**Bill ID:** S5352
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5352 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5352)

## Summary

Requires individuals to provide advanced notice prior to applying to operate certain facilities or services.

 AN ACT to amend the mental hygiene law, in relation to  requiring  indi-
  viduals  to  provide  advanced  notice  prior  to  applying to operate
  certain facilities or services

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1.  Section  19.21  of  the  mental hygiene law is amended by
 adding a new subdivision (e) to read as follows:
  (E) THE OFFICE SHALL REQUIRE INDIVIDUALS PLANNING TO APPLY TO  OPERATE
 FACILITIES  AND  SERVICES  RELATED TO ALCOHOLISM PROGRAMS, SUBSTANCE USE
 DISORDER PROGRAMS, AND CHEMICAL DEPENDENCE PROGRAMS, AND ANY PROGRAM  OR
 FACILITY PURPORTING TO PROVIDE SUCH SERVICES, TO PROVIDE ADVANCED NOTICE
 TO  MUNICIPALITIES  AND COMMUNITY BOARDS IN THE COMMUNITIES TO BE SERVED
 BY SUCH FACILITY OR SERVICE. SUCH NOTICE SHALL BE PROVIDED NO LESS  THAN
 SIXTY  DAYS  BEFORE  SUCH  INDIVIDUAL  APPLIES  TO OPEN SUCH FACILITY OR
 SERVICE.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
