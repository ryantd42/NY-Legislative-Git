# Commending Salutatorian Areej Nawaz of Westbury High School, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2510 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2510)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2510
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Salutatorian  Areej  Nawaz of Westbury
  High  School,  in  recognition  of  her  outstanding
  accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions, to honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to  commend
Salutatorian  Areej Nawaz of Westbury High School, in recognition of her
outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian Areej Nawaz represents the best  of  developed
potential  inherent  in  our  most  precious  resource,  our  youth; her
achievements have brought enduring honor to her family and community and
should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Salutatorian Areej Nawaz takes leave  of  Westbury  High
School, she bestows a legacy of academic achievement, school involvement
and community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Salutatorian  Areej  Nawaz may now stand with pride as she
assesses her achievements, experiences the satisfaction  of  her  labors
and the joy of her accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of
a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Salutatorian Areej Nawaz of Westbury High School, in recognition
of her outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Salutatorian Areej Nawaz.
