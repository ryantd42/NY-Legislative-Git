# Requires the empire state development corporation to establish an office of accountability and compliance

**Bill ID:** S9572
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9572 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9572)

## Summary

Requires the empire state development corporation to establish an office of accountability and compliance, the sole mission of which is to monitor projects for compliance to terms of general project plans and other related agreements.
