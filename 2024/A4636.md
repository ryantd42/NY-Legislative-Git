# Requires the state police to investigate accusations of police misconduct

**Bill ID:** A4636
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4636 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4636)

## Summary

Requires the superintendent of state police to investigate accusations of police misconduct filed against town or village police.
