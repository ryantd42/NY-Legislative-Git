# Modifies the definition of renewable energy systems

**Bill ID:** S7994
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7994 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7994)

## Summary

Modifies the definition of renewable energy systems to include systems that generate electricity or energy through biomass located within a military installation.
