# Requires certain municipal positions undergo continuing legal education courses in ethics

**Bill ID:** A10168
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10168 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10168)

## Summary

Requires certain municipal positions undergo continuing legal education courses in ethics and the format in which such courses may be completed.
