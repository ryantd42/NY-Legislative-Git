# Establishes the New York state worker protection and labor enforcement fund

**Bill ID:** A9281
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9281 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9281)

## Summary

Establishes the New York state worker protection and labor enforcement fund to supplement the department of labor's labor law enforcement duties.
