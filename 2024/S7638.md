# Exempts from the mortgage recording tax mortgages executed by a veteran first time homebuyer

**Bill ID:** S7638
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7638 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7638)

## Summary

Exempts from the mortgage recording tax mortgages executed by a veteran first time homebuyer.
