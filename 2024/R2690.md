# Relates to a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for grants in aid to certain not-for-profit arts and cultural organizations

**Bill ID:** R2690
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R2690 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/R2690)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2690
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R2859  of  2022
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  a  certain  appropriation for the
  2022-2023 state fiscal year additional grants in aid
  to  certain  not-for-profit  arts  and  cultural
  organizations,  as  required by a plan setting forth
  an itemized list of grantees with the amount  to  be
  received  by each, or the methodology for allocating
  such appropriation. Such plan shall  be  subject  to
  the  approval  of  the  temporary  president  of the
  senate and the director of the budget and thereafter
  shall be included in a resolution  calling  for  the
  expenditure of such monies, which resolution must be
  approved  by  a majority vote of all members elected
  to the senate upon a roll call vote
 
  RESOLVED, That funds originally appropriated in 2022  pursuant  to
section  1  of  chapter  53  of  the  laws  of 2023 and reappropriated
pursuant to section 1 of chapter 53 of the laws of 2024  which  enacts
the  aid to localities, local assistance account, additional grants in
aid to certain not-for-profit arts and  cultural  organizations.  Such
funds  shall  be  apportioned  pursuant  to  a  plan  setting forth an
itemized list of grantees with the amount to be received by  each,  or
the  methodology for allocating such appropriation. Such plan shall be
subject to the approval of the temporary president of the  senate  and
the  director  of  the  budget  and  thereafter shall be included in a
resolution  calling  for  the  expenditure  of  such  monies,  which
resolution  must be approved by a majority vote of all members elected
to the senate upon a roll call vote, in accordance with the  following
schedule  which  shall be added to the schedule originally approved in
Senate Resolution R2859 of 2022 as amended by R1399 of 2023:
 
LUMA Arts Initiative Inc. 
  $12,500
Salvatore Larussa Dance Company,  Inc. 
  $5,000
