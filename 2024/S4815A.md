# Relates to issuing temporary permits to certain fantasy sports operators

**Bill ID:** S4815AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4815AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4815AA)

## Summary

Relates to issuing temporary permits to certain fantasy sports operators that were not offering contests to persons located in New York prior to November 10, 2015.
