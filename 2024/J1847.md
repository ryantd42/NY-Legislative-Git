## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1847
 
BY: Senator KENNEDY
 
  COMMENDING  the  Amherst  Gaelic  League  upon the
  occasion of  celebrating  its  50th  Anniversary  on
  March 8, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the  Amherst Gaelic League upon the occasion of celebrating its
50th Anniversary on Friday, March 8, 2024; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Mission Statement and objective of the  Amherst  Gaelic
League (AGL) is to be of service to the needy; to exchange and promote a
spirit  of  good  will  and  benevolence  among  its members; to develop
educational programs, entertainment, and recreational  activities  based
on  Irish  tradition,  history,  and folklore; and to promote and foster
among its members the preservation, appreciation, and  understanding  of
Irish history and folklore in all its form; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout its past five decades, the Amherst Gaelic League
has  contributed  more  than  $300,000  to  bolster  numerous  local
organizations that appeal to its mission; and
 
  WHEREAS, Included in the extensive list of neighboring organizations
and programs which have benefitted from the generous support provided by
the Amherst Gaelic League are: Society of St.  Vincent  de  Paul-Refugee
Resettlement Program; Youth Team which is dedicated to the promotion and
preservation of Gaelic Games and Irish culture in the community; Western
New  York  Irish Famine Memorial; Buffalo Naval & Military Park; Amherst
Police Foundation; Children's Hospital's lung unit; and Ronald  McDonald
House; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Under  the capable leadership of current President Michael
Dolan,  the  Amherst  Gaelic  League  has  promoted  the  study  and
understanding  of  Irish  history  and  the  causes  and  results of the
emigration of persons from Ireland to the United States of  America  and
the division of Ireland; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Amherst Gaelic League also encourages and supports the
Irish citizens' study and understanding of the  principles  of  American
democracy  and  way  of  life through exchange programs which assist the
people of Ireland  through  the  utilization  of  teaching  foundations,
scholarships,  correspondence and other written materials, and visits to
the United States in addition to American citizens' journeys to Ireland;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the  Amherst  Gaelic  League  is  in  direct
correlation  to  the  efforts  of its members, whose involvement is, and
always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled
 
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Amherst Gaelic League, upon the occasion of celebrating  its
50th  Anniversary,  and to honor its members, past and present, who have
labored so conscientiously to secure and improve the quality of life for
the citizens of the community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Michael Dolan, President, the Amherst Gaelic League.
