# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Sergeant Elijah A. Briggs Memorial Highway"

**Bill ID:** S4900A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Rolison
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S4900A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4900A)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the town of Fishkill, Dutchess county as the "Sergeant Elijah A. Briggs Memorial Highway".
