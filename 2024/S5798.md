# Relates to the right to breast feed

**Bill ID:** S5798
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S5798 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5798)

## Summary

Makes certain terms gender neutral in provisions regarding the right to breast feed.
