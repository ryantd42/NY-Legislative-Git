# Repeals certain provisions of the correction law relating to the transfer of incarcerated individuals from a county jail to a state correctional institution

**Bill ID:** A10126
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Demond Meeks
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10126 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10126)

## Summary

Repeals provisions relating to the transfer of incarcerated individuals from a county jail to a state correctional institution.
