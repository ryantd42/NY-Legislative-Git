# Relates to education and outreach programs for individuals who have suffered a pregnancy loss or infant loss

**Bill ID:** S6082AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S6082AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6082AA)

## Summary

Includes within the health care and wellness education and outreach program offered by the department of health resources for individuals who have suffered a pregnancy loss or infant loss.
