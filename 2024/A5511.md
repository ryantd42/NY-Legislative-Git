# Requires social service's applications to be made available online

**Bill ID:** A5511
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5511 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5511)

## Summary

Requires all social service's applications to be made publicly available online on the department's website.
