## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1506
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  50th Anniversary of the Jewish
  Community Federation of the Mohawk  Valley  and  the
  Jewish Community Center of Utica, New York
 
  WHEREAS, Religious institutions, and the many spiritual, social, and
educational  benefits  they confer, play a vital role in the development
of the moral fabric of a responsible citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the tradition of this State and this  Nation  to  pay
tribute  to  those  institutions and individuals who have contributed to
the ethical and spiritual values of their communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body takes pleasure in  commemorating  the
50th Anniversary of the Jewish Community Federation of the Mohawk Valley
and the Jewish Community Center (JCC) of Utica, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Jewish  Community Federation of the Mohawk Valley and
the Jewish Community Center of Utica  strive  to  enrich  the  lives  of
individuals,  families,  the Jewish community, and all others that enter
their doors  with  their  non-profit  Community  Center  which  provides
cultural,  educational,  and athletic programming to all those living in
the greater Utica area, as well as others who live in central New  York;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Jewish Community Center of Utica is a place where Jews
and all others who live in the area are  welcome,  no  matter  where  or
whether  they worship, and regardless of their ages or lifestyles; there
is great value and potential in community members associating with  each
other in a safe, warm, and supportive environment; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  JCC  cultivates and promotes the experience of Jewish
traditions, values, and ethics, while fostering  meaningful  connections
between  people  from  all  backgrounds,  while  providing  countless
opportunities for educational, physical, and cultural enrichment; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Jewish Community Federation of the  Mohawk  Valley  and
the  Jewish  Community  Center  of Utica strongly believes in charitable
endeavors, providing a diverse array of  programs  to  their  community,
including:  Central  New  York's largest and most prestigious preschool,
reading programs, summer camp, as well as music, basketball, soccer, and
other exercise programs; and
 
  WHEREAS,  They  also  provide  adult  programming,  such  as  guest
lectures,  art programs, trips to nearby music performances, theatre and
ballet events, movie showings, book rap meetings, Mahjong,  as  well  as
basketball, pickleball, volleyball and exercise programs; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Jewish  Community Federation of the Mohawk Valley and
the Jewish  Community  Center  of  Utica  stands  on  the  threshold  of
tomorrow,  prepared  to  meet the challenges of the coming decades while
retaining that spiritual resolve which characterizes its past; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to take  note  of
enduring  religious  institutions  and to bring such institutions to the
attention of the people of this Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the 50th Anniversary of the Jewish Community  Federation  of
the  Mohawk  Valley  and  the  Jewish  Community  Center of Utica, fully
confident that this commemoration reflects the belief  in  those  values
which enhance the dignity and purpose of life; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Jewish Community Federation of the Mohawk Valley  and
the Jewish Community Center of Utica, New York.
