# Increases the occupancy tax rate authorized in the city of Yonkers; extends the effectiveness of an occupancy tax imposed in the city of Yonkers

**Bill ID:** S8900A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8900A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8900A)

## Summary

Increases the occupancy tax rate authorized in the city of Yonkers from 3 to 5 7/8ths; extends the effectiveness of the occupancy tax imposed in the city of Yonkers to September 1, 2027.
