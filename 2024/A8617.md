# Relates to notice of crude oil shipments to major facilities

**Bill ID:** A8617
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8617 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8617)

## Summary

Requires the owner or operator of a major facility to provide notice of crude oil shipments by railroad to major facilities.
