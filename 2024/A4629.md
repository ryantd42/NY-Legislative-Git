# Relates to comptroller approval of certain contracts during a declared state disaster emergency

**Bill ID:** A4629
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4629 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4629)

## Summary

Provides that where the governor suspends provisions relating to approval of certain contracts by state agencies, the comptroller shall review emergency contracts above a set threshold amount and submit a report to the chairs and ranking minority members of the senate finance committee and the assembly ways and means committee on such emergency contracts, noting any concerns and recommendations, within fourteen days of the submission of such emergency contract.
