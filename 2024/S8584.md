# Requires the New York state energy and research development authority inspect and certify solar energy systems as fire safe

**Bill ID:** S8584
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8584 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8584)

## Summary

Requires the New York state energy and research development authority inspect and certify solar energy systems as fire safe and that the results of such inspection are provided to the public.
