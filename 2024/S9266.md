# Relates to certain data required in an annual report on the administration of managed long term care plans

**Bill ID:** S9266
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9266 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9266)

## Summary

Requires certain data to be included in reports on the administration of managed long term care plans; changes reporting period to annually.
