# Authorizes Elijah Missionary Baptist Church to receive retroactive real property tax exempt status

**Bill ID:** A6183
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6183 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6183)

## Summary

Authorizes the city of Albany tax assessor to grant retroactive real property tax exempt status to Elijah Missionary Baptist Church.
