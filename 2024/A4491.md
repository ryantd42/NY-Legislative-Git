# Requires all school districts to purchase and provide assistive technology devices when an item is put on a student's individualized education program

**Bill ID:** A4491
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Ramos
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4491 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4491)

## Summary

Requires all school districts to purchase and provide assistive technology devices no later than two months after an item is put on a student's individualized education program.
