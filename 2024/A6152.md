# Requires the owner of certain sites to establish and maintain an environmental maintenance trust fund

**Bill ID:** A6152
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6152 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6152)

## Summary

Requires the owner of sites where any action is or was taken which may have a significant impact on the environment to establish and maintain an environmental maintenance trust fund.
