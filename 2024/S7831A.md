# Relates to assault on certain employees assigned to an airport, while in performance of their assigned duties, or in direct relation thereto

**Bill ID:** S7831A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7831A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7831A)

## Summary

Deems assault on certain employees assigned to an airport, while in performance of their assigned duties, or in direct relation thereto, to be assault in the second degree.
