# Provides the village of Ossining, Westchester county, with an exemption from restrictions on the hours of sale of alcoholic beverages at retail for on-premises consumption

**Bill ID:** A10017
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Dana Levenberg
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10017 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10017)

## Summary

Exempts the village of Ossining, Westchester county, from restrictions on the hours of sale of alcoholic beverages at retail for on-premises consumption.
