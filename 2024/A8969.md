# Authorizes the commissioner of the office of children and family services to conduct a study that examines barriers to the creation of child care providers

**Bill ID:** A8969
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8969 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8969)

## Summary

Authorizes the commissioner of the office of children and family services to conduct a study that examines barriers to the creation of child care providers and provide recommendations thereto to facilitate the creation of new child care providers.
