## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1797
 
BY: Senator GALLIVAN
 
  HONORING  Brandon  Sutton upon the occasion of his
  designation as recipient of the  Young  Entrepreneur
  of  the  Year  Award  by  the  Clarence  Chamber  of
  Commerce on March 21, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
commend  those distinguished citizens who would devote their life's work
and purpose to community service, contributing to  the  welfare  of  the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Brandon Sutton upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of  the
Young  Entrepreneur  of  the  Year  Award  by  the  Clarence  Chamber of
Commerce, to be celebrated at its Annual Awards Gala on Thursday,  March
21, 2024, at Samuel's Grande Manor in Clarence, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Brandon Sutton was nominated for this prestigious award for
his  steadfast  and  unremitting  commitment to his beloved community of
Clarence Center, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, A dynamic entrepreneur, Brandon Sutton is the driving force
behind Sutton Paving & Excavation Inc., an innovative  company  that  is
looking to revolutionize the paving and excavation industry; with a rich
family  history  deeply  rooted in the asphalt sector, his commitment to
excellence and meticulous attention to detail laid  the  foundation  for
his company's establishment in 2017; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Having  college  experience  in  mechanical  engineering,
Brandon Sutton brings a unique  perspective  to  his  leadership  style;
Sutton  Paving  has  risen  to  industry  leadership, transitioning from
residential  driveway  services  to  an  exclusive  concentration  on
large-scale  commercial  projects,  and  earning  distinctions  for
reliability and a dedicated commitment to innovation; and
 
  WHEREAS, Deeply committed to community service, Brandon  Sutton  has
served  as  an  advanced emergency medical technician and a firefighter;
his  heroism,  acknowledged  with  the  State's  highest  award  for
resuscitating  a  Clarence  resident in 2018, complements his pursuit of
excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  that  those  who
enhance  the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown a
long and sustained commitment to excellence certainly  have  earned  the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Brandon Sutton upon the occasion of his designation  as  recipient
of  the  Young Entrepreneur of the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of
Commerce on March 21, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Brandon Sutton.
