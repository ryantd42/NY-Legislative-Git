# Directs the metropolitan transportation authority and the New York city transit authority to rename the 110th Street - Central Park North subway station to the 110th Street - Malcolm X Boulevard station

**Bill ID:** A10520
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Inez E. Dickens
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10520 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10520)

## Summary

Directs the metropolitan transportation authority and the New York city transit authority to rename the 110th Street - Central Park North subway station to the 110th Street - Malcolm X Boulevard station.
