# Limits the Triborough bridge and tunnel authority with regard to the number of tolls charged on trucks

**Bill ID:** A9569
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9569 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9569)

## Summary

Limits the Triborough bridge and tunnel authority with regard to the number of tolls charged on trucks and the authority's power to raise its toll schedule.
