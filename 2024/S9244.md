# Exempts Massapequa school district from regulations regarding the use of indigenous mascots

**Bill ID:** S9244
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9244 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9244)

## Summary

Exempts Massapequa union free school district from laws, rules, or regulations regarding the use of indigenous names, mascots, and logos.
