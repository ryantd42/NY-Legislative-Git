# Exempts commuter vans from congestion pricing in New York city

**Bill ID:** S7708
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Liu
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7708 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7708)

## Summary

Exempts commuter vans from the congestion surcharge and the central business district tolling program in New York city.
