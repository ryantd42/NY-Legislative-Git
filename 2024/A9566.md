# Provides medical indemnity fund reimbursement for care provided by a family member certified as a complex care assistant

**Bill ID:** A9566
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9566 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9566)

## Summary

Provides medical indemnity fund reimbursement for care provided by a family member certified as a complex care assistant; directs the commissioner of health to develop a certification process for individuals as complex care assistants.

  § 2. The public health law is amended by adding a new  section  2999-k
 to read as follows:
  §  2999-K. CERTIFICATION OF COMPLEX CARE ASSISTANTS. 1. A COMPLEX CARE
 ASSISTANT CERTIFIED PURSUANT TO THIS  SECTION  SHALL  BE  AUTHORIZED  TO
 PROVIDE  CARE  FOR  A FAMILY MEMBER WHO IS A QUALIFIED PLAINTIFF, AND TO

 NINE HUNDRED NINETY-NINE-J OF THIS TITLE.
  2.  THE  COMMISSIONER  SHALL  ESTABLISH  A COURSE THAT MAY BE TAKEN BY

 INSTRUCTION IN THE FOLLOWING TASKS REQUIRED  TO  CARE  FOR  A  QUALIFIED
 PLAINTIFF:
  (A) MEDICATION ADMINISTRATION;
  (B) AIRWAY CLEARANCE THERAPIES;
  (C) TRACHEOSTOMY CARE;
  (D) INTRAVENOUS LINE CARE;
  (E) VENTILATOR CARE;
  (F) ENTERAL CARE;
  (G) ASSISTANCE WITH ACTIVITIES OF DAILY LIVING; AND
  (H) ANY OTHER TASKS AS DETERMINED BY THE COMMISSIONER.
  3.  THE COURSE UNDER SUBDIVISION ONE OF THIS SECTION SHALL CONCLUDE IN
 AN IN-PERSON EXAM, TESTING PROFICIENCY AND COMPETENCE OF EXAM-TAKERS  IN
 PERFORMING  THE  TASKS  REQUIRED TO CARE FOR A QUALIFIED PLAINTIFF UNDER
 SUBDIVISION TWO OF THIS SECTION. INDIVIDUALS WHO RECEIVE A PASSING GRADE
 IN SUCH EXAM, AS DETERMINED BY THE COMMISSIONER, SHALL BE CERTIFIED AS A
 COMPLEX CARE ASSISTANT.

  4-A.  THE  COMMISSIONER  SHALL  DEVELOP  A  METHOD BY WHICH QUALIFYING
