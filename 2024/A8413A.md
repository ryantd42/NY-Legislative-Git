# Requires the Triborough bridge and tunnel authority reimburse certain individuals from transit deserts

**Bill ID:** A8413A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8413A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8413A)

## Summary

Requires the Triborough bridge and tunnel authority to reimburse certain individuals from transit deserts when they travel into the central business district who need medical treatment or are providing religious services; provides that such reimbursement shall be one hundred percent of all associated fees.
