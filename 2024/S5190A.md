# Includes the space force as being members of the armed forces or veterans eligible for certain credits and benefits

**Bill ID:** S5190AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5190AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5190AA)

## Summary

Includes the Space Force as being members of the armed forces or veterans eligible for certain credits and benefits that are available to other active and veteran members of the armed forces.
