# Excludes certain actions from reporting requirements relating to environmental quality reviews

**Bill ID:** A4902
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Novakhov
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4902 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4902)

## Summary

Excludes from reporting requirements relating to environmental quality reviews actions to approve the siting or construction of housing in cities with a population of one million or more.
