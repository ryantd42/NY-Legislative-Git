# Grants retroactive membership with Tier IV status in the New York state teachers' retirement system to Peter Guarino

**Bill ID:** A5887A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Grants Peter Guarino retroactive membership with Tier IV status in the New York state teachers' retirement system.
