# Establishes light duty during pregnancy and parental leave for railroad employees

**Bill ID:** A8995A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8995A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8995A)

## Summary

Establishes light duty during pregnancy and parental leave for railroad employees.
