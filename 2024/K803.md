# Honoring Coach Tom Otis upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the Little League Coach of the Year Award on August 19, 2023

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jodi Giglio
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K803 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K803)

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 803
 
BY: M. of A. Giglio JA
 
  HONORING  Coach  Tom Otis upon the occasion of his
  designation as recipient of the Little League  Coach
  of the Year Award on August 19, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  Excellence  and  success  in  competitive  sports  can  be
achieved only through strenuous practice, team  play  and  team  spirit,
nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to commend those
notable athletic coaches within our midst who serve the  youth  of  this
great  Empire  State  and  who  have  distinguished themselves and their
communities through their  exemplary  athletic  achievements  and  their
outstanding commitment to excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Coach Tom Otis upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of  the
Little  League  Coach of the Year Award on Saturday, August 19, 2023, in
Williamsport, Pennsylvania, presented by Lance,  the  Positive  Coaching
Alliance,  Little  League, and former Little Leaguer and MLB player Todd
Frazier; and
 
  WHEREAS, A beloved local Westhampton coach,  Tom  Otis  was  awarded
this  year's Little League Coach of the Year Award and will also receive
a $5,000 grant toward future  programming  for  his  league  from  Lance
Sandwich Crackers; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  lifelong  East  End  resident,  Coach  Tom Otis grew up
playing Little League and participated in sports  for  his  whole  life;
when  his  son began showing an interest in sports, he found his passion
to utilize his skills to help the community by taking on a coaching role
with Little League; and
 
  WHEREAS, Living in Quogue, Coach Tom Otis has dedicated years to his
role as Little League coach, and also serves as league board member; and
 
  WHEREAS, Devoted to furthering the community,  Coach  Tom  Otis  has
served  as  the  backbone of his family business, Otis Ford, for several
years; he also volunteers his time to  teach  fire  education  in  local
schools  and is involved with charitable organizations, such as East End
Hospice and the Parent Teacher Association at his children's school; and
 
  WHEREAS, Balancing training with games that  incorporate  technique,
Coach  Tom  Otis  helps  kids  learn  the fundamentals of baseball while
making it fun with special effort made by him to  integrate  handshakes,
nicknames,  and  go-pro  videos  to  send  to  parents of their children
playing; and
 
  WHEREAS, With him throughout has been his beloved wife, Kim, and his
three sons, Thompson,  Winston,  and  August,  all  of  whom  have  felt
privileged  to  be  a  part  of  his  life  and  rejoice  in  his  many
achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, Coach Tom Otis's positive  attitude,  selfless  commitment,
goal-oriented  and  balanced  approach  to  instruction,  on  top of his
genuine concern for his  athletes,  have  contributed  to  his  enduring
success  as  a  coach, earning him the just respect of his community and
the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Coach  Tom Otis upon the occasion of his designation as recipient
of the Little League Coach of the Year Award on August 19, 2023; and  be
it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Coach Tom Otis.
