# Requires the New York state office for the prevention of domestic violence to establish trauma-informed standards for domestic violence abuse intervention programs

**Bill ID:** S9358
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Julia Salazar
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9358 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9358)

## Summary

Requires the New York state office for the prevention of domestic violence to establish trauma-informed standards for domestic violence abuse intervention programs; establishes requirements for such standards.
