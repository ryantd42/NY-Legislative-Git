# Extends the authorization for Chautauqua county to impose an additional one percent rate of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S6206
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S6206 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6206)

## Summary

Extends the authorization for Chautauqua county to impose an additional one percent rate of sales and compensating use taxes until November 30, 2025.
