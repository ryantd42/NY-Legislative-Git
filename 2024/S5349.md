# Provides for the imposition of sales and compensating use taxes in the county of Rockland

**Bill ID:** S5349
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S5349 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5349)

## Summary

Extends authorization to impose certain taxes in the county of Rockland.
