# Prohibits the sale or distribution of electronic cigarettes lacking federal food and drug administration premarket order of approval

**Bill ID:** A83
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A83 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A83)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale or distribution of electronic cigarettes lacking federal food and drug administration premarket order of approval; imposes penalties of not less than ten thousand dollars for violations.
