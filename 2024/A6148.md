# Requires the office of temporary and disability assistance promulgate regulations and create policies to encourage married adults or two adult households when determining the standard of need for eligibility

**Bill ID:** A6148
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6148 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6148)

## Summary

Requires the office of temporary and disability assistance promulgate regulations and create policies to encourage married adults or two adult households when determining the standard of need for eligibility for public assistance programs; and to exempt certain income and resources for such adults and households.
