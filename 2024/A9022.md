# Requires the New York state energy research and development authority conduct a study on Brooklyn College constructing a micro-grid

**Bill ID:** A9022
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9022 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9022)

## Summary

Requires the New York state energy research and development authority conduct a study on Brooklyn College constructing a micro-grid to study the feasibility, efficiency, and energy saving costs if such a micro-grid was constructed on campus.
