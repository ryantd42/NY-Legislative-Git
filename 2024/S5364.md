# Authorizes the city of Long Beach to alienate and discontinue the use of certain parklands

**Bill ID:** S5364
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S5364 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5364)

## Summary

Authorizes the city of Long Beach to alienate and discontinue the use of certain parklands to EW Offshore Wind Transport Corporation for the purpose of constructing, maintaining and operating a subterranean conduit and electrical distribution cable system, together with subterranean transition joint bays and link boxes, and appurtenances thereto, under city of Long Beach parkland.

  § 6. In the event that the city of Long  Beach  received  any  funding

 maintenance, or improvement of the parklands set forth in sections three
 and four of this act, the discontinuance and alienation  of  such  park-

  § 7. This act shall take effect immediately.
