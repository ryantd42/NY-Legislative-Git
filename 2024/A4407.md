# Authorizes police agencies to charge fees for searching, copying, and mailing police agency generated records

**Bill ID:** A4407
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Monica P. Wallace
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4407 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4407)

## Summary

Authorizes police agencies to charge fees for searching, copying, and mailing police agency generated records except for where the requesting party is a victim to or was charged with the crime related to such documents.
