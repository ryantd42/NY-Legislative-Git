# Provides for recertification simplification for Medicaid eligible recipients

**Bill ID:** A5980AB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5980AB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5980AB)

## Summary

Provides for automatic enrollment and recertification simplification for Medicaid managed care plans and long term care plans.
