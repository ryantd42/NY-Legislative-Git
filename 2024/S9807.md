# Authorizes the state to exchange parcels of land located in the city of Yonkers, county of Westchester, with 140 Warburton LLC

**Bill ID:** S9807
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S9807 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9807)

## Summary

Authorizes the state to exchange parcels of land located in the city of Yonkers, county of Westchester, with 140 Warburton LLC for land of equal or greater value.

  § 3. In exchange for the conveyance of such lands and  rights  as  are

  § 6. This act shall take effect immediately.
