# Designates sheriffs, under-sheriffs and deputy sheriffs of the city of New York as police officers

**Bill ID:** A5207

**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Designates sheriffs, under-sheriffs and deputy sheriffs of the city of New York as police officers.
