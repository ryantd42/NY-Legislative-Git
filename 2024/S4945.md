# Establishes a clean air account and makes an appropriation therefor

**Bill ID:** S4945
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4945 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4945)

## Summary

Establishes a clean air account; makes an appropriation therefor; specifies the manner in which such appropriation is to be utilized.
