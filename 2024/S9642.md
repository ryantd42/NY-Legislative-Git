# Provides insurance coverage for cranial prostheses

**Bill ID:** S9642
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9642 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9642)

## Summary

Provides insurance coverage for cranial prostheses for a diagnosed health condition, chronic illness, or injury.
