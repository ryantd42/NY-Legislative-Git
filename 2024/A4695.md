# Relates to transferring any unspent funds from the regents health care professional opportunity scholarship program to the regents physician loan forgiveness program

**Bill ID:** A4695
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4695 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4695)

## Summary

Transfers any unspent funds from the regents health care professional opportunity scholarship program to the regents physician loan forgiveness program; provides direction to distribute these funds to meet the same purposes as originally intended.
