# Commending Valedictorian Jennarose Maglione of Carle Place High School, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2517 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2517)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2517
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Valedictorian  Jennarose  Maglione  of
  Carle Place  High  School,  in  recognition  of  her
  outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions, to honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to  commend
Valedictorian  Jennarose  Maglione  of  Carle  Place  High  School,  in
recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Valedictorian Jennarose Maglione  represents  the  best  of
developed  potential  inherent in our most precious resource, our youth;
her achievements have brought enduring honor to her family and community
and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Valedictorian Jennarose Maglione takes  leave  of  Carle
Place  High School, she bestows a legacy of academic achievement, school
involvement and community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS, Valedictorian Jennarose Maglione may now stand  with  pride
as  she  assesses  her achievements, experiences the satisfaction of her
labors and the joy  of  her  accomplishments,  eager  to  face  the  new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Valedictorian Jennarose Maglione of Carle Place High School,  in
recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Valedictorian Jennarose Maglione.
