# Authorizes the establishment of permanent Covid-19 memorials and establishes a Covid-19 permanent memorial fund

**Bill ID:** S8483
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8483 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8483)

## Summary

Authorizes the establishment of permanent COVID-19 memorials; establishes a COVID-19 permanent memorial fund.
