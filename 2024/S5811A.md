# Extends the expiration of the provisions authorizing the county of Allegany to impose an additional one and one-half percent sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S5811AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S5811AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5811AA)

## Summary

Extends the expiration of the provisions authorizing the county of Allegany to impose an additional one and one-half percent sales and compensating use taxes until November 30, 2025.
