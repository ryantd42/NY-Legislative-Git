# Relates to authorizing the town of Dekalb, in the county of St. Lawrence, to reduce the maximum speed limit on certain public roadways

**Bill ID:** A9514
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9514 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9514)

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Dekalb, in the county of St. Lawrence, to reduce the maximum speed limit on a portion of United States route eleven.
