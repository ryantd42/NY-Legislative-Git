# Establishes a stakeholder group in the Oswego river basin

**Bill ID:** S7649AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [S7649AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7649AA)

## Summary

Establishes a stakeholder group in the Oswego river basin to coordinate water release.
