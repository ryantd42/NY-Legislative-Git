# Relates to permitting farmstand cannabis sales

**Bill ID:** A8893
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8893 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8893)

## Summary

Permits any individual or organization with an adult-use cultivator license to be entitled to sell minimally processed cannabis to adults over the age of twenty-one from a single building or farmstand on the farm where such cannabis is cultivated.
