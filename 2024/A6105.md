# Excludes from federal adjusted gross income the amount of any service award, benefit or allowance paid to a duly enrolled member of an auxiliary police force

**Bill ID:** A6105
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian F. Curran
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6105 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6105)

## Summary

Excludes from federal adjusted gross income the amount of any service award, benefit or allowance paid to a duly enrolled volunteer member of an auxiliary police force.
