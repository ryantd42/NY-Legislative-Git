**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2339 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2339)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2339
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Bright Hope Lodge No. 62 upon the
  occasion  of  hosting  its  20th  annual  Parade  &
  Festival on June 8, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the  Bright  Hope Lodge No. 62 upon the occasion of hosting its
20th annual Parade & Festival on Saturday, June 8, 2024 at the  crossing
on Edgewood St. & Mamaroneck Ave in White Plains, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  dedicated  Masons of the Bright Hope Lodge No. 62 are
men with a social conscience and a charitable perspective, who are  also
known  as  Freemasons;  they  are  part of a fraternal organization that
shares common moral ideals and esoteric values which are  based  on  the
doctrine of Brotherly Love, Relief, and Truth; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  most  benevolent  gathering  of Masonic Brothers and
Sisters of the Eastern Star marks twenty years of the unity  inside  the
organization, highlighting excellent charitable works that are completed
through this fundraising event; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Bright  Hope  Lodge  No.  62  have continued to show their
efforts in the White Plains community and supporting their fellow Lodges
with their most honorable deeds and efforts to positively  impact  their
community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Continuing  to show their desire to improve the place they
live, Bright Hope Lodge No. 62 aims to uplift members of the  community,
while  understanding  their  focus  in strengthening the Black community
with their acts of kindness and the role they play in showing youth high
standards and morals; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Bright Hope Lodge No. 62  is  in  direct
correlation  to  the efforts of the Worshipful Master Lee Smith, through
the Most Worshipful Gregory R. Smith Jr. Grand Master and  its  members,
whose  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an
impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising
standard  of  excellence  in all endeavors on behalf of the organization
and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and commend
the members of the Bright Hope Lodge No. 62, Free and  Accepted  Masons,
past  and  present,  who  have  labored so conscientiously to secure and
improve the quality of life for the community it serves; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the  Bright  Hope Lodge No. 62 upon the occasion of hosting its
20th annual Parade & Festival on June 8, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Worshipful  Master  Lee  Smith  and  Most  Worshipful
Gregory  R. Smith Jr. Grand Master of Bright Hope Lodge No. 62, Free and
Accepted Masons of the State of New York.
