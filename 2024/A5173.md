# Relates to transfer of sick time

**Bill ID:** A5173
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5173 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5173)

## Summary

Allows certain employees to transfer or receive sick time from a fellow employee.
