# Establishes a co-occurring disorders patient bill of rights; appropriation

**Bill ID:** A10261
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10261 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10261)

## Summary

Establishes a co-occurring disorders patient bill of rights to be implemented by various agencies; requires a five-year plan for implementing such bill of rights.
