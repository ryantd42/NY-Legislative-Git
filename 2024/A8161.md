# Establishes the ammunition purchase certification act

**Bill ID:** A8161
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8161 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8161)

## Summary

Establishes the ammunition purchase certification act which requires purchasers of ammunition to have an ammunition purchase permit number.
