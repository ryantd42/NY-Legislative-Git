# Commending Class of 2024 Salutatorian, Brendan Kaminski, of Baldwin High School, in recognition of his outstanding accomplishments

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas

**PDF:** [J2519 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2519)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2519
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2024  Salutatorian, Brendan
  Kaminski, of Baldwin High School, in recognition  of
  his outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions to  honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend  Class  of
2024  Salutatorian,  Brendan  Kaminski,  of  Baldwin  High  School,  in
recognition of his outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian, Brendan  Kaminski,  represents  the  best  of
developed  potential  inherent in our most precious resource, our youth;
his achievements have brought enduring honor to his family and community
and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Salutatorian, Brendan Kaminski, takes leave  of  Baldwin
High  School,  he  bestows  a  legacy  of  academic  achievement, school
involvement, and community service to which others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian, Brendan Kaminski, may now stand with pride as
he assesses his achievements and experiences  the  satisfaction  of  his
labors  and  the  joy  of  his  accomplishments,  eager  to face the new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Class  of  2024 Salutatorian, Brendan Kaminski, of Baldwin High
School, in recognition of his outstanding  accomplishments;  and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Salutatorian, Brendan Kaminski.
