# Relates to the filing of financial disclosure statements

**Bill ID:** S6184AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S6184AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6184AA)

## Summary

Relates to the filing of financial disclosure statements; relates to the definition of "filing rate".
