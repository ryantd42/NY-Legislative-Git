# Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage American lobster

**Bill ID:** A10347
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10347 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10347)

## Summary

Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage American lobster to December 31, 2027.
