# Commending Kevin J. Conway upon the occasion of his designation as Grand Marshal of the 2023 New York City St. Patrick's Day Parade

**Bill ID:** K179
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K179 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K179)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 179
 
BY: M. of A. Fahy
 
  COMMENDING  Kevin  J.  Conway upon the occasion of
  his designation as Grand Marshal  of  the  2023  New
  York City St. Patrick's Day Parade
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
individuals  of  true  purpose  and  worthy  accomplishment  who  work
diligently to preserve and celebrate their rich cultural heritage; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Kevin J. Conway upon the occasion of his  designation  as  Grand
Marshal of the 2023 New York City St. Patrick's Day Parade; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  acknowledgment  of  this most prestigious appointment,
Kevin J. Conway will have the honor of leading the 262nd consecutive New
York City St. Patrick's Day Parade up Fifth Avenue on Friday, March  17,
2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  1994,  Kevin J. Conway began his illustrious financial
career, spanning almost three decades, with Clayton,  Dubilier  &  Rice;
rising  through the ranks, he served as Managing Partner from 2004-2016,
and for over 20 years,  he  has  held  the  title  of  Chairman  of  the
Investment Committee; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Under the luminous leadership of Kevin J. Conway, Clayton,
Dubilier & Rice acquired over 75 companies with an aggregate transaction
value of more than  $200  billion,  and  the  firm's  current  portfolio
employs more than 600,000 and generates annual revenues of approximately
$100 billion; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Kevin  J. Conway has truly been instrumental in helping to
build Clayton, Dubilier & Rice into  a  leading  global  private  equity
firm,  been active in many of the firm's organizational initiatives, and
works closely with the chief executive officer on  all  aspects  of  the
firm's operations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of the great State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Kevin  J.  Conway upon the occasion of his designation as Grand
Marshal of the 2023 New York City St. Patrick's Day Parade;  and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Kevin J. Conway.
