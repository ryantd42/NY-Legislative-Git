# Relates to operation of a vessel while under the influence of alcohol or drugs and repeals certain provisions of the navigation law relating thereto

**Bill ID:** A29
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A29 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A29)

## Summary

Provides for the repeal of existing boating while intoxicated laws and replaces them with laws related to operation of a boat while under the influence of alcohol or drugs which closer resemble the existing DWI laws.
