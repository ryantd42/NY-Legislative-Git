# Prohibits the sale or delivery of candy products containing THC in the state

**Bill ID:** A4934
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4934 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4934)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale or delivery, or agreement of sale or delivery, or giving away of candy products containing THC in the state.
