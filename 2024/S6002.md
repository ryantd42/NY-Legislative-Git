# Relates to segregated confinement

**Bill ID:** S6002
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6002 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6002)

## Summary

Relates to segregated confinement; prohibits segregated confinement for certain persons.
