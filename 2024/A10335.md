# Extends provisions of law relating to requiring certain agencies to submit regulatory agendas for publication in the state register

**Bill ID:** A10335
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10335 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10335)

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to requiring certain agencies to submit regulatory agendas for publication in the state register from December 31, 2024 until December 31, 2028.
