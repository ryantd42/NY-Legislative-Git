# Increases certain disability payments and specifying payments for volunteer firefighters and ambulance workers injured in the line of duty on or after July first, two thousand twenty-four

**Bill ID:** A4449
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4449 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4449)

## Summary

Increases temporary and permanent disability payments for volunteer firefighters and ambulance workers, and specifies weekly payment amounts for volunteer firefighters and ambulance workers injured in the line of duty on or after July first, two thousand twenty-four.
