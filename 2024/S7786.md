# Increases personal needs allowance amounts

**Bill ID:** S7786
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7786 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7786)

## Summary

Increases personal needs allowance amounts for individuals who are deemed eligible.
