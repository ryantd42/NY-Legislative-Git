# Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to conduct a feasibility study on the use of small modular reactors

**Bill ID:** A10702
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Durso
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10702 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10702)

## Summary

Directs the New York state energy research and development authority to conduct a feasibility study and prepare a report on the benefits and implementation of nuclear small modular reactors.
