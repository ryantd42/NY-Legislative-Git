# Establishes that making a terroristic threat is a qualifying offense for bail

**Bill ID:** S9603
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Steve Rhoads
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9603 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9603)

## Summary

Establishes that making a terroristic threat is a qualifying offense for bail.
