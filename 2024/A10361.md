# Requires consultation with tribal nations as part of the state budget process

**Bill ID:** A10361
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pamela J. Hunter
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10361 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10361)

## Summary

Directs the division of the budget to consult tribal nations, relevant state agencies, and other stakeholders regarding budget proposals that would impact such tribal nations.
