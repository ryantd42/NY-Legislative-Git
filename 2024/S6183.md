# Requires the Catholic Archdiocese follow certain procedures during the consolidation or merger of churches

**Bill ID:** S6183
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6183 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6183)

## Summary

Requires the Catholic Archdiocese follow certain procedures during the consolidation or merger of churches including requiring a vote by the parish councils and trustees of the consolidating or merging churches.
