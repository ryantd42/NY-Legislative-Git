# Relates to aggravated unlicensed operation of a motor vehicle which results in a death

**Bill ID:** S9920
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9920 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9920)

## Summary

Provides that aggravated unlicensed operation of a motor vehicle, by a person who has a history of reckless driving, which results in a death shall be a class E felony.
