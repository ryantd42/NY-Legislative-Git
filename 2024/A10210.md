# Extends certain provisions relating to establishing initial rents in respect of certain loans to owners of existing multiple dwellings

**Bill ID:** A10210
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** George Alvarez
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10210 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10210)

## Summary

Extends certain provisions relating to establishing initial rents in respect of certain loans to owners of existing multiple dwellings to June 30, 2027.
