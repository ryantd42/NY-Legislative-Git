# Includes fires caused by the manufacture of a controlled substance within the crime of arson in the first degree

**Bill ID:** A5418
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5418 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5418)

## Summary

Includes fires caused by the manufacture of a controlled substance within the crime of arson in the first degree.
