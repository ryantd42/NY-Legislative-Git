# Exempts certain security guards from annual in-service training courses

**Bill ID:** A8309
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nader Sayegh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8309 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8309)

## Summary

Exempts registered security guards with a bachelor's degree or higher from an accredited college or university in criminal justice or an equivalent from annual in service-training courses.
