# Adds Fishkill Creek to the definition of inland waterways

**Bill ID:** A10079
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10079 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10079)

## Summary

Adds Fishkill Creek in Dutchess County to the definition of inland waterways.
