# Relates to the election and seating of Landon C. Dais as a Member of the Assembly from the 77th Assembly District

**Bill ID:** E869
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carl Heastie
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E869 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/E869)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 869
 
BY: M. of A. Heastie
 
  in  relation  to  the  election and seating of
  Landon C. Dais as a Member of the Assembly from  the
  77th Assembly District
 
  WHEREAS,  There  is  a  vacancy  existing  in the membership of this
House, which was filled at a special election held on February 13, 2024;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The final official  canvass  of  the  votes  cast  at  said
election and certification thereof of the person elected thereat, cannot
be made at this time; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Board of Elections of the City of New York has advised
that the votes cast for the Office of Member of  Assembly  in  the  77th
Assembly  District  in  the  County  of  Bronx shown by its canvass were
respectively as  follows:  Landon  C.  Dais,  Democratic,  one  thousand
twenty-six; Norman Sobe McGill, Republican and Conservative, two hundred
eighty-eight; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  appears  that  the  results  of the votes cast in said
Assembly District cannot be affected by the final  official  canvass  of
such voters; and
 
  WHEREAS, Landon C. Dais has duly taken an oath of office as a Member
of Assembly; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  Landon  C.  Dais,  Democratic,  is duly elected and
qualified as a Member of the Assembly for his  respective  district  and
that he be seated in this House as such Member.
