# Authorizes people who transport severely disabled persons as an eligible person to receive disabled person plates

**Bill ID:** A10114
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stefani Zinerman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10114 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10114)

## Summary

Authorizes people who transport a severely disabled person as an eligible person to receive disabled person plates; defines who qualifies as eligible persons.
