# Requires all commercial buildings be retrofitted with motion detectors for controlling lighting

**Bill ID:** A5593
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5593 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5593)

## Summary

Requires all commercial buildings be retrofitted with motion detectors for controlling lighting.
