# Relates to authorizing certain free permits, stamps and privileges for qualified hunting instructors

**Bill ID:** A9090
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** David DiPietro
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9090 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9090)

## Summary

Authorizes a free bowhunting privilege, muzzle-loading privilege, turkey permit, duck stamp, and doe permit for qualified hunting instructors.
