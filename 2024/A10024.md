# Increases the tax exemption amount on clothing and apparel items from one hundred ten dollars to two hundred dollars

**Bill ID:** A10024
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10024 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10024)

## Summary

Increases the tax exemption amount on clothing and apparel items from one hundred ten dollars to two hundred dollars.
