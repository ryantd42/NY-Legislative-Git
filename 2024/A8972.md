# Relates to increasing the sales and compensating use tax in Dutchess county

**Bill ID:** A8972
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A8972 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8972)

## Summary

Increases the sales and compensating use tax in Dutchess county from 3.75% to 4%.
