# Establishes the commission for the modernization and revitalization of downstate medical center

**Bill ID:** S8732
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8732 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8732)

## Summary

Establishes the commission for the modernization and revitalization of downstate medical center; implements the recommendations of such commission.
