# Establishes a hyperbaric oxygen therapy pilot program

**Bill ID:** S9303
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Samra Brouk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S9303 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9303)

## Summary

Establishes a hyperbaric oxygen therapy pilot program to treat veterans suffering from traumatic brain injuries and post-traumatic stress disorder.

  §  2748.  DEFINITIONS.  AS  USED  IN THIS ARTICLE, THE FOLLOWING TERMS
 SHALL HAVE THE FOLLOWING MEANINGS:
  1. "AUTHORIZED MEDICAL PROFESSIONAL" SHALL MEAN A LICENSED  PHYSICIAN,
 PHYSICIAN'S  ASSISTANT, NURSE PRACTITIONER OR OTHER LICENSED HEALTH CARE
 PRACTITIONER LICENSED TO PRACTICE IN THIS STATE AND ACTING  WITHIN  SUCH
 PROFESSIONAL'S SCOPE OF PRACTICE.
  2. "HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERAPY" SHALL MEAN TREATMENT FROM AN AUTHORIZED
 MEDICAL  PROFESSIONAL  IN  EITHER  A  HYPERBARIC CHAMBER APPROVED BY THE
 UNITED STATES FOOD AND DRUG ADMINISTRATION, OR  SIMILAR  DEVICE  WITH  A
 UNITED  STATES  FOOD  AND  DRUG  ADMINISTRATION APPROVED INVESTIGATIONAL
 DEVICE EXEMPTION.
  3. "POST-TRAUMATIC STRESS DISORDER"  SHALL  MEAN  A  MENTAL  CONDITION
 DEVELOPED  AFTER  HAVING  EXPERIENCED  OR WITNESSED A TRAUMATIC OR LIFE-
 THREATENING EVENT AS SUCH TERMS ARE DEFINED BY THE MOST  RECENT  EDITION
 OF  THE DIAGNOSTIC AND STATISTICAL MANUAL OF MENTAL DISORDERS, PUBLISHED
 BY THE AMERICAN PSYCHIATRIC ASSOCIATION.
  4. "TRAUMATIC BRAIN INJURY" SHALL HAVE THE SAME MEANING AS DEFINED  IN
 SUBDIVISION  ONE OF SECTION TWENTY-SEVEN HUNDRED FORTY-ONE OF THIS CHAP-
 TER.
  5. "VETERAN" SHALL HAVE THE SAME MEANING  AS  DEFINED  IN  SUBDIVISION
 THREE OF SECTION ONE OF THE VETERANS' SERVICES LAW.

  § 2748-A. HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERAPY PILOT PROGRAM. 1. THE COMMISSIONER
 SHALL ESTABLISH A FIVE-YEAR PILOT PROGRAM TO PROVIDE TREATMENT TO VETER-
 ANS  SUFFERING  FROM  TRAUMATIC  BRAIN INJURIES OR POST-TRAUMATIC STRESS
 DISORDER BY MEANS OF HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERAPY.
  2.  THE  COMMISSIONER  SHALL  REQUIRE  PROVIDERS WHO ARE A PART OF THE
 HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERAPY PILOT PROGRAM SUBMIT AN ANNUAL REPORT  TO  THE
 COMMISSIONER.  SUCH  REPORT  SHALL  INCLUDE  DATA ANALYZING THE COST AND
 EFFICIENCY OF THE HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERAPY PROGRAM AND ANY  RECOMMENDA-
 TIONS BY THE PROVIDER TO IMPROVE OR EXPAND SUCH HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERA-
 PY PILOT PROGRAM.
  3.  ON  OR  BEFORE  DECEMBER  THIRTY-FIRST,  TWO  THOUSAND THIRTY, THE
 COMMISSIONER SHALL SUBMIT A REPORT TO THE GOVERNOR, THE TEMPORARY PRESI-
 DENT OF THE SENATE, THE SPEAKER OF THE ASSEMBLY, THE CHAIR OF THE SENATE
 COMMITTEE ON HEALTH, AND THE CHAIR OF THE ASSEMBLY HEALTH  COMMITTEE  ON
 DATA  RECEIVED  PURSUANT TO SUBDIVISION TWO OF THIS SECTION. SUCH REPORT
 SHALL INCLUDE AN ANALYSIS OF THE COST AND EFFICIENCY OF  THE  HYPERBARIC
 OXYGEN THERAPY PILOT PROGRAM AND THE NUMBER OF VETERANS RECEIVING TREAT-
 MENT THROUGH SUCH PILOT PROGRAM.
  §  2748-B.  HYPERBARIC  OXYGEN  THERAPY  AUTHORIZED. 1. ANY AUTHORIZED
 MEDICAL PROFESSIONAL MAY PRESCRIBE HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERAPY TO A VETER-
 AN DIAGNOSED WITH A TRAUMATIC  BRAIN  INJURY  OR  POST-TRAUMATIC  STRESS
 DISORDER.  AN  AUTHORIZED MEDICAL PROFESSIONAL WHO PRESCRIBES HYPERBARIC
 OXYGEN THERAPY SHALL DO SO IN A MANNER THAT COMPLIES WITH ACCEPTED STAN-
 DARDS OF CARE FOR HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERAPY.
  2. ANY VETERAN WHO IS A RESIDENT OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK AND HAS BEEN
 DIAGNOSED WITH A TRAUMATIC BRAIN INJURY OR POST-TRAUMATIC STRESS  DISOR-
 DER  MAY  RECEIVE  HYPERBARIC OXYGEN THERAPY SO LONG AS SUCH THERAPY HAS
 BEEN PRESCRIBED BY AN AUTHORIZED MEDICAL PROFESSIONAL.
  § 2. The commissioner of health shall promulgate any  rules  or  regu-
 lations necessary for the implementation of this article.
  § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
