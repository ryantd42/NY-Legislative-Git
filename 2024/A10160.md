# Relates to electric generating facility annual reports; and to repeal section 575-b of the real property tax law relating to solar or wind energy) systems

**Bill ID:** A10160
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Tague
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10160 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10160)

## Summary

Eliminates the necessity for solar and wind energy systems to supply certain information in electric generating facility annual reports; repeals provisions relating to solar or wind energy systems.
