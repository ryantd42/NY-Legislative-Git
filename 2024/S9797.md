# Relates to the use of property in a land bank for community gardens

**Bill ID:** S9797
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9797 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9797)

## Summary

Permits a land bank prioritize the use of property for a community garden.

  (e) A foreclosing governmental unit may, in its local law,  resolution

  (4) use as wildlife conservation areas; and
