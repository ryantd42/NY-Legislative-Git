# Requires insurers to send notices of renewal of certain policies to the insured no later than thirty days prior to the expiration of the policy

**Bill ID:** A4665
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4665 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4665)

## Summary

Requires insurers to send a notice of renewal or extension of certain policies to the insured no later than thirty days prior to the expiration of the policy.

  Section 1. The insurance law is amended by adding a new  section  3462

  §  3462.  NOTICE  OF RENEWAL REQUIRED. 1. NOTWITHSTANDING ANY CONTRARY
