# Establishes an animal abuser statewide registry

**Bill ID:** A5809
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5809 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5809)

## Summary

Establishes an animal abuser statewide registry with community notification requirements.
