# Excludes certain animals from the definition of "wildlife"

**Bill ID:** A5778
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5778 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5778)

## Summary

Excludes dogs, cats and any other domesticated animals from the definition of "wildlife".
