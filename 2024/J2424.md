# Honoring Sullivan County Treasurer Nancy Buck upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the Finance Officer of the Year Award by the New York State County Treasurers and Finance Officers Association

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [J2424 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2424)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2424
 
BY: Senator OBERACKER
 
  HONORING Sullivan County Treasurer Nancy Buck upon
  the  occasion of her designation as recipient of the
  Finance Officer of the Year Award by  the  New  York
  State  County  Treasurers  and  Finance  Officers
  Association
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
honor  those  distinguished  citizens who would devote their life's work
and purpose to community service, contributing to  the  welfare  of  the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State;
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Sullivan  County  Treasurer  Nancy  Buck  upon  the  occasion  of  her
designation as recipient of the Finance Officer of the Year Award by the
New York State County Treasurers and Finance Officers Association; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Within  every  community,  there  are  inevitably  some
individuals who, by virtue of  their  energy,  vision,  and  dedication,
command  the  greatest  respect  among their friends and colleagues, and
within the community at large; Nancy Buck is one such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Nancy  Buck  is  receiving  this  prestigious  award  in
recognition  of  her  20-year  commitment  to  the New York State County
Treasurers and Finance Officers Association; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Nancy  Buck  has  served  as  President,  Vice  President,
Secretary  and  Treasurer  of  the  New York State County Treasurers and
Finance Officers Association over her two decades  of  involvement  with
this vital organization; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  president, Nancy Buck was instrumental in bringing the
Association's Summer Conference to Sullivan  County,  attracting  nearly
100 treasurers and finance officers from across the State of New York to
the four-day gathering; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefit of others  which  Nancy  Buck  has  displayed
throughout her life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  has always been the objective of this Legislative Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment  to the betterment of their communities, and it is the intent
of this Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this  tribute  to
Nancy  Buck,  that  future  generations  may  know  and  appreciate  her
admirable character, her many benevolent  deeds,  and  the  respect  and
esteem in which she is held by her peers; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Sullivan County Treasurer Nancy Buck  upon  the  occasion  of  her
designation as recipient of the Finance Officer of the Year Award by the
New York State County Treasurers and Finance Officers Association; and
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Nancy Buck.
