# Relates to price gouging of medicine

**Bill ID:** A5653B
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Karines Reyes
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5653B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5653B)

## Summary

Prohibits selling a drug subject to a shortage for an unconscionably excessive price.
