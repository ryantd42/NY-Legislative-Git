# Establishes a painters' apprenticeship program for public housing residents

**Bill ID:** A9272A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9272A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9272A)

## Summary

Establishes a New York state public housing painters apprenticeship program for municipal housing authority residents.
