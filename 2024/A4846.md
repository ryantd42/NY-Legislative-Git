# Allows schools to provide water safety instruction to students in grades kindergarten through twelve

**Bill ID:** A4846
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4846 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4846)

## Summary

Allows schools to provide water safety instruction to students in grades kindergarten through twelve; allows for the commissioner of education to provide technical assistance to develop curricula for water safety instruction.
