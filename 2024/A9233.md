 CODE,  RULE,  OR  REGULATION  RELATING  TO  REAL PROPERTY, BUILDINGS, OR
 MULTIPLE DWELLINGS;
  B-6. OCCUPANCY OR USE OF DWELLING UNITS IN A CLASS A MULTIPLE DWELLING
 THAT WOULD VIOLATE SUBDIVISION EIGHT OF SECTION  FOUR  OF  THE  MULTIPLE
 DWELLING LAW;
