# Relates to the sale of motor vehicle tires

**Bill ID:** S8371
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8371 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8371)

## Summary

Requires that the manufacture date of motor vehicle tires be included on the receipt for the sale.
