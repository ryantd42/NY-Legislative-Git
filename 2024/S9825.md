# Extends provisions relating to the establishment of the mercury thermostat collection act

**Bill ID:** S9825
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S9825 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9825)

## Summary

Extends provisions relating to the establishment of the mercury thermostat collection act to January 1, 2026.
