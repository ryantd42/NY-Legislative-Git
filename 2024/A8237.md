# Requires that at least one employee at each day care center be trained in administering an opioid antagonist

**Bill ID:** A8237
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John Zaccaro Jr.
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8237 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8237)

## Summary

Requires that at least one employee at each family day care center, group family day care center, school-aged child care program, and child day care center be trained in administering an opioid antagonist.
