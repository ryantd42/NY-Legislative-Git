# Authorizes the sale of food or beverages at retail for consumption at certain premises

**Bill ID:** A9584
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A9584 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9584)

## Summary

Authorizes the sale of food or beverages at retail for consumption at certain premises located in the village of North Syracuse, county of Onondaga.
