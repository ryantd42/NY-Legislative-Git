# Relates to broadband mapping and expansion of such service

**Bill ID:** S4987
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4987 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4987)

## Summary

Requires the Empire State Development corporation's broadband program office to assist in the provision and expansion of broadband internet access service and to address inequities in the availability of such service.

  §  450.  BROADBAND  MAPPING PROGRAM.  1. DEFINITIONS. FOR PURPOSES OF

 MAINTAINED PURSUANT TO THIS ARTICLE.
  2. THE EMPIRE STATE DEVELOPMENT  CORPORATION  SHALL,  IN  COORDINATION

 INDICATING BROADBAND COVERAGE, INCLUDING MAXIMUM BROADBAND SPEEDS AVAIL-
 ABLE IN SERVICE TERRITORIES IN NEW YORK. THE  EMPIRE  STATE  DEVELOPMENT
 CORPORATION  AND THE CHIEF ADVISOR SHALL PROVIDE THE INITIAL MAP BY JULY
 FIRST, TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FOUR, OR AS SOON AS  PRACTICABLE,  AND  SHALL
 UPDATE THE MAP AT LEAST ANNUALLY.
