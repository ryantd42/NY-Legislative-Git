# Designates the Honor and Remember Flag as the official state flag for recognizing armed services members

**Bill ID:** S8929
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8929 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8929)

## Summary

Designates the "Honor and Remember Flag" as the official state flag for recognizing armed service members who have died as a result of serving the United States in any war or conflict the nation has been involved in since its inception.
