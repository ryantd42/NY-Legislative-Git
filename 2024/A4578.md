# Requires schools to develop a seizure action plan for students diagnosed with a seizure disorder

**Bill ID:** A4578
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John W. McGowan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4578 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4578)

## Summary

Requires schools to develop a seizure action plan for students diagnosed with a seizure disorder and have teachers trained for if a student has a seizure.
