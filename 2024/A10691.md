# Relates to certain criminal penalties for driving while impaired by alcohol or drugs

**Bill ID:** A10691
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10691 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10691)

## Summary

Relates to certain criminal penalties involving required alcohol and drug rehabilitation for driving while impaired by alcohol or drugs.
