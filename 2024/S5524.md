# Exempts from sales and use tax zero-emission school buses

**Bill ID:** S5524
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5524 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5524)

## Summary

Exempts from sales and use tax zero-emission school buses and any and all parts and pieces of equipment used in their operation.
