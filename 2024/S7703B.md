# Requires limited use motorcycles to be registered at point of sale

**Bill ID:** S7703BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S7703BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7703BB)

Requires limited use motorcycles to be registered at point of sale.
