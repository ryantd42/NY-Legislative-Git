# Establishes an Oneida county school milk pilot program

**Bill ID:** S7828AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S7828AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S7828AA)

## Summary

Establishes an Oneida county school milk pilot program allowing public schools in Oneida county to be reimbursed by the state for certain milk purchases.
