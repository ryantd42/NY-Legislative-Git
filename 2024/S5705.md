# Exempts new clean fuel vehicles and vehicles that meet the clean vehicle standards from first year of registration fees

**Bill ID:** S5705
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S5705 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5705)

## Summary

Exempts new clean fuel vehicles and vehicles that meet the clean vehicle standards from the first year of registration fees.
