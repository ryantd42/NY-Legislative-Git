**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2230 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2230)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2230
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Most  Excellent Prince Hall Grand
  Chapter of Holy Royal Arch Masons of  the  State  of
  New  York  and  Jurisdiction  upon  the  occasion of
  hosting  its  152nd  Annual  Grand  Convocation  on
  October 4, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy Royal Arch
Masons of the State of New York &  Jurisdiction  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its  152nd Annual Grand Convocation on Friday, October 4, 2024,
at the Crowne Plaza, Albany, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy Royal Arch
Masons (MEPHGCHRAM) of the State of New York  and  Jurisdiction  is  the
philanthropy arm of The Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy
Royal Arch Masons; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  more  than 500 volunteer members, the Most Excellent
Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy Royal Arch Masons of the State of  New
York serves the homeless, hungry, and the countless local families faced
with danger while living on the street; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Furthermore,  this  vital  organization  assists  disabled
veterans, provides scholarships, and provides for  the  local  community
based on its needs; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Remaining  true  to its mission, the Most Excellent Prince
Hall Grand Chapter of Holy Royal Arch Masons of the State  of  New  York
disburses charity and creates viable community development programs as a
pro-active  means  of combating many of the socio-economic issues facing
the communities it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy  Royal
Arch  Masons  of  the  State  of  New  York  also  seeks  collaborative
partnerships with the various social, civic and faith-based programs  in
the  community, fostering lasting relationships that will translate into
positive and  significant  changes  in  the  condition  of  individuals,
families and our neighborhoods; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter
of  Holy  Royal  Arch  Masons  of  the  State  of  New York is in direct
correlation to the  efforts  of  its  dedicated  members  and  the  Most
Excellent  Rex  H.  Garret Grand High Priest, whose involvement is, and
always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled
enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising  standard  of  excellence  in  all
endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  auspicious occasion presents an opportunity for this
Legislative Body to recognize and pay  tribute  to  the  Most  Excellent
Prince  Hall Grand Chapter of Holy Royal Arch Masons of the State of New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and commend
the  members  of  the  Most  Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy
Royal Arch Masons of the State of New York and  Jurisdiction,  past  and
present,  who  have labored so conscientiously to secure and improve the
quality of life for the community it serves; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the Most Excellent Prince Hall Grand Chapter of Holy Royal Arch
Masons of the State of New York and Jurisdiction upon  the  occasion  of
hosting its 152nd Annual Grand Convocation on October 4, 2024; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Most Excellent Prince  Hall  Grand  Chapter  of  Holy
Royal Arch Masons of the State of New York and Jurisdiction.
