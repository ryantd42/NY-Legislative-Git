# Repeals a provision of the civil service law relating to resolutions of disputes in the course of collective negotiations with certain deputy sheriffs

**Bill ID:** A8012
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8012 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8012)

## Summary

Repeals provisions relating to resolutions of disputes in the course of collective negotiations with certain deputy sheriffs to provide such deputy sheriffs with the same interest arbitration scope as municipal police officers.
