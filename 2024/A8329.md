# Requires health insurance policies to include coverage for doula services as required coverage for maternity care

**Bill ID:** A8329
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8329 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8329)

## Summary

Requires health insurance policies to include coverage for doula services as required coverage for maternity care.
