**Session:** 2024

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2583
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  the International Cricket Council upon
  the occasion of hosting the India vs.  U.S.A.  Match
  during  the historic ICC Men's T20 World Cup on June
  12, 2024
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the International Cricket Council upon the occasion  of  hosting
the  India  vs. U.S.A. Match during the historic ICC Men's T20 World Cup
on Wednesday, June 12, 2024, at the Nassau County International  Cricket
Stadium; and
 
  WHEREAS, Overseeing the administration and regulation of the game of
cricket,  the  International  Cricket Council (ICC) serves as the global
governing body for cricket, representing 108 member countries;  the  ICC
is  also  responsible  for organizing all ICC events and is committed to
enhancing the game's competitiveness, entertainment value, global reach,
and adhering to their values of fairness; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dating back to the 17th century, cricket was introduced  to
North  America  through  the  English  colonies,  and  the  sport spread
globally throughout the centuries; in  the  21st  century,  cricket  has
grown  immensely  and  introduced  the  Twenty20  format  in 2003, which
revolutionized the game with its fast-paced, three-hour matches; and
 
  WHEREAS, Cementing cricket's place in the global sports  arena,  the
inaugural Men's Twenty20 International took place in 2005; the sport has
achieved a passionate fan base and the ICC strives to reach a remarkable
level  of  engagement as these intense matches approach the world stage;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  India  vs.  U.S.A.  match  highlights  the  growing
prominence  of  cricket in the United States and showcases the country's
commitment to achieve sustained competitive excellence  and  to  inspire
the cricket community at all levels; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  U.S.  men's  national cricket team, which has been an
associate member of the ICC since 1965,  is  eager  to  demonstrate  its
progress  on  the  world  stage;  the upcoming match is more than just a
game, it represents the culmination of cricket's long-standing  presence
in North America and its evolution into a modern, dynamic sport; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With the U.S. cricket team striving for excellence and the
Indian cricket team representing its  storied  legacy,  this  auspicious
match will be a memorable occasion for cricket fans; and
 
  WHEREAS, Also known as Team U.S.A., the U.S. cricket team represents
the nation in international cricket and their primary goal is to achieve
 
sustained  competitive  excellence;  the  team  competes  in  various
competitions globally and has made significant strides  in  establishing
its reputation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Nominated in 2024, the captain of the U.S. cricket team is
Monank Patel; comprised of a mix of  homegrown  talent  and  players  of
diverse  backgrounds,  key  players  on  the team are Monank Patel, fast
bowler Ali Khan, and  all-rounder  Steven  Taylor,  all  of  whom  bring
experience and skill to the squad; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  U.S.  cricket team has participated in events such as
the ICC World Cricket League and ICC T20 World Cup Qualifiers; and
 
  WHEREAS, Aiming to increase participation and interest in the  sport
of  cricket  across  the  country,  the U.S. cricket team has emphasized
grassroots development in recent  years;  they  have  facilitated  youth
programs,  coaching  initiatives,  and  the  establishment  of  regional
leagues to nurture talent at the local level; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the  International  Cricket  Council  is  in
direct correlation to the efforts of its leadership, and dedicated staff
and  volunteers,  whose  involvement  are,  and  always  have  been,
characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and
an  uncompromising  standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of
the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the  International Cricket Council upon the occasion of hosting
the India vs. U.S.A. Match during the historic ICC Men's T20  World  Cup
on June 12, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to International Cricket Council.
