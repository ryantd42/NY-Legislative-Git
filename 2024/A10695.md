# Authorizes certain persons to possess and administer epinephrine by use of an epinephrine auto-injector device upon completion of appropriate training; requires the department of health to establish a registry

**Bill ID:** A10695
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10695 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10695)

## Summary

Authorizes certain persons to possess and administer epinephrine by use of an epinephrine auto-injector device upon completion of appropriate training; requires the department of health to establish a registry of the names of lay persons trained in the use of an epinephrine auto-injector device.
