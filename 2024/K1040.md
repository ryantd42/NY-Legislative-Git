# Congratulating Elle Hutton of Columbia High School upon the occasion of capturing the 2024 NYSPHSAA Girls Wrestling Championship for the 185-pound weight class on January 26, 2024

**Bill ID:** K1040
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K1040 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/K1040)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 1040
 
BY: M. of A. McDonald
 
  CONGRATULATING Elle Hutton of Columbia High School
  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing the 2024 NYSPHSAA
  Girls  Wrestling  Championship  for  the  185-pound
  weight class on January 26, 2024
 
  WHEREAS,  Excellence  and  success  in  competitive  sports  can  be
achieved only through strenuous practice, team  play  and  team  spirit,
nurtured by dedicated coaching and strategic planning; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  enhances  the  moral  and  physical
development of the young people of this State, preparing  them  for  the
future  by  instilling  in  them  the  value  of teamwork, encouraging a
standard  of  healthy  living,  imparting  a  desire  for  success  and
developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Elle
Hutton upon the occasion of capturing the 2024 NYSPHSAA Girls  Wrestling
Championship for the 185-pound weight class, held on Friday, January 26,
2024,  at  SRC  Arena, Onondaga Community College in Syracuse, New York;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Exemplifying outstanding talent, Elle Hutton won  all  four
of  the matches in her weight class to become the first female wrestling
state champion of Columbia High School as a junior; and
 
  WHEREAS, In the title match  against  Carmel  High  School's  Alexia
Manguel, Elle Hutton displayed remarkable talent as she turned the match
around  in  the  second  period  to  register a pin at 3:55 to claim the
championship and defeat her opponent; and
 
  WHEREAS, As a testament to her incredible athleticism,  Elle  Hutton
was named Spectrum Athlete of the Week; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's  colors of blue and white,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported Elle Hutton throughout her journey as  she  ended  her  season
with an impressive 26-3 record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  athletic  talent displayed by this scholar athlete is
due in great part to the efforts of a great team of coaches, skilled and
inspirational tutors, respected for their ability to  develop  potential
into excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  wrestling,  which demands athletic
prowess, speed and agility, Girls Wrestling Coaches Mike Merhige and Tim
Satalino, have worked hard to hone the skills of this champion, teaching
this outstanding athlete lessons which will prove invaluable both on and
off the mat; and
 
  WHEREAS, In light of her recent  accomplishments,  Elle  Hutton  has
brought  honor  and just pride to her school and community on the local,
and State level; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Elle  Hutton  and Coaches Mike Merhige and Tim Satalino of
Columbia High School upon the occasion of capturing  the  2024  NYSPHSAA
Girls  Wrestling  Championship for the 185-pound weight class on January
26, 2024; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Elle Hutton and Coaches Mike Merhige and Tim Satalino  of
Columbia High School.
