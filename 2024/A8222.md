# Relates to notices of violation of the city of New York's solid waste recycling laws

**Bill ID:** A8222
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Sam Berger
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A8222 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A8222)

## Summary

Relates to notices of violation of the city of New York's solid waste recycling laws; requires a photograph of the alleged violation to be mailed with the ticket.
