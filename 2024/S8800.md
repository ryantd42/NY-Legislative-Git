# Extends the authorization of the city of White Plains to impose an occupancy tax

**Bill ID:** S8800
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8800 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8800)

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the city of White Plains to enact a local law imposing an occupancy tax until December 31, 2027.
