# Grants peace officer status to animal control officers of the county of Schuyler

**Bill ID:** A5334AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [A5334AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5334AA)

## Summary

Grants peace officer status to animal control officers of the county of Schuyler; defines "animal control officer".

 AN ACT to amend the criminal procedure  law,  in  relation  to  granting

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  Section  2.10 of the criminal procedure law is amended by
 adding a new subdivision 87 to read as follows:
  87. EMPLOYEES OF THE COUNTY OF  SCHUYLER  SERVING  AS  ANIMAL  CONTROL
 OFFICERS FOR SUCH COUNTY; PROVIDED, HOWEVER, THAT NOTHING IN THIS SUBDI-
 VISION  SHALL  BE  DEEMED  TO  AUTHORIZE SUCH ANIMAL CONTROL OFFICERS TO
 CARRY, POSSESS, REPAIR OR DISPOSE OF A FIREARM  UNLESS  THE  APPROPRIATE
 LICENSE THEREFOR HAS BEEN ISSUED PURSUANT TO SECTION 400.00 OF THE PENAL
 LAW.  FOR  THE  PURPOSES  OF  THIS SUBDIVISION, THE TERM "ANIMAL CONTROL
 OFFICER" SHALL HAVE THE SAME MEANING AS DEFINED  BY  SECTION  TWENTY-ONE
 HUNDRED FORTY OF THE PUBLIC HEALTH LAW.
