# Amends a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for certain appropriations for services and expenses of economic development and community services organizations

**Bill ID:** R2688
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R2688 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/R2688)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2688
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  amending  Senate  Resolution  R2864  of  2022
  establishing  a  plan setting forth an itemized list
  of grantees for  a  certain  appropriation  for  the
  2022-23  state fiscal year for services and expenses
  of  economic  development  and  community  services
  organizations. Notwithstanding any  other  provision
  of  law  to  the  contrary, the amounts appropriated
  herein may be sub-allocated or  transferred  between
  other  agencies,  including  but not limited to, the
  department of economic development, as required by a
  plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees with
  the  amount  to  be  received  by  each,  or  the
  methodology  for allocating such appropriation. Such
  plan  shall  be  subject  to  the  approval  of  the
  temporary  president  of the senate and the director
  of the budget and thereafter shall be included in  a
  resolution  calling  for  the  expenditure  of  such
  monies, which  resolution  must  be  approved  by  a
  majority  vote  of all members elected to the senate
  upon a roll call vote
 
  RESOLVED, That funds originally appropriated in 2022  pursuant  to
section  1  of  chapter  53  of  the  laws  of 2022 and reappropriated
pursuant to section 1 of chapter 53 of the laws of 2024  which  enacts
the  aid  to  localities,  local  assistance account, for services and
expenses of economic development and community services organizations.
Notwithstanding any other  provision  of  law  to  the  contrary,  the
amounts  appropriated  herein  may  be  sub-allocated  or  transferred
between other agencies, including but not limited to,  the  department
of economic development. Such funds shall be apportioned pursuant to a
plan  setting forth an itemized list of grantees with the amount to be
received  by  each,  or  the  methodology  for  allocating  such
appropriation.  Such  plan  shall  be  subject  to the approval of the
temporary president of the senate and the director of the  budget  and
thereafter  shall  be  included  in  a  resolution  calling  for  the
expenditure  of  such  monies,  which resolution must be approved by a
majority vote of all members elected to the senate upon  a  roll  call
vote,  in  accordance  with  the following schedule which shall amend,
remove from, or add to the  schedule  originally  approved  in  Senate
Resolution R2864 of 2022, as amended by R1411 of 2023
