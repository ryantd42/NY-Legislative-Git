# Honoring Pat Paton upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the Senior of the Year Award by Hamilton County

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2461 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2461)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2461
 
BY: Senator WALCZYK
 
  HONORING  Pat  Paton  upon  the  occasion  of  her
  designation as recipient of the Senior of  the  Year
  Award by Hamilton County
 
  WHEREAS,  Senior citizens bring a wealth of experience and knowledge
to the increasingly active roles they play  in  today's  society;  their
past  contributions  and  future  participation are a vital part of, and
valuable asset to, the fabric of community life and activity; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  honor  Pat  Paton
upon  the  occasion of her designation as recipient of the Senior of the
Year Award by Hamilton County; and
 
  WHEREAS, Members of older generations have helped  build  and  shape
our  Nation  to  be  the  greatest  in the world; they have defended our
freedoms and values to ensure that those who come after  them  may  have
better  lives; nowhere in the Nation is the legacy of their efforts more
visible and more a part of the lives of all citizens than  here  in  the
State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Pat Paton has continually demonstrated extreme competence,
extraordinary intelligence, and diligent leadership  in  her  unwavering
service  to  her community and, consequently, has been selected for this
most prestigious honor; and
 
  WHEREAS, Born and  raised  in  Long  Island,  New  York,  Pat  Paton
graduated  from  Mary  Louis  Academy  in  Jamaica  Estates,  New  York;
furthering  her  education,  she  earned  her  college  degree  at  St.
Bonaventure University in St. Bonaventure, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Ever  active in her community, Pat Paton has been involved
with the Wells Senior Club since  2015,  serving  as  secretary  of  the
Travel  Committee;  Meals  on  Wheels  for the Wells meal site for seven
years, delivering to those in need; the Friends of  the  Wells  Library,
assisting  at book sales; the Wells Central School District as a pen pal
for second-grade students for three years; the Sacandaga Task Force  for
Senior  Living  (STFSL)  since  2017, contributing to the newsletter and
other publicity efforts, planning trips and working  at  the  Pasta  and
Wine  Tasting  fundraiser,  the latter for five years; the Fulton County
Office for the Aging, helping with Thursday  lunches  for  eight  years;
and, most recently, the Northville Team for Alzheimer's Respite; and
 
  WHEREAS, Before retirement, Pat Paton worked as a billing supervisor
for Whelan's International; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  a  lifelong  love  of  reading,  Pat  Paton has been
attending the Northville Library Book Group for five years and  actively
writes  poetry;  winning  first  place  and  other awards, Pat Paton has
participated in the Hamilton County Senior Arbor Day Poetry Contest  for
five years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by
individuals for the benefit of others  which  Pat  Paton  has  displayed
throughout her life; and
 
  WHEREAS, Pat Paton has enriched the lives of her family, friends and
colleagues  through  her  love  and  respect for others, and through the
wisdom which comes from many years of living life to the  fullest;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Pat Paton upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the
Senior of the Year Award by Hamilton County; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Pat Paton.
