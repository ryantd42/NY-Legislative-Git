# Relates to the qualification to hold the position of assistant district attorney in the county of Rensselaer

**Bill ID:** S5962
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5962 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5962)

## Summary

Relates to removing the residency requirement to hold the position of assistant district attorney in the county of Rensselaer, provided that such assistant district attorney resides in an adjoining county.
