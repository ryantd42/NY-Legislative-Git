# Provides for the suspension of public assistance to owners of privately owned residential housing in New York city who are in violation of certain housing standards

**Bill ID:** A4709
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4709 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4709)

## Summary

Provides for the suspension of public assistance to owners of privately owned residential housing in NYC who are in violation of certain New York city department of housing preservation and development standards.

 class C violations which are the subject of suspension of assistance.
