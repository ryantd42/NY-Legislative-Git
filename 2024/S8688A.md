**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S8688A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8688A)

 guilty AND NOT WITHSTANDING AN OTHERWISE ENFORCEABLE WAIVER OF THE RIGHT
