# Allows New York states sire stakes eligibility for foals sired by New York state stallions; repealer

**Bill ID:** S8085
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S8085 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8085)

## Summary

Allows New York sire stakes eligibility for foals sired by New York state stallions, either in-state or out-of-state and born in-state.
