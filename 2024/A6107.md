# Extends the effectiveness of chapter 435 of the laws of 2014

**Bill ID:** A6107
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A6107 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6107)

## Summary

Extends the effectiveness of certain provisions relating to defining spearguns and allowing recreational spearfishing in New York's marine and coastal waters.
