# Establishes an online insurance verification system for proof of insurance; repealer

**Bill ID:** A5120
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5120 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5120)

## Summary

Establishes an online insurance verification system for proof of insurance; allows the use of third-party vendors and providers; requires motor vehicle insurers to provide policy information to the online verification system; establishes an online insurance verification advisory council; requires certain information to be confidential and proprietary information which is not subject to disclosure.
