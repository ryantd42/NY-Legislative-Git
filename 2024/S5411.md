# Relates to establishing the crime of domestic violence

**Bill ID:** S5411
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5411 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5411)

## Summary

Establishes the crime of domestic violence punishable as a class A misdemeanor.
