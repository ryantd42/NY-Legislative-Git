# Relates to information available pertaining to rental histories of rent-stabilized units through FOIL applications

**Bill ID:** A33
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A33 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A33)

## Summary

Relates to information available pertaining to rental histories of rent stabilized units through FOIL applications by the NYS division of housing and community renewal.
