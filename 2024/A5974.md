# Directs the superintendent of state police to develop and institute arrest policies and procedures when the arrested individual has responsibility for a person with an intellectual or developmental disability

**Bill ID:** A5974
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** MaryJane Shimsky
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5974 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5974)

## Summary

Directs the superintendent of state police to develop and institute arrest policies and procedures for instances where police are arresting an individual who has responsibility for a person with an intellectual or developmental disability.
