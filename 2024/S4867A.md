# Requires the county seat in Fulton county to have at least one polling place designated for early voting

**Bill ID:** S4867A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4867A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4867A)

## Summary

Requires the county seat in Fulton county to have at least one polling place designated for early voting.

 AN ACT to amend the election law, in relation to exempting the county of
  Fulton from the requirement that at least one  early  voting  site  be
  located in the largest city in such county

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Paragraph (b) of subdivision 2  of  section  8-600  of  the
 election  law, as amended by chapter 781 of the laws of 2021, is amended
 to read as follows:
  (b) The largest city in the county or, if there  is  no  city  in  the
 county,  the  municipality  with  the  highest population in each county
 based on the latest federal decennial census,  or  the  county  seat  in
 Washington  county, shall have at least one polling place designated for
 early voting, and to the extent practicable if such city or municipality
 has public transportation routes, such polling place shall  be  situated
 along such transportation routes.  PROVIDED, HOWEVER, THAT THE COUNTY OF
 FULTON  SHALL  BE REQUIRED TO HAVE AT LEAST ONE POLLING PLACE DESIGNATED
 FOR EARLY VOTING AS REQUIRED UNDER THIS PARAGRAPH, BUT SHALL  BE  EXEMPT
 FROM THE REQUIREMENT UNDER THIS PARAGRAPH THAT AT LEAST ONE SUCH POLLING
 PLACE  DESIGNATED  FOR  EARLY  VOTING  BE LOCATED IN THE LARGEST CITY OR
 OTHER MUNICIPALITY HAVING THE HIGHEST POPULATION IN SUCH COUNTY.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
