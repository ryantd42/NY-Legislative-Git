# Commending Class of 2024 Valedictorian Lillian Lin and Salutatorian Kira Nguyen of Great Neck South High School, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J2449 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J2449)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2449
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING Class of 2024 Valedictorian Lillian Lin
  and  Salutatorian  Kira  Nguyen  of Great Neck South
  High School, in  recognition  of  their  outstanding
  accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions, to honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend  Class  of
2024  Valedictorian  Lillian  Lin  and Salutatorian Kira Nguyen of Great
Neck  South  High  School,  in  recognition  of  their  outstanding
accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian  Lillian  Lin  and  Salutatorian Kira Nguyen
represent the best of developed potential inherent in our most  precious
resource,  our  youth; their achievements have brought enduring honor to
their families and communities and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, As Valedictorian Lillian Lin and Salutatorian  Kira  Nguyen
take  leave  of  Great  Neck  South High School, they bestow a legacy of
academic achievement, school involvement and community service to  which
others may aspire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian Lillian Lin and Salutatorian Kira Nguyen may
now stand with pride as they assess their achievements,  experience  the
satisfaction of their labors and the joy of their accomplishments, eager
to  face  the new experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Class  of  2024 Valedictorian Lillian Lin and Salutatorian Kira
Nguyen of  Great  Neck  South  High  School,  in  recognition  of  their
outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Valedictorian Lillian Lin and Salutatorian Kira Nguyen.
