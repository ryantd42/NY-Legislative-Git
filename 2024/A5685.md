# Enforces fire prevention and building codes and appointing an oversight officer and their duties

**Bill ID:** A5685
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A5685 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5685)

## Summary

Relates to enforcing fire prevention and building codes and allowing the secretary of state to appoint an oversight officer and describes the duties of such oversight officer.
