# Relates to the payment of arrears for certain taxes and placing limitations on housing development fund company regulatory agreements

**Bill ID:** S4965
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4965 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4965)

## Summary

Modifies certain eligibility requirements; extends the time period for the payment of arrears for certain taxes; places limitations on housing development fund company regulatory agreements.
