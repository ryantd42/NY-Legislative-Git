# Directs the public service commission to create an agrivoltaics incentive program

**Bill ID:** S8926
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S8926 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8926)

## Summary

Directs the public service commission, in consultation with the New York State energy research and development authority, to create an agrivoltaics incentive program to support agrivoltaic projects designed to maximize agricultural and environmental co-benefits.

 struggling farmers. Agrivoltaic projects  serve  as  an  alternative  to
