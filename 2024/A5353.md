# Modifies the date for kindergarten enrollment

**Bill ID:** A5353
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5353 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5353)

## Summary

Modifies the date for kindergarten enrollment from when a minor who becomes five or six years of age on or before the first of December to the first of September.
