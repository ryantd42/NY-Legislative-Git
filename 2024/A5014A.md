# Designates as peace officers certain Ogdensburg Bridge and Port Authority security personnel

**Bill ID:** A5014A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [A5014A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5014A)

## Summary

Designates as peace officers certain Ogdensburg Bridge and Port Authority security personnel specifically for such airport.
