# Enacts the "AIM higher act"

**Bill ID:** A9928
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A9928 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9928)

## Summary

Provides state aid to certain municipalities to offset tax exempt land, and certain housing and infrastructure costs for the state fiscal year beginning April 1 next succeeding the effective date, and each fiscal year thereafter; directs the director of the budget to create a formula for the amounts of such aid, and to select municipalities to receive such aid.
