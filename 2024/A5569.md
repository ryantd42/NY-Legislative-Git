# Prohibits point source pollution in the Long Island Sound and Great South Bay

**Bill ID:** A5569
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5569 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5569)

## Summary

Prohibits certain types of pollution in the Long Island Sound and Great South Bay.
