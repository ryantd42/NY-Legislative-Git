# Extends the time period for the conveyance of lands for the establishment of the Gateway National Recreation Area

**Bill ID:** A5695
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Michael Tannousis
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A5695 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5695)

## Summary

Extends the time period for the conveyance of lands for the establishment of the Gateway National Recreation Area until June 30, 2025.
