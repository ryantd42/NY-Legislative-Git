# Establishes procurement goals for New York state food products purchased by state agencies

**Bill ID:** A4406A
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Donna Lupardo
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4406A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4406A)

## Summary

Establishes procurement goals that certain percentages of all food products purchased by state agencies shall be New York state food products.
