# Relates to requirements for issuing civil service examination announcements

**Bill ID:** S8073
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Robert Jackson
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S8073 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S8073)

## Summary

Clarifies certain requirements for issuing civil service examination announcements.
