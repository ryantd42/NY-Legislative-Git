# Directs the MTA to rename the Eltingville transit center the Iraq War and Afghanistan War Veterans' Memorial Transit Center

**Bill ID:** A6035BA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Mike Reilly
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A6035BA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A6035BA)

## Summary

Directs the MTA to rename the Eltingville transit center the Iraq War and Afghanistan War Veterans' Memorial Transit Center; provides for the repeal of such provisions upon the expiration thereof.
