# Commending the Canastota Lions Club upon the occasion of celebrating its 75th Anniversary on November 10, 2023

**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J582 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/J582)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 582
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMENDING  the  Canastota  Lions  Club  upon  the
  occasion of  celebrating  its  75th  Anniversary  on
  November 10, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay tribute to those fraternal organizations of high purpose and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the  Canastota  Lions Club upon the occasion of celebrating its
75th Anniversary, to be celebrated on  April  22,  2023,  at  Theodore's
Restaurant in Canastota, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Canastota  Lions  Club  was chartered on November 10,
1947, with 31 original members; and
 
  WHEREAS, In great service to their community,  the  Canastota  Lions
Club  supports  community  service projects, community youth recognition
programs, historical projects, and various foundations to help those  in
need; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  members  of  the  Canastota Lions Club participate in
multiple projects throughout the year,  including  the  construction  of
eyeglass  collection boxes which are located throughout the community to
collect glasses for recycling and use in Central America;  additionally,
the  club  uses  a vision screening camera to screen one to six year old
children for amblyopia; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Canastota Lions Club also supports a program  for  free
eye  examinations  and  glasses  for those in need, as well as GLAS, the
public library, the Opportunity Shop, the Canastota  Loaves  and  Fishes
program,  and  those who have suffered disasters in the local community;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The Canastota Lions Club, helping to provide  opportunities
for  leadership  development,  community  outreach, and overall personal
development for their community's children, sponsor the Oneida-Canastota
Leo Club, where young people interested in the same goals and efforts as
the Lions participate in community service  and  the  Young  Leaders  in
Service Program; and
 
  WHEREAS,  To  recognize  youth in the community, the Canastota Lions
Club  hosts  a  Student  Academic  Achievement  Night  for  local  honor
students, provides multiple scholarships for graduating seniors, hosts a
Senior  Student  Athletes  Recognition  Dinner, and participates in many
other events; and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore, showing support for the senior citizens in the
community, the Canastota Lions Club sponsors an annual  dinner  for  the
community's senior citizens program; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Lions  Club also support both state and international
projects such as the Empire State Special Needs  Experience  Camp,  Camp
Hickory,  the  Leader  Dog  Program,  the  Upstate University Center for
Vision Research,  and  Lions  Club  International  Foundation  to  serve
mankind both in the local community and throughout the world; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  the  Canastota  Lions  Club is in direct
correlation to the efforts of its dedicated members,  whose  involvement
is,  and  always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an
unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard  of  excellence  in
all endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Canastota Lions Club upon the occasion  of  celebrating  its
75th  Anniversary,  and to honor its members, past and present, who have
labored so conscientiously to secure and improve the quality of life  in
the community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Canastota Lions Club.
