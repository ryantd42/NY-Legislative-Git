# Establishes the "Fallen Skywalkers Memorial Bridge" in the city of Buffalo in Erie County

**Bill ID:** S5724
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S5724 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5724)

## Summary

Establishes the "Fallen Skywalkers Memorial Bridge" in the city of Buffalo in Erie county.
