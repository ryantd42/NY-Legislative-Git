# Relates to the filing of objections to support magistrate determinations in child support, paternity and parentage proceedings in family court

**Bill ID:** S9712
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S9712 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S9712)

## Summary

Relates to the filing of objections to support magistrate determinations in child support, paternity and parentage proceedings in family court and to the time-limit for appeals in all categories of family court cases.
