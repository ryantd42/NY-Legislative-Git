# Relates to public financing contributions that are not matchable for purposes of public financing

**Bill ID:** A10650
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Jonathan Jacobson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10650 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10650)

## Summary

Provides that election contributions made in cash, or in bitcoin or any other form of cryptocurrency are not matchable for purposes of public financing.
