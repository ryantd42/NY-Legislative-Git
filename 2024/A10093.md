# Prohibits the importation of live bait fish and provides penalties for failure to comply with such prohibition

**Bill ID:** A10093
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Alex Bores
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A10093 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10093)

## Summary

Prohibits the importation of live bait fish; provides penalties for failure to comply with such prohibition.
