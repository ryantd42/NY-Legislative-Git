# Requires contractors and subcontractors working on covered projects submit their payrolls or transcripts to the fiscal officer

**Bill ID:** A9265AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A9265AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A9265AA)

## Summary

Requires contractors and subcontractors working on covered projects submit their payrolls or transcripts to the fiscal officer; directs the department to create a database for such records that are publicly available for inspection.
