# Relates to prohibiting the inclusion of a confession of judgment in a contract or agreement for a financial product or service

**Bill ID:** S5256
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S5256 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5256)

## Summary

Prohibits the inclusion of a confession of judgment in a contract or agreement for a financial product or service.
