# Provides that persons with a disability shall be eligible to receive a fishing license for a reduced fee

**Bill ID:** A5907AA
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A5907AA PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A5907AA)

## Summary

Provides that persons with a disability shall be eligible to receive a fishing license free for five dollars.
