# Extends the temporary "commission to prevent childhood drowning" to improve water safety in New York state

**Bill ID:** A10334
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A10334 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A10334)

## Summary

Extends the temporary "commission to prevent childhood drowning" to improve water safety in New York state another year.
