# Gives state income tax credit to volunteer firefighters and members of a volunteer ambulance corps in good standing up to $1200

**Bill ID:** S5944
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5944 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5944)

## Summary

Gives state income tax credit to volunteer firefighters and members of volunteer ambulance corps in good standing up to $1200; requires such member must be in good standing for a minimum of five years and maintain continued eligibility.
