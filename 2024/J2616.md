## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2616
 
BY: Senator SEPULVEDA
 
  HONORING  GFR  Media  for  its  exceptional
  contributions to the  Puerto  Rican  Parade  and  El
  Junte  Boricua  Week  in  New  York, and its ongoing
  support of the Puerto Rican diaspora in New York
 
  WHEREAS, The State of New York, renowned for its cultural  diversity
and  vibrant  artistic  community,  proudly  recognizes  and  honors
organizations that promote and celebrate the rich heritage of the Puerto
Rican community; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  honor  GFR  Media
for  its  influential role in La Parada Puertorriquena de Nueva York and
El Junte Boricua Week, recognizing the company's profound impact on  the
cultural  representation and celebration of Puerto Rican heritage in one
of the most iconic diaspora events in the United States; and
 
  WHEREAS, GFR Media has consistently demonstrated excellence  in  its
coverage  and  participation  during  these  events, providing exclusive
real-time updates, engaging the community through interactive activities
on its float, and facilitating important networking  opportunities  that
enhance  community  bonds  and cultural pride among Puerto Ricans in New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, El Junte Boricua  Week,  an  initiative  supported  by  GFR
Media,  serves as a significant cultural platform that promotes heritage
tourism and showcases the arts, culture, and unity of the  Puerto  Rican
diaspora,  encouraging  visits  to  the  island  and  fostering economic
benefits for local businesses; and
 
  WHEREAS, GFR Media's innovative media strategies  and  collaborative
efforts  during  the  Puerto Rican Parade and El Junte Boricua Week have
not only enriched the experiences of attendees  but  have  also  ensured
that  the  achievements  and  cultural  vibrancy  of  the  Puerto  Rican
community are prominently featured and celebrated across  diverse  media
platforms; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  dedication  of  GFR  Media  to promoting Puerto Rican
culture through comprehensive coverage of such pivotal events highlights
its commitment to fostering  a  sense  of  pride,  unity,  and  cultural
continuity among Puerto Ricans both in New York and globally; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  State  of  New  York  acknowledges  the  significant
contributions of GFR Media to the cultural and social landscape  of  New
York,  enhancing  the visibility of Puerto Rican culture and providing a
platform  for  storytelling  and  cultural  expression  that  uplifts,
educates, and inspires; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor GFR Media for its exceptional contributions to  the  Puerto  Rican
Parade and El Junte Boricua Week in New York, and its ongoing support of
the Puerto Rican diaspora in New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to GFR Media.
