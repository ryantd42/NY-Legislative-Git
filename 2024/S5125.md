# Relates to enacting the New York deforestation protection act

**Bill ID:** S5125
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S5125 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S5125)

## Summary

Relates to enacting the "New York deforestation protection act"; provides that the department of environmental conservation shall adopt rules to protect forest resources related to timber, pests and invasive species and water and land management.

  IT IS THE PUBLIC POLICY OF THE STATE TO CONTROL FOREST PESTS THREATEN-

 § 9-2305. POWERS OF THE DEPARTMENT; PESTS AND INVASIVE SPECIES.
  1. THE DEPARTMENT IS VESTED WITH THE AUTHORITY AND JURISDICTION IN ALL
 MATTERS RELATING TO THE PREVENTION,  DETECTION  AND  CONTROL  OF  FOREST

 UNITED  STATES  DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE AND OTHER FEDERAL AGENCIES AND

  4. EVERY OWNER OF FOREST LANDS OR TIMBER SHALL EXERCISE EVERY  REASON-

 SHALL  MEET OR EXCEED THE FOLLOWING MINIMUM STANDARDS AND BE CARRIED OUT

 SILVICULTURAL  OR  MANAGEMENT  GUIDES  OR  HANDBOOKS.  THE
 REGULATIONS/STANDARDS SHALL:

 ACTIVITY IN A PROTECTION DISTRICT DESCRIBED IN  PARAGRAPH  (B)  OF  THIS
 SUBDIVISION THAT REQUIRES A PERMIT.
