## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 2361
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  130th Anniversary of Morehouse
  Appliances of New Hartford, New York
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  the
achievements  of  those  individuals  and  businesses  that  contribute
substantially to the economic vitality  of  their  communities,  thereby
enhancing economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  130th  Anniversary  of  Morehouse  Appliances  of  New
Hartford, New York, celebrated in-store with exciting  giveaways,  tasty
refreshments, and engaging demos; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Morehouse  Appliances  was  originally  founded  as  H.D.
Morehouse in 1894 in Utica by Henry D. Morehouse; with a great sense  of
familial  pride  and service to the family's community, the store is now
run by Dan Morehouse, the great-grandson of  founder,  Henry  Morehouse;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  first Morehouse made a business of making and selling
horseshoes many decades ago and has adapted to the ever-changing  times;
Morehouse  Appliances,  as  we  know  it  today, celebrates 130 years of
innovation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This business consistently  has,  and  continues  to  offer
stellar  customer  service,  dependability,  expansive  knowledge,  and
offerings of the best quality appliances on the market; and
 
  WHEREAS, A credit to  the  trust  and  reliability  this  accredited
business  has  created  with  residents  of  their community and beyond,
Morehouse Appliances is one of the longest  Maytag  dealerships  in  the
entire United States; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  Morehouse  Appliances  is  in  direct
correlation to the efforts of its hardworking and dedicated  staff,  all
of  whom  are  considered integral parts of the success of the business;
their  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an
impressive  commitment,  an  unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising
standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of  the  business  and
the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges this exceptional business, its contributions to  the  local
and  state  economies,  and its vibrant role in improving the quality of
life in its community, fully confident that it will  continue  to  enjoy
the  sparkling successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the  130th  Anniversary  of  Morehouse  Appliances  of  New
Hartford, New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Morehouse Appliances.
