# Relates to the confinement of incarcerated individuals under the age of eighteen

**Bill ID:** S6001
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S6001 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S6001)

## Summary

Relates to the confinement of incarcerated individuals under the age of eighteen who shall not be placed in segregated confinement for any reason.
