# Prohibits the New York city water board from enacting any rules and regulations less favorable to the consumer

**Bill ID:** S4843
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S4843 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/S4843)

## Summary

Prohibits the NYC water board from enacting any rules and regulations less favorable to the consumer, including timeframes for appeals and receiving refunds.
