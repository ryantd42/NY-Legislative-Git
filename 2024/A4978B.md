# Requires daycare facilities to maintain evacuation and lockdown plans for children with disabilities during an emergency situation

**Bill ID:** A4978BB
**Session:** 2024
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A4978BB PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2024/A4978BB)

## Summary

Requires daycare facilities to maintain evacuation and lockdown plans for children with disabilities during an emergency situation.

  2.  EACH  EMERGENCY  EVACUATION AND LOCKDOWN PLAN REQUIRED PURSUANT TO
 THIS SECTION SHALL  INCLUDE,  BUT  NOT  BE  LIMITED  TO,  THE  FOLLOWING
 ELEMENTS:
  (A) PROCEDURES FOR EVACUATING CHILDREN WITH DISABILITIES;
  (B) LOCKDOWN PROCEDURES FOR CHILDREN WITH DISABILITIES;
  (C) A LIST OF CHILDREN ATTENDING THE DAYCARE FACILITY WHO HAVE A DISA-
 BILITY  AND  WHO WOULD NEED ASSISTANCE DURING AN EVACUATION, LOCKDOWN OR
 ANY OTHER EMERGENCY SITUATION; AND
  (D) THE LOCATION AND TYPE OF EVACUATION OR LOCKDOWN ASSISTANCE DEVICES
 OR ASSISTIVE TECHNOLOGIES THAT ARE AVAILABLE WITHIN THE DAYCARE  FACILI-
 TY.
  3.  TO  THE EXTENT PRACTICABLE, EACH EMERGENCY EVACUATION AND LOCKDOWN
 PLAN SHALL TAKE INTO ACCOUNT THE PARTICULAR DISABILITIES THE CHILDREN IN
 THE DAYCARE FACILITY MAY HAVE.
  4. (A) EACH DAYCARE FACILITY SHALL MAKE THE PLAN REQUIRED PURSUANT  TO
 THIS SECTION AVAILABLE UPON REQUEST TO:
  (I) LOCAL LAW ENFORCEMENT AND EMERGENCY RESPONDERS;
  (II) EMPLOYEES AND OTHER PERSONNEL OF THE PROGRAMS AS DEEMED NECESSARY
 FOR  PURPOSES  OF  BEING ABLE TO CARRY OUT AND COMPLY WITH THE EMERGENCY
 EVACUATION AND LOCKDOWN PLANS SET FORTH IN THIS SECTION; AND
  (III) A PARENT OR CUSTODIAN; PROVIDED, HOWEVER, A PARENT OR  CUSTODIAN
 MAY  ONLY  RECEIVE PLAN INFORMATION THAT PERTAINS DIRECTLY TO A CHILD IN
 THEIR CARE AND CUSTODY.
  (B) ALL INFORMATION RELATED TO A YOUTH'S DIAGNOSES,  DISABILITIES,  OR
 IDENTIFICATION MUST REMAIN CONFIDENTIAL AS REQUIRED BY FEDERAL AND STATE
 LAW.
  4.  A  COPY  OF  THE  PLAN SHALL BE MAINTAINED AT ALL TIMES IN A PLACE
 EASILY ACCESSIBLE BY LAW ENFORCEMENT, EMERGENCY RESPONDERS  AND  DAYCARE
 FACILITY EMPLOYEES.
  5.  ON  AN  ANNUAL BASIS, EVERY DAYCARE FACILITY SHALL UPDATE THE PLAN
 AND PROVIDE THE OFFICE OF CHILDREN  AND  FAMILY  SERVICES,  PARENTS  AND
 FACILITY  EMPLOYEES WITH NOTICE DETAILING THE UPDATED PROVISIONS OF SUCH
 PLAN, IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS SET FORTH IN THIS SECTION.
