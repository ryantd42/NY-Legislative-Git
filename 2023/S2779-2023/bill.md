# Allows New York states sire stakes eligibility for foals sired by New York state stallions

**Bill ID:** S2779
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Allows New York sire stakes eligibility for foals sired by New York state stallions, either in-state or out-of-state.
