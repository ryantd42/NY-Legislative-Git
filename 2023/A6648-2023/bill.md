# Prohibits cancellation, non-renewal or conditioning renewal of automobile insurance when dispute as to pending claim exists

**Bill ID:** A6648
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monique Chandler-Waterman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits cancellation, non-renewal or conditioning renewal of automobile insurance of a policyholder solely as a result of a dispute or a complaint pending against the insurer.
