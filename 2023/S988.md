# Relates to the definition of person for the purposes of homicide

**Bill ID:** S988
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S988 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S988)

## Summary

Designates the definition of person for the purposes of homicide as Mya's law.
