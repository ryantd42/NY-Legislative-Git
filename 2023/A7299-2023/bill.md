# Establishes the drinking water quality institute to make recommendations to the department of health relating to ensuring the safety of potable water

**Bill ID:** A7299
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Josh Jensen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the drinking water quality institute to make recommendations to the department of health relating to ensuring the safety of potable water.
