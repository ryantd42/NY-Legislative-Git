# Relates to requiring helicopters be equipped with flight recorders, cockpit voice recorders and terrain awareness and warning systems

**Bill ID:** S1658
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1658 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1658)

## Summary

Requires helicopters be equipped with flight recorders, cockpit voice recorders and terrain awareness and warning systems.
