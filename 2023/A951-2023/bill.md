# Requires digital billboards to display each image for at least 60 consecutive seconds

**Bill ID:** A951
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A951 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A951)

## Summary

Requires digital billboards to display each image for at least 60 consecutive seconds.
