# Establishes the offense of unlawful possession of firearms by persons under twenty-one

**Bill ID:** A442
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A442 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A442)

## Summary

Establishes the offense of unlawful possession of firearms by persons under twenty-one.
