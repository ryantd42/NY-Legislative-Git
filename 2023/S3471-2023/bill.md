# Requires all school districts to have a licensed physical education teacher

**Bill ID:** S3471
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires all school districts to have a licensed physical education teacher.

  2. The board of education or trustees of every school  district  regu-
