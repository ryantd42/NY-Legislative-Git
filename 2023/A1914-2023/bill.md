# Establishes a school speed zone camera demonstration program in the city of Jamestown

**Bill ID:** A1914
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a school speed zone camera demonstration program in the city of Jamestown in up to nine school speed zones.
