# Allows veterans to fish for free in state parks

**Bill ID:** A845
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A845 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A845)

## Summary

Allows veterans of the armed forces of the United States to fish for free in state parks under the jurisdiction of a state agency.
