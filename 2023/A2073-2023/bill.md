# Requires the employment address of certain sex offenders to be reported to the division of criminal justice services

**Bill ID:** A2073
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stephen Hawley
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the employment address of certain sex offenders to be reported to the division of criminal justice services.
