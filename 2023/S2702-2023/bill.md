# Relates to the operation of all-terrain vehicles by minors

**Bill ID:** S2702
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Increases age for use of ATVs by minors from age ten to fourteen.
