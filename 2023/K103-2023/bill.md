# Congratulating the Syracuse University Men's Soccer Team upon the occasion of winning the 2022 NCAA Men's Soccer Tournament and National Championship

**Bill ID:** K103
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 103
 
BY: M. of A. Magnarelli
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Syracuse  University  Men's
  Soccer Team upon the occasion of  winning  the  2022
  National  Collegiate  Athletic  Association  (NCAA)
  Men's Soccer Tournament and National Championship
 
  WHEREAS,  Excellence  and  success  in  competitive  sports  can  be
achieved  only  through  strenuous  practice,  teamwork  and a dedicated
coaching staff; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  enhances  the  moral  and  physical
development  of  the  young people of this State, preparing them for the
future by instilling in  them  the  value  of  teamwork,  encouraging  a
standard  of  healthy  living,  imparting  a  desire for success, and by
developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate and recognize the Syracuse  University  Men's  Soccer  Team
upon  the  occasion  of  winning  the  2022 National Collegiate Athletic
Association (NCAA) Men's Soccer Tournament  and  National  Championship;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  their 8th NCAA Tournament, the Syracuse University
Men's Soccer Team won the program's first  ever  National  Championship,
defeating Indiana University 7-6 in a penalty kick shootout; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Orange finished their season with an impressive overall
record  of  19-2-4, the most wins in a single season in program history;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The title marks the 80th National Championship in  Syracuse
Athletics'  history  of  31 team titles and 49 individual championships;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Syracuse University is the fourth ever ACC program  to  win
its  regular  season  division,  conference tournament and NCAA National
Championship in a single-season; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Coach  Ian  McIntyre  guided  the  team  to  the  National
Championship  in  his  13th  season  as Head Coach, earning his 2nd NCAA
College Cup, 7th NCAA  Tournament  Appearance  and  2nd  Atlantic  Coast
Conference  (ACC) title; he was also named National College Coach of the
Year for the second time in 2022; and
 
  WHEREAS, Goalkeeper Russell Shealy was named the 2022 Men's  College
Cup  Defensive  Most  Outstanding Player, while Nathan Opoku earned Most
Outstanding Offensive Player; they were  joined  on  the  All-Tournament
Team  by  Levonte  Johnson,  Curt  Calov,  Christian  Curti  and Jeorgio
Kocevski; and
 
  WHEREAS, A school record five Syracuse  men's  soccer  players  were
selected in the 2023 Major League Soccer SuperDraft, Abdi Salim, Levonte
Johnson, Amferny Sinclair, Russell Shealy, Buster Sjoberg; and
 
 
  WHEREAS, The outstanding athletic talent, teamwork and sportsmanship
displayed  by  the Syracuse University Men's Soccer Team is due in great
part to the skilled and supportive efforts of Head Coach  Ian  McIntyre,
Associate  Head  Coach  Jukka  Masalin,  Assistant  Coach  Sean  Lawlor,
Assistant Provost for  Student-Athlete  Academic  Development  Tommy  J.
Powell and Communications Assistant Nick Economides; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout the season, the support of fans within both the
Syracuse University  and  Central  New  York  communities  showed  great
enthusiasm; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Members  of  the  Syracuse  University  Men's  Soccer Team
rallied behind one another in establishing  a  brotherhood  of  athletic
ability,  good sportsmanship, honor and scholarship, while demonstrating
these team players are of the highest caliber, while contributing to the
spirit of excellence that is  a  tradition  of  their  university;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the outstanding members of the  Syracuse  University  Men's
Soccer Team: Sam Coss, Russell Shealy, Christian Curti, Abdi Salim, Noah
Singelmann,  Amferny  Sinclair,  Julio  Fulcar,  Curt  Calov,  Jeorgio
Kocevski, Julius Rauch, Nathan Opoku, Lorenzo Boselli, Gavin Wigg, Colin
Biros, Levonte Johnson, Louie  Bulger,  Michal  Gradus,  Giona  Leibold,
Camden  Holbrook,  Buster  Sjoberg,  Trevor  Carabin,  Stephen Betz, Olu
Oyegunle, Jackson Glenn, Andrea DiBlasio, Parker Kump, Tony Shaw,  Aidan
Arber,  Francesco Pagano, Antonino Pagano, Jahiem Wickham, Noah Lechelt,
Marc Chin and Lucas Daunhauer on their outstanding  season  and  overall
team record; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to members of the Syracuse University Men's Soccer Team, and
the aforementioned coaches and staff.
