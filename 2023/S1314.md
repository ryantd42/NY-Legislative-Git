# Relates to waiving certain fees charged for motor vehicle license plates

**Bill ID:** S1314
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1314 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1314)

## Summary

Provides that the commissioner of motor vehicles shall waive the payment of fees for license plates if the new plates required are due to a change in plate design.
