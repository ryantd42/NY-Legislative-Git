# Provides for an annual growth amount for tuition and regional rate reimbursement for approved school-age and preschool special education programs

**Sponsor:** Edward Ra
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for an annual growth amount for tuition and regional rate reimbursement for approved school-age and preschool special education programs.
