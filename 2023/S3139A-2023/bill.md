# Requires the division of housing and community renewal to provide an annual report on the New York access to home programs

**Bill ID:** S3139AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Requires the division of housing and community renewal to provide an annual report on the New York access to home programs.
