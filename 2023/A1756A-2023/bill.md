# Creates a tax credit for employers who help reduce the governmental education loans of their employees

**Bill ID:** A1756AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates a tax credit for employers who help reduce the governmental education loans of their employees.
