# Limits the naming of municipalities and subdivisions thereof

**Bill ID:** S3345
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the name of municipalities and subdivisions thereof to include the word swastika.
