# Authorizes on-site inspections of housing in violation of zoning laws for purposes of enforcing illegal housing laws and regulations

**Bill ID:** A2696
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jake Ryan Blumencranz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes on-site inspections of housing in violation of zoning laws for purposes of enforcing illegal housing laws and regulations.
