# Relates to the establishment of the New York state promise program; appropriation

**Bill ID:** S1840
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1840 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1840)

## Summary

Relates to the establishment of the New York state promise program for community college courses; makes an appropriation therefor.
