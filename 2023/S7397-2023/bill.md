# Relates to the suspension and revocation of certain learner's permits and the return of numbered plates and registration suspensions in certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** S7397
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michael Gianaris
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for the suspension and revocation of certain learner's permits, and the return of numbered plates and registration suspensions in certain circumstances.
