# Designates certain emergency and public safety dispatchers and operators as first responders

**Bill ID:** A7151
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Designates certain emergency and public safety dispatchers and operators as first responders.
