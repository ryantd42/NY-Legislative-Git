# Prohibits the transfer of unexpended moneys from funds receiving moneys from a dedicated fee into any other fund

**Bill ID:** A1989
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stephen Hawley
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the transfer of unexpended moneys from funds receiving moneys from a dedicated fee into any other fund.
