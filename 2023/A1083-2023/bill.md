# Adds a school business official to the Teachers' Retirement System (TRS) Board

**Bill ID:** A1083
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1083 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1083)

## Summary

Adds a school business official to the Teachers' Retirement System (TRS) Board.

 AN ACT to amend the education law, in relation to adding a school  busi-

  Section 1. The opening paragraph of subdivision 2 of  section  504  of

 amended and a new paragraph g is added to read as follows:
  The retirement board shall consist of [ten] ELEVEN members as follows.

 OF THE ASSOCIATION OF SCHOOL BUSINESS OFFICIALS OF NEW YORK.
