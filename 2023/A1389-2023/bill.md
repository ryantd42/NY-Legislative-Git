# Requires a local social services district to accept or deny an application for public assistance no later than thirty days after the date of the application; repealer

**Bill ID:** A1389
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1389 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1389)

## Summary

Requires a local social services district to accept or deny an application for public assistance as soon as possible but no later than thirty days after the date of the application.
