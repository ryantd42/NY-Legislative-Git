# Provides for insurance coverage for electrolysis procedures when deemed medically necessary

**Bill ID:** S7304
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for insurance coverage for electrolysis procedures to stop the growth of hair, when deemed medically necessary.
