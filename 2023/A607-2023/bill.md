# Directs the department of agriculture and markets, in cooperation with NYSERDA, to produce and distribute guidance and educational materials for farmers on the use of agrivoltaics in farming

**Bill ID:** A607
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Didi Barrett
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A607 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A607)

## Summary

Directs the department of agriculture and markets, in cooperation with NYSERDA, to produce and distribute guidance and educational materials for farmers on the use of agrivoltaics in farming no later than one year after chapter 629 of the laws of 2022 takes effect.
