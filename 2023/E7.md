# Requires the Speaker of the Assembly to appoint a two person committee to inform the Governor the Assembly is ready to proceed with business

**Bill ID:** E7
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E7 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/E7)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 7
 
BY: M. of A. Peoples-Stokes
 
  establishing  a  committee  of the Assembly to
  advise the Governor
 
  RESOLVED, That a committee of two be appointed  by  the  Speaker  to
advise the Governor of the election of Carl E. Heastie as Speaker of the
Assembly  and  to inform her that the Assembly is organized and ready to
proceed with business.
