# Increases the penalty for rape in the first degree

**Bill ID:** A4286
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Lemondes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases the penalty for rape in the first degree from a B felony to an A-II felony.
