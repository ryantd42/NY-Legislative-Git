# Provides that prescriptions covered by insurance, that would otherwise be required to be filled by mail order, may be filled at a retail pharmacy under certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** S3370
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Provides that prescriptions covered by insurance, that would otherwise be required to be filled by mail order, may be filled at a retail pharmacy under certain circumstances.
