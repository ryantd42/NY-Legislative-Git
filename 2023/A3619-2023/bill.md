# Relates to the members of the franchise oversight board and the duties and responsibilities of such board

**Bill ID:** A3619
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Extends the oversight functions of the board to apply to the New York city off-track betting corporation.
