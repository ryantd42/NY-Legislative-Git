# Raises the weight limit for all terrain vehicles and updates the fee schedule for all terrain vehicle registration

**Bill ID:** S7024
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Raises the weight limit for all terrain vehicles; updates the fee schedule for all terrain vehicle registration; requires certain funds collected from all terrain vehicle registration fees to be deposited to the credit of the New York state autism awareness and research fund.
