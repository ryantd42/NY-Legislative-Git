# Establishes a tax credit for Gold Star children

**Bill ID:** A2014
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Charles Fall
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a tax credit for Gold Star children in the amount of $1,500.
