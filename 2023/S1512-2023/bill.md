# Relates to the number of senators and the apportionment of senate districts

**Bill ID:** S1512
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1512 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1512)

## Summary

Relates to the number of senators and the apportionment of senate districts; provides that each senate district shall be comprised of one county in its entirety.
