# Provides an exemption for tangible personal property and services sold by a cemetery

**Bill ID:** S685
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S685 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S685)

## Summary

Provides an exemption for tangible personal property and services sold by a cemetery.
