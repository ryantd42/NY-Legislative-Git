# Relates to establishing the offense of aggravated rape and criminal sentencing for defendants convicted of certain sexual offenses

**Bill ID:** S3276
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the offense of aggravated rape; provides criminal sentencing mandates for defendants convicted of certain sexual offenses.
