# Increase the types of firearms that are to be included in the firearm ballistic identification database

**Bill ID:** S6831
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the types of firearms that are to be included in the firearm ballistic identification databank.
