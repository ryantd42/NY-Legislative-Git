# Provides absentee ballots to all qualified voters; repealer

**Bill ID:** A4270
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires absentee ballots to be provided to all qualified voters; relates to the delivery of absentee ballots.
