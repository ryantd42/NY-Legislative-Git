# Relates to the taxation of electronic cigarette cartridges

**Bill ID:** A2119
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the taxation of electronic cigarette cartridges as tobacco products.
