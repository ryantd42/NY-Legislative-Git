# Commending Kim & Jeff Lichtenstein upon the occasion of their designation for special recognition by Residents Forward at their Caring For Port Washington Gala on June 2, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1101
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1101
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Kim  &  Jeff  Lichtenstein  upon  the
  occasion  of  their  designation  for  special
  recognition by Residents Forward at their Caring For
  Port Washington Gala on June 2, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay tribute to individuals of high  purpose  and  worthy  accomplishment
whose  endeavors  have  faithfully served the welfare of the citizens of
the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Kim & Jeff Lichtenstein upon the occasion of  their  designation
for  special  recognition  by Residents Forward at their Caring For Port
Washington Gala on Friday, June 2, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Kim & Jeff Lichtenstein  are  the  proud  parents  of  four
daughters; their cherished family resides in Sands Point, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Dedicated  to  the  advancement  of  her  community,  Kim
Lichtenstein is  an  active  volunteer  and  community  organizer,  most
recently  helping with the launch of Commune on Main Street; she is also
working with Residents Forward to launch Phase Two of  Let's  Walk  Port
Washington; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  true,  experienced,  asset  to  Port Washington and the
greater area, Jeff Lichtenstein was a panelist at the Residents  Forward
Youth Climate Summit in the Food & Waste Workshop; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Kim  & Jeff Lichtenstein upon the occasion of their designation
for special recognition by Residents Forward at their  Caring  For  Port
Washington Gala; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Kim & Jeff Lichtenstein.
