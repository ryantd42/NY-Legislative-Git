# Establishes a New York Main Street development center in the division of housing and community renewal

**Bill ID:** S6518
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes a New York Main Street development center in the division of housing and community renewal.
