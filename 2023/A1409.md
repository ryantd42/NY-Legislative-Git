# Relates to the judicial process relating to a dog that caused death to a companion animal, farm animal or domestic animal while trespassing on property

**Bill ID:** A1409
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A1409 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1409)

## Summary

Relates to the judicial process relating to a dog that causes the death of a companion animal, farm animal or domestic animal while trespassing on property.
