# Relates to drivers' license suspensions for traffic fatalities

**Bill ID:** A693
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A693 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A693)

## Summary

Requires the suspension of the license to operate a motor vehicle of any person who was operating a motor vehicle at the time of an accident which resulted in a fatality.
