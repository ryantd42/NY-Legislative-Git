# Relates to requiring space for donate life registration in the initial process setting up an online member service portal with an insurer

**Bill ID:** S839
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S839 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S839)

## Summary

Requires space for donate life registration in the initial process setting up an online member service portal with an insurer.
