# Increases the number of county court judges in Saratoga county

**Bill ID:** A7019BB
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases the number of county court judges in Saratoga county by one justice.
