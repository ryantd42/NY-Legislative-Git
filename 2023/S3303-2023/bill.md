# Requires compliance with the uniform land use review procedure for the disposition of school property in New York city

**Bill ID:** S3303
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires compliance with the uniform land use review procedure for the disposition of school property in New York city.
