# Decreases the length of the term of office for the governor and lieutenant-governor to two years

**Bill ID:** A3464
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Decreases the length of the term of office for the governor and lieutenant-governor to two years.
