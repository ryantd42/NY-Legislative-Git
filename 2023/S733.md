# Enacts the Multichannel Video Programming Distributor Competition Act

**Bill ID:** S733
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S733 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S733)

## Summary

Enacts the Multichannel Video Programming Distributor Competition Act; defines terms; imposes a 5% excise on gross receipts.
