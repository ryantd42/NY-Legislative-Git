# Prohibits political contributions by businesses that have been awarded state contracts

**Bill ID:** S2018
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2018 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2018)

## Summary

Prohibits political contributions by businesses that have been awarded state contracts.
