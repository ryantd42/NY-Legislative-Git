# Amends section 2 of Rule IV of the Assembly Rules, in relation to the broadcast of committee meetings

**Bill ID:** E219
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** Lost

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 219
 
BY: M. of A. Barclay
 
  AMENDING  section  2  of  Rule  IV of the Assembly
  Rules, in relation to  the  broadcast  of  committee
  meetings
 
  RESOLVED, That subdivision g of section 2 of Rule IV of the Assembly
Rules is amended to read as follows:
 
  g.  The audio [and/or] and video broadcast of committee meetings shall
be made available and archived  on  the  Assembly  Internet  site  [when
practicable],  and shall be subject to the same requirements as apply to
televised proceedings of Assembly sessions pursuant to Rule X.
