# Requires schools to accept credits for educational programs at OCFS facilities as transfer credits

**Bill ID:** S3413
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires schools to accept credits for educational programs at OCFS facilities as transfer credits.
