# Relates to reexamination of licensees

**Bill ID:** S7078AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Permits the commissioner of motor vehicles to require any licensed driver to submit to an examination or reexamination prior to his or her normal renewal date upon receipt of a recommendation from a court having jurisdiction of traffic offenses, or the governor or chief magistrate of a state or territory stating that the driver's ability to operate a motor vehicle safely is questionable.
