# Provides for diaper changing accommodations in certain buildings

**Bill ID:** S3218
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires diaper changing accommodations be made available to all persons regardless of gender in certain buildings.
