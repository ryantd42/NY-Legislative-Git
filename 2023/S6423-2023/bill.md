# Exempts protective face masks or shields worn to help slow the spread of airborne diseases from sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S6423
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Exempts protective face masks or shields worn to help slow the spread of airborne diseases from sales and compensating use taxes.
