# Includes pharmacists in the list of persons and officials required to report child abuse

**Bill ID:** A6420
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes pharmacists in the list of persons and officials required to report child abuse or mistreatment.
