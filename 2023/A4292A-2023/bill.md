# Relates to certain tuition payments by school districts for general education and special students residing in such school districts

**Bill ID:** A4292A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Excludes certain tuition payments by school districts for general education and special students residing in such school districts from the calculation of the tax levy limit.
