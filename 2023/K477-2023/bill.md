# Congratulating Timothy Hilker, United States Marine, upon the occasion of his retirement as President of the Association of School Business Officials (ASBO New York

**Bill ID:** K477
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 477
 
BY: M. of A. Woerner
 
  CONGRATULATING  Timothy  Hilker,  former  United
  States Marine, upon the occasion of  his  retirement
  as  President  of the Association of School Business
  Officials  (ASBO  New  York)  and  for  his  special
  recognition  at  their  Annual  Luncheon  and Awards
  Ceremony on June 6, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
citizens of the State of  New  York  whose  lifework  and  service  have
enhanced  the quality of life in their communities and this great state;
and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body  is  proud  to  congratulate  Timothy
Hilker  upon  the  occasion  of  his  retirement  as  President  of  the
Association of School Business Officials (ASBO New  York)  and  for  his
special  recognition  at  their  Annual  Luncheon and Awards Ceremony on
Tuesday, June 6, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Timothy Hilker had a successful career in the United Stated
Marine Corps, proudly serving his country for six years; and
 
  WHEREAS, Following his service, Timothy Hilker earned his Bachelor's
Degree in Accounting from the University at  Buffalo  and  his  Master's
Degree  in  Business  Administration and School District Business Leader
Certification from Niagara University; and
 
  WHEREAS, Timothy  Hilker  would  serve  as  District  Treasurer  for
Niagara  Wheatfield  Central  School  District,  and  as School Business
Administrator of Hadley-Luzerne and Galway Central School Districts; and
 
  WHEREAS, Serving as the Assistant  Superintendent  for  Business  at
Saratoga  Springs  City  School  District  since  2016, Timothy Hilker's
remarkable leadership shows through his continued devotion to  education
and school business officials; and
 
  WHEREAS, Timothy Hilker has been committed to the development of the
school  business  profession  through his work as a school administrator
and as President of the Association of School Business Officials of  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Due to his tireless commitment, Timothy Hilker received the
George  A. Perry Volunteer Service Award in 2019 and the Dr. Joan Colvin
Starfish Award in 2021 from the Association of School Business Officials
of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout his career, Timothy Hilker has worked tirelessly
to ensure school business officials in New  York  State  have  the  best
chance to succeed; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of the great State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Timothy Hilker, United States Marine, upon the occasion  of
his  retirement  as  President  of  the  Association  of School Business
Officials (ASBO New York) and  for  his  special  recognition  at  their
Annual Luncheon and Awards Ceremony on June 6, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Timothy Hilker.
