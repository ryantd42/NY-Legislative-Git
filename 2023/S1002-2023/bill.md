# Requires insurance coverage for home test kits for sexually transmitted diseases

**Bill ID:** S1002
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1002 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1002)

## Summary

Requires health insurance coverage for home test kits for sexually transmitted diseases.
