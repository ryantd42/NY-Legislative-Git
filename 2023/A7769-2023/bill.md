# Establishes Diwali as a school holiday in public schools in the city of New York

**Bill ID:** A7769
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Establishes Diwali as a school holiday in public schools in the city of New York.
