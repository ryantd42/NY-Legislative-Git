# Relates to farm worker meal and lodging minimum wage credits

**Bill ID:** A3364
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to farm worker meal, lodging and utilities minimum wage credits.
