# Provides statutory provisions for proving and establishing lost or destroyed lifetime trusts

**Bill ID:** A7750
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Charles Lavine
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides a mechanism for establishing the existence of a writing creating or amending a lifetime trust which was either lost or destroyed.
