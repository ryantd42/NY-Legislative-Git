# Allows physician assistants to serve as primary care practitioners for purposes of Medicaid managed care plans

**Bill ID:** A7725
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Allows physician assistants to serve as primary care practitioners for purposes of Medicaid managed care plans.
