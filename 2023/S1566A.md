# Requires the commissioner of education to make recommendations to the board of regents relating to the adoption of instruction on dating violence prevention and healthy relationships

**Bill ID:** S1566AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Julia Salazar
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1566A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1566A)

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of education to make recommendations to the board of regents relating to the adoption of age-appropriate instruction on dating violence prevention and healthy relationships for students in grades seven through twelve.
