# Relates to coverage for punitive damages and civil penalties

**Bill ID:** A654
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A654 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A654)

## Summary

Permits a liability policy to provide coverage for punitive damages, civil penalties and other non-compensatory damages.
