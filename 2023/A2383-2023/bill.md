# Authorizes the town of New Berlin, county of Chenango, to reduce the maximum speed limit along a West Shore Road in such town to fifteen miles per hour

**Bill ID:** A2383
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the town of New Berlin, county of Chenango, to reduce the maximum speed limit along West Shore Road in such town to 15 miles per hour.
