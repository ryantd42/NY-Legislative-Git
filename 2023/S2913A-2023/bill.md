# Establishes the "Hunger-Free Campus Act"

**Bill ID:** S2913A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides grants to address food insecurity among students at public institutions for higher education.
