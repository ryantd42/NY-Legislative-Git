# Congratulating the Hammond Central School Varsity Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Girls Basketball Class D Championship

**Bill ID:** K403
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 403
 
BY: M. of A. Gray
 
  CONGRATULATING  the Hammond Central School Varsity
  Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing
  the NYSPHSAA Girls Basketball Class  D  Championship
  on March 19, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Hammond Varsity Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing the
NYSPHSAA Girls Basketball Class D Championship, to  be  celebrated  with
the  community at a banquet in the school gym on Thursday, May 25, 2023;
and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and applause of their excited fans,  the  Red
Devils  defeated  Oxford  Academy in a decisive 61-37 victory to win the
championship on Sunday, March  19,  2023,  at  Hudson  Valley  Community
College in Troy, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's  colors  of red and white,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Red Devils throughout their journey as  they  ended  their
season with an impressive overall 21-1 record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  basketball, which demands athletic
prowess, speed and  agility,  Head  Coach  Alyssa  Cosby  and  Assistant
Coaches John Arquitt, Katina Dillon and Chet Truskowski have worked hard
to hone the skills of this championship team, teaching these outstanding
athletes  lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the court;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach Alyssa Cosby and all of the outstanding athletes
on the Hammond Central School Varsity Girls Basketball Team have clearly
utilized dedication, determination and teamwork in providing  a  lasting
contribution  to  the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their
school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the  members  of  the  Hammond Central High School Varsity
Girls Basketball Team: Zoey Cunningham, Raelee Downs, Ava Howie, Landree
Kenyon, Hailee Manning,  Mikayla  Jones,  Sadey  Sprabary,  Mia  Tulley,
Laurall  Vinch  and  Isabelle  Woodstock;  Head  Coach Alyssa Cosby; and
Assistant Coaches John Arquitt, Katina Dillon and Chet  Truskowski  upon
the  occasion  of  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA  Girls  Basketball  Class  D
Championship on March 19, 2023; and be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members of the Hammond Central School  Varsity  Girls
Basketball  Team,  Head  Coach  Alyssa Cosby, and Assistant Coaches John
Arquitt, Katina Dillon and Chet Truskowski.
