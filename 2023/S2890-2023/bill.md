# Requires the board of elections to provide email notifications to voters whenever they change the location of a polling place

**Bill ID:** S2890
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the board of elections to provide email notifications to voters whenever they change the location of a polling place.
