# Relates to prohibiting the admission of COVID-19 positive residents to residential facilities for persons with developmental disabilities

**Bill ID:** S7313
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the admission of COVID-19 positive residents to residential facilities for persons with developmental disabilities.
