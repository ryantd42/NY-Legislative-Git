# Provides that microbusinesses in New York are eligible for small business revolving loans and the linked deposit interest rate reductions

**Bill ID:** S6490
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Provides that microbusinesses in New York are eligible for small business revolving loans and the linked deposit interest rate reductions.
