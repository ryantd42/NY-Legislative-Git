# Authorizes the vacating of an arbitration award on the basis of arbitrator disregard of the law

**Bill ID:** A3775
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Helene Weinstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the vacating of an arbitration award on the basis of arbitrator disregard of the law.
