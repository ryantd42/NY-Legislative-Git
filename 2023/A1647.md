# Requires notification procedures to be established giving advance notice of certain construction projects in school buildings or a district leasing school property

**Bill ID:** A1647
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1647 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1647)

## Summary

Requires notification procedures to be established requiring notice to be given prior to certain construction projects in school buildings or a district leasing school property.
