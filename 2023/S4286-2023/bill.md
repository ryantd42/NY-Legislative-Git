# Requires the use of canines for contraband screening at correctional facilities housing one hundred or more incarcerated individuals

**Bill ID:** S4286
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the use of canines for contraband screening at correctional facilities housing one hundred or more incarcerated individuals.
