# Relates to amendments to transportation contracts for the purpose of zero-emission school bus compliance

**Bill ID:** A7289
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michael Benedetto
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for amendments to transportation contracts for the purpose of zero emission school bus requirement compliance.
