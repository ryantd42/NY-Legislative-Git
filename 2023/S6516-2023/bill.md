# Provides for municipal residency restrictions

**Bill ID:** S6516
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Provides for municipal residency restrictions; sets permissible maximum distance from a school grounds at 1,500 feet.
