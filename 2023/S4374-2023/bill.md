# Relates to the metropolitan transportation authority's voting members; and repeals certain provisions of such law relating thereto

**Bill ID:** S4374
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Changes the structure of the metropolitan transportation authority's voting members; requires a two-thirds vote for such authority to take on any new obligations.
