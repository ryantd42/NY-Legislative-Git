# Increases compensation of election inspectors to $300 and coordinators to $350 in New York city

**Bill ID:** A2565
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Colton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases compensation of election inspectors to $300 and coordinators to $350 in New York city.
