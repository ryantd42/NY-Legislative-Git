# Directs the commissioner of the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation to provide for the re-designation of Donald J. Trump State Park

**Bill ID:** S3166
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation to conduct a legal review for the re-designation of Donald J. Trump State Park, in Putnam and Westchester counties to a new name.

 AN ACT directing the commissioner of the office of parks, recreation and

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. The commissioner of the office  of  parks,  recreation  and
 historic preservation shall conduct a legal review of the re-designation
 of  Donald  J.  Trump State Park, in Putnam and Westchester counties, to
 determine whether the authority exists to change the name of such  park.
 Within  one  year  of  the  effective date of this act, the commissioner
 shall submit the findings of such review to the governor, the  temporary
 president  of  the  senate,  and  the speaker of the assembly.  If such
 review determines that such legal  authority  exists,  the  commissioner
 shall  change  the name of such park. In the event the name of such park
 is changed, the office of parks, recreation  and  historic  preservation
 shall  provide  for  the  installation  and  maintenance  of appropriate
 signage and any other administrative actions necessary to implement  the
 name change and to improve the park for the enjoyment of the public.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
