# Establishes a small business tax credit for salary and wages paid to employees for time spent at sexual harassment prevention training

**Bill ID:** A6620
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a small business tax credit for salary and wages paid to employees for time spent at sexual harassment prevention training.
