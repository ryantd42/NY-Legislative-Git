# Relates to the eligibility of enrolled members of Southampton Village Ocean Rescue for the tax exemption on real property authorized by section 466-c of the real property tax law

**Bill ID:** A1353AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A1353A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1353A)

## Summary

Provides that enrolled members of Southampton Village Ocean Rescue shall be eligible for the tax exemption on real property authorized by section 466-c of the real property tax law.
