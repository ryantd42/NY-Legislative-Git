# Provides coverage for remotely connected insulin pens for certain patients with diabetes

**Bill ID:** A1859
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides coverage of remotely connected insulin pens under the pharmacy benefit of medical assistance for eligible patients with diabetes.
