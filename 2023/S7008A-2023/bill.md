# Authorizes the City of Troy, County of Rensselaer, to enter into a PILOT Agreement with Hillside Redevelopment Associates, granting an exemption from real property taxes

**Bill ID:** S7008A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Authorizes the City of Troy, County of Rensselaer, to enter into a PILOT Agreement with Hillside Redevelopment Associates, granting an exemption from real property taxes.
