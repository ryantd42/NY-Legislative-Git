# Commemorating the 75th Anniversary of the Yates County Chamber of Commerce on June 1, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1143
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1143
 
BY: Senator O'MARA
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  75th  Anniversary of the Yates
  County Chamber of Commerce on June 1, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize that
the quality and character of life in the communities  across  the  great
State of New York is reflective of certain exemplary organizations whose
purpose  is  dedicated  to  serving  the  community and the needs of its
citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commemorate the 75th Anniversary of the Yates County Chamber of Commerce
on Thursday, June 1, 2023, to be  celebrated  at  the  Chamber's  Annual
Awards Dinner; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  its founding 75 years ago, the Yates County Chamber
of  Commerce  has  spoken  to  the  needs  of  the  business  community,
creatively promoting and advocating for business in the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  humble  beginnings, the Penn Yan Chamber of Commerce
merged with the Dundee Chamber of Commerce in the late 1990s  to  become
the Yates County Chamber of Commerce; and
 
  WHEREAS,  After  the merger, a Welcome Center was built to act as an
Information Center for visitors; the Chamber works in  partnership  with
Yates  County  as  its  Tourism  Promotion  Agency (TPA), as the Chamber
strategically develops and implements a  tourism  marketing  program  on
behalf of county government to increase visitation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Welcome  Center  is one of the many services that the
Yates County Chamber of Commerce provides; an  active  events  committee
works  year-round  to  provide exciting community events and improve the
quality of life for the county's residents; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Yates County Chamber of Commerce enjoys a  relationship
with  the Keuka Wine Trail (KWT) as its administrative team; the Chamber
assists in the production of collateral  materials,  management  of  the
website, and coordination for ticket sales to KWT events; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success of the Yates County Chamber of Commerce is in
direct correlation to the efforts of its dedicated Board  of  Directors,
staff,  and  volunteers,  whose  involvement  is,  and  always has been,
characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and
an  uncompromising  standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of
the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, Steeped in a proud and  distinguished  history,  the  Yates
County Chamber of Commerce may take just pride in its purposeful growth,
as  it  looks forward to continuing to serve the business, cultural, and
intellectual needs of the community; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate the 75th Anniversary of the Yates County Chamber of Commerce
on June 1, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Yates County Chamber of Commerce.
