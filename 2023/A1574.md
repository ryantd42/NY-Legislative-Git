# Relates to owner's policy of liability insurance

**Bill ID:** A1574
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jo Anne Simon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1574 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1574)

## Summary

Relates to an owner's policy of liability insurance; increases the limits for liability arising out of bodily injury to or death of any person.
