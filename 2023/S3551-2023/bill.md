# Requires a surety bond for contractors and subcontractors where no public fund has been established for the financing of a public improvement

**Bill ID:** S3551
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires a surety bond for contractors and subcontractors where no public fund has been established for the financing of a public improvement.
