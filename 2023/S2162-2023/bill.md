# Imposes an additional tax on income attributable to long-term capital gain

**Bill ID:** S2162
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Imposes an additional tax on income attributable to long-term capital gain.
