# Establishes the state diesel emissions reduction revolving loan fund

**Bill ID:** A454
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A454 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A454)

## Summary

Establishes the diesel emissions reduction program and the state diesel emissions reduction revolving loan fund.
