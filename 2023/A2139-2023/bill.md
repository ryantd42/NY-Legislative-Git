# Requires a school nurse to be on the premises during school hours in any school having an enrollment of 100 pupils or more

**Bill ID:** A2139
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Colton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires a school nurse to be on the premises during school hours in any school having an enrollment of 100 pupils or more.
