# Relates to increasing penalties for violations involving the use of pre-recorded phone messages by telephone solicitors

**Bill ID:** A863
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A863 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A863)

## Summary

Increases the penalty for any person who violates the provision that a pre-recorded message must disconnect upon the caller hanging up the telephone.
