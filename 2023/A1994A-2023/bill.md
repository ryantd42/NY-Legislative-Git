# Prohibits discrimination of students on school property or at a school function based on religious attire, clothing or facial hair

**Bill ID:** A1994A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Charles Fall
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits discrimination of students on school property or at a school function based on religious attire, clothing or facial hair.
