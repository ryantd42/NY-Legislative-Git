# Commending Keith McNamara upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition by the Long Island Builders Institute

**Bill ID:** J730
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 730
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING Keith McNamara upon the occasion of his
  designation  for  special  recognition  by  the Long
  Island Builders Institute
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize  that
the  quality  and  character  of life in the communities across New York
State are reflective of the concerned and  dedicated  efforts  of  those
individuals  that  are  devoted  to the welfare of the community and its
citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Keith McNamara upon the occasion of his designation for  special
recognition  by  the  Long Island Builders Institute to be celebrated at
its 81st Annual Dinner Dance Gala on Friday, May 5, 2023, at  the  North
Hills Country Club; and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  this  auspicious  event,  Keith  McNamara  will be
honored for his many contributions to the building industry and for  his
steadfast  and  unremitting  commitment  to  the  Long  Island  Builders
Institute (LIBI) and his community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Keith McNamara joined the Long Island Builders Institute in
1998, and  shortly  thereafter,  became  active  in  various  committees
including  the  Trade  Expo,  Golf  Outing,  Membership,  and  Associate
Council; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 2021, Keith McNamara was elected to the  LIBI  Board  of
Directors  where  he currently holds an Associate seat; he also held the
title of Associate Council President in 2022, and now serves as its Vice
President; and
 
  WHEREAS, Keith McNamara supports Long Island Home Builders  Care  by
taking part in the Annual Walk A Thon and Holiday Magic, delivering toys
to  children  in  foster  care, and headed up the building of one of the
Children's  Custom  Playhouses  that  was  donated  to  the  New
Interdisciplinary School in Yaphank during the 2021 Trade Expo; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Keith  McNamara  earned  his Bachelor of Science degree in
business  and  economics  and  a  minor  in  marketing  from  the  State
University of New York at Oneonta; he has worked for Alside Supply since
2014,  and  most  recently,  holds  the title of District/Regional Sales
Manager; and
 
  WHEREAS, A resident of Centereach, New York, Keith  McNamara  enjoys
skiing, golfing and rooting for his favorite football team, the New York
Jets; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  him  throughout  have  been  his  wife and their two
daughters, all of whom feel privileged to be a  part  of  his  life  and
rejoice in his achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they  should be recognized and applauded by all the citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Keith McNamara upon the occasion of his designation for  special
recognition by the Long Island Builders Institute; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Keith McNamara.
