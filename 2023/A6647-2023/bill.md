# Requires a uniform processes for considering applications to purchase condominiums or cooperatives

**Bill ID:** A6647
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monique Chandler-Waterman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires a uniform process for considering applications to purchase condominiums or cooperatives.
