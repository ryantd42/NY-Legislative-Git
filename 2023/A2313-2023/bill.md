# Relates to on duty auxiliary police officers

**Bill ID:** A2313
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to an increase in punishment for certain actions against on-duty auxiliary police officers such as criminally negligent homicide, assault or menacing of such officer.

  A  PERSON  IS GUILTY OF ASSAULT ON AN ON-DUTY AUXILIARY POLICE OFFICER

 § 120.19 MENACING AN ON-DUTY AUXILIARY POLICE OFFICER.
  A  PERSON  IS  GUILTY  OF MENACING AN ON-DUTY AUXILIARY POLICE OFFICER
