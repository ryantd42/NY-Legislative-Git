# Provides that the commissioner of environmental conservation shall place and operate certain air quality monitoring systems in proximity to N.Y. city airports

**Bill ID:** A666
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A666 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A666)

## Summary

Provides that the commissioner of environmental conservation shall place and operate certain air quality monitoring systems in proximity to NY city airports.
