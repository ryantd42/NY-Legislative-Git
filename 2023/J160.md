## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 160
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  MOURNING  the  death  of  Harriet  Marlene  Rock
  Gooding, distinguished citizen and dedicated  member
  of her community
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of this Legislative Body to pay proper
tribute to individuals of great  character  whose  lives  exemplify  the
highest ideals of humanity; and
 
  WHEREAS, Harriet Marlene Rock Gooding of Brooklyn, New York, died on
Tuesday, December 13, 2022, at the age of 66; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Harriet Marlene Rock Gooding was a warm and kind woman who
truly lived life to the fullest; and
 
  WHEREAS, Harriet Marlene Rock  Gooding  was  a  cherished  daughter;
loving  wife;  devoted  mother,  grandmother,  and godmother; and caring
sister and aunt; she was always ready and willing to lend a helping hand
to one of her treasured family members or friends; and
 
  WHEREAS, Predeceased by her mother, Gloria Rock Hughes, and husband,
John Gooding, Harriet Marlene Rock Gooding is survived by her two  sons,
Kerron  Rock  and  Shane  Gooding; one granddaughter, Amiah Gooding; one
daughter-in-law, Channel Isidore; five sisters, Margaret Fagan, Danielle
Casimir, Juliet Charles, Roslyn Jones and Michele Lashley; two brothers,
Earle Charles and David Cadogan; and three godchildren,  Jamila  Cometa,
Karima Fuentes and Randi Tescum; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Harriet  Marlene  Rock  Gooding  was also survived by five
aunts, Marva Lewis, Emlyn Pilgrim, Linda  Mathias,  Sheila  Murrell  and
Hyacinth  Mullin;  numerous  cousins, Kerry, Shemise, Hakeem, Railin and
Paige Worrell; Ian Rock; Marilyn and  Dwayne  Francis;  Phyllis  Harvey;
Harriet  and Rhonda Johnson; Ivor Boyce; Derrick Rock; Carmichael, Isaac
and Phyllis Jones; Jocelyn, Hamilton, Jarret,  Jahswad,  Franklyn,  Kim,
Kianna,  Ellyn and Antoinette Rock; Hyacinth Bourne; Marina, Andrew, and
Mark Jemmott; Jerry, Tony, Jaelyn, and Taegyn Babb; and  Rawle,  Coleen,
and Natasha Agard; as well as many others; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  remarkable  woman  was  also  related  to the Agard,
Austin, Boyce, Burnett, Collymore, Greaves, Griffith, Reid and  Yearwood
families;  and  friend  to  Deshanna Scott, Damion Harris, Mayorie Clue,
Andy Spencer,  Chantel  Isidore,  Viola  Taylor,  Linda  Taylor-Roberts,
Bashkim  Aruci,  Marilyn Nelson, Erick Daly, Devin Casimir, Kemp Moriah,
Stacy White, Shavon Thompson and a multitude of  others  who  will  long
cherish her memory; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Armed  with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of
compassion, Harriet Marlene Rock Gooding leaves behind  a  legacy  which
will  long  endure  the  passage of time and will remain as a comforting
memory to all she served and befriended; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
mourn  the  death of Harriet Marlene Rock Gooding, distinguished citizen
and dedicated member of her community; and be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the family of Harriet Marlene Rock Gooding.
