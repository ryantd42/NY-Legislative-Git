# Mourning the death of Jazmine Chandler-Tabb, devoted wife and mother, and distinguished member of her community

**Bill ID:** J965
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 965
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  MOURNING  the  death  of  Jazmine  Chandler-Tabb,
  devoted wife and mother, and distinguished member of
  her community
 
  WHEREAS, There are certain outstanding members of our community who,
through their selfless commitment and dedication, have served to  better
the  quality of life in our community and have had a measurable positive
impact on the lives of its residents; Jazmine Chandler-Tabb was one such
individual; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is with  profound  sorrow  and  deep  regret  that  this
Legislative  Body  records  the passing of Jazmine Chandler-Tabb, noting
the significance of her purposeful life and accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Jazmine Chandler-Tabb of the Bronx, New York, died on April
7, 2023, at the age of 36; and
 
  WHEREAS, Born to Paulette Chandler and Lionel  Douglin  on  May  27,
1986,  Jazmine  Chandler-Tabb  graduated  in  2005  from Bishop Loughlin
Memorial High School where she was a dedicated scholar, devoted  friend,
and standout basketball player; and
 
  WHEREAS,  After earning a college scholarship, Jazmine Chandler-Tabb
went on to attend Virginia State University, joining its  Chi  Gamma  Xi
Multicultural  Sorority  and  continuing  to  build  her  everlasting
friendships; and
 
  WHEREAS, Jazmine Chandler-Tabb and the  love  of  her  life,  Natiya
"Nikki"  Joseph,  were  united  in marriage on May 3, 2021, and together
they raised their daughter, Aniya; and
 
  WHEREAS, With a steadfast and unwavering commitment to local  youth,
Jazmine  Chandler-Tabb  worked as a Sports-based youth development coach
at Urban Dove Charter School; she was inspired by her students  who  she
truly loved; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In addition, this talented young woman was an entrepreneur
and ran two companies, A Wells Security and Inner Child Network; and
 
  WHEREAS, Renowned for her smile, stature, sense of humor, big  hugs,
and  beautiful hazel brown eyes, Jazmine Chandler-Tabb's inviting spirit
and positive energy have left a lasting impression on  all  those  lucky
enough to have known her; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Jazmine  Chandler-Tabb's  infinite  selflessness  and
benevolence will shine on through her family's vivid and happy memories;
her insight and strength will forever serve as a beacon of  love,  light
and hope to the countless lives she touched; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Armed  with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of
compassion, Jazmine Chandler-Tabb leaves behind a legacy which will long
endure the passage of time and will remain as a comforting memory to all
who were privileged to have known and loved such an amazing  woman;  she
 
will  be  deeply  missed  and  truly merits the grateful tribute of this
Legislative Body; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
mourn the death of Jazmine Chandler-Tabb, and  to  express  its  deepest
condolences to her family; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the family of Jazmine Chandler-Tabb.
