# Relates to prohibiting the state's participation in artificial pheasant propagation activities

**Bill ID:** A508
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A508 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A508)

## Summary

Prohibits the state's participation in artificial pheasant propagation activities; closes any state-owned or operated pheasant production facility.
