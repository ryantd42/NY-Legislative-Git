# Extends certain provisions relating to expanding the child health insurance plan

**Bill ID:** A7425
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karines Reyes
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends certain provisions relating to expanding the child health insurance plan until 2025.
