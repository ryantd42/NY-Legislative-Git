# Modernizes the statutory peace officer designation of certain employees of the Triborough bridge and tunnel authority

**Bill ID:** S796
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S796 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S796)

## Summary

Modernizes the statutory peace officer designation for sworn officers of the Triborough bridge and tunnel authority.
