# Requires insurance coverage for medical genetic testing for adoptees and persons placed in foster care

**Bill ID:** S7303
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires insurance coverage for medical genetic testing for adoptees and persons placed in foster care.
