# Expands the description of certain services which are not prohibited by statutes governing the practice of nursing

**Bill ID:** A7704A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karen McMahon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Expands the description of certain services which are not prohibited by statutes governing the practice of nursing, including direct support staff to nurses.

  (V)  TASKS  PROVIDED  BY  DIRECT  SUPPORT  STAFF IN NON-FACILITY BASED

 TASKS  MAY BE PERFORMED BY DIRECT SUPPORT STAFF PURSUANT TO SUBPARAGRAPH
 (VI) OF THIS PARAGRAPH SUBJECT TO THE REQUIREMENTS SET FORTH THEREIN; OR
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
