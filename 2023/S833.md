# Amends the effectiveness of a chapter of the laws of 2022 amending the insurance law relating to the provision of supplemental spousal liability insurance

**Bill ID:** S833
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S833 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S833)

## Summary

Amends the effectiveness of a chapter of the laws of 2022 amending the insurance law relating to the provision of supplemental spousal liability insurance to persons who are insured for loss resulting from the ownership, maintenance and use of a motor vehicle.
