# Relates to decriminalizing the possession of buprenorphine

**Bill ID:** A4013A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Decriminalizes the possession of buprenorphine from certain crimes.
