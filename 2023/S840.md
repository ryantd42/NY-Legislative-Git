# Relates to the percentage responsibility of the state for federally assisted projects

**Bill ID:** S840
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S840 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S840)

## Summary

Relates to the percentage responsibility of the state for federally assisted projects; relates to the state share of municipal projects where the municipality funds a complete street design.
