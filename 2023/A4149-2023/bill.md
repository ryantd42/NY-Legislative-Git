# Requires public libraries to carry opioid antagonists and requires training for staff in the administration of opioid antagonists

**Bill ID:** A4149
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires public libraries to carry opioid antagonists; requires training for staff in the administration of opioid antagonists.
