# Relates to safety and quality labels on certain foods and beverages

**Bill ID:** A6596
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karines Reyes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires perishable and packaged food to be clearly marked with safety and quality dates.
