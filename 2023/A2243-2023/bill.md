# Relates to prohibiting the sale or purchase of minor children

**Bill ID:** A2243
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the sale or purchase of minor children; makes such crime a class B felony.
