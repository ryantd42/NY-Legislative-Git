# Authorizes cities and villages to collect delinquent real property taxes

**Bill ID:** A1634
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1634 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1634)

## Summary

Authorizes cities and villages to collect delinquent real property taxes.
