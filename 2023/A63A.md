# Enacts the "total access to menstrual products (TAMP) act"

**Bill ID:** A63AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A63A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A63A)

## Summary

Requires that all female-designated and gender-neutral bathrooms in the state of New York provide menstrual products at no cost.
