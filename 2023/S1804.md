# Requires gas and electric corporations to provide sixty days' notice to customers whenever there is a service rate or charge increase

**Bill ID:** S1804
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** Vetoed
**PDF:** [S1804 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1804)

## Summary

Requires gas and electric corporations to provide sixty days' notice to customers whenever there is a service rate or charge increase.
