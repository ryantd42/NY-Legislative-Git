# Relates to certain payments to the horsemen's organization

**Bill ID:** S2076
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S2076 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2076)

## Summary

Relates to certain payments to the horsemen's organization; extends such provisions to 2024.
