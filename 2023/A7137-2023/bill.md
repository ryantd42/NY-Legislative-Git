# Requires the Thruway Authority to provide at least twelve charging stations at each rest area and gas station complex

**Bill ID:** A7137
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jonathan Jacobson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the Thruway Authority to provide at least twelve charging stations at each rest area and gas station complex owned, leased or operated by the Thruway Authority.
