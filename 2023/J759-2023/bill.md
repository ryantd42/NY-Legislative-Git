# Honoring Dr. Franklin Caesar upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the Father John Foley Vision of Cristo Rey Award on May 9, 2023

**Bill ID:** J759
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 759
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING  Dr. Franklin Caesar upon the occasion of
  his designation as  recipient  of  the  Father  John
  Foley Vision of Cristo Rey Award on May 9, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the sense of this Legislative Body to take note of
and  publicly  acknowledge  individuals  of  remarkable  integrity  and
character whose endeavors have enhanced the quality of education in this
great State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Dr. Franklin Caesar upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of
the Father John Foley Vision of Cristo Rey Award, to  be  celebrated  at
Cristo  Rey  Brooklyn High School's Annual Gala on Tuesday, May 9, 2023;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Born in Grenada, West Indies, Franklin Caesar emigrated  to
Brooklyn, New York in 1956; he was educated as an English teacher; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Franklin  Caesar  began  his  teaching  career at his alma
mater, Xavier High School, where he held a myriad of  instructional  and
leadership roles; and
 
  WHEREAS,  While  at  Xavier,  Franklin  Caesar  earned his doctorate
degree in Education at Fordham University; he went on to  teach  at  the
Fordham  Graduate  School of Education as an Adjunct Assistant Professor
for over a decade; and
 
  WHEREAS, Moving on from Xavier  High  School,  Dr.  Franklin  Caesar
worked  in  the  Central  Islip  School  District  serving  as Assistant
Principal and Principal at the primary and  secondary  levels;  he  also
served  as President of the Administrators' Union and Senior Director of
Secondary School Curriculum; and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore,  to  recognize  his  tireless  and  unwavering
commitment  to  his  students,  Dr.  Franklin  Caesar  received the Bene
Merenti Medal to commemorate his long and exceptional service to  Xavier
High School; in 1995, he was inducted into the Xavier Hall of Fame; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  2021, the Board of Trustees of Xavier High School also
honored Dr. Franklin Caesar by having his portrait  commissioned;  later
that year, the Dr. Franklin N. Caesar Conference Room was dedicated; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Within  every  community,  there  are  inevitably  some
individuals who, by virtue of  their  energy,  vision,  and  dedication,
command  the  greatest  respect  among their friends and colleagues, and
within  the  community  at  large;  Dr.  Franklin  Caesar  is  one  such
individual; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Brooklyn community is indeed fortunate and blessed to
have such a dedicated and respected educational leader; his  wisdom  and
contributions  reach  into every classroom, truly making a difference in
the lives of those lucky enough to work with him; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  A  significant contributor to education in New York State,
Dr.  Franklin Caesar continually demonstrates an impressive  dedication,
an  unparalleled  devotion and an unremitting enthusiasm for the quality
of the educational programs and  opportunities  offered  at  Cristo  Rey
Brooklyn  High  School  for  the benefit of its faculty and students, as
well as for the entire community; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Dr.  Franklin  Caesar  upon  the  occasion  of his designation as
recipient of the Father John Foley Vision of Cristo Rey Award on May  9,
2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Dr. Franklin Caesar.
