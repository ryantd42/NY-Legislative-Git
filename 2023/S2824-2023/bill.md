# Allows for distinctive plates to be issued for members of the New York city fire riders motorcycle club

**Bill ID:** S2824
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Allows for distinctive plates to be issued for members of the New York city fire riders motorcycle club.
