# Provides that the results of urine tests conducted pursuant to a judicial diversion program shall be private and confidential

**Bill ID:** A6830B
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that the results of urine tests conducted pursuant to a judicial diversion program shall be private and confidential.
