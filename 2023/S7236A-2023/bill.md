# Relates to improving student mental health and mental health literacy in institutions of higher education in the state, and requiring the amendment or adoption of related protocols, policies and programs

**Bill ID:** S7236AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Samra Brouk
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to improving student mental health and mental health literacy in institutions of higher education in the state; requires the amendment or adoption of related protocols, policies and programs in SUNY, CUNY and independent institutions of higher education.

  § 237-A. STUDENT MENTAL HEALTH, MENTAL HEALTH LITERACY AND TRAINING IN
 MASTER  PLANS  OF  INDEPENDENT  INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION. 1. THE
 REGENTS PLAN FOR HIGHER EDUCATION, REQUIRED TO BE DEVELOPED  AND  TRANS-

 STUDENTS  EXPERIENCING MENTAL HEALTH ISSUES OR CRISES; IMPROVE RESPONSES
 TO MENTAL HEALTH NEEDS AND CRISES; REVIEW AND IMPROVE SUICIDE PREVENTION
 AND INTERVENTION POLICIES; AND PROVIDE APPROPRIATE STAFF TRAINING TO ALL
 FULL-TIME FACULTY, ADMINISTRATORS, ATHLETIC COACHES, STUDENT-FACING  AND
 SUPPORT STAFF, AS WELL AS ADJUNCT PROFESSORS, TO IMPLEMENT THE ABOVE-RE-
 FERENCED PROTOCOLS AND POLICIES.

 TY, THE CITY UNIVERSITY, OR THE DEPARTMENT AND CONSIDER WHETHER TO ADOPT
 SUCH PROTOCOLS AND POLICIES, IN WHOLE OR IN PART, FOR INCLUSION IN THEIR
 MASTER PLANS, FOR PURPOSES OF IMPROVING STUDENT  MENTAL  HEALTH,  MENTAL
 HEALTH LITERACY, AND RESPONSES TO MENTAL HEALTH NEEDS AND CRISES IN SUCH
 INSTITUTIONS.
