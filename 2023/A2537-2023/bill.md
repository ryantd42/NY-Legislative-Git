# Provides for the reimbursement of costs of the New York environmental protection and spill compensation fund in the department of audit and control

**Bill ID:** A2537
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Colton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the reimbursement of costs of the New York environmental protection and spill compensation fund in the department of audit and control.
