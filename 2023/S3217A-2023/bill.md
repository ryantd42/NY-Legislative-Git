# Requires exterior cages for air conditioners

**Bill ID:** S3217A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires exterior cages for air conditioners installed in any multiple dwelling.
