# Extends provisions allowing the county of Fulton to impose a mortgage recording tax

**Bill ID:** A7118
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Smullen
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends provisions allowing the county of Fulton to impose a county recording tax on obligation secured by a mortgage on real property.
