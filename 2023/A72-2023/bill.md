# Relates to creating a bridge suicide barrier fund

**Bill ID:** A72
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nader Sayegh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A72 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A72)

## Summary

Creates a fund to be used to research certain bridges being equipped with suicide barriers.
