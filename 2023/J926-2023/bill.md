# Commending the Capitol Theatre of Rome, New York upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Rome Arts Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony on May 7, 2023

**Bill ID:** J926
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 926
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMENDING  the  Capitol Theatre of Rome, New York
  upon the occasion of hosting its  Annual  Rome  Arts
  Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony on May 7, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to honor and pay
tribute  to  those  cultural  institutions  and  organizations  whose
commitment  to  promoting  the  richness  and  diversity of the arts and
entertainment world has  contributed  to  the  strength,  vitality,  and
cultural  enrichment  of  their  communities  and the great State of New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the Capitol Theatre of Rome,  New  York  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its Annual Rome Arts Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony on Sunday,
May 7, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Capitol Civic Center, Inc. is a nonprofit organization;
its mission is to provide cultural and educational opportunities to  the
community  through  the  performing  and visual arts in Rome, New York's
historic 1928 theater and auxiliary event spaces; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Rome Arts Hall of Fame began in 2005 with a mission  to
honor  individuals,  living or deceased, who have a strong connection to
the Rome community and have a significant involvement in the performing,
visual or literary arts; the class of 2023 is the sixteenth group to  be
named to the Rome Arts Hall of Fame; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A performance by Rome Free Academy's show choir "Rhapsody"
will be a featured part of this year's program; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of the Capitol Theatre  of  Rome  is  a  direct
result  to the unceasing efforts of the staff, the surrounding community
at large, and the devotion of  each  Capitol  Board  Member:  President,
Steve Merren; Vice President, Vicky Stockton; Treasurer, Doug Swarthout;
Secretary,  Norm  Landis;  and Members, James Genovese, John A. Herbowy,
Esq., Maya Nelson, Joe Pfeiffer, Jr., Maria Rich, Roberta  Striker,  Ray
Tucker, Gail Tucker, and Dr. Mitchell Wilbert; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The arts are the building blocks of civilization; they are
central  to  human  expression;  they  enlighten  us  and  inspire  the
imagination;  educational  institutions,  individuals, and organizations
which further such worthy purposes  richly  merit  the  recognition  and
appreciation of this Legislative Body and the people of the State of New
York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Capitol Theatre of Rome,  New  York  upon  the  occasion  of
hosting  its  Annual Rome Arts Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony on May 7,
2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Capitol Theatre of Rome, New York.
