# Relates to certain training for real estate brokers

**Bill ID:** A7325
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires VA home loan training in the required training for real estate brokers.
