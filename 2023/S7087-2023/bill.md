# Provides for administration of certain funds for zero-emission school buses and supporting infrastructure

**Bill ID:** S7087
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for administration of certain funds for zero-emission school buses and supporting infrastructure by NYSERDA.
