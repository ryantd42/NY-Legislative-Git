# Honoring Trooper Francis W. Rush IV upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the Brummer Award by the New York State Police on May 17, 2023

**Bill ID:** K428
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Matthew Slater
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 428
 
BY: M. of A. Slater
 
  HONORING  Trooper  Francis  W.  Rush  IV  upon the
  occasion of his  designation  as  recipient  of  the
  Brummer  Award  by  the New York State Police on May
  17, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is incumbent upon the people of the State of New York to
recognize  and  acknowledge  those  within  our  midst  who  have  made
significant contributions to the quality of life therein; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to honor Trooper
Francis W. Rush IV upon the occasion of his designation as recipient  of
the  Brummer  Award by the New York State Police to be celebrated at its
47th Annual Superintendent's Commendation and  Awards  Day  Ceremony  on
Wednesday,  May  17,  2023,  at  the State Police Academy in Albany, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, This prestigious award was established to honor outstanding
individuals, dedicated  to  their  service  to  law  enforcement,  whose
valiant efforts protect the people of New York State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Trooper  Francis  W.  Rush IV acted bravely in the line of
duty to save the life of a young driver in a submerged vehicle in August
of 2022; his decisive  and  life-saving  actions  exemplify  the  finest
traditions of New York State Police; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefit of others which Trooper Francis  W.  Rush  IV
has displayed throughout his life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body that those who
enhance the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown  a
long  and  sustained  commitment to excellence certainly have earned the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor Trooper Francis W. Rush IV upon the occasion of his designation as
recipient  of  the Brummer Award by the New York State Police on May 17,
2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Trooper Francis W. Rush IV.
