# Prohibits certain fees from being imposed by licensed wholesalers

**Bill ID:** A2482
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Prohibits certain fees from being imposed by licensed wholesalers on licensed retailers.
