# Commending the Golden Compass Club of Master Craftsman Lodge No. 110 of the Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge of New York upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Awards Banquet on August 19, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1308
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1308
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Golden  Compass  Club  of  Master
  Craftsman Lodge  No.  110  of  the  Most  Worshipful
  Prince  Hall  Grand  Lodge  of  New  York  upon  the
  occasion of hosting its  Annual  Awards  Banquet  on
  August 19, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay  tribute  to  those  organizations  of  high  purpose  and  worthy
accomplishment whose endeavors have faithfully served the welfare of the
citizens of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the  Golden Compass Club of Master Craftsman Lodge No. 110 upon
the occasion of hosting its Annual Awards Banquet  on  Saturday,  August
19, 2023, at Woodlawn Volunteer Fire Hall, Buffalo, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  dedicated  Masons  of the Most Worshipful Prince Hall
Grand Lodge of  New  York  are  men  with  a  social  conscience  and  a
charitable  perspective, who are also known as Freemasons; they are part
of a fraternal organization that shares common moral ideals and esoteric
values which are based on the doctrine of  Brotherly  Love,  Relief  and
Truth; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Golden Compass Club of Master Craftsman Lodge No. 110
of the Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge of  New  York  has  truly
demonstrated  continuous  service to the residents and communities which
it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, Established by a group of like-minded young men,  the  club
was  formed  in  August  of  1973,  and  its Dispensation was granted on
October 24, 1974 by Most Worshipful Grand Master Charles McGarrah; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Golden Compass Club of Master Craftsman Lodge  No.  110
received  its  Warrant  of  Constitution  on  June  14,  1978,  by  Most
Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Master William Richardson; ceremonies  were
done  verbally  by Right Worshipful Solomon Wallace and Right Worshipful
Thomas Swan; and
 
  WHEREAS, Master Craftsman Lodge No. 110 conducted its first  meeting
after being chartered on the fourth Wednesday in July of 1978, at Bethel
AME  Church  in Lackawanna, New York; its Principal Officers at the time
of being chartered were  Worshipful  Master  Earl  E.  Saunders,  Senior
Warden Edward M. Tally, and Junior Warden Fred Harge; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Today,  the  success  of the Golden Compass Club of Master
Craftsman Lodge No. 110 is in  direct  correlation  to  the  efforts  of
Worshipful  Master  Shad Miles, Senior Warden Joshua Hutchen Sr., Junior
Warden Samuel Walker Jr., and its dedicated members,  whose  involvement
is,  and  always has been, characterized by an impressive commitment, an
unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard  of  excellence  in
all endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves;
and
 
 
  WHEREAS, The Freemasons of this Grand Lodge are a standing testament
to  resilience,  strength,  and  unity  through  a  common  sense  of
brotherhood; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is proudly moved to honor and commend
the members of the Golden Compass Club of  Master  Craftsman  Lodge  No.
110, past and present, who have labored so conscientiously to secure and
improve the quality of life for the community it serves; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Golden Compass Club of Master Craftsman Lodge No. 110 of the
Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge of New York upon the occasion of
hosting its Annual Awards Banquet on August 19, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Golden Compass Club of Master Craftsman Lodge No. 110
of the Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge of New York.
