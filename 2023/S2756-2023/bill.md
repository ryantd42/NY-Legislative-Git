# Provides for issuing non-driver identification cards to certain persons with suspended driving privileges

**Bill ID:** S2756
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Issues non-driver identification cards to certain persons with suspended driving privileges due to a driving while intoxicated arrest.
