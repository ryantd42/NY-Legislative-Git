# Establishes the misdemeanor of interfering in the election process by electronic means

**Bill ID:** A1344
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Gina Sillitti
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1344 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1344)

## Summary

Establishes the misdemeanor of interfering in the election process by electronic means.
