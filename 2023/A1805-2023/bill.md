# Relates to prohibiting tinted windows in sport utility vehicles

**Bill ID:** A1805
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits tinted windows in sport utility vehicles.
