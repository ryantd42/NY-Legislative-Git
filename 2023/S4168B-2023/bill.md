# Includes the Shelter Island Public Library Society with libraries eligible for the financing of projects through the dormitory authority

**Bill ID:** S4168B
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Includes the Shelter Island Public Library Society with libraries eligible for the financing of projects through the dormitory authority.
