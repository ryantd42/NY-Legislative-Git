# Provides standards for the number of water closets in certain areas of public assembly and increases the required number of water closets for women

**Bill ID:** S6841
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides standards for the number of water closets in certain areas of public assembly; increases the required number of water closets for women; sets deadlines for compliance.
