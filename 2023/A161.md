# Establishes a forestry stewardship and habitat conservation credit for personal income and business franchise taxes

**Bill ID:** A161
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A161 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A161)

## Summary

Establishes a forestry stewardship and habitat conservation credit for personal income and business franchise taxes.
