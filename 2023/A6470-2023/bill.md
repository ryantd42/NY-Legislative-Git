# Authorizes and requires the board of regents to set acceptable standards for the issuance of diplomas to students who are homeschooled

**Bill ID:** A6470
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monique Chandler-Waterman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes and requires the board of regents to set acceptable standards for the issuance of diplomas to students who are homeschooled.
