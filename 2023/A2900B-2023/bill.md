# Directs the city comptroller of the city of New York to conduct annual audits of compliance with the affordable New York housing program

**Bill ID:** A2900BB
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Emily Gallagher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs the city comptroller of the city of New York to conduct annual audits of compliance with the affordable New York housing program.
