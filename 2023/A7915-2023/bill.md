# Prohibits insurers from requiring a property owner to disclose the presence of tenants receiving rental assistance

**Bill ID:** A7915
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits insurers from requiring a property owner to disclose the presence of tenants receiving rental assistance.
