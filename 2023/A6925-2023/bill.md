# Relates to removing cause for absentee ballot voting

**Bill ID:** A6925
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes ballot by mail by removing cause for absentee ballot voting.
