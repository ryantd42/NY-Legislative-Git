# Requires a New York state legend on all bell jar tickets sold in New York state

**Bill ID:** A3519
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires a New York state legend on all bell jar tickets sold in New York state.
