# Relates to aid for career education

**Bill ID:** S1121
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1121 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1121)

## Summary

Authorizes aid for career education to begin in grade nine instead of grade ten; changes the amount per pupil.
