# Prohibits disposal of spoils containing toxic pollutants into the waters of the marine district

**Bill ID:** S2808
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the disposal of any dredged spoils containing toxic pollutants into the waters of the marine district.
