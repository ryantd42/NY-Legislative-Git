# Permits on-premises retail licensees to purchase up to twelve bottles of wine and liquor per week from off-premises retail licensees

**Bill ID:** A3071
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Permits on-premises retail licensees to purchase up to twelve bottles of wine and liquor per week from off-premises retail licensees.
