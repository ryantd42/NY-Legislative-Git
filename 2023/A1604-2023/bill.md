# Relates to owner liability for failure of operator to move over when approaching a police, fire, or emergency vehicle

**Bill ID:** A1604
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1604 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1604)

## Summary

Relates to owner liability for failure of operator to move over when approaching a police, fire, or emergency vehicle.
