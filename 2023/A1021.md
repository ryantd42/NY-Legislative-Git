# Makes an appropriation to the Island Park School District to offset a loss of tax revenue

**Bill ID:** A1021
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Eric Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1021 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1021)

## Summary

Makes an appropriation of seven million dollars over seven years to the Island Park School District for the purpose of stabilizing the Island Park School District and offsetting a loss of tax revenue.
