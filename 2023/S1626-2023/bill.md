# Sets a cap on penalties for certain unpaid real property tax due and payable or remaining unpaid

**Bill ID:** S1626
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1626 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1626)

## Summary

Sets a state-wide three percent cap on penalties for unpaid real property tax due and payable or remaining unpaid during the period from March 7, 2020 to June 30, 2021.
