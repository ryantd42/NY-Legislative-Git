# Requires student suicide prevention training and policies in public school districts

**Bill ID:** A1613
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela J. Hunter
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1613 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1613)

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of education to adopt rules to require that all public school personnel receive at least two hours of suicide awareness and prevention training each year and each public school district to adopt a policy on student suicide prevention.
