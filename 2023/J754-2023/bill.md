# Honoring Gary Edwards upon the occasion of being inducted into the National Wrestling Hall of Fame on September 24, 2023

**Bill ID:** J754
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 754
 
BY: Senator STEC
 
  HONORING  Gary  Edwards upon the occasion of being
  inducted into the National Wrestling Hall of Fame on
  September 24, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
outstanding athletes who have  distinguished  themselves  through  their
exceptional performance, attaining unprecedented success and the highest
level of personal achievement; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Gary Edwards upon the occasion  of  being  inducted  into  the  National
Wrestling  Hall  of  Fame,  to  be celebrated on Saturday, September 24,
2023, at the Double Tree Hotel in East Syracuse, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Upstate New York Chapter of the National Wrestling Hall
of Fame is dedicated to the main goals of the  National  Hall  of  Fame:
recognizing  excellence  in  wrestling  in  the  Upstate  New York area;
preserving the rich and colorful history of  wrestling  in  Upstate  New
York;  and  lastly,  inspiring  future  generations  of  New  Yorkers to
participate in one of the oldest and respected sports in the world; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 1961, Gary Edwards began  wrestling  as  a  junior  high
student at Peru Central School; since then, he has spent the majority of
his life dedicated to the sport; and
 
  WHEREAS,  His  successful  career  deeming  him  a  legendary  area
wrestling coach, Gary Edwards was an assistant coach to Coach Riggs, the
first Peru wrestling coach to take his  teams  out  of  the  section  to
compete, starting in 1977; during his time under Riggs, the team won 122
matches in its longest winning streak; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Gary  Edwards  served  as  co-head  coach  to Peru Central
School's wrestling team for 21 years and  has  lead  the  team  to  many
Section  VII  individual  championships,  state  finals,  and  NYSPHSAA
Tournaments; and
 
  WHEREAS, For over 30 years, Gary Edwards  has  also  served  as  the
Champlain  Valley  Athletic Conference wrestling chairman and as Section
VII chairman for the past 17 years; and
 
  WHEREAS, Selflessly devoting his time to youth in Peru, Gary Edwards
enjoys taking pictures for the wrestlers in their matches and  at  state
competitions; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Retiring  from  Peru Central School District in 2002, Gary
Edwards still remains actively involved in the community and substitutes
for the school today; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to playing sports, Gary Edwards has served  the
young people of his community for many years as a coach of numerous Peru
youth  sports programs including coaching junior varsity boys soccer for
 
four years, varsity boys soccer for 22 years, and also assisted  on  the
boys soccer team and the boys and girls track & field teams; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  him throughout have been his wife Nancy Edwards, who
has felt privileged to be  a  part  of  his  life  and  rejoice  in  his
achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  his life thus far, Gary Edwards has graciously
given countless hours of his time and energy to the residents of Peru as
a skilled and inspirational tutor, respected for his ability to  develop
potential into excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Gary Edwards has clearly made a contribution to the spirit
of excellence which is a tradition of his Peru community; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of such outstanding athletic accomplishments are brought to
our attention, they should be recognized by all  the  citizens  of  this
great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Gary Edwards upon the occasion of being inducted into the National
Wrestling Hall of Fame on September 24, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Gary Edwards.
