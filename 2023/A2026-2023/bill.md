# Prohibits the sale of mature and violent video games to minors

**Bill ID:** A2026
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the sale to minors of any video game that has a mature or violent rating.
