# Relates to the designation of workers' compensation coverage for certain employees

**Bill ID:** A3531
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits workers compensation carriers from forcing policyholders to cover individuals who are not their employees.
