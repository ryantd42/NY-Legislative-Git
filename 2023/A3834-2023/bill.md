# Relates to the rededication of unused funds in the New York state local government records management improvement fund to such fund

**Bill ID:** A3834
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the mandatory rededication of unused funds in the New York state local government records management improvement fund to such fund.
