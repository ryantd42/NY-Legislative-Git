# Congratulating the Mount Sinai High School Varsity Cheerleading Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Division II Small Schools Cheerleading Championship

**Bill ID:** K406
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jodi Giglio
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 406
 
BY: M. of A. Giglio JA
 
  CONGRATULATING the Mount Sinai High School Varsity
  Cheerleading Team upon the occasion of capturing the
  NYSPHSAA  Division  II  Small  Schools  Cheerleading
  Championship on March 4, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
Mount  Sinai  High School Varsity Cheerleading Team upon the occasion of
capturing their fifth NYSPHSAA Division II  Small  Schools  Cheerleading
Championship  on  Saturday,  March  4,  2023,  at  the  Visions Veterans
Memorial Arena in Binghamton, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The superlative Mount Sinai Cheerleading  program  has  won
more  state  titles  than  any  other sport in school history, and Coach
Megan Wesolowski was named the 2023 Suffolk Coach of the Year; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's  colors  of  red,  black,  and
white,  family,  friends,  and  the  community  at  large  loyally  and
enthusiastically supported the Mustangs throughout their journey as they
ended their season with an impressive overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as competitive cheerleading, which  demands
athletic  prowess,  strength  and agility, Head Coaches Megan Wesolowski
and Matthew Kenefick and Assistant Coach Casey Wesolowski worked hard to
hone the skills of this championship team,  teaching  these  outstanding
athletes  lessons  which  will prove invaluable both on and off the mat;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coaches Megan Wesolowski and Matthew Kenefick and  all
of  the  outstanding  athletes  on  the  Mount Sinai High School Varsity
Cheerleading Team have clearly utilized  dedication,  determination  and
teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence
which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate  the  members  of  the  Mount  Sinai  High  School  Varsity
Cheerleading  Team:  Rosalie  Calamas,  Hailey  Carrera,  Laila  Cuocco,
Rosalie Eletto, Alana Fragoletti, Ava  Furbush,  Elizabeth  Fusco,  Lyla
Giarrizzo,  Lilah  Jurgens,  Ashley  Kandell,  Hailey  Loddigs,  Brooke
Napolitano, DeAnna Pesce, Addisyn  Romelien,  and  Olivia  Salmon;  Head
Coaches  Megan  Wesolowski  and  Matthew  Kenefick; and Assistant Coach,
Casey Wesolowski upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Division II
Small Schools Cheerleading Championship; and be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members  of  the  Mount  Sinai  High  School  Varsity
Cheerleading  Team,  Head  Coaches Megan Wesolowski and Matthew Kenefick
and Assistant Coach Casey Wesolowski.
