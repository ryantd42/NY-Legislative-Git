# Congratulating Ruby Elaine Weston Reid upon the occasion of celebrating her 100th Birthday

**Bill ID:** J767
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 767
 
BY: Senator COMRIE
 
  CONGRATULATING  Ruby  Elaine  Weston Reid upon the
  occasion of celebrating her 100th Birthday
 
  WHEREAS, Senior citizens bring a wealth of experience and  knowledge
to  the  increasingly  active  roles they play in today's society; their
past contributions and future participation are a  vital  part  of,  and
valuable asset to, the fabric of community life and activity; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the custom of this Legislative Body to commemorate
and recognize certain milestones celebrated by citizens  of  this  great
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  Ruby  Elaine  Weston  Reid  upon the occasion of her 100th
Birthday to be celebrated March 21, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Born to Joseph and Clarice Weston in Manhattan,  New  York,
Ruby  Elaine  Weston Reid and her family later moved to Queens where her
and her sister, Joyce, attended PS 40Q and then Jamaica High School; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ruby Elaine  Weston  Reid  attended  the  Lincoln  Hospital
School of Nursing and successfully became a registered nurse; she worked
as a head nurse in the emergency room of Queens Hospital Center and then
became a nursing supervisor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  She married her beloved husband Charles Reid in 1942, with
whom she had two sons and one daughter; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ruby Elaine Weston Reid was very  active  in  the  church's
Children's  Ministry,  stopping  only because of the pandemic in 2020 at
the age of 97; in addition, she loved gardening, cooking and hosted many
activities for the children in her community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout her years, she has loved gardening, cooking, and
hosting many activities for the children in  her  community;  she  is  a
devout  Seventh  Day  Adventist  Christian  and  has  been active in her
church's Children's Ministry; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ruby Elaine Weston  Reid,  with  her  zest  for  life,  has
inspired and enhanced the lives of her family and friends; and
 
  WHEREAS, This exceptional centenarian has experienced the incredible
joys  and  sorrows  characteristic  of  and  reserved for those with the
stamina and courage to savor a full life; and
 
  WHEREAS, Ruby Elaine Weston Reid has enriched  the  lives  of  those
around  her  through  her joyous and sincere love for others and through
the profound charm and wisdom which comes only from a fullness of years;
and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the intent  of  this  Legislative  Body  to  publicly
recognize  those  who  have  reached  such a remarkable age and who have
 
witnessed and celebrated the  innovations,  cultural  developments,  and
awesome  achievements  of  this  country  during the last century, while
themselves contributing to the  growth  and  excellence  of  this  great
Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Ruby Elaine Weston Reid upon the  occasion  of  celebrating
her 100th Birthday; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Ruby Elaine Weston Reid.
