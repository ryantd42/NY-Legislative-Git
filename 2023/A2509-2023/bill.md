# Places a moratorium on tax lien sales in N.Y. city due to the financial hardships placed on property owners as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic

**Bill ID:** A2509
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Erik Dilan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Places a moratorium on tax lien sales in a city with a population of one million or more due to the financial hardships placed on property owners as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.
