# Permits the electronic filing of papers in local criminal courts in certain instances

**Bill ID:** A3561
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Permits the electronic filing of papers in local criminal courts in certain instances.
