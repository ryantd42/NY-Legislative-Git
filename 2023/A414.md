# Relates to annual reports by lobbyists disclosing clients that they advocated on behalf of before any state or local governmental entity

**Bill ID:** A414
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A414 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A414)

## Summary

Requires lobbyists to provide annual reports disclosing clients that they have advocated on behalf of before any state or local governmental entity.
