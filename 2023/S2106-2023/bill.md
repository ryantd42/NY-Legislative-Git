# Relates to prohibiting wind turbines from being located in a certain area of the city of New York

**Bill ID:** S2106
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2106 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2106)

## Summary

Prohibits wind turbines from being located within 1500 feet of a residential area or commercial shopping district within the city of New York.
