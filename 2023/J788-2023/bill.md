# Honoring Dave Burrell upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the Chenango County Distinguished Citizen's Award on May 11, 2023

**Bill ID:** J788
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 788
 
BY: Senator OBERACKER
 
  HONORING  Dave  Burrell  upon  the occasion of his
  designation as  recipient  of  the  Chenango  County
  Distinguished Citizen's Award on May 11, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend those distinguished citizens who would devote their life's  work
and  purpose  to  community  service, contributing to the welfare of the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern  and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Dave  Burrell  upon  the occasion of his designation as recipient of the
Chenango County Distinguished  Citizen's  Award,  to  be  celebrated  on
Thursday,  May  11,  2023,  at  Canasawacta Country Club in Norwich, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dave Burrell has  been  the  Owner/President  of  Burrell's
Excavating,  Inc. since purchasing the business from his father, Douglas
Burrell, in 1984; this extraordinary man's commitment  to  family,  work
and  community  was  handed down by his father who served as a full-time
firefighter in the  City  of  Norwich,  and  owning  and  operating  two
businesses; and
 
  WHEREAS,  When  Dave  Burrell  purchased  the  business  nearly four
decades ago, he made the decision to concentrate on  excavating;  today,
along  with  his  son, Matthew, Burrell's Excavating, Inc., employs more
than 40 people with continued growth each year; and
 
  WHEREAS, A dedicated businessman, Dave Burrell's first love  is  for
his  family;  he  and  his beloved wife, Kim, were united in marriage in
1977, and together they raised their two wonderful children, Matthew and
Courtney, in Norwich, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Dave Burrell is steadfastly committed to Kim, to  Matt  and
his wife Alicia and their three children, Alivia, Gavin and Kate, and to
Courtney and her children, Cameron and Vivian, while honoring the memory
of their grandson David who passed away at 14 months old; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Furthermore,  he  considers  his  employees  family  and
attributes his success to the people he employs and does  not  take  his
success for granted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  a Norwich native, Dave Burrell gives tirelessly of his
time and energies to the local community; he proudly  supported  Norwich
Youth Sports, including Little League, when his children were young, and
continues  the  tradition  with  his grandchildren as a supporter of the
YMCA Camp Thompson, sponsoring the Gus Macker 3-on-3 Tournament and  the
YMCA Turkey Trot; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Additionally,  Dave  Burrell  supports the annual Toys for
Tots program and Hospice Plant Sale, helping in whatever way is  needed;
he  was  also  instrumental  in  bringing  Santa's  Hut back to downtown
Norwich for families to enjoy each holiday season; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  Under  his  able  leadership,  Burrell's  Excavating  was
recognized in 2008 as Small Business of the Year by  Commerce  Chenango;
Dave  was subsequently awarded the Commitment to Community Award in 2011
by Commerce Chenango  in  recognition  of  his  generosity,  leadership,
loyalty and determination; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefit of others which Dave  Burrell  has  displayed
throughout his life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  has always been the objective of this Legislative Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment  to the betterment of their communities, and it is the intent
of this Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this  tribute  to
Dave  Burrell,  that  future  generations  may  know  and appreciate his
admirable character, his many benevolent  deeds,  and  the  respect  and
esteem in which he is held by his peers; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Dave Burrell upon the occasion of his designation as recipient  of
the  Chenango  County  Distinguished  Citizen's  Award,  and to wish him
continued success in all his future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Dave Burrell.
