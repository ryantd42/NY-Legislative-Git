# Relates to the time election day workers must be at poll sites to prepare for the opening of polls

**Bill ID:** S2871
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to the time election day workers must be at poll sites to prepare for the opening of polls; one hour instead of one-half hour.
