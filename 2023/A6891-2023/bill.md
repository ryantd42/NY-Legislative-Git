# Relates to the regulation of live restraint cable devices to take wildlife

**Bill ID:** A6891
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the regulation of live restraint cable devices to take wildlife; authorizes regulations by the department of environmental conservation.

 AN ACT to amend the environmental conservation law, in relation  to  the
  regulation  of  live  restraint  cable  devices  to take wildlife; and

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1.  Subdivision  5  of section 11-1101 of the environmental
 conservation law is amended to read as follows:
  5. A. Except as provided in subdivision 1 or 2 of section  11-0523  OF
 THIS  ARTICLE  with  respect  to  destructive  and menacing wildlife, no
 person shall (a) set or use a trap of the leg-gripping type having teeth
 in the jaws; (b) set a LEG HOLD trap so that  wildlife  when  caught  is
 suspended;  (c)  use,  locate or set a snare or a device consisting of a
 noose of any material whereby an animal may be taken.
  B. NOTWITHSTANDING THE PROVISIONS OF PARAGRAPH A OF THIS  SUBDIVISION,
 THE  DEPARTMENT  MAY, BY REGULATION, AUTHORIZE THE USE OF LIVE RESTRAINT
 CABLE DEVICES TO TAKE COYOTE AND OTHER SPECIES AS ALLOWED BY THE DEPART-
 MENT IN THE STATE.  THE USE OF LIVE RESTRAINT CABLE DEVICES  SHALL  ONLY
 BE  PERMITTED FOR A PERIOD OF TIME SPECIFIED BY THE DEPARTMENT BEGINNING
 NO EARLIER THAN DECEMBER FIRST.  SUCH REGULATIONS  SHALL  INCLUDE  LIVE
 CABLE  RESTRAINT  DEVICES  CONSISTING OF AT LEAST ONE SWIVEL, A RELAXING
 LOCK, A DEER STOP, AND A BREAK AWAY DEVICE. SUCH REGULATIONS SHALL  ALSO
 REQUIRE  THAT  ANY  PERSON USING A LIVE RESTRAINT CABLE SHALL CHECK SUCH
 DEVICE AT LEAST ONCE EVERY TWENTY-FOUR HOURS.  FOR THE PURPOSES OF  THIS
 PROVISION:
  (A)  A  SWIVEL  IS  A  DEVICE  INCORPORATED  AT  THE  END OF THE CABLE
 RESTRAINT WHICH ALLOWS THE CAPTURED ANIMAL TO FREELY ROTATE;
  (B) A RELAXING LOCK IS A DEVICE THAT ALLOWS THE  CABLE  LOOP  TO  STOP
 RESTRICTING WHEN THE CAPTURED ANIMAL STOPS PULLING;
