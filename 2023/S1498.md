# Relates to the transfer and use of certain state land to the city of New Rochelle for the construction of municipal fire services and affordable housing

**Bill ID:** S1498
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S1498 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1498)

## Summary

Relates to the transfer and use of certain state land to the city of New Rochelle for the construction of municipal fire services and affordable housing; extends authority to transfer.
