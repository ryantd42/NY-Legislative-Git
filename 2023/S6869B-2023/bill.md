# Relates to beverage container handling fees paid to dealers or operators of redemption centers

**Bill ID:** S6869B
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the handling fee paid by deposit initiators to dealers or operators of a redemption center from 3.5 cents to 4.5 cents.
