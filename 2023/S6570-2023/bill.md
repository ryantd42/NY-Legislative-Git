# Relates to updating rate components for the non-capital component of rates of payment by governmental agencies for inpatient services provided by residential health care facilities

**Bill ID:** S6570
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to requiring rate components for the non-capital component of rates of payment by governmental agencies for inpatient services provided by residential health care facilities to be updated no later than 1/1/2025, and every 5 years thereafter using the most currently available cost report data.
