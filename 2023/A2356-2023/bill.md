# Relates to the Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association of Upper Mountain, Inc.

**Bill ID:** A2356
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo J. Morinello
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Expands the use of foreign fire insurance premium taxes by the Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association of Upper Mountain, Inc.
