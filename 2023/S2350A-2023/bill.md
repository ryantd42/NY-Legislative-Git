# Expands eligibility to the brownfield redevelopment tax credit to certain taxpayers

**Bill ID:** S2350A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Expands eligibility to the site preparation credit component of the brownfield redevelopment tax credit.
