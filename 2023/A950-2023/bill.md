# Requires licensed day care facilities to contact a child's parent or guardian if they are absent from day care without providing prior notice

**Bill ID:** A950
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A950 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A950)

## Summary

Requires licensed day care facilities to contact a child's parent or guardian if they are absent from day care, with no prior notice given, within thirty minutes after such child is recorded as absent.
