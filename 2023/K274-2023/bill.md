# Commemorating the 130th Anniversary of the Village of Pawling on February 9, 2023

**Bill ID:** K274
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 274
 
BY: M. of A. Beephan
 
  COMMEMORATING the 130th Anniversary of the Village
  of Pawling on February 9, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of this Legislative Body to honor and
commemorate  the  distinguished  histories  of  the  communities  which
comprise the noble body of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the 130th Anniversary of the Village of Pawling on February
9, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Village of Pawling is located right in  the  middle  of
the  Town  of  Pawling;  it  is  also  home  to the world's shortest St.
Patrick's Day Parade at only 223 feet; and
 
  WHEREAS, Since the establishment it has had a mayor  and  a  village
board; the village also holds a private high school and a train station;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Home  to many, the population of the village is just under
2,000 residents; and
 
  WHEREAS, Of historic origin, and remaining fruitful over the ebb and
flow of decades of growth and change, the Village of  Pawling  continues
to provide the quintessential quality of life for its residents; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  residents  of  the  Village  of Pawling have a bright
outlook for the future of their community,  which  remains  a  beautiful
place in which to live and raise a family; and
 
  WHEREAS, In recognition of the Village of Pawling's rich history and
enduring contribution to the State of New York, this Legislative Body is
proud  to  pay  tribute  to  this spirited town upon the occasion of its
130th Anniversary; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the 130th Anniversary of the Village of Pawling on February
9, 2023, recognizing the significance of the role it continues  to  play
in the life of the community of the State of New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Village of Pawling.
