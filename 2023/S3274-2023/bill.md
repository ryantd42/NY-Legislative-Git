# Increases the penalty for robbery of property from a bank; makes the crime a class C felony

**Bill ID:** S3274
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the penalty for robbery of property from a bank; makes the crime a class C felony.
