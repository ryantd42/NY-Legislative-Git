# Allows first responders who are diagnosed with post-traumatic stress disorder to request line of duty sick leave

**Bill ID:** A3701
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Allows first responders who are diagnosed with post-traumatic stress disorder to request line of duty sick leave.
