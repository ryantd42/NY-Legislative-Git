# Requires cable companies to charge the same rate for the same service

**Bill ID:** A1198
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1198 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1198)

## Summary

Requires cable companies to charge the same rate for the same service to different subscribers.
