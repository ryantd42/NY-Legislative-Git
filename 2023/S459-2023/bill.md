# Requires the parole board to grant parole to incarcerated individuals who successfully participate in a temporary release program without interruption immediately prior to appearance before the board

**Bill ID:** S459
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S459 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S459)

## Summary

Requires the parole board to grant parole to incarcerated individuals who successfully participate in a temporary release program for two years without interruption immediately prior to appearance before the board.
