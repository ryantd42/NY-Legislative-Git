# Honoring Rachelle J. Easie upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the Robert J. Uplinger Award by Stuy Park Lions Club, Inc.

**Bill ID:** J415
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 415
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING  Rachelle  J.  Easie upon the occasion of
  her  designation  as  recipient  of  the  Robert  J.
  Uplinger  Award  by  Stuy  Park  Lions Club, Inc. on
  February 17, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
honor  those  distinguished  citizens who would devote their life's work
and purpose to community service, contributing to  the  welfare  of  the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Rachelle J. Easie upon the occasion of her designation as  recipient  of
the  Robert  J.  Uplinger  Award  by  Stuy  Park  Lions Club, Inc. to be
celebrated at its 30th Annual  Pearl  Red  and  White  Gala  on  Friday,
February 17, 2023, at Antun's, Queens Village, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Stuy  Park  Lions  Club  is  part  of  Lions  Clubs
International, one of the largest service organizations  in  the  world,
and is deeply committed to meeting the challenges of public service; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Born  and raised in Brooklyn, New York, Rachelle Joy Easie
graduated from Bay Ridge High School and earned her degree  from  Audrey
Cohen  College;  she  then  began her career in childcare, spanning over
three decades, at the George C. Conliffe Child Care Center, where she is
affectionately known as "Miss Ray-Ray " ; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to her work at the center,  Rachelle  J.  Easie
has  been  a  Girl Scout Leader for Brownie Troop 2725 at Berean Baptist
Church in Brooklyn, and has  been  a  faithful  member  of  The  Concord
Baptist Church of Christ for over 50 years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rachelle  J. Easie is also a vital member of the Stuy Park
Lions Club where  she  has  served  in  numerous  leadership  capacities
including  Lion  Tamer,  Secretary,  Assistant Secretary, and Membership
Chair; and
 
  WHEREAS, With her throughout have been  her  husband  of  39  years,
Austin  and their daughter, Nichelle, both of whom feel privileged to be
a part of her life and rejoice in her achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of  such  noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the  citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Rachelle  J.  Easie  upon  the  occasion  of  her  designation  as
recipient of the Robert J. Uplinger Award by Stuy Park Lions Club, Inc.;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Rachelle J. Easie.
