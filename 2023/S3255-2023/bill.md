# Extends the statute of limitations for claims resulting from unlawful discriminatory practices to three years

**Bill ID:** S3255
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the statute of limitations for claims resulting from unlawful discriminatory practices to three years.
