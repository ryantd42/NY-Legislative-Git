# Relates to animal insurance policies

**Bill ID:** S1715
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1715 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1715)

## Summary

Prohibits animal insurance policies from limiting or excluding pre-existing conditions.
