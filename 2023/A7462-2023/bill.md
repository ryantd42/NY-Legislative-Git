# Directs the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation to fund and conduct the empire state games on an annual basis

**Bill ID:** A7462
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Philip Ramos
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs the office of parks, recreation and historic preservation to fund and conduct the empire state games on an annual basis.
