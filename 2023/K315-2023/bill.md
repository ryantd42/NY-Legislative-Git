# Commemorating the 70th Anniversary of Racing at Vernon Downs Casino Hotel

**Bill ID:** K315
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian D. Miller
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 315
 
BY: M. of A. Miller
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  70th  Anniversary of Racing at
  Vernon Downs Casino Hotel
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  the
achievements  of  those  individuals  and  organizations that contribute
substantially to the economic vitality  of  their  communities,  thereby
enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the 70th Anniversary of Racing at Vernon Downs Casino Hotel
on Saturday, July 1, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Harness racing debuted at Vernon Downs Casino  Hotel  on  a
revolutionary six-furlong oval; the first race on opening night, July 1,
1953, was won by Bob Abbe II; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The track, located in Vernon, New York, featured 24 barns,
each with 24 stalls  and  beautiful  natural  backdrop  with  hills  and
sprawling countryside; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over  the  years, Vernon Downs racing has become known for
hosting prestigious stake races and drawing celebrities, like baseball's
Mickey Mantle and Whitey Ford, and some of the best equine  names,  such
as  Bret  Hanover,  Ring  Eyed  Pete, Nevele Pride, Albatross, Niatross,
Moni-Maker and Bunny Lake; and
 
  WHEREAS, The company  will  mark  this  milestone  anniversary  with
celebrations  honoring  Vernon  Downs' long-running legacy of fast-paced
harness racing, ranging  from  a  slate  of  competitive  races  on  its
7/8-mile oval, to festive entertainment; and
 
  WHEREAS, This year, Vernon Downs' Opening Day is Saturday, April 15,
2023;  the  day will bring in countless patrons, both first-time excited
attendees, and long-time loyal guests, all ready to enjoy  the  weather,
food, deals, and fun that Vernon Downs consistently offers; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges this exceptional business, its contributions to  the  local
and  state  economies,  and its vibrant role in improving the quality of
life in its community, fully confident that it will  continue  to  enjoy
the  sparkling successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate the 70th Anniversary of Racing at Vernon Downs Casino Hotel;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Vernon Downs Casino Hotel.
