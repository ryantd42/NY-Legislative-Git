# Relates to the definition of "protected consumer"

**Bill ID:** A2578AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Defines a "protected consumer" as an individual who is under the age of eighteen years.
