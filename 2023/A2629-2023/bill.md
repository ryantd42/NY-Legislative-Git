# Requires kindergartens to operate full day programs

**Bill ID:** A2629
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires kindergartens to operate full day programs in the 2025-2026 school year.
