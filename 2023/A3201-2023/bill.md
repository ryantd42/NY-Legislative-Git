# Relates to the display of live races and special events on telephones

**Bill ID:** A3201
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides specific authority to video stream live races to any New York licensed track or off-track betting corporation which accepts internet wagers.
