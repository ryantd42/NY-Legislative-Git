  3. THE COMMISSIONER SHALL ENSURE THE CONFIDENTIALITY  OF  EACH  SURVEY
 PARTICIPANT  BY USING SELF-ADMINISTERED QUESTIONNAIRES AND OTHER SIMILAR
 SURVEY METHODS, WHICH WILL ENSURE THE RESPONDENT'S ANONYMITY.
