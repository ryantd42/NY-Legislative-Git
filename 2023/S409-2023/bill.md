# Relates to the amount of the indemnity bond or insurance required by common carrier ambulettes and paratransit vehicles

**Bill ID:** S409
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S409 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S409)

## Summary

Provides for a minimum indemnity bond or insurance policy of $500,000 required by certain common carrier ambulettes and paratransit vehicles.
