# Relates to effectiveness of judgments entered by the New York City environmental control board; repealer

**Bill ID:** S268
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S268 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S268)

## Summary

Removes the eight year expiration for judgments entered by the New York City environmental control board.
