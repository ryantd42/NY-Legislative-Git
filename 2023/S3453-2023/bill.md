# Removes cap on funds for teacher resource and computer training centers for city school district for the city of New York

**Bill ID:** S3453
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Removes the cap on funds for teacher resource and computer training centers for the city school district for the city of New York.
