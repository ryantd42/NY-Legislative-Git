# Honoring Lisa Marschall upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by HEARTS of Port Washington on February 10, 2023

**Bill ID:** J319
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 319
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  HONORING  Lisa  Marschall upon the occasion of her
  designation for special  recognition  by  HEARTS  of
  Port Washington on February 10, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  The  unity  of  our  State  and  Nation  is built upon the
compassion of good individuals such as Lisa  Marschall  who  uphold  the
values of community life and who, through their great actions, epitomize
the best of humanity; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to  honor Lisa
Marschall upon the occasion of her designation for  special  recognition
by  HEARTS  of Port Washington, to be celebrated at its Annual Red Party
on Friday, February 10, 2023, at Polish American Hall, Port  Washington,
New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  HEARTS  (Helping Enrich the Arts) of Port Washington is an
independent, grassroots,  not-for-profit  community  organization  whose
mission  is  to  add meaningful value to children's lives by supporting,
promoting, and enriching the arts in public schools and  throughout  the
Port Washington community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  Lisa  Marschall,  the  arts,  especially  music,  are
paramount; she began singing and dancing  from  an  early  age,  joining
every  choir  and  vocal  group  in  Roslyn public schools; furthermore,
thanks to a favorite aunt, she began playing guitar at age 10; and
 
  WHEREAS, Lisa Marschall was later exposed  to  theatrical  hair  and
makeup  in  high  school,  and  after  earning  a  Master  of  Business
Administration degree in Finance from New  York  University  (NYU),  she
became a professional hair and makeup artist; and
 
  WHEREAS,  After  relocating to Port Washington, Lisa Marschall began
to donate her services to several local  theater  groups  including  the
Port  Summer Show, Weber and Schreiber theater groups, and the Community
Synagogue Theater Company; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  a  steadfast  and  unwavering  commitment  to  her
community,  Lisa Marschall has given tirelessly of her time and energies
to the district's HSAs, Parent Council and Compact committees; and
 
  WHEREAS, Lisa Marschall  has  been  involved  with  HEARTS  of  Port
Washington since its inception, beginning as a volunteer before becoming
a  Board  member  and co-chairing several Red Party events; she has been
involved in every PortFest since the inaugural festival in 2012, and  is
now  a leader who brings everything together to benefit the organization
and the community-at-large; and
 
  WHEREAS, With her throughout have been her husband, Kai,  and  their
two  children,  Emma and Jacob, all of whom feel privileged to be a part
of her life and rejoice in her many achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by  an
individual  for the benefit of others which Lisa Marschall has displayed
throughout her life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  has always been the objective of this Legislative Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment  to the betterment of their communities, and it is the intent
of this Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this  tribute  to
Lisa  Marschall,  that  future  generations  may know and appreciate her
admirable character, her many benevolent  deeds,  and  the  respect  and
esteem in which she is held by her peers; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Lisa Marschall upon the occasion of her  designation  for  special
recognition  by  HEARTS  of  Port  Washington, and to wish her continued
success in all her future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Lisa Marschall.
