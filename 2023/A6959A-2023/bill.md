# Authorizes the town of Warwick, county of Orange to alienate certain lands held in public trust in the town of Warwick, county of Orange

**Bill ID:** A6959A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Warwick, county of Orange to alienate certain lands held in public trust in the town of Warwick, county of Orange.
