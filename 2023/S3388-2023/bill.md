# Relates to certain housing accommodations by tenants over sixty-two years of age or a tenant with a disability

**Bill ID:** S3388
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Liz Krueger
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to rent regulated and rent stabilized housing accommodations by tenants over sixty-two years of age or a tenant with a disability.
