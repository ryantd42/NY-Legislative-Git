# Expands the empire state apprenticeship tax credit to include additional credit for people with disabilities

**Bill ID:** S7037
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Ramos
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Expands the empire state apprenticeship tax credit to include additional credit for people with disabilities.
