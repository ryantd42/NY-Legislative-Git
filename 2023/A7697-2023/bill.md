# Exempts residents of lower Manhattan and Staten Island from congestion surcharge pricing

**Bill ID:** A7697
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Charles Fall
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Exempts residents of lower Manhattan and Staten Island from the congestion surcharge imposed under the tax law.
