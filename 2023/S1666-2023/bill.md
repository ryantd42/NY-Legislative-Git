# Authorizes Greene county to enter into an agreement with a contiguous county to share the construction, financing, improvement and/or maintenance of a county jail

**Bill ID:** S1666
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1666 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1666)

## Summary

Authorizes Greene county to enter into an agreement with a contiguous county to share the construction, financing, improvement and/or maintenance of a county jail.

  Section  1.  (a)  Notwithstanding  any  inconsistent provision of law,

 share the construction, financing, improvement, maintenance and/or hous-
 ing  of persons in the custody of the sheriff of a county jail. Any such
 shared county jail shall be physically located in Greene county or  such
 contiguous  county. Any agreement made pursuant to this section shall be

 pal  law. For the purposes of this section, the term "contiguous county"

 described  (with  respect  to  towns  and  villages) in subdivision 4 of
 section 750 of the general municipal law.
  (b) A shared county jail established pursuant to this section shall be
 considered the county jail of Greene county and such  contiguous  county
 for  the  purposes  of any relevant provision of law and be considered a
 local correctional facility, as such term is defined in  subdivision  16
 of section 2 of the correction law, for all purposes.
  (c)  For  purposes  of subdivision 2 of section 70.20 of the penal law
 and any other relevant provision of the criminal procedure law  and  any
 other  law, such shared county jail shall be considered to be located in
 Greene county and such contiguous county and be  considered  the  county
 jail  of  the  county  for  all courts located in Greene county and such
 contiguous county.  Further, a person taken  into  custody  pursuant  to
