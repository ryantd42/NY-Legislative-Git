# Creates distinctive license plates for the United States army 10th mountain division

**Bill ID:** S4386
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Creates distinctive license plates for members and supporters of the United States army 10th mountain division.
