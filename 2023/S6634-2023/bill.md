# Requires health insurance plans to provide coverage for epi-pens for individuals 18 years old and younger

**Bill ID:** S6634
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires health insurance plans to provide coverage for epi-pens for individuals 18 years old and younger.
