# Relates to the payment of arrears for certain taxes

**Bill ID:** A462
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A462 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A462)

## Summary

Modifies certain eligibility requirements and extends the time period for the payment of arrears for certain taxes.
