# Establishes a pilot program authorizing certain nurse aides to administer medication in residential health care facilities in St. Lawrence county

**Bill ID:** A2973
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Scott Gray
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes nursing home medication aides to administer medication in residential health care facilities in St. Lawrence county.
