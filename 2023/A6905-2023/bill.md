# Provides for lactation accommodation in public transportation facilities act

**Bill ID:** A6905
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the lactation accommodation in public transportation facilities act.

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "lactation

 876 of the laws of 1976, is amended to read as follows:
  § 361-c.  [Tourist  information]  SERVICE facilities. 1. The authority

 food  is offered for sale, provided that: the department of commerce and
 or local and regional  tourist  promotion  agencies  shall  provide  the

 for distribution to the traveling public.

  (A) INCLUDES, AT A MINIMUM, A CHAIR AND AN ELECTRICAL OUTLET; AND

 THE  NORTHWAY, THE COMMISSIONER SHALL PROVIDE FOR A PRIVATE ROOM OR AREA

  § 4. The public health law is amended by adding a new  section  2505-c

  §  2505-C.  PRIVATE PLACE FOR NURSING MOTHERS. 1. ON OR BEFORE JANUARY
 FIRST, TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FOUR, THE MANAGER OF A  RAILROAD  STATION  OR
 BUS STATION, CONSISTING OF AN INDOOR WAITING AREA WITH PUBLIC RESTROOMS,

 CORPORATION, THAT CONDUCTS COMMERCIAL OPERATIONS AND THAT HAS MORE  THAN
 FIVE  HUNDRED  THOUSAND PASSENGERS A YEAR, SHALL PROVIDE A ROOM OR OTHER
 LOCATION FOR MEMBERS OF THE PUBLIC TO EXPRESS  BREAST  MILK  IN  PRIVATE
 THAT MEETS BOTH OF THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS:
  (A) INCLUDES, AT A MINIMUM, A CHAIR AND AN ELECTRICAL OUTLET; AND
  (B) IS LOCATED OUTSIDE OF THE CONFINES OF A PUBLIC RESTROOM.
  2.  UPON  CONSTRUCTION  OF A NEW STATION THAT HAS INDOOR WAITING AREAS
 WITH PUBLIC RESTROOMS OR THE REPLACEMENT, EXPANSION, OR RENOVATION OF AN
 EXISTING STATION, A RAILROAD OR BUS STATION SHALL PROVIDE A SINK IN  ANY
 ROOM  OR OTHER LOCATION DESIGNATED TO COMPLY WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THIS
 SECTION.
  3. AS USED IN THIS SECTION, "RENOVATION OF AN EXISTING STATION"  MEANS
 THE  REPURPOSING  OF  MORE  THAN TWENTY-FIVE PERCENT OF THE SPACE IN THE
 STATION.
  4. NOTHING IN THIS SECTION SHALL APPLY TO RAILROAD  STATIONS  OPERATED
 FOR  THE  SOLE  PURPOSE  OF  A  SUBWAY STATION OR RAPID TRANSIT RAILROAD
 STATION.
  § 5. This act shall take effect on the thirtieth day  after  it  shall
 have become a law.
