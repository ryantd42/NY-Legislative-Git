# Requires nursing certificate and degree education programs to include clinical education and allows for a portion of such clinical education to be completed through simulation experience

**Bill ID:** A3076A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Donna Lupardo
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Requires nursing certificate and degree education programs to include clinical education; allows for one-third of such clinical education to be completed through simulation experience.
