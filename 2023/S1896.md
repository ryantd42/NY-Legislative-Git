# Relates to assault in the third degree

**Bill ID:** S1896
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1896 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1896)

## Summary

Relates to assault in the third degree by a person against an operator, driver or passenger on a bus, taxicab, aerial tramway, subway or other motor vehicle.
