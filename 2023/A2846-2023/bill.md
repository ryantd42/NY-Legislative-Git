# Requires teachers employed by the Buffalo school district to reside within Buffalo city limits

**Bill ID:** A2846
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires teachers employed by the Buffalo school district to reside within Buffalo city limits.
