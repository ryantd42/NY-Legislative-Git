# Relates to allowing municipalities to accept retroactive applications for real property tax exemptions from certain veterans

**Bill ID:** S3580
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to allowing municipalities to accept retroactive applications for real property tax exemptions from certain veterans.
