# Classifies emergency medical services as essential services for the purposes of state aid

**Bill ID:** S4586
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Classifies emergency medical services as essential services for the purposes of state aid.
