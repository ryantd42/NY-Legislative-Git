# Prohibits the comptroller from using environment, social, and governance criteria when investing the state pension fund

**Bill ID:** A4090
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Samuel Pirozzolo
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the comptroller from using environment, social, and governance criteria as a screening method for selecting companies and funds to invest the state pension fund in.
