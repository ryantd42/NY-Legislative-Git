 UNIVERSITY OF NEW YORK ENDOWMENT FUND.
  3. AMOUNTS TRANSFERRED TO OR DEPOSITED INTO  THE  ENDOWMENT  SHALL  BE

 FROM THE ENDOWMENT SHALL BE USED TO SUPPLEMENT,  RATHER  THAN  SUPPLANT,
