# Authorizes superintendent of financial services to conduct a study and make recommendations on title insurance premium rates in N.Y. state

**Bill ID:** A413
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A413 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A413)

## Summary

Authorizes the superintendent of financial services to conduct a study and make recommendations on title insurance premium rates in NYS.
