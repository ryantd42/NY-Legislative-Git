# Relates to the location of the principal office of the cannabis control board and office of cannabis management

**Bill ID:** A7676
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that the principal office of the cannabis control board and office of cannabis management shall be located in Albany county.
