# Prohibits the delivery of an absentee ballot or an application therefore to an address associated with a candidate's campaign

**Bill ID:** S6653
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the delivery of an absentee ballot or an application therefore to an address associated with a candidate's campaign.
