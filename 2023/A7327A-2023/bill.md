# Relates to increasing the discretionary purchasing threshold for certified minority and women-owned business enterprises in New York city

**Bill ID:** A7327A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases the discretionary purchasing threshold for certified minority and women-owned business enterprises in New York city to $1.5 million.
