# Relates to the imposition of sales and compensating use taxes by the county of Albany

**Bill ID:** A6695
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Relates to the imposition of sales and compensating use taxes by the county of Albany; extends authorization for such tax until 11/30/2025.
