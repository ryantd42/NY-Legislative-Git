# Provides for a deduction from personal gross income for expenses incurred in the adoption of a child in the foster care system

**Bill ID:** S598A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Julia Salazar
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for a deduction from personal gross income for expenses incurred in the adoption of a child in the foster care system.
