# Honoring Melissa Campanelli Williams upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of the Founder's Award by HEARTS of Port Washington on February 10, 2023

**Bill ID:** J316
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 316
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  HONORING  Melissa  Campanelli  Williams  upon  the
  occasion of her  designation  as  recipient  of  the
  Founder's  Award  by  HEARTS  of  Port Washington on
  February 10, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, The unity of  our  State  and  Nation  is  built  upon  the
compassion  of  good individuals such as Melissa Campanelli Williams who
uphold the values  of  community  life  and  who,  through  their  great
actions, epitomize the best of humanity; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to honor Melissa
Campanelli Williams upon the occasion of her designation as recipient of
the Founder's Award by HEARTS of Port Washington, to  be  celebrated  at
its  Annual  Red  Party on Friday, February 10, 2023, at Polish American
Hall, Port Washington, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, HEARTS (Helping Enrich the Arts) of Port Washington  is  an
independent,  grassroots,  not-for-profit  community  organization whose
mission is to add meaningful value to children's  lives  by  supporting,
promoting,  and  enriching the arts in public schools and throughout the
Port Washington community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Melissa Campanelli Williams is the co-founder of the  Women
in Retail Leadership Circle, a members-only networking group that offers
enterprise-level  women in the retail industry a place to learn, connect
and grow; she is also the brand and content director of the  Retail  and
Travel Group at NAPCO Media; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Prior  to  these  esteemed  positions,  Melissa Campanelli
Williams spent ten years at DM News, where she first  served  as  senior
editor and then as deputy editor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  leading  expert  in  small business e-commerce, Melissa
Campanelli Williams is the author of two books, Entrepreneur  Magazine's
Open  an  Online Business in 10 Days and 55 Surefire Internet Businesses
You Can Start for Under $5,000; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  a  steadfast  and  unwavering  commitment  to  her
community,  Melissa Campanelli Williams serves on the advisory boards of
Gifting Brands, the New York Fashion Tech Lab,  and  Intelligence  Node;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Melissa Campanelli Williams grew up in Port Washington, and
moved  back  to  her hometown in 2015; since her return, she has devoted
her time and energies to  helping  several  arts  organizations  in  the
community,  including  the  Port  Summer  Show  where  she  was  named
co-president in 2021, and HEARTS of Port Washington where she  has  been
on the Red Party committee since 2018 and volunteered with many PortFest
events; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  her  throughout have been her two children, Caroline
and Christopher, both of whom feel privileged to be a part of  her  life
and rejoice in her many achievements; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefit of others which Melissa  Campanelli  Williams
has displayed throughout her life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  has always been the objective of this Legislative Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment  to the betterment of their communities, and it is the intent
of this Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this  tribute  to
Melissa  Campanelli  Williams,  that  future  generations  may  know and
appreciate her admirable character, her many benevolent deeds,  and  the
respect and esteem in which she is held by her peers; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Melissa Campanelli Williams upon the occasion of  her  designation
as recipient of the Founder's Award by HEARTS of Port Washington, and to
wish  her  continued  success  in  all  her  future endeavors; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Melissa Campanelli Williams.
