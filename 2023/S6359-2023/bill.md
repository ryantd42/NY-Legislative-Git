# Relates to authorizing the use of funds from state or local sources for the provision or sale of New York milk in New York schools

**Bill ID:** S6359
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to authorizing the use of funds from state or local sources for the provision or sale of New York milk in New York schools; requires the milk advisory council to participate in certain reporting requirements relating to consumption and availability of New York milk in New York schools; directs the department of agriculture and markets to undertake a study to increase processing of milk in New York state.

  § 915-A. PROVISION OR SALE OF NEW YORK MILK IN NEW YORK SCHOOLS.  1.

 HAVE THE FOLLOWING MEANINGS:
  (A)  "NEW  YORK  MILK"  MEANS RAW MILK PRODUCED BY THE MILKING OF COWS

 TRANSPORTED TO A DAIRY PROCESSING FACILITY LOCATED WITHIN THE GEOGRAPHIC

 INTENDED FOR DISTRIBUTION TO CONSUMERS.
  (B) "NEW YORK SCHOOL"  MEANS  ANY  SCHOOL  DISTRICT,  CHARTER  SCHOOL,

 ATE UNIT LOCATED WITHIN THIS STATE.
  (C) "REDUCED FAT NEW YORK MILK" MEANS NEW  YORK  MILK  CONTAINING  TWO
 PERCENT MILK FAT. THE TERM "REDUCED FAT NEW YORK MILK" SHALL INCLUDE NEW
 YORK MILK THAT HAS BEEN FLAVORED.
  (D)  "WHOLE  NEW  YORK  MILK"  MEANS NEW YORK MILK CONTAINING AT LEAST
 THREE PERCENT MILK FAT. THE TERM "WHOLE NEW YORK MILK" SHALL INCLUDE NEW
 YORK MILK THAT HAS BEEN FLAVORED.

 study to assess ways to increase the processing of milk within the state
 of New York  for  intrastate  distribution  and  consumption,  including
 expanding processing facilities.

  (a) Identify potential incentives for dairy processors to expand their
