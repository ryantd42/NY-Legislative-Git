# Establishes a pilot program for the dispensing of hearing aids in the county of Westchester

**Bill ID:** A2478
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a pilot program for the dispensing of hearing aids in the county of Westchester.
