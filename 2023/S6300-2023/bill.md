# Relates to creating a feebate program for medium and heavy duty vehicles

**Bill ID:** S6300
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the levy of an efficiency fee or an efficiency rebate based on the MPG or MPGe rating of medium and heavy duty vehicles.

  § 499-E. PURPOSE OF ARTICLE. THERE IS HEREBY CREATED A FEEBATE PROGRAM
 IN WHICH ALL COVERED VEHICLES ARE SUBJECT TO EITHER AN EFFICIENCY FEE OR
 AN EFFICIENCY REBATE, BASED UPON SUCH COVERED  VEHICLE'S  FUEL  ECONOMY.

 AVAILABILITY, ACTUAL AND PROJECTED COVERED  MOTOR  VEHICLE  REGISTRATION
 DATA, AND ANY OTHER SUCH FACTOR THE COMMISSIONER DEEMS RELEVANT.
  §  499-F.  DEFINITIONS.  AS  USED IN THIS ARTICLE, THE FOLLOWING TERMS
 SHALL HAVE THE FOLLOWING MEANINGS:
  1. "COVERED VEHICLE" OR "COVERED MOTOR VEHICLE" SHALL MEAN ALL  MEDIUM
 AND  HEAVY  DUTY  VEHICLES WITH A GROSS VEHICLE WEIGHT RATING OF GREATER
 THAN TEN THOUSAND POUNDS REGISTERED ON OR AFTER THE  EFFECTIVE  DATE  OF
 THIS SECTION.

  2.  "EFFICIENCY  FEE"  SHALL  MEAN  A  FEE  LEVIED UPON THE OWNER OF A
 COVERED VEHICLE BASED ON SUCH VEHICLE'S FUEL ECONOMY PURSUANT TO  SUBDI-
 VISION TWO OF SECTION FOUR HUNDRED NINETY-NINE-H OF THIS ARTICLE.
  3.  "EFFICIENCY  REBATE"  SHALL  MEAN A REBATE GIVEN TO THE OWNER OF A
 COVERED VEHICLE BASED ON SUCH VEHICLE'S FUEL ECONOMY PURSUANT TO  SUBDI-
 VISION THREE OF SECTION FOUR HUNDRED NINETY-NINE-H OF THIS ARTICLE.
  4.  "FEEBATE PROGRAM" SHALL REFER TO THE SYSTEM OF EFFICIENCY FEES AND
 EFFICIENCY REBATES CREATED IN THIS ARTICLE.
  5. "FUEL ECONOMY" SHALL REFER TO THE NUMBER OF MILES A COVERED VEHICLE
 CAN TRAVEL USING A SPECIFIC AMOUNT OF FUEL OR A COMBINATION OF FUELS, AS
 REFLECTED IN SUCH VEHICLE'S MILES PER GALLON OR MILES PER GALLON  EQUIV-
 ALENT.
  6.  "GROSS  VEHICLE  WEIGHT RATING" SHALL MEAN THE WEIGHT OF A VEHICLE
 CONSISTING OF THE UNLADEN  WEIGHT  AND  THE  MAXIMUM  CARRYING  CAPACITY
 RECOMMENDED BY THE MANUFACTURER OF SUCH VEHICLE.
  7.  "MILES PER GALLON" OR "MPG" SHALL REFER TO THE MEASURE OF DISTANCE
 THAT A COVERED VEHICLE RUNNING ON GASOLINE OR DIESEL FUEL CAN TRAVEL PER
 GALLON OF SUCH FUEL AS RATED BY THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AGENCY.
  8. "MILES PER GALLON GASOLINE EQUIVALENT" OR "MPGE" SHALL REFER TO THE
 MEASURE OF DISTANCE THAT A COVERED VEHICLE RUNNING ON  NON-LIQUID  FUELS
 CAN  TRAVEL  PER UNIT OF ENERGY AS RATED BY THE ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION
 AGENCY.
  9. "REVENUE NEUTRALITY" OR "REVENUE NEUTRAL" SHALL MEAN  A  GOAL  THAT
 THE EFFICIENCY FEES DESCRIBED IN SUBDIVISION TWO OF SECTION FOUR HUNDRED
 NINETY-NINE-H  OF  THIS ARTICLE COVER THE COST OF THE EFFICIENCY REBATES
 DESCRIBED IN SUBDIVISION THREE OF SUCH SECTION.
  10. "NEAR ZERO EMISSIONS" SHALL MEAN A VEHICLE THAT USES ZERO EMISSION
 TECHNOLOGIES OR TECHNOLOGIES THAT PROVIDE A  PATHWAY  TO  ZERO  EMISSION
 OPERATIONS  OR  THAT  INCORPORATES OTHER TECHNOLOGIES THAT SIGNIFICANTLY
 REDUCE EXHAUST EMISSIONS OF ANY GREENHOUSE GAS, CRITERIA  POLLUTANT,  OR
 PRECURSOR  POLLUTANT  UNDER  ANY  AND ALL POSSIBLE OPERATIONAL MODES AND
 CONDITIONS.
  11. "ZERO EMISSION" SHALL MEAN A VEHICLE POWERED BY MEANS OF A BATTERY
 OR FUEL CELL OR A COMBINATION THEREOF, OR ANOTHER SOURCE OF POWER,  THAT
 PRODUCES  ZERO  EXHAUST EMISSIONS OF ANY GREENHOUSE GAS, CRITERIA POLLU-
 TANT OR PRECURSOR POLLUTANT UNDER ANY AND ALL POSSIBLE OPERATIONAL MODES
 AND CONDITIONS.
