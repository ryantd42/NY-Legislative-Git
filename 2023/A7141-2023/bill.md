# Provides an exemption of any award from the September eleventh victim compensation fund from application to the satisfaction of money judgments

**Bill ID:** A7141
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides an exemption of any award from the September eleventh victim compensation fund from application to the satisfaction of money judgements.
