# Provides for rules and regulations for state aid reimbursement for the purchase of opioid antagonists by municipalities

**Bill ID:** A516
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A516 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A516)

## Summary

Provides for rules and regulations for state aid reimbursement under article six of the public health law for the purchase of opioid antagonists by municipalities.
