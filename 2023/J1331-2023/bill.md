# Commending Elejmal Temple No. 185 upon the occasion of hosting its Potentate Ball from July 21-23, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1331
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1331
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  Elejmal  Temple  No.  185  upon  the
  occasion of hosting its  Potentate  Ball  from  July
  21-23, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize that
the quality and character of life in the  communities  across  New  York
State  are  reflective  of  the concerned and dedicated efforts of those
organizations and individuals who would devote themselves to the welfare
of the community and its citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Elejmal  Temple  No.  185  upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  its
Potentate  Ball  from  July  21-23,  2023, at the Royal Regency Hotel in
Yonkers, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The first Nobel  to  demit  to  Elejmal  Temple  was  Nobel
William  Seldon of Medina Temple No. 19, followed by Nobles Edward Woods
and Nathan Wilburn of Medina No. 19 and Noble Earkett T. Haggins of  Abu
Bekr No. 91; and
 
  WHEREAS,  On  August  12, 1974, Potentate Wilburn Gaither spoke with
D.T. Dorothy M. Johnson, The Imperial Deputy of the Desert of  New  York
about  the forming of an Isis Club for Elejmal Temple; two days later, a
letter was received from D.T. Johnson, the Imperial Deputy, dated August
13,  1974  informing  him  of  a  meeting  arranged  with  the  Imperial
Commandress,  D.T.  Lucille I. Boyd, in convention city at the Daughters
Headquarters, the Benjamin Franklin Hotel for discussion on this matter;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In January of 1974, with Past Matron Addie Gray No.  62  as
president, the Isis Club of Elejmal was formed; under Past Potentate Eli
J.  Allen,  the Daughters were created, the Temple dedicated and Elejmal
Court was placed under Dispensation on August 14, 1976, with 30  members
and Elejmal Court No. 171 was chartered; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Elejmal's  Talent  and  Scholarship  pageant has given the
Court and Temple a first-place winner who represented New York State  at
their  Annual Imperial Shrine Convention; they also had two second place
winners on the state level; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Clown Unit under Noble Moody has five members, and  the
Motor  Patrol  under  the  leadership  of  Noble Richard Upshaw has five
members; and
 
  WHEREAS, The  Marching  Unit  under  the  Command  of  Commander  of
Display,  Past Potentate Samuel Robinson and the various Presidents, has
received eight trophies from  Imperial  Council,  co-winners  Membership
Increase  1975-1976;  Third  Place Marching Unit, Longest Marching Unit,
Los Angeles, 1976; Third Place, Marching Unit, Kansas City, 1978; L.M.S.
Runner Up, 1979; First  Place,  Marching  Unit,  New  York  City,  1979;
Imperial  Council Membership Increase by 10%, 1980; Third Place Marching
 
Unit, 1980; and First Place, New York States Association, I.B.P.O.E.  of
White Plains, 1981; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Elejmal  Thundering  Third  Drum  Corps,  a  group of
approximately 40 young girls and boys, ages six through 18 who were once
named the Yonder Challenger's; in 1974, they marched for the first  time
under  the  Temple  Banner  in  White  Plains; today, this Drum Corps is
sponsored by Elejmal Temple; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
organizations and individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Elejmal  Temple  No.  185  upon  the  occasion  of  hosting its
Potentate Ball from July 21-23, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Elejmal Temple No. 185.
