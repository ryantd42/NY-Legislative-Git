# Commemorating the 102nd Anniversary of Brookdale Hospital Medical Center in Brooklyn, New York, on April 11, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1010
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1010
 
BY: Senator MYRIE
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  102nd Anniversary of Brookdale
  Hospital Medical Center in Brooklyn,  New  York,  on
  April 11, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize those
institutions  which  have  faithfully  served  the  health  needs of the
community and its citizens with diligence and compassionate  commitment;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  102nd Anniversary of Brookdale Hospital Medical Center
in Brooklyn, New York, on Tuesday, April 11, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Brookdale Hospital Medical Center opened its doors  to  the
public  for  the first time on April 11, 1921, with one building housing
75 beds between the neighborhoods of Brownsville,  East  New  York,  and
Canarsie; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Originally  called  the  Brownsville  and  East  New  York
Hospital, it was renamed Beth-El Hospital in 1932;  at  this  time,  the
hospital, under the directorship of Jacob Rutstein, greatly expanded its
facilities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  1963, the hospital was renamed Brookdale Hospital, and
in 1971, it became the  Brookdale  Hospital  Medical  Center;  in  1993,
Brookdale  Hospital Medical Center opened the first designated long-term
AIDS center in Brooklyn, which was later named the  Treatment  for  Life
Center; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Today,  the  Brookdale  Hospital  Medical Center is one of
Brooklyn's largest voluntary nonprofit teaching hospitals and a regional
tertiary care center, serving most  of  Eastern  Brooklyn;  it  provides
24-hour  emergency services, numerous outpatient programs, and long-term
specialty care; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition, Brookdale Hospital Medical Center has  one  of
Brooklyn's  largest  full-service  emergency  departments  and one of 15
Level 1 Trauma Centers in New York City; it is also one of 14  New  York
State  DOH-designated  Stroke Centers in Brooklyn, and provides numerous
outpatient Ambulatory care  services  in  both  on-campus  and  off-site
facilities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  Brookdale  Hospital Medical Center is in
direct correlation to the efforts  of  its  administration,  and  staff,
whose  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,  characterized  by  an
impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and  an  uncompromising
standard of excellence in all endeavors; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Venerable  and  trustworthy  institutions,  tending to the
needs of the community and its citizenry,  represent  the  threads  from
which the fabric of society is woven; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This Legislative Body is proud to recognize the benevolent
good works of health care institutions, sharing the pride of its history
and accomplishments, and anticipating its future of continued growth  in
quality and personalized care; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
extend congratulations and just tribute to  Brookdale  Hospital  Medical
Center,  commemorating  its  102nd  Anniversary  of  service and care on
behalf of the Brooklyn, New York community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Brookdale Hospital Medical Center.
