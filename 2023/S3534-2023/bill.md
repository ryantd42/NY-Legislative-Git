# Provides a liquor license exemption for the Mitzpah Towers property located in Syracuse

**Bill ID:** S3534
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Provides a liquor license exemption for the Mitzpah Towers property located in the city of Syracuse in the county of Onondaga.
