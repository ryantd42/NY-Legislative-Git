# Relates to notice to the commander of a military station of certain municipal actions

**Bill ID:** A1154
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1154 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1154)

## Summary

Relates to notice to the commander of a military station of certain municipal actions that occur within 3000 feet of the property to be affected by the proposed action.
