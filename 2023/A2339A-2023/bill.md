# Relates to establishing certain cultural and religious observances as school holidays in certain districts

**Bill ID:** A2339AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to establishing Eid al-Fitr, Eid al-Adha, Diwali, Tihar, Bandi Chhor Divas and Onam as school holidays.
