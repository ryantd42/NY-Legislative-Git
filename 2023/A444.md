# Criminalizes knowingly causing a minor to attend a place where exhibition of animal fighting is being conducted

**Bill ID:** A444
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A444 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A444)

## Summary

Criminalizes knowingly causing a minor to attend a place where exhibition of animal fighting is being conducted, establishes such offense is considered a misdemeanor.
