# Requires telemarketers to add a customer's telephone number to all do-not-call lists maintained by such telemarketer

**Bill ID:** S1769
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1769 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1769)

## Summary

Requires telemarketers to add a customer's telephone number to all do-not-call lists maintained by such telemarketer when a customer requests to add his or her number to any do-not-call list maintained by such telemarketer.
