# Extends the effectiveness of provisions relating to limiting the check cashing exemption for national banks and other regulated entities

**Bill ID:** S6930
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the effectiveness of provisions relating to limiting the check cashing exemption for national banks and other regulated entities to August 1, 2025.
