# Congratulating Samantha Vitale upon the occasion of earning the distinguished Gold Award, the most prestigious of Scouting honors

**Bill ID:** J850
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 850
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  CONGRATULATING  Samantha  Vitale upon the occasion
  of earning the distinguished Gold  Award,  the  most
  prestigious of Scouting honors
 
  WHEREAS,  Samantha  Vitale,  through  her  diligence  and  rigorous
efforts, has achieved Scouting's highest and most prestigious honor, the
Gold Award; the ceremony will be held on Tuesday, June 6, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Samantha Vitale is a member of Girl Scouts  of  the  United
States of America in Nassau County, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  America has achieved her greatest heights of glory through
the dedication, determination and tireless efforts  of  the  outstanding
citizens of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our
youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain her glory; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Girl Scouts of America and its members are dedicated to
the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  1916,  Senior  Girl Scouts have sought to cap their
years in Girl Scouting with the  prized  Gold  Award,  which  challenges
girls to change the world; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  order  to  earn the Gold Award, a girl must complete a
rigorous set of  service,  leadership  development,  and  skill-building
tasks  leading  up  to  the  final and most challenging requirement, the
design and completion of a Take Action Project; this project must be  an
issue  of  interest  to  the  girl  and her community, employ team-based
solutions, and educate and inspire others; and
 
  WHEREAS, For her project entitled "The Battle of Burnout,"  Samantha
Vitale addressed the issue of burnout among teens and young adults; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This outstanding young woman has been involved in Scouting
for years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has attained all of the
necessary achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, Samantha Vitale is a credit to her family, her school,  her
community,  her  State  and  her Nation, and no doubt her future will be
filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations,  in
recognition  of  the  significance  of  this meritorious achievement, to
congratulate and honor Samantha Vitale upon the occasion of earning  the
Gold Award; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Samantha Vitale.
