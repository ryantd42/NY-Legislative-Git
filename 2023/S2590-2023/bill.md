# Extends the term for claiming unclaimed property from three to five years

**Bill ID:** S2590
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Extends the term for claiming unclaimed property from three to five years.
