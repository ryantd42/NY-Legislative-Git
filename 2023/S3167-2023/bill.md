# Requires the department of agriculture and markets to promote participation in Nourish New York program for black indigenous and people of color site controlled farms

**Bill ID:** S3167
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the department of agriculture and markets to promote participation in Nourish New York, including financial and technical support, for black, indigenous and people of color site controlled farms.
