**Bill ID:** A1478
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1478 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1478)

## Summary

Requires banks to send account notifications in certain circumstances.
