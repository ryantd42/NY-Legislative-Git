# Relates to electronic financial disclosures of members and candidates of the legislature and requires creation of electronic version of disclosure statement

**Bill ID:** S3544A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to electronic financial disclosures of members and candidates of the legislature; requires creation of electronic version of disclosure statement; directs the commission on ethics and lobbying in government shall make available an editable, electronic, readable and searchable version of the annual statement of financial disclosure by January 1, 2025.
