  Section 1.  The general business law  is  amended  by  adding  a  new

  ยง  337-A. FONT SIZE FOR ADVERTISEMENTS. 1. THE OFFER, TERMS, AND EXPI-

 PUBLICATION FOR THE PURPOSE OF COMMERCIAL SALE OF A PRODUCT  OR  SERVICE
 SHALL  BE  IN  A  LEGIBLE  FONT NOT SMALLER THAN SIZE ELEVEN POINT FONT.

 SECTION,  THE BOARD SHALL CONDUCT AN INVESTIGATION AND UPON CONFIRMATION
 OF NON-CONFORMITY WITH SUBDIVISION ONE OF THIS SECTION SHALL  LEVY  UPON
 THE PUBLISHER OF SUCH PUBLICATION:
  A.  ON  FIRST  VIOLATION  OF  THIS  SECTION  A PENALTY OF ONE THOUSAND
 DOLLARS;
  B. ON SECOND VIOLATION OF THIS  SECTION  A  PENALTY  OF  TEN  THOUSAND
 DOLLARS;
  C.  ON  THIRD VIOLATION AND SUCCESSIVE VIOLATIONS THEREAFTER A PENALTY
 OF TWENTY-FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS.
  EACH DAY THAT SUCH PUBLICATION IS PUBLISHED  OR  OTHERWISE  CIRCULATED
 SHALL CONSTITUTE A SEPARATE VIOLATION OF THIS SECTION.
  3.  THE  STATE  CONSUMER PROTECTION BOARD SHALL MAINTAIN A REGISTRY OF
 OFFENSES AND SHALL INSTITUTE RULES AND REGULATIONS  FOR  THE  SUBMISSION
 AND ADJUDICATION OF ALLEGEDLY NON-CONFORMING PUBLICATIONS.
