# Honoring Daniel C. Thalmann posthumously upon the occasion of his induction into the Liberty Central School District Wall of Fame on June 23, 2023

**Bill ID:** J931
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 931
 
BY: Senator OBERACKER
 
  HONORING  Daniel C. Thalmann posthumously upon the
  occasion of his induction into the  Liberty  Central
  School District Wall of Fame on June 23, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, There are certain outstanding members of our community who,
through  their selfless commitment and dedication, have served to better
the quality of life in our community and have had a measurable  positive
impact  on  the  lives of its residents; Daniel C. Thalmann was one such
individual; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  honor  Daniel  C.
Thalmann  posthumously  upon  the  occasion  of  his  induction into the
Liberty Central School District Wall of Fame, to be  celebrated  with  a
reception  and ceremony on Friday, June 23, 2023, at Liberty High School
in Liberty, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Founded in 2011, the Liberty Central School  District  Wall
of  Fame  provides  a  wonderful  opportunity to honor and celebrate the
outstanding accomplishments of graduates or attendees  who  have  walked
the halls of Liberty's schools; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A 1981 graduate of Liberty High School, Daniel C. Thalmann
is  being  posthumously  honored  for  the  lasting  and  outstanding
contributions  he  made to society throughout his distinguished life and
career; and
 
  WHEREAS, Daniel C. Thalmann was born in Liberty, and truly dedicated
his life to his beloved community; and
 
  WHEREAS, After graduating, Daniel C.  Thalmann  proudly  served  his
country  as  a  member of the United States Coast Guard for three years;
upon the completion of his military service, he returned to Liberty  and
opened his business, Thalmann's Service Center, in 1989, where he always
looked out for his customers and helped those in need; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  a  steadfast  and  unwavering  commitment  to  his
community, Daniel C. Thalmann gave tirelessly of his time  and  energies
to  several  local  organizations,  including the Elks Club, Lions Club,
Liberty Rotary Club, Boys & Girls Club, Liberty Cub Scout Pack 717, AYSO
Soccer, Little Leagues, Soap Box Derby and the Liberty Fire  Department;
he was named a Paul Harris Fellow for his contributions to Rotary; and
 
  WHEREAS, A truly selfless man, Daniel C. Thalmann put his family and
the  Liberty  community above all else; his death in 2022 left a hole in
the community felt by many; and
 
  WHEREAS, Daniel C. Thalmann's infinite selflessness and  benevolence
will shine on through his family's vivid and happy memories; his insight
and  strength  will forever serve as a beacon of love, light and hope to
the countless lives he touched; and
 
  WHEREAS, Armed with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a  sense  of
compassion,  Daniel  C.  Thalmann leaves behind a legacy which will long
 
endure the passage of time and will remain as a comforting memory to all
who were privileged to have known and loved such an amazing man; he will
be  deeply  missed  and  truly  merits  the  grateful  tribute  of  this
Legislative Body; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Daniel C. Thalmann posthumously upon the occasion of his induction
into the Liberty Central School District Wall of Fame on June 23,  2023;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the family of Daniel C. Thalmann.
