# Provides for a 75-25 percent split in the New York city transit authority's operating surplus between the authority and the Metropolitan transportation authority

**Bill ID:** S4120
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for a 75-25 percent split in the New York city transit authority's operating surplus between the authority and the Metropolitan transit authority where previously the split was 50-50.
