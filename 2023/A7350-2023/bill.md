# Relates to the veterans employment task force

**Bill ID:** A7350
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Establishes a task force on veterans employment opportunities in the department of veterans' services.
