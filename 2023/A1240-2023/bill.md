# Relates to requiring hotels and motels to notify employees of the presence of bedbugs on the premises and requires development of training on eradication of bedbugs

**Bill ID:** A1240
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1240 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1240)

## Summary

Requires hotels and motels to notify employees of the presence of bedbugs on the premises; requires the development and administration of training on methods and procedures for the eradication of bedbugs upon hiring and each year thereafter.

  4. TRAINING SHALL BE  ADMINISTERED  WITHIN  A  REASONABLE  TIME  AFTER
 HIRING AND ONCE AGAIN EACH YEAR THEREAFTER.
  5.  TRAINING  SHALL  INCLUDE  BEDBUG  IDENTIFICATION,  PROCEDURES  FOR
