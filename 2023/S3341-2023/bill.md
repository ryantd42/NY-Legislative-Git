# Relates to increasing the age required to use ultraviolet radiation devices

**Bill ID:** S3341
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the age required to use ultraviolet radiation devices to twenty-one years of age.
