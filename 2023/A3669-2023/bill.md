# Relates to licensed pet breeders and brokers

**Bill ID:** A3669
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to licensed pet breeders and brokers; requires a pet dealer to only purchase a dog or cat for resale from a breeder or broker licensed by the United States department of agriculture or exempt from United States department of agriculture licensing per United States department of agriculture regulation, and such breeder or broker shall be in possession of all other licenses required for a breeder or broker by the state in which such breeder or broker is located.

  § 409.  LICENSED  PET  BREEDERS  AND  BROKERS.  NOTWITHSTANDING  ANY
 PROVISION OF LAW TO THE CONTRARY, A PET DEALER SHALL ONLY PURCHASE A DOG
 OR CAT FOR RESALE FROM A BREEDER OR BROKER LICENSED BY THE UNITED STATES

 AGRICULTURE LICENSING PER UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE  REGU-

 A BROKER THAT HAS RECEIVED:
  1.  A  DIRECT OR CRITICAL VIOLATION OF THE ANIMAL WELFARE ACT FROM THE
 UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE WITHIN THE PAST TWO YEARS;
  2. AN INDIRECT NO ACCESS VIOLATION ON EACH  OF  THE  TWO  MOST  RECENT
 INSPECTION  REPORTS  ISSUED  BY THE UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF AGRICUL-
 TURE;
  3. THREE OR MORE INDIRECT OR NON-CRITICAL  VIOLATIONS,  PROVIDED  SUCH
 VIOLATIONS  PERTAIN  TO THE HEALTH AND WELFARE OF AN ANIMAL AND WERE NOT
 ADMINISTRATIVE IN NATURE.  THE  INDIRECT  VIOLATIONS  PURSUANT  TO  THIS
 SUBDIVISION SHALL NOT INCLUDE THE VIOLATIONS PURSUANT TO SUBDIVISION TWO
 OF THIS SECTION; OR
  4.  ANY  VIOLATIONS ON A STATE INSPECTION REPORT RELATED TO THE HEALTH
 AND WELFARE OF THE ANIMAL FROM THE BREEDER OR BROKER'S STATE  OF  ORIGIN
 WITHIN THE PAST TWO YEARS, IF APPLICABLE.
