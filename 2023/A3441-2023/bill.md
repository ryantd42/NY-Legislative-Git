# Relates to training and registration requirements for certain kidney dialysis patient care technicians

**Bill ID:** A3441
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes training and registration requirements for kidney dialysis patient care technicians; such training shall include clearly defined criteria for annual evaluations.

 COMMISSIONER  OF  EDUCATION,  SHALL  ESTABLISH  STANDARDIZED  TRAINING

 REGISTER WITH THE DEPARTMENT TO BECOME A KIDNEY  DIALYSIS  PATIENT  CARE
