# Relates to the opening of liquor stores on certain days

**Bill ID:** S186
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S186 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S186)

## Summary

Provides that liquor stores may open at ten a.m. on Sundays.
