# Prohibits employers from requiring low-wage employees to enter into covenants not to compete and requires employers to notify potential employees of any requirement to enter into a covenant not to compete

**Bill ID:** A1807
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits employers from requiring low-wage employees to enter into covenants not to compete; requires employers to notify potential employees of any requirement to enter into a covenant not to compete.
