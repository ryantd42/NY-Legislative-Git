# Providing for the hours of meeting by the Senate for the years 2023-2024

**Bill ID:** R6
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R6 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/R6)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 6
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  SENATE  RESOLUTION  providing  for  the  hours  of
  meeting by the Senate for the years 2023-2024
 
  RESOLVED, That unless otherwise ordered, the hours  of  meeting  of
the  Senate  shall  be as follows: Mondays and Tuesdays at 3:00 P.M.,
Wednesdays and all other days at 11:00 A.M.
