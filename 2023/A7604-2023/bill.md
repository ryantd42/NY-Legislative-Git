# Repeals provision of vehicle and traffic law relating to periodic inspections of police vehicles

**Bill ID:** A7604
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to periodic inspections of police vehicles; removes exemption for police vehicles for periodic inspections.
