# Relates to a rebate for residents of Kings county who frequently commute across the Verrazzano-Narrows bridge

**Bill ID:** S13
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S13 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S13)

## Summary

Relates to a rebate for residents of Kings county who commute across the Verrazzano-Narrows bridge more than ten times per month while using a New York customer service E-ZPass account.
