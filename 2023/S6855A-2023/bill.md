# Prohibits the retention of any amount of payment due and owing for materials delivered and accepted for a public or private construction project

**Bill ID:** S6855AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the retention of any amount of payment due and owing for materials delivered and accepted for a public or private construction project.
