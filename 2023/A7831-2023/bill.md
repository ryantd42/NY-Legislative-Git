# Allows attendance at individualized education program (IEP) meetings to be covered under paid family leave

**Bill ID:** A7831
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jo Anne Simon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Allows attendance at individualized education program (IEP) meetings to be covered under paid family leave.
