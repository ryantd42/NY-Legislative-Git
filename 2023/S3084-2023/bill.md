# Exempts institutions of higher education from the metropolitan commuter transportation mobility tax

**Bill ID:** S3084
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Exempts institutions of higher education from the metropolitan commuter transportation mobility tax.
