# Prohibits a government entity from requiring individuals to receive a COVID-19 vaccine

**Bill ID:** S1264
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1264 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1264)

## Summary

Prohibits a government entity from requiring individuals to receive a COVID-19 vaccine.
