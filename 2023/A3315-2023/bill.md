## Summary

Relates to requiring approval by the comptroller for a toll increase by the New York state thruway authority.

 AN ACT to amend the public authorities law,  in  relation  to  requiring
  approval  by the comptroller for a toll increase by the New York state
  thruway authority

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Subdivision 8 of section 354 of the public authorities law,
 as amended by section 2 of part TT of chapter 54 of the laws of 2016, is
 amended to read as follows:
  8.  (A)  Subject to agreements with noteholders or bondholders, to fix
 and collect such fees, rentals and charges for the use of the thruway or
 any part thereof necessary or convenient, with  an  adequate  margin  of
 safety, to produce sufficient revenue to meet the expense of maintenance
 and  operation  and to fulfill the terms of any agreements made with the
 holders of its notes or bonds, and to establish the  rights  and  privi-
 leges granted upon payment thereof.
  (B) NOTWITHSTANDING ANY OTHER LAW, RULE OR REGULATION TO THE CONTRARY,
 PRIOR  TO  ANY  INCREASE  IN THE FEES, RENTALS AND CHARGES AUTHORIZED IN
 PARAGRAPH (A) OF THIS  SUBDIVISION,  THE  AUTHORITY  SHALL  SUBMIT  SUCH
 PROPOSED  INCREASE TO THE COMPTROLLER FOR REVIEW AND APPROVAL. THE COMP-
 TROLLER SHALL REVIEW ANY SUCH PROPOSED INCREASE AND  WITHIN  SIXTY  DAYS
 MAKE PUBLIC A REPORT OF HIS OR HER FINDINGS AND APPROVAL OR DISAPPROVAL.
 A  COPY  OF THE COMPTROLLER'S REPORT SHALL BE SENT TO THE AUTHORITY, THE
 GOVERNOR, THE CHAIR OF THE SENATE FINANCE COMMITTEE, THE  CHAIR  OF  THE
 ASSEMBLY  WAYS  AND  MEANS  COMMITTEE AND THE RANKING MINORITY MEMBER OF
 EACH OF SUCH COMMITTEES.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
