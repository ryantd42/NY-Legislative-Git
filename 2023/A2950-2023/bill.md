# Establishes a child care tax credit

**Bill ID:** A2950
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mike Reilly
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to establishing a child care tax credit; credit shall be twenty-five percent of the expenses of child care.
