# Congratulating Lori S. Chlad upon the occasion of her retirement after 42 years of distinguished service to Colgate University

**Bill ID:** J710
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 710
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Lori S. Chlad upon the occasion of
  her  retirement  after  42  years  of  distinguished
  service to Colgate University in Hamilton, New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge
those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  Lori S. Chlad upon the occasion of her retirement after 42
years of distinguished service to Colgate University  in  Hamilton,  New
York;  this momentous occasion will be celebrated at a party held in her
honor on Thursday, April 27, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Lori S. Chlad began  her  long  and  distinguished  career,
spanning  more  than  four  decades, at Colgate University on August 11,
1980, as secretary  to  the  director  of  the  Colgate  entrepreneurial
development center; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  September  of  1981,  Lori S. Chlad moved to the human
resources  department  where  she  held  various  positions,  including
employment services coordinator, assistant director, associate director,
and  director  of human resources; in November of 2012, she was promoted
to the title of  Associate  Vice  President  for  Human  Resources,  the
position from which she will officially retire on April 30, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  her  tenure,  Lori  S.  Chlad was committed to
providing support for current employees and assisting with the hiring of
new employees, who in turn, work tirelessly to provide students with one
of the best higher education experiences in the world; and
 
  WHEREAS, For the past 42 years, Lori  S.  Chlad  rendered  faithful,
conscientious  and  valuable  service  to  the  personnel  of  Colgate
University,  who  truly  make  it  a  distinctive,  leading  American
university; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  her  capacity  as  Associate  Vice President for Human
Resources, Lori S. Chlad served with loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS, In her official acts, Lori S. Chlad was governed by a  keen
sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  her  tenure with Colgate University, Lori S. Chlad
earned the admiration, esteem and  affection  of  her  colleagues;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Lori S. Chlad upon the occasion of her retirement after  42
years  of  distinguished  service to Colgate University, and to wish her
much success in all her future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Lori S. Chlad.
