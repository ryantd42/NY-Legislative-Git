# Creates a pilot program to examine the effects of waiving the state civil service examination fee

**Bill ID:** A6771
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates a pilot program to examine the effects of waiving the state civil service examination fee; requires an annual report on the results of such program.
