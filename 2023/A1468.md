# Authorizes the department of economic development to give a preference to any tourist promotion agency that is promoting the sport of rugby

**Bill ID:** A1468
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Conrad
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1468 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1468)

## Summary

Authorizes the department of economic development to give a preference to any tourist promotion agency that is promoting the sport of rugby.
