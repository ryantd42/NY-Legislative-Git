# Includes within the class D felony of assault in the second degree, the assault of a driver of a for-hire vehicle or transportation network company driver

**Bill ID:** A1840
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Ron Kim
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes within the class D felony of assault in the second degree, the assault  of a driver of a for-hire vehicle or transportation network company driver.
