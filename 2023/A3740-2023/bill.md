# Relates to the legislative power of the University of the State of New York

**Bill ID:** A3740
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe DeStefano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to legislative review of rules and regulations adopted by the board of regents that will increase school district costs.
