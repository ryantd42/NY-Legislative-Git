# Requires the Department of Homeless Services to notify the affected community board prior to locating transitional housing for the homeless

**Bill ID:** S1917
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1917 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1917)

## Summary

Relates to requiring the Department of Homeless Services to notify the affected community board prior to locating transitional housing for the homeless; requires written notification sixty days before the department enters into a contractual arrangement with a transitional housing provider or otherwise finalizes its decision to use or expand a location as transitional housing.

  Section  1. The administrative code of the city of New York is amended

  § 21-324.1 COMMUNITY NOTIFICATION REQUIREMENT. 1. WHEN THE  DEPARTMENT
 INTENDS TO USE A NEW LOCATION AS TRANSITIONAL HOUSING FOR ELIGIBLE HOME-
 LESS  FAMILIES  AND  INDIVIDUALS,  OR  EXPAND AN EXISTING LOCATION WHERE

 CATION IN WRITING TO THE COMMUNITY BOARD FOR THE COMMUNITY  DISTRICT  IN
 WHICH  THE  TRANSITIONAL  HOUSING  WILL BE LOCATED. SUCH NOTICE SHALL BE
 PROVIDED AT LEAST  SIXTY  DAYS  BEFORE  THE  DEPARTMENT  ENTERS  INTO  A
 CONTRACTUAL  ARRANGEMENT  WITH A TRANSITIONAL HOUSING PROVIDER OR OTHER-
 WISE FINALIZES ITS DECISION TO USE OR EXPAND A LOCATION AS  TRANSITIONAL
 HOUSING.
  2.  THE  NOTIFICATION  SHALL  INCLUDE  THE ADDRESS OF THE TRANSITIONAL
 HOUSING, THE NUMBER OF PEOPLE WHO WILL BE HOUSED, THE NAME OF THE PERSON
 OR ENTITY OPERATING THE TRANSITIONAL HOUSING, THE NAME OF ANY  ORGANIZA-
 TION,  WHETHER  FOR-PROFIT  OR  NOT-FOR-PROFIT,  THAT  WILL BE PROVIDING
 SERVICES TO THE OCCUPANTS OF THE TRANSITIONAL HOUSING, THE TYPE OF TRAN-
 SITIONAL HOUSING, A DESCRIPTION OF THE SERVICES THAT WILL  BE  PROVIDED,
 THE  RATIONALE  FOR  CHOOSING SUCH LOCATION, AND AN APPROXIMATE SCHEDULE
 FOR WHEN SUCH TRANSITIONAL HOUSING WILL BE USED OR EXPANDED.
  3. THIS SECTION SHALL APPLY TO ANY TRANSITIONAL HOUSING FACILITY OPER-
 ATED OR CONTRACTED FOR, BY OR ON BEHALF OF THE DEPARTMENT, REGARDLESS OF
 ITS SIZE AND CAPACITY.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
