# Requires batteries for bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters to be tested and approved by certain testing laboratories

**Bill ID:** A7889AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Christopher Eachus
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires batteries for bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters to be tested and approved by certain testing laboratories.
