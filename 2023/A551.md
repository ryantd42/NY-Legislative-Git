# Relates to reducing the speed for owner liability for failure of operator to comply with certain posted maximum speed limits

**Bill ID:** A551
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A551 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A551)

## Summary

Reduces the speed for owner liability for failure of operator to comply with certain posted maximum speed limits to more than seven miles per hour over such posted maximum speed limits.
