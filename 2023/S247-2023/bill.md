# Creates a sales and use tax exemption for school buses and related items

**Bill ID:** S247
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S247 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S247)

## Summary

Creates a sales and use tax exemption for school buses and related items.
