# Establishes the volunteer firefighters of New York higher education learning plan

**Bill ID:** A1397
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1397 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1397)

## Summary

Establishes the volunteer firefighters of New York higher education learning plan.
