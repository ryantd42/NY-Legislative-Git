# Extends provisions of law relating to the adoption and submission of an annual budget by the Trustees of the Freeholders and Commonalty of the town of Southampton

**Bill ID:** A7645
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to the adoption and submission of an annual budget by the Trustees of the Freeholders and Commonalty of the town of Southampton from January 1, 2024 until January 1, 2025.
