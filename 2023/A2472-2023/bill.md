# Provides for the issuance of free hunting, fishing and trapping licenses to members of the Seneca nation who reside in the state

**Bill ID:** A2472
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the issuance of free hunting, fishing and trapping licenses to members of the Seneca nation who reside in the state.
