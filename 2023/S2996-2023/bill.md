# Requires cell towers to be equipped with a back-up power source in case of a power outage

**Bill ID:** S2996
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires cell towers to be equipped with a back-up power source in case of a power outage.
