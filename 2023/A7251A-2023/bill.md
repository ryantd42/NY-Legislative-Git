# Clarifies provisions related to the overtime ceiling for corrections officers of the Westchester county correction department

**Bill ID:** A7251A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Clarifies provisions related to the overtime ceiling for correction officers of the Westchester county correction department for the purposes of retirement benefits.
