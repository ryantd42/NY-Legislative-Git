# Directs the department of transportation to conduct a study to evaluate the standard approach of setting or adjusting speed limits

**Bill ID:** S6904
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the department of transportation to conduct a study to evaluate the standard approach of setting or adjusting speed limits, specifically the usage of 85th percentile rule.
