# Relates to sharing information obtained from applicants and licensees

**Bill ID:** S1047AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S1047A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1047A)

## Summary

Relates to sharing information obtained from applicants and licensees and requesting financial institutions for the purpose of consideration and compliance.
