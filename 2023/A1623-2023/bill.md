# Establishes a program for certain elderly inmates

**Bill ID:** A1623
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Latoya Joyner
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A1623 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1623)

## Summary

Establishes a program for adults over fifty who are under community supervision or who will be released from a state correctional facility.
