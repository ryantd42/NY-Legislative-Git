# Provides free use of state parks for certified first responders who participated in the rescue effort at the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001

**Bill ID:** A3142
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mike Reilly
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to providing free use of state parks for certified first responders who participated in the rescue effort at the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001.
