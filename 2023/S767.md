# Relates to operation of an all-terrain vehicle by a minor on public or private property

**Bill ID:** S767
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S767 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S767)

## Summary

Relates to operation of an all-terrain vehicle by a minor on public or private property; requires a helmet on private and public property.
