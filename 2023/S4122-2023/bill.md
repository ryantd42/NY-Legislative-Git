# Provides that conviction of assault on a social worker which prevents him or her from performing a lawful duty is a felony

**Bill ID:** S4122
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides that conviction of assault on a social worker which prevents him or her from performing a lawful duty is a felony.
