# Creates the concentrated animal feeding operation environmental compliance revolving loan program

**Bill ID:** A2620
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates the concentrated animal feeding operation environmental compliance revolving loan program.
