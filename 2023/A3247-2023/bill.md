# Relates to providing an income tax credit for retired disabled police officers

**Bill ID:** A3247
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides a $750 income tax credit for retired disabled police officers.
