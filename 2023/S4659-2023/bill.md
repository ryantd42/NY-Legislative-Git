# Requires certain procedures for charter school students facing suspension

**Bill ID:** S4659
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires certain procedures for charter school students facing suspension; makes such procedures consistent with other schools.
