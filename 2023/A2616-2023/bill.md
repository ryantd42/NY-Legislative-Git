# Relates to exempting heat and power generating equipment from taxation

**Bill ID:** A2616
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes that combined heat and power generating equipment shall be exempt from taxation.
