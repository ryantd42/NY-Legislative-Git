# Permits pregnant women to enroll in health insurance during a special enrollment period without penalty

**Bill ID:** A2656
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Latrice Walker
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Permits pregnant women to enroll in the state health insurance exchange at any time without penalty.
