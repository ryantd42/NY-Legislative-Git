# Relates to the purchase or lease of zero emission vehicles and charging or fueling infrastructure

**Bill ID:** A990
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A990 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A990)

## Summary

Relates to the purchase or lease of zero emission vehicles and charging or fueling infrastructure by state agencies and the necessary requirements when making such procurements.
