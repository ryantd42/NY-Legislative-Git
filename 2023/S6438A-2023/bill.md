# Directs that certain proceeds from the sale of forfeited property be deposited into the general fund of the respective state or local government of which the claiming agent is an agency or part; repealer

**Bill ID:** S6438A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs that certain proceeds from the sale of forfeited property be deposited into the general fund of the respective state or local government of which the claiming agent is an agency or part rather than a law enforcement purposes subaccount.
