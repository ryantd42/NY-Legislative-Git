# Extends provisions of law relating to the New York telecommunications relay service center

**Bill ID:** A3164
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to the New York telecommunications relay service center from June 30, 2024 until June 30, 2027.
