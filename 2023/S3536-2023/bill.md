# Prohibits the packaging, sale and distribution of confectionery and candy designed to resemble a tobacco product

**Bill ID:** S3536
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the packaging, sale and distribution of confectionery and candy designed to resemble a tobacco product.

  D.  "CONFECTIONERY  OR  CANDY  PRODUCT  DESIGNED TO RESEMBLE A TOBACCO
 PRODUCT" SHALL MEAN ANY CONFECTIONERY OR CANDY PRODUCT MADE AS A FACSIM-
 ILE OF A CIGARETTE, PIPE OR CIGAR AND IS CLEARLY DESIGNED TO SIMULATE OR

  2. NO PERSON, FIRM, OR CORPORATION SHALL MANUFACTURE, SELL OR HOLD FOR
 SALE CONFECTIONERY OR CANDY PRODUCTS WHICH ARE MADE AS A FACSIMILE OF  A
 CIGARETTE, PIPE OR CIGAR AND ARE CLEARLY DESIGNED TO SIMULATE OR MIMIC A
 TOBACCO PRODUCT.
  3.  NO  PERSON,  FIRM, OR CORPORATION MANUFACTURING OR SELLING CONFEC-
 TIONERY OR CANDY  PRODUCTS  SHALL  USE,  FOR  ADVERTISING  OR  PACKAGING

 SIMILAR TO THE NAME, SYMBOL, DEVICE OR MARK USED IN ADVERTISING OR  SALE
 OF A TOBACCO PRODUCT.
  4.  NO  OTHER  PROVISION  OF LAW AUTHORIZING THE SALE OF CONFECTIONERY
 AND/OR CANDY PRODUCTS SHALL AUTHORIZE THE SALE OF  CONFECTIONERY  AND/OR
 CANDY PRODUCTS DESIGNED TO RESEMBLE A TOBACCO PRODUCT.
  5.  THIS  SECTION SHALL APPLY TO CONFECTIONERY AND CANDY OF ALL TYPES,
 AND SIMILAR PRODUCTS THAT ARE REGARDED AS CANDY OR CONFECTIONERY,  BASED
 ON THEIR NORMAL USE OR MARKETING.
  6.  VIOLATION  OF  THE  PROVISIONS OF THIS SECTION SHALL BE AN OFFENSE
 SUBJECT TO A CIVIL FINE OF NOT LESS THAN ONE HUNDRED DOLLARS AND NO MORE
 THAN FIVE HUNDRED DOLLARS.
