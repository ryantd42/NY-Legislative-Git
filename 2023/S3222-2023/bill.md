# Establishes an emergency insulin program

**Bill ID:** S3222
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes an emergency insulin program and corresponding emergency insulin program trust fund to facilitate emergency access to analog insulins.

 SECTION 2894. EMERGENCY INSULIN PROGRAM.
  § 2894. EMERGENCY INSULIN PROGRAM. 1. THE COMMISSIONER  IS  AUTHORIZED

 LATER THAN APRIL FIRST, TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FOUR.

  (I) "ANALOG INSULINS" MEANS AT A MINIMUM  SHORT-ACTING,  RAPID-ACTING,
 INTERMEDIATE AND LONG ACTING INSULIN DRUGS APPROVED BY THE FOOD AND DRUG
 ADMINISTRATION  THAT, WHEN ADMINISTERED, TO CERTAIN RECIPIENTS ARE LIFE-
 SAVING, AND HELP AVOID SERIOUS ADVERSE EFFECTS ASSOCIATED WITH TYPE  ONE

  § 95-1. EMERGENCY INSULIN PROGRAM  TRUST  FUND.  1.  THERE  IS  HEREBY
 ESTABLISHED  IN  THE  JOINT  CUSTODY  OF  THE  STATE COMPTROLLER AND THE
 COMMISSIONER OF TAXATION AND FINANCE A SPECIAL FUND TO BE KNOWN  AS  THE
 "EMERGENCY INSULIN PROGRAM TRUST FUND".
  2.  SUCH FUND SHALL CONSIST OF ALL MONIES APPROPRIATED FOR ITS PURPOSE
 AND ALL MONIES RECEIVED, OR TO BE PAID INTO OR CREDITED TO SUCH FUND  BY
 ANALOG  INSULIN  MANUFACTURERS  PURSUANT  TO SUBDIVISION FOUR OF SECTION
 TWENTY-EIGHT HUNDRED NINETY-FOUR OF THE  PUBLIC  HEALTH  LAW.  NOTHING
 CONTAINED  HEREIN  SHALL  PREVENT  THE STATE FROM RECEIVING ASSESSMENTS,
 GRANTS, GIFTS OR BEQUESTS FOR THE PURPOSES OF THE  FUND  AS  DEFINED  IN
 THIS SECTION AND DEPOSITING THEM INTO THE FUND ACCORDING TO LAW.
  3.  MONIES OF THE FUND SHALL BE EXPENDED ONLY TO SUPPORT THE EMERGENCY
 INSULIN PROGRAM ESTABLISHED PURSUANT TO ARTICLE TWENTY-EIGHT-CC  OF  THE
 PUBLIC  HEALTH  LAW  ADMINISTERED  BY THE COMMISSIONER OF HEALTH AND FOR
 FUNDING EMERGENCY ACCESS TO ANALOG INSULINS.
  4. MONIES SHALL BE PAYABLE FROM THE FUND ON THE AUDIT AND  WARRANT  OF
 THE  COMPTROLLER  ON VOUCHERS APPROVED AND CERTIFIED BY THE COMMISSIONER
 OF HEALTH.
  § 3. This act shall take effect immediately.
