# Relates to to the setback requirement in the Peconic and Gardiner's Bays for the Suffolk County Lease Program and Sugar Kelp production

**Bill ID:** S6558
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Decreases the setback requirement in the Peconic and Gardiner's Bays for the Suffolk County Lease Program and Sugar Kelp production from 1,000 feet to 500 feet.
