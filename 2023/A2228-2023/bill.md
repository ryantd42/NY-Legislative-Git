# Relates to the Sanborn Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association, Inc., in relation to its purpose and the use of fire insurance premiums

**Bill ID:** A2228
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo J. Morinello
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Expands the use of foreign fire insurance premium taxes by the Sanborn Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association, Inc.
