# Requires the state athletic commission to promulgate regulations for authorized combined professional and amateur mixed martial arts events

**Bill ID:** A2770
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the state athletic commission to promulgate regulations for authorized combined professional and amateur mixed martial arts events no later than June 30, 2023.
