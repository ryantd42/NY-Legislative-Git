# Relates to tax abatement for rent-controlled and rent regulated property occupied by and real property owned by senior citizens or persons with disabilities

**Bill ID:** A2122A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nily Rozic
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Relates to tax abatement for rent-controlled and rent regulated property occupied by and real property owned by senior citizens or persons with disabilities.
