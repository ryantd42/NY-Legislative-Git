# Establishes the chief administrator of the courts shall submit annually a report on the Drug Treatment Court Program during the preceding year

**Bill ID:** S2591
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the chief administrator of the courts shall submit annually a report on the administration, function and effectiveness of the Drug Treatment Court Program during the preceding year.
