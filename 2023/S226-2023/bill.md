# Prohibits the use of DNA phenotyping in criminal prosecutions and proceedings

**Bill ID:** S226
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michael Gianaris
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S226 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S226)

## Summary

Prohibits the use of DNA phenotyping in criminal prosecutions and proceedings.
