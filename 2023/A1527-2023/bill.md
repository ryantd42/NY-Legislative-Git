# Relates to excluding expenditures for school resource officers employed by a municipality and assigned to a school district from tax levy limitations

**Bill ID:** A1527
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1527 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1527)

## Summary

Excludes expenditures for school resource officers employed by a municipality and assigned to a school district from tax levy limitations.
