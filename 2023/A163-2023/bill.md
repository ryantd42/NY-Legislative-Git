# Establishes a special fund to study the development of a Lyme disease vaccine

**Bill ID:** A163
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A163 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A163)

## Summary

Establishes a special fund to examine and evaluate current research and progress in the development of a Lyme disease vaccine.
