# Requires e-mail service providers to implement a procedure to authenticate an individual's identity when such individual creates an e-mail account

**Bill ID:** A2357
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires e-mail service providers to implement a procedure to authenticate an individual's identity when such individual creates a new e-mail account.
