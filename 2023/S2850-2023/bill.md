# Relates to the purchase of domestically produced personal protective equipment

**Bill ID:** S2850
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to the purchase of personal protective equipment and medical supplies produced or manufactured in the state of New York.
