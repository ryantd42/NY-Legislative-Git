# Requires certain disclosures in advertisements involving virtual tokens

**Bill ID:** A2318
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires certain disclosures in advertisements involving virtual tokens.
