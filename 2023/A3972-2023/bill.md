# Establishes "Jeremiah's Law"

**Bill ID:** A3972
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Enacts "Jeremiah's law"; establishes the offense of filing a false report concerning a minor.
