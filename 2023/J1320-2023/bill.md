# Commemorating the 15th Anniversary of the Best In Show Pet Resort of Mineola, New York

**Bill ID:** J1320
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1320
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMEMORATING  the 15th Anniversary of the Best In
  Show Pet Resort of Mineola, New York
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize those
small businesses within the State of New York which  contribute  to  the
economic health and vitality of the communities they serve; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  15th  Anniversary  of  the  Best In Show Pet Resort of
Mineola, New York, to be celebrated at an event on  Saturday,  June  10,
2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, After traveling across the country visiting all the premier
pet resorts, Dr. Raymond Polley, DVM founded the Best In Show Pet Resort
in 2008, to bring that outstanding pet care to Long Island; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition  to establishing the Best In Show Pet Resort,
Dr.  Raymond Polley founded  Garden  City  Veterinary  Care  which  was
relocated to Nassau county in 2008; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Today, the Best In Show Pet Resort is the first all-suite,
state of the art, luxury pet care facility in  Mineola,  and  under  the
luminous  leadership  of General Manager Jennifer Nucci, it remains true
to its mission of providing their customer's pets  with  the  whole  dog
experience; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Each  and every dog receives customized care and attention
from friendly,  experienced  pet  care  attendants  including  exercise,
socialization,  mental  stimulation,  or life skills to strengthen their
overall wellbeing; and
 
  WHEREAS, Small businesses such as the Best In Show Pet Resort  exert
a  strong,  positive  influence  on the communities they serve and are a
crucial element in the economy of New York State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the  15th  Anniversary  of  the  Best In Show Pet Resort of
Mineola, New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Dr.  Raymond Polley, Owner, and Jennifer Nucci, General
Manager, Best In Show Pet Resort.
