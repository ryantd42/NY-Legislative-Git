# Relates to sentencing for the crime of aggravated cruelty to animals

**Bill ID:** A158
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A158 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A158)

## Summary

Relates to sentencing for the crime of aggravated cruelty to animals; removes the two year imprisonment cap.
