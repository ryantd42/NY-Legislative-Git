# Requires liability insurance for bicycles, bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters in N.Y. city

**Bill ID:** A7940
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires liability insurance for bicycles, bicycles with electric assist and electric scooters in cities having a population of one million or more.
