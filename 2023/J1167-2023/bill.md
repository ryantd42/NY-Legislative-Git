# Commending Salutatorian Maria Santos of Garden City High School, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** J1167
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1167
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING  Salutatorian  Maria  Santos  of Garden
  City High School, in recognition of her  outstanding
  accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions, to honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to  commend
Salutatorian  Maria Santos of Garden City High School, in recognition of
her outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian Maria Santos represents the best of  developed
potential  inherent  in  our  most  precious  resource,  our  youth; her
achievements have brought enduring honor to her family and community and
should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian Maria Santos may now stand with pride  as  she
assesses  her  achievements,  experiences the satisfaction of her labors
and the joy of her accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of
a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Salutatorian  Maria  Santos  of  Garden  City  High  School, in
recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Salutatorian Maria Santos.
