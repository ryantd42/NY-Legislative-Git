# Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage winter flounder

**Bill ID:** S6954
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage winter flounder from 2023 to 2026.
