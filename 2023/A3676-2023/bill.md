# Requires insurance companies to issue joint checks for payment to an insured and a health care provider in certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** A3676
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires insurance companies to issue joint checks for payment to an insured and a health care provider in certain circumstances.
