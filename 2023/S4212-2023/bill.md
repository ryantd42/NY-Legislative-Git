# Relates to waiving certain fees associated with the issuance of an enhanced driver's license

**Bill ID:** S4212
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to waiving all additional fees associated with the issuance of an enhanced driver's license if such license is required within thirty days of the issuance or reissuance of a standard driver's license.
