# Relates to regulating the sale of certain balloons

**Bill ID:** A404
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A404 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A404)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of electrically conductive balloons.
