# Establishes the crime of sexual harassment

**Bill ID:** A7865
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jo Anne Simon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the crime of sexual harassment; makes such crime a class A misdemeanor.
