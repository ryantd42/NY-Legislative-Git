# Relates to hiring and promoting employees on a permanent basis at municipal and/or authority owned or operated airports

**Bill ID:** A6736AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the hiring and promotion of employees on a permanent basis at municipal and/or authority owned or operated airports.
