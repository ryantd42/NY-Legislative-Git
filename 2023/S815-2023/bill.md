# Relates to members of transportation authorities

**Bill ID:** S815
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S815 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S815)

## Summary

Relates to members of transportation authorities; provides that any local or statewide transit advocacy organization may recommend one or more transit dependent individuals to be considered; makes related provisions.
