# Relates to conducting appeals, within the department of family assistance, by means of electronic communications systems conference with independent hearing officers

**Bill ID:** A1729
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Permits appeals to the department of family assistance to be conducted by means of a conference telephone, video conference or similar communications systems with and by independent hearing officers.
