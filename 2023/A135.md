# Relates to establishing a payroll tax for the purpose of child care for families with children under three years of age within certain counties

**Bill ID:** A135
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A135 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A135)

## Summary

Establishes a payroll tax in the counties of Onondaga, Madison, Cortland, Oswego and Cayuga on certain employers for the purposes of addressing child care affordability, accessibility, and quality for families with children under three years of age.
