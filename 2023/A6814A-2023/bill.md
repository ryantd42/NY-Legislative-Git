# Requires fire-resistant material applicators to undergo training and certification

**Bill ID:** A6814AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monica P. Wallace
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires fire-resistant material applicators to undergo training and certification; imposes fines for violations.
