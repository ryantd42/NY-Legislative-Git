# Providing for the election of Wayne P. Jackson to the office of Sergeant-at-Arms

**Bill ID:** E4
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E4 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/E4)

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 4
 
BY: M. of A. Aubry
 
  PROVIDING  for the election of Wayne P. Jackson to
  the office of Sergeant-at-Arms of the Assembly
 
  RESOLVED, That  Wayne  P.  Jackson  be  and  he  hereby  is  elected
Sergeant-at-Arms of the Assembly for the years 2023 and 2024.
