# Establishes an elderly dental insurance coverage program; appropriation

**Bill ID:** S45
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S45 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S45)

## Summary

Establishes an elderly dental insurance coverage program; specifies eligibility requirements; specifies penalties for fraud and abuse; appropriates $20,000,000 therefor.
