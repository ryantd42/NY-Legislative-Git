# Relates to requiring the re-inspection of dwellings with mold violations

**Bill ID:** A3065
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the re-inspection of dwellings with mold violations.
