# Relates to the standard of care in the treatment of substance abuse disorder

**Bill ID:** A897
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A897 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A897)

## Summary

Requires the standard of care in the treatment of substance abuse disorder be held to the same standard of care as medication assisted treatment.
