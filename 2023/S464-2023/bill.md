# Prohibits consumer reporting agencies from requiring or requesting certain consumer information

**Bill ID:** S464
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S464 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S464)

## Summary

Relates to prohibiting consumer reporting agencies from requiring or requesting a copy of a consumer's social security card when furnishing consumer reports or investigative consumer reports to third parties.
