# Relates to the Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association of Lewiston Fire Company No. 1

**Bill ID:** A2216
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo J. Morinello
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Expands the use of foreign fire insurance premium taxes by the Volunteer and Exempt Firemen's Benevolent Association of Lewiston Fire Company No. 1.
