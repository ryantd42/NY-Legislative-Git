# Establishes a no fare program for emergency responders on September 11th

**Bill ID:** A2780
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a no fare program for transportation on the Long Island Rail Road for emergency responders on September 11th.
