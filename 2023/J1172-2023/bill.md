# Commending Salutatorian Yesica Arriola Hercules of Uniondale High School, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** J1172
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1172
 
BY: Senator THOMAS
 
  COMMENDING Salutatorian Yesica Arriola Hercules of
  Uniondale  High  School,  in  recognition  of  her
  outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions,  to  honor the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to  commend
Salutatorian Yesica  Arriola  Hercules  of  Uniondale  High  School,  in
recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian Yesica Arriola Hercules represents the best of
developed  potential  inherent in our most precious resource, our youth;
her achievements have brought enduring honor to her family and community
and should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS, Salutatorian Yesica Arriola Hercules  may  now  stand  with
pride  as she assesses her achievements, experiences the satisfaction of
her labors and the joy of her accomplishments, eager  to  face  the  new
experiences of a challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Salutatorian Yesica Arriola Hercules of Uniondale  High  School,
in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Salutatorian Yesica Arriola Hercules.
