# Requires not-for-profit hospitals inform patients that low to moderate income persons may qualify for help with medical bills through such hospitals charitable programs

**Bill ID:** S1476
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1476 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1476)

## Summary

Requires not-for-profit hospitals inform patients that low to moderate-income persons may qualify for help with medical bills through such hospitals' charitable programs by disclosing such information to patients, posting such information conspicuously, and through social workers.
