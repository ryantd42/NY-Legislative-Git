# Prohibits the administration of psychoactive drugs to an animal in a zoo in certain situations

**Bill ID:** A3545
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Gonzalez-Rojas
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the administration of psychoactive drugs to an animal in a zoo in order to allow another animal to procreate with such animal.
