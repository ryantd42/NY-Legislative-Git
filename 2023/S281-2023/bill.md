# Relates to periods of limitation applicable to timeliness of prosecution

**Bill ID:** S281
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S281 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S281)

## Summary

Excludes any period of time during which a person who was wrongly convicted of a crime was serving a sentence of incarceration from the time limit applicable to timeliness of prosecution.
