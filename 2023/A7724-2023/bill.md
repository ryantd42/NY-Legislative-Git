# Relates to restricted clinical laboratory licenses

**Bill ID:** A7724
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Sarah Clark
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Updates the scope of restricted clinical laboratory licenses to include the practice of molecular testing.
