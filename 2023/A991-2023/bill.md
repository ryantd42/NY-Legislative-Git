# Exempts certain income and resources provided to persons enrolled in certain pilot programs in determining eligibility for public assistance benefits

**Bill ID:** A991
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A991 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A991)

## Summary

Exempts certain income and resources provided to persons enrolled in certain pilot programs in determining eligibility for public assistance benefits; requires federal waivers be sought where necessary.
