# Increases the number of county court judges in Schenectady county

**Bill ID:** S6996A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases the number of county court judges in Schenectady county from one judge to two.
