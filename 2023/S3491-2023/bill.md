# Establishes a home heating oil rebate program

**Bill ID:** S3491
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alexis Weik
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes a home heating oil rebate program for residential home heating oil customers to receive a rebate of one dollar per gallon of grade number two heating oil purchased from July 1, 2023 to June 30, 2024, of a limit up to five hundred gallons.
