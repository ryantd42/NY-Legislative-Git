# Relates to appeals made by employers or insurance carriers for modification, rescission or review of an award or decision

**Bill ID:** S7025
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to appeals made by employers or insurance carriers for modification, rescission or review of an award or decision.
