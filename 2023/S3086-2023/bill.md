# Requires applicants for the renewal of a driver's license to prove they have acceptable vision to operate a motor vehicle

**Bill ID:** S3086
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires applicants for the renewal of a driver's license to prove they have acceptable vision to operate a motor vehicle.
