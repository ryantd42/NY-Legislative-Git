# Classifies worksite employees of professional employers as employees of the professional employer for certain insurance purposes

**Bill ID:** S7134
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Ramos
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Classifies worksite employees of professional employers as employees of the professional employer for certain insurance purposes.
