# Relates to falsely filing an extreme risk protection order

**Bill ID:** A3359
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mike Reilly
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the crime of falsely filing an extreme risk protection order to be punishable as a class E felony.
