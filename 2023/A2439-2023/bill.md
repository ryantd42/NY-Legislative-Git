# Relates to the powers and duties of the chancellor of education in the city of New York; extends the provisions of chapter 91 of the laws of 2002 amending the education law and other laws

**Bill ID:** A2439
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Colton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the powers and duties of the chancellor of education in the city of New York; relates to the reorganization of the New York city school construction authority, board of education and community boards.
