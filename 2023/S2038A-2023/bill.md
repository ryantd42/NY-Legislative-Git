# Increases from $300 to $725 the monthly rent subsidy payable for housing for a foster child living independently in certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** S2038A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jabari Brisport
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases from $300 to $725 the monthly rent subsidy payable for housing for a foster child living independently in certain circumstances.
