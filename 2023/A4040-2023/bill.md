# Makes certain liquor license fees payable in installments

**Bill ID:** A4040
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Makes certain liquor license fees payable in installments; reduces the fee for certain types of licenses.
