# Relates to first responders coming into contact with saliva by spitting, throwing, tossing, or expelling from or by an individual

**Bill ID:** S3121AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to first responders, including correction officers, coming into contact with saliva by spitting, throwing, tossing, or expelling from or by an individual.
