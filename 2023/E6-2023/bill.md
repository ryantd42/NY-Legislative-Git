# Provides for the adoption of the Rules of the Assembly for 2023-2024

**Bill ID:** E6
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E6 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/E6)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 6
 
BY: M. of A. Peoples-Stokes
 
  PROVIDING  for  the  adoption  of the Rules of the
  Assembly for 2023-2024
 
  RESOLVED, That  the  Rules  of  the  Assembly  for  2021  and  2022,
including  the  temporary  emergency  procedures  set  forth in Assembly
Resolution 854 adopted on March 30, 2020, as last amended, be adopted as
the Rules of the Assembly for 2023 and 2024.
