# Prohibits the application of fail-first or step therapy protocols to coverage for the diagnosis and treatment of mental health conditions

**Bill ID:** A6704
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the application of fail-first or step therapy protocols to coverage for the diagnosis and treatment of mental health conditions.
