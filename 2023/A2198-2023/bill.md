# Relates to coverage for, and notice to insureds of, colorectal cancer screenings; repealer

**Bill ID:** A2198
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Makes technical amendments to chapter requiring insurance coverage for colorectal cancer screenings.
