# Relates to providing notice of quorum requirements at zoning board of appeals and planning board meetings

**Bill ID:** A3026
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to providing notice of quorum requirements to applicants for building or site permits, variances, or subdivision plats at zoning board of appeals, local planning board, county planning board, and regional planning council meetings.

  Section 1. Subdivision 13 of section 81-a of the general city  law  is
 amended by adding a new paragraph (c) to read as follows:
  (C)  NOTICE  OF QUORUM REQUIREMENTS. AT THE START OF EACH MEETING OF A
 BOARD OF APPEALS AT WHICH THE ADOPTION OF  A  MOTION  OR  RESOLUTION  TO
 APPROVE  AN  APPLICATION  FOR  A  BUILDING  OR SITE PERMIT, VARIANCE, OR
 SUBDIVISION PLAT SHALL BE VOTED UPON PURSUANT TO PARAGRAPH (A)  OF  THIS
 SUBDIVISION,  THE  CHAIRPERSON  OF THE BOARD OF APPEALS SHALL INFORM ANY
 APPLICANT FOR A BUILDING OR SITE PERMIT, VARIANCE, OR  SUBDIVISION  PLAT
 OF THE FOLLOWING:
  (I)  THE  NUMBER OF MEMBERS OF THE BOARD OF APPEALS WHO ARE PRESENT OR
 ABSENT AT SUCH MEETING;
  (II) THE NUMBER OF VOTES OF THE MEMBERS OF THE BOARD  OF  APPEALS  WHO
 ARE  CURRENTLY  PRESENT  AT  SUCH MEETING THAT IS REQUIRED TO APPROVE AN
 APPLICATION FOR A BUILDING OR SITE PERMIT, VARIANCE, OR SUBDIVISION PLAT
 PURSUANT TO PARAGRAPH (A) OF THIS SUBDIVISION; AND
  (III) THE RIGHT OF SUCH APPLICANT TO TABLE  THEIR  APPLICATION  FOR  A
 BUILDING  OR  SITE  PERMIT, VARIANCE, OR SUBDIVISION PLAT UNTIL A FUTURE
 MEETING OF THE BOARD OF APPEALS IF  SUCH  APPLICANT  BELIEVES  THAT  THE
 NUMBER  OF  MEMBERS OF THE BOARD OF APPEALS WHO ARE PRESENT OR ABSENT AT
 SUCH MEETING WILL  SUBSTANTIALLY  IMPAIR  SUCH  APPLICANT'S  CHANCES  OF
 GETTING  THEIR  APPLICATION  FOR A BUILDING OR SITE PERMIT, VARIANCE, OR
 SUBDIVISION PLAT APPROVED.
