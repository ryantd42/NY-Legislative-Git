# Prohibits the sale of magnetic jewelry which is meant to mimic a tongue piercing and requires a warning printed on other products containing a small magnet

**Bill ID:** A785
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A785 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A785)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of jewelry that includes a small magnet which is meant to mimic a tongue, lip or nose piercing; requires a warning printed on the packaging of any other products containing a small magnet.
