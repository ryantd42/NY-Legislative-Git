# Provides for the notification of surviving family of homicide victims of the availability of victim support services programs

**Bill ID:** A4078
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Khaleel Anderson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the notification of surviving family of homicide victims of the availability of victim support services programs by the office of victim services.
