# Extends the authorization to impose a county recording tax on obligations secured by a mortgage on real property in Albany County

**Bill ID:** S6762
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authorization to impose a county recording tax on obligations secured by a mortgage on real property in Albany County.
