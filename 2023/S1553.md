# Requires school entrances to be locked during the school day

**Bill ID:** S1553
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1553 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1553)

## Summary

Requires that entrances to public and nonpublic schools be locked at all times during the school day.
