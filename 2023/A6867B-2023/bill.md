# Prohibits restrictions on insurance policies based solely on the policyholder harboring or owning any dog of a specific breed or mixture of breeds

**Bill ID:** A6867B
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Prohibits restrictions on insurance policies based solely on the policyholder harboring or owning any dog of a specific breed or mixture of breeds.
