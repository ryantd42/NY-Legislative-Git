# Congratulating Brady Francis Bosse upon the occasion of receiving the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout, the most prestigious of Scouting honors, in April of 2023

**Bill ID:** J1051
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1051
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  CONGRATULATING  Brady  Francis  Bosse  upon  the
  occasion of  receiving  the  distinguished  rank  of
  Eagle  Scout,  the  most  prestigious  of  Scouting
  honors, in April of 2023
 
  WHEREAS, Through his diligence and rigorous efforts,  Brady  Francis
Bosse  has  achieved Scouting's highest and most prestigious rank, Eagle
Scout; the Eagle Court of Honor ceremony will be held  on  Sunday,  June
18, 2023, at the Plandome Village Green in Plandome, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Starting  as a Tiger Cub in Pack 101 and earning the Arrow
of Light award, Brady Francis Bosse is a member of Boy Scouts of America
Troop 71 in Plandome, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, In Troop 71, Brady Francis Bosse has earned 36 merit badges
and served in  various  leadership  positions,  including  Troop  Guide,
Patrol  Leader,  Assistant  Patrol  Leader,  and  Quartermaster; he also
attended summer camps at Aquehonga Ten Mile  River  Scout  Camp  and  at
Onteora Scout Reservation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  America has achieved its greatest heights of glory through
the dedication, determination and tireless efforts  of  the  outstanding
citizens of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our
youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain its glory; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Boy Scouts of America and its members are dedicated to the
development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, The highest achievement of leadership in  Scouting,  earned
by  approximately  five  percent of all Boy Scouts, is the distinguished
rank of Eagle Scout; and
 
  WHEREAS, This outstanding young man has been  involved  in  Scouting
for  many  years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has attained all
the necessary achievements, including completion of a community  service
project, which was successfully planned, developed, and executed; and
 
  WHEREAS, For his project, Brady Francis Bosse designed, planned, and
built  a 32 footlong weatherproof wooden walkway over a muddy section on
the Virginia Trail at Udalls Cove Park and Preserve in Little Neck,  New
York;  after months of planning, he supervised a team of scouts, adults,
friends, and family to install the new walkway and lay down  130  square
feet of fresh woodchips to help naturally improve 500 feet of the trail;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Brady  Francis  Bosse's  project fundraiser at Shake Shack
allowed him to fully cover the cost of the project as well as provide an
additional donation to Udalls; and
 
  WHEREAS, His dedication to the safety and well-being of the citizens
of  Plandome,  New  York  is  represented  through  his  project  and
 
demonstrates  his  devotion  to both community and commitment to others;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  he  goes forward in his academic career, Brady Francis
Bosse has recently finished his junior year at  Chaminade  High  School,
where  he  was  enrolled  in honors and Advanced Placement classes; this
summer, he will attend the New York Boys State program and represent the
Diocese of  Rockville  Centre  on  pilgrimage  to  World  Youth  Day  in
Portugal,  while continuing the ideals and principles of the Boy Scouts;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Brady Francis Bosse's perseverance in  rising  through  the
ranks  to  Eagle  Scout  is a strong indication of his ability to master
difficult tasks; his accomplishment will  enable  him  to  emerge  as  a
leader,  fully  equipped  to  meet  the  challenges  of  today's complex
society; and
 
  WHEREAS, This new Eagle Scout is a credit to his family, his school,
his community, his State and his Nation, and no doubt his future will be
filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations,  in
recognition  of  the  significance  of  this meritorious achievement, to
congratulate and honor Brady Francis Bosse  on  achieving  the  rank  of
Eagle Scout; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Brady Francis Bosse.
