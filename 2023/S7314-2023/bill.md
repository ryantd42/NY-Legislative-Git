# Establishes the "save our small-business grant program" to aid certain small businesses impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic

**Bill ID:** S7314
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the "save our small-business grant program" to aid certain small businesses impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic.
