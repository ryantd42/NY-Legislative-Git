# Relates to overrides of school district tax levies

**Bill ID:** S989
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S989 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S989)

## Summary

Permits passage of a school budget that will require a tax levy that exceeds the tax levy limit if approved by a majority of the votes cast.
