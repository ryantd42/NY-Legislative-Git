# Requires insurance companies to provide at least ninety days of rehabilitation services to an insured upon a doctor's referral

**Bill ID:** S7010A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires insurance companies to provide at least ninety days of rehabilitation services to an insured upon a doctor's prescription.
