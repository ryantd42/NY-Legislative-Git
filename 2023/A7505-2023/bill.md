# Prohibits alcohol sales to persons inside of motor vehicles

**Bill ID:** A7505
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Prohibits alcohol sales to persons inside of motor vehicles unless the licensee is specifically authorized to do so.
