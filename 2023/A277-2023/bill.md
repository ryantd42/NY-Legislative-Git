# Relates to reporting voting rates for parole commissioners

**Bill ID:** A277
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A277 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A277)

## Summary

Requires that annual reports include the voting rates for parole commissioners for persons appearing before them for release.
