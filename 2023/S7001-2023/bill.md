# Relates to selecting the city court judges in the city of Jamestown

**Bill ID:** S7001
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** George Borrello
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Provides for the selection of a city court judge for the city of Jamestown.
