# Relates to eligibility for medical assistance for infants up to age three

**Bill ID:** A7180
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides eligibility for medical assistance benefits for infants up to age three.
