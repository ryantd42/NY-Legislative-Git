# Relates to the cost of maintenance and operation of local veterans' service agencies

**Bill ID:** S1140
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1140 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1140)

## Summary

Provides that the state shall cover the cost of maintenance and operation of local veterans' service agencies up to certain set limits.
