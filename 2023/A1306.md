# Provides for defense and indemnification of parents serving in certain authorized positions in N.Y. city community school districts in suits by school officials

**Bill ID:** A1306
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1306 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1306)

## Summary

Provides for defense and indemnification of a duly elected officer of a parents' association in New York City community school districts in suits by community board members or superintendents or school principals.
