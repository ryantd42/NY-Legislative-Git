# Waives the residency requirement for the Herkimer county commissioner of social services

**Bill ID:** A6770
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian D. Miller
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Waives the residency requirement for the Herkimer county commissioner of social services, provided that such person remains a resident of an adjoining county.
