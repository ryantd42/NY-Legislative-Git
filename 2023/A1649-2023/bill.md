# Amends the definitions of pet dealers

**Bill ID:** A1649
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1649 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1649)

## Summary

Amends the definitions of pet dealers; decreases the number of animals a person can distribute to fit under the exemption.
