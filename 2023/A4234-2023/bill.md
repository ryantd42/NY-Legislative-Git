# Relates to the creation of an online standardized medical physical examination form

**Bill ID:** A4234
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the health commissioner to establish an online standardized medical physical examination form.
