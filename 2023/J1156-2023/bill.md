# Commending Class of 2023 Valedictorian Ava Rush of the P.S./M.S. 207 Rockwood Park, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** J1156
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1156
 
BY: Senator PERSAUD
 
  COMMENDING Class of 2023 Valedictorian Ava Rush of
  the  P.S./M.S.  207 Rockwood Park, in recognition of
  her outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions,  to  honor the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2023 Valedictorian Ava Rush of  the  P.S./M.S.  207  Rockwood  Park,  in
recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian  Ava  Rush  represents the best of developed
potential inherent  in  our  most  precious  resource,  our  youth;  her
achievements have brought enduring honor to her family and community and
should be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian  Ava  Rush  may  now stand with pride as she
assess her achievements, experiences the satisfaction of her labors  and
the  joy  of her accomplishments, eager to face the new experiences of a
challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Class  of  2023  Valedictorian  Ava  Rush  of the P.S./M.S. 207
Rockwood Park, in recognition of her outstanding accomplishments; and be
it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Valedictorian Ava Rush.
