# Requires certain pharmacies to establish, pay for, and operate a drug take back system

**Bill ID:** S1681
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1681 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1681)

## Summary

Requires certain pharmacies to establish, pay for, and operate a drug take back system at no cost to customers.
