# Provides for a waiver of certain liquor license renewal fees

**Bill ID:** A4082
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for a waiver of certain liquor license renewal fees during the calendar year 2023.
