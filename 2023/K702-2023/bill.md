# Congratulating Patricia Zippilli upon the occasion of her retirement after 20 years of dedicated service to the residents of the County of Rockland as the Commissioner of Jurors

**Bill ID:** K702
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 702
 
BY: M. of A. Zebrowski
 
  CONGRATULATING Patricia Zippilli upon the occasion
  of  her  retirement  after  20  years  of  dedicated
  service to the residents of the County  of  Rockland
  as the Commissioner of Jurors
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body, in keeping with
its time-honored traditions, to recognize and pay tribute to  those  who
have  served to better the quality of life in our community and have had
a measurable positive impact on the lives  of  its  residents;  Patricia
Zippilli is one such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud to congratulate
Patricia Zippilli upon the occasion of her retirement after 20 years  of
dedicated service as Rockland County Commissioner of Jurors; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Patricia  Zippilli is a lifelong resident of the County of
Rockland and has proudly served her community for decades; and
 
  WHEREAS, During that time, she served on the Board of Directors  for
the  YMCA  and  on  the  Children's  Center at the Family Court Advisory
Committee; and
 
  WHEREAS, Patricia Zippilli remains an active and vital member of the
North Rockland Rotary; and
 
  WHEREAS, This extraordinary woman has set an  inspiring  example  of
what  it means to be a public servant, to both the employees of the Jury
Commission and the County of Rockland, as well as to  the  residents  of
Rockland County; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  distinguished leader has continually demonstrated an
impressive dedication to the people of Rockland County, an  unparalleled
devotion  and  an unremitting enthusiasm as Rockland County Commissioner
of Jurors; and now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate Patricia Zippilli upon the occasion of her retirement after
20  years  of  outstanding  service  as  Rockland County Commissioner of
Jurors, to extend to her our sincere and grateful  appreciation  and  to
wish  her  well  in  all her future endeavors and to extend good wishes,
happiness and good health on her well-deserved  retirement;  and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Patricia Zippilli.
