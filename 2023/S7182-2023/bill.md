# Relates to sex trafficking

**Bill ID:** S7182
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the penalty of sex trafficking and sex trafficking of a child from a class B felony to a class A-II felony.
