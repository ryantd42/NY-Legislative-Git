# Prohibits the lease of state forests, reforestation areas, wildlife management areas and unique areas for the purpose of gas production

**Bill ID:** A4003A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Prohibits the lease of state forests, reforestation areas, wildlife management areas and unique areas for the purpose of gas and oil production.
