# Requires certain employees of charitable organizations to complete a course of instruction in the law and ethics of fundraising

**Bill ID:** S2667
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires certain employees of charitable organizations to complete a course of instruction in the law and ethics of fundraising.
