# Prohibits elected officials from collecting retirement while still in office when the retirement is earned from an elective public office

**Bill ID:** S3456
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits elected officials from collecting retirement while still in office when the retirement is earned from an elective public office.
