# Relates to lowering the age for universal hunting licenses from 14 years old to 12 years old

**Bill ID:** S2611
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to lowering the age for universal hunting licenses from 14 years old to 12 years old.
