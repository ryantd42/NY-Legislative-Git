# Relates to notification and hearings for proposed construction or removal of bicycle lanes or racks

**Bill ID:** S4330A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to notification and hearings for proposed construction or removal of bicycle lanes or racks; requires a presentation at a public hearing when the department of transportations proposes to construct or remove a bicycle lane or rack; defines terms.

  Section 1. The administrative code of the city of New York is  amended

  §  19-159.7  NOTIFICATION  OF  BICYCLE  LANE  OR  RACK CONSTRUCTION OR
 REMOVAL.  A. DEFINITIONS. AS USED IN THIS SECTION, THE  FOLLOWING  TERMS
 SHALL HAVE THE FOLLOWING MEANINGS:
  1. "AFFECTED COUNCIL MEMBER" MEANS THE COUNCIL MEMBER OR MEMBERS WHOSE
 DISTRICTS  WOULD CONTAIN A PROPOSED BICYCLE LANE OR BICYCLE RACK THAT IS
 TO BE CONSTRUCTED OR REMOVED.
  2. "AFFECTED COMMUNITY BOARD" MEANS A COMMUNITY BOARD OR BOARDS  WHOSE
 DISTRICTS  WOULD CONTAIN A PROPOSED BICYCLE LANE OR BICYCLE RACK THAT IS
 TO BE CONSTRUCTED OR REMOVED.
  3. "BICYCLE LANE" MEANS A PORTION OF THE ROADWAY THAT HAS BEEN  MARKED
 OFF OR SEPARATED FOR THE PREFERENTIAL OR EXCLUSIVE USE OF BICYCLES.
  4.  "BICYCLE  RACK"  MEANS A FIXED-IN-PLACE STAND, SOLIDLY ANCHORED TO
 THE GROUND OR OTHER FIXED OBJECT, WHICH ALLOWS A BICYCLE TO LEAN AGAINST
 IT IN AN UPRIGHT POSITION WITH BOTH WHEELS ON A LEVEL SURFACE.
  B. WHEN THE DEPARTMENT INTENDS TO CONSTRUCT OR REMOVE A  BICYCLE  LANE
 OR  BICYCLE RACK, THE DEPARTMENT SHALL PROVIDE NOTICE THROUGH ELECTRONIC
 AND WRITTEN MEANS TO THE AFFECTED COUNCIL MEMBER OF THE AFFECTED COUNCIL
 DISTRICT, THE STATE SENATOR OF THE AFFECTED SENATE DISTRICT, THE  MEMBER
 OF  THE  ASSEMBLY  OF  THE  AFFECTED  ASSEMBLY DISTRICT AND THE DISTRICT
 MANAGER OF THE AFFECTED COMMUNITY BOARD PRIOR  TO  THE  CONSTRUCTION  OR
 REMOVAL  OF  SUCH  LANE  OR RACK. SUCH NOTICE SHALL BE PROVIDED AT LEAST
