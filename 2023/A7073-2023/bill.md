# Relates to a license to sell liquor at retail for consumption on certain premises

**Bill ID:** A7073
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian F. Curran
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Provides an exemption from certain requirements for a license to sell liquor at retail for consumption on certain premises.
