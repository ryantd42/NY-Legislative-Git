# Extends the effectiveness of certain provisions relating to the special powers of the New York state environmental facilities corporation

**Bill ID:** A7383
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karen McMahon
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the effectiveness of certain provisions relating to the special powers of the New York state environmental facilities corporation to 2026.
