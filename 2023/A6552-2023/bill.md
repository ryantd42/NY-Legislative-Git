# Extends authorization for the county of Lewis to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A6552
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends authorization for the county of Lewis to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes until November 30, 2025.
