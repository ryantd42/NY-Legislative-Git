# Relates to flags sold in the state of New York

**Bill ID:** A1131
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1131 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1131)

## Summary

Requires any American flag and New York state flag sold in New York state to be manufactured in the United States.
