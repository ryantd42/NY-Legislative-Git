# Authorizes the board of trustees of the state university of New York to offer certain other non-resident tuition rates

**Bill ID:** S2066
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2066 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2066)

## Summary

Authorizes the board of trustees of the state university of New York to offer certain other non-resident tuition rates.
