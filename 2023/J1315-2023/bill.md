# Commending the Salute to Youth Project, Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its 70th Anniversary Platinum Jubilee on October 29, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1315
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1315
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  the Salute to Youth Project, Inc. upon
  the  occasion  of  hosting  its  70th  Anniversary
  Platinum Jubilee on October 29, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is incumbent upon the people of the State of New York to
recognize  and  acknowledge  those  within  our  midst  who  have  made
significant contributions to the quality of life therein; and
 
  WHEREAS, From time to time  this  Legislative  Body  takes  note  of
certain  extraordinary  individuals  and  organizations  it  wishes  to
recognize for their valued contributions to the success and progress  of
society and publicly acknowledge their endeavors which have enhanced the
basic humanity among us all; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend the Salute
to Youth Project, Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its 70th Anniversary
Platinum  Jubilee on Sunday, October 29, 2023, at Terrace on the Park in
Queens, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Established in 1960, Salute to Youth  Project,  Inc.  is  a
non-profit  organization primarily funded by the Brooklyn Chapter of the
Links,  Inc.  which  provides  scholarships  and  financial  awards  to
college-bound high school seniors; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  its  inception,  Salute  to Youth Project, Inc. has
given more than $425,000 in financial awards to more than 600  deserving
students;  its  awardees  have graduated from many fine universities and
colleges,  including  Columbia  University,  Spelman  College,  Harvard
University, the University of Pennsylvania and Morehouse College; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Salute  to Youth Project's current award recipients attend
SUNY Oswego, SUNY Stony Brook, CUNY LaGuardia and Medgar Evers, Syracuse
University, Temple University, California Institute  of  the  Arts,  The
College  of  Wooster,  City  University,  Trinity  College  and McDaniel
College; most of the students have maintained a GPA of better than  3.00
during their first semester; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition  to  supporting  students  who  seeks  higher
education, Salute to Youth Project, Inc. supports youth who  participate
in  other  programs  such  as  the Annual Women of African-American Film
Festival held at Long Island  University  in  May,  Mentoring  Programs,
college  trips  to  campuses  along  the  Eastern Seaboard, and cultural
enrichment activities; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Salute to Youth Project, Inc. will continue to discover
and reward  outstanding  community  young  people  who  are  our  future
leaders; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over  its  distinguished  70-year  history,  this  vital
organization has demonstrated continuous service to  the  residents  and
communities which it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  the  Salute to Youth Project, Inc. is in
direct correlation to the efforts of its Chairperson Stephanie N. Smith,
Vice Chairperson Gay  Bullock,  Ph.D.,  Recording  Secretary  Cassaundra
Manning,  Esq.,  Treasurer  Dorothy  Nelson,  and  Financial  Secretary
Patricia  Ramseur,  whose  involvement  is,  and  always  has  been,
characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and
an  uncompromising  standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of
the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the  Salute to Youth Project, Inc. upon the occasion of hosting
its 70th Anniversary Platinum Jubilee on October 29,  2023;  and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Salute to Youth Project, Inc.
