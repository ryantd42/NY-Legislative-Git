# Honoring John A. Bulgaro upon the occasion of his retirement after 57 years of distinguished service to Teamsters Local 294

**Bill ID:** J385
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 385
 
BY: Senator BRESLIN
 
  HONORING  John A. Bulgaro upon the occasion of his
  retirement after 57 years of  distinguished  service
  to Teamsters Local 294
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative  Body  seeks  to  acknowledge  those
individuals whose  professional  lives  and  civic  endeavors  serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
John A. Bulgaro upon the occasion of his retirement after  57  years  of
distinguished  service  to  Teamsters  Local  294,  to  be celebrated on
Thursday, March 23, 2023, with a dinner at the Crowne Plaza Albany - The
Desmond Hotel; and
 
  WHEREAS, For the past 57 years, John A. Bulgaro  rendered  faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to Teamsters Local 294; and
 
  WHEREAS, John A. Bulgaro joined the Teamsters Local 294 when he went
to  work  for  Motor  Leasing,  eventually becoming the Shop Steward; he
later quickly moved up the ranks, becoming a  Business  Agent  in  1983,
then  the  Secretary-Treasurer  in 1993, and finally, President in 1999,
the esteemed position from which he will retire; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his capacity as President, John A. Bulgaro  served  with
loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  his  tenure,  John  A.  Bulgaro  successfully grew
Teamsters Local 294 from a $300,000 to a $5,000,000 organization; during
that  same  period,  membership  grew  from  2,800  to  nearly  6,000
individuals; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  his  official  acts, John A. Bulgaro was governed by a
keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of  human  problems;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  John  A.  Bulgaro  spearheaded  development  of  the local
Drivers Training Program, securing funding for the truck and trailer for
a Program that now has over 600 students; and
 
  WHEREAS, Truly caring about his community, John A.  Bulgaro  started
the  Teamsters  Care Program, which provides scholarships, assistance to
families in need, aid to the sick, help for victims of fires  and  other
tragedies, and numerous other charitable activities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Giving of his time and expertise, John A. Bulgaro has also
served tirelessly on the NYS Teamsters Health  and  Hospital  Fund,  the
Local  294 Health and Hospital Fund, the NYS Teamsters Pension Fund, and
the Upstate Bakery and Drivers Industry Pension Fund; and
 
  WHEREAS, John A. Bulgaro also holds, today, several key positions in
New York State labor leadership; he has been elected by his peers to the
Presidency of the 15,000-member Joint Council 18, being  the  first  and
 
only  individual  from  Albany  to  serve  in  this  prestigious  role;
additionally, he currently serves as  Vice-President  of  the  New  York
State AFL/CIO; and
 
  WHEREAS, During his tenure with Teamsters Local 294, John A. Bulgaro
earned  the  admiration,  esteem,  and affection of his colleagues; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor John A. Bulgaro upon the occasion of his retirement after 57 years
of distinguished service; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to John A. Bulgaro.
