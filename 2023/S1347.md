# Exempts hot tubs or spas with safety covers from certain standards of the New York state uniform fire prevention and building code

**Bill ID:** S1347
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S1347 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1347)

## Summary

Exempts hot tubs or spas with safety covers from certain standards of the New York state uniform fire prevention and building code.
