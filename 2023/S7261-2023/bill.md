# Relates to birth certificates for foreign adoptions

**Bill ID:** S7261
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to birth certificates for foreign adoptions; makes certain provisions retroactive.
