# Creates an enhanced real property tax circuit breaker credit

**Bill ID:** S275
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S275 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S275)

## Summary

Creates an enhanced real property tax circuit breaker credit.
