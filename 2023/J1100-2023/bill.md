# Commending Deborah Shapiro upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by Residents Forward at their Caring For Port Washington Gala on June 2, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1100
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1100
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Deborah  Shapiro  upon the occasion of
  her designation for special recognition by Residents
  Forward at their Caring For Port Washington Gala  on
  June 2, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
pay tribute to individuals of high  purpose  and  worthy  accomplishment
whose  endeavors  have  faithfully served the welfare of the citizens of
the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Deborah Shapiro upon the occasion of her designation for special
recognition by Residents Forward at their  Caring  For  Port  Washington
Gala on Friday, June 2, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Deborah  Shapiro  has  served  on  the  Residents  Forward
Advocacy Board and has been instrumental  in  helping  the  organization
stay  abreast  and  voice  concerns  about proposed developments in Port
Washington; and
 
  WHEREAS, A true asset  to  the  Port  Washington  Residents  Forward
Board,  Deborah  Shapiro  is  Senior Vice President of Site Assessment &
Remediation, as well as a Qualified Environmental Professional at  AKRF,
a  leading  business  in  environmental,  planning  and  engineering
consulting; and
 
  WHEREAS, In her professional role, Deborah Shapiro  supervises  site
assessment  and  remediation  assignments,  routinely collaborating with
developers, non-profit organizations, architects, local community groups
and businesses, and  government  agencies  to  manage  the  delivery  of
cost-effective and on-time projects; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Deborah  Shapiro's great depth of knowledge and experience
allows  her  to  understand  and  comprehend  technical  language,  and
accurately  and  concisely  relay  that  information to her fellow board
members when needed, ultimately aiding the Port Washington community  at
large; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend Deborah Shapiro upon the occasion of her designation for special
recognition  by  Residents  Forward  at their Caring For Port Washington
Gala; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Deborah Shapiro.
