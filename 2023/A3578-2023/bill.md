# Enacts the Municipal Oversight Of Natural Systems By Allowing Non-Toxic Options Act

**Bill ID:** A3578
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick Burke
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Allows municipalities to regulate pesticides such that they do not conflict with any laws, rules or regulations related thereto.
