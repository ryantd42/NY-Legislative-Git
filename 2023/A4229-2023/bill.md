# Creates the offense of operating as a major firearms trafficker

**Bill ID:** A4229
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the class A-I felony of operating as a major firearms trafficker.
