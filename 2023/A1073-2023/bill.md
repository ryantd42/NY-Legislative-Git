# Relates to the department of buildings keeping records regarding fatal construction accidents

**Bill ID:** A1073
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1073 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1073)

## Summary

Relates to the department of buildings keeping records regarding fatal construction accidents and posting all fatal accidents in the incident list.
