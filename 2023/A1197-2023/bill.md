# Allows the commissioner to immobilize or impound limousines in certain situations

**Bill ID:** A1197
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1197 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1197)

## Summary

Allows the commissioner to immobilize or impound limousines where such vehicle has an out-of-service defect or a defect related to its horn.
