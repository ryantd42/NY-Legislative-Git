# Provides for the election of Alejandra Paulino as Secretary of the Senate for the years 2023-2024

**Bill ID:** R3
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R3 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/R3)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 3
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  that Alejandra Paulino of Albany, New York, be,
  and she hereby is, elected Secretary of  the  Senate
  for the years 2023-2024
 
  RESOLVED,  That  Alejandra  Paulino of Albany, New York, be, and she
hereby is, elected Secretary of the Senate for the years 2023-2024.
