# Requires certain group health insurance policies to provide coverage for hearing aids

**Bill ID:** A1042
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1042 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1042)

## Summary

Requires group health insurance policies offered in the large group market to provide coverage for hearing aids.
