# Provides that elderly and long-time residents of public housing shall not be subject to transfers based on family composition

**Bill ID:** A3137
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Daniel Rosenthal
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Provides that elderly and long-time residents of public housing shall not be subject to transfers based on family composition.
