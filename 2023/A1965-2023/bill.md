# Authorizes two or more school districts in certain counties to enter into a contract to establish and operate a regional high school

**Bill ID:** A1965
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes two or more school districts in certain counties to enter into a contract to establish and operate a regional high school.
