# Provides for E-ZPass availability and waiving deposit fees for a certain period after a rate increase

**Bill ID:** S1361AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S1361A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1361A)

## Summary

Relates to E-ZPass availability and waiving deposit fees for a certain period after a rate increase.
