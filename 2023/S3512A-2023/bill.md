# Authorizes Christopher Walser to take the competitive civil service examination for the position of police officer and be placed on the eligible list for employment as a full-time police officer

**Bill ID:** S3512AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes Christopher Walser to take the competitive civil service examination for the position of police officer and be placed on the eligible list for employment as a full-time police officer for the village of Skaneateles police department.
