# Relates to requiring a study to determine the effects that exposure to long range acoustic devices have on an individual's hearing

**Bill ID:** A509
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A509 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A509)

## Summary

Requires a study by the department of health to determine the effects that exposure to long range acoustic devices have on an individual's hearing.
