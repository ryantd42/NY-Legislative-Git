# Provides that any charges assessed by a credit card company for late payment shall not exceed 1.5 percent of the unpaid balance of any bill or fifteen dollars

**Bill ID:** A1496
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1496 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1496)

## Summary

Provides that any charges assessed by a credit card company for late payment shall not exceed one and one-half percent of the unpaid balance of any bill or fifteen dollars.
