# Directs the department of health to extend the New York state Medicaid doula services pilot program

**Bill ID:** S4173
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Sean Ryan
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the department of health to extend the New York state Medicaid doula services pilot program until March 1, 2027.
