# Relates to the rights to contract for the burial of cremains in pet cemeteries and requiring notice relating thereto

**Bill ID:** S2763
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to the rights to contract for the burial of cremains in pet cemeteries; requires notice relating thereto.
