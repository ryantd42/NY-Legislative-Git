# Relates to waiving the twenty-four hour waiting period for solemnizing a marriage after a license is issued for persons serving in the armed forces; repealer

**Bill ID:** A2921
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Josh Jensen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Waives the twenty-four hour waiting period for solemnizing a marriage after a license is issued for persons serving in the armed forces.
