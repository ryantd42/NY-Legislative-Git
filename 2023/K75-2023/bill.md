# Commending the Clarence Chamber of Commerce upon the occasion of hosting its 50th Anniversary Awards Gala on March 9, 2023

**Bill ID:** K75
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 75
 
BY: M. of A. Norris
 
  COMMENDING  the  Clarence Chamber of Commerce upon
  the occasion of hosting its 50th Anniversary  Awards
  Gala on March 9, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize the
achievements of those organizations which  substantially  contribute  to
the  economic vitality of their communities and the entire Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the Clarence Chamber of Commerce upon the  occasion  of  hosting
its 50th Anniversary Awards Gala on Thursday, March 9, 2023, at Samuel's
Grande Manor, Clarence, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Established in 1954, the Clarence Chamber of Commerce is a
dynamic organization dedicated to fostering the growth of  business  and
the  community  through  marketing  support,  educational  enrichment,
business mentoring and networking opportunities; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Clarence Chamber  of  Commerce  consistently  works  to
offer  top-quality  services  and new opportunities for its members, and
advance the local community through outstanding promotion,  support  and
advocacy as the area's leading economic development organization; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  nearly  seven  decades,  this  vital organization has
served  to  encourage  and  support  new  businesses  and  professional
opportunities,  facilitate  new  partnerships, and promote the growth of
current businesses in the community; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Clarence Chamber of Commerce is comprised of members of
the community and local business leaders  working  together  to  support
economic growth and prosperity; and
 
  WHEREAS, Through offering an exchange of ideas, as well as education
and  advocacy on behalf of its members, the Clarence Chamber of Commerce
has fostered the growth  and  well-being  of  its  membership,  and  has
enhanced the quality of life in Erie County; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Clarence  Chamber  of Commerce also endorses a buying
local campaign, and provides scholarships to students and  donations  to
various organizations and businesses; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A solid cornerstone of its community, the Clarence Chamber
of Commerce will continue to serve as the leading resource sought by the
public  for  information  about  tourism,  local  events,  and  business
education for many years to come; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over  its  distinguished  history, the Clarence Chamber of
Commerce has  demonstrated  continuous  service  to  the  residents  and
communities which it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  the  Clarence  Chamber of Commerce is in
direct correlation to the efforts of its Board of  Directors,  Executive
Board,  and  its  members,  whose  involvement  is, and always has been,
characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and
an  uncompromising  standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of
the organization and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  with  great  pleasure  that  this  Legislative  Body
acknowledges  this exceptional organization and its contributions to the
local and State economies, fully confident it will continue to enjoy the
sparkling success it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the  Clarence  Chamber of Commerce upon the occasion of hosting
its 50th Anniversary Awards Gala on March 9, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Clarence Chamber of Commerce.
