# Relates to the manufacturing of portable electronic devices

**Bill ID:** A2810
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires portable electronic devices manufactured for sale in the state to be equipped with an ATSC 3.0 chip.
