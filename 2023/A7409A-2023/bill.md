# Relates to the Wheatfield Volunteer Firefighter's Benevolent Association

**Bill ID:** A7409AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo J. Morinello
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Expands the use of foreign fire insurance premium taxes by the Wheatfield Volunteer Firefighter's Benevolent Association.
