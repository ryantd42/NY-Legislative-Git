# Includes temporarily erected structures in the definition of substandard or insanitary area

**Bill ID:** S3425
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Includes temporarily erected structures in the definition of "substandard or insanitary area".
