# Creates the mobile high-technology training facilities program

**Bill ID:** A6682
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates the mobile high-technology training facilities program to award one to one matching grants on a competitive basis to qualified applicants for the design, development and/or outfit of mobile high-technology training facilities that complement certificate, degree or other recognized credentialing programs.
