# Establishes the crime of unlawful tampering of the milk supply in the first degree

**Bill ID:** A6852
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David DiPietro
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the crime of unlawful tampering of the milk supply in the first degree.

  Section  1. The penal law is amended by adding a new section 145.28 to

 ยง 145.28 UNLAWFUL TAMPERING OF THE MILK SUPPLY IN THE FIRST DEGREE.
  1.  A PERSON IS GUILTY OF UNLAWFUL TAMPERING OF THE MILK SUPPLY IN THE
 FIRST DEGREE WHEN, WITH INTENT TO  CAUSE  A  SUBSTANTIAL  IMPAIRMENT  OR
 ADULTERATION  OF  RAW MILK, AND HAVING NO RIGHT TO DO SO NOR ANY REASON-
 ABLE GROUND TO BELIEVE THAT HE OR SHE HAS SUCH RIGHT, HE OR SHE  DAMAGES
 OR  TAMPERS  WITH  SUCH  RAW MILK SUPPLY, AND THEREBY CAUSES SUBSTANTIAL
 IMPAIRMENT OR ADULTERATION OF SUCH RAW MILK.
  2. A PERSON WHO UNLAWFULLY TAMPERS WITH A MILK SUPPLY SHALL BE  LIABLE
 TO  THE  OWNER  OF  SUCH  MILK  SUPPLY  FOR THE FAIR MARKET VALUE OF THE
 IMPAIRED OR ADULTERATED MILK SUPPLY AT THE TIME  OF  THE  IMPAIRMENT  OR
 ADULTERATION. THE OWNER OF SUCH MILK SUPPLY SHALL, TO THE EXTENT PERMIT-
 TED  BY  LAW,  BE  RELIEVED AND HELD HARMLESS FROM ANY AND ALL LIABILITY
 IMPOSED UNDER THIS SECTION.
  UNLAWFUL TAMPERING OF THE MILK SUPPLY IN THE FIRST DEGREE IS A CLASS D
 FELONY.
