# Provides for reimbursements for certain home health services that meet the definition of core public health services

**Bill ID:** S6641AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Provides for reimbursements for certain home health services that meet the definition of core public health services.
