# Allows residents who are employed by employers based in another state to obtain property/casualty or liability coverage under a policy issued to their employer

**Bill ID:** A1466
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1466 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1466)

## Summary

Allows residents who are employed by employers based in another state to obtain property/casualty or liability coverage under a policy issued to their employer in that state.
