# Relates to agreements related to renewable energy development rights on reforestation areas

**Bill ID:** S1918
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S1918 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1918)

## Summary

Relates to agreements, such as leases or easements, related to renewable energy development rights on reforestation areas.
