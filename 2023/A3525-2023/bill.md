# Provides for an apportionment to the Yonkers city school district based upon universal prekindergarten enrollment

**Bill ID:** A3525
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for an apportionment to the Yonkers city school district based upon universal prekindergarten enrollment.

  Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary,

 amount equivalent to the base year total  of  universal  prekindergarten
 pupils served multiplied by the value calculated by subtracting from the
 selected  foundation  aid per pupil, the quotient calculated by dividing
 the base year universal prekindergarten grant by the base year total  of
 universal prekindergarten pupils.
