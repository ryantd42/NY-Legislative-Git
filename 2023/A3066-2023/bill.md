# Exempts veterans organizations in New York city from city sewer and water charges

**Bill ID:** A3066
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Exempts veterans organizations in New York city, as defined in section 452 of the real property tax law, from payment of city sewer and water charges.
