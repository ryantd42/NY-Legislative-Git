# Enacts the "renewable energy bill of rights"; declares that each natural person who is a resident of this state has the right to generate, consume and export renewable energy

**Bill ID:** S2627
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Enacts the "renewable energy bill of rights"; declares that each natural person who is a resident of this state has the right to generate, consume and export renewable energy.
