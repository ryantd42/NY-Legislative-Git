# Imposes a moratorium on increasing gas and electric prices, rates and charges

**Bill ID:** S3501

**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Imposes a moratorium on increasing gas and electric prices, rates and charges.
