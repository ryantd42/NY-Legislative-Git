# Requires the board of education in the city of Buffalo to submit the school district budget to the voters for approval prior to adoption

**Bill ID:** A2745
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the board of education in the city of Buffalo to submit the school district budget to the voters for approval prior to adoption.
