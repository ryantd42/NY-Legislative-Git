# Establishes guidelines for DAs with regard to electronic conversion of certain records, books or papers in the care or custody of DAs that are more than ten years old

**Bill ID:** A2502
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to establishing guidelines for DAs with regard to electronic conversion of certain records, books or papers in the care, custody or control of DAs that are more than 10 years old.
