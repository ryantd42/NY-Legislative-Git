# Designates unlawful discriminatory practices relating to persons recovering from alcohol and/or substance abuse

**Bill ID:** S1175
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1175 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1175)

## Summary

Relates to unlawful discriminatory practices by government agencies relating to persons recovering from alcohol and/or substance abuse.
