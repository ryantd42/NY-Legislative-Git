# Requires stretch limousines to be retired from use after ten years from manufacture or the accumulation of three hundred fifty thousand miles

**Bill ID:** A3103
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires stretch limousines to be retired from use after ten years from manufacture or the accumulation of three hundred fifty thousand miles, whichever comes first.

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. Section 375 of the vehicle and traffic law  is  amended  by
 adding a new subdivision 55 to read as follows:
  55.  (A)  ANY STRETCH LIMOUSINE REGISTERED IN THIS STATE WHICH IS USED
 IN THE BUSINESS OF TRANSPORTING PASSENGERS  FOR  COMPENSATION  SHALL  BE
 RETIRED  FROM  USE  ONCE SUCH STRETCH LIMOUSINE REACHES TEN YEARS OF AGE
 FROM ITS ORIGINAL DATE OF  MANUFACTURE,  OR  ACCUMULATES  THREE  HUNDRED
 FIFTY THOUSAND MILES, WHICHEVER COMES FIRST.
  (B)  FOR  PURPOSES OF THIS SUBDIVISION, "STRETCH LIMOUSINE" SHALL MEAN
 AN ALTERED MOTOR VEHICLE HAVING A  SEATING  CAPACITY  OF  NINE  OR  MORE
 PASSENGERS,  INCLUDING  THE  DRIVER,  COMMONLY REFERRED TO AS A "STRETCH
 LIMOUSINE" AND WHICH IS USED IN THE BUSINESS OF TRANSPORTING  PASSENGERS
 FOR COMPENSATION.
