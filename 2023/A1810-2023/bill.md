# Creates the school board empowerment act

**Bill ID:** A1810
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates a school board empowerment act which allows local school districts to declare a fiscal emergency under designated conditions and fiscally restructure so as to minimize adverse impacts on student education; provides for the repeal of such provisions upon the expiration thereof.
