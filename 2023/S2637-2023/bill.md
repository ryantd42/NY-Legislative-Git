# Reduces the salary of members of the state legislature and provides that the regular legislative session shall end by March 31 annually

**Bill ID:** S2637
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Reduces the salary of members of the state legislature; provides that the regular legislative session shall end by March 31 annually.
