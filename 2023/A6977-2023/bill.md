# Grants police officer status to members of the uniformed correction force of N.Y. city department of correction

**Bill ID:** A6977
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Grants police officer status to members of the uniformed correction force of the N.Y. city department of correction who complete a certain training program.
