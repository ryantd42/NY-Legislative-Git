# Makes life imprisonment without parole mandatory for defendants convicted of murder in the first degree and the victim is a police officer

**Bill ID:** A3128
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Makes life imprisonment without parole mandatory for defendants convicted of murder in the first degree and the victim is a police officer.
