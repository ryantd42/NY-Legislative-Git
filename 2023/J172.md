# Honoring Yadlynd Cherubin upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by ANS Association on January 28, 2023

**Bill ID:** J172
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J172 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/J172)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 172
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING Yadlynd Cherubin upon the occasion of her
  designation  for  special  recognition  by  ANS
  Association on January 28, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, The unity of  our  State  and  Nation  is  built  upon  the
compassion  of  individuals,  such  as  Yadlynd Cherubin, who uphold the
values of community life and who, through their great actions, epitomize
the best of humanity; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is  justly  proud  to  honor  Yadlynd
Cherubin upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by
ANS  Association to be celebrated at its I Am Beautiful Conference Award
Ceremony on Saturday, January 28, 2023, at K & L  Manor,  Brooklyn,  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  An  empowering  networking  group, ANS Association assists
men, women and youth with education and mentorship while providing vital
resources and  development;  truly  dedicated  to  its  community,  this
extraordinary  organization  supports  countless individuals through its
many  initiatives,  including  food  distribution,  health  care  fairs,
business expos, networking events and youth empowerment groups; and
 
  WHEREAS, Yadlynd Cherubin is a Licensed Real Estate Associate Broker
with  Keller  Williams  with  more than 18 years of experience marketing
residential properties throughout Brooklyn, Queens, and Long Island; she
is also a certified Commercial and Investment Real Estate representative
which gives her a strong edge on the competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, In this capacity, clients, competitors and colleagues alike
consider Yadlynd Cherubin an authentic, competent,  highly  intelligent,
resourceful  and  hardworking broker who always goes above and beyond to
ensure smooth real estate transactions which  exceed  all  expectations;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  the CEO and Owner of The Legacy Team, Yadlynd Cherubin
has been transformational in her approach to helping buyers, sellers and
investors participate in real estate ownership and community engagement;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore, in 2021, Yadlynd Cherubin founded  the  Launch
with  Legacy Team: Mentorship Program which provides one-on-one coaching
and teaches competency and leadership development; and
 
  WHEREAS, A native New  Yorker,  Yadlynd  Cherubin  offers  her  real
estate  clients  an  exceptional  blend  of  real  financial investment,
ownership, efficacy, and client empowerment; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by  an
individual  for  the  benefit  of  others  which  Yadlynd  Cherubin  has
displayed throughout her life; and
 
  WHEREAS, It has always been the objective of this  Legislative  Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
 
commitment to the betterment of their communities, and it is the  intent
of  this  Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this tribute to
Yadlynd Cherubin, that future generations may know  and  appreciate  her
admirable  character,  her  many  benevolent  deeds, and the respect and
esteem in which she is held by her peers; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Yadlynd Cherubin upon the occasion of her designation for special
recognition by ANS Association; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Yadlynd Cherubin.
