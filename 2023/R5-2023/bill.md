# Provides for the election of Catherine Kirkland as the Official Stenographer of the Senate for the years 2023-2024

**Bill ID:** R5
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [R5 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/R5)

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 5
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  THAT  Catherine  Kirkland  be,  and she hereby is,
  elected the Official Stenographer of the Senate  for
  the years 2023-2024
 
  RESOLVED, That Catherine Kirkland be, and she hereby is, elected the
Official Stenographer of the Senate for the years 2023-2024.
