# Relates to the powers of the commissioner of health in administering the elderly pharmaceutical insurance coverage program

**Bill ID:** S3054
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of health to create an electronic online system for registration into the elderly pharmaceutical insurance coverage program.
