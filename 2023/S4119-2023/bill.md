# Limits the provision of charitable bail to misdemeanor defendants who are first time offenders

**Bill ID:** S4119
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Limits the provision of charitable bail to misdemeanor defendants who are first time offenders.
