# Relates to foreclosure by action in rem

**Bill ID:** A909
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Latoya Joyner
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A909 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A909)

## Summary

Places additional notice requirements on the city when conveying a property to a third party.
