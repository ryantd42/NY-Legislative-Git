# Relates to factors for consideration for applications for a license to operate a bank or storage facility

**Bill ID:** A117AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A117A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A117A)

## Summary

Adds additional factors to be considered by the department of health for applications for a license to operate a bank or storage facility.
