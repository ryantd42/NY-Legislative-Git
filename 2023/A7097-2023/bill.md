# Extends the authority of the county of Columbia to impose an additional real estate transfer tax

**Bill ID:** A7097
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Scott H. Bendett
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the authority of the county of Columbia to impose an additional real estate transfer tax by two years.
