 CLERK OF THE MUNICIPALITY IN WHICH THE REAL PROPERTY IS  LOCATED  WITHIN
 FIVE CALENDAR DAYS OF THE FILING OF THE NOTICE OF PENDENCY FOR THE FORE-
 CLOSURE ACTION.
