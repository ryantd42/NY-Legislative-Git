# Congratulating Viola D. Greene-Walker upon the occasion of her retirement after 30 years of distinguished service as District Manager of Community Board No. 16

**Bill ID:** K461
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Latrice Walker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 461
 
BY: M. of A. Walker
 
  CONGRATULATING  Viola  D.  Greene-Walker  upon the
  occasion  of  her  retirement  after  30  years  of
  distinguished  service  as  District  Manager  of
  Community Board No. 16
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this  Legislative  Body  to  acknowledge
those  individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate Viola D. Greene-Walker upon the occasion of her  retirement
after 30 years of distinguished service as District Manager of Community
Board No. 16; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A graduate of Brooklyn College, Viola D. Greene-Walker has
served as the District Manager of Community Board No.  16,  representing
the Ocean Hill-Brownsville community since 1993; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  this  capacity,  Viola  D.  Greene-Walker  has  been
responsible for the  daily  monitoring  and  coordination  of  municipal
services  to  the  residents;  as  an  advocate, she has interacted with
municipal service agencies to resolve complaints and improve the quality
of life for the 85,000 people who live in the community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Viola D. Greene-Walker also worked closely with the members
of Community Board No. 16 to identify and assess the capital and expense
budget needs of the district as they relate to the citywide budget; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition,  Viola  D.  Greene-Walker  established
relationships  with  block  and  tenant  associations,  community-based
service agencies, churches, businesses,  and  elected  officials  within
Community  District  No.  16 in order to make it a better place to live,
work, learn and play; and
 
  WHEREAS, For her steadfast and unremitting commitment  to  Community
District  No.  16,  Viola  D.  Greene-Walker  has  been the recipient of
numerous awards and accolades; and
 
  WHEREAS, For the past 30  years,  Viola  D.  Greene-Walker  rendered
faithful,  conscientious and valuable service to Community Board No. 16;
as District Manager, she served with loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS, In her official acts, Viola D. Greene-Walker  was  governed
by  a  keen  sense  of  duty  and  always showed a unique grasp of human
problems; and
 
  WHEREAS, During her tenure with Community Board  No.  16,  Viola  D.
Greene-Walker  earned  the  admiration,  esteem  and  affection  of  her
colleagues; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Viola D. Greene-Walker upon the occasion of her retirement
 
after 30 years of distinguished service as District Manager of Community
Board No. 16, and to wish her well in all her future endeavors;  and  be
it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Viola D. Greene-Walker.
