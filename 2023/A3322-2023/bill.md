# Establishes a task force to address policies related to the treatment of transgender, gender nonconforming, non-binary, and intersex individuals in custody

**Bill ID:** A3322
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Establishes a task force to address policies related to the treatment of transgender, gender nonconforming, non-binary, and intersex individuals in the custody of the department of corrections and community supervision.
