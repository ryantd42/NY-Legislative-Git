# Decreases contribution limits

**Bill ID:** A2161
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nily Rozic
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Reduces campaign contribution limits for candidates for election to a public office or party position to a maximum of $2,800.
