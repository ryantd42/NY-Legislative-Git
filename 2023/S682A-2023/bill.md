# Relates to methods for cancelling contracts

**Bill ID:** S682AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S682A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S682A)

## Summary

Requires that a business allow a consumer to cancel a contract between the two parties using the same method that was used to enter into the contract; excludes businesses operating pursuant to certain franchises.
