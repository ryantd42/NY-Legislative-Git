# Allows municipalities to regulate public places where cannabis can be consumed

**Bill ID:** S7199
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Allows municipalities to regulate public places where cannabis can be consumed.
