# Relates to crossbows; repealer

**Bill ID:** A2631
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the taking of wildlife by the use of a crossbow; makes related provisions.
