# Increases the sale price threshold for food and drink sold in vending machines for purposes of being exempt from certain taxation

**Bill ID:** S4144A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the sale price threshold for food and drink sold in vending machines to be exempt from certain taxation.
