# Requires all goods, except food, sold at retail in state parks, recreational facilities and historic sites to be produced in the United States

**Bill ID:** A3181
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeff Gallahan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires all goods, except food, sold at retail in state parks, recreational facilities and historic sites to be produced in the United States.
