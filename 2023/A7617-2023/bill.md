# Authorizes private membership establishments to have certain licenses for sale of alcohol

**Bill ID:** A7617
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Authorizes private membership establishments to have certain licenses for sale of alcohol for consumption on premises.
