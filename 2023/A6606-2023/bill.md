# Relates to preventing the use of funds, financial incentives, subsidies or tax exemptions for projects with non-public occupants

**Bill ID:** A6606
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prevents the use of funds, financial incentives, subsidies or tax exemptions for projects where the occupier of the project is not disclosed.
