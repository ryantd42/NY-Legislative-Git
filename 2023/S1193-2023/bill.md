# Ensures ovarian cancer survivors have the right to access screenings for health conditions

**Bill ID:** S1193
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S1193 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1193)

## Summary

Ensures ovarian cancer survivors have the right to access screenings for health conditions.
