# Prohibits the operation of a bicycle while under the influence of alcohol or drugs

**Bill ID:** S1374
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Simcha Felder
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1374 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1374)

## Summary

Prohibits the operation of a bicycle while under the influence of alcohol or drugs; applies upon public highways, private roads open to motor vehicle traffic, any other parking lot, and sidewalks.
