# Establishes that wine tastings may be conducted under the auspices of a retailer

**Bill ID:** A278
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A278 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A278)

## Summary

Establishes that persons licensed to sell wine may do so on the licensed premises under the auspices of an official agent of a farm winery, winery, wholesaler, importer, or retailer.
