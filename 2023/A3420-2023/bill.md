 AN ACT to amend the  environmental  conservation  law,  in  relation  to

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. Section 11-1703 of the environmental  conservation  law  is
 amended by adding a new subdivision 8 to read as follows:
  8.  NO  PERSON  SHALL  SELL  OR KNOWINGLY INTRODUCE INTO A FREE-LIVING
 STATE, LIVE GENETICALLY MODIFIED SALMON.  UNDER THIS SUBDIVISION, GENET-
 ICALLY MODIFIED SALMON SHALL MEAN PRODUCED FROM OR WITH AN  ORGANISM  OR
 ORGANISMS WITH GENETICS ALTERED MATERIALLY THROUGH THE APPLICATION OF:
  (A)  IN  VITRO  NUCLEIC  ACID TECHNIQUES, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO
 RECOMBINANT  DEOXYRIBONUCLEIC  ACID  (DNA)  TECHNIQUES  AND  THE  DIRECT
 INJECTION OF NUCLEIC ACID INTO CELLS OR ORGANELLES; OR
  (B)  THE  FUSION  OF  CELLS BEYOND THE TAXONOMIC FAMILY THAT OVERCOMES
 NATURAL PHYSIOLOGICAL, REPRODUCTIVE, OR RECOMBINANT  BARRIERS  AND  THAT
 ARE NOT TECHNIQUES USED IN TRADITIONAL BREEDING AND SELECTION.
