# Requires public disclosure of certain state agency materials, and authorizes the office of information technology services to publish a technical standards manual for the publishing of records

**Bill ID:** S802
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** José M. Serrano
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S802 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S802)

## Summary

Requires public disclosure of certain state agency materials, and authorizes the office for technology to publish a technical standards manual for the publishing of records on the internet by state agencies.
