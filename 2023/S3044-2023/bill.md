# Provides for voter requests for an absentee ballot from the board of elections by telephone

**Bill ID:** S3044
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for voter requests for an absentee ballot from the board of elections by telephone.
