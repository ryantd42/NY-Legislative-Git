# Congratulating the Tioga High School Wrestling Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Division II Dual Meet Wrestling Championship

**Bill ID:** J889
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 889
 
BY: Senator O'MARA
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Tioga  High  School Wrestling
  Team upon the occasion  of  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA
  Division  II  Dual  Meet  Wrestling  Championship on
  January 28, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
Tioga  High  School  Wrestling  Team  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing
back-to-back NYSPHSAA Division II Dual Meet Wrestling Championships; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and  applause  of  their  excited  fans,  the
Tigers  defeated  Central Valley Academy by a decisive score of 49-18 to
capture the New York State title on Saturday, January 28, 2023,  at  the
SRC  Arena and Events Center on the campus of Onondaga Community College
in Syracuse, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to this monumental win, the  Tigers  won  their
fourth  2023  NYSPHSAA  Division  II Wrestling Championship on Saturday,
February 25, 2023, at the MVP Arena in  Albany,  New  York;  during  the
event,  the  team  broke  the  New  York State scoring record with 157.5
points, crowned three individual state champions, and  had  a  total  of
nine state place finishers; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's  colors  of blue and gold,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Tigers throughout their journey as they ended their season
with an impressive overall record  of  13-0,  with  a  36-match  winning
streak; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  wrestling,  which demands athletic
prowess, speed and agility, Coaches Kris Harrington, Jason  Bellis,  Jon
Martin,  and  Ryan  Macwhinnie  worked  hard  to hone the skills of this
championship team, teaching these  outstanding  athletes  lessons  which
will prove invaluable both on and off the mat; and
 
  WHEREAS, Coaches Kris Harrington, Jason Bellis, Jon Martin, and Ryan
Macwhinnie  and all of the outstanding athletes on the Tioga High School
Wrestling Team  have  clearly  utilized  dedication,  determination  and
teamwork in providing a lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence
which is a tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the members of the Tioga High School Wrestling Team: Austin
 
Babcock, Deakon Bailey, Kali Bailey, Caden Bellis,  Levi  Bellis,  Logan
Bellis,  Trent  Browne, Kadin Cole, Jayden Duncanson, Ousmane Duncanson,
Max Dydynski, Garrett  Godfrey,  Bradley  Guiles,  Drew  Macumber,  Tate
McCauley,  Brayden  Macwhinnie, Kaya Nelson, Tyler Roe, Conner Streeter,
Gianni Silvestri, Brennan Sindoni, Emily  Sindoni,  John  Woodcock,  and
Jordan Young; and Coaches Kris Harrington, Jason Bellis, Jon Martin, and
Ryan  Macwhinnie  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing  the  2023 NYSPHSAA
Division II Dual Meet Wrestling Championship; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to the members of the Tioga High School Wrestling Team, and
Coaches Kris Harrington, Jason Bellis, Jon Martin, and Ryan Macwhinnie.
