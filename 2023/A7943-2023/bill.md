# Directs the commissioner of motor vehicles to establish a mandatory bicycle with electric assist and electric scooter training course and an optional bicycle training course

**Bill ID:** A7943
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of motor vehicles to establish a mandatory bicycle with electric assist and electric scooter training course; directs the commissioner of motor vehicles to establish an optional bicycle training course.
