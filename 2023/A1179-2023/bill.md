# Relates to increasing the maximum years of study under the tuition assistance program

**Bill ID:** A1179
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Khaleel Anderson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1179 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1179)

## Summary

Increases the maximum time limits for tuition assistance program awards.
