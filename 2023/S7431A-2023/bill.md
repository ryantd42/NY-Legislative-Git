# Extends the authority of the town of Newburgh to impose a hotel and motel tax

**Bill ID:** S7431AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Rolison
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the authority of the town of Newburgh to impose a hotel and motel tax for an additional two years.
