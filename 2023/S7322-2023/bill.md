# Authorizes crimes committed by members of street gangs or a criminal enterprise to be eligible for bail

**Bill ID:** S7322
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Authorizes crimes committed by members of street gangs or a criminal enterprise to be eligible for bail.
