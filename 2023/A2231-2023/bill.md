# Enacts Jacobe's Law

**Bill ID:** A2231
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mary Beth Walsh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Enacts Jacobe's Law requiring school administrators to contact the parents or guardians of students when bullying or harassment has occurred.
