# Commemorating the 196th Anniversary of the Town of Union Vale, celebrated on Monday, March 6, 2023

**Bill ID:** K273
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 273
 
BY: M. of A. Beephan
 
  COMMEMORATING the 196th Anniversary of the Town of
  Union Vale, celebrated on Monday, March 6, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of this Legislative Body to honor and
commemorate  the  distinguished  histories  of  the  communities  which
comprise the noble body of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the 196th Anniversary of the Town of Union Vale, celebrated
on Monday, March 6, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Town of Union Vale is in the  middle-northern  part  of
the district, and is home to just under 5000 residents; and
 
  WHEREAS,  There  is  an airport located in the Town for recreational
flying, two parks, Tymor Park and Frederick E.  Godfrey  Memorial  Park;
the Town also holds a beautiful golf course; and
 
  WHEREAS, Of historic origin, and remaining fruitful over the ebb and
flow  of  decades of growth and change, the Town of Union Vale continues
to provide the quintessential quality of life for its residents; and
 
  WHEREAS, The residents of the Town  of  Union  Vale  have  a  bright
outlook  for  the  future  of their community, which remains a beautiful
place in which to live and raise a family; and
 
  WHEREAS, In recognition of the Town of Union Vale's rich history and
enduring contribution to the State of New York, this Legislative Body is
proud to pay tribute to this spirited Town  upon  the  occasion  of  its
196th Anniversary; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the 196th Anniversary of the Town of Union Vale,  celebrated
on  March 6, 2023, recognizing the significance of the role it continues
to play in the life of the community of the State of New York; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Town of Union Vale.
