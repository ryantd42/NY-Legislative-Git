# Relates to requiring a valid prescription in order to purchase, obtain or use an electronic cigarette; repealer

**Bill ID:** A187
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A187 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A187)

## Summary

Requires a prescription from a licensed physician in order to obtain an electronic cigarette.

  Section  1.  The  public health law is amended by adding a new section
 1399-ddd to read as follows:
  § 1399-DDD. PRESCRIPTION REQUIRED FOR ELECTRONIC CIGARETTE USE. 1. FOR
 PURPOSES OF THIS SECTION, "ELECTRONIC CIGARETTE"  SHALL  HAVE  THE  SAME
 MEANING  AS  IN SUBDIVISION THIRTEEN OF SECTION THIRTEEN HUNDRED NINETY-
 NINE-AA OF THIS ARTICLE.
  2. (A) ELECTRONIC CIGARETTES SHALL BE MADE AVAILABLE ONLY TO  INDIVID-
 UALS OVER THE AGE OF EIGHTEEN WHO HAVE BEEN PRESCRIBED TO USE SUCH ELEC-
 TRONIC  CIGARETTES  BY  A  LICENSED  PHYSICIAN  AUTHORIZED TO ISSUE SUCH
 PRESCRIPTIONS. PURCHASING,  OBTAINING  OR  USING  ELECTRONIC  CIGARETTES
 WITHOUT  A VALID PRESCRIPTION FROM A LICENSED PHYSICIAN SHALL BE PROHIB-
 ITED AND BE DEEMED A VIOLATION OF THIS SECTION.
  (B) ELECTRONIC CIGARETTES SHALL ONLY BE  MADE  AVAILABLE  THROUGH  ANY
 INDIVIDUAL,  FIRM, CORPORATION OR ASSOCIATION WHO IS LICENSED AND REGIS-
 TERED TO OPERATE AS A PHARMACY PURSUANT TO ARTICLE ONE  HUNDRED  THIRTY-
 SEVEN OF THE EDUCATION LAW.
  3.  THE COMMISSIONER IS AUTHORIZED TO PROMULGATE RULES AND REGULATIONS
 TO IMPLEMENT THE PROVISIONS OF THIS SECTION.
  § 2. Subdivision 11 of section 1399-n of  the  public  health  law  is
 REPEALED.
