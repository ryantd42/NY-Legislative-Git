# Permits the sale of tonic water, bitters, maraschino cherries and dealcoholized wine at liquor stores

**Bill ID:** S3568AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Permits the sale of tonic water, bitters, maraschino cherries and dealcoholized wine at liquor stores.
