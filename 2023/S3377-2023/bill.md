# Provides for the suspension of a vehicle registration after a third violation for overtaking and passing a stopped school bus

**Bill ID:** S3377
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Provides for the suspension of a vehicle registration after a third school bus safety camera violation for overtaking and passing a stopped school bus.
