# Relates to the calculation of increases in income limiting eligibility under the program for elderly pharmaceutical insurance coverage

**Bill ID:** A630
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A630 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A630)

## Summary

Limits the calculation of increases in income limiting eligibility under the program for elderly pharmaceutical insurance coverage to one subsequent calendar year.
