# Includes certain activity in transit and transportation facilities with the crime of criminal trespass in the second degree

**Bill ID:** A3127
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes certain activity in transit and transportation facilities within the crime of criminal trespass in the second degree.
