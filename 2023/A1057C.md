# Relates to online dating services

**Bill ID:** A1057CC
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A1057C PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1057C)

## Summary

Relates to how online dating services handle fraudulent members; requires certain disclosures.
