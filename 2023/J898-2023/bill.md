# Commending the Association of School Business Officials of New York upon the occasion of celebrating its 75th Anniversary

**Bill ID:** J898
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 898
 
BY: Senator MAYER
 
  COMMENDING  the  Association  of  School  Business
  Officials  of  New  York  upon  the  occasion  of
  celebrating its 75th Anniversary
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend those organizations of commitment  and  high  achievement  whose
purpose and endeavors prove a dedication to the pursuit of excellence in
the education of the youth of this noble Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  the  Association  of School Business Officials of New York upon
the occasion of its 75th Anniversary to  be  celebrated  at  its  Annual
Education  Summit and Expo from June 4-7, 2023, in Saratoga Springs, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The theme for this year's conference is  "Together  Towards
Tomorrow, 75 Years of Advancing the Business of Education"; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Association  of School Business Officials of New York
(ASBO New York), formerly the  New  York  State  Association  of  School
Business Officials (NYSASBO), was established in 1948; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This vital nonprofit membership organization is affiliated
with the Association of  School  Business  Officials  International  and
chartered by the New York State Board of Regents; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For 75 years, ASBO New York has supported the professional
growth of their members who are recognized as experts in school  finance
and  operations,  and  leaders in education who tirelessly give of their
efforts to ensure quality education for all students; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of ASBO New York is in  direct  correlation  to
the  efforts  of its members, whose involvement is, and always has been,
characterized by an impressive commitment, an unbridled enthusiasm,  and
an  uncompromising  standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of
the institution and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
organizations  of such noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the  citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend the Association of School Business Officials of  New  York  upon
the occasion of celebrating its 75th Anniversary; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Association of School Business Officials of New York.
