# Prohibits the use of drilling fluids and flowback water from wells, pools or fields on any highway

**Bill ID:** S1538AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S1538A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1538A)

## Summary

Prohibits the use of drilling fluids and flowback water from wells, pools or fields on any highway for any purpose including but not limited to de-icing or dust suppression.
