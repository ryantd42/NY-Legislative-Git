# Provides for reporting the race, ethnicity and salary ranges of women employed in service of the state

**Bill ID:** S1908
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1908 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1908)

## Summary

Reports the number of women employed in each office and/or position in the service of the state and the race, ethnicity and salary range of each such employee.
