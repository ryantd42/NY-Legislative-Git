 AN ACT to amend the real property law, in relation to the definition  of
  residence and residential real property
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Paragraph (k) of subdivision 2 of section 265-a of the real
 property law, as added by chapter 308 of the laws of 2006, is amended to
 read as follows:
  (k) "Residence" and "residential real property" means residential real
 property consisting of one- to four-family dwelling units[, one of which
 the equity seller occupies or occupied at a time  immediately  prior  to
 the equity sale as his or her primary residence].
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
