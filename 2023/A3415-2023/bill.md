# Allows alcoholic beverages as prizes in any game of chance

**Bill ID:** A3415
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Allows alcoholic beverages as prizes in any game of chance.
