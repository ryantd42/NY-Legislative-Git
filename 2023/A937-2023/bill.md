# Relates to reimbursement for students attending a boards of cooperative educational services program

**Bill ID:** A937
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jonathan Jacobson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A937 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A937)

## Summary

Provides reimbursement to districts for any student attending a boards of cooperative educational services program.
