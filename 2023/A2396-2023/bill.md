# Grants a $100,000 real property tax exemption to conservation clubs and rod and gun clubs owning certain land having an assessed value of $500,000 or less

**Bill ID:** A2396
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Grants a $100,000 real property tax exemption to conservation clubs and rod and gun clubs owning land acquired prior to January 1, 2023, having an assessed value of $500,000 or less.
