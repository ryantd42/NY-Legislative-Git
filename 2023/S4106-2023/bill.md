# Imposes strict liability on manufacturers, owners, and operators of unmanned motor vehicles

**Bill ID:** S4106
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Imposes strict liability on manufacturers, owners, and operators of unmanned motor vehicles.
