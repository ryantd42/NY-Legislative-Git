# Modifies the requirements for the New York state science, technology, engineering and mathematics incentive program

**Bill ID:** A4050
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Modifies the requirements for the New York state science, technology, engineering and mathematics incentive program for when an award gets converted to a student loan.
