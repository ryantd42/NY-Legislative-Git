# Establishes a credit for the purchase and installation of a security alarm system

**Bill ID:** S6556
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Simcha Felder
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes a credit for the purchase and installation of a security alarm system equal to two hundred fifty dollars.

 AN ACT to amend the tax law, in relation to establishing  a  credit  for
  the purchase and installation of a security alarm system
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Section 606 of the tax law  is  amended  by  adding  a  new
 subsection (ooo) to read as follows:
  (OOO)  CREDIT FOR INSTALLATION OF A HOME SECURITY SYSTEM. (1) GENERAL.
 AN INDIVIDUAL TAXPAYER SHALL BE ALLOWED A ONE-TIME  CREDIT  FOR  TAXABLE
 YEARS  BEGINNING  ON  OR  AFTER  JANUARY FIRST, TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-FOUR
 AGAINST THE TAX IMPOSED BY THIS ARTICLE FOR THE  COST  TO  PURCHASE  AND
 INSTALL  A  SECURITY  ALARM  SYSTEM ON RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY EQUAL TO TWO
 HUNDRED FIFTY DOLLARS.
  (2) DEFINITION. FOR THE PURPOSE OF THIS  SUBSECTION,  "SECURITY  ALARM
 SYSTEM"  SHALL  MEAN  AN  ASSEMBLY  OF EQUIPMENT AND DEVICES OR A SINGLE
 DEVICE, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO A SECURITY FENCE, A SECURITY  ALARM
 SYSTEM AND/OR A SECURITY SURVEILLANCE SYSTEM DESIGNATED TO DETECT AND/OR
 SIGNAL  AN UNAUTHORIZED INTRUSION INTO RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY OR TO SIGNAL
 AN ATTEMPTED ROBBERY, BURGLARY, THEFT, PILFERAGE  OR  OTHER  LOSS  AT  A
 PROTECTED  RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY, AND WITH RESPECT TO SUCH SIGNAL, POLICE
 OFFICERS ARE EXPECTED TO RESPOND, WHICH MAY BE INSTALLED,  SERVICED  AND
 MAINTAINED BY A PERSON LICENSED TO ENGAGE IN SUCH ACTIVITIES PURSUANT TO
 ARTICLE SIX-D OF THE GENERAL BUSINESS LAW.
  (3)  ELIGIBILITY.  TO  QUALIFY  FOR  THE  CREDIT  PRESCRIBED  IN  THIS
 SUBSECTION, AN INDIVIDUAL TAXPAYER SHALL PROVIDE PROOF OF  PURCHASE  AND
 INSTALLATION IN THE FORM OF A RECEIPT.
  (4)  WHEN  CREDIT  ALLOWED. THE CREDIT PROVIDED FOR IN THIS SUBSECTION
 SHALL BE ALLOWED WITH RESPECT TO  THE  TAXABLE  YEAR,  COMMENCING  AFTER
 JANUARY  FIRST,  TWO  THOUSAND  TWENTY-FOUR, IN WHICH THE SECURITY ALARM
 SYSTEM IS PURCHASED.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately and apply to taxable years
 on and after January 1, 2024.
