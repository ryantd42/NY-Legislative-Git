# Increases the amount of allowance that trial and grand jurors are entitled to in each court of the unified court system

**Bill ID:** S2744A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases the amount of allowance that trial and grand jurors are entitled to in each court of the unified court system.
