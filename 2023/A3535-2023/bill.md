# Establishes daylight saving time as the year-round standard time of the state and the city of New York

**Bill ID:** A3535
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes daylight saving time as the year-round standard time of the state and the city of New York.
