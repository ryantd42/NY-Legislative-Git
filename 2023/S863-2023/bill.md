# Relates to security and protection of New York national guard and air national guard recruiting centers

**Bill ID:** S863
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S863 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S863)

## Summary

Relates to security and protection of New York national guard and air national guard recruiting centers; provides that personnel be permitted to carry a firearm.
