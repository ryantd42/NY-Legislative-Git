# Extends provisions of the public health law relating to medical reimbursement and welfare reform

**Bill ID:** A7373
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karines Reyes
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends provisions of the public health law relating to medical reimbursement and welfare reform until 2025.
