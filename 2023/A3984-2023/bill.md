# Prohibits landlords from refusing to deal with certain people solely because those people were involved in prior summary proceedings

**Bill ID:** A3984
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits landlords from refusing to deal with certain people solely because those people were involved in prior summary proceedings.
