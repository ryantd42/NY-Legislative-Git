# Relates to the display of a charter bus driver qualifications

**Bill ID:** A1838A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Ron Kim
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the display of charter bus driver qualifications prominently while they are on duty.
