# Relates to the creation of a pre-specialized high schools admissions test and preparation program

**Bill ID:** A3377
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates a pre-specialized high schools admissions test and preparation program for all sixth grade students in New York city.
