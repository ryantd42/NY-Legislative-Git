# Establishes the rural emergency first responder program revolving fund

**Bill ID:** S4492
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the rural emergency first responder program revolving fund; requires grants be made for qualified farmedic programs.
