# Establishes certain rights for shareholders in limited-profit housing companies

**Bill ID:** S1507
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1507 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1507)

## Summary

Establishes certain rights for shareholders in limited-profit housing companies.
