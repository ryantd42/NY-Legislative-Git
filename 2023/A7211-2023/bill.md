# Provides for coverage of mental health and alcohol and substance use services under the child health insurance plan

**Bill ID:** A7211
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Sarah Clark
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for coverage of mental health and alcohol and substance use services under the child health insurance plan.
