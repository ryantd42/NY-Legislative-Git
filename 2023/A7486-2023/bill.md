# Relates to the definition of manual worker

**Bill ID:** A7486
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the definition of manual worker; provides such worker's primary duty is to engage in physical labor.
