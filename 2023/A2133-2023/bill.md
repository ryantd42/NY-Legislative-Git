# Relates to prohibiting the use of gas-powered leaf and lawn blowers during certain months

**Bill ID:** A2133
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the use of gas-powered leaf and lawn blowers between May 1st and September 30th.
