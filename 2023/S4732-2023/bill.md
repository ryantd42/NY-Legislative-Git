# Increases the trademark registration and renewal fee

**Bill ID:** S4732
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the trademark registration and renewal fee from fifty dollars to one hundred dollars.
