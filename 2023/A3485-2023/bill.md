# Includes peace officers in certain offenses against public safety

**Bill ID:** A3485
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe DeStefano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes peace officers in offenses of unlawful fleeing in a motor vehicle.
