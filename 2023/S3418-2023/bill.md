# Requires a weekly update of the prescription drug retail price list

**Bill ID:** S3418
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires a weekly update of the department of health's prescription drug retail price list.
