# Relates to the applicability of open meetings and freedom of information laws to certain not-for-profit corporations

**Bill ID:** A3715C
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Relates to the applicability of open meetings and freedom of information laws to certain not-for-profit corporations.
