# Relates to certain requirements for economic development grant recipients

**Bill ID:** A363
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A363 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A363)

## Summary

Relates to certain requirements for economic development grant recipients; requires the submission of enumerated information.
