# Prohibits individuals convicted of stealing public funds from being elected or appointed as a member of a school board of education

**Bill ID:** A7127
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Taylor Darling
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits individuals convicted of stealing public funds from being elected or appointed as a member of a school board of education.
