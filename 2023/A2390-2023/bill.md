# Establishes business franchise and personal income tax credits for capital investments made in certain small businesses

**Bill ID:** A2390
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes business franchise and personal income tax credits for capital investments made in certain small businesses.
