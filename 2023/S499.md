# Authorizes municipalities to contract for services through contracts let by any other governmental entity

**Bill ID:** S499
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S499 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S499)

## Summary

Authorizes municipalities to contract for services through contracts let by any other governmental entity.
