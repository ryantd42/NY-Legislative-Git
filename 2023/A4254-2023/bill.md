# Establishes tax credits for local newspaper subscriptions, newspaper payrolls, and local media advertising

**Bill ID:** A4254
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David McDonough
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes tax credits for local newspaper subscriptions, newspaper payrolls, and local media advertising.
