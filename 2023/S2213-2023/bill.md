# Reimburses counties who have purchased or will purchase document management software designed specifically for the child welfare industry

**Bill ID:** S2213
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Reimburses counties who have purchased or will purchase document management software designed specifically for the child welfare industry.
