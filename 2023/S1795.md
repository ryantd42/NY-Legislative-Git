# Requires the department of environmental conservation to develop and propose an appropriation to fund the charge New York drive clean rebate program

**Bill ID:** S1795
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1795 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1795)

## Summary

Requires the department of environmental conservation to develop and propose an appropriation to fund the charge New York drive clean rebate program.

 AN ACT to amend the  environmental  conservation  law,  in  relation  to
  requiring  the department of environmental conservation to develop and
  propose an appropriation to fund  the  charge  New  York  drive  clean
  rebate program
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Subdivision 2 of section  19-0306-b  of  the  environmental
 conservation  law  is  amended  by  adding  a new paragraph d to read as
 follows:
  D. AN APPROPRIATION TO FUND THE CHARGE NEW  YORK  DRIVE  CLEAN  REBATE
 PROGRAM.  SUCH APPROPRIATION SHALL BE NO LESS THAN FIFTY MILLION DOLLARS
 PER FISCAL YEAR, AND SUCH APPROPRIATION SHALL REMAIN IN EFFECT AT  LEAST
 UNTIL  THE  YEAR TWO THOUSAND TWENTY-EIGHT. THE DEPARTMENT, IN CONSULTA-
 TION WITH THE NEW YORK STATE ENERGY RESEARCH AND  DEVELOPMENT  AUTHORITY
 AND  OTHER  INTERESTED  PARTIES, SHALL MEANS TEST THE APPROPRIATION. THE
 UPPER LIMIT OF SUCH A MEANS TEST SHALL NOT  EXCEED  SIXTY-FIVE  THOUSAND
 DOLLARS  IN  NET  INCOME  FOR  A SINGLE TAX FILER AND ONE HUNDRED THIRTY
 THOUSAND DOLLARS IN NET INCOME FOR A JOINT FILER. NO  SINGLE  TAX  FILER
 AND  NO  JOINT  FILER  SHALL RECEIVE A REBATE GREATER THAN FIVE THOUSAND
 DOLLARS.
