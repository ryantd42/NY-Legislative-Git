# Relates to dysmenorrhea and use of sick leave

**Bill ID:** A1835
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Allows employees to use dysmenorrhea as a reason for use of sick leave.
