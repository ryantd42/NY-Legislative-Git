# Prohibits registered sex offenders from using or being within five hundred feet of any state or municipal-owned park

**Bill ID:** A2404
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits registered sex offenders from using or being within five hundred feet of any state or municipal-owned park.
