# Relates to the HTLV-1 and HTLV-2 viruses

**Bill ID:** A3526
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes the HTLV-1 and HTLV-2 viruses to the public health department's health care and wellness education and outreach program.
