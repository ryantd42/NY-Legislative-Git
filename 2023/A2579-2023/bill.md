# Provides that certified handicapped individuals may use an ATV on public land for transportation purposes only, during the hunting season

**Bill ID:** A2579
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that certified handicapped individuals may use an ATV on public land for transportation purposes only, during the hunting season.
