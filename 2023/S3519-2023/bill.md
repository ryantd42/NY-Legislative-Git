# Mandates commercial insurance coverage of peer support services as part of treatment for substance use disorder

**Bill ID:** S3519

**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Mandates commercial insurance coverage of peer support services as part of treatment for substance use disorder.
