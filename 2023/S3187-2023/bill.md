# Directs the NY power authority to oversee mandated energy reduction efforts in state and public authority owned and managed buildings

**Bill ID:** S3187
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the NY power authority to oversee mandated energy reduction efforts in state and public authority owned and managed buildings.

  Section  1.  Section 1005 of the public authorities law is amended by

 RESPONSIBILITY  OF  THE  STATE AGENCIES AND AUTHORITIES. NOTWITHSTANDING
 ANY LAW TO THE CONTRARY, ALL "AFFECTED STATE ENTITIES"  ("ASES"),  WHICH

 EXECUTIVE OR THE MAJORITY OF BOARD MEMBERS, EXCEPT FOR THE PORT AUTHORI-

 REDUCING THEIR ENERGY USE FOR ALL STATE-OWNED AND MANAGED  BUILDINGS  BY

 FIRST, TWO THOUSAND SIXTEEN FOR SUCH BUILDINGS BY APRIL FIRST, TWO THOU-
 SAND TWENTY-NINE ("TARGET").
  (A) THE AUTHORITY SHALL ESTABLISH A CENTRAL MANAGEMENT AND IMPLEMENTA-
 TION TEAM ("CMIT") TO OVERSEE  THE  ASES  AND  ADMINISTER  ANY  PROGRAMS
 NECESSARY TO ACHIEVE THE TARGET. THE CMIT IS HEREBY AUTHORIZED TO:
  (I) TAKE ALL APPROPRIATE MEASURES TO ENSURE THAT THE TARGET IS MET;
  (II) DIRECT ASES TO COMPLY WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SUBDIVISION;
  (III)  CREATE  GUIDELINES  ("GUIDELINES")  TO ASSIST ASES IN COMPLYING
 WITH THE REQUIREMENTS OF THIS SUBDIVISION, AND  THEREAFTER  UPDATE  SUCH
 GUIDELINES AS NECESSARY;
