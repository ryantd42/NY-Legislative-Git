# Congratulating the Horace Greeley High School Boys Swimming and Diving Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Boys Swimming and Diving Championship on March 4, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1097
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1097
 
BY: Senator HARCKHAM
 
  CONGRATULATING the Horace Greeley High School Boys
  Swimming  and  Diving  Team  upon  the  occasion  of
  capturing the  NYSPHSAA  Boys  Swimming  and  Diving
  Championship on March 4, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Horace Greeley High School  Boys  Swimming  and  Diving  Team  upon  the
occasion of capturing back-to-back the NYSPHSAA Boys Swimming and Diving
Championship on Saturday, March 4, 2023, at Ithaca College; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  front  of  a  capacity crowd and a fan base of family,
friends and teammates, the Horace Greeley High School Boys Swimming  and
Diving  Team  won  the title with an outstanding score of 247 points, 50
more than the second-place team; and
 
  WHEREAS, The athletic talent displayed by these young men is due  in
great  part  to  the efforts of Head Coach Margaret Kaplan and Assistant
Coaches Mike Darland and Jeanne  Kellachan,  skilled  and  inspirational
tutors,  respected  for  their  ability  to  develop  potential  into
excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sports competition instills the values of  teamwork,  pride
and  accomplishment,  and  the members of the Horace Greeley High School
Boys Swimming and Diving Team have clearly made a  contribution  to  the
spirit  of  excellence  which  is  a  tradition  of  their  school; now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the  Horace  Greeley  High School Boys Swimming and Diving
Team upon the occasion of  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA  Boys  Swimming  and
Diving Championship on March 4, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members  of  the  Horace  Greeley  High  School  Boys
Swimming  and  Diving Team: Jayden Holmes, Jack Cornish, Zayaan Hussain,
Sebastian Divina, Daniel Lee, Danyaal Hussain,  Evan  Wohl,  Ziheng  Ma,
Naveen  Arjune, Arjun Menon, Erik Nadecki, Eric Engel, Lawrence Gulotta,
Grant Hwang, Tristan Payne, Xiaochuan  Liang,  Jonathan  McCall,  Hudson
Chung, Michael Weyne, Jonas Balcetis, Warren Fu, George Martin, Jonathan
Serra,  Trey  Schlomann,  David  Coppola, Adam Kamal, Oliver Engel, Jack
Moran, Benjamin Chang, Jed  Wei,  Patrick  Fang,  Jules  Martin,  Connor
McHugh,  Gabriel  Grada,  and  Jamie  Lynch; Head Coach Margaret Kaplan,
 
Assistant Coaches Mike Darland  and  Jeanne  Kellachan,  Managers  Ellie
Slive and Rachel Zhang, Athletic Trainers Rachel Marino and Tim Lampron,
and Athletic Director Jamie Block.
