# Creates a pedestrian safety rating system for motor vehicles

**Bill ID:** S343
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S343 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S343)

## Summary

Creates a pedestrian safety rating system for motor vehicles which shall be posted on the department of motor vehicles' website.
