# Relates to unclassified civil service of the state; and authorizes the Syracuse Hancock International Airport to hire and promote employees on a permanent basis

**Bill ID:** S6335BA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** Vetoed

## Summary

Relates to unclassified civil service of the state; authorizes the Syracuse Hancock International Airport to hire and promote employees on a permanent basis.
