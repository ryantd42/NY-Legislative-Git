# Requires certain not-for-profit corporations have a board of directors which reflects the ethnic demographics of the communities that they serve

**Bill ID:** A753
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela J. Hunter
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A753 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A753)

## Summary

Requires not-for-profit corporations have a board of directors which reflects the ethnic demographics of the communities that they serve to be able to receive state funds.
