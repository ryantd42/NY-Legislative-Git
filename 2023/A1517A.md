# Relates to exempting school buses from sales and use tax

**Bill ID:** A1517AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1517A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1517A)

## Summary

Exempts school buses and parts, equipment, lubricants and fuel purchased and used in their operation from sales and use tax.
