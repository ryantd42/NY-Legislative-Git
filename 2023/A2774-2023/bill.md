# Creates stricter fines and criminal penalties for passing a stopped school bus

**Bill ID:** A2774
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Increases fines and penalties for passing a stopped school bus.
