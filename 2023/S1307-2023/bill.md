# Relates to absentee ballot tracking

**Bill ID:** S1307
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1307 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1307)

## Summary

Requires the boards of elections to provide a secure website or web portal through which voters can track their absentee application or absentee ballot.
