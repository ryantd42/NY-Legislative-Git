# Extends the authorization of the city of Oswego to impose an additional rate of sales taxes until 2025

**Bill ID:** S6506
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the city of Oswego to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes from 2023 to 2025.
