# Congratulating the Randolph High School Varsity Boys Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSM Boys Basketball Class C Championship

**Bill ID:** K329
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 329
 
BY: M. of A. Giglio JM
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Randolph  High School Varsity
  Boys Basketball Team upon the occasion of  capturing
  the NYSPHSM Boys Basketball Class C Championship
 
  WHEREAS,  Individual  and team championships are highly sought after
in high school sports; this  Legislative  Body  commends  rare  athletic
achievements  and  pays  special  recognition  to  those who pursue such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Athletic competition helps to enhance both  the  moral  and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate the
Randolph High School Varsity Boys Basketball Team upon the  occasion  of
capturing the NYSPHSM Boys Basketball Class C Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  To  the  praise  and  applause  of their excited fans, the
Cardinals defeated Haldane High School in a thrilling 58-55  victory  to
win  their  first championship in program history on Saturday, March 18,
2023, at the Cool Insuring Arena in Glens Falls, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's colors of royal red and white,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Cardinals throughout their journey  as  they  ended  their
season with an impressive overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  a  sport  such  as  basketball, which demands athletic
prowess, speed and agility, Head Coach Kevin Hind, Assistant Coach Bryce
Morrison, and Junior Varsity Coach Jon Peterson have worked hard to hone
the  skills  of  this  championship  team,  teaching  these  outstanding
athletes  lessons which will prove invaluable both on and off the court;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach Kevin Hind and all the outstanding  athletes  on
the  Randolph  High  School  Varsity  Boys  Basketball Team have clearly
utilized dedication, determination, and teamwork in providing a  lasting
contribution  to  the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their
school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the  members  of  the  Randolph  High  School Varsity Boys
Basketball Team: Owen  Nelson,  Roan  Kelly,  Drew  Hind,  Xander  Hind,
Tristan  Farnham,  Griffin Nelson, Carson Conley, Cooper Freeman, Broden
Brooks, Baker Huntington, Jaiden Huntington, and Elial Bryan; Head Coach
Kevin Hind; Assistant Coach Bryce Morrison: and Junior Varsity Coach Jon
Peterson upon the occasion of  capturing  the  NYSPHSM  Boys  Basketball
Class C Championship on March 18, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members of the  Randolph  High  School  Varsity  Boys
 
Basketball  Team, Head Coach Kevin Hind, Assistant Coach Bryce Morrison,
Junior Varsity Coach Jon Peterson, and Ball Boys  Grady  Huntington  and
Austin Bushey.
