# Repeals sections of law relating to private roads

**Bill ID:** S501
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S501 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S501)

## Summary

Relates to private roads; repeals various provisions relating to the application for the creation of a private road.
