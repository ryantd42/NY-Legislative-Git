# Establishes a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for certain appropriations for the 2019-2020 state fiscal year for grants in aid for drug, violence and crime control and prevention programs

**Bill ID:** R1393
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1393
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R2141  of  2019
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  a  certain  appropriation for the
  2019-20 state fiscal year  for  grants  in  aid  for
  drug,  violence,  and  crime  control and prevention
  programs; and grants for civil or criminal  domestic
  violence  legal  services  or  veterans  civil  or
  criminal legal  services,  as  required  by  a  plan
  setting  forth an itemized list of grantees with the
  amount to be received by each,  or  the  methodology
  for  allocating such appropriation.  Such plan shall
  be  subject  to  the  approval  of  the  temporary
  president  of  the  senate  and  the director of the
  budget  and  thereafter  shall  be  included  in  a
  resolution  calling  for  the  expenditure  of  such
  monies, which  resolution  must  be  approved  by  a
  majority  vote  of all members elected to the senate
  upon a roll call vote

the  budget  and  thereafter shall be included in a resolution calling
for the expenditure of such monies, which resolution must be  approved
by  a  majority  vote of all members elected to the senate upon a roll
