# Relates to prohibiting the sale of beverage containers which use plastic packaging containers

**Bill ID:** A3969
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of beverage containers connected to each other by a separate holding device constructed of plastic.
