# Relates to labeling requirements for electric assisted bicycles

**Bill ID:** S946
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S946 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S946)

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of electric assisted bicycles unless the packaging contains a notice stating that the operation of such electric assisted bicycle on any public highway, road or sidewalk may be unlawful.
