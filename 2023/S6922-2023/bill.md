# Requires the department of transportation to establish a central registry of reports, audits, plans and public inspections for freight rail operators, rail cars and freight lines

**Bill ID:** S6922
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the department of transportation to establish a central registry of reports, audits, plans and public inspections regarding freight rail operators, rail cars and freight lines or routes and rights of way.
