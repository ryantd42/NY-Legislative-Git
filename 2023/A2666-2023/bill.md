# Prohibits use of safety strobe light to change a traffic signal

**Bill ID:** A2666
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jake Ryan Blumencranz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits use of a safety strobe light to change a traffic signal by an unauthorized person.
