# Relates to training police officers on how to respond to situations involving individuals with autism spectrum disorder and other developmental disabilities

**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey

## Summary

Relates to training police officers on how to respond to situations involving individuals with autism spectrum disorder and other developmental disabilities.
