# Relates to authorizing bail for certain offenses committed during a riot

**Bill ID:** A2175
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes bail for class A misdemeanors and felonies committed during a riot.
