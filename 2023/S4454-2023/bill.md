# Relates to expedited teaching licensing by certain military spouses

**Bill ID:** S4454
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to expedited teaching licensing by certain military spouses that hold a license in good standing in another state.
