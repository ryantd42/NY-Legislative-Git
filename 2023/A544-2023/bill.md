# Relates to calculating foundation aid using the three year average small area income and poverty estimate poverty rate

**Bill ID:** A544
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A544 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A544)

## Summary

Relates to calculating foundation aid for school districts using the three year average small area income and poverty estimate poverty rate.
