# Requires pharmacists to include lot numbers or unique identifying numbers on prescription containers

**Bill ID:** A3867
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David McDonough
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires pharmacists to include lot numbers or unique identifying numbers on prescription containers.
