# Relates to adding W-18 to the schedule of controlled substances

**Bill ID:** S1758
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1758 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1758)

## Summary

Adds W-18(4-chloro-N-{1-{2-(4-nitrophenyl)ethyl} 2piperidinylidene}benzenesulfonamide) to the schedule of controlled substances.
