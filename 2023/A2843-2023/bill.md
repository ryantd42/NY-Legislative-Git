# Establishes a parking placard review board

**Bill ID:** A2843
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a parking placard review board to undertake a review of the impact of parking placard use within the city of New York.
