# Requires the state university of New York and the city university of New York provide access to contraceptive drugs and devices

**Bill ID:** A6497
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monica P. Wallace
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the state university of New York and the city university of New York provide access to contraceptive drugs and devices.
