# Provides for the expungement of syringe convictions

**Bill ID:** A7466
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Provides for the expungement of syringe convictions of criminally possessing a hypodermic instrument.
