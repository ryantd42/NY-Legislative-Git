# Establishes the class E felony of dissemination of false missing child information

**Bill ID:** S4280
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the class E felony of dissemination of false missing child information.
