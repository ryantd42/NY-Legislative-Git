# Commending Jamaica's Promise upon the occasion of hosting its Jamaica Independence Gala on August 19, 2023

**Bill ID:** J957
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 957
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  Jamaica's Promise upon the occasion of
  hosting its Jamaica Independence Gala on August  19,
  2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to commend those
illustrious organizations of the State  of  New  York  whose  focus  and
intent  have  made  a substantial impact on the quality of life in their
communities; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Jamaica's Promise upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  its  Jamaica
Independence  Gala  on  Saturday,  August  19,  2023, at New York Hilton
Midtown, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  2001,  Ambassador  Audrey  Marks  developed  Jamaica's
Promise  by  modeling  the  foundation  after  America's  Promise  and
approaching the United States Secretary of State for support; and
 
  WHEREAS, In her capacity as patron for Jamaica's Promise, Ambassador
Audrey Marks has continued to further a comprehensive system by which to
support Jamaica's youth in a myriad of ways,  ranging  from  scholarship
funds and fundraising to direct mentorship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Jamaica's  Promise is a voluntary, non-profit organization
that aims to mobilize resources from Jamaicans both in the United States
and abroad, and friends of Jamaica, for the purpose of  alleviating  the
social  conditions, lack of education, and skill training on the Island;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Dedicated to enriching  the  future  generation,  Jamaica's
Promise supports young people between the ages of 0 to 25 who are unable
to  pursue their education or skill training due to economic challenges;
the Foundation plans to increase  access  to  invaluable  resources  and
opportunities for these at-risk youths; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Intending  to  bring  together  nonprofits,  community
organizations, businesses, and government organizations  with  a  common
goal, Jamaica's Promise is devoted to advancing the welfare of Jamaica's
underprivileged  youths;  this Gala with provide an avenue for people to
contribute to charitable causes for Jamaicans; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of Jamaica's Promise is in  direct  correlation
to  the  efforts  of  its  dedicated  volunteers  and  sponsors,  whose
involvement is, and always has  been,  characterized  by  an  impressive
commitment,  an  unbridled enthusiasm, and an uncompromising standard of
excellence in all endeavors  on  behalf  of  the  organization  and  the
community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought to our attention, they should be celebrated  and  recognized  by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Jamaica's Promise upon  the  occasion  of  hosting  its  Jamaica
Independence Gala on August 19, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Ambassador Audrey Marks, patron, Jamaica's Promise.
