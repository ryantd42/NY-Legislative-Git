# Congratulating Emily Sindoni upon the occasion of capturing the 126-pound National High School Coaches Association Girls National Wrestling Tournament

**Bill ID:** J883
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 883
 
BY: Senator O'MARA
 
  CONGRATULATING  Emily Sindoni upon the occasion of
  capturing  the  126-pound  wrestling  title  at  the
  National  High  School  Coaches  Association  Girls
  National Wrestling Tournament on March 26, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Emily
Sindoni upon the occasion of capturing the 126-pound wrestling title  at
the  National  High  School Coaches Association Girls National Wrestling
Tournament on Sunday, March 26, 2023, in Virginia Beach, Virginia; and
 
  WHEREAS, In front of a capacity crowd and  a  fan  base  of  family,
friends and teammates, Emily Sindoni defeated her opponent from Maryland
in a decisive 7-1 victory to earn her second National title; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition to this monumental triumph, Emily Sindoni won
the 132-pound wrestling title at the inaugural NYSPHSAA Girls  Wrestling
Invitational  on  Friday,  January 27, 2023, at the SRC Arena and Events
Center on the campus of Onondaga  Community  College  in  Syracuse,  New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, A standout wrestler for the Tioga High School Tigers, Emily
Sindoni  ended  her  season  with  an  outstanding  record,  and was the
recipient of  the  prestigious  national  Tricia  Saunders  High  School
Excellence Award by the National Wrestling Hall of Fame, which was based
equally  on  outstanding  wrestling  success, scholastic achievement and
citizenship or community service; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a  sport  such  as  wrestling,  which  demands  athletic
prowess,  speed  and agility, Coaches Kris Harrington, Jason Bellis, Jon
Martin, and Ryan Macwhinnie worked hard  to  hone  the  skills  of  this
champion,  teaching  this  outstanding  athlete lessons which will prove
invaluable both on and off the mat; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Emily  Sindoni  has  clearly  utilized  dedication,
determination  and  teamwork  in providing a lasting contribution to the
spirit of excellence which is a tradition of her school; now, therefore,
be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Emily Sindoni upon the occasion of capturing the 126-pound
National  High  School  Coaches  Association  Girls  National  Wrestling
Tournament on March 26, 2023; and be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Emily Sindoni, and Coaches Kris Harrington, Jason Bellis,
Jon Martin, and Ryan Macwhinnie.
