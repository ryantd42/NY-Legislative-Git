# Establishes incapacity to consent when a person is a witness to or subject of an investigation under certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** S2628
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes incapacity to consent when a person is a witness to or subject of an investigation under certain circumstances.
