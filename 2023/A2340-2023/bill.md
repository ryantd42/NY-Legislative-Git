# Relates to the ceremonial designation of the Franklin Delano Roosevelt Mid-Hudson bridge as the "Franklin and Eleanor Roosevelt Mid-Hudson Bridge"

**Bill ID:** A2340
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jonathan Jacobson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Designates the Franklin Delano Roosevelt Mid-Hudson bridge as the "Franklin and Eleanor Roosevelt Mid-Hudson Bridge".
