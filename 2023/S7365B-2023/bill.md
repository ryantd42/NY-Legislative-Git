# Relates to pistol converters

**Bill ID:** S7365BB
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Requires reasonable controls and procedures to be taken to prevent the installation and use of a pistol converter.
