# Prohibits waste transfer stations from being located near public housing projects

**Bill ID:** S3208
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits marine solid waste transfer stations from being located near public housing projects.
