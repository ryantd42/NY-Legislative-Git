# Repeals the portion of the criminal procedure law dealing with discovery and the ability for a defendant to request access to a crime scene or other relevant premises

**Bill ID:** A3360
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Edward Ra
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Repeals provisions dealing with discovery and the ability for a defendant to request access to a crime scene or other relevant premises.
