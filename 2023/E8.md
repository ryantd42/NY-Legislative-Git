# Requires the Speaker of the Assembly to appoint a two person committee to advise the Senate of the election of Carl E. Heastie as Speaker of the Assembly

**Bill ID:** E8
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Helene Weinstein
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E8 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/E8)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 8
 
BY: M. of A. Weinstein
 
  ESTABLISHING a committee of the Assembly to advise
  the Senate
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  committee  of two be appointed by the Speaker to
advise the Senate of the election of Carl E. Heastie as Speaker  of  the
Assembly  and  to  inform  that  Honorable  Body  that  the  Assembly is
organized and ready to proceed with business.
