# Establishes programs to encourage the removal and replacement of single-wall steel home heating oil storage tanks which are leaking or otherwise pose a threat to the environment

**Bill ID:** S4701
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Thomas F. O'Mara
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the department of environmental conservation and office of temporary and disability assistance to establish programs to encourage the removal and replacement of single-wall steel home heating oil storage tanks which are leaking or otherwise pose a threat to the environment.

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. The department of environmental conservation  shall  estab-

  § 2. (a) The commissioner of the department of environmental conserva-

 single-walled  home  heating oil tanks are phased out of use in New York

  (b) After January 1, 2027, home heating oil storage tanks  for  single

 with double-bottom-wall steel tanks, double-wall steel tanks with  plas-
 tic liners, fiberglass tanks of both single and double wall construction
 or  with  such  other  storage tanks which are capable of preventing and
 containing oil spills,  including  those  having  secondary  containment
 capacity equal to or exceeding 110 percent of the primary storage capac-
 ity.
  §  3. The commissioner of the department of environmental conservation
 shall develop and promulgate regulations which shall provide  that  home
 heating oil storage tanks for newly constructed single family residences
 shall  be  double-bottom-wall  steel tanks, double-wall steel tanks with
 plastic liners, or such other tanks which are capable of preventing  and
 containing  oil  spills,  including  those  having secondary containment
