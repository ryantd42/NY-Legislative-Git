# Eases the vehicle registration process for the agricultural industry by extending the length of seasonal permits

**Bill ID:** A2687
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Allows the department of transportation to issue seasonal agricultural permits for oversize vehicles.
