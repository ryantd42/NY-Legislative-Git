# Establishes the power authority of the state of New York shall make low cost hydropower available to certain hospitals

**Bill ID:** A2219
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo J. Morinello
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the power authority of the state of New York shall make low cost hydropower available to hospitals and municipal housing authorities located within Niagara County.
