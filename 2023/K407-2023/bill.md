# Honoring Chief James Schrammel upon the occasion of his retirement after 32 years of distinguished service to the Glens Falls Fire Department

**Bill ID:** K407
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 407
 
BY: M. of A. Woerner
 
  HONORING  Chief  James Schrammel upon the occasion
  of his retirement after 32  years  of  distinguished
  service to the Glens Falls Fire Department
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge
those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Chief James Schrammel upon the occasion of his retirement after 32 years
of distinguished service to the Glens Falls Fire Department on Saturday,
May 6, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, For the past  32  years,  Chief  James  Schrammel  rendered
faithful,  conscientious,  and  valuable service to the Glens Falls Fire
Department; and
 
  WHEREAS, Chief James Schrammel's notice of retirement was  announced
on  Tuesday,  April 25, 2023, at the Common Council meeting where he was
reporting on the state of the fire  department;  the  announcement  came
exactly 13 years from the day he was named Chief; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his official acts, Chief James Schrammel was governed by
a keen sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Chief  James  Schrammel  has  been an integral part of the
successes experienced by the Glens Falls Fire Department  thus  far;  he
has  been  greatly  involved  in recruitment efforts and training of new
firefighters to become the best they can be in relation to  serving  the
Department; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In his capacity as Fire Chief, James Schrammel served with
loyalty, honor and distinction; and
 
  WHEREAS, During his tenure with the  Glens  Falls  Fire  Department,
Chief  James  Schrammel  earned the admiration, esteem, and affection of
his colleagues; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor Chief James Schrammel upon the occasion of his retirement after 32
years of distinguished service; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Chief James Schrammel.
