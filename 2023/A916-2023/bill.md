# Relates to extending the two hundred foot rule to certain treatment facilities

**Bill ID:** A916
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Latoya Joyner
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A916 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A916)

## Summary

Relates to extending the two hundred foot rule to certain treatment facilities for mental illness and chemical dependence operating under the mental hygiene law.
