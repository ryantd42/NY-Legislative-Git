# Relates to providing loan forgiveness to CASACS

**Bill ID:** A1017
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1017 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1017)

## Summary

Provides loan forgiveness to credentialed alcoholism and substance abuse counselors (CASACs) who reside in the state and work in a facility licensed by the state.
