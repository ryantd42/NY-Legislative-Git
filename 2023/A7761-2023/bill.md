# Establishes the village incorporation commission

**Bill ID:** A7761
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Establishes the village incorporation commission to evaluate and make decisions regarding village incorporation elections.
