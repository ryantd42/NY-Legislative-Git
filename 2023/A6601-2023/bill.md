# Prohibits state chartered banking institutions from investing in and providing financing for private prisons

**Bill ID:** A6601
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karines Reyes
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Prohibits state chartered banking institutions from investing in and providing financing for private prisons.
