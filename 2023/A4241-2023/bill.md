# Relates to establishing certain requirements for the dormitory prior to such authority issuing bonds and notes to finance certain projects

**Bill ID:** A4241
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to establishing certain requirements for the dormitory authority of the state of New York prior to such authority issuing bonds and notes to finance projects for a private institution or organization.
