# Permits companies to assess various insurance quotes without having binding agreements with insurance brokers

**Bill ID:** A3189
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mike Reilly
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Permits companies to assess various insurance quotes without having binding agreements with insurance brokers.
