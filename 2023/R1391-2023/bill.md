# Establishes a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for a certain appropriation for the 2022-2023 state fiscal year for grants in aid to certain agricultural organizations

**Bill ID:** R1391
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1391
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R2861  of  2022
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  a  certain  appropriation for the
  2022-2023 state fiscal year for  grants  in  aid  to
  certain agricultural organizations
 
  RESOLVED,  that pursuant to and as required by moneys appropriated
in Section 1 of chapter 53 of the laws of 2022, which enacts  the  aid
to  localities, local assistance account from grants in aid to certain
agricultural organizations, including sub-allocation  or  transfer  to
any  department,  agency,  or  public  authority.  Funds  from  this
appropriation shall be allocated pursuant to a plan  approved  by  the
temporary president of the senate and the director of the budget which
sets  forth  either an itemized list of grantees with the amount to be
received by each or the methodology for allocating such appropriation.
Such plan and the grantees listed therein  shall  be  subject  to  the
approval  of  the  director  of  the  budget  and  thereafter shall be
included in a resolution calling for the expenditure of  such  monies,
which  resolution  must  be approved by a majority vote of all members
elected to the senate upon a roll call vote  in  accordance  with  the
following schedule and as adopted by Senate Resolution R2861 of 2022:
 
CNY Lyme & Tick-Borne Disease Alliance
  50,000
Teens for Food Justice
  20,000
Teens for Food Justice, Inc. 
  10,000
The Lincoln Park Conservancy, Inc. 
  20,000
