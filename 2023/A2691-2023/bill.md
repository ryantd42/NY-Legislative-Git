# Requires the secretary of state to email a copy of all new laws passed to representatives of local governments

**Bill ID:** A2691
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jake Ryan Blumencranz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the secretary of state to email a copy of all new laws passed to representatives of local governments.
