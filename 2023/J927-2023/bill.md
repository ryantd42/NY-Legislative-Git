# Commending the Junior Members of the East Norwich Volunteer Fire Company No. 1 upon their designation as the NVFC National Junior Firefighter Program of the Year and the FASNY Youth Group of the Year

**Bill ID:** J927
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 927
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  the Junior Members of the East Norwich
  Volunteer Fire Company No. 1 upon  the  occasion  of
  their  designation  as  the  NVFC  National  Junior
  Firefighter Program of the Year and the FASNY  Youth
  Group of the Year on June 4, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  The  volunteer firefighters of New York State are renowned
throughout the Nation; their reputation is synonymous with excellence in
the arduous field of fire protection and prevention; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend the East Norwich Volunteer Fire Company No. 1 upon the  occasion
of  their designation as the NVFC National Junior Firefighter Program of
the Year and the FASNY Youth Group of the  Year,  to  be  celebrated  on
Sunday,  June  4,  2023,  at  the  East Norwich Fire Company No. 1 Award
Ceremony; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Volunteer  fire  departments  are  an  integral  part  of
community  protection  services  in  every  locality  of  our State; the
courageous and devoted junior firefighters of East Norwich Fire  Company
No. 1 have valiantly responded to all types of fire emergencies and have
rightfully won the praise and respect of the community which they serve;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  two  esteemed  awards,  the  NVFC  National  Junior
Firefighter Program of the Year, and the FASNY Youth Group of  the  Year
Award,  were  awarded to the East Norwich Fire Company No. 1 as a direct
result of the commitment,  diligence,  and  dedicated  service  of  this
year's  Junior  Firefighters:  Matthew  Bambino;  Xavier  Canales; Talia
DelValle;  Caitlin  Dias;  Robert  Gobbo;  Anthony  Izquierdo;  Lucas
Izquierdo;  Omek  Kumar;  Michael Lewis; Khadeejah Memon; Clare Murcott;
William Myers; Anthony Petrizzo; Thomas  Rahilly;  Nick  Syrett;  Joseph
Travaglia; Ivy Valerie; Daniel Wulforst; and Steven Wulforst; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  brave  junior  volunteer firefighters of East Norwich
Volunteer Fire Company No. 1 are prepared to risk their lives every  day
to  help save the lives of others and to protect homes and property from
the destruction caused by fire; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  that  we  should
give  special  recognition  to  those  who  work  so assiduously for the
betterment of their communities, and  acknowledge  publicly  the  heroic
good  works  performed  by  the volunteer fire departments of this great
Empire State, their officers, and auxiliaries; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  the East Norwich Volunteer Fire Company No. 1 upon the occasion
of their designation as the NVFC National Junior Firefighter Program  of
the  Year  and the FASNY Youth Group of the Year on June 4, 2023; and be
it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the aforementioned East Norwich Junior Firefighters.
