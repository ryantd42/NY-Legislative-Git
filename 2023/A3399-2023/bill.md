# Exempts sales of bottled water from sales and use taxes

**Bill ID:** A3399
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Exempts retail sales of bottled water from the imposition of sales and use taxes.
