# Relates to eligibility for unemployment benefits for certain persons under quarantine for coronavirus (COVID-19)

**Bill ID:** S1582
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1582 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1582)

## Summary

Provides eligibility for unemployment benefits for certain persons under quarantine for coronavirus (COVID-19).
