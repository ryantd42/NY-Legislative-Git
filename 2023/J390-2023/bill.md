# Honoring Adam Crosley upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the 2023 Trailblazer Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce

**Bill ID:** J390
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 390
 
BY: Senator STEC
 
  HONORING  Adam  Crosley  upon  the occasion of his
  designation as recipient  of  the  2023  Trailblazer
  Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend the leaders  of  commerce  and  industry  whose  accomplishments
contribute  to  the economic health and prosperity of the communities of
the State of New  York,  and  whose  purposeful  endeavors  as  involved
members  of  the community serve to enhance the quality of life therein;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Adam  Crosley  upon  the occasion of his designation as recipient of the
2023 Trailblazer Award by the North Country Chamber of  Commerce,  which
was celebrated at its 111th Annual Awards Dinner, "A Winter Wonderland,"
on  Friday,  January 27, 2023, at the West Side Ballroom in Plattsburgh,
New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Adam Crosley is receiving this prestigious  award  for  his
steadfast  and  unremitting commitment to his Plattsburgh, New York, and
North Country communities; he has distinguished  himself  as  a  leading
example of both excellence in business and in community support; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Principal of Crosley Mortgage Team at Crown Home Mortgage,
Adam Crosley began his illustrious career  after  earning  his  mortgage
license in September of 2008; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  more  than  20  years  of  lending experience on his
resume, the  SUNY  Plattsburgh  graduate  also  spends  his  time  as  a
motivational  speaker  and  mentor  for  up-and-coming lenders on how to
successfully run your own branch and mortgage operations; and
 
  WHEREAS, A true asset and leader within his community, Adam  Crosley
is  actively  involved  with many local organizations, including the Boy
Scouts of America; furthermore, in an effort to invest in his community,
he also recently purchased Booth Drive Plaza in Plattsburgh,  New  York,
where his office is currently located; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown by an
individual for the benefits of others which Adam Crosley  has  displayed
throughout his life; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body that those who
enhance the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown  a
long  and  sustained  commitment to excellence certainly have earned the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Adam Crosley upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of
the 2023 Trailblazer Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce; and
be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Adam Crosley.
