# Relates to requirements for reducing emissions from medium and heavy-duty vehicles

**Bill ID:** S6298A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Enacts an advanced clean fleets law to reduce vehicle emissions for medium- and heavy-duty vehicles.
