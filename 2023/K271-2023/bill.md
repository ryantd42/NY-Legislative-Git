# Commemorating the 235th Anniversary of the Town of Amenia on March 7, 2023

**Bill ID:** K271
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 271
 
BY: M. of A. Beephan
 
  COMMEMORATING the 235th Anniversary of the Town of
  Amenia on March 7, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of this Legislative Body to honor and
commemorate  the  distinguished  histories  of  the  communities  which
comprise the noble body of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  235th  Anniversary  of  the Town of Amenia on March 7,
2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Amenia borders  Connecticut  and  was  made  into  a  steel
manufacturing  place  for  the army during the Revolutionary War when it
was originally founded; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  population  of  Amenia  has  remained  around  10,000
residents since its establishment; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  bustling  community,  Amenia  has two metro north train
stations for the Harlem Line and the excellent school system of Webutuck
Elementary School and Webutuck High School; and
 
  WHEREAS, Of historic origin, and remaining fruitful over the ebb and
flow of decades of growth and change, the Town of  Amenia  continues  to
provide the quintessential quality of life for its residents; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  residents of the Town of Amenia have a bright outlook
for the future of their community, which remains a  beautiful  place  in
which to live and raise a family; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  recognition  of  the Town of Amenia's rich history and
enduring contribution to the State of New York, this Legislative Body is
justly proud to pay tribute to this spirited town upon the  occasion  of
its 235th Anniversary; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the 235th Anniversary of the Town  of  Amenia  on  March  7,
2023,  recognizing  the significance of the role it continues to play in
the life of the community of the State of New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Town of Amenia.
