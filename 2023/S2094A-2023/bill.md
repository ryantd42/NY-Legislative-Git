# Authorizes the town of Southampton, county of Suffolk, to enact a homestead exemption

**Bill ID:** S2094AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S2094A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2094A)

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Southampton, county of Suffolk, to enact by local law a homestead exemption; provides that such exemption shall not exceed $50,000 in full assessed value.
