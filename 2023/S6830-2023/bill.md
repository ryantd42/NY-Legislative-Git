# Requires credit reporting agencies to furnish proof of identity theft to creditors upon debtor's request

**Bill ID:** S6830
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires credit reporting agencies to furnish proof of identity theft to creditors upon debtor's request.
