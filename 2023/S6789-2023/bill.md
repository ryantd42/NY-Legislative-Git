# Relates to certain mined land-plans

**Bill ID:** S6789
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for a minimum 10 foot buffer from the water table for all mining plans for mining on Long Island.
