# Extends the ability of the county of Albany to impose and collect taxes on occupancy of hotel or motel rooms in Albany county

**Bill ID:** A7009
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the ability of the county of Albany to impose and collect taxes on occupancy of hotel or motel rooms in Albany county.
