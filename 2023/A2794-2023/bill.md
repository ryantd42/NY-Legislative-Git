# Requires the department of health to develop and maternal health care providers to distribute written information about episiotomy to maternity patients

**Bill ID:** A2794
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the department of health to develop and maternal health care providers to distribute written information about episiotomy to maternity patients.
