# Relates to real property tax exemptions for disabled veterans

**Bill ID:** A3471
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Increases, at local option, real property tax exemptions for combat veterans who are 100% permanently disabled.
