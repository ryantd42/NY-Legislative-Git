# Extends the authorization for the county of Schoharie to impose a county recording tax on obligation secured by a mortgage on real property

**Bill ID:** S4585A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authorization for the county of Schoharie to impose a county recording tax on obligation secured by a mortgage on real property.
