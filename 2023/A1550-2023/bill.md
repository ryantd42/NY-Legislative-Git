# Allows courts to hold a pre-trial community safety and well-being hearing

**Bill ID:** A1550
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1550 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1550)

## Summary

Allows courts to hold a pre-trial community safety and well-being hearing.
