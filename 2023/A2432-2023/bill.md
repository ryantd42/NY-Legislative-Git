# Increases the amount in the tax stabilization reserve fund to 5% of state fiscal year disbursements

**Bill ID:** A2432
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases the amount in the tax stabilization reserve fund to 5% of state fiscal year disbursements.
