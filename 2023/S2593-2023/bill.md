 AN  ACT  to  amend the limited liability company law and the partnership
  law, in relation to the  definition  of  office  and  the  content  of
  notices published in newspapers

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Subdivision (s) of section 102  of  the  limited  liability
 company law is amended to read as follows:
  (s)  "Office of the limited liability company" means the office of the
 limited liability company, the location of which is stated in the  arti-
 cles  of organization of a domestic limited liability company, or in the
 application for authority of a foreign limited liability  company.  Such
 office  [need  not be a place where business activities are conducted by
 such limited liability company] SHALL BE A PLACE IN  THE  COUNTY  WITHIN
 THIS STATE OF THE PRINCIPAL AND ACTUAL BUSINESS LOCATION OF THE COMPANY.
 IN NO EVENT SHALL IT ONLY BE THE ADDRESS OF ITS REGISTERED AGENT, A POST
 OFFICE  MAILBOX,  OR  A  PRIVATE  MAILBOX AT A MAIL LOGISTICS CENTER FOR
 PURPOSES OF SATISFYING THE REQUIREMENTS  OF  SECTION  TWO  HUNDRED  SIX,
 EIGHT  HUNDRED TWO, ONE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED THREE OR ONE THOUSAND THREE
 HUNDRED SIX OF THIS CHAPTER.
  § 2. Paragraph 3-a of subdivision (a) of section 206  of  the  limited
 liability  company law, as amended by chapter 44 of the laws of 2006, is
 amended to read as follows:
  (3-a) the street address of the principal business location[, if any];
  § 3. Subparagraph 4-a of paragraph (i) of subdivision (b)  of  section
 802  of  the  limited liability company law, as amended by chapter 44 of
 the laws of 2006, is amended to read as follows:
  (4-a) the street address of the principal business location[, if any];

  § 4. Clause (iii-a) of subparagraph (A) of paragraph 2 of  subdivision
 (c)  of section 1203 of the limited liability company law, as amended by
 chapter 44 of the laws of 2006, is amended to read as follows:
  (iii-a)  the  street  address  of the principal business location[, if
 any];
  § 5. Subparagraph 4-a of paragraph (i) of subdivision (d)  of  section
 1306  of  the limited liability company law, as amended by chapter 44 of
 the laws of 2006, is amended to read as follows:
  (4-a) the street address of the principal business location[, if any];
  § 6. Subdivision (j) of section 121-101 of  the  partnership  law,  as
 added by chapter 950 of the laws of 1990, is amended to read as follows:
  (j)  "Office  of  limited partnership" means the office of THE LIMITED
 PARTNERSHIP, the location of which  is  stated  in  the  certificate  of
 limited  partnership of a domestic limited partnership, or in the appli-
 cation for authority of a foreign limited partnership or  any  amendment
 thereof.  Such office [need not be a place where business activities are
 conducted by such limited partnership] SHALL BE A PLACE  IN  THE  COUNTY
 WITHIN  THIS  STATE OF THE PRINCIPAL AND ACTUAL BUSINESS LOCATION OF THE
 PARTNERSHIP. IN NO EVENT SHALL IT ONLY BE THE ADDRESS OF ITS  REGISTERED
 AGENT,  A  POST OFFICE MAILBOX, OR A PRIVATE MAILBOX AT A MAIL LOGISTICS
 CENTER FOR PURPOSES OF SATISFYING THE REQUIREMENTS OF SECTION 121-201 OR
 121-902 OF THIS CHAPTER.
  § 7. Subparagraph 3-a of paragraph (i) of subdivision (c)  of  section
 121-201  of the partnership law, as amended by chapter 44 of the laws of
 2006, is amended to read as follows:
  (3-a) the street address of the principal business location[, if any];
  § 8. Subparagraph 4-a of paragraph (i) of subdivision (d)  of  section
 121-902  of the partnership law, as amended by chapter 44 of the laws of
 2006, is amended to read as follows:
  (4-a) the street address of the principal business location[, if any];
  § 9. This act shall take effect immediately.
