# Commending I AM C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D. upon the occasion of hosting its Annual Fundraising Gala on June 8, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1135
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1135
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING I AM C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D. upon the occasion
  of  hosting  its  Annual Fundraising Gala on June 8,
  2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  to  publicly
recognize those extraordinary individuals who have made contributions to
the cultural enrichment of the State of New York, and to the success and
progress of society during their life's work; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  I  AM  C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D. upon the occasion of hosting its Annual
Fundraising Gala on Thursday, June 8, 2023, at the Williamsburg Hotel in
Brooklyn, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, For their  true  dedication  to  their  community  and  the
furtherance  of  I  AM  C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D.'s mission, this year's honorees
include the Dreamville Foundation; Arnie Weissmann, Editor in  Chief  of
Travel  Weekly;  Danielle  Browne Esq., Founder of The Browne Firm PLLC;
Nikki Bethel, President & CEO of the Emma Bowen Foundation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  I  AM  C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D.  is  a  registered  non-profit
organization  dedicated  to  promoting self-empowerment and appreciation
for cultural richness and diversity  amongst  high  school  students  of
color  in  the  New  York  Metropolitan  Area; the I AM C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D.
acronym stands for Confident, Unique, Leading,  Tenacious,  Unstoppable,
Regal, Educated, and Daring; and
 
  WHEREAS,  I AM C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D. is devoted to empowering high school
youth of color to become innovative global citizens through experiential
learning, mentorship and cultural experiences, while being  passionately
dedicated  to  promoting  self-empowerment and appreciation for cultural
richness and  diversity  by  creative  global  leaders  of  tomorrow  to
envision  a  life  beyond  their  immediate communities while fulfilling
personal and professional goals towards success; and
 
  WHEREAS, Through mentorship, leadership workshops, and travel abroad
experiences,  the  organization  takes  a  holistic  approach  to  youth
development,  supporting  their  social,  intellectual,  and  mental
wellbeing; and
 
  WHEREAS, I AM C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D. focuses on travel  abroad,  community
service,  mentorship,  the  Community Day Street Festival, and the Youth
Empowerment Series programs, truly enriching the youth  participants  of
the organization; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  annual  evening  of  culture  and  excellence  is  a
culminating celebration of the program year, the I  AM  C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D.
courageous students, and bold change makers of today; this special night
fuels  the  heartbeat  of  the  organization- the Cultural Series Summer
Travel Program; this summer, students will travel abroad to Egypt; and
 
  WHEREAS, The current Board Members overseeing I AM  C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D.
include  Iman  Dean Strong; Melissa Shields; Maleah Land; Reggie Hudson;
Bunmi Olosunde; Monique Holaman Esq.; Urian Smith; and  Rondel  Charles,
acknowledging  these  exceptional individuals and their contributions to
the  organization,  fully confident that they will continue to enjoy the
sparkling successes they have experienced thus far; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  I  AM  C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D. upon the occasion of hosting its Annual
Fundraising Gala on June 8, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to I AM C.U.L.T.U.R.E.D., and the aforementioned honorees.
