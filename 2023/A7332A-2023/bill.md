# Relates to the rights, benefits and privileges that members of a retirement system who retire from such system and later rejoin such retirement system

**Bill ID:** A7332AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that members of a retirement system who retire from such system and later rejoin the retirement system are reinstated with the same rights, benefits and privileges they had prior to retirement.
