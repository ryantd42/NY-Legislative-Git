# Relates to the delivery of telehealth services by veterinarians

**Bill ID:** A6745
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Permits the delivery of telehealth services by veterinarians under certain conditions.
