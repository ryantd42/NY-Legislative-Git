# Requires the regents exam to be waived for any student who scores a 3, 4 or 5 on the advanced placement examination in the subject of the regents exam

**Bill ID:** A2541
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the regents exam to be waived for any student who scores a 3, 4 or 5 on the advanced placement examination in the subject of the regents exam.
