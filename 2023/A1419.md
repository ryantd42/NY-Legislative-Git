# Eliminates the photo image fee for veterans who have their license amended with a veteran notation

**Bill ID:** A1419
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1419 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1419)

## Summary

Eliminates the photo image fee for veterans who have their license or identification cards amended with a veteran notation.
