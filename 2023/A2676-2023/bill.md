# Establishes a task force to study the potential designation of economic empowerment zones in the state of New York

**Bill ID:** A2676
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a task force to study the potential designation of economic empowerment zones for the mining of cryptocurrencies in the state of New York.

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  a. A task force is hereby established to study the poten-

  b. For purposes of this act:
  (i) "cryptocurrency" shall mean a digital currency in which encryption
 techniques are used to regulate the generation of units and currency and
 verify the transfer of funds, operating  independently  from  a  central
 bank;
  (ii)  "mining"  shall  mean  the process of spending computer power to
 process transactions and add such transactions  to  a  cryptocurrencies'
 blockchain;
  (iii)  "blockchain"  shall mean a digital ledger in which transactions
 made in cryptocurrencies are recorded chronologically and publicly; and
  (iv) "economic empowerment zone" shall mean an area within  the  state
 that  has  been  designated by such term pursuant to section 1391 of the
 federal Internal Revenue Code (26 Unites States Code ยง 1391).

  (ii)  how such eligibility requirements and processes would apply to a
 locality attempting to receive an economic empowerment zone  designation
 for the mining of cryptocurrencies;
  (iii)  the  potential impact of such a designation on the workforce of
 such locality;
