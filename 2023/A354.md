# Requires the addition of fog lines on all state highways, roads and other public ways under the jurisdiction of the department of transportation when repaved

**Bill ID:** A354
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A354 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A354)

## Summary

Requires the addition of fog lines on all state highways and other public ways under the jurisdiction of the department of transportation when such highway or public way is repaved.
