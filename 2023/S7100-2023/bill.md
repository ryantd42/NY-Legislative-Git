# Increases the state discretionary buying threshold without a formal competitive process for certain commodities from certain businesses

**Bill ID:** S7100
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the state discretionary buying threshold without a formal competitive process for certain commodities from certain businesses, including construction services.
