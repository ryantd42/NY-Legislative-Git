# Relates to the imposition of additional rates of sales and compensating use taxes by Erie county

**Bill ID:** A6629
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the imposition of additional rates of sales and compensating use taxes by Erie county to November 30, 2025.
