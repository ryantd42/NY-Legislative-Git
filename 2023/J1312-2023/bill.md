**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1312
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  HONORING  Right  Worshipful Dayequan El-Amin, Most
  Worshipful Gregory R. Smith, Most Worshipful Gregory
  R. Smith Jr., Right Worshipful Reverend  Dr.  Darren
  Morton,  and  Derek  Phillips  upon  the occasion of
  their designation for special recognition by  Electa
  Chapter  No.  14,  Prince  Hall  Order Eastern Star,
  State of New York, on June 24, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
honor  those  distinguished  citizens who would devote their life's work
and purpose to community service, contributing to  the  welfare  of  the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Right Worshipful Dayequan El-Amin, Most  Worshipful  Gregory  R.  Smith,
Most  Worshipful  Gregory  R.  Smith  Jr., Right Worshipful Reverend Dr.
Darren Morton, and Derek Phillips upon the occasion of their designation
for special recognition by Electa Chapter  No.  14,  Prince  Hall  Order
Eastern Star, State of New York, at its Father Appreciation Luncheon and
Father-Daughter  Dance,  "Salute to Our Kings," on June 24, 2023, at the
Prince Hall Masonic Temple in Harlem, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Chartered as a member of the Prince Hall family on June  6,
1916,  Electa  Chapter  No.  14  has provided outstanding service to the
surrounding community for over 100 years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  year's  distinguished  honorees  include:  Right
Worshipful  Dayequan  El-Amin,  Adelphic Union Lodge No. 14, Outstanding
Father Award; Most Worshipful Gregory R. Smith, Past Grand  Master  Most
Worshipful  Prince Hall Grand Lodge of NY, Legacy Award; Most Worshipful
Gregory R.  Smith Jr., Grand Master Most Worshipful  Prince  Hall  Grand
Lodge  of NY, Legacy Award; Right Worshipful Reverend Dr. Darren Morton,
Comptroller City of Mount Vernon, Outstanding Father  Award;  and  Derek
Phillips, Real Dads Network, Community Service Award; and
 
  WHEREAS,  During  the  luncheon, the Scholarship Committee will also
present the 2023 Reach for the  STARS  Scholarship  to  three  deserving
youth, and will recognize three outstanding fathers for their dedication
to their family, the CRAFT and the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Individuals  who give of their time and energies and serve
the best interests of their communities are an asset beyond remuneration
and cannot be sufficiently extolled; and
 
  WHEREAS, These 2023 honoree have all given not only  of  their  time
and  energies  but also of their competence, intelligence and leadership
and consequently have been designated for special honor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Rare  indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by
individuals  for  the  benefit  of others which these men have displayed
throughout their lives; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of  such  noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the  citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Right Worshipful Dayequan  El-Amin,  Most  Worshipful  Gregory  R.
Smith,  Most  Worshipful Gregory R. Smith Jr., Right Worshipful Reverend
Dr. Darren Morton,  and  Derek  Phillips  upon  the  occasion  of  their
designation  for  special  recognition  by Electa Chapter No. 14, Prince
Hall Order Eastern Star, State of New York, on June 24, 2023; and be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the aforementioned honorees.
