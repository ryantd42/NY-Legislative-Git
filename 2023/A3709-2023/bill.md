# Authorizes certain local municipal agencies to use surveillance cameras to combat illegal dumping

**Bill ID:** A3709
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes certain local municipal agencies to use surveillance cameras to combat illegal dumping.
