# Relates to imposing liability on owners of motor vehicles for failure to comply with vehicle sound limits

**Bill ID:** S1982
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1982 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1982)

## Summary

Imposes liability on owners of motor vehicles for failure to comply with vehicle sound limits in the city of New York.
