 TWO OF SECTION FOUR HUNDRED FIFTY-NINE-A OF THE SOCIAL SERVICES LAW,  TO
 A FEDERAL, STATE OR LOCAL AGENCY.
  Any  complaint  alleging such an unlawful discriminatory practice must
