# Relates to processing fees resulting from credit card or other non-cash payments selected by passengers of taxicabs and for-hire transportation

**Bill ID:** S1847
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1847 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1847)

## Summary

Grants authority for medallion owners and designated agents to pass along credit card processing fees to the customer.
