# Requires certain waste transported by rail to be covered with hard tarping

**Bill ID:** S2022
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S2022 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2022)

## Summary

Requires putrescible and non-putrescible waste to be covered by a hard tarp when transported by rail.
