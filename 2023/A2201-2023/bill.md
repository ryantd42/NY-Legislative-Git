# Directs the commissioner of health to promulgate a list of prescription drugs for which an emergency thirty-day supply cannot be obtained

**Bill ID:** A2201
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Catalina Cruz
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of health to promulgate a list of prescription drugs for which an emergency thirty-day supply cannot be obtained during a state emergency.
