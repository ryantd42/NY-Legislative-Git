# Allows for the taxation of certain state properties for school tax purposes

**Bill ID:** S41
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S41 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S41)

## Summary

Allows for the taxation of certain state properties in western New York for school tax purposes.
