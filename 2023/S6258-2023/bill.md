# Requires every assessing unit to conduct a revaluation of assessment at least every eight years

**Bill ID:** S6258
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires every assessing unit to conduct a revaluation of assessment at least every eight years.
