# Establishes a procedure for appointing a president or chairperson upon a vacancy of such position and corrects certain gendered language

**Bill ID:** S3437
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a procedure for appointing a president or chairperson upon a vacancy of such position; corrects certain gendered language.
