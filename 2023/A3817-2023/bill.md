# Provides that the intentional murder of a child under the age of twelve shall be a first degree murder offense

**Bill ID:** A3817
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that the intentional murder of a child under the age of twelve shall be a first degree murder offense.
