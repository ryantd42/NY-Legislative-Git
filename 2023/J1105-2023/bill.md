# Honoring Joe Goossen upon the occasion of being inducted into the International Boxing Hall of Fame on June 10-11, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1105
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1105
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  HONORING  Joe  Goossen  upon the occasion of being
  inducted into the International Boxing Hall of  Fame
  on June 10-11, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge
outstanding professionals  in  the  boxing  industry  who  distinguished
themselves  through  their  exceptional  performance,  attaining
unprecedented success and the highest level of personal achievement; Joe
Goossen is one such individual and his  life  of  achievements  inspired
others  and  contributed  to  the respect and admiration of the sport of
boxing; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Joe  Goossen  upon the occasion of being inducted into the International
Boxing Hall of Fame at Hall of Fame  Weekend  on  June  10-11,  2023  in
Canastota, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Working in the boxing industry his whole life, Joe Goossen
rose to fame when he trained brothers Gabriel Ruelas and Rafael  Ruelas,
both of whom became world champions; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  2005, Joe Goossen was inducted into the Boxing Hall of
Fame for his work training champions; and
 
  WHEREAS, Now a sacred spot in  the  world  of  boxing,  Joe  Goossen
opened his Goose Boxing Gym located in Van Nuys, California; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  him  throughout has been his brother, Dan, who was a
promoter and boxing agent of stars such as Mike Tyson, Floyd Mayweather,
Paul Williams, and Bernard Hopkins, who has felt privileged to be a part
of his life and rejoice in his achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, Joe Goossen clearly made a contribution to  the  spirit  of
excellence  which  is  a  tradition  of the International Boxing Hall of
Fame; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of such outstanding athletic accomplishments are brought to
our attention, they should be recognized by all  the  citizens  of  this
great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor  Joe  Goossen  upon  the  occasion  of  being  inducted  into  the
International Boxing Hall of Fame on June 10-11, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Joe Goossen.
