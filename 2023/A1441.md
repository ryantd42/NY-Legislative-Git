# Establishes a credentialing pilot program for direct support professionals

**Bill ID:** A1441
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1441 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1441)

## Summary

Relates to establishing a credentialing pilot program for direct support professionals to assist and enhance the field of direct care support.
