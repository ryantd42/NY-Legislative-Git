# Elevates the level of assault against domestic violence victims to assault in the second degree

**Bill ID:** A3272
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karen McMahon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Elevates the level of assault against domestic violence victims to assault in the second degree, a class D felony.
