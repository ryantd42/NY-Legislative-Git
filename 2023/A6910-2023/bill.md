# Provides for the establishment, incorporation and certification of addiction and substance abuse facilities in the city of New York

**Bill ID:** A6910
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Inez E. Dickens
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of the office of alcoholism and substance abuse services to ensure persons have accessible healthcare, treatment capacities and harm reduction services.
