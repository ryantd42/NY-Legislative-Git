# Relates to hate crimes and bail eligibility

**Bill ID:** S4665
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to hate crimes, as defined in article four hundred eighty-five of the penal law, and bail eligibility.
