# Establishes a multi-agency study group to conduct a study and to make recommendations regarding the optimal pension and retirement plan alternatives which could be made available in this state

**Bill ID:** A6839AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a multi-agency study group to conduct a study and to make recommendations regarding the optimal pension and retirement plan alternatives which could be made available to not-for-profit human services corporations in this state.
