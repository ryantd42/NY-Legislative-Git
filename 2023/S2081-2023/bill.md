# Requires the administration and the instruction in preparation of the TASC examination in the Chinese and Korean languages for pupils who are proficient in these languages

**Bill ID:** S2081
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2081 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2081)

## Summary

Requires the administration and the instruction in preparation of the TASC examination in the Chinese and Korean languages for pupils who are proficient in these languages and of limited English proficiency.
