# Allows the New York state AFL-CIO to select a member to the metropolitan transportation authority

**Bill ID:** A1779
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Colton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Allows the New York state AFL-CIO to select one of the five voting members to the metropolitan transportation authority.
