# Requires issuers of credit cards to give the cardholder at least 45 days notice before closing the cardholder's account

**Bill ID:** S223
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S223 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S223)

## Summary

Requires issuers of credit cards to give the cardholder at least 45 days written notice via email, text message, or written letter before closing the cardholder's account.
