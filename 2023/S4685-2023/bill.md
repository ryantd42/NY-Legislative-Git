# Requires gluten cross contamination safeguards in food establishments owned, operated or leased by any department or agency of the state

**Bill ID:** S4685
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires gluten cross-contamination safeguards in food establishments owned, operated or leased by any department or agency of the state.

 AN ACT to amend the state finance law, the public  health  law  and  the
  education  law,  in  relation  to requiring gluten cross-contamination
  safeguards in food establishments owned, operated  or  leased  by  any
  department or agency of the state or the state university of New York
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. The state finance law is amended by adding  a  new  section
 146-a to read as follows:
  §  146-A.  CONTRACTS FOR FOOD SERVICE ESTABLISHMENTS.  NOTWITHSTANDING
 ANY OTHER PROVISIONS OF LAW, ALL NEW  CONTRACTS,  RENEWAL  CONTRACTS  OR
 CHANGE  ORDERS  FOR AN EXISTING CONTRACT MADE AND AWARDED BY ANY DEPART-
 MENT OR AGENCY OF THE STATE OR THE STATE UNIVERSITY OF NEW YORK, FOR THE
 OPERATION OF A FOOD SERVICE ESTABLISHMENT,  SHALL  CONTAIN  A  PROVISION
 REQUIRING  THE  CONTRACTOR  OR  VENDOR OPERATING SUCH ESTABLISHMENT IN A
 STATE-OWNED, OPERATED OR LEASED BUILDING TO TAKE REASONABLE  PRECAUTIONS
 TO  AVERT  THE CROSS-CONTAMINATION OF GLUTEN-FREE FOODS PREPARED BY SUCH
 ESTABLISHMENT BY FOOD CONTAINING GLUTEN.
  § 2. Section 206 of the public health law is amended by adding  a  new
 subdivision 32 to read as follows:
  32.  THE COMMISSIONER, IN CONSULTATION WITH THE COMMISSIONER OF EDUCA-
 TION, SHALL PROMULGATE RULES AND REGULATIONS SPECIFYING REASONABLE METH-
 ODS TO AVERT THE  CROSS-CONTAMINATION  OF  GLUTEN-FREE  FOODS  BY  FOODS
 CONTAINING  GLUTEN DURING THE PREPARATION OF SUCH FOOD IN CAFETERIAS AND
 RESTAURANTS OR ANY OTHER FOOD ESTABLISHMENT  OPERATED  BY  A  VENDOR  OR
 CONTRACTOR  ON PROPERTIES OWNED, OPERATED OR LEASED BY ANY DEPARTMENT OR
 AGENCY OF THE STATE OR THE STATE UNIVERSITY OF NEW YORK.
  § 3. The education law is amended by adding a  new  section  101-c  to
