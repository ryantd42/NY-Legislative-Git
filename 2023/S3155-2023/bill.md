# Establishes the New York state fast food franchisor accountability act

**Bill ID:** S3155
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jabari Brisport
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the New York state fast food franchisor accountability act to hold fast food restaurants jointly liable for violations of certain laws, orders, rules and regulations relating to employment and worker safety.
