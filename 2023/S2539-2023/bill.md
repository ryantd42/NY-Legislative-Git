# Relates to the Long Island Rail Road weekend reduced fare program

**Bill ID:** S2539
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Amends the Long Island Rail Road weekend reduced fare program to include the East New York station.
