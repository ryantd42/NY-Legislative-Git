# Authorizes the village of Canton to reduce the speed limit on certain public roadways

**Bill ID:** S4231
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the village of Canton to reduce the speed limit on East Main Street to forty miles per hour.
