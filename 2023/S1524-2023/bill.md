# Provides test preparation for certain students intending to apply to take the specialized high school admissions test

**Bill ID:** S1524
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1524 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1524)

## Summary

Provides test preparation for students in the seventh grade and eighth grade intending to apply to take the specialized high school admissions test.
