# Authorizes distinctive license plates for supporters of the conservation of the Seneca white deer

**Bill ID:** A2953
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeff Gallahan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes distinctive license plates for supporters of the conservation of the Seneca white deer; establishes the New York state "preserve the Seneca white deer fund".

 PROVIDED, HOWEVER, THAT AN ADDITIONAL ANNUAL SERVICE CHARGE  OF  TWENTY-
 FIVE DOLLARS SHALL BE CHARGED FOR SUCH PLATE.

 received  prior  to  plate  design, production and issuance at least two
 hundred orders for such distinctive plate together with  the  additional

  § 3. The state finance law is amended by adding a new section  97-aaaa

  §  97-AAAA.  THE NEW YORK STATE "PRESERVE THE SENECA WHITE DEER FUND".
