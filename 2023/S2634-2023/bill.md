# Provides a tax credit to certain landowners who allow snowmobile access on such land

**Bill ID:** S2634
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides a tax credit to certain landowners who allow snowmobile access on such land.
