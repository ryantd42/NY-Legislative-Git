# Requires hospitals and medical facilities to notify nursing homes or assisted living facilities of a positive COVID-19 test result for a resident of such nursing home or assisted living facility

**Bill ID:** A1105
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jonathan Jacobson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1105 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1105)

## Summary

Requires hospitals and medical facilities to notify nursing homes or assisted living facilities upon receipt of a positive COVID-19 test result for a resident of such nursing home or assisted living facility; provides penalties for violations.
