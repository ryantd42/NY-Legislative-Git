# Relates to the New York state vaccination awareness campaign

**Bill ID:** A2130
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the New York state vaccination awareness campaign and directs the department of health to establish and contract with a consultant to promote and maintain the New York state vaccination awareness campaign; establishes the gift for the New York state vaccination awareness campaign and allows a check-off space on the personal income tax return to enable a taxpayer to make such contribution; makes related provisions.
