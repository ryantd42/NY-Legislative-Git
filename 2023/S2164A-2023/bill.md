# Authorizes the commission on ethics and lobbying in government to provide a training course concerning anti-sexual harassment training for lobbyists and requiring lobbyists to complete the course annually

**Bill ID:** S2164A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Liu
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the commission on ethics and lobbying in government to provide a training course concerning anti-sexual harassment training for lobbyists; requires lobbyists to complete the course annually.
