# Relates to including video lottery gaming in certain gambling offenses

**Bill ID:** S7453
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Includes video lottery gaming in certain gambling offenses.

 § 225.55 Gaming fraud in the second degree.
