# Authorizes the awarding of punitive damages to persons aggrieved by sexual harassment

**Bill ID:** A1788
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the awarding of punitive damages to persons aggrieved by sexual harassment.
