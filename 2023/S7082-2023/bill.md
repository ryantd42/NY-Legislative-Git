# Provides free use of state parks for members or citizens of state or federally recognized Native American nations

**Bill ID:** S7082
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Provides free use of the public campsites, parks and other public places of recreation in this state for members or citizens of state or federally recognized Native American nations.
