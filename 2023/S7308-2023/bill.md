# Makes an appropriation to the Island Park School District to offset a loss of tax revenue

**Bill ID:** S7308
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Makes an appropriation of seven million dollars over seven years to the Island Park School District for the purpose of stabilizing the Island Park School District and offsetting a loss of tax revenue.

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. The sum of seven million dollars ($7,000,000), or  so  much

 School District out of any moneys in the state treasury in  the  general
 fund to the credit of the local assistance account, not otherwise appro-
 priated,  and  one  million  dollars  of which shall be made immediately

 July  first for six subsequent years, for the purpose of stabilizing the
 Island Park School District and offsetting a loss of tax  revenue.  Such
 moneys  shall  be payable on the audit and warrant of the comptroller to
 such school district as prescribed by law.
