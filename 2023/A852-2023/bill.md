# Authorizes licensed clinical social workers to provide services to injured employees under workers' compensation coverage

**Bill ID:** A852
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A852 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A852)

## Summary

Authorizes licensed clinical social workers to provide services to injured employees under workers' compensation coverage; establishes special authorization shall be provided by the clinical social work practice committee.
