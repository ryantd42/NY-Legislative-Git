# Relates to vehicles in the borough of Manhattan

**Bill ID:** A410
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A410 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A410)

## Summary

Eliminates the Manhattan resident parking tax exemption; increases the car registration fee for cars registered to residents of Manhattan.
