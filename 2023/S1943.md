# Relates to preventing temporary questioning of person solely because such person is wearing a mask during a state emergency for an epidemic or pandemic

**Bill ID:** S1943
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1943 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1943)

## Summary

Prevents temporary questioning of person solely because such person is wearing a mask during a state emergency for an epidemic or pandemic.
