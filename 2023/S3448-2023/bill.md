# Amends certain definitions relating to tobacco products and changes the minimum pack sizes for tobacco products and the tax amount for tobacco products

**Bill ID:** S3448
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Amends certain definitions relating to tobacco products; changes the minimum pack sizes for tobacco products and the tax amount for tobacco products.
