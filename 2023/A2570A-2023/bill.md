# Requires a system for notifying customers of bed bug infestations on MTA subways, trains and buses, within 24 hours of discovering such infestation

**Bill ID:** A2570A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Colton
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires a system for notifying customers of bed bug infestations on MTA subways, trains and buses, within 24 hours of discovering such infestation.
