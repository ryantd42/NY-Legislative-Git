# Providing for the election of Marina B. Pugliese to the office of Official Stenographer of the Assembly

**Bill ID:** E3
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Philip Ramos
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 3
 
BY: M. of A. Ramos
 
  PROVIDING  for  the election of Marina B. Pugliese
  to  the  office  of  Official  Stenographer  of  the
  Assembly
 
  RESOLVED,  That  Marina  B.  Pugliese  be  and she hereby is elected
Official Stenographer of the Assembly for the years 2023 and 2024.
