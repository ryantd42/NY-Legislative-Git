# Creates the anti-SLAPP act

**Bill ID:** S6784
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Creates the anti-SLAPP act; provides that discovery shall be suspended pending a decision on the motion.
