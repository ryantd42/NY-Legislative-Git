# Designates the second Monday in July each year as a public holiday, to be known as Abolition Commemoration day

**Bill ID:** A3732
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Designates the second Monday in July each year as a public holiday, to be known as Abolition Commemoration Day.
