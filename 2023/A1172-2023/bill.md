# Requires landlords to provide fire extinguishers in all rented apartments

**Bill ID:** A1172
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1172 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1172)

## Summary

Requires landlords to provide fire extinguishers in all rented apartments; authorizes the promulgation of standards related thereto.
