# Enacts "Cesar's law" to require the retaking of parolees who abscond from the supervision of the state board of parole

**Bill ID:** S4318
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Enacts "Cesar's law" to require the retaking of parolees who abscond from the supervision of the state board of parole.
