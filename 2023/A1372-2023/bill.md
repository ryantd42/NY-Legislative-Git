# Relates to compensation for injury or death of a companion animal

**Bill ID:** A1372
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1372 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1372)

## Summary

Provides for compensation of an owner for the unjustifiable injury or death of a companion animal.
