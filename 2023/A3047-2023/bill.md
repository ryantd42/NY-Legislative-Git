# Relates to harness racing

**Bill ID:** A3047
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to harness racing; limits racing at harness tracks to only registered standardbred horses.
