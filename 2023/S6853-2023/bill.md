# Provides that residential buildings shall be deemed eligible buildings for the green building tax credit

**Bill ID:** S6853
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides that residential buildings shall be deemed eligible buildings for the green building tax credit.
