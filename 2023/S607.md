# Relates to offenses involving thefts of identity; repealer

**Bill ID:** S607
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S607 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S607)

## Summary

Relates to offenses involving theft of identity; creates five tiers of crimes, based on the financial loss to the victim(s) and the number of people victimized.
