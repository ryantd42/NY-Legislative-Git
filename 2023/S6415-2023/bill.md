# Relates to the establishment of an escrow account in any court which holds a special proceeding to recover real property

**Bill ID:** S6415
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires any court which holds a special proceeding to recover real property to establish an escrow account where the tenant shall submit any owed rent into such account.
