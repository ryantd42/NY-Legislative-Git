# Relates to prohibiting denial of entry to a public place or denial of service solely on the basis of wearing a mask in public during an epidemic or a pandemic

**Bill ID:** S1934
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1934 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1934)

## Summary

Prohibits denial of entry to a public place or denial of service solely on the basis of wearing a mask in public during an epidemic or a pandemic.
