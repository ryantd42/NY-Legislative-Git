# Requires businesses that use bicycles with electric assist to require certain employees to watch an online safety course video

**Bill ID:** A7938
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alex Bores
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires businesses that use bicycles with electric assist to require certain employees to watch an online safety course video once per year.
