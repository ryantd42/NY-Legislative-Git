# Extends the authorization granted to the county of Franklin to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S4088AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the authorization granted to the county of Franklin to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes until November 30th, 2025.
