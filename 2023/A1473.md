# Establishes the abortion access fund and allows taxpayers to make gifts to the fund

**Bill ID:** A1473
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karines Reyes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1473 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1473)

## Summary

Establishes the abortion access fund; allows for individuals to designate a gift to the fund on their personal income tax returns.
