# Requires an updated photo to be taken in order to renew a driver's license

**Bill ID:** A2941
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires an updated photo be taken in order to renew a driver's license.
