# Extends the boundaries of the Woodmere fire district to include the village of Woodsburgh

**Bill ID:** A1274
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Eric Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1274 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1274)

## Summary

Extends the boundaries of the Woodmere fire district to include the village of Woodsburgh.
