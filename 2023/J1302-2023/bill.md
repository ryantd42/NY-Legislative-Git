# Mourning the death of Frank Borello, distinguished citizen and devoted member of his community

**Bill ID:** J1302
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1302
 
BY: Senator HARCKHAM
 
  MOURNING the death of Frank Borello, distinguished
  citizen and devoted member of his community
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to pay tribute to
citizens  of  the  State  of  New York whose lifework and civic endeavor
served to enhance the quality of life in their communities and the great
State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Frank Borello of Bronx, New York, died on May  3,  2023  at
the age of 84; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Born on July 4, 1938, Frank Borello was the son of Anthony
and Mary Borello; and
 
  WHEREAS, After valiantly serving in the United  States  Navy,  Frank
Borello  went  on  to  have a career as a foreman with the Department of
Sanitation in New York City until his retirement; and
 
  WHEREAS, Frank Borello distinguished himself in his  profession  and
by his sincere dedication and substantial contribution to the welfare of
his community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  An  individual  with  many  passions,  Frank Borello loved
anything outdoors, especially golf, fishing, and boating; and
 
  WHEREAS, Frank Borello was married to his wife, Ann, for a loving 44
years and is survived by one  brother  and  many  cousins,  nieces,  and
nephews; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Armed  with a humanistic spirit and imbued with a sense of
compassion, Frank Borello leaves behind a legacy which will long  endure
the  passage  of  time  and will remain as a comforting memory to all he
served and befriended; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
mourn  the  death  of  Frank  Borello, distinguished citizen and devoted
member of his community; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the family of Frank Borello.
