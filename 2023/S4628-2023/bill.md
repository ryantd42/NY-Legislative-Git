# Requires a public hearing prior to procurements valued over one hundred thousand dollars are made overseas by public authorities

**Bill ID:** S4628
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires a public hearing before procurements valued over one hundred thousand dollars are made overseas by public authorities.
