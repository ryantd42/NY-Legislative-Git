# Relates to free use of state parks, recreation facilities and historic sites by veterans

**Bill ID:** S7300
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides that veterans who are NY residents may use state parks, recreation facilities and historic sites without fees.
