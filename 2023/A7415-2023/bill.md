# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "SP4 George Harold Fry Memorial Highway"

**Bill ID:** A7415
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stephen Hawley
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the county of Genesee, as the "SP4 George Harold Fry Memorial Highway".
