# Creates the crime of falsely accusing someone of a crime

**Bill ID:** S4291
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Creates the crime of falsely accusing someone of a crime.
