# Requires employers to engage in a good faith cooperative dialogue with employees who request accommodation due to a religious practice or observance

**Bill ID:** A2003
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Charles Fall
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires employers to engage in a good faith cooperative dialogue with employees who request accommodation due to a religious practice or observance.
