# Prohibits insurers from selling policies of insurance which duplicate existing coverage relative to long term health care policies

**Bill ID:** S2742
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits insurers from selling policies of insurance which duplicate existing coverage relative to long term health care policies.
