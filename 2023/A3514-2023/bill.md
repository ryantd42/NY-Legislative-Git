# Clarifies the definition of floor area

**Bill ID:** A3514
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the definition of floor area and what is excluded in the measurement of floor area.
