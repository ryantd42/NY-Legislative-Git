# Includes offenses involving the possession, display or discharge of certain firearms as qualifying offenses for the purpose of allowing a principal to be eligible to be held on bail

**Bill ID:** A1503
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1503 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1503)

## Summary

Includes offenses involving the possession, display or discharge of a firearm, rifle, shotgun, machine-gun, or disguised gun as qualifying offenses for the purpose of allowing a principal to be eligible to be held on bail.
