# Relates to the establishment and priorities of the New York state council on hunger and food policy

**Bill ID:** A615
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A615 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A615)

## Summary

Clarifies the provisions relating to the establishment and priorities of the New York state council on hunger and food policy.
