# Relates to extending the additional one percent sales tax for Wayne county

**Bill ID:** A3645AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Manktelow
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the additional one percent sales and compensating use taxes in the county of Wayne.
