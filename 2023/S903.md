# Includes the right to strike as an employee right

**Bill ID:** S903
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jabari Brisport
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S903 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S903)

## Summary

Includes the right to strike as an employee right.
