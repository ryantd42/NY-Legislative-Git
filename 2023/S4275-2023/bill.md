# Permits prescription refills to be filled fifteen days prior to their refill date during a state disaster emergency

**Bill ID:** S4275
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Permits prescription refills to be filled fifteen days prior to their refill date upon the declaration of a state disaster emergency; excludes controlled substances.
