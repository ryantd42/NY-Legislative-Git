# Eliminates additional application fees and annual fees for special license plates issued to veterans of the armed forces of the United States

**Bill ID:** A4114
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Marianne Buttenschon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Eliminates additional application fees and annual fees for special license plates issued to veterans of the armed forces of the United States.
