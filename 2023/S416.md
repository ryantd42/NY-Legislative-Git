# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the Trooper James M. MacLarnon Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** S416
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S416 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S416)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in Nassau county as the "Trooper James M. MacLarnon Memorial Bridge".
