# Authorizes localities to remove citizenship requirements for police and firefighters in their jurisdictions

**Bill ID:** A6462
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monique Chandler-Waterman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes localities to remove citizenship requirements for police and firefighters in their jurisdictions.
