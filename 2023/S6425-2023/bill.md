# Relates to increasing fines for certain speed camera violations

**Bill ID:** S6425
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Imposes increasing fines for subsequent speed camera violations in the city of New York.
