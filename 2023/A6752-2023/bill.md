# Increases the unemployment insurance minimum benefit amount

**Bill ID:** A6752
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jen Lunsford
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases the unemployment insurance minimum benefit amount to $650 per week.
