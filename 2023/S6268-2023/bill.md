# Grants a real property assessment freeze and property tax freeze to persons sixty-five years of age or older

**Bill ID:** S6268
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Grants a real property assessment freeze and property tax freeze to persons sixty-five years of age or older.
