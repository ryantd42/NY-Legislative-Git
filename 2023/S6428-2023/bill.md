# Provides for insurance coverage of comprehensive annual medical examinations for firefighters

**Bill ID:** S6428
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Provides for insurance coverage of comprehensive annual medical examinations for firefighters due to their increased risk for cancer and cancer-related diseases.
