# Authorizes the state board of law examiners to procure quarters for such board in the county of Albany

**Bill ID:** S7518
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the state board of law examiners to procure proper and suitable quarters for such board in the county of Albany.
