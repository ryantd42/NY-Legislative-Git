# Requires certain health insurance policies to include coverage for the cost of certain infant and baby formulas

**Bill ID:** A1500
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1500 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1500)

## Summary

Requires certain health insurance policies to include coverage for the cost of certain infant and baby formulas.
