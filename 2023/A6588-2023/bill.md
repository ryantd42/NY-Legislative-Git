# Extends the authorization for imposition of additional sales tax in the county of Cortland

**Bill ID:** A6588
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the authorization for imposition of additional sales tax in the county of Cortland until November 30, 2025.
