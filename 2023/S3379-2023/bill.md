# Requires health insurers to provide coverage for long term medical care for Lyme disease and other tick borne related pathogens

**Bill ID:** S3379
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Requires health insurers to provide coverage for long term medical care for Lyme disease and other tick borne related pathogens.
