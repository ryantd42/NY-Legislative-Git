# Commending Alan Dagistino upon the occasion of being named the 2022 Veteran of the Year by the Rockland County Veterans Coordinating Council

**Bill ID:** K682
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 682
 
BY: M. of A. Zebrowski
 
  COMMENDING  Alan  Dagistino  upon  the occasion of
  being named the 2022 Veteran  of  the  Year  by  the
  Rockland County Veterans Coordinating Council
 
  WHEREAS,  Members  of the Armed Services from the State of New York,
who have served so  valiantly  and  honorably  in  wars  in  which  this
country's  freedom was at stake, as well as in the preservation of peace
in peace time, deserve a special salute from this Legislative Body; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the intent of this Legislative Body to commend  those
who  have  served in times of active conflict, as well as those who have
served in times of peace; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
commend Alan Dagistino upon the occasion of being named the 2022 Veteran
of the Year by the Rockland County Veterans Coordinating Council; and
 
  WHEREAS, This auspicious occasion was celebrated at  a  Ceremony  on
November  6,  2022,  in  front  of the Rockland County Courthouse in New
City, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Alan Dagistino proudly served his country as  a  member  of
the United States Armed Forces during the Vietnam War; and
 
  WHEREAS, Alan Dagistino is a life member of the local chapter of the
Vietnam  Veterans  of  America  333,  and has held many positions in the
group over the years, including President; and
 
  WHEREAS, Alan Dagistino is a shining example of the impact a  person
can  have  when  they  selflessly  serve their country and then go on to
serve their community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Our Nation's veterans deserve to be recognized,  commended,
and thanked by the people of the State of New York for their service and
for  their  dedication  to  their  communities,  their  State, and their
Nation; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend Alan Dagistino upon the occasion of being named the 2023 Veteran
of the Year by the Rockland County Veterans Coordinating Council; and be
it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Alan Dagistino.
