# Relates to the amount of damages recoverable in a cause of action for unlawful interference with protected rights

**Bill ID:** A969
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Chris Burdick
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A969 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A969)

## Summary

Relates to the amount of damages recoverable in a cause of action for unlawful interference with protected rights which is up to three times the amount of compensatory damages.
