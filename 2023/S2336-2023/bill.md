# Relates to licensing tenant screening bureaus

**Bill ID:** S2336
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to licensing tenant screening bureaus; provides that no person shall act as a tenant screening bureau without first obtaining a license from the department of state.
