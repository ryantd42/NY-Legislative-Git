# Details the procedure that certain school districts must follow when dealing with disciplinary actions for bus drivers and bus drivers' assistants

**Bill ID:** S7415
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Shelley Mayer
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Details the procedure that certain school districts must follow when dealing with disciplinary actions for bus drivers and bus drivers' assistants.
