# Extends the authorization of the county of Onondaga to impose an additional rate of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A3075AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Onondaga to impose an additional rate of sales and compensating use taxes from November 30, 2023 until November 30, 2025.
