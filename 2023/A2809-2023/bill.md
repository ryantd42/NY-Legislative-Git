# Relates to authorizing a personal income tax deduction for tolls, fees and other related commuting expenses paid by a taxpayer in the course of commuting to and from his or her place of employment

**Bill ID:** A2809
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to authorizing a personal income tax deduction for tolls, fees and other related commuting expenses paid by a taxpayer in the course of commuting to and from his or her place of employment.

 AN ACT to amend the tax law,  in  relation  to  authorizing  a  personal
  income  tax  deduction  for  tolls,  fees  and other related commuting
  expenses paid by a taxpayer in the course of commuting to and from his

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1. Subsection (c) of section 612 of the tax law is amended by
 adding a new paragraph 47 to read as follows:
  (47) (A) THE AMOUNT PAID OR INCURRED DURING THE TAXABLE YEAR  FOR  ANY
 TOLLS,  FEES AND/OR ANY OTHER RELATED COMMUTING EXPENSES IMPOSED FOR THE
 USE OF A PUBLIC HIGHWAY OR BRIDGE OR PUBLIC  TRANSPORTATION  WITHIN  THE
 STATE,  OR  TOLLS,  FEES  AND/OR  ANY  OTHER  RELATED COMMUTING EXPENSES
 IMPOSED BY A PUBLIC AUTHORITY, ESTABLISHED BY THE LAWS  OF  THIS  STATE,
 FOR  THE USE OF A PUBLIC HIGHWAY OR BRIDGE OR PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION; AND
 SUCH TOLLS, FEES AND/OR OTHER RELATED COMMUTING EXPENSES WERE PAID WHILE
 A TAXPAYER WAS IN THE COURSE OF COMMUTING TO AND FROM HIS OR  HER  PLACE
 OF  EMPLOYMENT  OR  FOR  THE PURPOSE OF COMMUTING TO AND FROM HIS OR HER
 PLACE OF EMPLOYMENT.
  (B) FOR THE PURPOSES OF THIS PARAGRAPH, "RELATED  COMMUTING  EXPENSES"
 SHALL  INCLUDE  BUT  NOT  BE  LIMITED  TO  THE COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH THE
 PURCHASE AND MAINTENANCE OF METROCARDS, COMMUTER RAILROAD PASSES, MONTH-
 LY BUS PASSES AND EZ PASSES.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately and shall apply to taxable
