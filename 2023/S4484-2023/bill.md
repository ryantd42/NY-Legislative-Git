# Adds certain offenses committed by a family member to the list of crimes qualifying for bail

**Bill ID:** S4484
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Adds certain offenses committed by a family member to the list of crimes qualifying for bail.
