# Requires labor peace agreements in contracts for certain retail or distribution projects involving public agencies

**Bill ID:** S6894
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires labor peace agreements as a precondition for contracts for certain retail or distribution projects in which a public agency has a substantial proprietary interest.

 LEAST TEN EMPLOYEES, OR AT LEAST ONE DISTRIBUTION CENTER WHICH WILL HAVE
