# Provides that persons arrested for domestic violence forfeit bail when violating an order of protection

**Bill ID:** S2549
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides that persons arrested for domestic violence forfeit bail when violating an order of protection.
