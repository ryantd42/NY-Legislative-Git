# Requires the provision of additional insurance coverage for pre-term labor hospitalizations, monitoring and counseling

**Bill ID:** A1096
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1096 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1096)

## Summary

Requires insurance policies to provide coverage for pre-term labor hospitalizations, home visits to monitor pre-term labor patients and counseling.
