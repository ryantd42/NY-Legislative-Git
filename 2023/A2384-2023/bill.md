 AN  ACT to amend part EEE of chapter 59 of the laws of 2018, relating to
  an online application  system  for  taxpayers  to  submit  claims  for
  reimbursements  of  certain  payments, in relation to state reimburse-
  ments for interest on federal tax liability resulting from real  prop-

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Section 1 of part EEE of chapter 59 of the  laws  of  2018,
 relating  to an online application system for taxpayers to submit claims
 for reimbursements of certain payments, is amended to read as follows:
  Section 1. The tax department shall be required to set  up  an  online
 application  system for taxpayers to submit claims for reimbursements of
 payments of interest on fixed and final determinations of  underpayments
 of federal tax liability for the 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2021 tax year that
 arise  from the taxpayers' reliance on amendments to the tax law OR REAL
 PROPERTY TAX LAW enacted in the year 2018.  In  order  to  receive  such
 reimbursement, taxpayers shall be required to submit their reimbursement
 claims  to  the  department  of  taxation  and finance within 60 days of
 making their payments of interest to the internal revenue service.
