# Establishes the failure to pass act restricting driving in a passing lane without overtaking or passing a vehicle

**Bill ID:** S4620
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the failure to pass act restricting driving in a passing lane without overtaking or passing a vehicle and sets penalties for violations.
