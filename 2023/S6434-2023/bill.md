# Authorizes the county of Clinton, to employ retired former members of the division of state police as special patrol officers

**Bill ID:** S6434
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Clinton to employ retired former members of the division of state police as special patrol officers.
