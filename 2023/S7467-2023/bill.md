# Relates to licensing restrictions for manufacturers and wholesalers of alcoholic beverages on licensees who sell at retail and for on-premises consumption

**Bill ID:** S7467
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Exempts certain pieces of property from licensing restrictions for manufacturers and wholesalers of alcoholic beverages on licensees who sell at retail and for on-premises consumption.
