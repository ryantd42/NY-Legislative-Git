# Requires certain for-hire vehicles to install and maintain an interior rear-facing video camera

**Bill ID:** A2806B
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires certain for-hire vehicles to install and maintain an interior rear-facing video camera.
