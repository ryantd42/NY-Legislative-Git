# Requires food allergy awareness in food service establishments and online food ordering services

**Bill ID:** S819
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S819 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S819)

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of public health to post certain notices regarding food allergy awareness in the twelve most common non-English languages spoken by limited-English proficient individuals in the state.
