# Includes the right to strike as an employee right

**Bill ID:** A906
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Emily Gallagher
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A906 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A906)

## Summary

Includes the right to strike as an employee right.
