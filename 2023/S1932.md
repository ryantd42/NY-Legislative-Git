# Relates to requirements of motor vehicles

**Bill ID:** S1932
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1932 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1932)

## Summary

Requires certain motor vehicles to have rear passenger detection warning systems.
