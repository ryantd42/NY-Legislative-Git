# Establishes the veterans service organizations fund

**Bill ID:** A3160
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the congressional chartered veterans service organizations fund; provides for gifts made to such fund on an individual's personal income tax return.
