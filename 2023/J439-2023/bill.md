# Commending Resilient Young Minds, Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its Annual I'm So Resilient Gala on February 25, 2023

**Bill ID:** J439
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 439
 
BY: Senator PARKER
 
  COMMENDING  Resilient  Young Minds, Inc. upon the
  occasion of hosting its Annual I'm So Resilient Gala
  on February 25, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  commend  those
illustrious organizations of the State of New York who have made stellar
efforts on behalf of young people and their futures; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  Resilient  Young  Minds,  Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its
Annual I'm So Resilient Gala, A Purple Carpet Affair, to  be  celebrated
on Saturday, February 25, 2023, at Phaze 2 in Brooklyn, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Resilient  Young  Minds,  Inc.  (RYM)  is  a  non-profit
organization in Brooklyn, New York, which aims to  teach  at-risk  youth
ages  10-17,  their  parents,  and young adults how to fight trauma with
resilience on personal, professional, and social levels; and
 
  WHEREAS, Offering customized resilience retreats or camps, Resilient
Young Minds, Inc. exposes participants to a new environment and provides
access to long term mentorship or coaching; and
 
  WHEREAS, The funds raised at the Annual Gala directly supports RYM's
Youth Summer Resilience Sleepaway camp; and
 
  WHEREAS, Awards are given based on RYM's three levels of resilience:
personal, professional, and social; additionally, the  participants  are
celebrated from the previous year who have completed at least one of the
camps  or  volunteered,  and  they are honored for the amazing work they
have done to identify trauma  and  apply  resilience  coping  skills  to
access their healing; and
 
  WHEREAS,  There  will  be  an "I'm So Resilient Leader Impact Award"
presented to a community leader who promotes  or  advocates  for  mental
health  in  their  communities,  and  also  a career scholarship will be
provided to high school seniors or a parent who  would  like  to  create
generational wealth for their family through starting a business; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Resilience  Camp,  formally  known  as YES Academy, is the
primary youth program of RYM, and  the  program  selects  10-30  at-risk
youth, ages 10-17, who have experienced a traumatic event from a pool of
applicants; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  one-week  sleepaway  camp,  Resilience  Camp, teaches
social-emotional  learning,  resilience,  and  self-esteem  building
workshops  that  challenge  participants  to  identify  their trauma, in
healthy ways, and access healing; and
 
  WHEREAS, The purpose of Resilience Camp is to remove  at-risk  youth
from  their  usual environment into one that is peaceful, safe, and will
empower youth to face their internal conflicts,  begin  the  process  to
 
overcome  them,  and  convert  their  trauma into a motivational tool to
achieve their professional goals; and
 
  WHEREAS,  All  youth  participants  in  the  sleepaway  camp will be
provided a mentor to serve as their personal resilience  coach  for  the
duration  of the experience, and many mentors stay in contact with their
mentee after the camp is over to remain a positive  influence  on  them;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  This vital organization is truly committed to igniting the
potential of its youth,  positioning  them  for  long  term  success  as
leaders in the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success  of  Resilient Young Minds, Inc. is in direct
correlation to the efforts of its dedicated staff, whose involvement is,
and always has been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment,  an
unbridled  enthusiasm,  and  an uncompromising standard of excellence in
all endeavors on behalf of the organization and the community it serves;
and
 
  WHEREAS, This auspicious occasion presents an opportunity  for  this
Legislative  Body to recognize and pay tribute to Resilient Young Minds,
Inc.; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals and organizations of such noble aims and accomplishments are
brought  to  our  attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by
all the citizens of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commend  Resilient  Young  Minds,  Inc. upon the occasion of hosting its
Annual I'm So Resilient Gala, A Purple Carpet Affair,  on  February  25,
2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Resilient Young Minds, Inc.
