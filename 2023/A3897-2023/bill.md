# Relates to the child and dependent care credit

**Bill ID:** A3897
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the child and dependent care credit; increases the percentage of the federal credit.
