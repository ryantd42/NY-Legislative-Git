# Requires the issuance of certificates of occupancy for rental property damaged by fire

**Bill ID:** S2072
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2072 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2072)

## Summary

Requires the issuance of certificates of occupancy for rental property damaged by fire.
