# Increases penalties for criminal use of a firearm in the first degree when committing a drug related felony offense

**Bill ID:** A4225
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John K. Mikulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases penalties for criminal use of a firearm in the first degree when committing a drug related felony offense.
