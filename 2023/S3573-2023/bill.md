# Relates to the compulsory age for full time instruction from six to five years of age and certain exceptions for minor children

**Bill ID:** S3573
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to the compulsory age for full time instruction from six to five years of age and certain exceptions for minor children.
