# Provides lifetime, premium-free insurance through the New York state of health marketplace to a person who donates a kidney during the course of his or her lifetime

**Bill ID:** A3462
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides lifetime, premium-free insurance through the New York state of health marketplace to a person who donates a kidney during the course of his or her lifetime; establishes the kidney donor insurance fund.
