# Relates to photo speed violation monitoring systems in school speed zones in the city of New York

**Bill ID:** A3401
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Charles Fall
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Reduces the radial distance from school buildings for such school speed zones.
