# Requires individuals and corporations to obtain a sound permit from a municipality in certain situations

**Bill ID:** S3099
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires individuals and corporations to obtain a sound permit from a municipality in certain situations.
