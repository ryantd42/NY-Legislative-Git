# Creates the federal contract workers assistance program

**Bill ID:** S1704
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1704 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1704)

## Summary

Creates the federal contract workers assistance program to permit the state to provide loans to certain small businesses affected by the federal government shutdown.
