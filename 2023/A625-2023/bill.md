# Exempts hot tubs or spas with safety covers from certain standards of the New York state uniform fire prevention and building code

**Bill ID:** A625
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A625 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A625)

## Summary

Exempts hot tubs or spas with safety covers from certain standards of the New York state uniform fire prevention and building code.
