# Relates to housing navigation services for those with intellectual and developmental disabilities

**Bill ID:** S3196
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a curriculum and task force to provide housing navigation services for those with intellectual and developmental disabilities.
