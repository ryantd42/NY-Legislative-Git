# Prohibits the use of a facial recognition system by a landlord on any residential premises

**Bill ID:** S2478
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the use of a facial recognition system by a landlord on any residential premises.
