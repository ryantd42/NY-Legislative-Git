# Relates to the transfer of funds from a candidate's political committee for a federal office to a candidate's political committee for a state, county or local office

**Bill ID:** A192
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A192 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A192)

## Summary

Places restrictions on the transfer of funds from a candidate's political committee for a federal office to a candidate's political committee for a state, county or local office.
