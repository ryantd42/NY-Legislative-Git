# Provides for health insurance coverage for nutritional care provided through licensed nutritionists

**Bill ID:** S6371
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for health insurance coverage for nutritional care provided through licensed nutritionists.
