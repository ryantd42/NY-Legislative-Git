# Provides access to medication abortion prescription drugs at SUNY and CUNY campuses

**Bill ID:** S1213BB
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S1213B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1213B)

## Summary

Provides access to medication abortion prescription drugs at SUNY and CUNY campuses by employing or contracting with individuals authorized to prescribe such drugs, or by providing referrals.
