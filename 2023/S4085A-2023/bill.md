# Designates a portion of the state highway system in the town of Lewisboro as the "John Jay High School Veterans Memorial Highway"

**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the town of Lewisboro as the "John Jay High School Veterans Memorial Highway".
