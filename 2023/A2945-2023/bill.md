# Enacts the "American made flag act" to require that American flags purchased for use by the state government be made in the United States

**Bill ID:** A2945
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mike Reilly
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Enacts the "American made flag act" to require that American flags purchased for use by the state government be made in the United States.
