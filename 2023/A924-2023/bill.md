# Relates to allowing for case workers to apply for education and training vouchers for children in foster care

**Bill ID:** A924
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A924 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A924)

## Summary

Allows for case workers to apply for education and training vouchers for children in foster care.
