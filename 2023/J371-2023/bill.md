# Congratulating Charlee Denzler upon the occasion of receiving the distinguished Girl Scout Gold Award, the most prestigious of Scouting honors

**Bill ID:** J371
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 371
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  CONGRATULATING  Charlee  Denzler upon the occasion
  of  receiving  the  distinguished  Girl  Scout  Gold
  Award, the most prestigious of Scouting honors
 
  WHEREAS,  Charlee  Denzler,  through  her  diligence  and  rigorous
efforts, has achieved Scouting's highest and most prestigious honor, the
Girl Scout  Gold  Award;  the  Willistons,  Albertson,  Roslyn,  Mineola
(W.A.R.M.)  Girl  Scout  Association  Award  Ceremony  will  be  held on
Tuesday, February 28, 2023, at Chaminade High School's Activity Athletic
Center in Mineola, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Charlee Denzler is a member of Girl Scouts of America Troop
1805; and
 
  WHEREAS, America has achieved her greatest heights of glory  through
the  dedication,  determination  and tireless efforts of the outstanding
citizens of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our
youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain her glory; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Girl Scouts of America and its members are dedicated to
the development of character and leadership in the youth of this Nation;
and
 
  WHEREAS, Since 1916, Senior Girl Scouts have  sought  to  cap  their
years  in  Girl  Scouting  with  the prized Girl Scout Gold Award, which
challenges girls to change the world; and
 
  WHEREAS, In order to earn the Girl Scout Gold  Award,  a  girl  must
complete  a  rigorous  set  of  service,  leadership  development,  and
skill-building tasks leading  up  to  the  final  and  most  challenging
requirement, the design and completion of a Take Action Project; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  project must be an issue of interest to the girl and
her community, employ team-based  solutions,  and  educate  and  inspire
others;  for  her  project  entitled  Charlee's  Closet, Charlee Denzler
created a closet at Hour Children with  personal  hygiene  products  for
women  and  children,  and educated them on their proper use, as well as
the importance of health and self-esteem; and
 
  WHEREAS, This outstanding young woman has been involved in  Scouting
for years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has attained all of the
necessary achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Charlee Denzler is a credit to her family, her school, her
community, her State and her Nation, and no doubt  her  future  will  be
filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations, in
recognition of the significance  of  this  meritorious  achievement,  to
congratulate  and  honor  Charlee  Denzler  upon  the  occasion  of  her
designation as recipient of the Girl Scout Gold Award; and be it further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Charlee Denzler.
