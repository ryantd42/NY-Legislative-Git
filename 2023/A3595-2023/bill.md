# Prohibits political candidates from being nominated by more than one party for any public office

**Bill ID:** A3595
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits political candidates from being nominated by more than one party for any public office.
