# Exempts community colleges from the imposition of the metropolitan commuter transportation mobility tax

**Bill ID:** A1085
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A1085 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1085)

## Summary

Exempts community colleges from the imposition of the metropolitan commuter transportation mobility tax.
