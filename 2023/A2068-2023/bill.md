# Relates to the establishment of regional arts and cultural affairs grants

**Bill ID:** A2068
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes regional arts, cultural affairs, and parks grants for ten regions throughout the state for capital projects.
