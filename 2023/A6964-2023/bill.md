# Establishes distinctive "Alzheimer's Awareness" license plates and provides that the funding for such be deposited in the Alzheimer's disease assistance fund

**Bill ID:** A6964
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to establishing distinctive "Alzheimer's Awareness" license plates; provides that the funding for such be deposited in the "Alzheimer's disease assistance fund".
