# Requires the termination of any police officer or peace officer who uses physical force which is not justifiable or which is not part of procedure

**Bill ID:** A739
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A739 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A739)

## Summary

Requires the termination of any police officer or peace officer who uses physical force which is not justifiable or which is not part of procedure regardless of if harm is done any person or if any complaint is filed against such police officer or peace officer.
