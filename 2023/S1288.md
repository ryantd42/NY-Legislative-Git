# Requires the department of health to provide at least one site in each county of the state which offers COVID-19 vaccinations

**Bill ID:** S1288
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1288 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1288)

## Summary

Requires the department of health to provide at least one site in each county of the state which offers COVID-19 vaccinations and to establish an enrollment portal which shall enable residents to be notified once they become eligible for the vaccine.
