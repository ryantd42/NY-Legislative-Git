# Relates to requiring additional licensed pharmacists to be on staff during any period in which more than two hundred prescriptions are dispensed

**Bill ID:** S515
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S515 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S515)

## Summary

Requires at least two licensed pharmacists to be on staff in a pharmacy during any period in which more than two hundred prescriptions are dispensed.
