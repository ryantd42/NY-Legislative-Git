# Honoring Trooper Eric J. Abruzese upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the Superintendent's Commendation Award by the New York State Police on May 17, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1061
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Rolison
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1061
 
BY: Senator ROLISON
 
  HONORING  Trooper  Eric  J.  Abruzese  upon  the
  occasion of his  designation  as  recipient  of  the
  Superintendent's  Commendation Award by the New York
  State Police on May 17, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is incumbent upon the people of the State of New York to
recognize  and  acknowledge  those  within  our  midst  who  have  made
significant contributions to the quality of life therein; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to honor Trooper Eric
J.  Abruzese  upon the occasion of his designation as recipient of the
Superintendent's Commendation Award by the New York State Police  to  be
celebrated  at  its 47th Annual Superintendent's Commendation and Awards
Day Ceremony on Wednesday, May 17, 2023, at the State Police Academy  in
Albany, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, This prestigious award was established to honor outstanding
individuals,  dedicated  to  their  service  to  law  enforcement, whose
valiant efforts protect the people of New York State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Trooper Eric J. Abruzese acted bravely in the line of  duty
to  rescue  three small children from a dangerous barricaded man who was
holding them hostage and who had threatened to kill them;  his  decisive
and  life-saving  actions  exemplify  the  finest traditions of New York
State Police; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed  is  the  impressive  dedication  shown  by  an
individual  for the benefit of others which Trooper Eric J. Abruzese has
displayed throughout his life; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  that  those  who
enhance  the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown a
long and sustained commitment to excellence certainly  have  earned  the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Trooper Eric J. Abruzese upon the occasion of his  designation  as
recipient  of  the  Superintendent's  Commendation Award by the New York
State Police on May 17, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Trooper Eric J. Abruzese.
