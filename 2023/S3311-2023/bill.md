# Relates to tax exemptions for properties in violation of zoning laws

**Bill ID:** S3311
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits tax exemptions for properties in violation of zoning laws.
