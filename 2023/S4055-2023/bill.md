# Allows certificates of marriage from foreign governments and religious institutions as acceptable documentation to change a name on a driver's license or non-driver identification card

**Bill ID:** S4055
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Allows valid certificates of marriage from foreign governments and religious institutions as acceptable documentation to change a name on a driver's license, learner's permit, or identification card.
