# Prohibits sex offenders from operating automobiles participating in mobile application based transportation networks or being employed by such transportation networks

**Bill ID:** S4660
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza

## Summary

Prohibits sex offenders from operating automobiles participating in mobile application based transportation networks or being employed by such transportation networks.
