# Relates to the election and seating of Stacey Pheffer Amato as a Member of the Assembly from the 23rd Assembly District

**Bill ID:** E9
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carl Heastie
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E9 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/E9)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 9
 
BY: M. of A. Heastie
 
  In  relation  to  the  election and seating of
  Stacey Pheffer Amato as a  Member  of  the  Assembly
  from the 23rd Assembly District
 
  WHEREAS,  There  is  a  vacancy  existing  in the membership of this
House, which was filled at the election held on November 8, 2022; and
 
  WHEREAS, The final official  canvass  of  the  votes  cast  at  said
election and certification thereof of the person elected thereat, cannot
be made at this time; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Board of Elections of the City of New York has advised
that the votes cast for the Office of Member of  Assembly  in  the  23rd
Assembly  District  in  the  County  of Queens shown by its canvass were
respectively as follows: Stacey Pheffer Amato,  Democratic  and  We  the
People,  sixteen  thousand one hundred and eighty-five; Thomas Sullivan,
Republican and Conservative, sixteen thousand one hundred  and  seventy;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  appears  that  the  results  of the votes cast in said
Assembly District cannot be affected by the final  official  canvass  of
such voters; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Stacey Pheffer Amato has duly taken an oath of office as a
Member of Assembly; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That Stacey Pheffer Amato, Democratic and We  the  People,
is  duly  elected  and  qualified  as  a  Member of the Assembly for her
respective district and that she be seated in this House as such Member.
