# Relates to expenses incurred by local government agencies and/or not-for-profit service providers or their employees providing mental health, addiction treatment services, and support programs

**Bill ID:** R1400
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1400
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R2867  of  2022
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  a  certain  appropriation for the
  2022-2023  state  fiscal  year  for  services  and
  expenses  or  reimbursement  of expenses incurred by
  local  government  agencies  and/or  not-for-profit
  service  providers  or  their  employees  providing
  mental health,  addiction  treatment  services,  and
  support programs as required by a plan setting forth
  an  itemized  list of grantees with the amount to be
  received by each, or the methodology for  allocating
  such  appropriation.  Such  plan shall be subject to
  the approval  of  the  temporary  president  of  the
  senate and the director of the budget and thereafter
  shall  be  included  in a resolution calling for the
  expenditure of such monies,  which  resolution  must
  be  approved  by  a  majority  vote of all members
  elected to the senate upon a roll call vote
 
  RESOLVED, That pursuant to and as required by  money  appropriated
in section 1 of chapter 53 of the laws of 2022 which enacts the aid to
localities,  local  assistance  account, for grants for services and
expenses or reimbursement of expenses  incurred  by  local  government
agencies  and/or  not-for-profit  service providers or their employees
providing mental health,  addiction  treatment  services  and  support
programs.  Such funds shall be apportioned pursuant to a plan setting
forth an itemized list of grantees with the amount to be  received  by
each,  or the methodology for allocating such appropriation. Such plan
shall be subject to the approval of the  temporary  president  of  the
senate  and  the  director of the  budget  and  thereafter shall be
included in a resolution calling for the expenditure of  such  monies,
which  resolution  must  be approved by a majority vote of all members
elected to the senate upon a roll call vote, in  accordance  with  the
