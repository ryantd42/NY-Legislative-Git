# Directs the commissioner of housing and community renewal to require certain housing quality standards inspections of Section 8 housing

**Bill ID:** A1481
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela J. Hunter
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1481 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1481)

## Summary

Directs the commissioner of housing and community renewal to require housing quality standards inspections of Section 8 housing when a tenant moves out or vacates.
