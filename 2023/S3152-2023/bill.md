# Relates to criminal possession and criminal sale of a controlled substance

**Bill ID:** S3152
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides that possession or sale of a stimulant weighing 15 grams or more shall be punishable as a class A-I felony.
