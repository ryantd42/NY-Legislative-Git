# Relates to removing the local share requirements associated with increasing the age of juvenile jurisdiction

**Bill ID:** S784
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S784 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S784)

## Summary

Removes the local share requirements associated with increasing the age of juvenile jurisdiction.
