# Allows certain defenses to parking violations despite entering of a default judgment

**Bill ID:** S4194
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Allows certain defenses to parking violations despite entering of a default judgment.
