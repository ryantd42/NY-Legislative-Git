# Includes urban farming within farm operations for purposes of agricultural districts

**Bill ID:** A3948
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes urban farming within farm operations for purposes of agricultural districts.
