# Honoring Connie Wheelock upon the occasion of her retirement after 46 years of distinguished service to Oneida County, New York

**Bill ID:** J1282
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1282
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  HONORING  Connie Wheelock upon the occasion of her
  retirement after 46 years of  distinguished  service
  to Oneida County, New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body to acknowledge
those individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve  to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body  is  justly  proud  to
honor Connie Wheelock upon the occasion of her retirement after 46 years
of distinguished service to Oneida County, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  the past 46 years, Connie Wheelock rendered faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to Oneida County; and
 
  WHEREAS, Connie Wheelock began her career  in  February  1977  as  a
License  Examiner  at  the  Department  of  Motor  Vehicles;  she  began
employment with the Department of Social Services in December  of  1986,
and was employed with the department for more than 30 years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  She  provided  exemplary  service  to  the  area  of Staff
Development and has proven to be an asset to both DSS Administration and
the Office of the County Executive in her role as Senior  Administrative
Assistant; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout her time with the Department of Motor Vehicles,
the Department  of  Social  Services,  and  the  Office  of  the  County
Executive,  Connie Wheelock served with loyalty, honor, and distinction;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In her official acts, Connie Wheelock  was  governed  by  a
keen  sense  of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems;
and
 
  WHEREAS, During her tenure with the Office of the County  Executive,
Connie  Wheelock  earned  the  admiration,  esteem, and affection of her
colleagues; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor Connie Wheelock upon the occasion of her retirement after 46 years
of distinguished service to Oneida County; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Connie Wheelock.
