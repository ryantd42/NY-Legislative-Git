# Relates to prohibiting members of law enforcement from using tear gas

**Bill ID:** A3727
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits members of law enforcement from using tear gas at any time.
