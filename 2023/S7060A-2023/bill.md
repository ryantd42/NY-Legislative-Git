Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Fredrick "Rick" Delles Memorial Highway".

 HIGHWAY".
  § 2. Section 342-x of the highway law, as amended by chapter 49 of the

  §  342-x.  Portion of the state highway system to be designated as the

 be designated and known as the "Purple Heart Highway".
