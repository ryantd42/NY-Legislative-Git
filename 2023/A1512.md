# Relates to the powers of boards of education to temporarily lease necessary space

**Bill ID:** A1512
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kimberly Jean-Pierre
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1512 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1512)

## Summary

Expands the powers of boards of education to temporarily lease necessary space to include when facilities are under construction.
