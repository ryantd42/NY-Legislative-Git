# Relates to reimbursement of costs associated with capture and return rehabilitation of animals escaped from slaughterhouses

**Bill ID:** A386
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A386 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A386)

## Summary

Requires the owner of any place or establishment where animals or fowls are slaughtered or butchered for food to reimburse costs associated with capture and return or rescue and rehabilitation of escaped animals.
