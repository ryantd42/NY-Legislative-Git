# Creates an income driven repayment plan for private student loans held by New York state banking institutions

**Bill ID:** S6372
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Creates an income driven repayment plan for private student loans held by New York state banking institutions.
