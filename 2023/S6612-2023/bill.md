# Relates to compensation for directors of the regional off-track betting corporation

**Bill ID:** S6612
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Relates to compensation for directors of the regional off-track betting corporation; requires off-track betting corporations located within Genesee County to report on actual use and implementation of the approved marketing promotion plan from the previous year, which shall include the name and address of each person receiving any promotional item or thing of value in excess of fifty dollars.
