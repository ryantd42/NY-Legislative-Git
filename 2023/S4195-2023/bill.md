# Enacts the Kelsey Smith Act to require wireless service providers to provide call location information to law enforcement during an emergency

**Bill ID:** S4195
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Enacts the Kelsey Smith Act to require wireless service providers to provide call location information to law enforcement during an emergency.
