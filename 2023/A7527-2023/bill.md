# Designates certain zip codes as cease and desist zones

**Bill ID:** A7527
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenny Burgos
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Designates certain zip codes within Bronx county and Mount Vernon in Westchester county as cease and desist zones.
