# Creates a tax credit for employment of persons on probation or parole

**Bill ID:** S2105
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2105 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2105)

## Summary

Creates a tax credit for employment of persons on probation or parole.
