# Prohibits exemptions for owners of vacant or unoccupied storefront properties

**Bill ID:** A1685
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1685 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1685)

## Summary

Relates to prohibiting exemptions for owners of vacant or unoccupied storefront properties.
