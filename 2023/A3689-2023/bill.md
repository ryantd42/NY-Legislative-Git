# Clarifies the regulation of cemetery corporations

**Bill ID:** A3689
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Clarifies that state regulated cemetery operations are under the sole jurisdiction of the state cemetery board.
