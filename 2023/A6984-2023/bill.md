# Includes in the class E felony of vehicular assault in the second degree, the causing of serious physical injury while operating a vehicle with suspended, revoked or no license

**Bill ID:** A6984
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes in the class E felony of vehicular assault in the second degree, the causing of serious physical injury while operating a vehicle with suspended, revoked or no license; creates the crime of vehicular homicide.
