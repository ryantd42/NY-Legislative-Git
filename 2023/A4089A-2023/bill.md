# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the Steve Nicoli memorial highway

**Bill ID:** A4089AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Maher
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the town of Montgomery, county of Orange, the "Steve Nicoli Memorial Highway".
