# Relates to the date of school district annual organizational meetings; and repeals certain provisions of the education law relating thereto

**Bill ID:** A6829
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the date of school district annual organizational meetings; allows boards of education to meet on any date on or before the Monday after July 20th for their annual organizational meeting.
