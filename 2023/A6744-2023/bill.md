# Relates to family leave

**Bill ID:** A6744
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Matthew Slater
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Relates to family leave; includes being called to active duty within the contiguous United States, Hawaii, Alaska, or any other U.S. territory.
