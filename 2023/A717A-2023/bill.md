# Empowers the council on the arts to designate East Harlem as a cultural district

**Bill ID:** A717A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Eddie Gibbs
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Empowers the council on the arts to designate East Harlem as a cultural district.
