# Requires free education for grades pre-K through undergraduate

**Bill ID:** A409
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A409 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A409)

## Summary

Requires free education for grades pre-K through undergraduate degree programs.
