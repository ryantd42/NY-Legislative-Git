# Commending Sabrina Guo upon the occasion of being named a 2023 United States Presidential Scholar

**Bill ID:** J1034
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1034
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Sabrina Guo upon the occasion of being
  named a 2023 United States Presidential Scholar
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of  this  Legislative  Body  to  honor  the
achievements  of  its  young  citizens  and  leaders  of tomorrow, whose
character and accomplishments exemplify the ideals and values  cherished
by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  Sabrina  Guo  upon  the  occasion  of being named a 2023 United
States Presidential Scholar; and
 
  WHEREAS, The United States Presidential Scholar program has  honored
more  than  8,000  of  the  Nation's top students since its inception in
1964; of the 3.7 million students expected to graduate high school  this
year, only about 5,000 students qualified for this honor; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Sabrina  Guo,  a  student  at  Syosset  High  School,  was
recognized as a Presidential Scholar, one of the Nation's highest honors
for high school students; and
 
  WHEREAS, A motivated individual,  Sabrina  Guo  founded  Girl  Pride
International,  a  global  non-profit  that  uplifts  socioeconomically
disadvantaged,  marginalized,  refugee,  and  migrant  girls  through
advocacy, education, leadership, and material support; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Passionate  about  helping  others,  during  the  Covid-19
Pandemic Sabrina Guo founded Long Island Laboring Against  Covid-19,  or
LILAC, a relief organization that donates personal protective equipment,
meals and funds while uplifting at-risk communities, specifically aiding
disproportionately  affected  areas  whose  residents  are predominantly
people of color; and
 
  WHEREAS, Academically, Sabrina Guo has distinguished  herself  as  a
young  woman  of superlative ability, talent, and maturity, establishing
standards of excellence and achievement,  purposefulness  of  life,  and
responsibility rarely achieved in youthful years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As  a  testament to her work ethic and academic abilities,
Sabrina Guo has been named a 2023 Coca-Cola Scholar along  with  several
other  academic  awards  and  plans to attend Yale University, where she
will  concentrate  on  Pre-Law  studies  at  the  intersection  of  law,
business, education, and public policy; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Sabrina  Guo  of  Syosset  High School was one of just 161
seniors across the country to be recognized with this esteemed honor for
her outstanding academic achievement, artistic  excellence,  leadership,
citizenship  and  service  as  well  as  contribution  to her school and
community; and
 
  WHEREAS, An outstanding role model for her classmates,  Sabrina  Guo
has truly brought honor to her family, school, and community; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  Sabrina  Guo  may  stand  with  pride in her achievements,
experiencing  the  satisfaction  of  her  labors  and  the  joy  of  her
accomplishments,  eager  to face the challenge of new experiences as she
continues her education; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sabrina Guo represents the potential inherent in  our  most
precious  resource, our youth; this Legislative Body is pleased to honor
this young woman, recognizing her achievements and the  promise  of  her
future; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Sabrina Guo upon the occasion  of  being  named  a  2023  United
States Presidential Scholar; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Sabrina Guo.
