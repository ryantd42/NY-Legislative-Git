# Creates a wage tax credit for employers who employ New York national guard men and women, reservists, volunteer firefighters and EMS personnel

**Bill ID:** A2044
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stephen Hawley
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates a wage tax credit for employers who employ New York national guard men and women, reservists, volunteer firefighters and EMS personnel.
