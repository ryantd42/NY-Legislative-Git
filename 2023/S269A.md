# Increases the contribution that resident taxpayers may make to family tuition accounts

**Bill ID:** S269AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S269A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S269A)

## Summary

Increases the contribution that a resident taxpayer may make to family tuition accounts during a taxable year.
