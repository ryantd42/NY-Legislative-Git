# Relates to creating a tax credit for small businesses which are required to have and maintain marine bulkheads

**Bill ID:** A2863
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates a tax credit for small businesses which are required to have and maintain marine bulkheads for the full cost of such maintenance during the taxable year.
