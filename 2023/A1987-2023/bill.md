# Relates to motorcycle awareness on state highways

**Bill ID:** A1987
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Maritza Davila
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to motorcycle awareness on state highways by affixing signs stating "Watch for Motorcycles".
