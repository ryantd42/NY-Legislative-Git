# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the Trooper Edward L. Cunningham Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** S7015AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steve Rhoads
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in Nassau county as the "Trooper Edward L. Cunningham Memorial Bridge".
