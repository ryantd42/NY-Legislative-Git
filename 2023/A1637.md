# Relates to violations and a mandatory surcharge for parking in a bicycle lane

**Bill ID:** A1637
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jo Anne Simon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1637 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1637)

## Summary

Relates to violations and a mandatory surcharge for parking in a bicycle lane which shall be paid to the clerk of the court of administrative tribunal that made the determination of liability.
