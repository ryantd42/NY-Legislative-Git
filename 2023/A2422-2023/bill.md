# Eliminates the maximum age for taking the civil service examination for appointment as a police officer or as an environmental conservation officer

**Bill ID:** A2422
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Eliminates the maximum age for taking the civil service examination for appointment as a police officer or as an environmental conservation officer.
