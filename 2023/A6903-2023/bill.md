# Makes technical corrections to a definition for purposes of the excelsior linked deposit program

**Bill ID:** A6903
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Makes technical corrections to the definition of "certified service-disabled veteran-owned business enterprise" for purposes of the excelsior linked deposit program.
