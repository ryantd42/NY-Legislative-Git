# Establishes special permits to practice as a clinical laboratory practitioner

**Bill ID:** S6981
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes special permits to practice as a clinical laboratory practitioner which are valid for not more than 180 days or until the time an appropriate exam is taken.
