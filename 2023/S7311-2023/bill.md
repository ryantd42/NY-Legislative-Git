# Relates to making a murder as the result of a drive by shooting murder in the first degree

**Bill ID:** S7311
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Canzoneri-Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Makes a murder as the result of a drive by shooting, murder in the first degree.

 AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to making  a  murder  as  the

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. Subparagraph (xiii) of paragraph (a) of  subdivision  1  of

 graph (xiii) is added to read as follows:
  (XIII) THE VICTIM WAS KILLED WHILE THE DEFENDANT WAS IN THE COURSE  OF

 GRAPH,  A  "DRIVE  BY  SHOOTING"  IS  DEFINED AS WHEN A PERSON, WITH THE

 HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE OF THE VEHICLE AND TRAFFIC LAW; OR
