# Relates to requiring motor vehicle dealers to search for recalls and make certain repairs prior to selling a used motor vehicle

**Bill ID:** A6549
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jenifer Rajkumar
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires motor vehicle dealers to search for recalls and make certain repairs prior to selling a used motor vehicle.
