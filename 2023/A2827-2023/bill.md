# Relates to unclaimed lottery prizes

**Bill ID:** A2827
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires unclaimed lottery prizes to be paid into the state treasury, to the credit of the state lottery fund.
