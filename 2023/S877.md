# Relates to the enforcement of a judgment against certain trust and annuities in the case of minor victims of sexual abuse

**Bill ID:** S877
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S877 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S877)

## Summary

Permits the enforcement of a judgment against certain trust and annuities in the case of minor victims of sexual abuse.
