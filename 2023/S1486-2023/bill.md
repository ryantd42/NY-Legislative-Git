# Relates to the priority of persons with responsibility to determine the disposition of human remains

**Bill ID:** S1486
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1486 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1486)

## Summary

Relates to the priority of persons with responsibility to determine the disposition of human remains; provides that persons designated by a member of the military shall have first priority.
