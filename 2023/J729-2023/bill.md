# Commending Steven Krieger, Esq. upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition by the Long Island Builders Institute

**Bill ID:** J729
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 729
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Steven Krieger, Esq. upon the occasion
  of his designation for special  recognition  by  the
  Long Island Builders Institute
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize that
the quality and character of life in the  communities  across  New  York
State  are  reflective  of  the concerned and dedicated efforts of those
individuals that are devoted to the welfare of  the  community  and  its
citizenry; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend  Steven  Krieger,  Esq. upon the occasion of his designation for
special  recognition  by  the  Long  Island  Builders  Institute  to  be
celebrated  at its 81st Annual Dinner Dance Gala on Friday, May 5, 2023,
at the North Hills Country Club; and
 
  WHEREAS, During this auspicious event, Past President Steven Krieger
will be honored for his contribution to the building  industry  and  for
his  steadfast  and  unremitting  commitment to the Long Island Builders
Institute (LIBI) and his community; and
 
  WHEREAS, Steven Krieger joined the LIBI in 2006, and was elected  to
its  Board  of Directors in 2014; through the years, he rose through the
ranks, holding the positions of Secretary  in  2018,  and  President  in
2022;  currently,  he  serves as the Chairman of the Board of Directors;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In his many leadership roles, Steven Krieger  has  actively
promoted  the importance of both LIBI and Long Island Home Builders Care
by recruiting new members  and  donating  time  and  resources  to  both
aspects of the organization; and
 
  WHEREAS,  As one of the founding partners of B2K Development, Steven
Krieger has been an integral  part  of  building  the  communities,  the
brands,  and  the  relationships  that the company is so well-known for;
armed with over 30 years of industry experience and  innovation,  he  is
equipped  with  unrivaled  insight  and expertise, bringing an unmatched
precision when it comes to identifying viable opportunities and creating
value through profitable real estate dealings; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Steven  Krieger  calls  upon  his  industry  knowledge  to
masterfully  negotiate  complex  contracts  and  transactions, including
purchase, sale, and acquisition agreements, as well as  loan  agreements
in  excess  of  $2  billion  dollars;  from obtaining entitlements right
through construction, he has an aptitude for  fast-tracking  deals  from
concept  to  completion,  paving  the  way to creating extraordinary and
vibrant residential and commercial developments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout his impressive career thus far,  Steven  Krieger
has  built  a  formidable  foundation  of  trust and rapport through his
various ventures, including repositioning and managing retail and office
buildings for both private and  institutional  owners,  and  negotiating
 
intricate  deals  with  sellers  and  lenders;  he  has  also  led  B2K
Development's foray into the purchasing of notes and mortgages from both
local and institutional lenders; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Some  of  his  most  significant  accomplishments  include
attaining the very first Industrial Development Agency (IDA) funding for
an assisted living community in the State of New York,  the  procurement
of  land  use  approvals  and municipal entitlements for over 3,000 beds
within The Bristal - B2K Development's division of exceptional  assisted
living  communities,  and securing the entitlements for the construction
of over 2,000 housing units in the tri-state area,  as  well  as  credit
tenant retailers Lifetime Fitness, Rite-Aid, QuickChek, and Stop & Shop;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  an  enduring  commitment  to  the  community, Steven
Krieger lends his time, resources, and expertise to organizations  close
to  his  heart  such  as  Long  Island  Housing Partnership, Vision Long
Island, and the Long Island Real Estate Group; and
 
  WHEREAS, Steven Krieger earned his  degree  cum  laude  from  Tulane
University  and  studied  abroad  at  the London School of Economics and
Political Science where he was a  member  of  the  traveling  basketball
team;  later,  he  received  his law degree from the Benjamin N. Cardozo
School of Law, Yeshiva University; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is  the  custom  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of  such  noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, they should be recognized and applauded by all  the  citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Steven Krieger, Esq. upon the occasion of  his  designation  for
special  recognition  by  the  Long Island Builders Institute; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Steven Krieger, Esq.
