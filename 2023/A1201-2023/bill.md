# Establishes the emergency preparedness month act and exempts prefabricated go-bags and individual emergency preparedness items

**Bill ID:** A1201
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1201 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1201)

## Summary

Establishes the "emergency preparedness month act"; exempts prefabricated go-bags and individual emergency preparedness items from sales tax during specified time periods.
