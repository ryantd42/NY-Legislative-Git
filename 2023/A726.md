# Provides for the humane removal of downed animals

**Bill ID:** A726
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A726 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A726)

## Summary

Provides for the humane treatment and removal of downed animals.
