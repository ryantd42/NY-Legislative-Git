# Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage whelk and conch

**Bill ID:** A7442
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dana Levenberg
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authority of the department of environmental conservation to manage whelk and conch from 2023 to 2026.
