# Extends provisions of law relating to certain rental vehicle protections

**Bill ID:** S7059
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to certain rental vehicle protections from June 30, 2023 until June 30, 2028.
