# Requires written in-person or electronic notifications to be utilized for registration in the donate life registry

**Bill ID:** A614
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A614 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A614)

## Summary

Clarifies that registration in the donate life registry takes effect when entry of the individual's enrollment data is made into the registry.
