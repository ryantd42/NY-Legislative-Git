# Authorizes local water and sewerage authorities to charge fees for surface runoff

**Bill ID:** S4169
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Enacts the water bill fairness act; authorizes local water and sewerage authorities to charge fees for surface runoff.
