# Establishes a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for a certain appropriation for the 2018-2019 state fiscal year

**Bill ID:** R1392
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1392
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R5911  of  2018
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  a  certain  appropriation for the
  2018-19 state fiscal year for services and  expenses
  of  military base retention and research efforts, as
  required by such appropriation and subdivision 5  of
  section 24 of the state finance law. This plan shall
  be  subject  to  the  approval  of  the  temporary
  president of the Senate  and  the  director  of  the
  budget  and  thereafter  this  resolution  must  be
  approved by a majority vote of all  members  elected
  to the Senate upon a roll call vote
 
  RESOLVED,  that  pursuant  to,  and  as  required  by,  moneys
appropriated in section 1 of chapter 53  of  the  laws  of  2018  that
enacts  the  aid  to  localities  budget,  to the New York state urban
development corporation under the economic  development  program  from
the general fund/local assistance account for services and expenses of
military  base  retention and research efforts shall be allocated only
pursuant to a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees with the
amount to be received by each, or the methodology for allocating  such
appropriation.  This  plan,  which shall be subject to the approval of
the temporary president of the senate and the director of  the  budget
and  thereafter  shall  be  approved by a majority vote of all members
elected to the senate upon a roll  call  vote,  is  to  allocate  such
appropriation  as follows and as adopted by Senate Resolution R5911 of
