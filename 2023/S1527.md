# Directs the department of transportation to study the cost of administering state highway rights of way projects

**Bill ID:** S1527
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1527 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1527)

## Summary

Directs the department of transportation to study the cost of administering utility projects in state highway rights of way.
