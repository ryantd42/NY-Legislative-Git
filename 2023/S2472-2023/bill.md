# Provides for third trimester syphilis testing

**Bill ID:** S2472
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Cordell Cleare
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for third trimester syphilis testing in addition to such testing in the first trimester.
