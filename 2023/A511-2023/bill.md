# Increases transparency in ticket reselling marketplaces

**Bill ID:** A511
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenny Burgos
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A511 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A511)

## Summary

Increases transparency in ticket reselling marketplaces by requiring ticket resellers have accounts which show their name and a photo of them.
