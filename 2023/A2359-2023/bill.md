# Prohibits the withholding of state funding for service providers for people with developmental disabilities due to the receipt of certain COVID-19 federal funds

**Bill ID:** A2359
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Sarah Clark
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the withholding of state funding for service providers for people with developmental disabilities due to the receipt of certain COVID-19 federal funds.
