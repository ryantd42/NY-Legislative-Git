# Commending Sharon Tucker upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition after 60 years of dedicated service to the 3-G Fire Department

**Bill ID:** K333
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 333
 
BY: M. of A. Blankenbush
 
  COMMENDING  Sharon Tucker upon the occasion of her
  designation for special recognition after  60  years
  of  dedicated  service to the 3-G Fire Department in
  Glenfield, New York
 
  WHEREAS, The quality and character of life in this Nation,  and  the
great  State of New York, is reflective of the purposeful lives and good
works of those patriotic and civic-minded community members who dedicate
their lives and careers to the service of others; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly  proud  to  commend  Sharon
Tucker  upon  the  occasion  of  her designation for special recognition
after 60 years of dedicated service to the 3-G  Fire  Department  to  be
celebrated at its Annual Banquet on Saturday, May 13, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Sharon  Tucker  joined  the  3-G  Fire  Department Ladies'
Auxiliary in 1963; throughout her  six  decades  of  service,  she  held
several  positions within the Auxiliary including: President, Secretary,
and for the last 20 years, Treasurer; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout her tenure, Sharon Tucker has been  instrumental
in the success of many of the organization's activities and has assisted
on  numerous  fire  scenes, working tirelessly to provide support to the
3-G Fire Department and to aid the  volunteer  firefighters  in  various
capacities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  addition  to  her  late  husband,  Frederick, with her
throughout have been her two daughters, Robin and  Becky,  all  of  whom
feel  privileged  to  be  a  part  of  her  life  and  rejoice  in  her
achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sharon Tucker is deserving of the highest  commendation  of
this  Legislative Body for her commitment to the 3-G Fire Department, as
she has helped to advance the perception of her community  as  a  caring
and united community; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Sharon Tucker upon the occasion of her designation  for  special
recognition  after  60  years  of  dedicated  service  to  the  3-G Fire
Department in Glenfield, New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Sharon Tucker.
