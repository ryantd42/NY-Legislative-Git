# Commemorating the 202nd Anniversary of the Town of LaGrange on February 9, 2023

**Bill ID:** K275
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 275
 
BY: M. of A. Beephan
 
  COMMEMORATING the 202nd Anniversary of the Town of
  LaGrange on February 9, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of this Legislative Body to honor and
commemorate  the  distinguished  histories  of  the  communities  which
comprise the noble body of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate the 202nd Anniversary of the Town of LaGrange on February 9,
2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Town of LaGrange, originally named Freedom when it was
established, has a population of just over 15,000 residents; and
 
  WHEREAS, The notorious Taconic State Parkway cuts right through  the
center of LaGrange as travelers pass through the quaint town; and
 
  WHEREAS,  An  active  community,  LaGrange is home to Arlington High
School and LaGrange Middle School; the town also holds James Baird State
Park and Freedom Park; and
 
  WHEREAS, Of historic origin, and remaining fruitful over the ebb and
flow of decades of growth and change, the Town of LaGrange continues  to
provide the quintessential quality of life for its residents; and
 
  WHEREAS, The residents of the Town of LaGrange have a bright outlook
for  the  future  of their community, which remains a beautiful place in
which to live and raise a family; and
 
  WHEREAS, In recognition of the Town of LaGrange's rich  history  and
enduring contribution to the State of New York, this Legislative Body is
proud  to  pay  tribute  to  this spirited town upon the occasion of its
202nd Anniversary; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate the 202nd Anniversary of the Town of LaGrange on February 9,
2023,  recognizing  the significance of the role it continues to play in
the life of the community of the State of New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Town of LaGrange.
