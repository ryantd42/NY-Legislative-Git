# Providing for the election of Mary-Anne E. Dandles to the office of Clerk of the Assembly

**Bill ID:** E2
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 2
 
BY: M. of A. Cook
 
  PROVIDING for the election of Mary-Anne E. Dandles
  to the office of Clerk of the Assembly
 
  RESOLVED,  That  Mary-Anne  E.  Dandles be and she hereby is elected
Clerk of the Assembly for the years 2023 and 2024.
