# Increases the tax exemption amount on clothing and apparel items from one hundred ten dollars to two hundred fifty dollars

**Bill ID:** A3318
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Increases the tax exemption amount on clothing and apparel items from one hundred ten dollars to two hundred fifty dollars.
