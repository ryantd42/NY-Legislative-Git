# Relates to the applicability of certain labor law requirements to construction projects involving parking facilities with EV charging capability

**Bill ID:** S2621
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Liz Krueger
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Exempts certain projects from requirements for providing EV charging capability and from certain labor law provisions for public projects.
