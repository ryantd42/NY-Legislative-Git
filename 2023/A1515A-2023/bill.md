# Authorizes the county of Cattaraugus to discontinue as parklands and lease certain reforested lands in the town of Perrysburg

**Bill ID:** A1515A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Cattaraugus to discontinue as parklands and lease certain reforested lands in the town of Perrysburg.
