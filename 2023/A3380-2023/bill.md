# Provides for the revocation of a person's driver's license who has been convicted of operating a vessel while under the influence of alcohol or drugs

**Bill ID:** A3380
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the revocation of a person's driver's license who has been convicted of operating a vessel while under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
