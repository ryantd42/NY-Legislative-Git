# Exempts innovative and alternative septic systems from sales and use taxes

**Bill ID:** S2989
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Exempts innovative and alternative septic systems that provide for enhanced treatment technologies from sales and use taxes.
