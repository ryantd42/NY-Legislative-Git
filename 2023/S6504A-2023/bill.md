# Establishes an Alzheimer's disease outreach and education program

**Bill ID:** S6504A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Iwen Chu
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Establishes an Alzheimer's disease outreach and education program.

 COMMISSIONER,  IN  ADMINISTERING  THIS SECTION, SHALL SEEK TO COORDINATE
 THE DEPARTMENT'S PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES UNDER THIS SECTION  WITH  OTHER
 PUBLIC  AND  PRIVATE  PROGRAMS,  AND  MAY UNDERTAKE JOINT OR COOPERATIVE
 PROGRAMS WITH OTHER PUBLIC OR PRIVATE ENTITIES.
