# Memorializing Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim June 21, 2023, as Community Organizers Day in the State of New York

**Bill ID:** J1122
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Senate Committee

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1122
 
BY: Senator FERNANDEZ
 
  MEMORIALIZING  Governor  Kathy  Hochul to proclaim
  June 21, 2023, as Community Organizers  Day  in  the
  State of New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the sense of this Legislative Body to set aside days
in the State of New York which recognize those who  selflessly  work  to
improve the quality of life for others by coming together and discussing
ways to solve common social problems; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
memorialize  Governor  Kathy  Hochul  to  proclaim  June  21,  2023,  as
Community Organizers Day in the State of New York, in  conjunction  with
the observance of National Community Organizers Day; and
 
  WHEREAS,  These  organizers  often work without salaries, accolades,
promotions, or pensions but for the love of community; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is important to celebrate and acknowledge the importance
of community  organizing  and  the  immense  contributions  of  selfless
community organizers in improving the quality of our lives; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  day  brings  together  family,  friends,  and  local
authorities in an effort to inspire everyone with what can be done  when
we all work together, and encourage more individuals to get involved and
make a positive impact on their communities; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
memorialize Governor Kathy Hochul to proclaim June 21, 2023 as Community
Organizers Day in the State of New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copies of this Resolution, suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to The Honorable Kathy Hochul, Governor of the State of New
York and to Sheikh Musa Drammeh, Cofounder of Community Organizers Day.
