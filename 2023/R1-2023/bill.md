# Providing for the election of Senator Andrea Stewart-Cousins as Temporary President of the Senate for 2023-2024

**Bill ID:** R1
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michael Gianaris
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1
 
BY: Senator GIANARIS
 
  PROVIDING  for  the  election  of  Andrea
  Stewart-Cousins as Temporary President of the Senate
  for the years 2023-2024
 
  RESOLVED, That Senator Andrea Stewart-Cousins be,  and  she  hereby
is, elected Temporary President of the Senate for the years 2023-2024.
