# Prohibits mortgagee from refusing to accept partial mortgage payments

**Bill ID:** S1705
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1705 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1705)

## Summary

Prohibits mortgagee from refusing to accept partial mortgage payments from a mortgagor which does not bring the mortgagor current on his or her mortgage debt.
