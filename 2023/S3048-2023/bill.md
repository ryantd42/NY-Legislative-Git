# Changes the time period for the annual national change of address system check for registered voters

**Bill ID:** S3048
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Changes the time period for the annual national change of address system check for registered voters to January.
