# Requires ticket distributors to refund the purchase price of tickets within forty-five days after a cancelled or rescheduled event

**Bill ID:** A2595
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires ticket distributors to refund the purchase price of tickets within forty-five days after a cancelled or rescheduled event.
