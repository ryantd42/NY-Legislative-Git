# Extends certain provisions authorizing the county of Hamilton to impose a county recording tax on obligations secured by mortgages on real property

**Bill ID:** S7430
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends certain provisions authorizing the county of Hamilton to impose a county recording tax on obligations secured by mortgages on real property to December 1, 2025.
