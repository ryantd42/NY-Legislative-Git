# Authorizes conditional adult-use cultivator licensees to sell their own cannabis products and authorizes the office of cannabis management to create a program for licensees to process cannabis into distillate

**Bill ID:** A6593
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Donna Lupardo
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes conditional adult-use cultivator licensees to sell their own cannabis products; authorizes the office of cannabis management to create a loan or grant program to help such adult-use cultivator licensees to process cannabis into distillate.
