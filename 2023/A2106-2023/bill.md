# Requires the sale or delivery of cannabis products by distributors to retailers be in exchange for cash to be paid at the time of delivery or on terms requiring payment by such retailer for such cannabis

**Bill ID:** A2106
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Crystal Peoples-Stokes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the sale or delivery of cannabis products by distributors to retailers be in exchange for cash to be paid at the time of delivery or on terms requiring payment by such retailer for such cannabis products.
