# Relates to the establishment of regional arts, cultural affairs, and parks accessibility and sustainability grants

**Bill ID:** A526
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert C. Carroll
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A526 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A526)

## Summary

Establishes regional arts, cultural affairs, and parks accessibility and sustainability grants for ten regions throughout the state.
