# Requires child restraint systems to be equipped with sensors

**Bill ID:** A1115
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1115 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1115)

## Summary

Requires retailers to equip child restraint systems with sensors to notify drivers if a child has been left in the car.
