# Commending Class of 2023 Valedictorian Thasnim Tabassoom and Salutatorian Thasin Tabassoon of the Academy of Conservation and Environment, in recognition of their outstanding accomplishments

**Bill ID:** J1159
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1159
 
BY: Senator PERSAUD
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2023  Valedictorian Thasnim
  Tabassoom and Salutatorian Thasin Tabassoom  of  the
  Academy  of  Conservation  and  Environment,  in
  recognition of their outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions,  to  honor the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2023 Valedictorian Thasnim Tabassoom and Salutatorian  Thasin  Tabassoom
of  the Academy of Conservation and Environment, in recognition of their
outstanding accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Valedictorian Thasnim  Tabassoom  and  Salutatorian  Thasin
Tabassoom represent the best of developed potential inherent in our most
precious  resource,  our youth; their achievements have brought enduring
honor to their  family  and  community  and  should  be  recognized  and
saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian  Thasnim  Tabassoom  and Salutatorian Thasin
Tabassoom may now stand with pride as they  assess  their  achievements,
experience  the  satisfaction  of  their  labors  and  the  joy of their
accomplishments, eager to face the  new  experiences  of  a  challenging
world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2023 Valedictorian Thasnim Tabassoom  and  Salutatorian
Thasin  Tabassoom  of  the  Academy  of Conservation and Environment, in
recognition of their outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Valedictorian Thasnim Tabassoom and Salutatorian Thasin
Tabassoom.
