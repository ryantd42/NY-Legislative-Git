# Relates to apportionments under the shared superintendent program; appropriation

**Bill ID:** S2254
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for apportionments to school districts under the shared superintendent program; makes an appropriation therefor.
