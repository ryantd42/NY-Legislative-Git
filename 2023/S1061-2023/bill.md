# Relates to required training for school district officers

**Bill ID:** S1061
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1061 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1061)

## Summary

Relates to school district officers required to complete certain training to acquaint such officer with the powers, functions and duties of boards of education; relates to topics to be included in such training.
