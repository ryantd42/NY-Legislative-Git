# Congratulating the Millbrook High School Girls Basketball Team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Basketball Class C Championship on March 18, 2023

**Bill ID:** J993
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 993
 
BY: Senator HINCHEY
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Millbrook  High  School Girls
  Basketball Team upon the occasion of  capturing  the
  NYSPHSAA  Basketball  Class  C Championship on March
  18, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
Millbrook  High  School  Girls  Basketball  Team  upon  the  occasion of
capturing  their  second  consecutive  NYSPHSAA  Basketball  Class  C
Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  To  the  praise  and  applause  of their excited fans, the
number one ranked Blazers defeated the number four ranked Randolph  High
School  in a thrilling 59-45 victory to capture the New York State title
on Saturday, March 18, 2023, at Hudson Valley Community College in Troy,
New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the school's colors of navy blue and  grey,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported  the  Blazers  throughout  their  journey  as they ended their
season with an impressive overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport  such  as  basketball,  which  demands  athletic
prowess,  speed and agility, Head Coach Adam Peek, and Assistant Coaches
Jason Zmudosky and Colin Wohlford have worked hard to hone the skills of
this championship team,  teaching  these  outstanding  athletes  lessons
which will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and
 
  WHEREAS, Head Coach Adam Peek and all of the outstanding athletes on
the  Millbrook  High  School Girls Basketball Team have clearly utilized
dedication,  determination  and  teamwork  in  providing  a  lasting
contribution  to  the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of their
school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the  members of the Millbrook High School Girls Basketball
Team: Beth Bosan, Natalie Fox, Emily Grasseler, Hudson Heitmann,  Sailor
Heitmann,  Lydia Kascak, Sarah Marchant, Charlie Moore, Hannah Ross, and
Ella Wilson; Head Coach Adam Peek, and Assistant Coaches Jason  Zmudosky
and  Colin  Wohlford  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA
Basketball Class C Championship on March 18, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the members of the Millbrook High School Girls Basketball
Team,  Head  Coach  Adam  Peek, and Assistant Coaches Jason Zmudosky and
Colin Wohlford.
