# Relates to mandatory dispositions and the commission of a felony offense while in possession of a firearm

**Bill ID:** A4222
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John K. Mikulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to mandatory dispositions and the commission of a felony offense while in possession of a firearm; provides an additional five years imprisonment.
