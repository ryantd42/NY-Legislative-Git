# Commemorating the Schuylerville Garden Club upon the occasion of celebrating its 80th Anniversary

**Bill ID:** K634
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Carrie Woerner
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 634
 
BY: M. of A. Woerner
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  Schuylerville Garden Club upon
  the occasion of celebrating its 80th Anniversary
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  and
commend  those  organizations  of  commitment and high achievement whose
purpose and endeavor have been dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in
floral  design  and  the  promotion  of  civic  and  environmental
responsibility in this noble Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  Schuylerville  Garden  Club  upon  the  occasion  of
celebrating its 80th Anniversary; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Schuylerville Garden Club was organized  on  March  24,
1938,  with  nine  charter  members and Clyde Maybee serving as founding
president; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Schuylerville Garden Club became federated in 1951 as a
member of the National Garden Clubs and New York State Federated  Garden
Clubs, District IV; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  its inception, eight decades ago, the Schuylerville
Garden Club has held its meetings on the second Wednesday of each month;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  the  1940s,  defense  bonds  were  discussed  at  the
Schuylerville  Garden  Club  meetings,  and  it  was decided that floral
contest winners would receive "War Stamps" as their winning prize; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Schuylerville Garden Club now has 40  members  who  all
take  an  active  part  in  meeting  the  club's goals: to stimulate the
knowledge and love  of  gardening,  to  study  flower  arrangements  and
horticulture, to promote conservation and to encourage beautification in
the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over the years, the Schuylerville Garden Club has taken on
many projects including village and town planting, an annual flower show
that has been held  since  1938,  the  maintenance  of  gardens  at  the
historic  Schuyler  House  and at the Blue Star Memorial, making holiday
favors for Seniors, organizing preschool projects, and decorating public
buildings for the holidays and the high  school's  graduation  ceremony;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Schuylerville  Garden  Club  donates  to  multiple
organizations and events including Dollars for  Scholars,  Schuylerville
Community  Theater,  The  Festival of Trees, DEC Camperships, The Nature
Conservancy, World  Gardening,  the  Schuylerville  Food  and  Emergency
Relief and various programs at the local library; and
 
  WHEREAS, For this year's 80th Annual Flower Show, to be held on July
13-14,  2023,  the  Schuylerville  Garden  Club will showcase photos and
 
memorabilia in commemoration of its special anniversary  and  milestone;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Steeped  in  a  proud  and  distinguished  history,  the
Schuylerville Garden Club may look back with  pride  and  hold  true  of
purpose  in  its  commitment  to serving the Schuylerville community and
beyond; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the  Schuylerville  Garden  Club  upon  the  occasion  of
celebrating its 80th Anniversary, noting the propriety of its goals, the
constancy of its commitment, and the significance of its accomplishments
in improving its community now and in the future; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Evelyn Rybaltowski, President of The Schuylerville Garden
Club.
