# Requires members of a boat club complete a boating safety course

**Bill ID:** A6406
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Requires owners, operators, employees, customers and persons using a boat club shall successfully complete a boating safety course.
