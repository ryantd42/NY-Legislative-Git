# Modifies the requirements for financial disclosures for certain state and legislative officials, officers and employees

**Bill ID:** S6817
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nathalia Fernandez
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Modifies the requirements for financial disclosures for certain state and legislative officials, officers and employees.
