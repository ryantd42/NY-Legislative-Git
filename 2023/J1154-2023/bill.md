# Commending Class of 2023 Valedictorian Davianna David and Salutatorian Armari McKinney of the School of the Future Brooklyn

**Bill ID:** J1154
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1154
 
BY: Senator PERSAUD
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2023 Valedictorian Davianna
  David and Salutatorian Armari McKinney of the School
  of the Future  Brooklyn,  in  recognition  of  their
  outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to act in accord
with its long-standing traditions, to honor  the  youth  of  today,  the
leaders  of  tomorrow,  whose  character  and achievements exemplify the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to commend  Class  of
2023  Valedictorian  Davianna  David and Salutatorian Armari McKinney of
the School of the Future Brooklyn, in recognition of  their  outstanding
accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian  Davianna  David  and  Salutatorian  Armari
McKinney represent the best of developed potential inherent in our  most
precious  resource,  our youth; their achievements have brought enduring
honor to their families and communities and  should  be  recognized  and
saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian  Davianna  David  and  Salutatorian  Armari
McKinney may now stand with pride as  they  assess  their  achievements,
experience  the  satisfaction  of  their  labors  and  the  joy of their
accomplishments, eager to face the  new  experiences  of  a  challenging
world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2023  Valedictorian  Davianna  David  and  Salutatorian
Armari  McKinney of the School of the Future Brooklyn, in recognition of
their outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Valedictorian  Davianna  David  and Salutatorian Armari
McKinney.
