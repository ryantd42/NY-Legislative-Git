# Permits the possession of firearms in sensitive areas for the purposes of performing historical battle reenactments

**Bill ID:** A4133
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Permits the possession of firearms in sensitive areas for the purposes of performing historical battle reenactments.
