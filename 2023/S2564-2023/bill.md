# Requires the department of education to provide annual notifications to school districts to combat cyber crime

**Bill ID:** S2564
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the department of education to provide annual notifications to school districts to combat cyber crime.
