# Relates to assaults on nursing professionals and sanitarians

**Bill ID:** A3547
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to assaults on nursing professionals and sanitation workers while such person was performing an assigned duty.
