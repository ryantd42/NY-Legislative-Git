# Prohibits legislative bills from embracing more than 1 subject

**Bill ID:** S1435
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1435 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1435)

## Summary

Prohibits legislative bills from embracing more than 1 subject, with the exception of budget bills.
