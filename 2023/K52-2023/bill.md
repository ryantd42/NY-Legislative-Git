# Congratulating the Fillmore High School Boys Varsity Soccer Team upon the occasion of capturing the 2022 NYSPHSAA Soccer Class D Championship on November 13, 2022

**Bill ID:** K52
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 52
 
BY: M. of A. Giglio JM
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Fillmore  High  School  Boys
  Varsity Soccer Team upon the occasion  of  capturing
  the  2022  NYSPHSAA  Soccer  Class D Championship on
  November 13, 2022
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
Fillmore  High  School  Boys  Varsity  Soccer  Team upon the occasion of
capturing the 2022 NYSPHSAA Soccer Class D Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and  applause  of  their  excited  fans,  the
number five ranked Eagles defeated number seven ranked Chazy High School
in a decisive 2-0 victory to capture the New York State title on Sunday,
November 13, 2022, at Middletown High School's Faller Field; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly  donning  the  school's colors of green and white,
family, friends, and the community at large loyally and enthusiastically
supported the Eagles throughout their journey as they ended their season
with an impressive overall record of 23-0; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as soccer, which demands athletic  prowess,
speed and agility, Head Coach Jarrett Vosburg and Assistant Coaches Mike
Witkowski  and Jordan Mullen have worked hard to hone the skills of this
championship team, teaching these  outstanding  athletes  lessons  which
will prove invaluable both on and off the field; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Head  Coach  Jarrett  Vosburg  and  all of the outstanding
athletes on the Fillmore High  School  Boys  Varsity  Soccer  Team  have
clearly  utilized  dedication, determination and teamwork in providing a
lasting contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition of
their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  the  members  of  Fillmore High School Boys Varsity Soccer
Team: Nolan Krzeminski, Mitchell Ward, Isaiah Sisson, Eben Schilke, Jude
Beardsley, Sam Cool, Henry  Sardina,  James  Hillman,  Layton  Sanasith,
Damon  Wood, Ray Muzaid-Omar, Jack Cool, Joe Derck, Zach Sisson, Brayden
Hennard, Luke Colombo, Ernie Lipscomb, Brent Zubikowski,  Lucas  Hersee,
Aiden  Wagner,  Ben  Beardsley,  Noah  Strickland,  Henry  Decker, Brian
Ashton, Carson Sanasith and Jack Boon; Head Coach Jarrett  Vosburg;  and
Assistant  Coaches Mike Witkowski and Jordan Mullen upon the occasion of
capturing the 2022 NYSPHSAA Soccer  Class  D  Championship;  and  be  it
further
 
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the members of the  Fillmore  High  School  Boys  Varsity
Soccer  Team,  Head  Coach  Jarrett  Vosburg  and Assistant Coaches Mike
Witkowski and Jordan Mullen.
