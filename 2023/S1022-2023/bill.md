# Relates to personal cultivation facilities for cannabis

**Bill ID:** S1022
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1022 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1022)

## Summary

Permits the personal cultivation of cannabis in a licensed personal cultivation facility.
