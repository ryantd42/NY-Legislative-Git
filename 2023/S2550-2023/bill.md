# Relates to the offense of assault in the second degree and employees of domestic violence shelters and persons seeking shelter in such shelters

**Bill ID:** S2550
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Expands the offense of assault in the second degree to include the infliction of physical injury to employees of domestic violence shelters and persons seeking shelter in such shelters.
