**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1442 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1442)

## Summary

Authorizes the free use of campsites to any person who is semi-ambulatory.
