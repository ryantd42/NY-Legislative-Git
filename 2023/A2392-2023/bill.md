# Modifies the effect of termination of criminal actions and proceedings against a minor

**Bill ID:** A2392
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Erik Dilan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the expungement of certain records following the termination of criminal actions and proceedings against certain minors.
