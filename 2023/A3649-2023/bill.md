# Exempts certain youths program volunteers from civil liability

**Bill ID:** A3649
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Manktelow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Exempts volunteers in recreational activities, including but not limited to, little league coaches, managers, assistants and members of the board of directors from civil liability while providing services or rendering assistance to a player or participant as a result of his or her acts of commission or omission arising out of or in the course of his or her rendering such services or assistance during competitive games and practices.
