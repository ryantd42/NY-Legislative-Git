# Relates to the purchase of green cleaning and other custodial products

**Bill ID:** S2740
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to the purchase of green cleaning and other custodial products at state agencies and authorities.
