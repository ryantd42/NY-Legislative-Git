# Establishes unpaid leave of absence as a reasonable accommodation under the state's human rights law

**Bill ID:** A4300
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Phil Steck
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes unpaid leave of absence as a reasonable accommodation under the state's human rights law.
