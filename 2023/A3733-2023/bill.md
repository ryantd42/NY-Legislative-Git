# Grants a total exemption from real property taxation for school tax purposes for certain persons seventy-five years of age or over

**Bill ID:** A3733
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Grants a total exemption from real property taxation for school tax purposes for certain persons seventy-five years of age or over.
