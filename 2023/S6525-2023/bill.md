# Relates to preferences under the affordable home ownership development program for veterans with service-related disabilities

**Bill ID:** S6525
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to preferences under the affordable home ownership development program for veterans with service-related disabilities; defines "veteran with a service-related disability".
