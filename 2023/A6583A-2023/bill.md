# Establishes November twenty-sixth of each year as a day of commemoration known as Sojourner Truth Day

**Bill ID:** A6583A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Sarahana Shrestha
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Establishes November twenty-sixth of each year as a day of commemoration known as "Sojourner Truth Day".
