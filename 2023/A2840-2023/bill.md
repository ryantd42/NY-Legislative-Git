# Provides for a mandatory ten-year minimum sentence of imprisonment for certain felony convictions of public servants

**Bill ID:** A2840
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to providing for a mandatory ten-year minimum sentence of imprisonment for certain felony convictions of public servants.
