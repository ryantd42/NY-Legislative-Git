# Relates to exempting private and parochial schools and day care centers from immunization requirements

**Bill ID:** A6621
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David DiPietro
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Exempts private and parochial schools and day care centers from immunization requirements.
