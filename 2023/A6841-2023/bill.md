# Prohibits the elimination of building systems or equipment used for the combustion of fossil fuels in new building construction

**Bill ID:** A6841
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the elimination of building systems or equipment used for the combustion of fossil fuels, including, natural gas, propane and fuel oil in new building construction.
