# Establishes voluntary licensure of master plumbers by the department of state

**Bill ID:** A3871
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes voluntary licensure of master plumbers by the department of state.

 MEMBER OF THE BOARD AND SHALL SERVE AS THE CHAIR.
  2. THE BOARD SHALL MEET NOT LESS THAN THREE TIMES A YEAR.
  3. THE BOARD SHALL PROMULGATE RULES AND REGULATIONS ESTABLISHING:
  (A) THE SCOPE OF THE PROFESSION OF BEING A MASTER PLUMBER;
  (B) QUALIFICATIONS FOR BEING A LICENSED MASTER PLUMBER;
  (C)  AN EXAMINATION TO DETERMINE THE KNOWLEDGE OF PERSONS APPLYING FOR
