# Relates to pretrial release data reporting

**Bill ID:** S6728
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the frequency of pretrial release data reporting to every four months.
