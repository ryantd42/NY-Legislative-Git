# Authorizes a school district in the village of Tuckahoe, by resolution, to limit transportation of students to 7 miles

**Bill ID:** A4145
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes a school district in the village of Tuckahoe, by resolution, to limit transportation of students to 7 miles.
