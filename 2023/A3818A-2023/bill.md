# Extends the answer time when a major capital improvement rent increase has been filed to ninety days

**Bill ID:** A3818A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Extends the answer time when a major capital improvement rent increase has been filed to ninety days.
