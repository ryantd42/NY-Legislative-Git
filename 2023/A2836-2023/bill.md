# Directs the New York thruway authority to develop and implement a toll discount program waiving additional costs for tolls related to travel on the New York state thruway for certain individuals

**Bill ID:** A2836
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs the New York thruway authority to develop and implement a toll discount program waiving additional costs for tolls related to travel on the New York state thruway for certain individuals who do not maintain an E-Z Pass account.
