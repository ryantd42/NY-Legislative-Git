# Relates to requiring hospitals to provide forms in Punjabi

**Bill ID:** S2903
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires hospitals to provide medical forms in Punjabi.
