# Authorizes the department of environmental conservation to engage in cooperative purchasing and authorizes intergovernmental agreements

**Bill ID:** A2673
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the department of environmental conservation to engage in cooperative purchasing; authorizes intergovernmental agreements between public agencies.
