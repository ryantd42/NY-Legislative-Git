# Relates to the coverage of air ambulance services in the child health plus program

**Bill ID:** S959
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S959 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S959)

## Summary

Provides for the coverage of air ambulance services in the child health plus program.
