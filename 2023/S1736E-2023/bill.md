# Requires electric vehicle charging stations and electric vehicle ready parking spaces in certain new construction

**Bill ID:** S1736EA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Liz Krueger
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Requires new construction that includes dedicated off-street parking to provide electric vehicle charging stations and electric vehicle ready parking spaces.
