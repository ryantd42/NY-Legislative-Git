# Directs the department of health to create an informational pamphlet regarding the laparoscopic power morcellation surgery technique

**Bill ID:** S2463BB
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs the department of health to create an informational pamphlet regarding the laparoscopic power morcellation surgery technique.
