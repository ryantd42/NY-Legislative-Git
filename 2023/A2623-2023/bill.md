# Relates to licensing laser hair removal technicians

**Bill ID:** A2623
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to licensing laser hair removal technicians; establishes laser hair removal rules, regulations and standards.
