# Disallows the suspension or revocation of a domestic violence victim's driver's license or registration for non-payment or lapse of insurance coverage

**Bill ID:** S2645
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mario Mattera
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Disallows the suspension or revocation of a domestic violence victim's driver's license or registration for non-payment or lapse of insurance coverage upon a proper showing of current insurance and a court order or attorney affirmation that such individual is in fact a victim of domestic violence.
