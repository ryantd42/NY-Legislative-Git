 AN ACT to authorize George A. Amendola Tier II status in  the  New  York
  state and local employees' retirement system
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Notwithstanding any  other  provision  of  law,  George  A.
 Amendola, who was first eligible to join the public retirement system as
 of  September  20, 1974 and who was employed by the Niagara Falls school
 district from September 20, 1974 to June 2, 1978 and who for reasons not
 ascribable to his own negligence was not  afforded  the  opportunity  to
 join the New York state and local employees' retirement system with Tier
 II status, shall be deemed to have become a member of the New York state
 and  local  employees'  retirement  system as of September 20, 1974; and
 shall have Tier II status, if, no later than one year after  the  effec-
 tive  date  of  this  act he shall file a written request with the state
 comptroller; and upon receipt of such application, the comptroller shall
 recalculate George Amendola's retirement  benefits  and  thereafter  pay
 such benefits accordingly as if he shall have been deemed to have joined
 such retirement system on September 20, 1974.
