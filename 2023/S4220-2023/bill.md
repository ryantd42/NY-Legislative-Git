# Authorizes municipalities to purchase private residential real property located in a floodplain

**Bill ID:** S4220
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Authorizes municipalities to purchase private residential real property located in a floodplain.
