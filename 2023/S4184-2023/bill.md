# Creates a homeowners' bill of rights to inform consumers with regard to insuring against catastrophic loss

**Bill ID:** S4184
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Creates a homeowners' bill of rights; directs the superintendent of financial services to issue a consumer guide on insuring against catastrophic losses.
