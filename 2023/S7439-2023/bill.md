# Authorizes the town of Southampton, county of Suffolk, to alienate certain parklands for the purpose of installing an algae skimmer, and to replace such alienated parkland with a new dedicated parkland

**Bill ID:** S7439
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Authorizes the town of Southampton, county of Suffolk, to alienate certain parklands for the purpose of installing an algae skimmer in Lake Agawam, and to replace such alienated parkland with a new, dedicated parkland.
