# Makes the inability to pay child support an affirmative defense to non-support of child offenses, rather than an element of such offenses

**Bill ID:** S4089
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Makes the inability to pay child support an affirmative defense to non-support of child offenses, rather than an element of such offenses.
