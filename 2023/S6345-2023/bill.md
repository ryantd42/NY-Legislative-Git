# Authorizes the county of Jefferson to impose additional sales tax

**Bill ID:** S6345
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Authorizes the county of Jefferson to impose additional sales tax until November 30th, 2025.
