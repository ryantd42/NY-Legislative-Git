# Makes rent stabilized dwelling units eligible for certain protections

**Bill ID:** S2088
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [S2088 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2088)

## Summary

Makes rent stabilized dwelling units eligible for certain protections regarding deposits.
