# Requires public schools to provide at least one flavored milk option at scheduled meal periods

**Bill ID:** A3143

**Sponsor:** Chris Tague
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires public schools to provide at least one flavored milk option at scheduled meal periods.
