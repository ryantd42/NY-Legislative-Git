# Authorizes cities, villages and towns to reduce the speed limit to less than twenty-five miles per hour in areas designated for senior citizens

**Bill ID:** A392
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Catalina Cruz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A392 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A392)

## Summary

Authorizes cities, villages and towns to reduce the speed limit to less than twenty-five miles per hour but not lower than fifteen miles per hour in designated areas where there are senior citizen centers and senior citizen housing complexes.
