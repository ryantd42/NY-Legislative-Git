# Provides that any review of apportionment by the legislature shall be brought in the supreme court of Albany county

**Bill ID:** S3292BA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Zellnor Myrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides that any review of apportionment by the legislature shall be brought in the supreme court of Albany county.
