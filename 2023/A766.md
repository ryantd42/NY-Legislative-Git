# Restricts the locations of gunsmiths and dealers in firearms in relation to day cares, educational facilities, and places of worship

**Bill ID:** A766
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A766 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A766)

## Summary

Restricts the locations of gunsmiths and dealers in firearms in relation to child day care or educational facilities, and places of worship.
