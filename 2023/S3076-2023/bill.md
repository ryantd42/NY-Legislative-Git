# Defines a real estate team as two or more persons associated with the same real estate brokerage who hold themselves out or operate as a team

**Bill ID:** S3076
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Defines a real estate team as two or more persons associated with the same real estate brokerage who hold themselves out or operate as a team.
