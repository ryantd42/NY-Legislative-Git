# Enacts the Justice for Rachael Act

**Bill ID:** A1945
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick Burke
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Enacts the Justice for Rachael Act; creates dangerousness hearings.
