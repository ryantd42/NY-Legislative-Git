# Prohibits the inclusion of confidential clauses in contracts entered into for the purchase of services or commodities by the state

**Bill ID:** S2509
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the inclusion of confidential clauses in contracts entered into for the purchase of services or commodities by the state.

 AN ACT to amend the state finance law, in relation  to  prohibiting  the
  inclusion  of  confidential  clauses in contracts entered into for the

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  Paragraph  a of subdivision 3 of section 163 of the state
 finance law is amended by adding a new subparagraph (viii)  to  read  as
 follows:
  (VIII)  CONSISTENT  WITH  GUIDELINES  ISSUED  BY THE STATE PROCUREMENT
 COUNCIL, NO STATE AGENCY  SHALL  INCLUDE  ANY  CONFIDENTIAL  CLAUSES  IN
 CONTRACTS  WITH  THE  STATE.  AS USED IN THIS SUBPARAGRAPH "CONFIDENTIAL
 CLAUSE" INCLUDES ANY CLAUSE NOT MADE AVAILABLE TO THE PUBLIC THROUGH THE
 PROCUREMENT OPPORTUNITIES NEWSLETTER DESCRIBED IN  SECTION  ONE  HUNDRED
 FORTY-TWO OF THE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT LAW.
