# Establishes a plan setting forth an itemized list of grantees for certain appropriations in the 2014-2015 state fiscal year

**Bill ID:** R1409
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1409
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R6134  of  2014
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  certain  appropriations  in  the
  2014-2015 state fiscal year
 
  RESOLVED (if the  concur), In addition to  grants
allocated  pursuant to chapter 54 of the laws of 2014 within
the City University of New York and State University of  New
York Capital:
  Notwithstanding  any  provision  of law this appropriation
shall be allocated only pursuant to a plan setting forth  an
itemized  list of projects with the amount to be received by
each, or the methodology for allocating such  appropriation.
Such  plan shall be subject to the approval of the temporary
president of the senate and the director of the  budget  and
thereafter shall be included in a resolution calling for the
expenditure  of  such  monies,  which  resolution  must  be
approved by a majority vote of all members  elected  to  the
senate  upon  a  roll  call  vote  and  as adopted by Senate
Resolution R6134 of 2014
