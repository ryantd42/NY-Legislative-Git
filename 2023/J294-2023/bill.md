# Congratulating Paul C. Landers upon the occasion of his retirement after 40 years of devoted service to the field of disabilities services

**Bill ID:** J294
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 294
 
BY: Senator OBERACKER
 
  CONGRATULATING  Paul  C. Landers upon the occasion
  of his retirement after 40 years of devoted  service
  to the field of disabilities services
 
  WHEREAS,  Individuals  and  organizations  devoted  to improving the
quality of life for others, enabling them to build lives of dignity  and
self-worth are deserving of the highest praise and recognition; and
 
  WHEREAS, From time to time, society calls upon these individuals and
organizations  to  be  recognized  for  the  significance  of  their
contributions and efforts to assist  others  in  developing  the  skills
necessary to be successful, productive citizens; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This Legislative Body is justly proud to congratulate Paul
C.  Landers upon the occasion of  his  retirement  after  40  years  of
devoted service to the field of disabilities services; and
 
  WHEREAS,  With  a  steadfast  and  unwavering  commitment to serving
individuals with disabilities, Paul C.  Landers  began  his  illustrious
career  with Pathfinder Village in 2000; he quickly climbed the ranks to
become President and Chief  Executive  Officer  in  2008,  the  esteemed
position from which he will officially retire; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Pathfinder  Village  is a community where people with Down
syndrome and other developmental disabilities discover their  own  value
and  talents,  and  share  these  gifts  with  others;  furthermore, its
residents are valued for their abilities and contributions, and they are
encouraged to choose friends and develop goals; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under the able leadership of Paul  C.  Landers,  Pathfinder
Village  promotes  a healthy, progressive environment which respects the
individual, supporting a life of value and independence; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout Paul C. Landers' 15 years as President and  CEO,
this  vital  organization has advanced its mission from a long-term care
provider to a more  diverse  services  provider,  most  notably  in  the
post-secondary education, adult day services and aging supports; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Additionally,  Paul  C.  Landers  has been instrumental in
Pathfinder's  evolution  as  a  community  through  expanded  integrated
connections,  both  on  and  off-campus,  and  strengthened  strategic
community partnerships; and
 
  WHEREAS, During his tenure as President and  CEO,  Paul  C.  Landers
served  with  loyalty,  honor  and  distinction, earning the admiration,
esteem and affection of his colleagues; and
 
  WHEREAS, In his well-deserved retirement, Paul C. Landers  plans  to
spend  more  time  with  his  family, especially his five grandchildren,
while continuing to support Pathfinder Village from the sideline; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed is  the  occurrence  of  such  a  compassionate
blending  of  strength,  leadership,  intellect,  and commitment as that
 
demonstrated by Paul  C.  Landers  over  a  lifetime  of  sacrifice  and
dedication to others; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Paul C. Landers upon the occasion of his  retirement  after
40  years  of devoted service to the field of disabilities services; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Paul C. Landers.
