# Provides benefits to Metro-North employees involved in or who witness a serious injury or fatality of another person in the course of an assigned duty

**Bill ID:** A6408
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steven Otis
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides benefits to Metro-North employees involved in or who witness a serious injury or fatality of another person in the course of an assigned duty.
