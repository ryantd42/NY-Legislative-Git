# Relates to including a representative of the drug or medical device manufacturer industry on the state board of pharmacy

**Bill ID:** A2484
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that there shall be a representative of the drug or medical device manufacturer industry on the state board of pharmacy.
