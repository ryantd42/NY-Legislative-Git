# Establishes the John J. Marchi government studies scholarship program for certain CUNY students

**Bill ID:** S4656
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the John J. Marchi government studies scholarship program for CUNY students enrolled in government or government related studies.
