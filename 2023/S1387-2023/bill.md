# Requires cable companies to charge the same rate for the same service

**Bill ID:** S1387
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1387 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1387)

## Summary

Requires cable companies to charge the same rate for the same service to different subscribers.

  Section  1. Subdivision 1 of section 225 of the public service law, as

  1. Except as otherwise provided in this section, and  by  federal  law
