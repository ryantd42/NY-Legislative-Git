# Relates to the crime of resisting arrest

**Bill ID:** A3584
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Josh Jensen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the crime of resisting arrest; provides that a second violation within 5 years shall be a class E felony.
