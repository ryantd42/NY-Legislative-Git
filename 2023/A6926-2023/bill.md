# Relates to the calculation of voting power for committee members in districts that have changed or new districts

**Bill ID:** A6926
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the calculation of voting power for committee members in districts that have changed or new districts since the last preceding gubernatorial election.
