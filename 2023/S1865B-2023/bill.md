# Requires an education campaign about the 9-8-8 suicide and crisis lifeline

**Bill ID:** S1865BB
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Samra Brouk
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [S1865B PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1865B)

## Summary

Requires student identification cards to contain the new national suicide prevention lifeline number, 9-8-8, and the crisis text line.
