# Establishes the fibroid study fund

**Bill ID:** A545
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Latoya Joyner
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [A545 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A545)

## Summary

Establishes the fibroid study fund to expend moneys to the department of health to study fibroids, the treatment of fibroids, potential barriers to the access of treatment for fibroids, and differences in symptoms and access to treatment for women of color.
