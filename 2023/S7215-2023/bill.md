# Permits special education services and programs for preschool children with handicapping conditions to establish reserve funds

**Bill ID:** S7215
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Permits special education services and programs for preschool children with handicapping conditions to establish reserve funds.
