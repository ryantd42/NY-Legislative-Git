# Provides that written notice of premium rate changes be given not less than sixty days prior to the effective date of such rate changes

**Bill ID:** A2018
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stephen Hawley
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides written notice of premium rate changes not less than sixty days prior to the effective date of such rate changes.
