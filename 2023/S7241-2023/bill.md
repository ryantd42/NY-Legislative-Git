# Requires certain health care providers to disclose the fact that the provider is on probation to current and new patients

**Bill ID:** S7241
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Liz Krueger
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires certain health care providers to disclose the fact that the provider is on probation to current and new patients.
