# Provides that conviction of assault on a social services worker which prevents him or her from performing a lawful duty is a felony

**Bill ID:** S4687
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides that conviction of assault on a social services worker which prevents him or her from performing a lawful duty is a felony.
