# Allows for mortgagors to cancel or renegotiate forbearance agreements made during a state disaster emergency

**Bill ID:** S6987
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Allows for mortgagors to cancel or renegotiate forbearance agreements made during a state disaster emergency.
