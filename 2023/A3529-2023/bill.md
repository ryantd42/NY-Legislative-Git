# Authorizes certain vehicle registration refunds to be refunded on a prorated monthly basis

**Bill ID:** A3529
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes vehicle registration refunds for the registration period commencing after the first twelve month period of such registration to be refunded on a prorated monthly basis.
