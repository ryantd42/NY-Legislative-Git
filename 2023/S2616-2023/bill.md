# Establishes reduced residential rates for electric and natural gas service to furloughed or non-pay federal workers

**Bill ID:** S2616
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes reduced residential rates for electric and natural gas service to furloughed or non-pay federal workers.
