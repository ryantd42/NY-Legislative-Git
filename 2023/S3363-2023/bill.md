# Relates to HIV testing in certain criminal cases

**Bill ID:** S3363
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to the time when court ordered HIV testing in certain criminal cases must be conducted.
