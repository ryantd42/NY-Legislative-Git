# Relates to drivers of buses, taxis and livery vehicles using hands-free mobile telephones

**Bill ID:** A2874
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits drivers of a bus, taxi or livery from using hands-free mobile telephones; provides for certain exceptions.
