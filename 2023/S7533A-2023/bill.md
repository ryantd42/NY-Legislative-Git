# Relates to the Nourish New York program

**Bill ID:** S7533AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Permits the Nourish New York program to distribute certain funds without an application or competitive procurement process.

  1. The commissioner shall, to the extent permitted by state or federal
 appropriations for such purpose,  facilitate  programming  that  ensures

 BE  DISTRIBUTED  EQUALLY TO FOOD BANKS WITHIN SUCH REGION. REGIONAL FOOD
