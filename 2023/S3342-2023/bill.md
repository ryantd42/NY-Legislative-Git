# Relates to providing residential and day services to certain individuals with a developmental disability

**Bill ID:** S3342
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides residential and day services to certain individuals with a developmental disability where such services are provided by a certified integrated residential community program.
