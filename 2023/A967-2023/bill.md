# Relates to notice of certain proceedings to convey title to abandoned commercial and industrial real property to a city, town, or village; repealer

**Bill ID:** A967
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A967 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A967)

## Summary

Repeals alternative notice provisions for certain proceedings to convey title to abandoned commercial and industrial real property to a city, town, or village.
