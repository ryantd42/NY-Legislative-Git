# Establishes a plan setting forth an itemized list for a certain appropriation for the 2022-23 state fiscal year additional grants in aid to certain not-for-profit arts and cultural organizations

**Bill ID:** R1399
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1399
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  AMENDING  Senate  Resolution  R2859  of  2022
  establishing a plan setting forth an  itemized  list
  of  grantees  for  a  certain  appropriation for the
  2022-2023 state fiscal year additional grants in aid
  to  certain  not-for-profit  arts  and  cultural
  organizations,  as  required by a plan setting forth
  an itemized list of grantees with the amount  to  be
  received  by each, or the methodology for allocating
  such appropriation. Such plan shall  be  subject  to
  the  approval  of  the  temporary  president  of the
  senate and the director of the budget and thereafter
  shall be included in a resolution  calling  for  the
  expenditure of such monies, which resolution must be
  approved  by  a majority vote of all members elected
  to the senate upon a roll call vote
 
  RESOLVED, That pursuant to and as required by money appropriated  in
section  1  of  chapter  53  of the laws of 2022 which enacts the aid to
localities, local  assistance  account,  additional  grants  in  aid  to
certain not-for-profit arts and cultural organizations. Such funds shall
be  apportioned  pursuant  to  a  plan setting forth an itemized list of
grantees with the amount to be received by each, or the methodology  for
allocating  such  appropriation.  Such  plan  shall  be  subject  to the
approval of the temporary president of the senate and  the  director  of
the  budget and thereafter shall be included in a resolution calling for
the expenditure of such monies, which resolution must be approved  by  a
majority  vote  of  all  members  elected to the senate upon a roll call
vote, in accordance with the following schedule and as adopted by Senate
