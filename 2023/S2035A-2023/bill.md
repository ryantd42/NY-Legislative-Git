# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the Samuel Trombley Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** S2035AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2035A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2035A)

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in the town of Ellenburg, county of Clinton as the "Samuel Trombley Memorial Bridge".
