# Relates to restrictions and penalties for certain transactions involving used catalytic converters

**Bill ID:** S1560
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1560 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1560)

## Summary

Relates to restrictions and penalties for certain transactions involving used catalytic converters; establishes penalties for possessing, selling or buying a catalytic converter without a permit or proper documentation; defines terms.
