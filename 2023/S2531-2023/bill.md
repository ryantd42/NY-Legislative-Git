# Relates to the transport or delivery of wine pursuant to a farm distillery license

**Bill ID:** S2531
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Permits the transport or delivery of wine pursuant to a farm winery license in a vehicle owned by an employee of the licensee.
