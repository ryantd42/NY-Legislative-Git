# Relates to exempting the registration of vintage snowmobiles

**Bill ID:** S1919
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1919 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1919)

## Summary

Exempts vintage snowmobiles from registration requirements.
