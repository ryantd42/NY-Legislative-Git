# Creates the offense of fostering the sale of stolen goods

**Bill ID:** S1644
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1644 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1644)

## Summary

Creates the offense of fostering the sale of stolen goods.

 ยง 165.66 FOSTERING THE SALE OF STOLEN GOODS.
  A PERSON IS GUILTY OF FOSTERING THE SALE OF STOLEN GOODS WHEN:

 OF STOLEN GOODS.
  FOSTERING THE SALE OF STOLEN GOODS IS A CLASS A MISDEMEANOR.
