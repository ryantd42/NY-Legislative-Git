# Requires the public school curriculum for children in kindergarten through grade twelve to include a minimum of two hours per week instruction in civics lessons

**Bill ID:** A2091
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nily Rozic
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the public school curriculum for children in kindergarten through grade twelve to include a minimum of two hours per week instruction in civics lessons.
