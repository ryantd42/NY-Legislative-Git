# Exempts daycare facility and childcare facility employers from providing for COVID-19 sick leave

**Bill ID:** A6407
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Karl Brabenec
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Exempts daycare facility and childcare facility employers from providing for COVID-19 sick leave.
