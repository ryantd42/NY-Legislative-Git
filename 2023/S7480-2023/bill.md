# Extends provisions of law relating to the Terence Cardinal Cooke health care center

**Bill ID:** S7480
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** José M. Serrano
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends provisions of law relating to the Terence Cardinal Cooke health care center until December 31, 2028.
