# Relates to eligibility for certain New York city transit authority members changing to transit operating positions from other positions to participate in the twenty-five-year and age fifty-five retirement program

**Bill ID:** A7719
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides eligibility for certain New York city transit authority members changing to transit operating positions from other positions to participate in the twenty-five-year and age fifty-five retirement program.
