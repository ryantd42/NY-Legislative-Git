# Provides for the appointment of 4 more members to the board of the city of Yonkers parking authority

**Bill ID:** A3546
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the appointment of 6 more members to the board of the city of Yonkers parking authority.
