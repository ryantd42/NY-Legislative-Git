# Relates to the display of blue and green lights on certain motor vehicles

**Bill ID:** S4523
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pamela Helming
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes that one or more green or blue lights may be displayed on certain motor vehicles.
