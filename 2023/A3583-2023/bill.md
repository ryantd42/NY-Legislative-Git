# Excludes environmental contamination from consideration as a factor for real property assessment

**Bill ID:** A3583
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Excludes environmental contamination from consideration as a factor for assessment.
