# Relates to the effectiveness of certain curriculum requirements curriculum related to human trafficking awareness to be included in alcohol training awareness programs

**Sponsor:** James Skoufis

**PDF:** [S826 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S826)
