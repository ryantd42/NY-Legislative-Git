# Requires shelter allowances be set at up to one hundred percent of the fair market rent for the local social services district

**Bill ID:** S2982
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires shelter allowances be set at up to one hundred percent of the fair market rent for the local social services district.
