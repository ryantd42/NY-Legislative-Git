# Requires a study of the fiscal impacts of changing education funding from a real property tax-based mechanism to an income tax-based mechanism

**Bill ID:** A3799
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires a study of the fiscal impacts of changing education funding from a real property tax-based mechanism to an income tax-based mechanism.
