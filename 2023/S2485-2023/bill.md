# Relates to requiring certain land development or construction projects to be reevaluated through a land use review process

**Bill ID:** S2485
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires any land development or construction project approved one year prior to the COVID-19 pandemic to be reevaluated through a land use review process if work has not been commenced on such project.

 ation  and addressing any adverse effects that the COVID-19 pandemic has

 not considered during the initial approval process.
