# Relates to informal caregiver training

**Bill ID:** A1845
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Ron Kim
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to informal caregiver training; defines "person in need of assistance"; expands who may serve as an "informal caregiver".
