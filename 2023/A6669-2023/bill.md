# Provides an insurance premium reduction for landscape businesses whose employees complete a safety course for the landscape industry

**Bill ID:** A6669
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Albert A. Stirpe
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides an insurance premium reduction for landscape businesses whose employees complete a safety course for the landscape industry.
