# Authorizes a special account to create safe city streets, safe city, safe schools program in city of Yonkers

**Bill ID:** A3212
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the city of Yonkers to establish a separate fund into which revenue earmarked for the safe city streets, safe streets, safe schools program will be deposited.
