# Requires a custodial parent who lives with a sex offender to provide that information to a non-custodial parent

**Bill ID:** A4262
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Lemondes
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires a custodial parent who lives with a sex offender to provide that information to a non-custodial parent.
