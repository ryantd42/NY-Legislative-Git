# Establishes the metropolitan commuter transportation district fee tax credit for persons required to pay such fee

**Bill ID:** A3807
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the metropolitan commuter transportation district fee tax credit for persons required to pay such fee.
