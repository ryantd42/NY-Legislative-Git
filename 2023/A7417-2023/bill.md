# Decreases the distance from the high water mark certain lands under water of Gardiner's and Peconic bays have to be to be ceded to a county

**Bill ID:** A7417
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Decreases the distance from the high water mark certain lands under water of Gardiner's and Peconic bays have to be ceded to a county from one thousand feet to five hundred feet.
