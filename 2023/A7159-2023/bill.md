# Prohibits the eviction of tenants for using medical marihuana for a certified medical use

**Bill ID:** A7159
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the eviction of tenants for using medical marihuana for a certified medical use.
