# Prohibits the manufacture, distribution and sale of children's foam mats containing formamide

**Bill ID:** S2983
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the manufacture, distribution and sale of children's foam mats containing formamide.

 AN ACT to amend the  environmental  conservation  law,  in  relation  to

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Article 37 of the environmental conservation law is amended
 by adding a new title 4 to read as follows:
  TITLE IV
  FORMAMIDE
 SECTION 37-0401. SHORT TITLE.
  37-0403. DEFINITIONS.
  37-0405. FOAM MAT PRODUCTS.
  37-0407. PREEMPTION.
  37-0409. RULES AND REGULATIONS.
 § 37-0401. SHORT TITLE.
  THIS  TITLE  SHALL  BE  KNOWN  AND MAY BE CITED AS THE "FORMAMIDE-FREE
 CHILDREN'S PRODUCT ACT".
 § 37-0403. DEFINITIONS.
  AS USED IN THIS TITLE: (A) "FOAM MAT PRODUCT" MEANS  ALL  MATS,  PADS,
 MATTRESSES  AND CUSHIONS DESIGNED FOR USE BY CHILDREN THREE YEARS OLD OR
 YOUNGER, INCLUDING MATS, PADS, MATTRESSES AND CUSHIONS  USED  AS  OR  IN
 CONNECTION  WITH  BEDDING,  CHANGING PLATFORMS, STROLLERS AND CARRIAGES,
 HIGH CHAIRS, CAR SEATS, PLAY PENS AND TOYS.
  (B) "FORMAMIDE" MEANS THE CHEMICAL IDENTIFIED BY CAS  NUMBER  75-12-7,
 INCLUDING METHANAMIDE AND CARBAMALDEHYDE.
 § 37-0405. FOAM MAT PRODUCTS.
  1.  BEGINNING  DECEMBER  FIRST,  TWO  THOUSAND TWENTY-FIVE, NO PERSON,
 FIRM, PARTNERSHIP, ASSOCIATION, LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY OR CORPORATION

 SHALL SELL OR OFFER FOR SALE IN THIS STATE ANY FOAM MAT PRODUCT CONTAIN-
 ING FORMAMIDE.
  2.  THE  PROVISIONS  OF  THIS  SECTION  SHALL NOT APPLY TO THE SALE OR
 DISTRIBUTION OF FOAM MAT PRODUCTS  RESOLD  OR  OFFERED  FOR  RESALE,  OR
 DISTRIBUTED BY CONSUMERS FOR CONSUMER USE.
 § 37-0407. PREEMPTION.
  JURISDICTION  IN  ALL  MATTERS PERTAINING TO FORMAMIDE WHICH ARE REGU-

 AS IT SHALL DEEM NECESSARY TO IMPLEMENT THE PROVISIONS OF THIS TITLE.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
