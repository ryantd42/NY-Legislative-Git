# Relates to the eligibility of enrolled members of Southampton Village Ocean Rescue for the tax exemption on real property authorized by section 466-a of the real property tax law

**Bill ID:** S2158A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Provides that enrolled members of Southampton Village Ocean Rescue shall be eligible for the tax exemption on real property authorized by section 466-c of the real property tax law.
