# Requires continuing medical education for physicians

**Bill ID:** A566
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A566 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A566)

## Summary

Requires physicians who are re-registering for their license to complete fifty hours of continuing medical education.
