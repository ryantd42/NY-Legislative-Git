# Authorizes a community member or community ambassador to apply for involuntary admission of a person alleged to be mentally ill

**Sponsor:** Jodi Giglio

## Summary

Authorizes a community member or community ambassador to apply for involuntary admission of a person alleged to be mentally ill.
