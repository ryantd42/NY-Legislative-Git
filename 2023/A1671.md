# Requires the department of motor vehicles to approve certain out-of-state accident prevention courses

**Bill ID:** A1671
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** David Weprin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1671 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1671)

## Summary

Requires the DMV to promulgate rules and regulations to approve certain out-of-state or national accident prevention courses.
