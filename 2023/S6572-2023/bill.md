# Extends the authorization of the county of Cayuga to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S6572
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Cayuga to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes to 11/30/2025.
