# Prohibits the imposition of different premium rates for disability insurance based on gender

**Bill ID:** A3781
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the imposition of different premium rates for disability insurance based on gender.
