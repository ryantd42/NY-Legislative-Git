# Relates to the stock transfer tax

**Bill ID:** A2613
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Erik Dilan
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs a percentage of the stock transfer tax to the metropolitan transportation authority special assistance fund.
