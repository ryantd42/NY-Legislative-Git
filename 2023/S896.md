# Relates to the use of campaign funds for attorney's fees

**Bill ID:** S896
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S896 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S896)

## Summary

Prohibits the use of campaign funds to pay attorney's fees for certain actions brought against a candidate or public official.
