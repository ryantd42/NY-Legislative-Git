# Relates to the accessibility of congressional, senatorial, assembly and election district maps

**Bill ID:** S852
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [S852 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S852)

## Summary

Relates to the accessibility of congressional, senatorial, assembly and election district maps in downloadable digital file formats compatible with geographic information (GIS) software.
