# Relates to metropolitan commuter transportation district registration fee exemptions for farm vehicles

**Bill ID:** A2637
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Removes the requirement that farm vehicles pay the metropolitan commuter transportation district (MCTD) supplemental registration fee.
