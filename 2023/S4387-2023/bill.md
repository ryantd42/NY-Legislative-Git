# Designates the animal control officer in the town of Sand Lake as a peace officer

**Bill ID:** S4387
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Designates the animal control officer in the town of Sand Lake as a peace officer.

 AN ACT to amend the criminal procedure law, in relation  to  designating
  the animal control officer in the town of Sand Lake as a peace officer
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Section 2.10 of the criminal procedure law  is  amended  by
 adding a new subdivision 60 to read as follows:
  60.  THE  ANIMAL  CONTROL OFFICER OF THE TOWN OF SAND LAKE, RENSSELAER
 COUNTY, WHO AT THE DISCRETION OF THE TOWN BOARD OF THE TOWN OF SAND LAKE
 MAY BE DESIGNATED AS A CONSTABLE FOR THE PURPOSE  OF  ENFORCING  ARTICLE
 TWENTY-SIX  OF  THE  AGRICULTURE AND MARKETS LAW, AND FOR THE PURPOSE OF
 ISSUING APPEARANCE TICKETS PERMITTED UNDER ARTICLE SEVEN  OF  SUCH  LAW;
 PROVIDED,  HOWEVER,  THAT NOTHING IN THIS SUBDIVISION SHALL BE DEEMED TO
 AUTHORIZE SUCH OFFICER TO CARRY, POSSESS, REPAIR OR DISPOSE OF A FIREARM
 UNLESS THE APPROPRIATE LICENSE THEREFOR  HAS  BEEN  ISSUED  PURSUANT  TO
 SECTION 400.00 OF THE PENAL LAW.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
