# Relates to the powers and duties of the comptroller to correct certain administrative errors

**Bill ID:** S575
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S575 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S575)

## Summary

Provides a corrective remedy for certain administrative errors, omissions or inaccuracies relating to membership and benefits under the retirement system.
