# Clarifies that making a referral of alleged improper conduct of a police officer to district attorney within 48 hours of conduct is not an improper employer practice

**Bill ID:** A647
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A647 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A647)

## Summary

Provides that it shall not be an improper practice for a public employer to immediately refer to the appropriate district attorney any issue regarding the alleged improper conduct of a police officer within forty-eight hours of such conduct.
