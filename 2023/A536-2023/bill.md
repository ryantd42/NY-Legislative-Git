# Relates to prohibiting the use of wastewater from high-volume hydraulic fracturing for road and land spreading

**Bill ID:** A536
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A536 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A536)

## Summary

Prohibits the use of wastewater from high-volume hydraulic fracturing for road and land spreading, for dust control or de-icing.
