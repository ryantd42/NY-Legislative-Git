# Repeals the Empire state film production credit and the Empire state film post production credit

**Bill ID:** S1134
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1134 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1134)

## Summary

Repeals the Empire state film production credit and the Empire state film post production credit.
