# Amends Assembly Rule III relating to introduction

**Bill ID:** E225
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** Lost

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 225
 
BY: M. of A. Barclay
 
  amending section 2 of Rule III of the Assembly
  Rules, in relation to the timely bill introduction
 
  RESOLVED, That subdivision f  of  section  2  of  Rule  III  of  the
Assembly  Rules  is  amended  by  adding  a  new  paragraph 3 to read as
follows:
 
  (3) Any Assembly bill introduced shall be  assigned  a  number  within
five  business days after being presented by the introducer to the Index
Clerk.
