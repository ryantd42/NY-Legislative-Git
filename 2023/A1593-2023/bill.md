# Relates to distribution of a decedent's estate

**Bill ID:** A1593
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1593 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1593)

## Summary

Prohibits a distributive share from passing to a person convicted of committing or attempting to commit a sex offense.
