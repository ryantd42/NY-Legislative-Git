# Requires standards for the new construction of electric vehicle charging stations to require such stations to have an external cutoff switch

**Bill ID:** A7510A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Christopher Eachus
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires standards for the new construction of electric vehicle charging stations to require such stations to have an external automatic cutoff switch.

 AN  ACT  to  amend the executive law, in relation to requiring standards
  for the new construction of  electric  vehicle  charging  stations  to
  require such stations to have an external automatic cutoff switch

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Section 378 of the executive law is amended by adding a new
 subdivision 21 to read as follows:
  21. STANDARDS REQUIRING NEW CONSTRUCTION OF ELECTRIC VEHICLE,  INCLUD-
 ING  BICYCLES  WITH  ELECTRIC  ASSIST  AND  ELECTRIC  SCOOTERS, CHARGING
 STATIONS TO INCLUDE AN EXTERNAL AUTOMATIC CUTOFF SWITCH.  AN  AUTOMATIC
 CUTOFF  SWITCH  SHALL  BE  A  HAND-OPERATED  SWITCH  WHICH IS CAPABLE OF
 CUTTING OFF ALL POWER AND ELECTRICITY TO THE VEHICLE, BICYCLE OR SCOOTER
 WITHOUT THE NEED OF A KEY OR TO UNLOCK  THE  DEVICE.  FOR  RESIDENTIAL
 ELECTRIC  VEHICLE  CHARGING STATIONS, SUCH CUTOFF SWITCH SHALL BE PLACED
 OUTSIDE OF THE HOUSE OR GARAGE.  FOR BICYCLES WITH ELECTRIC  ASSIST  AND
 ELECTRIC SCOOTERS, SUCH CUTOFF SWITCH SHALL BE LOCATED ON THE OUTSIDE OF
 THE PHYSICAL CHARGING DEVICE AND SHALL BE EASILY ACCESSIBLE.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
