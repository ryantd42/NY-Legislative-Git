# Honoring the New York Wine and Grape Foundation's Gold Medalists on July 12, 2022

**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [K13 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/K13)

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 13
 
BY: M. of A. Thiele
 
  HONORING  the New York Wine and Grape Foundation's
  Gold Medalists on July 12, 2022
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  recognize  the
achievements  of  those  individuals  and  businesses  that  contribute
substantially to the economic vitality  of  their  communities,  thereby
enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
the New York Wine and Grape Foundation's  Gold  Medalists  at  its  36th
Annual New York Wine Classic on Tuesday, July 12, 2022; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  36th  Annual  New  York  Wine Classic is a nationally
recognized competition highlighting the best of New York's wine; and
 
  WHEREAS, Seeing an unprecedented growth over the past few years  and
allowing  wine  enthusiasts  to  enjoy  this unique part of the New York
experience across the state, these extraordinary  vineyards  have  truly
show-cased why New York State is a premier wine destination; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  year's  distinguished Gold Medalists include: McCall
Wines, Clovis Point Vineyard and Winery, Coffee Pot Cellars,  Duck  Walk
Vineyards,  Harbes  Vineyard,  Paumanok  Vineyards, Jamesport Vineyards,
Jason's Vineyard,  Lieb  Cellars,  Pellegrini  Vineyards,  Suhru  Wines,
Osprey's  Dominion, Pindar Vineyards, Sannino Vineyard, Sparkling Pointe
Vineyards and Winery, Palmer Vineyards, and Wolffer Estate Vineyard; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Over  their  distinguished  histories,  these  esteemed
vineyards  have  demonstrated  continuous  service  to the residents and
communities which they serve; and
 
  WHEREAS, The success of these vineyards is in direct correlation  to
the  efforts  of their dedicated owners and staff, whose involvement is,
and always has been,  characterized  by  an  impressive  commitment,  an
unbridled  enthusiasm,  and  an uncompromising standard of excellence in
all endeavors on behalf of  the  businesses  and  the  communities  they
serve; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges these exceptional businesses, their  contributions  to  the
local  and  state  economies,  and  their  vibrant role in improving the
quality of life in their communities, fully confident they will continue
to enjoy the same sparkling successes they have  experienced  thus  far;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor the New York Wine and Grape Foundation's Gold  Medalists  on  July
12, 2022; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  copies  of  this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the aforementioned Gold Medalist Vineyards.
