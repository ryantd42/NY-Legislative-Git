# Directs the office of mental health to develop materials relating to suicide prevention for school-aged children; authorizes school districts to include suicide prevention in health class curriculum

**Bill ID:** S4176
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the office of mental health to develop materials relating to suicide prevention and identifying signs of depression for school-aged children; authorizes school districts to include suicide prevention in health class curriculum.
