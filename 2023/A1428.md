# Prohibits the distribution and sale of certain lasers

**Bill ID:** A1428
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrion Aubry
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1428 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1428)

Relates to prohibiting the distribution and sale of certain lasers.
