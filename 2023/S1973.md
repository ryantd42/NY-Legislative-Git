# Requires dental insurance plans to automatically carry over a portion of the enrollee's benefit amount for use in the succeeding year

**Bill ID:** S1973
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Ramos
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1973 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1973)

## Summary

Requires dental insurance plans to automatically carry over a portion of the enrollee's unused benefit amount of up to 25% of the total benefit amount for use in the succeeding year.
