# Requires professional engineers, land surveyors and professional geologists to report structural faults within forty-eight hours

**Bill ID:** A3098
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jodi Giglio
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires professional engineers, land surveyors and professional geologists to report structural faults within forty-eight hours.
