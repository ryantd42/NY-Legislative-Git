# Expands who can hold membership in an adult-use cooperative license

**Bill ID:** S7504
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Expands who can hold membership in a licensed adult-use cooperative to include persons other than natural persons.
