# Extends current exemptions of income and resources for public assistance programs

**Bill ID:** S6944
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends current exemptions of income and resources for public assistance programs under the welfare reform act of 1997.
