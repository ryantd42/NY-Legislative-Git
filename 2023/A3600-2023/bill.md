# Relates to noncommercial aviation

**Bill ID:** A3600
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Manktelow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that there is no duty to keep premises safe for persons engaging in noncommercial aviation.
