# Provides for a tax credit for certain persons relocating to the state to provide or receive reproductive care or gender-affirming care

**Bill ID:** S6637
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for a tax credit for certain persons relocating to the state to provide or receive reproductive care or gender-affirming care.
