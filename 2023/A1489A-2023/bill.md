# Relates to delinquent tax interest rates

**Bill ID:** A1489A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Fred Thiele
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Relates to setting a minimum and maximum delinquent tax interest rate for payments due on residential real property.
