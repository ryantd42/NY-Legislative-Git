 AN ACT to amend the state finance law and the general municipal law,  in
  relation  to  contractual  relationships with minority and women-owned

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1.  Section  139-f  of  the state finance law is amended by
 adding a new subdivision 5 to read as follows:
  5. ANY CONTRACTOR WHO SUBCONTRACTS AND/OR HAS  A  JOINT  VENTURE  WITH
 MINORITY  AND  WOMEN-OWNED BUSINESSES ON A PUBLIC WORK PROJECT AND PRIOR
 TO COMPLETION OF SUCH PROJECT  THE  CONTRACTOR  CEASES  ITS  CONTRACTUAL
 RELATIONSHIP  WITH ANY TO ALL MINORITY AND WOMEN-OWNED BUSINESSES, HE OR
 SHE SHALL REPORT SUCH CHANGE IN RELATIONSHIP TO THE PUBLIC OWNER OF SUCH
 PROJECT AND THE DIVISION OF MINORITY AND WOMEN'S  BUSINESS  DEVELOPMENT.
 FAILURE  TO  REPORT  WITHIN  SEVEN  CALENDAR DAYS SHALL RESULT IN A FINE
 DETERMINED BY THE DIRECTOR OF THE DIVISION OF MINORITY AND WOMEN'S BUSI-
 NESS DEVELOPMENT.
