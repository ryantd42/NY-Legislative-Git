# Commemorating the 50th Anniversary of the SUNY Westchester Educational Opportunity Center

**Bill ID:** J1200
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrea Stewart-Cousins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1200
 
BY: Senator STEWART-COUSINS
 
  COMMEMORATING  the  50th  Anniversary  of the SUNY
  Westchester Educational Opportunity Center
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  and
honor  those  commemorative historical events which symbolize the growth
and development of  the  educational  institutions  which  comprise  the
communities of this great State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  50th  Anniversary  of  the  Westchester  Educational
Opportunity Center, to be celebrated on Thursday, June 8, 2023,  at  the
Graduation  &  Completion  Ceremony  held  by SUNY Westchester Community
College in Yonkers, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Westchester EOC provides access to  quality  education  and
serves  as  the  center of influence for training and career development
for students and the community; they  are  committed  to  fostering  the
personal  growth, intellectual development, and economic independence of
students while supporting the economic revitalization and well-being  of
the community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Providing  a  comprehensive  array  of  tuition-free
developmental,  educational,  and  vocational  training  programs  for
eligible  adults  in  a  supportive environment, Westchester EOC enables
students to obtain the skills and credentials they need to pursue higher
education and secure career-oriented employment; and
 
  WHEREAS, Westchester EOC's vocational program offerings are designed
to prepare  students  for  careers  in  healthcare,  business,  and  the
knowledge  economy with select hands-on skills; in addition to receiving
occupational-skills  training  for  specific  careers,  many  of  their
vocational  students  also  obtain  industry  or state certifications in
their field of study; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Offering  courses  meant  to  accommodate  working-adult
schedules,  Westchester  EOC's  language  and  foundational  education
programs are  designed  to  improve  student's  basic  skills  in  math,
reading,  writing  and  critical  thinking;  students  who  successfully
complete  these  classes  can  typically  enroll  in  vocational  or
more-advanced academic programs; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Westchester  EOC's  College  Prep  Academy  helps  prepare
students for the college placement  exam,  to  minimize  the  number  of
developmental  or  remedial  classes  a  student  might  need  once they
register  for  college;  furthermore,  they  provide  college  tours,
workshops,  and guidance to help make the transition to higher education
easier and more successful; and
 
  WHEREAS, Partnering with well-established and significant employers,
other educational institutions and training  providers,  community-based
organizations,  and state and local government agencies, Westchester EOC
provides  internships  and  externships  for  their  students,  offer
 
customized  training,  and  refer  trained  and  screened candidates for
available job openings; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Serving  as  a  resource  on various workforce development
issues in the County, Westchester EOC provides workshops at a variety of
public forums and venues relating to public policy, federal legislation,
and the needs of underserved populations; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Steeped  in  a  proud  and  distinguished  history,  the
Westchester Educational Opportunity Center may look back with pride upon
its  humble  beginnings, find just satisfaction in its purposeful growth
and hold true of purpose in its commitment to  serving  the  educational
needs of the citizens of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to join with the
Westchester Educational Opportunity Center in  the  celebration  of  its
50th  Anniversary,  recognizing  on  this  auspicious  occasion that the
quality and dignity of life are empowered by the faithful  and  diligent
efforts of such noble institutions of learning; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the 50th Anniversary of  the  SUNY  Westchester  Educational
Opportunity Center; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the SUNY Westchester Educational Opportunity Center.
