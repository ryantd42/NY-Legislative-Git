# Relates to increasing the cap on earnings for eligible individuals with no dependents receiving tuition assistance program awards

**Bill ID:** S2578
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the cap on earnings for eligible individuals with no dependents receiving tuition assistance program awards from ten thousand to thirty thousand dollars.
