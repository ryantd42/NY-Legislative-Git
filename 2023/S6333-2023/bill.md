# Relates to childcare and dependent care reimbursement for volunteer members of public bodies

**Bill ID:** S6333
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Samra Brouk
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for reimbursement of expenses for childcare and dependent care for volunteer members of public bodies.
