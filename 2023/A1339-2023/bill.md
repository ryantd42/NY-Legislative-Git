# Provides that only products containing milk may use the term "milk product" on labels and advertisements

**Bill ID:** A1339
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1339 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1339)

## Summary

Provides that only products containing milk may use the term "milk product" on labels and advertisements; defines "milk product".
