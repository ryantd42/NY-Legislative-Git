# Directs the public service commission to prohibit the attachment of wireless equipment or any other like attachments to existing utility poles in certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** A3054
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Edward Ra
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs the public service commission to prohibit the attachment of wireless equipment or any other like attachments to existing utility poles in certain circumstances.

 AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to  prohibiting  the
  attachment  of wireless equipment and other like attachments to exist-
  ing utility poles in certain circumstances

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1.  Section  119-a  of  the  public service law is amended by
 adding a new subdivision 4-a is added to read as follows:
  4-A. THE COMMISSION SHALL PROHIBIT THE ATTACHMENT OF  WIRELESS  BROAD-
 CAST  OR  RELAY EQUIPMENT, OR ANY OTHER LIKE ATTACHMENTS, TO ANY UTILITY
 POLE WHICH WAS INSTALLED ON, BEFORE OR AFTER THE EFFECTIVE DATE OF  THIS
 SUBDIVISION,  OR  UNLESS  AN  EXISTING EASEMENT IS EXPLICITLY AMENDED IN
 WRITING AND FILED TO INCLUDE WIRELESS BROADCAST OR RELAY  EQUIPMENT,  OR
 ANY  OTHER  LIKE ATTACHMENTS TO A UTILITY POLE, OR UNLESS A NEW EASEMENT
 IS WRITTEN AND FILED TO ALLOW THE ATTACHMENT OF  WIRELESS  BROADCAST  OR
 RELAY EQUIPMENT, OR ANY OTHER LIKE ATTACHMENTS, TO A UTILITY POLE.  SUCH
 AMENDMENTS  TO  AN  EXISTING EASEMENT OR A NEW EASEMENT SHALL NOT AFFECT
 ANY ADJOINING PROPERTY OR PROPERTY OWNERS.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
