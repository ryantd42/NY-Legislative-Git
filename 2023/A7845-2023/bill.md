# Relates to establishing a program of health care quality innovation and improvement through home care

**Bill ID:** A7845
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Cunningham
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a program of health care quality innovation and improvement through home care.
