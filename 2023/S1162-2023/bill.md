# Provides a religious exemption for the requirement to have a photograph taken for a driver's license or learner's permit

**Bill ID:** S1162
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1162 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1162)

## Summary

Provides a religious exemption for the requirement to have a photograph taken for a driver's license or learner's permit.

 AN  ACT to amend the vehicle and traffic law, in relation to providing a
  religious exemption for the requirement to have a photograph taken for
  a driver's license or learner's permit

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1.  Section  507 of the vehicle and traffic law is amended by
 adding a new subdivision 6 to read as follows:
  6. PHOTOGRAPH RELIGIOUS EXEMPTION. A PERSON SHALL NOT BE  REQUIRED  TO
 HAVE  THEIR  PHOTOGRAPH TAKEN FOR A DRIVER'S LICENSE OR LEARNER'S PERMIT
 WHEN SUCH PERSON HOLDS GENUINE AND SINCERE RELIGIOUS BELIEFS  WHICH  ARE
 CONTRARY  TO HAVING A PHOTOGRAPH TAKEN; PROVIDED, HOWEVER, THAT LICENSES
 ISSUED WITHOUT A PHOTOGRAPH PURSUANT TO THIS SUBDIVISION  SHALL  NOT  BE
 USED FOR PROOF OF AGE REQUIRED FOR ENTRY TO AN ESTABLISHMENT OR PURCHASE
 OF AGE-RESTRICTED PRODUCTS.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
