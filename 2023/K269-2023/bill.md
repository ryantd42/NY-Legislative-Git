# Commemorating the 148th Anniversary of the Town of Wappingers, New York, on May 20, 2023

**Bill ID:** K269
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 269
 
BY: M. of A. Beephan
 
  COMMEMORATING the 148th Anniversary of the Town of
  Wappingers, New York, on May 20, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of this Legislative Body to honor and
commemorate  the  distinguished  histories  of  the  communities  which
comprise the noble body of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  148th Anniversary of the Town of Wappingers, New York,
on Saturday, May 20, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, Established in 1875 and formed from part of  Fishkill,  the
Town  of  Wappingers was named after the Wappinger Native Americans that
used to reside here; in its early days, Wappingers was a mill town which
used the Wappingers Creek to help run its many factories; and
 
  WHEREAS, Today, the Town of Wappingers offers  two  marinas  on  the
Hudson  River,  and the Wappingers Central School District is the second
biggest school district in New York, consisting of the Wappingers Junior
High School, Van Wyck Middle School and Roy C. Ketcham High School; and
 
  WHEREAS, Of historic origin, and remaining fruitful over the ebb and
flow of decades of growth and change, the Town of  Wappingers  continues
to provide the quintessential quality of life for its residents; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  residents  of  the  Town  of Wappingers have a bright
outlook for the future of their community,  which  remains  a  beautiful
place in which to live and raise a family; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In recognition of the Town of Wappingers' rich history and
enduring contribution to the State of New York, this Legislative Body is
proud to pay tribute to this spirited town  upon  the  occasion  of  its
235th Anniversary; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the 148th Anniversary of the Town of Wappingers,  New  York,
recognizing  the  significance  of  the role it continues to play in the
life of the community of the State of New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Town of Wappingers, New York.
