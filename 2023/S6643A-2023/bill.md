# Increases the amount of money furnished to incarcerated individuals upon release

**Bill ID:** S6643AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for eligible incarcerated individuals to receive a monthly stipend upon release from a state correctional facility.
