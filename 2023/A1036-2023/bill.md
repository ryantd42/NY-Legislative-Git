# Requires managed care providers that provide coverage for prescription drugs to provide coverage for home delivery of any covered prescription drug

**Bill ID:** A1036
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rodneyse Bichotte Hermelyn
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1036 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1036)

## Summary

Requires managed care providers that provide coverage for prescription drugs to provide coverage for home delivery of any covered prescription by any non-mail-order retail pharmacy in the managed care provider network.
