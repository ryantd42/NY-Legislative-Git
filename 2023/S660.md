# Relates to the qualifications of voters and civic education of students

**Bill ID:** S660
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S660 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S660)

## Summary

Lowers the voting age to 16 years; requires that students receive education in civics and be given the opportunity to register to vote in the classroom.
