# Relates to increasing the frequency that visitation or inspections of facilities operated or licensed by the office for people with developmental disabilities must be made each year

**Bill ID:** S2651
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Increases the frequency that visitation or inspections of facilities operated or licensed by the office for people with developmental disabilities must be made each year.
