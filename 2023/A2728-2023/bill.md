# Offers installment payment plans as an option for persons with violations of toll collection regulations

**Bill ID:** A2728
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Offers installment payment plans as an option for persons with violations of toll collection regulations before having their registration suspended.
