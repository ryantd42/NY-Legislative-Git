# Limits tinted green and brown to compostable plastic bags for the purposes of bag waste reduction

**Bill ID:** A4144
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that bags exempt from bag waste reduction shall not be tinted green or brown unless it is a compostable plastic bag.
