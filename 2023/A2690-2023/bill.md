# Prohibits certain children from riding on a motorcycle

**Bill ID:** A2690
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits children under the age of twelve from riding on a motorcycle.
