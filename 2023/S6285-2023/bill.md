# Relates to cemetery trust funds

**Bill ID:** S6285
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Relates to cemetery trust funds and the maintenance and preservation of cemetery grounds.
