# Grants the metropolitan transportation authority the power to suspend vehicle registration in certain cases

**Bill ID:** A3672
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Grants the metropolitan transportation authority the power to suspend vehicle registration in certain cases.
