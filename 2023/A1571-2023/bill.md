# Relates to requiring antique, classic or collectors cars be inspected for safety biennially instead of annually

**Bill ID:** A1571
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1571 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1571)

## Summary

Relates to requiring that antique, classic or collectors cars be inspected for safety biennially instead of annually.
