# Authorizes the city of Rochester to convey by appropriate instruments to the Rochester Pure Waters District, an easement in Genesee Valley Park, for installation and maintenance of sanitary sewer facilities

**Bill ID:** S6610AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeremy Cooney
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Authorizes the city of Rochester to convey by appropriate instruments to the Rochester Pure Waters District, an easement in Genesee Valley Park, for installation and maintenance of sanitary sewer facilities.

 AN  ACT  in  relation  to authorizing the city of Rochester to convey by
  appropriate instruments to the  Rochester  Pure  Waters  District,  an
  easement  in  Genesee Valley Park, for installation and maintenance of
  sanitary sewer facilities
 
  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section  1. The city of Rochester, acting by and through its governing
 body, is hereby authorized to convey by appropriate instruments  to  the
 Rochester  Pure  Waters District, a permanent easement in Genesee Valley
 Park described in section three of this act, for the purpose of enabling
 such water district to construct, operate, repair, replace, and maintain
 sanitary  sewer  facilities.  Upon  completion  of  any  repairs  or
 construction,  the  Rochester  Pure  Waters  District  shall restore the
 surface of the lands and the lands shall continue to be  used  for  park
 purposes.
  §  2.  The  authorization provided in section one of this act shall be
 effective only upon the condition that the city of  Rochester  dedicates
 an amount equal to or greater than the fair market value of the easement
 interest  being  alienated  pursuant to section one of this act, for the
 acquisition of new parklands and/or  capital  improvements  to  existing
 park and recreational facilities.
  § 3. The easements to be conveyed by the city of Rochester pursuant to
