# Extends provisions of law authorizing the county of Cortland to impose an additional mortgage recording tax

**Bill ID:** S6274
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Lea Webb
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends provisions of law authorizing the county of Cortland to impose an additional mortgage recording tax until December 1, 2025.
