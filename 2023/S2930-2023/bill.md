# Relates to orders not to resuscitate; repealer

**Bill ID:** S2930
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Gustavo Rivera
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Changes the controlling law for orders not to resuscitate for residents of mental hygiene facilities.
