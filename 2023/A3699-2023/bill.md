# Provides for special elections for the offices of comptroller and attorney general for vacancies during a term

**Bill ID:** A3699
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for special elections for the offices of comptroller and attorney general for vacancies during a term.
