# Congratulating Kayuta Drive-In upon the occasion of celebrating its 60th Anniversary on June 24, 2023

**Bill ID:** J935
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 935
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Kayuta  Drive-In upon the occasion
  of celebrating its 60th Anniversary on June 24, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, Within each community of this great State of New York there
are certain businesses that define the nature  of  the  region  and  the
people who inhabit it; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Kayuta  Drive-In,  a  legendary  Oneida  eatery,  has been
serving the families and residents of Central  New  York  for  60  years
thanks  to  a  dedication  to  quality  food  and ice cream, outstanding
customer  service,  and  a  belief  that  customers  will  appreciate  a
family-owned  company  that works hard every day to provide them with an
excellent experience; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Kayuta Drive-In is hosting a celebration  on  Saturday,
June  24,  2023,  at the restaurant with past employees, live music, and
discounted menu items; and
 
  WHEREAS, A family-owned and operated business since its inception in
1963, Kayuta Drive-In was started by James and Carol Minosh, parents  of
Lynn and James Jr.; and
 
  WHEREAS,  James  Minosh  Jr. now owns the family business, providing
the countless patrons who visit each season with the consistent  service
and delicious food they have come to know and love; and
 
  WHEREAS, Kayuta Drive-In is located in Remsen, New York, and is open
May  through  October  each  year, providing the perfect opportunity for
campers and  travelers  of  all  kinds  to  stop  by  on  their  way  to
destinations such as the Adirondacks or the Thousand Islands; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  fitting that this Legislative Body recognize local
businesses such as Kayuta Drive-In which have become institutions in the
community, as well as the successful  entrepreneurs  who  have  nurtured
them throughout the years; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Kayuta Drive-In upon the occasion of celebrating  its  60th
Anniversary on June 24, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to James Minosh Jr., Owner of Kayuta Drive-In.
