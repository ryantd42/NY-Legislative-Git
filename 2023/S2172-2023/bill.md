# Relates to prohibiting the use of head-mounted portable electronic devices while driving

**Bill ID:** S2172
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Liu
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the use of head-mounted portable electronic devices while driving.
