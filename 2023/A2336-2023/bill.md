# Relates to licensing examination requirements for licensed master social workers

**Bill ID:** A2336
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Permits licensed master social worker applicants to apply for their examinations after commencing their final semester of instruction.
