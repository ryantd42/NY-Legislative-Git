# Relates to the dates to file a designating petition

**Bill ID:** A6774
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alex Bores
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the dates to file a designating petition; provides for the repeal of such provisions.
