# Relates to designating, as peace officers, public safety officers of the Chautauqua Institution within the grounds of and properties owned by the Chautauqua Institution

**Bill ID:** A1712AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Designates, as peace officers, public safety officers of the Chautauqua Institution within the grounds of and properties owned by the Chautauqua Institution.
