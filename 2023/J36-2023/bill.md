## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 36
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  COMMEMORATING  the 80th Anniversary of Nunn's Home
  Medical Equipment of Rome, New York on July 19, 2022
 
  WHEREAS, It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize  the
achievements  of  those  individuals  and businesses which substantially
contribute to the economic vitality of their communities and the  entire
Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern  and  in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  80th  Anniversary  of Nunn's Home Medical Equipment of
Rome, New York on Tuesday, July 19, 2022; and
 
  WHEREAS, Nunn's Home Medical Equipment was founded by Martin J. Nunn
in 1942; since then, it has remained a family business, specializing  in
home medical equipment; the company networks with the best providers and
professionals  to  give  its  customers  the  highest  quality products,
personal service, and individual attention; and
 
  WHEREAS, Martin J. Nunn started his business after a friend  of  his
could  not  find  a  wheelchair;  he  ordered one wheelchair and 12 were
delivered; since he did not need all of them, he decided to sell or rent
theta to those who did, and subsequently, Nunn's Home Medical  Equipment
was founded; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Today,  after  more than eight decades of business, Nunn's
Home Medical Equipment remains in the family and is currently  owned  by
Erin and Shawn Weiman; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  success of Nunn's Home Medical Equipment is in direct
correlation to  the  efforts  of  its  staff  which  includes  certified
respiratory  therapists,  friendly  and  knowledgeable  customer service
representatives, certified fitters of therapeutic shoes  and  mastectomy
products,  highly trained delivery and service staff, and an experienced
billing staff, whose involvement is, and always has been,  characterized
by  an  impressive  commitment,  an  unbridled  enthusiasm,  and  an
uncompromising standard of excellence in all endeavors on behalf of  the
business and the community it serves; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For 80 years, Nunn's Home Medical Equipment has strived to
improve the health of those it serves with a commitment to excellence in
all it does through quality care and programs; and
 
  WHEREAS , It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this  Legislative  Body
acknowledges  this  exceptional  business  and  its contributions to the
local and State economies, fully confident it will continue to enjoy the
tremendous successes it has experienced thus far; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
commemorate  the  80th Anniversary of Nunn's Home Medical Equipment; and
be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Erin  and  Shawn Weiman, Nunn's Home Medical Equipment,
Rome, New York.
