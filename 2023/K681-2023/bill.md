# Congratulating Don Hammond upon the occasion of his retirement as the Mayor of the Village of Nyack, New York

**Bill ID:** K681
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 681
 
BY: M. of A. Zebrowski
 
  CONGRATULATING  Don  Hammond  upon the occasion of
  his retirement as the Mayor of the Village of Nyack,
  New York, after decades of dedicated service to  the
  residents  of the County of Rockland and the Village
  of Nyack
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body, in  keeping  with
its  time-honored  traditions, to recognize and pay tribute to those who
have served to better the quality of life in our  communities  and  have
had  a  measurable  positive  impact  on the lives of its residents; Don
Hammond is one such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate Don Hammond upon the occasion  of  his  retirement  as  the
Mayor  of  the  Village  of  Nyack, New York, after decades of dedicated
service to the residents of the County of Rockland and  the  Village  of
Nyack; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Don  Hammond grew up in the Village of Nyack and has spent
most of his life living there; he is  a  proud  graduate  of  the  Nyack
School System and served as a member of its Board of Education for seven
years; and
 
  WHEREAS,  For  over  30  years,  Don Hammond has served in executive
capacities for both national and regional  nonprofit  organizations;  he
served as President/CEO of Meals on Wheels in Rockland County, New York,
President/CEO  of the United Way of the Duchess-Orange Region, and Chief
Operating Officer of the Rockland County YMCA; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 2017, Don Hammond was elected Mayor of  the  Village  of
Nyack, and was officially sworn into office on December 21, 2017, by his
predecessor, former Mayor Jen Laird White; he has continued to hold this
esteemed  title until his upcoming retirement in 2023 after six years of
service; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  that  those  who
enhance  the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown a
long and sustained commitment to excellence certainly  have  earned  the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Mayor Don Hammond upon  the  occasion  of  his  retirement,
after decades of distinguished service to the residents of the County of
Rockland  and  the  Village of Nyack, and to wish him well in all of his
future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Don Hammond.
