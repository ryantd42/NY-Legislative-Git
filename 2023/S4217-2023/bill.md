# Enacts Michelle and Jordan's law relating to the penalties for a second conviction of participating in an unlawful speed contest or race

**Bill ID:** S4217
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Enacts Michelle and Jordan's law relating to the penalties for a second conviction of participating in an unlawful speed contest or race.
