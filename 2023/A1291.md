# Relates to taxation of state owned land

**Bill ID:** A1291
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jaime R. Williams
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1291 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1291)

## Summary

Provides for the taxation of state owned land for all purposes, exclusive of improvements and in accordance with any payment in lieu of taxes agreement.
