**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anthony H. Palumbo
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J10 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/J10)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 10
 
BY: Senator PALUMBO
 
  CONGRATULATING  Anice  Orvananos upon the occasion
  of winning the Division II Women's Foil  Gold  Medal
  at  the  2022 USA Fencing National Championships and
  July Challenge
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate Anice Orvananos upon the occasion of winning  the  Division
II  Women's  Foil  Gold  Medal  at  the  2022  USA  Fencing  National
Championships and July  Challenge  on  July  9,  2022,  in  Minneapolis,
Minnesota; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A fencing phenomenon at only 14 years-old, Anice Orvnanos,
of Riverhead, New York, surpassed a challenging  field  of  127  of  the
Nation's  best  foil  fencers on her journey to a national championship;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to her many accomplishments as a fencer,  Anice
Orvananos  is  an  exceptional student, maintaining impressive grades at
the Horace Mann School in the Bronx, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as fencing, which demands athletic prowess,
speed and agility, and mental preparation, Anice  Orvananos  has  worked
hard to hone her impressive skills; her loved ones and community eagerly
look forward to where she will go next in this sport; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Anice Orvananos upon the occasion of winning  the  Division
II  Women's  Foil  Gold  Medal  at  the  2022  USA  Fencing  National
Championships and July Challenge; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Anice Orvananos.
