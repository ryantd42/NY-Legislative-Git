# Establishes the healthcare workforce innovation fund; appropriation

**Bill ID:** S1637
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1637 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1637)

## Summary

Establishes the healthcare workforce innovation fund for the purpose of expanding the direct care provider and home health aide workforce pipelines; makes appropriation.
