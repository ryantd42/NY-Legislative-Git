# Allows rifles, shotguns, and firearms on college and university campuses

**Bill ID:** S1407
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1407 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1407)

## Summary

Allows rifles, shotguns, and firearms on college and university campuses.
