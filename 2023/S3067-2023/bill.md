# Establishes a commission on hosting the olympic games

**Bill ID:** S3067
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes a commission on Lake Placid and a partner municipality hosting the olympic games.

 CALL A MEETING. SEVEN MEMBERS SHALL CONSTITUTE A QUORUM. THE  COMMISSION
 MAY  MEET  AND HOLD PUBLIC AND/OR PRIVATE HEARINGS WITHIN OR WITHOUT THE
 STATE, AND SHALL HAVE ALL THE POWERS OF A LEGISLATIVE COMMITTEE PURSUANT

  5. THE MEMBERS OF THE AUTHORITY SHALL NOT RECEIVE A  SALARY  OR  OTHER

 SECTION.
