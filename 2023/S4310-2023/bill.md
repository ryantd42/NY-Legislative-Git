# Changes the definition of the word "weed"

**Bill ID:** S4310
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Changes the definition of the word "weed" to include certain types of plants and exclude those that are endangered or indigenous to this state.

 AN  ACT  to  amend  the  environmental  conservation law, in relation to

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  Subdivision  45  of  section 33-0101 of the environmental
 conservation law, as added by chapter 685 of the laws  of  1978  and  as
 renumbered  by  chapter  612  of the laws of 1983, is amended to read as
 follows:
  45. "Weed" means any plant [which  grows  where  not  wanted]  SPECIES

 PURSUANT  TO  STATE OR FEDERAL LAW, AND SHALL NOT INCLUDE ANY ENDANGERED
 SPECIES OR ANY PLANTS INDIGENOUS TO THIS STATE.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
