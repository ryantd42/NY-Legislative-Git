# Relates to the certain offenses and provisions related to the unlawful dissemination of a personal image; and relates to a private right of action for such offenses; repealer

**Bill ID:** S2221
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Monica Martinez
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Provides for the offense of unlawful dissemination of a personal image and a private right of action for such offense.
