# Exempts any land located in the state forest preserve in the Adirondack and Catskill parks from certain provisions of law relating to prohibiting firearms in public parks

**Bill ID:** S3040
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Exempts any land located in the state forest preserve in the Adirondack and Catskill parks from certain provisions of law relating to prohibiting firearms in public parks.
