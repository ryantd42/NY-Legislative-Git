# Requires the availability of hand sanitizer and toilet seat covers in all bathrooms

**Bill ID:** S2365
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the availability of hand sanitizer and toilet seat covers in all bathrooms in state-owned and state-leased buildings.
