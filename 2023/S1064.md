# Establishes an extended producer responsibility program for packaging

**Bill ID:** S1064
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1064 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1064)

## Summary

Establishes an extended producer responsibility program for packaging which creates provisions related to improved recycling practices; establishes the packaging responsibility fund.

 § 27-3403. RESPONSIBILITIES OF PRODUCERS.
  1.  THERE  IS  HEREBY  ESTABLISHED A PACKAGING PRODUCER RESPONSIBILITY
 PROGRAM BY WHICH PRODUCERS, PARTICIPATING INDIVIDUALLY OR  COLLECTIVELY,
 SHALL  BE  REQUIRED  TO  MAKE  CHANGES TO THEIR PRODUCT DESIGN TO REDUCE

 BE RESPONSIBLE FOR PACKAGING WASTE DISPOSAL.
  2. THE PRODUCER OR PRODUCERS SHALL PROVIDE ALL  INFORMATION  NECESSARY
 FOR  THE  DETERMINATION  OF  THE  PRODUCER'S PAYMENT OBLIGATIONS AND THE
 DETERMINATION OF THE PRODUCER'S COMPLIANCE WITH RESPECT TO THIS TITLE.

 MENT,  OR  A THIRD PARTY, SHALL CONDUCT A STATEWIDE REDUCTION, REUSE AND
 RECYCLING NEEDS ASSESSMENT, HEREAFTER "NEEDS  ASSESSMENT",  TO  IDENTIFY
 BARRIERS  AND OPPORTUNITIES FOR REDUCING, REUSING, AND RECYCLING PACKAG-
 ING. THE NEEDS ASSESSMENT SHALL AT A MINIMUM INCLUDE  AN  EVALUATION  OF
 CAPACITY COSTS, GAPS AND NEEDS FOR THE FOLLOWING FACTORS:
  (A)  CURRENT  BARRIERS  AFFECTING THE CREATION OF REDUCTION, REUSE AND
 REFILL PROGRAMS;
  (B) OPPORTUNITIES FOR THE CREATION  OF  REDUCTION,  REUSE  AND  REFILL
 PROGRAMS;

  (A) POLYVINYL CHLORIDE;
  (B) POLYSTYRENE; OR
  (C) POLYCARBONATE.

 NITION, IT SHALL PUBLISH A DETAILED STATEMENT OF  FINDINGS  AND  CONCLU-
 SIONS SUPPORTING SUCH DETERMINATION.
  4.  WITHIN  ONE  HUNDRED EIGHTY DAYS OF DESIGNATING A TOXIC SUBSTANCE,
 THE DEPARTMENT SHALL ADOPT REGULATIONS TO PROHIBIT THE NEWLY  DESIGNATED
 S. 1064  15
 
 TOXIC  SUBSTANCE  IN PACKAGING, WITH AN EFFECTIVE DATE NO LATER THAN TWO
 YEARS AFTER SUCH REGULATIONS ARE FINALIZED.

 FOR EACH VIOLATION NOT TO EXCEED TWENTY-FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS  FOR  EACH
