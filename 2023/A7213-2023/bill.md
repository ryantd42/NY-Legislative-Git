# Relates to transition and inauguration receipts and expenditures

**Bill ID:** A7213
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John T. McDonald III
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the statements of receipts, contributions, transfers and expenditures to transition and inauguration entities.
