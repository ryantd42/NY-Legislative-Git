# Honoring Amy G. Bass upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition by the Friends of the Port Washington Public Library on May 11, 2023

**Bill ID:** J928
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 928
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  HONORING  Amy  G.  Bass  upon  the occasion of her
  designation for special recognition by  the  Friends
  of  the  Port  Washington  Public Library on May 11,
  2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to  commend  those
illustrious  organizations  and individuals within the State of New York
whose focus and intent have made substantial impact on  the  quality  of
life in their communities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Amy G. Bass upon the occasion of her designation for special recognition
by the Friends of the Port Washington Public Library to be celebrated at
its 54th Richard Dodge Whittemore Annual Book  and  Author  Luncheon  on
Thursday,  May  11,  2023,  at the Garden City Hotel in Garden City, New
York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Friends of the Port Washington Public Library (FOL)  is
dedicated  to  strengthening the ties between the library and community,
and to  advancing  the  library's  mission  as  a  center  for  lifelong
learning, information, and culture; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Held each May, this year's Richard Dodge Whittemore Annual
Book and Author Luncheon is  honoring  Amy  G.  Bass,  the  FOL's  first
Luncheon Honoree; and
 
  WHEREAS, A Port Washingtonian since the age of three and graduate of
Schreiber  High  School, Amy G. Bass is a lifelong patron and devotee of
the FOL; and
 
  WHEREAS, Having joined in  1988,  Amy  G.  Bass  is  a  long-serving
President of the FOL Board; her dedication and grace has transformed the
FOL  into  an  indispensable  partner  to  the  library  and one of Port
Washington's most well-respected organizations; and
 
  WHEREAS, Amy G. Bass's commitment to the Port  Washington  community
is  evidenced  by  her  many leadership positions; her positions include
serving as President of the Board of Education for the  Port  Washington
School  District, founding President and now President Emerita of the Ed
Foundation,  board  member  of  the  League  of  Women  Voters  of  Port
Washington-Manhasset and chair of its annual State of the Town Luncheon,
and 30-year board member of the Community Chest of Port Washington; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It has always been the objective of this Legislative Body
to  honor  and  support  those  individuals  who  have  displayed  their
commitment  to the betterment of their communities, and it is the intent
of this Legislative Body to inscribe upon its records, this  tribute  to
Amy  G.  Bass,  that  future  generations  may know and appreciate their
admirable character, their many benevolent deeds, and  the  respect  and
esteem in which they are held by their peers; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that when
individuals of such noble aims and accomplishments are  brought  to  our
attention,  they should be celebrated and recognized by all the citizens
of this great Empire State; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Amy G. Bass upon the  occasion  of  her  designation  for  special
recognition  by the Friends of the Port Washington Public Library on May
11, 2023; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Amy  G.  Bass;  and  the Friends of the Port Washington
Public Library (FOL).
