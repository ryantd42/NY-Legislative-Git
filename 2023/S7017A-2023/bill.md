# Allows for child beneficiaries of deceased police officers and firefighters to receive accidental death benefits until age twenty-six

**Bill ID:** S7017A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Allows for child beneficiaries of deceased police officers and firefighters to receive accidental death benefits until age twenty-six.
