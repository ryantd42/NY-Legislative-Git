# Directs the department of health to assign at least 1 narcotics investigator to each county in a city having a population of one million or more

**Bill ID:** S3077
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the department of health to assign at least 1 narcotics investigator to each county in a city having a population of one million or more.
