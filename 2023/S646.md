# Relates to the use of wood in subway and railroad tracks

**Bill ID:** S646
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S646 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S646)

## Summary

Relates to the use of wood in subway and railroad tracks; prohibits the use of tropical hardwood.
