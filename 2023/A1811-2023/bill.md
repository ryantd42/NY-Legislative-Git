# Relates to mandatory influenza vaccine for persons attending daycare

**Bill ID:** A1811
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Mandates influenza vaccine for children attending licensed daycare.
