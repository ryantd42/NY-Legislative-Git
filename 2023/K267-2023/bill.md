# Commemorating the 152nd Anniversary of the Village of Wappingers Falls, New York, on September 22, 2023

**Bill ID:** K267
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 267
 
BY: M. of A. Beephan
 
  COMMEMORATING the 152nd Anniversary of the Village
  of Wappingers Falls, New York, on September 22, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of this Legislative Body to honor and
commemorate  the  distinguished  histories  of  the  communities  which
comprise the noble body of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the  152nd  Anniversary of the Village of Wappingers Falls,
New York, on September 22, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Village of  Wappingers  Falls  got  its  name  for  the
waterfalls  that  run  through  the  middle  of the village; these falls
helped Wappingers make a cotton mill and  they  were  also  one  of  the
biggest printworks in the country; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  village  holds  the  sixth oldest library in New York
State, Grinnell Library, and Mesier Park, which has been a  public  park
since 1891, hosts many annual events that focus around the bandstand and
tree-lined paths of the park; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The Wappingers Falls Police Station was built in 1940, and
still stands today; the New Hamburg Station on  Metro  North  Railroad's
Hudson Line serves the Village of Wappingers Falls; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 1984, the District was added to the National Register of
Historic Places; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Today,  more  than  6,000  residents  call  the Village of
Wappingers Falls home; and
 
  WHEREAS, Of historic origin, and remaining fruitful over the ebb and
flow of decades of growth and change, the Village  of  Wappingers  Falls
continues  to  provide  the  quintessential  quality  of  life  for  its
residents; and
 
  WHEREAS, The residents of the Village of  Wappingers  Falls  have  a
bright  outlook  for  the  future  of  their  community, which remains a
beautiful place in which to live and raise a family; and
 
  WHEREAS, In recognition of the Village  of  Wappingers  Falls'  rich
history  and  enduring  contribution  to  the  State  of  New York, this
Legislative Body is proud to pay tribute to this spirited town upon  the
occasion of its 152nd Anniversary; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the 152nd Anniversary of the Village of Wappingers Falls  on
September  22,  2023,  recognizing  the  significance  of  the  role  it
continues to play in the life of the community of the State of New York;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to the Village of Wappingers Falls.
