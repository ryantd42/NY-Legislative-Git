# Congratulating the Syracuse Strong Football Team upon the occasion of capturing the Gridiron Developmental Football League (GDFL) National Championship

**Bill ID:** J167
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [J167 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/J167)

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 167
 
BY: Senator MAY
 
  CONGRATULATING  the  Syracuse Strong Football Team
  upon  the  occasion  of  capturing  the  Gridiron
  Developmental  Football  League  (GDFL)  National
  Championship on September 3, 2022
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to pay tribute  to
those  exemplary  athletes  and organizations that excel in professional
athletics, distinguishing themselves through  notable  achievements  and
representing the great Empire State with pride and determination; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
congratulate  the  Syracuse  Strong  Football  Team upon the occasion of
capturing  the  Gridiron  Developmental  Football  League  National
Championship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  To  the  praise  and  applause  of their excited fans, the
number three ranked Syracuse  Strong  clinched  the  Championship  after
defeating  the  number  one  ranked  Inglewood Blackhawks in a thrilling
24-20 victory in Las Vegas, Nevada on Saturday, September 3, 2022; and
 
  WHEREAS, Proudly donning the team's colors, family members, friends,
and the  community  at  large  loyally  and  enthusiastically  supported
Syracuse  Strong  throughout  their  journey as they ended their regular
season with an impressive overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to this monumental win, Syracuse Strong was the
2020 Runner-up of the AAA AFNT  National  Championship,  2019  AAA  AFNT
National  Champion, 2019 Northeastern Football Alliance League Champion,
2019 Northeastern Football  Alliance  Eastern  Division  Champion,  2019
Battle  for  Route  Five  Trophy  Winner,  and  the  2018 Glory Bowl III
Champions; and
 
  WHEREAS, The  athletic  talent  displayed  by  the  Syracuse  Strong
Football  Team  is  due  in  great  part  to  the  efforts  of  Head
Coach/Offensive  Coordinator  Marty  Clanton,  Defensive
Coordinator/Defensive  Line Coach Jason Howard, Defensive Assistant Matt
Jones, Wide-Receiver/Special Teams Coach Derrick  Williams,  Runningback
Coach  Rodney  Thompson,  Strength  and Conditioning Coach Tex Jones and
Stats Coach Lee Jennings, skilled and  inspirational  tutors,  respected
for their ability to develop potential into excellence; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  hallmarks  of the Syracuse Strong Football Team, from
the opening game of the season to their outstanding win in the  Gridiron
Bowl  XIII,  were  a  brotherhood  of  athletic  ability,  of  good
sportsmanship, and of honor, demonstrating that these team  players  are
second to none; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Sports  competitions instill the values of teamwork, pride
and accomplishment; Head Coach Marty Clanton, his coaching staff and the
Syracuse Strong Football Team's outstanding athletes have clearly made a
contribution to the spirit of excellence which is a tradition  of  their
organization; now, therefore, be it
