# Relates to preventing the use of funds, financial incentives, subsidies or tax exemptions for projects in development

**Bill ID:** A2560
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Ron Kim
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prevents the use of funds, financial incentives, subsidies or tax exemptions for projects already in development.
