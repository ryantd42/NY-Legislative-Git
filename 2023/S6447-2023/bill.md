# Extends the authority of the county of Columbia to impose an additional 1 percent sales and compensating use tax

**Bill ID:** S6447
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michelle Hinchey
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authority of the county of Columbia to impose an additional 1 percent sales and compensating use tax by two years.
