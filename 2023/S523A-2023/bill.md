# Authorizes the city of New York to establish a residential parking system in the postal designation and area of Queens village near the UBS arena

**Bill ID:** S523A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the city of New York to establish a residential parking system in the postal designation and area of Queens village near the USB arena.
