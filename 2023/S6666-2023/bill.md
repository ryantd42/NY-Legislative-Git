# Relates to no longer permitting the use of electronic absentee voting applications; repealer

**Bill ID:** S6666
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to no longer permitting the use of electronic absentee voting applications; repeals all provisions relating thereto.
