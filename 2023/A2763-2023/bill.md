# Increases the penalty for certain traffic violations when such violations arise out of the same incident as a conviction for failure to move over and when such violations occurred in a work zone

**Bill ID:** A2763
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Chris Tague
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Doubles the penalties for use of mobile telephones and use of portable electronic devices when such violations arise out of the same incident as a conviction for failure to move over and when such violations occurred in a work zone.
