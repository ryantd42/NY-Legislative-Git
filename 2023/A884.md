# Relates to the disclosure of information on the fiscal health of a condominium, cooperative apartment building or cooperative community

**Bill ID:** A884
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A884 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A884)

## Summary

Relates to the disclosure of information on the fiscal health of a condominium, cooperative apartment building or cooperative community to prospective buyers, current owners and the condominium board or the cooperative board.
