# Replaces the terms "mentally retarded", "mental retardation" and variants thereof with "developmentally disabled" and "developmental disability"

**Bill ID:** S3313BA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Replaces the terms "mentally retarded", "mental retardation" and variants thereof with "developmentally disabled" and "developmental disability".
