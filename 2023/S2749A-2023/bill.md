# Establishes the New York state youth sports initiative grants fund

**Bill ID:** S2749A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the New York state youth sports initiative grants fund under the commissioner of children and family services.
