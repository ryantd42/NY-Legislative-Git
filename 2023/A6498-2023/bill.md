# Extends the authorization of the county of Oswego to impose an additional rate of sales taxes until 2025

**Bill ID:** A6498
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William A. Barclay
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Oswego to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes from 2023 to 2025.
