# Provides for certain fee exemptions for disabled veterans

**Bill ID:** A3199
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Chris Tague
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Grants all veterans with a disability seven free days of sporting activity in New York state.
