# Includes offenses regarding school crossing guards to aggravated harassment in the second degree

**Bill ID:** A1744
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes offenses against school crossing guards to aggravated harassment in the second degree.

 AN ACT to amend the penal law, in relation to including offenses against
  a school crossing guard to aggravated harassment in the second degree
 
  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Section 240.30 of the penal law is amended by adding a  new
 subdivision 3-a to read as follows:
  3-A.  WITH  INTENT TO HARASS, ANNOY, THREATEN OR ALARM ANOTHER PERSON,
 HE OR SHE STRIKES, SHOVES, KICKS, OR OTHERWISE SUBJECTS  ANOTHER  PERSON
 TO  PHYSICAL  CONTACT, WHICH INCLUDES SPITTING ON SUCH OTHER PERSON, AND
 SUCH OTHER PERSON IS A  SCHOOL  CROSSING  GUARD  APPOINTED  PURSUANT  TO
 SECTION TWO HUNDRED EIGHT-A OF THE GENERAL MUNICIPAL LAW; OR
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
