# Includes The Community Library in Cobleskill with libraries eligible for the financing of projects through the dormitory authority

**Bill ID:** S6813AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Peter Oberacker
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Includes The Community Library in Cobleskill, New York with libraries eligible for the financing of projects through the dormitory authority.
