# Relates to requiring liability insurance for owners of firearms, rifles and shotguns

**Bill ID:** A3314
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires liability insurance for owners of firearms, rifles and shotguns.
