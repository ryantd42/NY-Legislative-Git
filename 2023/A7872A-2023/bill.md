# Relates to health care proxy information and palliative care patients

**Bill ID:** A7872AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Requires health care practitioners to offer health care proxy information to palliative care patients.

  (b) information regarding other appropriate treatment  options  should

 palliative  care,  the  attending health care practitioner shall provide
