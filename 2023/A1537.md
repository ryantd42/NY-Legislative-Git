# Provides for insurance premium reductions for successful completion of a fraud prevention course

**Bill ID:** A1537
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Blankenbush
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1537 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1537)

## Summary

Provides for insurance premium reductions for successful completion of a fraud prevention course, known as the New York auto fraud prevention program.
