# Provides for the regulation of landfill methane gas control flares for the protection of raptors

**Bill ID:** A3994
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the regulation of landfill methane gas control flares for the protection of raptors.
