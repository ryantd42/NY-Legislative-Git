# Establishes representative requirements for district leadership teams and school leadership teams

**Bill ID:** S2949
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires district leadership teams and school leadership teams to include student representatives.
