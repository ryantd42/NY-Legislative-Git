# Extends the authority of the county of Orange to impose an additional rate of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S6554
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends the authority of the county of Orange to impose an additional rate of sales and compensating use taxes; provides for the use of the tax funds collected.
