# Provides press access to open meetings of an agency or authority and requires notice of such meetings to local governments

**Bill ID:** S1253
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1253 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1253)

## Summary

Provides press access to open meetings of an agency or authority; requires notice of such meetings to local governments.
