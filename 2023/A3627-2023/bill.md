# Relates to requiring lending institutions to supply customers with PINs to be used in conjunction with any chip-embedded credit card

**Bill ID:** A3627
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires lending institutions to supply customers with PINs to be used in conjunction with any chip-embedded credit card.
