# Congratulating George E. Wargo, Jr. upon the occasion of his retirement as Superintendent of Highways in Haverstraw, New York

**Bill ID:** K701
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 701
 
BY: M. of A. Zebrowski
 
  CONGRATULATING  George  E.  Wargo,  Jr.  upon  the
  occasion of  his  retirement  as  Superintendent  of
  Highways in Haverstraw, New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body, in keeping with
its time-honored traditions, to recognize and pay tribute to  those  who
have  served  to  better the quality of life in our communities and have
had a measurable positive impact on the lives of its  residents;  George
E.  Wargo, Jr. is one such individual; and
 
  WHEREAS,  George E. Wargo, Jr. spent over 40 years as an employee of
the Rockland  County  Highway  Department,  starting  his  career  as  a
laborer; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  2014, George E. Wargo Jr. has served as the Town of
Haverstraw Superintendent of Highways, ensuring  that  roads  and  other
public infrastructure are maintained; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Committed  to  serving his community, George E. Wargo, Jr.
has been a 40 year member and former chief of the West  Haverstraw  Fire
Department,  and has been involved in other community organizations such
as the Knights of Columbus Council No. 581, Sons of the American Legion,
St. Peter's CYO Program, Haverstraw Little League, North Rockland Sports
Hall of Fame, Central Hudson Valley Football Officials Association,  and
Just For Fun Baseball; and
 
  WHEREAS,  George  E.  Wargo,  Jr.  has  been  a  member  of  several
professional  associations,  including  the  American  Public  Works
Association,  Rockland County Superintendent of Highways Association and
the Damage Preventive Council; and
 
  WHEREAS, It  is  the  sense  of  this  Legislative  Body  that  when
individuals  of  such  noble aims and accomplishments are brought to our
attention, they should be celebrated and recognized by all the  citizens
of the great State of New York; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate George E. Wargo, Jr. upon the occasion of  his  retirement,
after  many  years of distinguished service to the residents of the Town
of Haverstraw and the County of Rockland, and to wish him well in all of
his future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to George E. Wargo, Jr.
