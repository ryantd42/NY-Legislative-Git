# Establishes the reasonable charge for electronic copies of medical records and patient information

**Bill ID:** A2667
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jake Ryan Blumencranz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the reasonable charge for electronic copies of medical records and patient information.
