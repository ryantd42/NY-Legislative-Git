# Relates to the authority of nurse practitioners and physician assistants to prescribe certain services

**Bill ID:** A7187
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Permits physician assistants to order home care services and clarify nurse practitioners' authority to order home care services.
