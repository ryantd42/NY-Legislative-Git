# Relates to the prohibition of certain acts in rodeos

**Bill ID:** A575
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A575 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A575)

## Summary

Prohibits certain acts in rodeos; imposes penalties for the violation of such acts.
