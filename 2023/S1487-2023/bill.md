# Appoints ad hoc members to the New York state board on electric generation siting and the environment

**Bill ID:** S1487
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1487 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1487)

## Summary

Relates to appointing ad hoc members to the New York state board on electric generation siting and the environment; provides notification of the status of proposed candidates for appointment.
