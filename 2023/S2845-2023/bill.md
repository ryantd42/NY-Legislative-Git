# Provides for the issuance of temporary retail permits by the state liquor authority; repealer

**Bill ID:** S2845
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew J. Lanza
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for the issuance of temporary retail permits by the state liquor authority if certain conditions are met.
