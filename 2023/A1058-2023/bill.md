# Rules and regulations requiring nursing homes and residential health care facilities to test all residents of such nursing homes and residential healthcare facilities for the novel coronavirus (COVID-19)

**Bill ID:** A1058
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jonathan Jacobson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1058 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1058)

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of health to promulgate rules and regulations requiring nursing homes and residential health care facilities to test all residents of such nursing homes and residential healthcare facilities for the novel coronavirus (COVID-19).
