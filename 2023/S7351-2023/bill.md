# Relates to including rape in the third degree, rape in the second degree and rape in the first degree as family offenses

**Bill ID:** S7351
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Includes rape in the third degree, rape in the second degree and rape in the first degree as family offenses.
