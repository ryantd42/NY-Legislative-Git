# Facilitates the marketing of any issue of serial bonds or notes of the city of Buffalo issued on or before a certain date

**Bill ID:** S6564
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Facilitates the marketing of any issue of serial bonds or notes of the city of Buffalo issued on or before June 30, 2024.
