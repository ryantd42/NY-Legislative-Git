# Creates a vending to food pantries and soup kitchens tax credit

**Bill ID:** A2607
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates a vending to food pantries and soup kitchens tax credit.
