# Authorizes the City of Newburgh to add unpaid housing code violation penalties, costs and fines to such city's annual tax levy

**Bill ID:** A7135AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jonathan Jacobson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the City of Newburgh to add unpaid housing code violation penalties, costs and fines to such city's annual tax levy in accordance with applicable law.
