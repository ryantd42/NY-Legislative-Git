# Relates to authorizing the Silver Lake Foundation Inc. to receive retroactive real property tax exempt status

**Bill ID:** S7571
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Scarcella-Spanton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes the assessor of Richmond county to grant the Silver Lake Foundation Inc. retroactive real property tax exempt status upon an application therefor.
