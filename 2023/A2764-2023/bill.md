# Creates a day care assistance program for members of the organized militia serving active duty; appropriation

**Bill ID:** A2764
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Chris Tague
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Creates a day care assistance program for members of the organized militia serving federal active duty outside the United States; appropriates $500,000 therefor.

 INFORMATIONAL MATERIALS REGARDING THE AVAILABILITY OF AID PROVIDED UNDER

 INFORMATION ON WHERE AND HOW TO APPLY FOR SUCH AID.
