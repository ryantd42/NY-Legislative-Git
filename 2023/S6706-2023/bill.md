# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the Trooper James R. Gohery Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** S6706
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steve Rhoads
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system in Nassau county as the "Trooper James R. Gohery Memorial Bridge".
