# Extends the temporary "commission to prevent childhood drowning"

**Bill ID:** A7609
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the temporary "commission to prevent childhood drowning" for an additional eighteen months.
