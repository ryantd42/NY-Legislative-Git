# Establishes the New York state infrastructure development bank; appropriation

**Bill ID:** S2965
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the New York state infrastructure development bank within the department of financial services; makes a $250,000,000 appropriation therefor.
