# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Trooper Edward J. Sweeney Memorial Bridge"

**Bill ID:** S6867
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steve Rhoads
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "Trooper Edward J. Sweeney Memorial Bridge" in the town of Hempstead, county of Nassau.
