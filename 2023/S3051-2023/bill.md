# Divides the one hundred ninth precinct of the New York city police department into subdivisions

**Bill ID:** S3051
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Toby Ann Stavisky
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Divides the one hundred ninth precinct of the New York city police department into two separate subdivisions.
