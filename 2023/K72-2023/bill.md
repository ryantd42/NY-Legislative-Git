# Honoring The Clarence Central School District's Family Support Center upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the Organization of the Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce on March 9, 2023

**Bill ID:** K72
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Norris
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 72
 
BY: M. of A. Norris
 
  HONORING  The  Clarence  Central School District's
  Family Support  Center  upon  the  occasion  of  its
  designation  as recipient of the Organization of the
  Year Award by the Clarence Chamber  of  Commerce  on
  March 9, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize and
commend those distinguished citizens who would devote their life's  work
and  purpose  to  community  service, contributing to the welfare of the
citizens of their communities and this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
The  Clarence  Central  School District's Family Support Center upon the
occasion of its designation as recipient of the Organization of the Year
Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce, to be celebrated at its  50th
Annual  Awards  Dinner  on  Thursday,  March 9, 2023, at Samuel's Grande
Manor in Clarence, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, The  Clarence  Central  School  District's  Family  Support
Center  is  receiving  this  prestigious  award  for  its  steadfast and
unremitting commitment to the beloved community of Clarence; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Family Support Center is focused  on  creating  direct,
school-based,  and  community-based  support  by  developing connections
between  families  and  students,  schools,  community  members,  and
community-based  agencies  and  organizations;  the  primary goal of the
Center is to enhance the physical, mental, social, and emotional  health
of individuals and in its community; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Resources which the Family Support Center provides include
connections to basic needs such as  food  and  clothing,  mental  health
counseling, educational and enrichment opportunities, as well as being a
safe space for individuals to come when they are in need; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  its fifth year of operation, the Family Support Center
is comprised of the following staff: Evan Vahratian, Coordinator;  Penny
Glena, Social Worker; and Joy Davey, Secretary Clerk; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  sense of this Legislative Body that those who
enhance the well-being and vitality of their community and have shown  a
long  and  sustained  commitment to excellence certainly have earned the
recognition and applause of all the citizens of this great Empire State;
now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  The Clarence Central School District's Family Support Center upon
the occasion of its designation as recipient of the Organization of  the
Year Award by the Clarence Chamber of Commerce; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to The Clarence Central  School  District's  Family  Support
Center.
