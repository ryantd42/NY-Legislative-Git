# Relates to requiring all buses manufactured for sale in this state to have the exhaust pipe at the upper right corner of the vehicle

**Bill ID:** A3524
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that all buses manufactured for sale in this state after July 1, 2025, shall have an exhaust system whereby the point of emission from the exhaust pipe is at the upper right hand corner of the rear of such vehicle.
