# Provides for a special election, at the discretion of the governor, to fill vacancies in the office of comptroller and attorney-general

**Bill ID:** S1514
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph A. Griffo
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1514 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1514)

## Summary

Provides for a special election, at the discretion of the governor, to fill vacancies in the office of comptroller or attorney-general.
