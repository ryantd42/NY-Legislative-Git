# Requires the commissioner to establish Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander history and civic impact curriculum or instructions for school districts

**Bill ID:** A6579AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Grace Lee
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the commissioner to establish Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander history and civic impact curriculum or instructions for school districts.
