# Relates to requiring affordable housing projects to calculate and only use the area median income for the specific county that the project is located in

**Bill ID:** A7778
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jo Anne Simon
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to requiring affordable housing projects to calculate and only use the area median income for the specific county that the project is located in for determining affordability.
