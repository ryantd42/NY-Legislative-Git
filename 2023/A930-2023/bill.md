# Relates to methods for cancelling contracts

**Bill ID:** A930
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jeffrey Dinowitz
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A930 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A930)

## Summary

Requires that a business allow a consumer to cancel a contract between the two parties using the same method that was used to enter into the contact.
