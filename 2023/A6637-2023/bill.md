# Provides that certain entities may not require a person to provide a copy of his or her criminal history record under certain circumstances

**Bill ID:** A6637
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amanda Septimo
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Provides that certain entities may not require a person to provide a copy of his or her criminal history record under certain circumstances.
