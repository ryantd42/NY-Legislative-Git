# Requires that all printed political campaign mailing pieces, containing or made of recyclable material, include a message about recycling the printed materials

**Bill ID:** S7210
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires that all printed political campaign mailing pieces, containing or made of recyclable material, include a message about recycling the printed materials.
