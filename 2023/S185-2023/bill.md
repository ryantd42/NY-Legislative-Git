# Increases the penalty for leaving the scene of an incident without reporting

**Bill ID:** S185
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S185 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S185)

## Summary

Increases the penalty for leaving the scene of an incident without reporting.
