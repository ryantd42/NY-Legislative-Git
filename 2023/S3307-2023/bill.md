# Relates to reimbursement for donor human breast milk

**Bill ID:** S3307
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes protocol for financial reimbursement for donor human breast milk.
