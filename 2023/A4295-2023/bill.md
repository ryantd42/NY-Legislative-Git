# Relates to prohibiting valuable consideration in exchange for human eggs

**Bill ID:** A4295
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michael J. Fitzpatrick
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Makes it unlawful to provide valuable consideration for the donation of human eggs for scientific research.
