# Establishes the New York college debt repayment program

**Bill ID:** S1098
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1098 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1098)

## Summary

Establishes the New York college debt repayment program.
