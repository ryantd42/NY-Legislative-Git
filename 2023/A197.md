# Relates to synthetic cannabinoids, synthetic cannabinoid analog and substituted cathinones and prohibiting the production and sale thereof

**Bill ID:** A197
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A197 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A197)

## Summary

Relates to synthetic cannabinoids, synthetic cannabinoid analog and substituted cathinones; prohibits the production and sale thereof.
