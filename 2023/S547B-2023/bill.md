# Directs the comptroller of the city of New York to conduct annual audits of compliance with the affordable New York housing program

**Bill ID:** S547B
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brad Hoylman-Sigal
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Directs the comptroller of the city of New York to conduct annual audits of compliance with the affordable New York housing program.
