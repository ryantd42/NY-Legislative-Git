# Relates to recovering attorney fees for legal services rendered under certain claims

**Bill ID:** A7089
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Magnarelli
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the cost for legal services in connection with any claim arising under the volunteer firefighter's benefit law.
