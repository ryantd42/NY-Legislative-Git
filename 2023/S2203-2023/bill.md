# Relates to establishing the private employers veterans' preference policy act

**Bill ID:** S2203
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Ortt
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Permits private employers to adopt a veterans' hiring preference policy.
