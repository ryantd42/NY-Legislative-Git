# Permits certain funeral service vehicles and other vehicles to use high occupancy vehicle lanes

**Bill ID:** A2708
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Permits certain funeral service vehicles and other vehicles to use high occupancy vehicle lanes.
