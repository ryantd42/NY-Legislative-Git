# Commending Marvin Makofsky upon the occasion of his designation for special recognition by The League of Women Voters of Port Washington-Manhasset on June 11, 2023

**Bill ID:** J1056
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1056
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  COMMENDING  Marvin  Makofsky  upon the occasion of
  his  designation  for  special  recognition  by  The
  League  of Women Voters of Port Washington-Manhasset
  on June 11, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, A great community is only as great  as  those  persons  who
provide  exemplary  service,  whether through participation in voluntary
programs, through unique personal achievement  in  their  profession  or
other endeavors, or simply through a lifetime of good citizenship; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commend Marvin Makofsky upon the occasion of his designation for special
recognition  by The League of Women Voters of Port Washington-Manhasset;
his accomplishments will be celebrated at a brunch on Sunday,  June  11,
2023, at the Manhasset Bay Yacht Club in Port Washington, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  League  of  Women Voters is a nonpartisan, grassroots
nonprofit dedicated to empowering everyone to fully participate in  this
country's democracy; with active Leagues in all 50 states, and more than
750  Leagues  across the country, members engage in advocacy, education,
litigation, and organizing to protect every American's freedom to  vote;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Marvin  Makofsky  is  the  founder  of Plant A Row for the
Hungry  in  Port  Washington;  its  mission  is  to  mobilize  the  Port
Washington  Community  to  grow  and distribute locally grown produce to
food insecure families and  impact  climate  change  as  it  relates  to
growing food; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  true  asset  to  its  community,  the organization also
educates  children  and  adults  about  proper,  sustainable  gardening
techniques  and provides opportunities for artists to create and display
their talents across the community via large  painted  pots  that  house
growing vegetables; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Other  initiatives of Plant A Row for the Hungry include a
summer adult and student vegetable growing program to fight  hunger  and
climate  change,  partnerships  with the Nicholas Center for Autism, and
the Helen Keller National Center Garden for  Adults  and  Students,  and
much more; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since  2010, Plant A Row for the Hungry has delivered over
57,000 pounds of produce for  those  in  need,  undoubtedly  creating  a
positive  impact  on countless members of the Port Washington community;
and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is pleased to have the opportunity to
express its highest admiration for Marvin  Makofsky,  and  to  wish  him
continued success in all his future endeavors; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Marvin Makofsky upon the occasion of his designation for special
 
recognition by The League of Women Voters of Port  Washington-Manhasset;
and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Marvin Makofsky.
