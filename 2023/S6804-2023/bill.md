# Establishes a rebuttable presumption of a hate crime when certain crimes are committed on, in or upon a house of worship

**Bill ID:** S6804
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Establishes a rebuttable presumption of a hate crime when certain crimes are committed on, in or upon a house of worship.
