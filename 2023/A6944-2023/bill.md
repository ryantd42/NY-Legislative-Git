# Relates to assaults upon certain employees of a transit agency or authority

**Bill ID:** A6944
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes traffic checkers in the existing 2nd degree assault provisions found in the penal law.
