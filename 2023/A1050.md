# Prevents fees from being charged for the issuance of certain license plates

**Bill ID:** A1050
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1050 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1050)

## Summary

Prevents fees from being charged for the issuance of license plates where the vehicle has legible plates.
