# Provides residency requirements for sex offenders

**Bill ID:** A2782
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Vivian Cook
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides residency requirements for sex offenders including criminal sanctions for violations; prohibits sex offenders from residing in a residence that is within one thousand five hundred feet of any school grounds.

  § 3. Section 168-w of the correction law, as relettered by chapter 604

 is added to read as follows:
  §  168-W. RESIDENCY REQUIREMENTS FOR SEX OFFENDERS. 1. NO SEX OFFENDER

 MEANING  AS  DEFINED  IN  SUBDIVISION  FOURTEEN OF SECTION 220.00 OF THE
