# Relates to making an appropriation for the purpose of establishing and expanding training programs for direct support professionals at boards of cooperative educational services

**Bill ID:** S1563
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Tedisco
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1563 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1563)

## Summary

Appropriates $5,550,000 for the purpose of establishing and expanding training programs for direct support professionals at boards of cooperative educational services.
