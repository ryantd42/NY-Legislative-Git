# Directs the Clerk of the Assembly to arrange seats for representatives of the press

**Bill ID:** E5
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Deborah Glick
**Status:** Adopted
**PDF:** [E5 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/E5)

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 5
 
BY: M. of A. Glick
 
  DIRECTING  the  Clerk  of  the Assembly to arrange
  seats for representatives of the press
 
  RESOLVED, That upon the approval of the Speaker, the  Clerk  of  the
Assembly  be  and  hereby  is  directed  to  arrange  seats  for  the
accommodation of the representatives of the press.
