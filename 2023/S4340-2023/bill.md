# Relates to the proper form of exhibits for submission

**Bill ID:** S4340
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the proper form of exhibits for submission; provides every exhibit shall be clearly identified, but no specific method of labeling shall be required.

 AN  ACT  to  amend  the civil practice law and rules, in relation to the
  proper form of exhibits
 
  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:

  Section 1. Rule 2101 of the civil practice law and rules is amended by
 adding a new subdivision (h) to read as follows:
  (H)  EXHIBITS.  EVERY  EXHIBIT  SHALL  BE  CLEARLY  IDENTIFIED, BUT NO
 SPECIFIC METHOD OF LABELING SHALL BE  REQUIRED.  THE  REJECTION  OF  ANY
 SUBMISSION SHALL NOT BE SOLELY BASED ON THE MISLABELING OF ANY EXHIBIT.
  § 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
