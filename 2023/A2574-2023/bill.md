# Relates to hair relaxer labeling

**Bill ID:** A2574
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Alicia Hyndman
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires that hair relaxer products display labels when they contain certain dangerous chemicals.
