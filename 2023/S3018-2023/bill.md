# Relates to establishing a computerized database containing information relating to the storage and handling of hazardous substances and emergency responses to the release of hazardous substances

**Bill ID:** S3018
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Leroy Comrie
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes a computerized database containing information relating to the storage and handling of hazardous substances and emergency responses to the release of hazardous substances.
