# Directs payment of certain taxes into the central business district tolling capital lockbox fund to be made without appropriation

**Bill ID:** A2895A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jessica Gonzalez-Rojas
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs payment of certain internet sales and use taxes into the central business district tolling capital lockbox fund partially dedicated for the MTA to be made without appropriation.
