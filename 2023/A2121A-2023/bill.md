# Authorizes municipalities to offer a real property tax exemption to certain volunteers who live in such municipality but who serve in a neighboring municipality

**Bill ID:** A2121A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Steve Stern
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Authorizes municipalities to offer a real property tax exemption to certain volunteers who live in that municipality but who serve in neighboring municipalities.
