# Exempts amounts received from any financial settlement for childhood sexual abuse for determining eligibility for or amount of benefits

**Bill ID:** S3359
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Exempts amounts received from any financial settlement for childhood sexual abuse for determining eligibility for or amount of benefits under any program provided under the social security law.
