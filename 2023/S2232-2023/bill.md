# Authorizes the assessor of the county of Nassau to grant the Mineola Union Free School District an exemption from real property taxes upon approval of the Nassau county legislature

**Bill ID:** S2232
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin Thomas
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Requires the approval by the Nassau county legislature for the assessor of the county of Nassau to then accept from the Mineola Union Free School District an application for exemption from real property taxes.
