# Relates to filing statements fixing or approving fees, commissions, or other compensation for certain court appointees by judges or justices

**Bill ID:** S7516
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Julia Salazar
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to filing statements fixing or approving fees, commissions, or other compensation for certain court appointees by judges or justices.
