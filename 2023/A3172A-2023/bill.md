# Provides that an automobile insurer may waive the inspection of a private passenger automobile prior to providing physical damage coverage

**Bill ID:** A3172A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kenneth Zebrowski
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Provides that an automobile insurer may waive the inspection of a private passenger automobile prior to providing physical damage coverage.
