# Relates to the regulation and licensing of on-demand pay providers

**Bill ID:** S2186
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jamaal Bailey
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to the regulation and licensing of employer-integrated and non-verified on-demand pay providers.

  § 374-AA. DEFINITIONS. FOR THE PURPOSES OF THIS ARTICLE, THE FOLLOWING
 TERMS SHALL HAVE THE FOLLOWING MEANINGS:
  1. "EARNED BUT UNPAID INCOME" MEANS EARNED INCOME  THAT  HAS  NOT  YET
 BEEN PAID TO A USER BY AN EMPLOYER.
  2.  "EARNED INCOME" MEANS MONEYS THAT HAVE ACCRUED TO THE BENEFIT OF A
 USER FOR SERVICES RENDERED TO AN EMPLOYER.
  3. "EMPLOYER" MEANS ANY PERSON, CORPORATION, LIMITED LIABILITY  COMPA-
 NY,  OR ASSOCIATION EMPLOYING A USER IN ANY OCCUPATION, INDUSTRY, TRADE,
 BUSINESS OR SERVICE, OR ANY OTHER PERSON WHO IS CONTRACTUALLY  OBLIGATED
 TO  PAY  A USER ANY SUM OF MONEY ON AN HOURLY, PROJECT-BASED, PIECEWORK,
 OR OTHER BASIS FOR LABOR OR SERVICES PROVIDED BY SUCH USER.  "EMPLOYER"
 SHALL  NOT  INCLUDE A CUSTOMER OF AN EMPLOYER OR OTHER THIRD PARTY WHOSE
 OBLIGATION TO MAKE ANY PAYMENT TO A USER IS BASED SOLELY ON SUCH  USER'S
 AGENCY RELATIONSHIP WITH THE EMPLOYER.
  4. "EMPLOYER-INTEGRATED ON-DEMAND PAY PROVIDER" MEANS AN ON-DEMAND PAY
 PROVIDER  THAT  OFFERS  ON-DEMAND  PAY  SERVICES  THROUGH  A CONTRACTUAL
 ARRANGEMENT WITH (I) AN EMPLOYER, OR  (II)  A  SERVICE  PROVIDER  TO  AN

  § 374-BB. EMPLOYER-INTEGRATED ON-DEMAND PAY PROVIDERS. 1.  NO  EMPLOY-
 ER-INTEGRATED  ON-DEMAND PAY PROVIDER SHALL OPERATE WITHOUT FIRST REGIS-
 TERING WITH THE SUPERINTENDENT.  AN  EMPLOYER-INTEGRATED  ON-DEMAND  PAY
 PROVIDER  SHALL  BE  DEEMED REGISTERED IF IT PROVIDES, ON A CONFIDENTIAL
 BASIS, THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION TO THE SUPERINTENDENT:
  (A) THE PROVIDER'S NAME AND ADDRESS;
  (B) AUDITED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS FOR THE PRIOR YEAR;
  (C) A STATEMENT OR SUMMARY OF FEES CHARGED TO USERS  OR  OBLIGORS  FOR
 ON-DEMAND PAY SERVICES BY THE PROVIDER IN NORMAL COURSE; AND
  (D)  COPIES  OF  THE  PROVIDER'S  END USER TERMS AND CONDITIONS AND/OR
 TERMS OF USE, AND PRIVACY POLICIES.
  2. EACH EMPLOYER-INTEGRATED ON-DEMAND PAY PROVIDER SHALL:
  (A) DELIVER TO THE SUPERINTENDENT ON A  CONFIDENTIAL  BASIS  NO  LATER
 THAN  JUNE  THIRTIETH  OF EACH CALENDAR YEAR THE FOLLOWING ITEMS FOR THE
 PRIOR CALENDAR YEAR:
  (I) THE PROVIDER'S AUDITED FINANCIAL  STATEMENTS,  PROVIDED,  THAT  IF
 AUDITED  FINANCIAL  STATEMENTS  ARE  UNAVAILABLE BY THE DATE SUCH ANNUAL
 REPORT IS DUE, SUCH PROVIDER SHALL PROVIDE  UNAUDITED  FINANCIAL  STATE-
 MENTS  AT  SUCH  TIME,  PROVIDED  THAT  PROVIDER SHALL FILE SUCH AUDITED
 FINANCIAL STATEMENTS TO THE SUPERINTENDENT PROMPTLY UPON RECEIPT OF SUCH
 FINANCIAL STATEMENTS; AND
  (II) COPIES OF USER COMPLAINTS FILED WITH THE BETTER  BUSINESS  BUREAU
 OR  THE  CONSUMER  FINANCIAL PROTECTION BUREAU, WITH THE RECORDED RESOL-
 UTION OF SUCH COMPLAINTS;
  (B) DEVELOP AND IMPLEMENT POLICIES AND PROCEDURES TO  QUICKLY  RESPOND
 TO  QUESTIONS  RAISED  BY  USERS  AND TO QUICKLY ADDRESS COMPLAINTS FROM
 USERS;
  (C) FULLY AND CLEARLY STATE ALL FEES FOR  ON-DEMAND  PAY  SERVICES  TO
 BEST PREVENT MISUNDERSTANDING BY PROSPECTIVE USERS;
  (D)  INFORM  USERS,  PRIOR  TO ENTERING INTO AN ON-DEMAND PAY SERVICES
 CONTRACT, OF SUCH USER'S RIGHTS UNDER THE PROGRAM;
  (E) AFFORD USERS THE RIGHT TO CANCEL, AT ANY TIME AND  WITHOUT  INCUR-
 RING A CHARGE, PARTICIPATION IN AN ON-DEMAND PAY SERVICES PROGRAM;
  (F)  ABSENT EVIDENCE OF FRAUD BY A USER, BE RESTRICTED FROM INITIATING
 A COLLECTION ACTION, SUING SUCH USER, OR REPORTING SUCH USER TO A CREDIT
 REPORTING AGENCY IF SUCH EMPLOYER-INTEGRATED ON-DEMAND PAY  PROVIDER  IS
 UNABLE TO BE REPAID FOR FUNDS DELIVERED TO SUCH USER;
  (G)  COMPLY  WITH  ALL APPLICABLE LAWS, RULES, REGULATIONS, AND ORDERS
 REGARDING THE DUTY TO SAFEGUARD A USER'S PERSONAL INFORMATION; AND
  (H) NOT SHARE ANY FEES WITH AN EMPLOYER OR PAY ANY OTHER  COMPENSATION
 TO AN EMPLOYER THAT IS DIRECTLY RELATED TO FEES RECEIVED FROM OR CHARGED
 TO USERS.
  3.  EMPLOYER-INTEGRATED  ON-DEMAND PAY PROVIDERS SHALL NOT BE REQUIRED
 TO BE REGISTERED AS A MONEY TRANSMITTER UNDER ARTICLE THIRTEEN-B OF THIS
 CHAPTER TO ENGAGE IN  THE  ACTIVITIES  OF  AN  ON-DEMAND  PAY  PROVIDER,

  §  374-CC.  NON-VERIFIED  ON-DEMAND  PAY  PROVIDERS. EACH NON-VERIFIED
 ON-DEMAND PAY PROVIDER SHALL BE SUBJECT TO:
  1. SECTION 190.40 OF THE PENAL LAW;
  2. THE LICENSURE REQUIREMENTS OF ARTICLE NINE OF THIS CHAPTER, AND ANY
 OTHER PROVISIONS OF THIS CHAPTER WOULD OTHERWISE  APPLY  TO  A  LOAN  OR
 CREDIT TRANSACTION; AND
  3.  THE FEDERAL TRUTH IN LENDING ACT, AND THE REGULATIONS IMPLEMENTING
 SUCH ACT, TO PROVIDE ANY DISCLOSURES REQUIRED FOR CLOSED-END LOANS.
  § 374-DD. CERTAIN ON-DEMAND PAY SERVICES TO BE  CONSIDERED  LOANS.  1.
 NON-VERIFIED  ON-DEMAND PAY SERVICES SHALL BE CONSIDERED A LOAN, AND ANY
 FEES OR OTHER REQUIRED OR OPTIONAL CONTRIBUTIONS OF SUCH SERVICES  SHALL
 BE  CONSIDERED  AS  INTEREST  WHEN  DETERMINING THE RATE OF INTEREST FOR
 PURPOSES OF COMPLIANCE WITH THIS CHAPTER.  ANY SUCH INTEREST RATES SHALL
 NOT EXCEED TEN PERCENT PER YEAR.
  2. EMPLOYER-INTEGRATED ON-DEMAND PAY SERVICES SHALL NOT BE  CONSIDERED
 A LOAN.
  §  374-EE.  PENALTIES. ANY CORPORATIONS, LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY, OR
 OTHER ENTITY THAT VIOLATES ANY PROVISION OF THIS ARTICLE SHALL BE LIABLE
 FOR A PENALTY, IN ADDITION TO ANY OTHER PENALTY IMPOSED BY LAW,  OF  NOT
 MORE THAN ONE THOUSAND DOLLARS FOR EACH VIOLATION.
  §  2.  This  act shall take effect on the ninetieth day after it shall
 have become a law.  Effective  immediately,  the  addition,  amendment
 and/or repeal of any rule or regulation necessary for the implementation
