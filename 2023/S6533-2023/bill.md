# Extends authorization for the county of Fulton to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** S6533
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Mark Walczyk
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Extends authorization for the county of Fulton to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes until November 30, 2025.
