# Extends the authorization of the county of Hamilton to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes

**Bill ID:** A6712
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Robert Smullen
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends the authorization of the county of Hamilton to impose an additional one percent of sales and compensating use taxes to November 30, 2025.
