# Provides for the release of patient or client clinical records to correctional facilities or a public health authority

**Bill ID:** S7450
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** John Mannion
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Provides for the release of patient or client clinical records to correctional facilities or a public health authority.
