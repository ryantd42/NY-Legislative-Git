# Relates to prohibiting the expenditure of moneys of the state to private institutions for projects that include the construction of a hotel

**Bill ID:** A4151
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Daniel O'Donnell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the expenditure of moneys of the state by the dormitory authority to private institutions for projects that include the construction of a hotel or similar lodging facility, not including student housing.
