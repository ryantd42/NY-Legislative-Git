# Requires state office buildings to be in compliance with certain greenhouse gas emissions limits three years before such limits apply statewide

**Bill ID:** S2069
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2069 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2069)

## Summary

Requires state office buildings to be in compliance with the greenhouse gas emissions limits established by the New York state climate leadership and community protection act three years before such limits apply statewide.
