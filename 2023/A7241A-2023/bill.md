# Relates to limiting recordkeeping and reporting duties of public notaries

**Bill ID:** A7241AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Charles Lavine
**Status:** Vetoed

## Summary

Limits recordkeeping and reporting duties of public notaries to electronic notarization acts.
