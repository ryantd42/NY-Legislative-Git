# Relates to the hours during which liquor and/or wine stores may be open on Sunday

**Bill ID:** S2854
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Signed by Governor

## Summary

Expands the hours during which liquor and/or wine stores may be open on Sunday to 10 A.M. to 10 P.M.
