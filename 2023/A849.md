# Prohibits using toxic ammunition in the taking of wildlife

**Bill ID:** A849
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A849 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A849)

## Summary

Prohibits using toxic ammunition in the taking of wildlife; allows any federally approved nontoxic ammunition in the taking of wildlife.
