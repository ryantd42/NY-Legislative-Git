# Commemorating the 124th Anniversary of the Village of Fishkill, New York, on June 13, 2023

**Bill ID:** K268
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anil Beephan Jr.
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 268
 
BY: M. of A. Beephan
 
  COMMEMORATING the 124th Anniversary of the Village
  of Fishkill, New York, on June 13, 2023
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  the  intent  of this Legislative Body to honor and
commemorate  the  distinguished  histories  of  the  communities  which
comprise the noble body of this great Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Attendant  to  such  concern,  and in full accord with its
long-standing traditions, this  Legislative  Body  is  justly  proud  to
commemorate  the 124th Anniversary of the Village of Fishkill, New York,
on June 13, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Village of Fishkill is residence  to  just  over  2,000
people,  with  Interstate  84  running  through the southern part of the
village; and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore, a richly historical village, Fishkill was part
of the Revolutionary War with Washington and Hamilton both being  within
the village walls; and
 
  WHEREAS, Of historic origin, and remaining fruitful over the ebb and
flow  of decades of growth and change, the Village of Fishkill continues
to provide the quintessential quality of life for its residents; and
 
  WHEREAS, The residents of the Village  of  Fishkill  have  a  bright
outlook  for  the  future  of their community, which remains a beautiful
place in which to live and raise a family; and
 
  WHEREAS, In recognition of the Village of  Fishkill's  rich  history
and  enduring  contribution  to  the State of New York, this Legislative
Body is proud to pay tribute to this spirited town upon the occasion  of
its 124th Anniversary; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commemorate the 124th Anniversary of the Village of Fishkill on June 13,
2023, recognizing the significance of the role it continues to  play  in
the life of the community of the State of New York; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to the Village of Fishkill.
