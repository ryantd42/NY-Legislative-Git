# Requires licensed or certified school social workers in each elementary, intermediate, middle, junior high and senior high school

**Bill ID:** S54
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S54 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S54)

## Summary

Requires at least one full-time licensed or certified social worker to be employed in each elementary, intermediate, middle, junior high school and senior high school throughout the state.
