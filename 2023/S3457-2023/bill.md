# Relates to information cable television companies must provide to subscribers

**Bill ID:** S3457
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to information cable television companies must provide to subscribers regarding availability of required equipment at retail.

 AN ACT to amend the public service law, in relation to information cable
  television companies must provide to subscribers regarding availabili-
  ty of required equipment at retail

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-
 BLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
 
  Section 1. Subdivision 4 of section 224-a of the public service law is
 amended by adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows:
  (D) EACH CABLE  TELEVISION  COMPANY  SHALL  PROVIDE  TO  EACH  OF  ITS
 SUBSCRIBERS  AT  THE  TIME  OF  THE  INITIAL  SUBSCRIPTION  A  WRITTEN
 DESCRIPTION OF: (1) THE EQUIPMENT REQUIRED FOR  SUCH  SUBSCRIPTION;  (2)
 INFORMATION REGARDING HOW OR WHERE A SUBSCRIBER MAY PURCHASE SUCH EQUIP-
 MENT  AT  RETAIL; AND (3) INFORMATION REGARDING WHAT RETAIL EQUIPMENT IS
 COMPATIBLE WITH THE CABLE TELEVISION COMPANY  SYSTEM.  FOR  PURPOSES  OF
 THIS  PARAGRAPH  "EQUIPMENT" SHALL INCLUDE BUT NOT BE LIMITED TO A MODEM
 OR ELECTRONIC DEVICE THAT MAKES POSSIBLE THE TRANSMISSION OF DATA TO  OR
 FROM A COMPUTER VIA TELEPHONE OR OTHER COMMUNICATION LINES.
  ยง 2. This act shall take effect immediately.
