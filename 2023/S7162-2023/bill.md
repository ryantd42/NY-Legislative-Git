# Relates to designating a portion of the state highway system as the Assemblyman Pat M. Casale Memorial Bridge

**Bill ID:** S7162
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jake Ashby
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Relates to designating a portion of the state highway system constituting the bridge crossing over the Hudson River on state route four hundred seventy as the "Assemblyman Pat M. Casale Memorial Bridge".
