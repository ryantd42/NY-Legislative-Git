# Relates to prohibiting the use or access of the TikTok application on state-issued electronic devices

**Bill ID:** A3317
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Prohibits the use or access of the TikTok application on state-issued electronic devices.
