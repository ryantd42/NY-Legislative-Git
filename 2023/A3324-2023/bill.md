# Requires consideration of the New York state climate leadership and community protection act in certain procurement contracts

**Bill ID:** A3324
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harvey Epstein
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires consideration of an offerer's compliance with the New York state climate leadership and community protection act in certain procurement contracts.
