# Excludes cigarettes on hand prior to September 1, 2023 from tax increase where a cigarette tax stamp has been affixed as of the close of business on August 31, 2023.

**Bill ID:** S7569
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Excludes cigarettes on hand prior to September 1, 2023 from the tax increase where a cigarette tax stamp has been affixed as of the close of business on August 31, 2023.
