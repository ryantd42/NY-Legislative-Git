# Requires insurance and Medicaid coverage for inpatient and outpatient substance abuse treatment for a period of not less than forty-five days

**Bill ID:** A2412
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Billy Jones
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires insurance and Medicaid coverage for inpatient and outpatient substance abuse treatment for a period of not less than forty-five days.
