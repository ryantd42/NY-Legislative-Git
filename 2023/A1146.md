# Allows for justices to reside in any town, village or city within the same assembly district as the court such justice serves

**Bill ID:** A1146
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jen Lunsford
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1146 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1146)

## Summary

Allows for justices to reside in any town, village or city contained in whole or in part within the same assembly district as the court such justice serves.
