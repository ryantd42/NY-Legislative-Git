# Includes Stewart International Airport in the definition of covered airport location for the purposes of minimum wage rates

**Bill ID:** A6528
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jonathan Jacobson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Includes Stewart International Airport in the definition of covered airport location for the purposes of minimum wage rates.
