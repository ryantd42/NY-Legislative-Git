# Qualifies when kidnapping or related offenses constitute a sex offense

**Bill ID:** S747
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Luis R. Sepúlveda
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S747 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S747)

## Summary

Qualifies when kidnapping or related offenses that are committed in furtherance of a sex crime will be considered a sex offense.
