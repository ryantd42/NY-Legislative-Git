# Extends certain provisions relating to the boarding of out of state incarcerated individuals

**Bill ID:** S7373
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Julia Salazar
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends certain provisions relating to the boarding of out of state incarcerated individuals until September 1, 2026.
