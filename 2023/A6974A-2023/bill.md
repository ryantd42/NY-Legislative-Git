# Requires certain state police vehicles to carry jumper cables and requires officers operating such vehicles offer to use such jumper cables on disabled vehicles

**Bill ID:** A6974A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nader Sayegh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires certain state police vehicles to carry jumper cables; requires officers operating such vehicles offer to use such jumper cables on disabled vehicles.
