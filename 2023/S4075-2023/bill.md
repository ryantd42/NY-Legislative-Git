# Establishes the "public higher education quality initiative endowment" fund; repealer

**Bill ID:** S4075
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the "public higher education quality initiative endowment" fund.

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-

  Section  1.  Section  92-ee of the state finance law is REPEALED and a

  ยง 92-EE. PUBLIC HIGHER EDUCATION QUALITY INITIATIVE ENDOWMENT FUND. 1.
 THERE IS HEREBY ESTABLISHED IN THE CUSTODY OF THE COMPTROLLER A FUND  TO
 BE  KNOWN AS THE "PUBLIC HIGHER EDUCATION QUALITY INITIATIVE ENDOWMENT",
 HEREINAFTER, "THE ENDOWMENT." NOTWITHSTANDING ANY  OTHER  PROVISIONS  OF

 INVESTED BY THE COMPTROLLER AS  THE  SOLE  TRUSTEE  OF  SUCH  ENDOWMENT,
 EXCEPT SUCH INCOME AS MAY BE ALLOCATED OR DISTRIBUTED BY THE COMPTROLLER
 PURSUANT TO APPROPRIATION.

 THEIR  COMMUNITY  COLLEGES  AND  SHALL  NOT  BE  USED TO HIRE ADDITIONAL

 YORK  RESEARCH  FOUNDATION, CITY UNIVERSITY OF NEW YORK RESEARCH FOUNDA-
 TION OR ANY CAMPUS FOUNDATION OR AFFILIATED ENTITY OF THE STATE  UNIVER-
 SITY  OF  NEW  YORK  OR  CITY  UNIVERSITY OF NEW YORK. PROVIDED FURTHER,
 MONEYS IN THE ENDOWMENT MAY NOT BE INTERCHANGED OR COMMINGLED  WITH  ANY
 OTHER ACCOUNT OR FUND.
