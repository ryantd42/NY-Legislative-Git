# Relates to requirements for sick leave when such employee is subject to a mandatory or precautionary order of quarantine or isolation

**Bill ID:** A1816
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Goodell
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides requirements for sick leave when an employee is subject to a mandatory or precautionary order of quarantine or isolation due to COVID-19.

  THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND  ASSEM-
