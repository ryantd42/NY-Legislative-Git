# Congratulating the Corning High School Boys Cross Country team upon the occasion of capturing the NYSPHSAA Boys Class A Cross Country Championship on November 12, 2022

**Bill ID:** K480
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Philip Palmesano
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 480
 
BY: M. of A. Palmesano
 
  CONGRATULATING  the Corning High School Boys Cross
  Country team upon  the  occasion  of  capturing  the
  NYSPHSAA  Boys Class A Cross Country Championship on
  November 12, 2022
 
  WHEREAS, Individual and team championships are highly  sought  after
in  high  school  sports;  this  Legislative Body commends rare athletic
achievements and pays special  recognition  to  those  who  pursue  such
excellence and become examples for the youth of this great Empire State;
and
 
  WHEREAS,  Athletic  competition  helps to enhance both the moral and
physical development of young athletes, preparing them for the future by
instilling in them the importance of teamwork, encouraging a standard of
healthy living, and developing a sense of fair play and competition; and
 
  WHEREAS, This Legislative Body is justly proud to  congratulate  the
Corning  High  School  Boys  Cross  Country  team  upon  the occasion of
capturing the NYSPHSAA  Boys  Class  A  Cross  Country  Championship  on
Saturday,  November  12,  2022, at Vernon-Verona-Sherrill High School in
Verona, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, To the praise and  applause  of  their  excited  fans,  the
Corning  High  School  Boys  Cross  Country  team  captured  the NYSPHAA
championship title in the Boys Class A 5000 Meter Race; and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to this monumental win, the Corning High School
Boys Cross Country team won the New York State  Class  A  Cross  Country
Team Championship with a score of 60; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Corning High School Boys Cross Country team's amazing
performance and first place win at the NIKE New  York  Regional  Country
Championship  led to them qualifying for the NIKE National Cross Country
Championship in Portland, Oregon held on December 3,  2022;  they  ended
their  season  of  hard work by earning the title of 10th in the Nation;
and
 
  WHEREAS, The talented athletes who make up the Corning  High  School
Boys  Cross  Country  team  include: Ashton Bange, Gabe Cornfield, Tevin
McLaren, DJ Cornfield, Marcus Homa, Joshua Beres, and Jaxyn Mahoney; and
 
  WHEREAS, In a sport such as long  distance  running,  which  demands
stamina, speed and determination, Head Coach Ray Lawson, Assistant Coach
Andrew  Cartwright, and Volunteer Thomas Ketchum worked hard to hone the
skills of these champions; teaching these outstanding  athletes  lessons
which will prove invaluable both on and off the track; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Proudly donning the school's colors of black and gold, the
team's  family,  friends,  and  the  community  at  large  loyally  and
enthusiastically  supported  the  Corning High School Boys Cross Country
team  throughout  their  journey  as  they  ended  the  season  with  an
impressive overall record; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Sports  competition instills the values of teamwork, pride
and accomplishment, the Corning High School Boys Cross Country team  has
clearly  made  a  contribution  to  the  spirit of excellence which is a
tradition of their school; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate the Corning High School Boys Cross Country  team  upon  the
occasion  of  capturing  the  NYSPHSAA  Boys  Class  A  Cross  Country
Championship on November 12, 2022; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  the  Corning  High School Boys Cross Country team, Head
Coach Ray Lawson,  Assistant  Coach  Andrew  Cartwright,  and  Volunteer
Thomas Ketchum.
