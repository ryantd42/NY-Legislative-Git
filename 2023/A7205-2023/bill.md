# Relates to the operation of a motor vehicle by deaf or hard of hearing drivers

**Bill ID:** A7205
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Harry B. Bronson
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Relates to the operation of a motor vehicle by deaf or hard of hearing drivers; provides that no deaf or hard of hearing driver shall be required to wear a hearing aid while operating a motor vehicle provided that their vehicle is equipped with a full view mirror.
