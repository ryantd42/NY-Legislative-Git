# Exempts certain veterans service organizations from taxes on the sales of beer, wine and soda

**Bill ID:** S1809
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joseph P. Addabbo Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1809 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1809)

## Summary

Exempts certain veterans service organizations from taxes on the sales of beer, wine and soda.
