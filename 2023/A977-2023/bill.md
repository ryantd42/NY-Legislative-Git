# Relates to notice procedures prior to approval of projects by industrial development agencies

**Bill ID:** A977
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Michaelle C. Solages
**Status:** Signed by Governor
**PDF:** [A977 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A977)

## Summary

Relates to notice procedures prior to approval of projects by industrial development agencies; includes electronic correspondence with read-receipts.
