# Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "NYPD Detective Brian P. Simonsen Memorial Bridge"

**Bill ID:** A7123AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jodi Giglio
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Designates a portion of the state highway system as the "NYPD Detective Brian P. Simonsen Memorial Bridge" located in the town of Brookhaven, Suffolk County.
