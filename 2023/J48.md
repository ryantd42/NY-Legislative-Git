## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 48
 
BY: Senator GRIFFO
 
  HONORING  Town  Board member Alan Trombley for his
  more than 35 years of distinguished service  to  the
  Town of Lee, New York
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is the sense of this Legislative Body to recognize and
honor those distinguished citizens of this great Empire State  who  have
devoted  themselves  to  faithfully  serving the best interests of their
community and the needs of its residents; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Town  Board  member  Alan  Trombley  for  his  more  than  35  years  of
distinguished service to the Town of Lee, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, For more than three decades, Alan Trombley gave  tirelessly
of  his  time and energies as a steadfast public servant to the citizens
of the Town of Lee, New  York,  providing  his  beloved  community  with
exceptional counsel and leadership; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Throughout  his illustrious tenure as a member of the Town
Board, Alan Trombley worked alongside fellow elected officials  and  the
community  to  grow  the  Town  of  Lee,  attracting  new businesses and
residents to the area; and
 
  WHEREAS, Furthermore, he was instrumental in  countless  initiatives
aimed  at  improving the quality of life for residents of the community;
and
 
  WHEREAS, In addition to his role with the Town Board, Alan  Trombley
spent his career as an educator for 36 years in the Rome School District
where he continues to serve as a substitute teacher; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Through his dedication and tireless efforts, Alan Trombley
has helped make the Town of Lee a place where  families  want  to  live,
tourists  want  to  visit, and businesses want to locate; his unwavering
commitment, willingness and ability to understand  and  respond  to  the
concerns  of  the people of Lee, have made a substantial contribution to
the betterment of this community; and
 
  WHEREAS, For more than 35 years, Alan  Trombley  rendered  faithful,
conscientious  and  valuable  service  to  the  Town of Lee, earning the
admiration, esteem and affection of his colleagues; and
 
  WHEREAS, Rare indeed is  the  occurrence  of  such  a  compassionate
blending  of  strength,  leadership,  intellect,  and commitment as that
demonstrated  by  Alan  Trombley  over  a  lifetime  of  sacrifice  and
dedication to others; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Town Board member Alan Trombley for his  more  than  35  years  of
distinguished  service  to  the Town of Lee, and to wish him well in all
his future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Alan Trombley.
