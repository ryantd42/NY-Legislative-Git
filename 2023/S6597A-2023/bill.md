# Requires restaurants and persons authorized to sell alcoholic beverages to have tangible menus available upon request

**Bill ID:** S6597AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian Kavanagh
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires restaurants and persons authorized to sell alcoholic beverages to have tangible menus available upon request.
