# Expands off-track betting to include the five counties comprising the city of New York; repealer

**Bill ID:** A3527
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** J. Gary Pretlow
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Expands off-track betting to include the five counties comprising the city of New York which shall now be considered part of the Catskill region.
