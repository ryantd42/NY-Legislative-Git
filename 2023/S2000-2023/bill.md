# Allows geriatricians to apply for both the physician loan repayment program and the physician practice support program

**Bill ID:** S2000
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Rachel May
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S2000 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S2000)

## Summary

Allows geriatricians to apply for both the physician loan repayment program and the physician practice support program, provided such geriatrician agrees to practice geriatrics for at least 5 years.
