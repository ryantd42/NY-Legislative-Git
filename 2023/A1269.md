**Bill ID:** A1269

**PDF:** [A1269 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1269)

 contained therein OR SPECIAL DISTRICTS ESTABLISHED PURSUANT  TO  ARTICLE
 NINETEEN-A OF THIS CHAPTER.

 OF THIS CHAPTER.
