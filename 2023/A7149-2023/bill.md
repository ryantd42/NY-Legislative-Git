# Excludes deer management professionals retained by the incorporated village of Lloyd Harbor from the hunting rifle ban on Long Island

**Bill ID:** A7149
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Keith Brown
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Excludes deer management professionals retained by the incorporated village of Lloyd Harbor from the hunting rifle ban on Long Island.
