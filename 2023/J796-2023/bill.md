# Congratulating Mark Carman upon the occasion of being named the Nassau County Mathematics Teacher of the Year on May 11, 2023

**Bill ID:** J796
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 796
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  CONGRATULATING  Mark  Carman  upon the occasion of
  being named the Nassau County Mathematics Teacher of
  the Year on May 11, 2023
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative  Body  to  commend  the
efforts  of those individuals in the teaching profession who distinguish
themselves in their dedication and commitment to their students; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing  traditions,  this  Legislative  Body  is  justly proud to
congratulate  Mark  Carman  upon  the  occasion  of  being  named  the
Mathematics  Teacher  of  the  Year  by  the  Nassau  County Mathematics
Teachers Association to be celebrated at its Annual Spring Reception  on
Thursday, May 11, 2023; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  dedicated  teacher  at  Roslyn High School, Mark Carman
sincerely cares about each  and  every  one  of  his  students  and  his
contributions  to  the  mathematics  profession are truly impressive and
worthy of recognition; and
 
  WHEREAS, Educators like Mark Carman provide a tremendous service  to
the  future  of  our  State  and  Nation, inspiring generations of young
people in their educational journeys and ensuring their success in their
future years; and
 
  WHEREAS, Mark Carman has clearly had an enduring impact on the youth
of Roslyn High  School,  as  well  as  on  the  community-at-large,  and
accordingly  merits  recognition  and  applause  for  his  impressive
dedication to others; and
 
  WHEREAS, Throughout his career thus far, Mark Carman has brought  an
uncompromising  standard  of  excellence to his work and a warm sense of
caring and concern to every young person he educates; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
congratulate  Mark  Carman  upon  the occasion of being named the Nassau
County Mathematics Teacher of the Year  on  May  11,  2023;  and  be  it
further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Mark Carman.
