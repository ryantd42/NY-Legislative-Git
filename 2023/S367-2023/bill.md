# Eliminates the requirement that hunters wear back tags during hunting season in the state; repealer

**Bill ID:** S367
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patrick M. Gallivan
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S367 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S367)

## Summary

Eliminates the requirement that hunters wear back tags during hunting season in the state.
