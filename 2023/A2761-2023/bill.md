# Requires public utility authorities to obtain legislative approval before raising rates or fees, or imposing new rates or fees

**Bill ID:** A2761
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jerett Gandolfo
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires public utility authorities to obtain legislative approval before raising rates or fees, or imposing new rates or fees.
