  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  ยง 13.19 Free use of campsites.  Notwithstanding the provisions of  any

 awarded by the federal government an allowance towards the  purchase  of

 places of recreation in this state, upon the same terms  and  conditions
 as  apply  to the general public, but without the payment of any fees or
 other charges for the use of such campsites, CABINS, COTTAGES, parks and
 other public places of recreation. USE OF CABINS AND COTTAGES UNDER THIS
