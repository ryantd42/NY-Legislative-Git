# Establishes the New York state first home savings program to authorize first time home buyers to establish savings accounts to buy their first home

**Bill ID:** A6933
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Philip Ramos
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes the New York state first home savings program to authorize first time home buyers to establish savings accounts to buy their first home.
