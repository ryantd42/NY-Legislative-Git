# Provides for the exemption of disabled veterans of the armed forces of the United States from New York state thruway tolls

**Bill ID:** A2051
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stephen Hawley
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides for the exemption of disabled veterans of the armed forces of the United States from New York State Thruway tolls.
