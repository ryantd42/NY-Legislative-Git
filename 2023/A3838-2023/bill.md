# Provides that the winnings from off-track betting, remaining unclaimed by April 1 each year, shall become part of the regional off-track betting corporation's operating account; repealer

**Bill ID:** A3838
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Provides that the winnings from off-track betting, remaining unclaimed by April 1 each year, shall become part of the regional off-track betting corporation's operating account.
