# Exempts the costs associated with expenditures resulting from the purchase of BOCES services and programs from the real property tax levy limit

**Bill ID:** A4152
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Conrad
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Exempts the costs associated with expenditures resulting from the purchase of BOCES services and programs from the real property tax levy limit.
