# Relates to a pilot program to increase enrollment, attendance, and success in college for students experiencing homelessness

**Bill ID:** S4248
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Andrew Gounardes
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes a pilot program under the HESC to increase enrollment, attendance, and success within SUNY/CUNY for students experiencing homelessness.
