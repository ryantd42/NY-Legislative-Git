# Requires agricultural trailers to be covered under the towing vehicles liability insurance

**Bill ID:** A4322
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Aileen Gunther
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires agricultural trailers being covered under the towing vehicles liability insurance.
