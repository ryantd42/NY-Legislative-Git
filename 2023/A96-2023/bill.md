# Enacts the "rebuild our schools act"

**Bill ID:** A96
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Nader Sayegh
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A96 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A96)

## Summary

Relates to the apportionment of moneys for capital outlays and debt service for school building purposes.
