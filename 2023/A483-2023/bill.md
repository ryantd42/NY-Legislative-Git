# Relates to establishing the crimes of cruelty to animals to threaten, intimidate or harass in the second degree and first degree

**Bill ID:** A483
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A483 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A483)

## Summary

Establishes the crimes of cruelty to animals to threaten, intimidate or harass in the second degree and cruelty to animals to threaten, intimidate or harass in the first degree when in the presence of a minor child.
