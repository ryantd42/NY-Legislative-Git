# Excludes tax levies necessary for expenditures increasing school safety or security from the school district tax levy limit

**Bill ID:** A3507
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe DeStefano
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Relates to excluding tax levies necessary for expenditures increasing school safety or security from the school district tax levy limit.
