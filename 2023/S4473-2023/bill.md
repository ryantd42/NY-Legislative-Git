# Establishes a tax credit for taxpayers who donate equipment or materials to first responder live training programs

**Bill ID:** S4473
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Establishes the first responder live training tax credit.
