# Establishes the New York state Ireland trade commission

**Bill ID:** S7083
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Timothy M. Kennedy
**Status:** Stricken

## Summary

Establishes the New York state Ireland trade commission to advance business and economic relations between New York state and the Island of Ireland.

  (3)  THE  LEGISLATIVE MEMBERS OF THE COMMISSION SHALL SERVE DURING THE

  (II) ELEVEN MEMBERS OF THE COMMISSION SHALL BE APPOINTED BY THE GOVER-

  2. THE TEMPORARY PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE SHALL DESIGNATE TWO CO-CHAIR-

  3.  (A)  THE  PURPOSE  OF  THE NEW YORK STATE IRELAND TRADE COMMISSION
 SHALL BE TO:
  (I) ADVANCE BILATERAL TRADE AND INVESTMENT BETWEEN THE STATE  AND  THE
 ISLAND OF IRELAND;
  (II)  INITIATE JOINT ACTION ON POLICY ISSUES OF MUTUAL INTEREST TO THE
 STATE AND THE ISLAND OF IRELAND;
  (III) PROMOTE BUSINESS AND ACADEMIC EXCHANGES BETWEEN  THE  STATE  AND
 THE ISLAND OF IRELAND;
  (IV)  ENCOURAGE  MUTUAL  ECONOMIC  SUPPORT  BETWEEN  THE STATE AND THE
 ISLAND OF IRELAND;
  (V) ENCOURAGE MUTUAL INVESTMENT IN THE INFRASTRUCTURE OF THE STATE AND
 THE ISLAND OF IRELAND; AND
  (VI) ADDRESS SUCH OTHER ISSUES AS DETERMINED BY THE COMMISSION.
  (B) THE COMMISSION SHALL REPORT ITS FINDINGS, RESULTS, AND RECOMMENDA-
 TIONS TO THE GOVERNOR, AND THE  LEGISLATURE,  WITHIN  ONE  YEAR  OF  ITS
 INITIAL  ORGANIZATIONAL MEETING AND BY FEBRUARY FIRST OF EACH SUCCEEDING
 YEAR FOR THE ACTIVITIES OF THE PRECEDING CALENDAR YEAR. THE REPORT SHALL
 BE IN WRITING AND INCLUDE RECOMMENDATIONS AS DEEMED APPROPRIATE  BY  THE
 COMMISSION TO EFFECTUATE ITS PURPOSE, AS PROVIDED BY THIS SECTION.
  4.  THE NEW YORK STATE IRELAND TRADE COMMISSION SHALL BE AUTHORIZED TO
 RAISE FUNDS, THROUGH DIRECT SOLICITATION OR  OTHER  FUNDRAISING  EVENTS,

 FORTH  IN  THIS  SECTION. THE FUNDS, GIFTS, GRANTS, OR BEQUESTS RECEIVED
