# Commending Class of 2023 Valedictorian Trinity Falayi and Salutatorians  Adrianna Pink and Osfarnia Prospere of P.S./I.S. 66

**Bill ID:** J1157
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Roxanne J. Persaud
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 1157
 
BY: Senator PERSAUD
 
  COMMENDING  Class  of  2023  Valedictorian Trinity
  Falayi and Salutatorians Adrianna Pink and  Osfarnia
  Prospere  of  P.S./I.S.  66, in recognition of their
  outstanding accomplishments
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this Legislative Body to act  in  accord
with  its  long-standing  traditions,  to  honor the youth of today, the
leaders of tomorrow, whose  character  and  achievements  exemplify  the
ideals and values cherished by this great State and Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS,  This  Legislative Body is justly proud to commend Class of
2023 Valedictorian Trinity Falayi and Salutatorians  Adrianna  Pink  and
Osfarnia  Prospere  of P.S./I.S. 66, in recognition of their outstanding
accomplishments; and
 
  WHEREAS, Valedictorian Trinity  Falayi  and  Salutatorians  Adrianna
Pink  and  Osfarnia  Prospere  represent the best of developed potential
inherent in our most precious resource, our  youth;  their  achievements
have brought enduring honor to their families and communities and should
be recognized and saluted; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valedictorian  Trinity  Falayi  and Salutatorians Adrianna
Pink and Osfarnia Prospere may now stand with pride as they assess their
achievements, experience the satisfaction of their labors and the joy of
their  accomplishments,  eager  to  face  the  new  experiences  of  a
challenging world; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
commend Class of 2023 Valedictorian  Trinity  Falayi  and  Salutatorians
Adrianna  Pink  and Osfarnia Prospere of P.S./I.S. 66, in recognition of
their outstanding accomplishments; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That copies of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted  to  Valedictorian Trinity Falayi and Salutatorians Adrianna
Pink and Osfarnia Prospere.
