# Establishes a billionaire mark-to-market tax

**Bill ID:** A3252
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Anna Kelles
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a billionaire mark-to-market tax taxing residents with one billion dollars or more in net assets.
