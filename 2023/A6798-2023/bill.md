# Prohibits the sale of sodium nitrate and sodium nitrite to any person under the age of twenty-one

**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Grace Lee
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Prohibits the sale of sodium nitrate and sodium nitrite to any person under the age of twenty-one.
