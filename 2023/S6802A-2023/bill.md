# Establishes a school speed zone camera demonstration program in the city of Albany

**Bill ID:** S6802A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Neil D. Breslin
**Status:** Senate Floor Calendar

## Summary

Establishes a school speed zone camera demonstration program in the city of Albany; repeals authorization of program December 31, 2028.
