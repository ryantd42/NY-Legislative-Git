# Requires the trustees of the The State University of New York to develop a SUNY-wide zero waste action plan

**Bill ID:** A7893
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Patricia Fahy
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Requires the trustees of The State University of New York to develop a SUNY-wide zero waste action plan.
