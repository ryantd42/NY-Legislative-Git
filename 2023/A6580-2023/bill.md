# Extends authorization of the county of Herkimer to impose a county recording tax on obligation secured by a mortgage on real property

**Bill ID:** A6580
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Brian D. Miller
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar

## Summary

Extends authorization of the county of Herkimer to impose a county recording tax on obligation secured by a mortgage on real property to 12/01/2025.
