# Exempts the sanitation police of the department of sanitation of the city of New York from training requirements for security guards

**Bill ID:** A7310
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Stacey Pheffer Amato
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Exempts the sanitation police of the department of sanitation of the city of New York from training requirements for security guards.
