# Congratulating Sydney Grimm upon the occasion of receiving the Girl Scout Silver Award, Cadette Scouting's highest award and Girl Scouting's second highest achievement

**Bill ID:** J357
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 357
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  CONGRATULATING  Sydney  Grimm upon the occasion of
  receiving  the  Girl  Scout  Silver  Award,  Cadette
  Scouting's  highest award and Girl Scouting's second
  highest achievement
 
  WHEREAS, Sydney Grimm, through her diligence and  rigorous  efforts,
has  achieved  Cadette  Scouting's  highest  and  Girl Scouting's second
highest  achievement,  the  Silver  Award;  the  Willistons,  Albertson,
Roslyn, Mineola (W.A.R.M.) Girl Scout Association Award Ceremony will be
held  on Tuesday, February 28, 2023, at Chaminade High School's Activity
Athletic Center in Mineola, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sydney Grimm is a member of  the  Girl  Scouts  of  America
Troop 1162; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Girl  Scouting has cultivated in our Nation's young people
the goals of building solid values of sisterhood and the  importance  of
working  with  others, stimulating both mind and body, instilling pride,
and encouraging community service and success; and
 
  WHEREAS, Girl Scouting has expanded the horizons  of  the  countless
hundreds  of  thousands who have participated in Scouting programs which
have made Scouting an American and international institution; and
 
  WHEREAS, Sydney Grimm completed a  Silver  Award  community  service
project  to  beautify  the Williston Park Post Office which she planned,
developed and executed; and
 
  WHEREAS, Along with the rest  of  her  troop,  Sydney  Grimm  worked
tirelessly  to  clean  the  weeds, trim bushes, lay down mulch, sand and
repaint railings, renovate two old worn four-foot benches to use  for  a
new  eight  foot  elegantly stained bench, repaint lanterns, replace all
the shutters on the front of the  building,  and  plant  beautiful  mums
outside the post office; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Sydney  Grimm  is  a credit to her family, her school, her
community, her State and her Nation, and no doubt  her  future  will  be
filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
congratulate Sydney Grimm, recognizing her achievement  in  earning  the
Girl  Scout  Silver  Award, and to wish her continued success in all her
future endeavors; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Sydney Grimm.
