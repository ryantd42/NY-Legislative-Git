# Relates to the issuance of distinctive license plates for members of veterans organizations

**Bill ID:** A1761
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** William Colton
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Directs the issuance of special number and distinctive license plates for military service members and members of veterans organizations without payment of service charges or renewal fees.

  THE  PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEW YORK, REPRESENTED IN SENATE AND ASSEM-

  Section 1. Subdivision 1-a of section 404 of the vehicle  and  traffic
 law,  as  amended by chapter 105 of the laws of 2017, is amended to read
 as follows:
  1-a. The commissioner may  issue  special  number  AND/OR  DISTINCTIVE
 LICENSE  plates  to applicants who are members of veterans organizations
 OR ACTIVE DUTY MILITARY MEMBERS in  the  same  manner  as  other  number
 plates  are  issued pursuant to this article. Such special number plates
 OR DISTINCTIVE PLATES shall be issued only upon (a) payment of  [a  one-
 time service charge of ten dollars in addition to] the regular registra-
 tion fee prescribed by section four hundred one of this article, and (b)
 submission  of  proof, satisfactory to the commissioner, that the appli-
 cant is presently AN ACTIVE DUTY MEMBER OR  a  member  of  the  veterans
 organization  for  which such plate is requested. NO ADDITIONAL FEES FOR
 CUSTOMIZATION OF SUCH PLATES SHALL BE REQUIRED. Such membership shall be
 verified annually by the applicant. Application for such plates shall be
 made in accordance with regulations promulgated by the commissioner with
 respect to the issuance of such plates. Provided, however, that  nothing
 contained  herein shall be deemed to supersede the provisions of section
 four hundred four-d, four hundred  four-e,  four  hundred  four-h,  four
 hundred  four-i,  four hundred four-j, four hundred four-k, four hundred
 four-p, four hundred four-aa, or four hundred four-dd of this article.
