# Honoring Linda Zazek upon the occasion of her retirement after 32 years of distinguished service to the Delaware Chenango Madison Otsego Board of Cooperative Educational Services

**Bill ID:** K568
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe Angelino
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Assembly Resolution No. 568
 
BY: M. of A. Angelino
 
  HONORING  Linda  Zazek  upon  the  occasion of her
  retirement after 32 years of  distinguished  service
  to  the  Delaware  Chenango  Madison Otsego Board of
  Cooperative Educational Services
 
  WHEREAS, It is the sense of this  Legislative  Body  to  acknowledge
those  individuals whose professional lives and civic endeavors serve to
enhance the stature of the State of New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Linda  Zazek  upon  the  occasion  of  her  retirement after 32 years of
distinguished service to the Delaware Chenango Madison Otsego  Board  of
Cooperative  Educational  Services  (DCMO  BOCES)  on Wednesday, June 7,
2023; and
 
  WHEREAS, For the past  32  years,  Linda  Zazek  rendered  faithful,
conscientious, and valuable service to DCMO BOCES; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Linda Zazek served as a Board Member for the Gilbertsville
Mt.  Upton School District from 1989-1997; in 1999, she began her tenure
with  the  Delaware  Chenango  Madison  Otsego  Board  of  Cooperative
Educational  Services,  the  esteemed  institution  from  which she will
retire; and
 
  WHEREAS, In 2003, she was elected to the  role  of  Vice  President,
dutifully serving in this capacity until 2013; and
 
  WHEREAS, The following year, 2014, she was elected to serve as Board
President,  bringing  her distinguished poise and professionalism to the
role for seven years; and
 
  WHEREAS, A testament to her character and  good  faith  service,  in
2007,  Linda Zazek was honored as Outstanding School Board Member by the
Chenango County School Board Association; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Linda  Zazek  always  served  with  loyalty,  honor,  and
distinction in the variety of roles she fulfilled; and
 
  WHEREAS,  In  her  official acts, Linda Zazek was governed by a keen
sense of duty and always showed a unique grasp of human problems; and
 
  WHEREAS, During her tenure with DCMO BOCES, Linda Zazek  earned  the
admiration,  esteem, and affection of her colleagues; now, therefore, be
it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its  deliberations  to
honor  Linda Zazek upon the occasion of her retirement after 32 years of
distinguished service; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Linda Zazek.
