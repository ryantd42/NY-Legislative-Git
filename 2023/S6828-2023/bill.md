# Relates to requiring the commissioner of health to establish means of educating proprietors and employees of public eating establishments about food allergens

**Bill ID:** S6828
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Kevin S. Parker
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Requires the commissioner of health to establish means of educating proprietors and employees of public eating establishments about food allergens.
