# Relates to the preservation of hair during chemotherapy

**Bill ID:** A38AA
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Linda Rosenthal
**Status:** Assembly Floor Calendar
**PDF:** [A38A PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A38A)

## Summary

Provides coverage for scalp cooling systems used to preserve hair during cancer chemotherapy treatment.
