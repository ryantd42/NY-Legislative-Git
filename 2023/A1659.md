# Authorizes a distinctive "guardians for schools" license plate; and establishes the guardians for schools fund

**Bill ID:** A1659
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Josh Jensen
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A1659 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A1659)

## Summary

Authorizes a distinctive "guardians for schools" license plate; establishes the guardians for schools fund.
