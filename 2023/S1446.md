# Establishes a fund for the creation of affordable housing

**Bill ID:** S1446
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Sanders Jr.
**Status:** In Senate Committee
**PDF:** [S1446 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1446)

## Summary

Establishes a fund for the creation of affordable housing for veterans and seniors.
