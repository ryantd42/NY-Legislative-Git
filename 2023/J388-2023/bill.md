# Honoring Banker Orchards Farm Market upon the occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2023 Small Business of the Year Award by the North Country Chamber of Commerce

**Bill ID:** J388
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Dan Stec
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 388
 
BY: Senator STEC
 
  HONORING  Banker  Orchards  Farm  Market  upon the
  occasion of its designation as recipient of the 2023
  Small Business  of  the  Year  Award  by  the  North
  Country Chamber of Commerce
 
  WHEREAS,  It is the custom of this Legislative Body to recognize the
achievements  of  those  individuals  and  businesses  that  contribute
substantially  to  the  economic  vitality of their communities, thereby
enhancing the economic well-being of the entire Empire State; and
 
  WHEREAS, Attendant to such concern, and  in  full  accord  with  its
long-standing traditions, this Legislative Body is justly proud to honor
Banker  Orchards  Farm  Market  upon  the occasion of its designation as
recipient of the 2023 Small Business of the  Year  Award  by  the  North
Country  Chamber  of  Commerce, which was celebrated at its 111th Annual
Awards Dinner, "A Winter Wonderland," on Friday, January  27,  2023,  at
the West Side Ballroom in Plattsburgh, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Banker  Orchards Farm Market is receiving this prestigious
award for its steadfast and unremitting commitment to  its  Plattsburgh,
New York, and the North Country communities; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Established  in  1991,  Banker  Orchards  Farm  Market has
tirelessly served its community for more than three decades; and
 
  WHEREAS, Under the able leadership  of  its  owner,  Nina  Sullivan,
Banker Orchards Farm Market offers a variety of freshly grown apples and
an  array  of  fresh  baked  goods, including apple, pumpkin, raspberry,
strawberry rhubarb, and blueberry pies; furthermore, this  extraordinary
establishment grows all its own apples and pumpkins; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Additionally,  Bankers  Orchards Farm Market has a Petting
Zoo onsite with pigs, goats, llamas, miniature donkeys and horses; it is
open to the public, as well as scheduled class trips  on  weekdays,  and
birthday parties on weekends; and
 
  WHEREAS,  A  true  Adirondack  Coast treasure, Nina Sullivan and her
devoted team have built a quality orchard that is  a  truly  stand-alone
destination; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Since its inception, Banker Orchards Farm Market has stood
as a beloved establishment which has provided  countless  families  with
lasting memories; and
 
  WHEREAS,  It  is  with  great  pleasure  that  this Legislative Body
acknowledges this exceptional business  and  its  contributions  to  the
local  and  State  economies,  fully  confident that it will continue to
enjoy  the  sparkling  successes  it  has  experienced  thus  far;  now,
therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED,  That  this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations to
honor Banker Orchards Farm Market upon the occasion of  its  designation
 
as  recipient  of the 2023 Small Business of the Year Award by the North
Country Chamber of Commerce; and be it further
 
  RESOLVED,  That  a  copy  of this Resolution, suitably engrossed, be
transmitted to Nina Sullivan, Banker Orchards Farm Market.
