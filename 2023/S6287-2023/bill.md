# Designates that portion of interstate eighty-four that lies within the county of Putnam as the "Putnam County Purple Heart Highway"

**Bill ID:** S6287
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Pete Harckham
**Status:** In Senate Committee

## Summary

Designates that portion of interstate eighty-four that lies within the county of Putnam as the "Putnam County Purple Heart Highway".
