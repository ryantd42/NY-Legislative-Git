# Excludes volunteer firefighters and emergency medical services employees from the permissive use of marijuana

**Bill ID:** A3487A
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Joe DeStefano
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Excludes volunteer firefighters and emergency medical services employees from the permissive use of marijuana.
