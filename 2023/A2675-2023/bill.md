# Establishes a commission to study and report on the potential implementation of legislation, rules and regulations for an open internet for New Yorkers

**Bill ID:** A2675
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Clyde Vanel
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Establishes a commission to study and report on the potential implementation of legislation, rules and regulations for an open internet for New Yorkers.
