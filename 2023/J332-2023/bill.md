# Congratulating Valeriana Pirozzi upon the occasion of receiving the distinguished Girl Scout Bronze Award, the most prestigious of Junior Scouting honors

**Bill ID:** J332
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Jack M. Martins
**Status:** Adopted

---

## Full Text

Senate Resolution No. 332
 
BY: Senator MARTINS
 
  CONGRATULATING Valeriana Pirozzi upon the occasion
  of  receiving  the  distinguished  Girl Scout Bronze
  Award,  the  most  prestigious  of  Junior  Scouting
  honors
 
  WHEREAS,  Valeriana  Pirozzi,  through  her  diligence  and rigorous
efforts, has achieved Girl Scout Junior's highest and  most  prestigious
honor,  the  Girl Scout Bronze Award; the Willistons, Albertson, Roslyn,
Mineola (W.A.R.M.) Girl Scout Association Award Ceremony will be held on
Tuesday, February 28, 2023, at Chaminade High School's Activity Athletic
Center in Mineola, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS, Valeriana Pirozzi is a member of  Girl  Scouts  of  America
Troop 1810; and
 
  WHEREAS,  America has achieved its greatest heights of glory through
the dedication, determination, and tireless efforts of  the  outstanding
citizens of this Nation; and
 
  WHEREAS, It is only with the continued diligence and devotion of our
youth, tomorrow's leaders, that America will maintain its glory; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Girl  Scouting has cultivated in our nation's young people
the goals of building solid values of sisterhood and the  importance  of
working  with  others, stimulating both mind and body, instilling pride,
and encouraging community service and success; and
 
  WHEREAS, Girl Scouting has expanded the horizons  of  the  countless
young  women  who have participated in the programs which have made Girl
Scouting an American and international institution; and
 
  WHEREAS, The Girl Scout Bronze Award requires the Girl Scout  Junior
to  learn the leadership and planning skills necessary to follow through
on a project that makes a positive  impact  on  her  community;  working
together  toward  this  award  demonstrates  her  commitment  to helping
others, improving her community and the world, and becoming the best she
can be; and
 
  WHEREAS, To earn the Bronze Award,  Valeriana  Pirozzi  completed  a
Bronze Award community service project which she planned, developed, and
executed; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Thinking  about  the  economic  impact  of  the  pandemic,
including job loss and  potential  loss  of  donations  that  charitable
organizations faced, along with their love for animals, Troop 1810 chose
to  collect  toys, leashes, and food for animals for Cove Animal Shelter
in Glen Cove, New York; and
 
  WHEREAS,  The  Bronze  Award  symbolizes  a  Girl  Scout  Junior's
accomplishments in Girl Scouting and community activities as she matures
and  works  to  better  her life and the lives of others; it is tangible
evidence of Valeriana Pirozzi's high character  and  commitment  to  her
community, New York State, and our Nation; and
 
 
  WHEREAS,  This outstanding young woman has been involved in Scouting
for years, has lived up to Scouting's ideals and has  attained  all  the
necessary achievements; and
 
  WHEREAS,  Valeriana  Pirozzi  is a credit to her family, her school,
her community, her State and her Nation, and no doubt her future will be
filled with accomplishments of equal importance; now, therefore, be it
 
  RESOLVED, That this Legislative Body pause in its deliberations,  in
recognition  of  the  significance  of  this meritorious achievement, to
congratulate and honor  Valeriana  Pirozzi  upon  the  occasion  of  her
designation  as  recipient  of  the  Girl  Scout Bronze Award; and be it
further
 
  RESOLVED, That a copy of this  Resolution,  suitably  engrossed,  be
transmitted to Valeriana Pirozzi.
