# Relates to permit and license applicant contributions

**Bill ID:** S1721
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** James Skoufis
**Status:** Stricken
**PDF:** [S1721 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/S1721)

## Summary

Prohibits contributions from permit and license applicants to certain entities and persons.
