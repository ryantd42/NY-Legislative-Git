# Authorizes an additional report and recommendations from the stretch limousine passenger safety task force and extends provisions of law relating thereto

**Bill ID:** A7087
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Angelo Santabarbara
**Status:** In Assembly Committee

## Summary

Authorizes an additional report and recommendations from the stretch limousine passenger safety task force; extends provisions of law relating thereto; requires such additional report be issued on or before December 1, 2024; extends such provisions of law until December 31, 2024.
