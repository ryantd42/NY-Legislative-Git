# Requires the surrender of antique firearms, black powder rifles, black powder shotguns, or muzzle-loading firearms following certain convictions

**Bill ID:** A949
**Session:** 2023
**Sponsor:** Amy Paulin
**Status:** In Assembly Committee
**PDF:** [A949 PDF](https://legislation.nysenate.gov/pdf/bills/2023/A949)

## Summary

Requires the surrender of antique firearms, black powder rifles, black powder shotguns, or muzzle-loading firearms following a conviction for a felony or serious offense or following a report pursuant to section 9.46 of the mental hygiene law.
